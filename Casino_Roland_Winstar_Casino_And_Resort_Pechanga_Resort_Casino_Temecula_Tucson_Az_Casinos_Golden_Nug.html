Leading sports betting operator Hollywoodbets announced a new sponsorship agreement with the South African professional rugby union team, The Sharks. The sponsorship, building upon an agreement between the duo dating back to 2019, designated the sports betting operator as the team's title sponsor. The latest title sponsorship complements Hollywoodbets' naming rights for the famous Hollywoodbets Kings Park stadium.

As a title sponsor of The Sharks, the betting operator will benefit from increased exposure. Hollywoodbets' logo will be displayed at the front and back of The Sharks' jerseys from the 2023/2024 rugby season. The sports betting operator will benefit from exposure via the team's jerseys for all competitions and the new jerseys will be available starting from this October.

The recent sponsorship agreement with The Sharks further solidifies Hollywoodbets' leadership position within the sports vertical in South Africa. At the same time, the agreement seeks to help promote further South African sports around the globe and support it as well.

The Sharks is a team with a rich history and it seeks to deliver thrilling experiences for its fans. The team is dedicated to sportsmanship and teamwork, which coincides with core values from Hollywoodbets. Joining forces, the two are expected to engage with new fans and deliver unique experiences for rugby fans and bettors alike. Under the new strategic sponsorship, Hollywoodbets vowed to bring engaging and thrilling activations.

Devin Heffer, Hollywoodbets' Brand and Communications Manager, shared his excitement about the latest agreement in a statement, saying that the company is honored to become a title sponsor of the famous South African rugby team. "Being a Durban-born and based company, we are massive Sharks fans! We know how much this team means to the fans and how it is an institution of our province," he added.

Heffer explained that Hollywoodbets will be delighted to support The Sharks' growth and help it reach new fans. He spoke about the values that the two organizations share and highlighted the importance of communities. "Through our title sponsorship of the Hollywoodbets Sharks, we look forward to further developing the future of South African rugby talent and working together in empowering our South African communities," added Heffer.

Dr Eduard Coetzee, The Sharks' CEO, was similarly excited and pointed out that the team is delighted to name Hollywoodbets as its new title sponsor. "The unwavering support, commitment to excellence, and investment over the last few years has been immense and we look forward to taking our valued partnership to the next level," he added. Finally, Coetzee said that the team is looking forward to the success the new sponsorship will bring to the duo.
