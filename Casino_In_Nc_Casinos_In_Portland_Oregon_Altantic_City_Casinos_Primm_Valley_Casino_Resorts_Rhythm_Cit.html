Multiplier Reveal, Free Spins & Dream Drop Jackpot

When the Multiplier Reveal is triggered, every start of every spin has two Multiplier Reveal symbols on the third and the fourth reels. Each symbol has its own counter and multiplier marked on it. The counter decreases by one unit every time the player comes across a winning cascade. When the counter reaches zero, the multiplier is engaged. This means all the nearby symbols are smashed, making room for fresh symbols. Landing three bonus symbols triggers the Free Spins bonus. An extra symbol awards one extra free spin.

The game’s hottest attraction is also one of the highlights of the slot industry: the Dream Drop Jackpot. This jackpot is triggered at random moments and allows players to win any of the five available progressive jackpots: Rapid, Midi, Maxi, Major and Mega.
