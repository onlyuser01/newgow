Multi-channel provider of 24/7 live betting services, Sports Information Services (SIS), announced a new deal with the operator Go+bet. The new deal marks the entry of SIS to the regulated market in Poland and brings for the first time the company’s leading greyhound and horse racing content for customers in the country.

Per the terms of the new deal, Go+bet customers in Poland will benefit from premium horse racing content provided by SIS. Such content will include events from Ireland and the UK, as well as international events from Germany, Hungary, Spain, Saudi Arabia, Canada and Dubai. Additionally, the clients will benefit from horse racing content from Latin America, Mexico and the United States. What’s more, the deal will see the delivery of greyhound racing content from Mexican, Irish and UK events.

The new collaboration reaffirms the growing interest in live racing content observed in Eastern Europe. Bettors like the option of having regular short-term wagering opportunities. This caused a spike in interest which helped increase the popularity of horse and greyhound racing, resulting in operators delivering attractive proposals.
