Golden State Warriors Boosted by Their Performance on the Weekend

After a surprising loss in Game 1, the Warriors made it 1-1 in the NBA Finals with a win on Sunday. They absolutely destroyed the Celtics, doing great both in the offense and defense. Sure, we’re used to them doing great in the offense. After all, they got Steph Curry on the roster, the man who scored 20+ points in 15 of his previous 17 matches.

What is kind of surprising is that the Warriors were able to keep the Celtics to just 88 points. They were marvelous in the defense, putting on one of the best defensive performances of the season. In fact, they conceded fewer points just in one game in 2022.

Boston Celtics Tend to Do Great at Home

With a 28-13 record at TD Garden, the Boston Celtics were one of the best home teams in the regular season. In the playoffs, they haven’t been as impressive on their turf. In fact, since the start of the postseason, we saw them lose four games in Boston. The most recent of those was the loss to the Miami Heat on May 27.

This Wednesday, oddsmakers think they’re going to win. It’s not just about the fact that they play at home; it’s also because some of their key men have been superb in the NBA Finals. Jayson Tatum was their best scorer in the previous game with 28 points, as well as six rebounds and three assists.

Jaylen Brown was also very great, scoring 17 points while making the same number of rebounds and assists as Tatum.

Golden State Warriors vs Boston Celtics Game 3 Prediction

Online sportsbooks see the Boston Celtics as the favorites in Game 3. However, judging by what we saw in the previous game, we think the Golden State Warriors mustn’t be underestimated. They definitely have the manpower to make it 2-1 in the NBA Finals.
