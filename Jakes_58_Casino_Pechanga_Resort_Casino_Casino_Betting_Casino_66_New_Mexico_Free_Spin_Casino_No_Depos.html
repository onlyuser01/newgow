Wizard Games, a NeoGames subsidiary and a leading iGaming content provider, expanded further its portfolio of content by announcing the launch of a new game. The company said Wednesday it released Book of Eternal Romance, its latest slot that tells an epic love story between Cleopatra and Marc Anthony.

The new slot game takes online players on an adventure to Egypt. The developer’s recent title uses standard card symbols that are low paying. However, the Book of Eternal Romance also features special high-paying symbols that include ancient Egyptian characters such as Bastet, Anubis, Marc Anthony and Cleopatra. The 5×3 slot game offers different hit frequencies and return-to-player (RTP) rates.

The Egyptian-Themed Slot Expands Further Wizard Games’ Offering

Simon Jagdhar, Wizard Games’ director of games studio, explained that the “Book of” series has proven to be popular within the gambling vertical. He added that the studio is excited to expand further its collection by introducing the new Egyptian-themed Book of Eternal Romance slot game.

“‘Book of’ is a popular genre that is universally enjoyed across the industry, and we’re delighted to launch Book of Eternal Romance, this time incorporating an Egyptian theme.“ Simon Jagdhar, director of games studio at Wizard Games

Players Can Benefit from Multipliers, Free Spins

During the base play of the slot game, the players can randomly trigger a feature called Eternal Romance. It opens an ancient book and spins a reel, showing a multiplier wheel. Once the reel stops on a specific multiplier, it is applied for the next spin, granting the possibility for significant payouts.

Additionally, the Book of Eternal Romance features Free Spins. Hitting three Scatter symbols opens the ancient book, giving the player a chance to select a special symbol. “Every fourth Scatter collected will unlock the next level of the feature, which awards three additional spins and the wheel with increased multiplier pay on the special symbol,” explained the game developer. Overall, there are four levels that can be unlocked, which can result in a win of up to 2,500x of the player’s bet.
