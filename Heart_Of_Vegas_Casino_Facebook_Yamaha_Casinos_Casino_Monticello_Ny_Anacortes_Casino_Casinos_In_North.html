Daniel Long Joins QTech Games

Leveraging his professional experience, Long is expected to help navigate QTech Games’ business development and accelerate the company’s growth within the digital entertainment vertical.

Sharing his excitement, Long said that joining the company at such an important time for its growth is delightful. He pointed out that his professional background confirms his ability to add value to organizations. QTech Games’ new CCO pointed out that the company has strong products but also acknowledged its leadership team that is focused on delivering unrivaled experiences for the company’s clients and partners.

“I’m delighted to join the QTech Games team at such an exciting time, helping define and implement commercial imperatives around aligning our partners and their customers.“ Daniel Long, chief commercial officer at QTech Games

Considering that QTech Games is a leading distributor of games in Asia, Long said that its products are both respected and trusted. Moreover, he acknowledged that it is thrilling to join the company’s team of professionals and embark on this new challenge. “I’m sure that we can now build on this success and gain even more market traction and brand recognition, as one of the largest providers of digital entertainment on the market,“ added Long
