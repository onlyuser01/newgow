Cantona was the perfect fit to represent William Hill’s new campaign, MD Alexis Zamboglou said

Eric Cantona, a French soccer legend, formerly playing for Manchester United, has joined British operator William Hill for a new advertising campaign. Under the slogan “Who You Got?”, the campaign will promote the gambling operator’s EPIC Value products.

William Hill’s new campaign launches today, just in time for the upcoming start of the 2023/24 soccer season. The campaign will leverage many new innovative marketing features to “generate excitement around the marquee moments in the sporting calendar.”

Cantona, who will serve as William Hill’s official brand ambassador for the next two years, will feature in the campaign across all media verticals. The campaign will run in the United Kingdom and Ireland, allowing local players to learn of the betting promotions available to them.

William Hill’s flagship sites will put up imagery of the legendary soccer athlete, highlighting his support for the operator’s cause. The company noted that the Who You Got? will allow players to see “King Eric” as they have never seen him before.

As teased by the operator, its new marketing campaign will highlight Cantona’s distinctive character on and off the pitch. Accompanied by Rudimental’s Feel the Love song, the bold and creative campaign is designed to appeal to both bettors and non-bettor sports fans.

Cantona’s Charisma Makes Him a Perfect Fit for the Campaign

Alexis Zamboglou, William Hill’s managing director for the United Kingdom and Ireland, expressed his thoughts on the new campaign. He said that the start of the new soccer season is a “huge moment” in the sports betting calendar, with demands peaking as fans and bettors prepare for the action.
