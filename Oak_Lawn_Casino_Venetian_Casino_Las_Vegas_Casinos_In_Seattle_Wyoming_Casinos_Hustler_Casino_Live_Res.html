EveryMatrix has appointed former Croatian Lottery executive Nikolina Gabelica as their new head of lottery

The provider of premium iGaming software, solutions, content, and services to worldwide Tier 1 operators and new brands, EveryMatrix, has attracted fresh talent to its top team in the person of former Croatian Lottery executive, Nikolina Gabelica.

Gabelica has been appointed the new head of lottery role. Her main responsibilities will cover leading the product and commercial strategy of the company for businesses belonging to the World Lottery Association and the European Lottery Association worldwide.

At the same time, the new head of lottery will need to focus on harnessing EveryMatrix’s “success in working with large-scale organizations such as the Hungarian National Lottery (SZRT) and Veikkaus,” according to the company’s statement.

Gabelica, “Hugely Respected in the Lottery World”

As commented by the Group’s chief executive officer, Ebbe Groes, Gabelica is “hugely respected in the lottery world with vast experience in the retail and digital requirements lotteries have today.”

Groes further acknowledged the constant need for adaptation to new tech shown by these businesses, along with the necessity to serve a new generation of customers searching for instant entertainment options across classic and online or mobile lotto products.

In this regard, EveryMatrix is already collaborating with some highly progressive members of the World Lottery Association who have successfully proven their ability to deliver game-changing technologies helping these businesses reach new heights.

The CEO wished “Niki” a warm welcome to the team while expressing plans to further extend their reach in the lottery space.

The New Head of Lottery, “Grateful” to Join EveryMatrix

Gabelica, who describes herself as a “seasoned and dedicated leader with over two decades of experience in the gaming industry,” has also commented on her new appointment.
