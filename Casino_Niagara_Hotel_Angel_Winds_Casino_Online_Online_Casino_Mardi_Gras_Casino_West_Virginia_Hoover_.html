Northern Sky review

Theme and Storyline

The aurora borealis, or Northern Lights, are a well known phenomenon that occurs as one gets closer and closer to the Arctic. Northern Sky uses this to create a stellar backdrop for its crystalline symbols. Bears, Caribou, Snowy Owls and Wolves inhabit the reels, rendered in a gorgeous diamond-esque style.

In the background a mountain rises towering out of snowy forests and the stars twinkle waiting for the bonus feature to trigger, bringing with it the aurora in all its colourful glory.

Hit at least one new symbol on any winline and you are guaranteed a respin

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The slot is a near perfect demonstration of how to build the visual and sound elements of a slot. The theme is strong, clear, and present in every visual detail. The symbols are beautiful and detailed, but also have strong silhouettes that make for easy-to-read reels at all magnifications.

Bigger cumulative wins are failry usual in the bonus round

The animations are simple but pretty, and they are timed to elicit that little bit of extra excitement when you land a win, or just miss a bonus symbol.

The sound, too, is well designed, building in time with the action and really drawing the player in.

Gameplay

Northern Sky is played on five reels with three rows and nine win lines.

Any winning combination pays out and then locks that combination in place for a free respin. Respins continue until no further winning combinations occur. The winnings are then paid out and the round ends.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

There is no Wild symbol in Northern Sky.

If you are lucky, you'll collect scatter symbols throughout the respin rounds

The Bonus feature is triggered when three bonus symbols land anywhere and at any time in one round (a round includes free respins). The Bonus round consists of ten free spins with only the high paying animal symbols on the reels.

Conclusion

Northern Sky does a fantastic job at the visual level. Luckily, this is also backed up under the hood, with excellent pacing and a respin mechanic that makes every win feel like a bonus round.
