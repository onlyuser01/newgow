The 2023 NASCAR Cup Series Championship Race is on the program on Sunday, November 5, at Phoenix Raceway, where four drivers will race for the title. Read our NASCAR Championship prediction to see who we think will become the 2023 champ

It’s time for the biggest NASCAR Cup Series race of 2023! The grand finale of the 2023 season is happening this coming Sunday in Phoenix where one of the drivers that made it to the Championship 4 will become the new champion.

Yes, the NASCAR Cup Series is going to get a new champion because last year’s winner Joey Logano failed to survive the Playoffs cut-off. Instead, the following four drivers are competing for the 2023 NASCAR title – Ryan Blaney, Christopher Bell, Kyle Larson, and William Byron.

As per NASCAR rules, each driver from the Championship 4 has 5000 ranking points, meaning that the previous results won’t matter in Phoenix. Instead, the one driver who finishes the race before the other three will be crowned the champion. This, however, doesn’t mean that they need to actually win the race. Anyone can do it, but judging by the 2023 NASCAR Phoenix odds, it’s likely that it’s gonna be one of the Championship 4 drivers.
