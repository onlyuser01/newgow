NeoGames, a tech-driven supplier of online gambling and lottery solutions announced a new deal between its sports betting arm BtoBet and PlayAlberta. This deal marks the brand’s foray into the lucrative Albertan market and comes a day after its parent company posted its Q2 results, highlighting stable growth and favorable expectations for its business.

As agreed, BtoBet will leverage its sports betting capabilities to power PlayAlberta with its digital sportsbook and solutions. To that end, the former party will supply its new partner with its proprietary fully-managed sportsbook solution that will dramatically improve the operator’s betting offering.

The migration of PlayAlberta’s online sportsbook to the BtoBet platform should wrap up in Q3 2023. The quick rollout is made possible by BtoBet’s impressive integration potential, speed-to-market capabilities and commitment to compliance. The two parties expect their partnership to expand at a fast pace.

PlayAlberta is an online gambling platform operated by Alberta Gaming, Liquor & Cannabis. The operator is also the only legal online gambling website in the province. While BtoBet is new to Alberta, the brand already has a presence in Canada, having secured a license in Ontario in early 2023.
