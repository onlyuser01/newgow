The new activation is expected to bring unique content and soccer branded games for fans of the sport

The freshly launched betting, media and soccer platform, Football.com, announced a landmark collaboration with one of the most recognizable faces in soccer. The company confirmed that it joined forces with the famous AS Roma manager and soccer legend, Jose Mourinho. Under the terms of the recent collaboration, the former soccer pro was designated as a Brand Ambassador for Football.com.

The company described the latest collaboration as a significant milestone within the soccer and iGaming vertical. Per the new collaboration, the duo agreed to deliver thrilling content that will cater to soccer fans and bettors alike.

The new agreement is expected to propel Football.com’s global footprint, which has already established its real-money gaming services in several markets where the sport is significantly popular. To elevate the experience for bettors and sports fans too, the new collaboration will see Mourinho participate in different activations such as articles.

Those articles are going to be released monthly and will be exclusive to Football.com, delivering interesting stories from the former professional soccer player. The exclusive content will cover Mourinho’s personal life, as well as unique moments from his extensive career within the sport.

Besides articles that will deliver valuable insights into the soccer world, the collaboration will deliver more activations. Mourinho and Football.com agreed to deliver soccer games, that are expected to bring further exciting moments for fans of the sport.

“Football has been my passion and my life, and I am delighted to be associated with the category-defining platform Football.com as their Brand Ambassador.“ Jose Mourinho, Brand Ambassador for Football.com

Sharing his excitement, Mourinho that it is thrilling for him to join the fast-growing platform as its Brand Ambassador. He acknowledged his extensive career in soccer, saying that it was a passion of his life. Finally, Mourinho added: “Together, we aim to redefine football-themed online entertainment, offering fans an experience like no other.”

“We are thrilled to welcome José Mourinho to the Football.com family.“ Elias Gallego, VP of business development at Football.com

Elias Gallego, Football.com’s VP of business development, was delighted to see Mourinho become the company’s ambassador and welcomed him to the team.
