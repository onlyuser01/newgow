Gameplay

Demon Pots is a five-reel, three-row slot, with 40 win lines.

The maximum win for this slot is 6,000x the player’s total bet.

The Ante Bet feature allows players to select a bet multiplier that may change the way the slot behaves. With a bet multiplier of 60x, players have an increased chance of triggering bonus features (see further details below).

If players wish they can buy directly into the Free Spins feature for 100x their total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Coin symbol.

The Coin symbol always appears with a random value from a predefined set. These values include 0.2x, 0.4x, 0.6x, 0.8x, 1x, 1.2x, and 2x the player’s total bet.

If a Wild symbol appears on the reels with a Coin symbol, all Coin symbol values are collected and paid.

Wild symbols can also trigger fireballs that activate the Coin Pots above the reels. The Coin Pots then sends 6 Coins to random positions on the reels. These Coins will have values ranging from 0.2x to 1x the player’s total bet.

If a Super Coins pot is activated, 6 Coins with values ranging from 0.4x to 2x the player’s bet are added to random positions on the reels.

When players activate the Win Spins pot, the Win Spins feature is activated. The feature begins with 6 respins, and the triggering Wild remains sticky at the center of the reels.

During the Win Spins feature, all respins are guaranteed to provide a random win. In the event that players activate the Win Spins pot during the Win Spins feature, the feature is retriggered.

The Free Spins feature begins when players activate the Free Spins pot, starting with 6 free spins. The Wild symbol that triggered the feature remains sticky at the center of the reels for the entire round. Any of the pots above the reels can be activated during the Free Spins feature.

Fully Stacked Wilds can land on reels 4 and 5 during the Free Spins feature. All Coin values are multiplied by 3x if a Stacked Wild lands on reel 5. Meanwhile, all Coin values are multiplied by 6x if Fully Stacked Wilds land on reels 4 and 5.

The Win Spins feature is available during the Free Spins feature. The Free Spins feature can also be retriggered in the event that the player activates the Free Spins pot.
