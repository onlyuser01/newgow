FanDuel, North America’s premier iGaming and online betting company, has teamed up the former NFL star Rob Gronkowski to release a new branded game. The fresh title is called Gronk Spike Cornhole and can be played for free or with real money.

The new game is the latest addition to FanDuel’s rapidly-expanding roster of Faceoff games where players can challenge each other for cash.

The new title is based on Gronkowski’s favorite yard game and allows players to change Gronk in a game of throwing bags. When playing Gronk Spike Cornhole, players will throw bags to the board by dragging their finger across the screen. Clever gamers can also take advantage of the mechanics and push Gronk’s bags off the board to score more points.

According to FanDuel, the new game allows players to experience the genuine thrill of a cornhole competition at the tip of their fingers.

FanDuel Faceoff is available for download, allowing players to experience exciting games where their skills matter and challenge real people for cash. Faceoff offers a large variety of genres, such as word, strategy, puzzle and sports games.

Gronkowski Wished Players Good Luck

Rob Gronkowski commented on the new release, sharing his excitement about the game. He shared that cornhole was a big part of his childhood and that he is excited to allow other players to experience the same excitement he did.

Gronkowski wished the best of luck to everyone who tries the new title, either for free or with money on the line.

I am so excited that Gronk Spike Cornhole will be available to FanDuel Faceoff players. I grew up playing cornhole against family and friends, and I was always the ultimate champion. Good luck to all those playing…for bragging rights or cash! Rob Gronkowski

FanDuel Continues to Solidify Itself as a Major NA Operator

The launch of the new game coincides with a new Michigan and Connecticut-oriented agreement between FanDuel and Bragg Gaming Group. Under the agreement, many of the supplier’s titles will become available to the operator’s customers in the aforementioned states.

In other news, FanDuel continues to support professional sports through agreements with sports leagues and teams. In early July, the operator signed a partnership agreement with the Canadian Football League, becoming its official sportsbook partner.

Earlier, the gambling company also reiterated its commitment to supporting women’s sports by renewing its partnership with the Women’s Tennis Association.

At the same time, FanDuel remains bullish on offering gambling in a safe and responsible manner, as attested by its recent appointment of Alison Kutler as VP of responsible gambling.
