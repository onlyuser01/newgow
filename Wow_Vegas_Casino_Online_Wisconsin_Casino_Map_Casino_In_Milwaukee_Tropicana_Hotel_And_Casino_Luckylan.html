Mega Mine review

Theme and Storyline

Mega Mine has a frontier mining theme.

Players will find themselves in the fresh air of the untamed wilderness beside a clear river. In the rocky terrain, they’ll discover a range of precious stones in tantalising hues.

This slot is a good pick for players who enjoy gem-themed or even Western-themed slots. The atmosphere has a clean, breathless sense of adventure about it that will make players feel ready to strike it rich.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack has an Old Western feel, blended with the splashes of a rushing river. This adds to the natural ambiance and makes players feel they are standing beside chilly water.

The reels themselves are packed with vivid gems from green emeralds to red rubies. Each one seems to be expertly cut to catch the light.

Behind the reels, players will see an idyllic wilderness scene filled with mountains, blue sky, pine trees, and a clear river and waterwheel. The graphics quality is decent and brings to mind a children’s animated feature film.

Through the gameplay, players are also transported deep into a mine where mystic fog drifts and lanterns gently glow. The glint of gems also illuminates the darkness of the deep underground world.

Overall, the soundtrack and graphics combine to create a pleasant frontier mining world.

Gameplay

Mega Mine is a five reel, four row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in at least three adjacent rows starting from the leftmost reel and going to the rightmost.

Players may opt to buy directly into the Free Spins feature (see further details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all regular symbols.

The Mystery Stacks will nudge into full view whenever they appear, revealing matching symbols. Mystery Stacks will show a value between 2 and 5, increasing by +1 for every row they nudge. The value shown when covering the reel corresponds to the number of symbols that each stack reveals.

Mystery Stacks may appear on any reel.

Mystery Stack symbol in the game

The Free Spin feature is triggered when players land 2 or more Scatter symbols. The feature begins with 6 free spins and players gain an extra free spin for each additional Scatter symbol that triggers the feature.

During the Free Spins feature, Nudging Mystery Stacks appear more often and may reveal Wild symbols.
