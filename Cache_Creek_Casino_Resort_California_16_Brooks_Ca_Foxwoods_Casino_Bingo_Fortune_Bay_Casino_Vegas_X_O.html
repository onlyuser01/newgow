The provider of high-availability network solutions, managed services and co-location for online companies, Continent 8 Technologies, announced a key addition to its commercial team. On Friday, the company revealed it appointed Andrew Mudge to the role of Regional Sales Director. The appointment of the new executive strengthens Continent 8 Technologies' global sales team and comes at a pivotal moment in its growth.

The new Regional Sales Director brings nearly a decade of experience within the gaming sector. In his new role, Mudge is expected to accelerate the company's growth and help it expand, reaching new customers. Leveraging his extensive experience, the former Viadex Global executive will contribute toward Continent 8 Technologies' expansion plan across the United States and beyond.

Currently, the company has established its presence in 30 US states and continues to expand its footprint. As Regional Sales Director, Mudge is expected to propel the company's US-facing growth, allowing its innovative and effective solutions, technology and services to reach new customers.

In a statement, Mudge revealed that it is thrilling to join Continent 8 Technologies at such a pivotal moment in the company's growth. "Having worked closely with the team during my eight years in the industry, I am very aware of the strong presence and dominant brand they have developed globally," he added. Finally, the new Regional Sales Director said: "I’m proud to be joining the leading infrastructure provider for gambling operators and suppliers, worldwide."

Brian Koh, Continent 8 Technologies' Vice President, Sales and Chief Commercial Officer, was also excited about the latest senior-level hire. He deemed Mudge a perfect addition to the company's growing team of experts, adding that he brings extensive experience and knowledge for the US sector. "This is an important region for us, and we are thrilled to be able to leverage his understanding of the market and the infrastructure that operators and suppliers need to succeed here," added Koh.

According to the Vice President, Sales and Chief Commercial Officer, Mudge's experience and the company's plan for continuous growth make the perfect combination. Finally, Koh said: "I’d like to take this opportunity to welcome Andrew to the global Continent 8 team and look forward to working with him as we strengthen our position as the leading infrastructure provider in the US and beyond."

Before joining Continent 8 Technologies, Mudge was a part of Viadex Global for more than eight years. During that time, he held important senior-level roles and helped navigate the company toward growth.
