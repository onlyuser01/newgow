Razor Returns review

Razor Returns is a shark-themed online slot game from Push Gaming. The game is a sequel to the hugely popular Razor Shark slot.

Players dive deep beneath the ocean and spend some time with a menacing pack of sharks as they bid to land some big prizes.

Win in the base game of the Razor Reveal slot

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The original Razor Shark slot was a huge hit for the studio, and Push has largely stuck with the same design. The reels are set deep beneath the water, with a group of menacing-looking sharps making up the higher-paying symbols on the grid.

The sharks have a cartoonish design, invoking memories of the popular Finding Nemo movies. There is nothing friendly about these creatures though, with the bite of the sharks being matched by the game’s biting gameplay.

Gameplay

Razor Returns looks scary, but the base game keeps things nice and simple.

The slot is played on a five-by-five grid, with the slot having 40 active paylines on each spin. Winning combinations are formed when players land matching combinations of three or more symbols across a payline, starting on the leftmost reel.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Razor Returns slot game really comes to life with its bonus features.

The first you are likely to encounter is the Mystery Symbol bonus. On any spin, Mystery Symbols, which are displayed as ocean plants, can land and reveal a matching symbol. These Mystery Symbols appear stacked and are then nudged down the reels in one position on each subsequent spin. To make this more exciting, the Mystery Symbols will change on each spin. It is possible to have more than one stack of Mystery Symbols active.

One of the highest possible multipliers in the Razor Reveal bonus round

If the Mystery Symbols reveal a Golden Shark symbol, then the Razor Reveal bonus is activated. For this, each position will turn into a mini-reel set and will display a selection of symbols. The symbols are as follows:

Players can trigger the Free Spins round by landing three or more Tornado Scatters. Three starts the round with x1 multiplier, while four and five start with x5 and x25, respectively. Before the round starts, a Gamble Feature is presented where players can spin to start the round with higher prizes. During the round, reels two and four are filled with Mystery Symbols. Each time these nudge down, they will boost the win multiplier by one. The spins continue until there are no Mystery Stacks left on the grid.

Depending on your location, you may be able to buy your way into the bonus rounds for predetermined amounts.
