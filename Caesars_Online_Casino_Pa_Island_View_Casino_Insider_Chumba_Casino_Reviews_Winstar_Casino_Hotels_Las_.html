Cascade+ Mechanic, Free Spins and Encore Mode

Thanks to the DoubleMax mechanic, players of Diamond Symphony DoubleMax have a chance to secure a prize of up to x20,000 their stake. Combining free spins and the Cascade+ mechanic further elevates the potential for big payouts. “The DoubleMax GEM ensures each cascading win increases a win multiplier by x2 and up to a maximum of x256, resulting in impressive prizes,” explained Yggdrasil.

Thanks to the Cascade+ mechanic, winning symbols are removed from the reel, enabling new symbols to fall into their place. This, in turn, can bring consequent wins. The engaging new title also offers a “free spins trail.” This functionality enables the players to track their progress toward free spins by securing consequent wins. The free spins trail allows the players to climb up, granting them between seven and 15 free spins.

Diamond Symphony DoubleMax offers an engaging functionality called Encore mode. This functionality enables the players to have a higher chance of winning a bonus in exchange for a 50% additional cost of their stake. According to Yggdrasil, Encore mode helps convert two “losing positions on the trail to respin positions.”

Taking the customer experience to a whole new level, Diamond Symphony DoubleMax also offers two Buy Bonus options. The cost for those options is 100x or 200x of the player’s stake but grants a chance for significant payouts, as well as free spins.
