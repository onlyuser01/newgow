ThriveFantasy, a provider of player prop fantasy sports betting markets, has teamed up with the Los Angeles Chargers, an NFL franchise. The operator is now the official Fantasy Sports partner for the Chargers, which will, in turn, enable the team’s fans to access daily fantasy contests throughout the entire season and benefit from other social activations.

ThriveFantasy prides itself on having created a platform that gives fans a chance at winning various fantasy contests, and now the app will also feature Chargers-only raffles that will add more value for users and help raise awareness for the partnership. Among the prizes that can be won are tickets to the Chargers’ home games at the SoFi Stadium.

Another Successful Partnership for ThriveFantasy

To mark the new collaboration, the operator will further donate an additional $25,000 to the Chargers Impact Fund, which the team runs to help tackle poverty in the greater Los Angeles region. Commenting on this partnership, ThriveFantasy founder and CEO Adam Weinstein had this to say: “This partnership allows us to both give back to those who need it most, at a time when so many are hurting, as well as offer creative ways to help the Chargers engage their fanbase.”

Chargers Impact Fund business operations head AG Spanos said that the organization was happy to welcome ThriveFantasy into the Chargers family at a time when fantasy sports continue to grow in popularity. He was appreciative of the donation that the company had forwarded.

“Thrive will give our fans a unique and engaging way to interact with their favorite team and, in this case, win cash prizes and tickets to Chargers Games at SoFi Stadium,” Spanos explained.
