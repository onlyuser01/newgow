Nuvei Corporation, a global payment tech company, has expanded its deal with Eyas Gaming, a new operator owned by gambling industry veterans. As per the agreement, Nuvei agreed to supply more of Eyas Gaming’s brands with its acclaimed payment solutions.

Eyas’ Brands to Adopt Nuvei’s Solutions

Following the extension of the deal, Eyas Gaming has secured access to Nuvei’s cashier solutions. Therefore, the provider will continue powering Merkur Slots and Merkur Casino, two of Eyas’ brands, with its acclaimed payment methods.

As a result, Eyas’ user base will be able to pay their deposits digitally, instead of using cash. This also applies to withdrawals, allowing players to transfer their money back into their banks, cards or digital wallets.

Moreover, the operator will now be able to allow its players to bill deposits on their mobiles. The latter solution is powered by Fonix, a direct carrier billing provider which recently teamed up with Nuvei.

Eyas first teamed up with Nuvei in October 2021. Since then, the payment solutions provider has been providing the operator’s brands with cashless payment methods. The partnership extension attests to the quality of Nuvei’s products and Eyas’ satisfaction with them.
