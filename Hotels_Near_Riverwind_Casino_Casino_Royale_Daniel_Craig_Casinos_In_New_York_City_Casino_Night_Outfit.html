Online software provider developing games for the iGaming industry, CT Interactive, announced it expanded its European presence. On Thursday, the company revealed that it entered the regulated gambling market in Slovakia.

Upon entering the Slovakian market, the company prepared an initial package with localized market-tailored games. The list of games includes Big Joker, Devil's Fruits, 20 Star Party, Lucky Clover, Hot 7s X 2, Hell's Sevens, Coffee Magic, Pick the Pig and Chilli Fruits. Although this list was deemed as an initial package, CT Interactive vowed to introduce its innovative Jackpot concepts along with more games for the Slovakian market soon.

Lachezar Petrov, CT Interactive's CEO, commented in a statement released on October 13, 2022. He revealed: "Now that we have entered the Slovak market, we are thrilled to continue delivering high-quality, enthralling games and gaming solutions to our partners."

Petrov explained that the company is pleased to enter the market in Slovakia and begin building a network of partners in the country. He acknowledged that the entry in Slovakia complements CT Interactive's strategy for growing and strengthening positions within regulated gambling markets. Last but not least, Petrov said: "We pride ourselves on being a reliable, trustworthy and secure partner to customers and players."

It was a busy month for CT Interactive. Besides its entry in Slovakia, the company announced a new partnership last week with leading operator TotoGaming. Before that, on October 10, the company revealed that 20 of its popular games gained a certificate from the Malta Gaming Authority, the gambling regulator in Malta.
