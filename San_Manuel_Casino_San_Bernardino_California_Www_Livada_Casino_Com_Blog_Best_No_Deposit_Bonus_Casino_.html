The fast-growing provider of content for the online gambling industry, Pragmatic Play, announced the release of a new bingo product. Expanding its offering further, the company announced Tuesday the release of Diamond Dazzle Bingo.

The title is a 90-ball bingo that offers innovative features and exciting prizes. What's more, Pragmatic Play's new product features a Diamond Dazzle bonus feature. Last but not least, the new title offers Diamond Dazzle Jackpot Game, giving online customers a chance for big prizes.

Claire McDaid, Pragmatic Play's VP of Bingo, shared her excitement in a statement released on December 6, 2022. She said that the new title delivers exciting experiences thanks to its in-game features. At the same time, McDaid pointed out that Diamond Dazzle Bingo is highly engaging and entertaining for online customers.

The company's VP of Bingo added: "Introducing the Diamond Dazzle community jackpot feature strategically means that spectators get that extra chance of winning even when the 1L, 2L and FH prizes have already been won." Finally, she pointed out that the new feature combined with the voting element creates additional excitement for the players.

According to additional details released by Pragmatic Play, each round of the new game features up to four prizes. At the start of each game, there are diamonds showing numbers between 1 and 24. "As the exhilarating game commences the numbers of the diamonds are called, and each diamond value is revealed to players before disappearing," the developer explained.

Whenever an online player wins a Full House, they are able to activate the Diamond Dazzle feature. This grants the lucky player a 25th diamond which depends on the numbers that appeared in the game before, as well as the remaining precious gems. The leading developer explained that customers can either accept the value they are offered with the diamonds or "take a risk and decline for the chance to win bigger rewards."

On the other hand, the new bingo title also offers a Diamond Dazzle Jackpot Game. Pragmatic explained that the unique jackpot is available for players who have placed a bet in the jackpot-winning game, granting them a chance to win a share of the jackpot. The Full House winner or winners share 50% of the jackpot while the remaining 50% will be "shared equally with other players who bought tickets into the game and guessed the same as the full house winner," the company revealed.

The newly released game coincided with the release of another Pragmatic Play title. This week, the company launched Virtual Sports Darts, the newest addition to its growing Virtual Sports portfolio.
