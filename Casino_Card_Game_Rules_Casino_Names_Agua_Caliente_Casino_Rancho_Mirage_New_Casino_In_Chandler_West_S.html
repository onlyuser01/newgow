Besides the expanded and dynamic grid counting six reels that are constantly changing throughout the game, players will also have a fun time discovering as many as 117,649 ways to win, by virtue of the Megaways mechanic.

To score a win, players need to land adjacent symbol combinations starting with the utmost left reel. Thanks to the special expanding symbol feature, symbols are randomly selected during any spin taking place in the base game.

The respective symbols will then expand and completely cover all positions corresponding to their reels, where they will pay from any position and give players more winning opportunities.

The Free Spins round can be engaged when landing at least three Scatter symbols during any spin. Players receive a minimum of 10 free games. During the same round, players are also guaranteed a special expanding symbol. The respective symbol will be in play for all free spins during the same bonus feature.
