Sweeping Mines for the Win

Aztec Gold Mines is a Minesweeper-based slot from the same provider that also created Gold Digger Mines, another hit game carrying the same fascinating mining theme. The new game asks players to first choose up to 15 mines. The goal of the game is for players to keep picking one tile after the other to beef up their total win while making sure not to reveal the mine that will abruptly end the round and results in a loss. The game allows players to choose their preferred level of volatility, difficulty, number of mines, and size of their bet. The maximum win is set at 288x the bet.

As the player continues to pick one square after the other, they will notice golden Win Multipliers or Montezuma Mine symbols behind them. The Montezuma Mine is the symbol that will trigger a loss. Players who opt for a larger number of Mines at the beginning of the game can cause the Win Multiplier to grow at a faster pace. At the same time, this will also make the Montezuma Mine symbol harder to avoid.
