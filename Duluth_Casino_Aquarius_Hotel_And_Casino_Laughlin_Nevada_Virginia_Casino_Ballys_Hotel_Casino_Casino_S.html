Relax Gaming Partners with Parimatch

Relax Gaming added another major operator to the company’s roster of iGaming operators that will now benefit from the Powered By Relax and Silver Bullet partnership offers. Relax reached an agreement today to provide the company’s rich content to Parimatch.

The deal will see Parimatch players enjoy a whole new set of unique and diverse games. Some of the now-available titles include Temple Tumble, Snake Arena, and the award-winning Money Train 2.

Players will also get to enjoy amazing content from over 50 third-party studios, including Fantasma Games, ReelPlay, and 4ThePlayer.

Parimatch is a twenty-year veteran of the sports betting business operating across the world, with strong forays in the iGaming space. Both Relax and Parimatch have enjoyed huge success over the years. The present agreement boosts both respective companies’ footprints further.

Daniel Eskola, CCO at Relax Gaming, said: “It’s a delight to announce this deal with Parimatch, a company that shares our values of quality and differentiation.”

Eskola also said that this is yet another move that will help Relax’s expansion goals, and it will also widen the company’s reach in new and existing markets.

Head of gambling products at Parimatch Eugene Saveliev added that his company had moved its focus over the years to become more technologically-oriented. Parimatch wants to widen its horizons into a bigger industry, and this agreement acts as a stepping stone for that goal.

Saveliev praised Relax and the company’s technical excellence. He further explained that Relax’s variety of content is a big highlight of their partnership and that he is positive that there will be more in the future.
