The much anticipated SBWA+ 2023 will soon be taking place, with the event planned from August 23 through August 25, 2023. Eventus International, the hosts of the event, are happy to welcome the Lagos State Lotteries and Gaming Authority (LSLGA) for the 8th edition of the vent. The organization is tasked with ensuring that regulatory innovation happens at a pace that meets industry and consumer needs and leaves no stone unturned.

LSLGA is engaged across a range of verticals, including sports betting, interactive gaming, and iGaming, and is bringing its vast expertise to Lagos, where the SBWA+ Summit 2023 is taking place. Meanwhile, the event promises a number of interesting opportunities for those attending.

Eventus International is confident in the success of the format and the significance of the event promising attending parties to benefit from industry insights into marketing and enhanced mobile reach across West Africa, collaborate with various established gaming tech leaders to strengthen their company offers, and generally navigate the changing regulatory landscape in the region.
