Leading sportsbook and platform provider NuxGame has announced a new partnership agreement with LSports, a provider of sports data feeds. Per the deal, the latter company will add its feeds to NuxGame, enhancing its offering.

LSports to Power NuxGame with Solutions

LSports’ product has many features that will help its new partner take its offering to the next level. Its low latency products cover many sports and markets, providing sports betting companies with a rich selection of options to engage their players with.

As agreed, NuxGame will get access to LSports’ BetBooster feature, which serves as an automated sports insights feed and bolsters customer satisfaction. BetBooster is based on historical data and AI-powered analysis and covers more than a hundred of the most popular betting markets.

Bet tips are given to players in both pull and push formats, further boosting engagement. The tips provided by BetBooster are integrated into a feed or widget, which allows for fast navigation and an intuitive user experience.

LSports also provides its partners with access to more than 100 bookmakers and over 105,000 pre-match events each month. These events cover around 60 sports, ensuring the diversity needed to captivate bettors. The product includes the OddService feed as well, which in itself provides access to over 100 bookmakers and real-time data for 2,500 sports and esports markets.

The Deal Will Generate Value for Everyone Involved

Yoav Ziv, LSports’ chief revenue officer, released a statement on the new deal. He promised NuxGame that Lsports’ solutions will allow the platform provider to take its business to the next level. Ziv is sure that his company’s cutting-edge tech will make sure that NuxGame remains a leading sportsbook platform provider.

Our unique technology and solutions like Betbooster and other front-end solutions will consolidate NuxGame’s position as one of the leading sportsbook platforms worldwide. Yoav Ziv, CRO, LSports

Daniel Heywood, NuxGame’s chief executive officer, also shared his thoughts on the agreement. He said that his team is very excited to announce the partnership with LSports Odds Services and noted that it will allow his company to offer a wider range of sports betting options. The agreement will generate more value for both companies, NuxGame’s partners and their end-customers.

We believe that this partnership will bring great value to both companies and we look forward to a successful collaboration. Daniel Heywood, CEO, NuxGame

NuxGame is a company that provides retail and online sports betting operators with platform solutions that come packed with many sports markets, odds feeds and all the other tools needed to engage players. The company also boasts flexible proprietary sportsbook software that has everything one might need to build successful platforms.
