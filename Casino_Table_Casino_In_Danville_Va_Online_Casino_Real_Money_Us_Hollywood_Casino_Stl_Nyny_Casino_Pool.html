Barn Festival review

Prepare for a cheerful farming adventure with Barn Festival.

Players will arrive at a countryside festival just in time to enjoy a bountiful harvest of fruits and veggies. With any luck, they’ll get to try all the tasty produce on offer.

Players dreaming of a cottage-core escape will particularly enjoy this slot’s aesthetic.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Among the symbols, players will see a range of fresh produce such as oranges, mushrooms, and corn. Each fruit and vegetable is animated in a colorful, cartoon style.

A dreamy farm scene can be viewed behind the reels. A little blue house, a red barn, and white picket fence give the setting a classic farm look. Sharp-eyed players will also spot a variety of flowers, butterflies, and bunting, upholding the cheerful vibe.

The festive soundtrack has a country and Western feel with plenty of banjo notes. The calls of roosters, mooing of cows, and tweets of songbirds add to the rural atmosphere.

Gameplay

Barn Festival is a six-reel, five-row slot that awards wins for landing matching symbols.

The number of matching symbols that appear, as well as the type of symbol, determines the size of payout.

Upon wins, the winning symbols vanish to allow new symbols to fall into their place.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Barn Festival features a Money symbol that can appear on any reel. Whenever it lands, the Money symbol is assigned a value at random from a predetermined set of 1x to 500x the player’s total bet.

If exactly 3 Money symbols appear on the reels simultaneously, their associated values are transformed into multipliers.

Landing 4 Money symbols initiates the Money Respin feature. The triggering Money symbols may be obtained via Cascades.

During the Money Respin feature, the reel structure is altered to have four reels and five rows. Regular symbols do not appear in the feature, but rather only Money symbols and a selection of special symbols.

The special symbols featured during the Money Respin feature include an Unlock symbol that opens additional reels before turning into a Money symbol, and a Collect symbol that gathers the values of all Money symbols. There is also a Multiply symbol that applies a multiplier to some of the Money symbols on the reels, and an Add Value symbol that adds value to any Money symbols on the reels.

The Money Respin feature begins with 3 spins and whenever a special symbol lands, the spin count becomes 3 again.

Players continue the Money Respin feature until the respin count is finished, or until the reels are entirely filled with Money symbols.
