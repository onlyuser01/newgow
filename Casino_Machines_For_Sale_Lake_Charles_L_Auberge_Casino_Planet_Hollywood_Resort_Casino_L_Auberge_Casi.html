3 Clown Monty review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around clowns.

In a chaotic carnival world, players will encounter a reel packed full of worse-for-wear clowns. Each of the red-nosed entertainers looks downtrodden or slightly insane, with their makeup slightly smudged.

Still, players will enjoy spotting the cream pies, smoking gorillas, and personified cars of this gritty circus. This is definitely not a game for anyone with a fear of clowns, but for fans of films such as Joker, the satirical vibe will hit the right spot.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

A creepy classic carnival tune plays, with its unstoppable notes full of violent cheer. The reels spin with the zing one would expect from a classic carnival game. Meanwhile, wins are applauded loudly, creating the sense that an audience is watching.

Each clown among the symbols has a different bright colour for their hair. With their smeared makeup and sad and maniacial expressions, it is clear these clowns have not led a joyful life.

Behind the reels, is a brick wall and an angrily smoking gorilla can be seen. This adds to the feeling that there are stakes in this game beyond a good time. Players will have fun getting into the rough and touch mood as the game goes on.

Overall, the music and graphics create a consistent atmosphere of a carnival crew down on their luck.

Gameplay

3 Clown Monty is a five reel, three row slot, with 10 win lines.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

Wild symbols may come with attached multipliers which are applied should that Wild be part of a winning combination.

If players land 2 Scatters, they can win extra Wild symbols or an additional Scatter symbol.

When players land 3 Scatters, this triggers the Free Spins feature and Multiplier Wilds are activated. Players choose a card to randomly decide the number of free spins they will receive.

During the Free Spins feature, Monty Spins are triggered and players will also choose a card at random to decide the number of Monty Spins. While in the Monty Spins feature, Wilds become sticky, remaining on the reels.

While in the Dealer’s Choice feature, Scatters will turn into Wild Scatters.
