Madame Ink review

Theme and Storyline

Madame Ink is themed around an asiatic tattooing.

Players will enter a tranquil tattoo parlor where they’ll be offered a selection of traditional body art. As they wince, enigmatic symbols including dragons, cranes, and lions will be etched on their skin.

This slot will appeal to players who are intrigued by the art of tattoos.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The rhythmic soundtrack is filled with drum beats and string notes that nod towards Madame Ink’s East Asian inspiration. Wins are celebrated with ethereal chimes that add a hint of magic to the atmosphere.

The reels take the form of parchment paper that showcases an array of tattoo designs. These include fierce red dragons, stylized blue lions, and graceful multi-colored cranes.

Each of the card suits appears on the reels as well, rendered with ornate patterns. However, the most striking symbol is Madame Ink herself with her beautifully tattooed back showcased as she faces away from the players.

Beyond the reels, players will see a pristine room with lanterns swaying in a gentle breeze. It seems like a calm place to receive a tattoo, and players won’t feel any nervousness.

Together, the soundtrack and animation create a unique world of spiritually uplifting tattoo art.

Gameplay

Madame Ink is a five-reel, three-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmosts reel. This results in a total of 243 ways to win.

The maximum win for Madame Ink is 5,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. At random, Wilds can expand to fill their entire reel.

The special Madame’s Mystery Reel may appear at the center of the reels where it transforms into a matching set of Tattoo symbols. Any Mystery symbols on the reels will also transform to match this set.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land 3 or more Scatters. Players may select the version of the feature they wish to play as follows:

12 free spins + active Wild Ink feature and no Madame’s Mystery feature.

6 free spins + active Madame’s Mystery feature and no Wild Ink feature.

3 free spins + both the Wild Ink feature and Madame’s Mystery feature active.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of Madame Ink is 96.21%, just above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.10 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of Madame Ink is medium, which makes it a less ideal match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, medium volatility tattoo-themed slot.

Though Madame Ink offers more modest volatility, its high RTP and intriguing theme make it an appealing option nonetheless.

The variety of bonus features add further interest to this engaging slot.
