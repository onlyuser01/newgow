Holland America Line will now offer expanded casino experiences to passengers aboard some of its best ships

Holland America, an American-owned cruise line, announced an expansion of the casino space on some of its ships. The company plans to add more machines, enhancing the experience for its casino-loving passengers.

Holland America Expands the Casinos aboard Its Ships

As announced by the cruise line, the casinos aboard its Pinnacle and Signature Class ships will now be armed with more than 140 gambling machines. Holland America noted that the newly-added cabinets are some of the freshest and most relevant slot and video poker machines in the entire gaming industry.

The Rotterdam, Nieuw Statendam, Koningsdam, Eurodam and Nieuw Amsterdam ships will benefit from more traditional slots, progressive jackpots and video poker. Furthermore, Holland America announced that its casinos will now offer on-demand slot tournaments, allowing its customers to take their engagement further by competing with one another.

These new additions will make sure that casino-loving passengers will get the casino experiences of their dreams while enjoying a luxurious cruise with Holland America.

Because of that, Materassi said that his team was able to utilize some extra space it had on its ships to expand the existing casinos. He reasserted that the newly-added machines offer best-in-class games and are something that will elevate the passenger experience.
