A Feature-Packed Experience

Armadillo Does Christmas is an exciting slot with big win potential, exciting features and a unique Trail Bonus. The 5×4 slot offers high volatility, providing players with a shot at big Christmas presents.

The game will see players join Armadillo Studios’ signature mascot who was dressed up for the occasion. By firing up the reels of Armadillo Does Christmas, players will collect a variety of symbols to score winning combinations and win prizes.

The chances of winning are increased by a multitude of features, such as Random Wilds that substitute random positions and Wild Reels that substitute randomly selected reels. A Colossal Reels feature, on the other hand, will see adjacent reels fuse to form larger 2×2, 3×3 or 4×4 symbols, further increasing players’ chances of scoring winning combinations.

Armadillo Studios’ cute mascot has additional presents in store and will provide Random Multipliers of 2x, 3x, 4x or 5x to the players. Adding to the festivities, a Symbol Upgrades feature will sometimes upgrade a randomly chosen low-paying symbol to a higher-paying one.

The fun is further bolstered by the Instant Win feature which can increase player’s prizes up to 200x.

Collect Special Symbols to Trigger Bonuses

Armadillo Does Christmas also sports a tabletop-inspired bonus game that is triggered when players land on three or more Bonus Symbols. During the bonus game, Armadillo will slide through the snow and collect various holiday-themed items.

If players collect 4 Whiskey symbols, they will trigger the Party Spin, firing up the reels for free. Impatient players can also trigger this bonus immediately through the Buy Bonus feature, in markets where this is permitted.
