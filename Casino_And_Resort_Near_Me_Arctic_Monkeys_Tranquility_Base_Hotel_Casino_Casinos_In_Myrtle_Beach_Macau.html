Wizard Games, the leading developer of games for the iGaming industry and NeoGames subsidiary, announced the release of a new slot game offering bonus features and engaging gameplay called Alice in Vegasland. As the name suggests, the game is inspired by the story of Alice in Wonderland and invites the players on a thrilling adventure to Las Vegas.

The new 5×3 slot game features 10 paylines and a variable RTP, as well as hit frequency. The studio’s latest title offers vibrant colors combining a Las Vegas-inspired theme with symbols directly related to the globally recognized fantasy book.

The reel of Alice in Vegasland is positioned in the center of the screen, while an animated version of Las Vegas can be seen in the background. Flashing lights surround the reel and crisp sound effects and animations further elevate the experience for the online players.

A Thrilling Adventure Down the Rabbit Hole

Players of Alice in Vegasland will be able to see classic Las Vegas-style symbols, including 7s, dice, chips, as well roulette. Additionally, Wizard Games’ new slot delivers Alice in Wonderland-themed symbols, such as a “Drink me” potion, a rabbit, a Cheshire Cat and Alice, among others.

Benedict McDonagh, Wizard Games’ managing director, shared his excitement about the new slot game, acknowledging that invites the players on an adventure to “Vegasland.” Speaking about the new slot game, he pointed out that it offers chances for big payouts while at the same time, providing engaging gameplay. Finally, McDonagh said that the studio is looking forward to seeing how the new game will be received by slot fans.

“With Vegas Free Spins, Rabbit Bonus Spins and more thrilling features waiting to be discovered, we’re really looking forward to seeing the reception to Alice in Vegasland. The rabbit hole awaits!“ Benedict McDonagh, managing director at Wizard Games

To secure prizes, Alice in Vegasland players need to match winning combinations within the game’s 10 paylines. Whenever players land three or more Book Scatters, they engage in a special bonus round called Vegas Free Spins. Before each spin within this bonus round, a random symbol is selected, which can help boost further the payout for the players.

On the other hand, players can also access a special Rabbit Bonus Spins round, given that they land 3 or more Rabbit Bonus symbols during the base game. Once this bonus round is activated, players are given 10 Rabbit Bonus Spins, while Wilds can boost the prizes by locking into position whenever they land.
