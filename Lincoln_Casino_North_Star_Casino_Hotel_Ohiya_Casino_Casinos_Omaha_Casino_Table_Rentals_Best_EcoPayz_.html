EcoPayz - A global provider with a wide range of services

The company behind ecoPayz, PSI-Pay Ltd, was authorised as an electronic money issuer in 2008 by the FSA in the United Kingdom. Their popularity grew and the next few years saw them become Mastercard members, as well launching the first-ever app from a financial institution, through ecoPayz's former guise EcoCard.

The app was a success and this led the company to expand their range of services. With them now offering much more than an ecoCard, a rebrand was completed in 2013, resulting in the ecoPayz we know today.

Since then, the company has become a global presence, offering 24/7 multilingual support to their customers in countries as diverse as India and Belgium. This outreach has been greatly enhanced through a partnership with Western Union, making them the only UK-based e-wallet in the Union's network.

Apart from the popular ecoAccount, customers also have access to financial options such as the prepaid ecoCard, the ecoVirtualcard - a digital card that works in tandem with the ecoAccount - and ecoVouchers, which allow you to pay online without using a credit card. Customers also have the opportunity to take part in an affiliate program that offers them rewards based on the referrals they make.

In May 2023, ecoPayz has been rebranded to Payz. Although many users still refer to the company and its services as "ecoPayz", the official name used by the company itself is now three letters shorter.
