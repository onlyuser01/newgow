The deal highlights the value SIS’ CS:GO product brings for operators and customers alike

Sports Information Services (SIS), a leading multi-content supplier of 24/7 live betting services, has agreed to provide the British operator Spreadex with high-integrity, event-based CS:GO content. As a result, the operator will secure access to many engaging esports events that will appeal to new-gen bettors.

Thanks to the current deal, Spreadex has secured access to two CS:GO 24/7 streams. In total, Spreadex players can expect a whopping 200,000 Competitive Gaming events a year.

SIS’s CS:GO esports suite features high-frequency 2v2 matches between professional players, providing sportsbooks and fans with many betting opportunities. The CS:GO product is a part of SIS’ Competitive Gaming portfolio and complements the supplier’s esoccer and ebasketball simulations, which are already live with Spreadex.

The CS:GO product comes with a rich selection of pre-match and in-play betting markets for fans of esports wagering. Thanks to ultra-low latency live streaming and on-screen graphics with betting prompts, consumers can closely follow the action without missing a beat or wagering opportunity.

SIS Competitive Gaming is notably the only esports betting product to ever earn the Esports Integrity Commission’s Gold Standards, attesting to the supplier’s commitment to integrity. Furthermore, SIS’ CS:GO offering fields referees with ESIC-based training, highlighting the supplier’s commitment to protecting its games from fraud. The skilled referees monitor each game and provide operators and traders with real-time communication.

The Deal Highlights the Value of SIS’ CS:GO Offering

Representatives from the two companies released statements on the new agreement. Spreadex’s head of sports trading, product and operations, Andrew Wallington, said that his team recognizes the importance of providing its audience with a diverse selection of content.

We are always looking to enhance the content that we provide to our audience, and going live with SIS’ CS:GO’s 24/7 offering represents a huge boost to our offering. Andrew Wallington, head of sports trading, product and operations, Spreadex

He added that his team has a long-standing relationship with SIS that will be enhanced by the current agreement.

Meanwhile, Dave Richardson, SIS’ commercial manager for the UK & Europe, noted that his company’s CS:GO offering is expanding “at a great pace.” He noted that this highlights the value it brings for operators and customers alike.

We are immensely proud to extend our partnership with Spreadex, an operator that fully understands the benefits of adding CS:GO to its sportsbook. Dave Richardson, commercial manager for the UK & Europe, SIS
