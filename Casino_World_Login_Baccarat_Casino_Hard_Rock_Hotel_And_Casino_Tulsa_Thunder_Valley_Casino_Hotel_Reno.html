DraftKings Becomes Betting Partner for Knicks, Rangers with MSG

US-based sportsbook DraftKings has signed a partnership with Madison Square Garden Company to represent NBA and NHL franchises the New York Knicks and the New York Rangers.

The partnership was announced on Thursday, January 2, and it also covers the Madison Square Garden Company (MSG) namesake arena.

Both the Knicks and Rangers, an NBA and NHL franchise respectively, are home-based in New York and the MSG Arena in particular.

Furthermore, the partnership builds upon a previously established tie-up with the company when DraftKings and MTG negotiated a DFS deal between the two parties back in 2015.

Moving forward, DraftKings hopes to boost awareness and exposure among sports fans.

The company, Mr. Skotarczak acknowledged, had established itself as one of the most successful sportsbooks.

DraftKings Chief Business Officer, Ezra Kucharz, was also pleased with the development.

Mr. Kucharz particularly liked the opportunity to work with “two of the most iconic franchises in all of sports that play here,” referring to the Madison Square Garden Arena.
