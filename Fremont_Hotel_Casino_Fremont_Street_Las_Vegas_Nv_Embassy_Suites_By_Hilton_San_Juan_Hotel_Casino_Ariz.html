Arcadia Becomes Part of Fusion Platform Family

Arcadia has focused on developing online casino experiences that are predicated on physical arcade machines, leading to unique gameplay for players. Arcadia is hoping to carve out a brand-new niche in the live gaming experience whereby arcade favorites can be integrated with the live casino games experience and provide players with brand new opportunities in the vertical.

The company’s first title, Fortune Pusher is already part of the Fusion platform. More games are expected to be added soon, Pariplay said in a press release.
