The NOVOMATIC Digital Gaming and Entertainment division, Greentube, announced that its subsidiary, Greentube USA, launched an innovative new iLottery game called Drop The Balls. Confirmation regarding the debut of the new title came Thursday and sees the company deliver a thrilling, engaging and unique title that stands out with rewarding features and provides unmatched experiences.

Greentube USA confirmed that the first lottery to benefit from the new iLottery game is the Washington DC Lottery. This will enable the operator to be the first one to deliver the Drop The Balls title to its customer base.

Describing the new innovative title, the game developer revealed that it represents a combination of bagatelle, pachinko and pinball. The uniquely designed game is developed especially to capture the attention of a wide range of audiences, allowing them to tap into thrilling experiences.

Although its debut was just announced, Drop The Balls is expected to become an instant hit within the iLottery vertical. The new title stands out with quality graphics, delivers realistic simulations and more importantly, provides engaging gameplay that is unlike any other iLottery game on the market.

According to Greentube, the new game grants the players an “opportunity to drop balls onto a playfield filled with obstacles and multiplier catchers, adding value to the balls as they drop.” A variety of different targets can boost the potential payouts with bonuses. Moreover, players that catch a bonus ball secure free balls for a special bonus round that grants a chance for even bigger prizes.
