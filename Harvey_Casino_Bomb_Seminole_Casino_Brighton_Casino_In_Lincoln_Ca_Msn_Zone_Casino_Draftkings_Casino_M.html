NOVOMATIC’s digital gaming division, Greentube, has appointed Felipe Ludeña as its chief sportsbook officer and Georg Gubo as managing director at ADMIRAL Sportwetten. Executive Shifts in NOVOMATIC Improve Know-How Exchange

Ludeña took a seat with the company’s board as the two companies sought to strengthen their collaboration. Gubo will also combine his existing position as managing director at ADMIRAL Sportwetten and his current position as Greentube’s COO.
