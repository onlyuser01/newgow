Sports Information Services (SIS), a leading multi-channel supplier of sports betting services and solutions, has agreed to sponsor the Gran Premio Nacional, a historic derby in Argentina. This move seeks to strengthen SIS’ presence in the region and reiterate its commitment to horse racing.

The Gran Premio Nacional is set to take place at the Hipódromo Argentino de Palermo racetrack in Buenos Aires on November 12. It is the oldest and most influential annual race in the LATAM country and attracts thousands of spectators each year.

SIS has an existing agreement with the Hipódromo Argentino de Palermo course and is its exclusive global distributor of pictures and data. By supporting the biggest event hosted at the hippodrome, SIS will nurture the growth of racing in Latin America as a whole.

The racecourse hosts 69 Group races all year long, including many important Group 1 events, such as Gran Premio República, Gran Premio Polla de Potrillos y Potrancas and Gran Premio Selección. As the Palermo course’s partner, SIS covers all those races via its 24/7 Live Betting services. The aforementioned solution supplies the provider’s partners with a constant stream of pictures, data and on-screen graphics. This content helps sports bettors grab exciting opportunities and make the most out of their bets.

SIS Is Looking Forward to the Race

Hernan Fucile, Hipódromo Argentino de Palermo’s director of simulcasting operations, praised the expanded agreement with SIS. He said that his team is glad to deepen its relationship with the provider, which, according to Fucile, is one of the most reputable and trusted names in international horse racing.

Fucile welcomed SIS’ commitment to horse racing and praised its decision to support the upcoming race.

The Gran Premio Nacional is sure to entertain and thrill racing enthusiasts both here at the course and those following the action around the globe. Hernan Fucile, director of simulcasting operations, Hipódromo Argentino de Palermo

Michele Fischer, SIS’ VP of content services, added that the Gran Premio Nacional is a major highlight of the racing calendar in the LATAM country. Because of that, she is happy to be the sponsor of this year’s race.

Racing in Latin America has long been of great importance to SIS and we are keen to continue to expand on our existing commitments with our valued partners. We look forward to a highly competitive race which is sure to attract great interest worldwide. Michele Fischer, VP of content services, SIS

SIS already has a significant presence in LATAM through agreements with many local racecourses. The sports betting services supplier delivers more than 27,000 horse racing events a year from countries like Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Mexico and Uruguay.
