3 Oaks Gaming has launched Dragon Wealth, a new slot with free spins with wild multipliers and a grand jackpot of x5000

The fast-growing distributor of iGaming content has announced the fresh release of the new Dragon Wealth title which is expected to turn into another hit for the studio operated by Green Rock Limited. The game carries players on an exciting adventure to the Far East and it is packed with free spins with wild multipliers, a grand jackpot capped at 5,000x, and a bonus game with multiplier reels for mouth-watering wins.

Travel to the Inner Depths of Mystical China

The 5×4 slot with 25 pay lines invites players to start exploring the Far East and reach China and its mystical Golden Dragons with supernatural powers.

3 Oaks Gaming’s managing director Sebastian Damian spoke about the new game that incorporates their already successful Hold & Win game mechanic used to attract “massive multipliers” that can generate huge wins. Damian also explained that the provider has made it a habit to add fresh and innovative elements to each new slot release, adding that Dragon Wealth makes no exception.

The slot comes with quality high-definition graphics, excellent animations, and a mesmerizing Asian-themed soundtrack. Together, all these elements offer an original oriental atmosphere that creates the illusion of good fortune and wealth.

The game is also filled with a plethora of excellent features ranging from the golden dragons that also represent the wilds in the game and the scatters that can trigger free spins.

Without a doubt, the new slot that joins other popular 3 Oaks Gaming titles like Green Chilli, Sticky Piggy, and Sunlight Princess has everything it needs to bring lucrative payouts to players.

5,000x Grand Jackpot and Respins Bonus with Multipliers

When landing a minimum of six bonus symbols represented by the glowing pearls, the Hold & Win game comes into play. During the game, only bonus symbols will be displayed on the grid.

Each of these symbols landing on the grid will either introduce a cash value or a mini (20x), minor (50x), or major (100x) jackpot prize. The 5,000x grand jackpot is triggered when all 20 reel spots are filled with these symbols.

Players receive three respins. Every time a new bonus symbol is locked in place, the meter will be seen resetting to three additional respins.

The respins bonus introduces a flaming blue multiplier symbol. When these icons are seen showing on the reels, their values are added to the multiplier meter above their corresponding reels.

At the end of the round, all symbols shown below a multiplier are increased by the respective value, for a potentially huge win.
