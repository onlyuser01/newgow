Pefaco Goes Online

Grupo Pefaco’s retail offerings enjoy significant popularity in West Africa. Because of this, the company has decided that it is time to take the next step and go online. Moobifun will use its experience with iGaming and online content to fully digitalize Pefacto’s products. The tech-savvy company will put its knowledge of online marketing to use to make the operator’s new online offer as appealing as possible.

The casino firm’s online offer will first target the six African countries where the company runs casinos. For reference, these include Benin, Burkina Faso, Burundi, the Ivory Coast, Niger and Togo. In addition, the agreement will see Moobifun power Pefaco with its proprietary Mobile Money solution, allowing players in the aforementioned six countries to make easy transfers when playing online.
