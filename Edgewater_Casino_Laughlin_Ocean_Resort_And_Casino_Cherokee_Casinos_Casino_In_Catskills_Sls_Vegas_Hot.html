CT Interactive, an online software provider that focuses on developing slot games, has signed a new content agreement with Betmexico. Under the deal, the supplier has agreed to provide the operator with an exclusive set of its games.

Thanks to the arrangement, a suite of CT Interactive titles is now available to customers of the Mexican operator. As one of the largest gambling companies in the Latin American country, Betmexico recognizes the importance of varied content and offerings. It is best known for its unique approach to gaming and its vast selection of products.

The deal, furthermore, expands CT Interactive’s presence in Latin America. The company has been steadily expanding its presence in the region over the past few months thanks to strategic deals with major local companies. The company has planned further developments in LATAM which it will announce at later dates.

CT Interactive Is Thrilled to Expand in LATAM

Representatives from the two companies addressed the deal, with Martin Ivanov, CT Interacitve’s director of business development for the Americas saying that his team is glad to launch its content with Betmexico.

We would like to welcome them to our growing customer base in Mexico and look forward to deepening our partnership as more portions of CT Interactive become available in the country. Martin Ivanov, director of business development Americas, CT Interactive

Diego Verano, the provider’s head of business development for the LATAM region, also commented on the matter, saying that CT Interactive is always looking to improve its offerings and create custom game packages for various player demographics. He added that his company’s content is enjoying a lot of attention in Mexico.

Finally, Patricio Garza, Betmexico’s director commercial, said that his company’s mission is to provide the best possible gaming experience to customers.

In order to do that, we need to constantly offer new, exciting products. CT Interactive’s award-winning portfolio of innovative games is an excellent tool for this purpose. Their games with unique designs and sound effects are already a hit with our customers. Patricio Garza, director commercial, Betmexico

The last few months saw CT Interactive launch with a variety of operators across several markets. Earlier this month, the company penned a content agreement with Kajot in Slovakia. Prior to that, the supplier rolled out its portfolio of games with Sbbet in Montenegro. In May, the supplier launched its games with ELIBET in Bulgaria.

In addition to signing new deals with operators, CT Interactive also minted agreements with major aggregators, such as SpinOro.
