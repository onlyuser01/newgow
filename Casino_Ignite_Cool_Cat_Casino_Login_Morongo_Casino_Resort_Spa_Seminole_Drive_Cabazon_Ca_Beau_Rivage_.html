The Developer’s New Slot Offers Rewarding Features

As the name suggests, Battle Ops features different military-related symbols. Such include grenade launchers, guns, medic pouches, army helmets, radio stations as well as standard card symbols presented in a camouflage pattern. To secure prizes of different sizes, players need to match different symbols across paylines. If lucky enough, players of the new slot can grab the game’s grand prize which offers a payout of up to 500x of the bet.

The slot game’s rewarding features include Expanding Wilds, Extra Spins and Free Spins. The most valuable symbol within the 5×3 slot game is the soldier. That symbol acts as wild and at the same time can expand and cover all symbols on the whole reel.

On the other hand, landing three or more scatter symbols anywhere on the reel enables the players to activate a special bonus round that grants them 12 free spins initially. If the players land a soldier symbol during this bonus round, they can benefit from a change of low-tier symbols to mid-tier symbols and an extra free spin.
