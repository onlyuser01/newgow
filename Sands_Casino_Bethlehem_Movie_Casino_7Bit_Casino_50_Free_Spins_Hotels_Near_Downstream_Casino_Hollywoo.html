The soundtrack has been cleverly chosen, making players feel like they have hopped on a camel and started riding through the sandy lands with the goal of reaching the hidden treasures waiting at the destination.

To score a win, players must land two, three, four, or five identical symbols starting, from left to right. As they explore the nicely designed sand dunes and plains, they can also come across three types of Free Spins bonuses. Each of them features a unique reward to make the slot even more fun.
