Professor BigWin review

Professor BigWin transports players to a science lab where they’ll meet an eccentric professor.

Beakers and vials smoke and shimmer as Professor BigWin conducts his experiments. If all goes well, this lab might just produce some precious elements.

Players looking for a quirky, light-hearted atmosphere will particularly appreciate this slot.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack of Professor BigWin has a whimsical melody as well as dramatic flair. Players will feel as if an explosive experiment might take place at any moment.

This slot’s host, the titular Professor BigWin, is a friendly man in a lab coat with hair that brings to mind Albert Einstein. He holds colorful liquids in his hands with the casual air of a mad scientist.

On the reels, players will see notepads, plasma globes, and jars of gold. Each item is animated in vivid hues that suggest this lab is far more playful than serious.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Professor BigWin creates a wacky world of science.

Gameplay

Professor BigWin is a five-reel, four-row slot. There are 40 win lines in total.

If players wish, they can buy directly into the Bonus for 100x their current bet.

The swift gameplay ensures this slot remains engaging.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Free Game symbol and Scatter symbol.

The Mystery Replacement feature prompts a selection of symbols to transform into matching symbols.

The Replicating Reels feature can be triggered when 2 Scatters appear on the reels, or at random after any spin that does not include 2 Scatters. Players are then awarded 1 Replicating Reels spin, and 2-5 reels will become Replicating Reels. Each position on the Replicating Reels is transformed into a selected symbol at the start of the round.

Bonus feature of the game

During the Replicating Reels feature, all reels that were not replicated will spin. Players are then awarded any available wins.

The Free Games feature is awarded when players land Free Games symbols stacked 4 symbols high on 2 or more reels. The feature begins with 7 free spins.

Every position that holds a triggering Free Games symbol will become a Replicating Reel at the start of the Free Games feature. Additional Replicating Reels may be added if players land Free Games symbols stacked 4 symbols high on another reel.

Throughout the Free Games feature, players can use the High Voltage Mode to increase their odds of triggering additional Free Games and Replicating Reels.
