Game Theme and Features

The game features a number of oriental symbols inviting players into the dragon’s cavern in search of a special Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game. The base game is designed in a simple, yet classic manner with 9 reels arranged in the classic 3×3 grid. The operator allows players to bypass the base game by using a dedicated Buy Feature. This feature lets them automatically enter the Hold the Jackpot Bonus Game in exchange for a small fee.

The gaming area displays zero regular lines. Payments are made as a result of the number of symbols that a player matches anywhere on the reels. A minimum of four matching symbols are necessary for a win to be triggered. The game’s highest reward reaches a whopping 2,187x the original bet. Players can change the game’s volatility and take more control over their gameplay while keeping a close eye on the wild symbols, bonus symbols, and the previously mentioned bonus game.

Re-Spins and Flaming Dragons

During the bonus round, players will witness some eye-catching game elements including fiery dragons. With the help of three Re-Spins, players will get the chance to fill the reels with additional Bonus symbols. Once the Hold the Jackpot bonus round comes to an end, players receive their rewards according to the number of special symbols they manage to land. Mini, Minor, and Major Jackpots can also be scooped up within the game. To do this, players must land anywhere between 6 to 8 Bonus symbols. At the same time, the Grand Jackpot requires 9 Bonus symbols in order to be triggered.
