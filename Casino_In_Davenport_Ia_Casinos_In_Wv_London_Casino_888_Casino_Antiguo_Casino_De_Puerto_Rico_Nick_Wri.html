Blueprint Gaming, a leading developer of slot games, has introduced a new addition to its leadership team, naming the experienced Nick Wright as its new chief commercial officer. The new hire will strengthen the company’s leadership, allowing it to double down on its goals.

The hire’s appointment comes as Blueprint Gaming continues to grow its product portfolio and platform capabilities.

In his new position, Wright will oversee Blueprint Gaming’s growth strategy in the United Kingdom and beyond. He will be tasked with growing the company and exploring opportunities for launches in new regulated markets.

Wright is an iGaming industry veteran with years of experience in the sector. His experience, Blueprint Gaming noted, “stretches back to the emergence of online gaming.” In addition, Wright boasts tenures at some of the most influential gaming companies.

Prior to his current job at Blueprint Gaming, Wright served as the senior vice president of commercial content at Bally’s Corporation. Before that, he worked as group head of business development of Gamesys, now a Bally’s subsidiary.

Wright has also worked in senior positions at other iGaming brands, such as Amaya Gaming and Playtech.
