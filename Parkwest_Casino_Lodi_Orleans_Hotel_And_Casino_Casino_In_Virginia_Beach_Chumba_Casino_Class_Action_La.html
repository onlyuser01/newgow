The leading aggregation platform and business-to-business (B2B) content provider, REEVO, announced it signed a new partnership with the fast-growing provider of engaging and quality casino games, Betsoft Gaming. Under the terms of the new deal, Betsoft Gaming's innovative game portfolio will be integrated into REEVO's platform. This strategic partnership will enable REEVO's vast network of operators to easily access games from the iGaming content supplier.

The latest collaboration complements REEVO's goal to revolutionize the iGaming vertical. Ultimately, the company plans to deliver exceptional experiences for the clients of its partner operators while at the same time boosting the reach of renowned iGaming providers.

Fernando Van Velzen, Betsoft Gaming's Head of Account Management, shared his excitement about the deal with REEVO in a statement. "We are very pleased to be working with a company of Reevo's caliber. Betsoft’s award-winning releases deliver the ultimate gaming experience to players and Reevo's platforms will elevate accessibility," he explained. Last but not least, Van Velzen pointed out: "Our shared values and commitment will ensure the development of a long-term and successful relationship for everyone."

The partnership was met with positivity by REEVO's Head of Sales, Petra Maria Poola. Commenting on the topic, she explained: "This partnership strengthens our commitment to delivering high-quality gaming content to our operator partners and their players." In conclusion, Poola said: "With the integration of Betsoft’s innovative games into our platform, we are confident that this partnership will be mutually beneficial."

The latest deal between the duo is expected to deliver more engaging and unique experiences to the iGaming vertical. Betsoft Gaming's extensive range of casino games that offer unique themes, mechanics and features will now be able to reach even more players than before.
