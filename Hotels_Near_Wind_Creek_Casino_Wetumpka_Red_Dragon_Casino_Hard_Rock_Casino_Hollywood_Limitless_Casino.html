Bolstering the Responsible Gaming Team

New additions to BetCity’s responsible gaming team den Boer and Moser bring expertise from the gaming and banking sectors and increase the company’s headcount at the brand’s head office in Amsterdam to over 60 people.

Moser is joining the sports betting firm from Dutch bank ABN AMRO, having spent the last three years serving in the bank’s new client and compliance department, while den Boer brings experience as a responsible gaming expert at Malta-headquartered Kindred Group, where she spent four-and-a-half years, Australian social gaming company VGW and Kaizen Gaming, her most recent employer.
