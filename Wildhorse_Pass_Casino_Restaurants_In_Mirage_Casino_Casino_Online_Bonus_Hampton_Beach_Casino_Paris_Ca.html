Jagdhar Has Experience in Game Development

Jagdhar will serve as Wizard Games’ director of game studio – a position he is well-suited for, considering his prior experiences at other iGaming companies. Before joining Wizard Games, the new hire served as the head of game design at Pragmatic Play. Previously, he had an almost ten-year-long tenure at NetEnt, where he most recently served as senior game product owner of slots.

Thanks to his experience with the iGaming sector, Jagdhar will be able to quickly adapt to Wizard Games. He will leverage his knowledge of game development to help the studio on its quest to roll out games that are both innovative and engaging.

As a director of game studio at the company, Jagdhar will be responsible for overseeing the entire production process. In addition, he will work hard to develop, enhance and scale up the company’s game production pipeline.
