Booongo: a popular provider with an entertaining slots selection

Booongo has been around since 2015, when a team of slots enthusiasts in Ukraine decided to launch their own start-up. They targeted the mobile gaming market, a rapidly growing industry at that point, and their focus was on producing cross-platform slot games that caught players' attention with their high level of quality.

One of the first big events in the company's history was obtaining the Curacao license in April 2017. This allowed them to operate in several popular iGaming markets, and they set up an office on the Caribbean Island to cement their presence. They also opened an office in Taiwan as they looked to expand into the Asian market.

As Booongo grew, so did its collection of slot games. With an emphasis on video slots, it released a wide selection of titles over the next few years, including its first progressive jackpot release, Book of Sun, in 2019. This marked a new line of games leading to the popular Aztec Sun: Hold and Win the following year. By this point, Booongo was also using its technical expertise to offer a back-office solution to casino operators.

We could view Booongo as an established game provider today. It has a wide network of partnerships with operators, and regularly features at top industry gaming events such as ICE London. Its management has recently stated a desire to move into the African and Latin American markets, so it's highly likely that you'll see more Booongo content out there in the future.

Booongo slots and software

Booongo's selection of immersive video slots take pride of place in their games collection. Not only do they boast some impressive graphics, but they also offer progressive jackpots that sometimes deliver generous payouts.

As mentioned before, Aztec Sun: Hold and Win, is a prime example of this. The prime jackpot here revolves around collecting 15 suns symbols – an extremely unlikely event, but fun to think about. The game's five reels, holding ancient Aztec symbols, flow smoothly as you play. Be sure to look out for the Temple scatter symbols that trigger a free spins feature, and the sun symbol that is the pathway to the bonus game.

Another Booongo slot with a progressive jackpot is Dragon Pearls: Hold and Win – one of several games dedicated to the company's presence in Asian markets. Based on the traditional belief that dragons bring wealth, the game challenges you to find where this particular dragon hides its pearls. These act as the Scatter symbols and six of them will start the bonus game – find all the pearls here and you get the Grand Jackpot, which is 1,000 times your bet.

Of course, you don't have to throw yourself into a world of video slots and progressive prizes if you don't want. Booongo also has simpler games, such as 777 Gems, a minimalist slot based on the classic slots of yesteryear; and Monkey Money a fun slot with bananas – lots of them.

It's also worth mentioning that Booongo's high line in game quality is powered by its own software, something that's available as a safe back-office solution to operators. It offers API integration, is available in 18 languages and transfers easily to any type of platform.
