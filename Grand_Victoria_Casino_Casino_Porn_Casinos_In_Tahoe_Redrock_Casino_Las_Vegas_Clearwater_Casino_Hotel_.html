The software provider of solutions for gaming and sports entertainment venues, VizExplorer, announced a new collaboration with Grand Traverse Resort & Casinos in North Michigan.

The brand new partnership was announced on Tuesday and will see Grand Traverse Resort & Casinos leverage VizExplorer’s leading marketing analysis and player development programs. Under the terms of the deal, the operator will use those solutions for its venues Leelanau Sands Casino & Lodge as well as Turtle Creek Casino & Hotel.

Under the terms of the new deal, VizExporer’s host VizOps will grant the casinos access to a comprehensive CRM tool that also features mobile access to player data. The venues will leverage that technology and can monitor player activity and receive real-time notifications. According to VizExplorer, the casinos will also benefit from “automated task management to streamline communication with and development of valuable players.” Additionally, the new collaboration includes CampaignViz, a unique tool that operators can use to streamline their campaigns. The tool offers options for analysis which also boosts the marketing efforts while at the same time reducing the time and helping operators select an optimal marketing approach.

“We are excited to partner with Grand Traverse Resort & Casinos and to be part of their companies’ data strategy.“ David Patent, CEO at VizExplorer

David Patent, VizExplorer’s CEO, revealed that the company is excited to join forces with Grand Traverse Resort & Casinos. He predicted that the new agreement will enable the operator to leverage a unique set of tools.

The Operator Offers Supreme Entertainment in Northern Michigan

Currently, Grand Traverse Resort owns and operates three venues in Northern Michigan that offer supreme gaming, dining and entertainment experiences. On the other hand, Leelanau Sands Casino is a popular location that offers 330 casino slot games, as well as plenty of table games such as blackjack, roulette, 3-card poker and craps. The venue features 50 guest rooms, dining outlets and a bingo hall.
