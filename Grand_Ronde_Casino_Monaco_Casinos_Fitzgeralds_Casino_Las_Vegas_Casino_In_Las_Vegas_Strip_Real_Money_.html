Game developer for the iGaming industry BF Games has signed a new partnership with consultancy agency SCCG Management. The partnership is formed to reflect BF Games’ broader ambitions for the market in the United States and North America in general. The company believes that its experience navigating regulated iGaming landscapes such as the United Kingdom and Poland already means that the developer is capable of building products that appeal to local players.

Yet, to truly achieve long-term synergies and pursue growth in North American iGaming, BF Games want to leave nothing to chance, leaning heavily on SCCG’s expertise to help guide it in what holds the potential to be the biggest online casino market. BF Games already has plans for what it sees as a lucrative opportunity, but it will be through SCCG Management’s expertise that the studio finds a better foothold in individual states and elsewhere in North America.

SCCG Management Founder Stephen A. Crystal welcomed this opportunity to see BF Games and SCCG join forces and secure further opportunities in both the iGaming and sports betting verticals. BF Games has all it takes to be a successful entrant in the region, Crystal assured.

BF Games CEO Ewa Kazmierska was similarly excited about the news, and confident that the company has a lot to offer to local players in the United States and North American markets. BF Games is also targeting a long-term journey into the market where it takes a step-by-step approach to secure maximum value.

While BF Games has already released successful products in Europe, it’s equally eager to carry on with similar releases in North America and create tailored content for local players, too.
