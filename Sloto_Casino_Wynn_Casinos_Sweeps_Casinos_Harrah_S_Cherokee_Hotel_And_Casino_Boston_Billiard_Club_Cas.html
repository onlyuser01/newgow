The company will present its Peak cabinets along with other engaging and innovative solutions and features at the upcoming 2023 Australasian Gaming Expo

The global gaming company powering operators within the lottery, betting and online gambling sector, International Game Technology (IGT), confirmed earlier this week that it will showcase its latest games, services, systems and cabinets at the upcoming 2023 Australasian Gaming Expo (AGE). As one of the most-anticipated events for the gambling industry in the region, the AGE is planned to take place between August 15 and August 17, 2023, in Sydney, Australia. The thrilling event will provide engagement opportunities for suppliers, providers as well as operators. Visitors and participants at AGE will be able to see new products and services, participate in seminars and tap into unique networking opportunities.

At stand 590, IGT will demonstrate its highly popular PeakDual27 and PeakCurve49 cabinets. The cabinets deliver features from the company’s popular Crystal cabinet series but enhance further the experience for the users with optimized screens. Additionally, the Peak cabinets offer an ergonomic and dynamic player panel with dual bash buttons, as well as curved dual-screen gameplay transitions.

Besides the Peak cabinets, IGT will present its revolutionary features, including Cash Ching, as well as Portal Link & Portal Loot. The global gaming company will showcase its award-winning global solution, Prosperity Link to the Australian market. Visitors to the upcoming event will also be able to receive more information regarding IGT’s Boost It Up feature.

Besides the aforementioned solutions, the IGT Systems team will demonstrate the company’s digital wallet solutions. Last but not least, IGT vowed to also present its IGT ADVANTAGE CLUB system that provides efficiencies for gambling operators, complementing cashless gaming.

Recently, IGT posted strong Q2 2023 results, outlining $1.06 billion in total revenue for the three months ended June 30, 2023. When compared to the same period last year, the Q2 results marked a growth of 3%.

The Company Is Excited to Showcase Its Products

Dallas Orchard, IGT’s chief operating officer, gaming APAC, explained that the upcoming expo provides a unique chance for the company to showcase its “localized solutions that are designed to deliver peak player entertainment and help grow customer revenues in the ANZ market.” He spoke about the ongoing commitment of the company to deliver games that stand out with a localized approach and help operators in different regions continue to grow.

“We invite customers in our AGE booth to meet IGT’s local studio teams and engage with game concepts, experience game design ethos and explore what our studios have in store for future game development.“ Dallas Orchard, chief operating officer, gaming APAC at IGT

Speaking about the AGE, Orchard said that IGT will be excited to showcase its latest additions. He outlined that the upcoming event will serve as the launch of the company’s Peak cabinets, which have already seen significant success in other markets such as North America. Finally, Orchard said that the company is looking forward to presenting its innovative features and solutions and invited customers to meet IGT at its AGE booth.
