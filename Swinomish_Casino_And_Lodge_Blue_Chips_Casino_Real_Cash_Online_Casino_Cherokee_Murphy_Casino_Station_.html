Partner The League, Partner The Teams

“It is with great enthusiasm that we announce these multi-year partnerships with the Celtics, 76ers, and Pacers” Ezra Kucharz, Chief Business Officer, DraftKings

Indiana Pacers Deal

Serving as the Official Daily Fantasy Sports and an Official Sports Betting Partner of the Indiana Pacers, DraftKings’ branding will appear in Bankers Life Fieldhouse and in the team’s post-game show, as well as in-game promotions and activations.

The sponsorship deal allows DraftKings to offer customers with experiences such as attending team practices and games and meeting former players, including offering VIP opportunities as “Fantasy Camp” and “Honorary Team Captain Presentations”.

“We are delighted to welcome DraftKings into the Indiana Pacers’ family of corporate partnerships…It truly is a win-win” Rick Fuson, Sports and Entertainment President and COO, Indiana Pacers

No Sports Betting With The Celtics, Yet

“We’re happy to collaborate with our hometown partner, DraftKings, to serve Celtics fans through a variety of group activations and game day elements, including the presentation of our starting lineup across social channels” Rich Gotham, President, Boston Celtics
