White Hat Studios, a leading provider of content in the American iGaming market, has formed a new partnership with PokerStars, a major online gambling operator. Under the agreement, the developer will supply its new partner with a selection of exciting slot and table games.

As per the deal, recent hits such as Peaky Blinders and Masters of Atlantis will make their way into PokerStars’ online casino portfolio. The award-winning slot Almighty Buffalo Megaways will also be available to customers of the online operator.

In addition to slots, White Hat Studios will also inject its expansive portfolio of table games into the operator’s offering. The new additions are expected to bolster PokerStars’ player acquisition and retention rates, helping it further solidify its position as a leading operator in the United States.

As agreed, the new additions to PokerStars’ content offering will be rolled out in five regulated states, including New Jersey, Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Connecticut and Michigan.

Earlier this year, the operator also secured games from Red Rake Gaming, another major casino content provider.

White Hat Studios’ vice president of sales and marketing, Daniel Lechner, commented on the matter, expressing his team’s delight about the collaboration with PokerStars. Lechner lauded the operator as a staple name in the American market and praised it for recognizing the quality of White Hat’s games.

We are thrilled to be partnering with PokerStars, a prominent name in the US iGaming market, and the deal exemplifies that the industry’s key players recognize the quality of our content. Daniel Lechner, VP of sales and marketing, White Hat Studios

Lechner added that the current agreement represents “another major milestone” for White Hat Studios, which recently went live in all regulated states. Thanks to the launch, the studio will be able to expand its footprint in the USA, the VP concluded.

The deal follows several recent agreements between White Hat Studios and other major US operators. Two weeks ago, the supplier penned a partnership agreement with Kindred, agreeing to power its operations in New Jersey and Pennsylvania with games.

Last month, White Hat Studios also rolled out content on BetPARX in three states, namely Pennsylvania, New Jersey and Michigan.
