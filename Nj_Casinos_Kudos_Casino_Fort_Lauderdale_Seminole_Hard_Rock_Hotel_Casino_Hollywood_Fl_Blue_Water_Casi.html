Bede Gaming, a company that specializes in the delivery of innovative software for the iGaming industry, has appointed a new chief technology officer

Dan Whiteley is joining the company and bringing a wealth of experience that he has accumulated working in similar positions across the industry. He has held stints with Flutter, CTS, and Jugo, and has more than 25 years of experience working in complex technological environments.

Bringing Top Tech Talent to Steer Bede Gaming

His prowess and expertise in this particular product area make him a very strong fit as part of Bede Gaming’s ecosystem. He is well-versed in digital transformation, IT service, security, operations, product engineering, and architecture, making him a 360-degree professional who is capable of carrying forth the Bede Gaming’s torch. Commenting on the opportunity to be part of the new team, Whiteley said:

I’m proud to become part of Bede’s journey. It’s a company with proven strength in engineering excellence, and the potential to build upon Bede’s current capabilities is an exciting prospect.

His appointment was similarly welcomed by Bede CEO Colin Cole-Johnson who said that having Whiteley as the company’s new CTO will prove pivotal for the brand’s further growth and technology strategy. Whiteley will be a crucial part of the company’s operations and bring an extensive experience that sets a brighter future for Bede as a whole.

Bede Gaming is a Gauselmann Group subsidiary since 2020 and the company has been one of the most prominent developers of software for the iGaming sector. Bede Gaming’s successes have been well-documented.
