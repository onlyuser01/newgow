Scales of Dead review

Theme and Storyline

Scales of Dead is themed around ancient Egyptian culture and mythology.

Players will explore the golden dunes of an endless desert until they discover some mysterious pyramids. Precious artifacts await along with a magical secret or two.

This slot will appeal to fans of ancient history.

Base game win in the Scales of Dead slot machine

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Scales of Dead has a dramatic soundtrack that brings to mind films such as Lawrence of Arabia. The reels spin with a metallic clink, giving the slot a realistic feel.

Jeweled scarab beetles, fierce cobras, and ornately dressed jackals can all be found among the symbols. Golden tombs, and ceremonial scepters also make an appearance.

Lower paying symbols take the form of richly-hued letters and numbers trimmed in gold. In shades of emerald green, ruby red, and sapphire blue, these symbols add a luxurious feel to this slot.

In the background, players will glimpse golden sand and a blue sky. Clouds drift slowly in the sky, but the bright sunlight suggests that this is a particularly sweltering desert day. In the distance, pyramids are visible, suggesting players are on a journey to explore the ancient structures.

Together, the soundtrack and animation of this slot create an engaging fantasy of ancient Egypt.

Gameplay

Scales of Dead is a five-reel, three-row slot, with 10 win lines. All winning combinations pay from left to right.

The swift pace of the gameplay ensures that players will easily stay engaged.

The maximum win for Scales of Dead is 10,000x of the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild Tomb symbol can substitute for any symbol to form a winning combination, with the exception of the Special Expanding Symbol.

The Wild Tomb symbol also functions as a Scatter symbol, paying out anywhere on the reels.

When players land at least 3 Scatters, the Free Spins feature begins. If players land 2 or more Scatter symbols during the Free Spins feature, the Sandstorm Free Spins feature begins. As many as 30 free spins can be awarded during the Sandstorm Free Spins round.

Expanding symbol is selected at random at the beginning of Free spins feature

At random, a symbol is selected to be the Special Expanding Symbol. These symbols have the ability to expand to cover their entire reel when they appear. These symbols also have an associated multiplier during the Sandstorm Free Spins feature.
