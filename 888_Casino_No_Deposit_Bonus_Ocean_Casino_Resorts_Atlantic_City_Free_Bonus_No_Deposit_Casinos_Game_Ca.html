Punk Toilet review

Theme and Storyline

Punk Toilet is themed around a rather stylish public bathroom, celebrating the punk music world.

Players will saunter down an alley not far from the pulsing sounds of a concert. What they’ll find is a sketchy toilet adorned with graffiti and pop art.

This slot is a good fit for players looking for a unique theme and a novel atmosphere. Of course, punk fans will enjoy the tribute to the iconic music genre.

The soundtrack is the ambient sounds of a nearby concert, infused with the aggressive rock vibe of the punk movement. This creates the feeling that players are attendees at a punk festival dashing off for a quick bathroom break.

Gameplay

Punk Toilet has a 3-3-3-3-1 reel setup.

Wins are calculated using an Any Way Wins system. This means that players must land matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel and going right.

The gameplay is smooth and players won’t have any difficulty staying engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol will substitute for whichever symbol needed to create the highest paying combination. It does not substitute for the Loo or Scatter symbols.

Players can open the locked "Urinal" positions at the bottom of reels 2, 3, and 4 if they land 1 or 2 Scatter symbols on those reels. The Scatter symbols then fall and transform into Wilds.

The Glory Door is a locked series of positions on reel 5. If players land the Loo symbol on reel 5, they will be presented with bonus options.

The xWays symbol only lands on the middle three reels and they can range from 2 to 6 symbols in size. When it lands it reveals a Wild symbol or a randomly selected regular symbol. If more than one xWays symbol lands, they will all reveal matching symbols.

When players land 3 Scatter symbols, the Free Spins feature begins, starting with 6 free spins. During this feature, the locked "Urinal" positions always open.

If players land 3 Scatter symbols and 1 Loo symbol, an enhanced Free Spins feature begins, starting with 8 free spins.
