Buffalo Bills Going for a Win in Florida

The Buffalo Bills are 10-6 overall with four wins in a row. They defeated a couple of real heavyweights lately, most notably, the Kansas City Chiefs and the Dallas Cowboys. Now, they need to do the same against the Miami Dolphins.

The good news is that the Dolphins probably won’t be too motivated as they’ve already done their job. The bad news is that Sunday’s game is played at Hard Rock Stadium where the Dolphins have been pretty good this season. Further, the Bills aren’t famous for being a team that does spectacularly well on the road.

In fact, their away record is 3-4 this season, but on the bright side, two of those three wins happened in their last two away games.

Will Miami Dolphins Take It Easy in Week 18?

The Dolphins are 11-5 on the season, with their most recent game seeing them get destroyed by the Baltimore Ravens in a battle for the No. 1 seed in the American Football Conference (AFC). The final score was 56-19 for the Ravens.

The Baltimore Ravens gave the Miami Dolphins a taste of their own medicine and they didn’t like it too much.
