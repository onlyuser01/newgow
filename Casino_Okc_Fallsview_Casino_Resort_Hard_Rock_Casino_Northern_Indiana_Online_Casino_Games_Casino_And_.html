Storm Lords: Game Symbols and Payouts

The studio has chosen 11 nicely-themed symbols for the reels, including the amulet scatter symbol, the coin bonus symbols, and the pair of expanding wild symbols. To score a win, players need to match a minimum of three up to five symbols, respectively. The green amulet symbol pays 2x, 10x, and 100x for combinations of three, spurt, and five identical symbols. 9s and 10x pay 5x, 10x, and 50x for three to five matching symbols, while three to five Js and Qs pay out 5x, 10x, and 80x. Players who land three to five Ks and As will hit the following win multipliers – 10x, 20x, and 100x.

Male characters on a yellow background and female characters on a pink background payout 10x, 50x, and 200x. The game also features two symbols that can pay out for two, three, four, or five symbols on a single payline. They are the male character on a red background which pays 2x, 20x, 80x, and 500x, and both Storm Lords land together, with payouts of 2x, 20x, 80x, and 800x.

The Storm Lord wild symbols replace the standard symbols in the game and they fill the entire reel when they pop on the second and fourth reels.

Storm Lords: Wilds, Treasure Coin & Scatters

The game’s wild symbols work by replacing all adjacent symbols with the exception of scatter symbols to generate winning combinations. As previously mentioned, the wilds can be seen popping on the second and fourth reels, further expanding to fill the perspective reels and creating some massive wins for players.

The Treasure Coin feature is triggered when players land a minimum of six treasure coins on any random position on the grid. In exchange, players receive three 3 re-spins while the treasure coins remain locked in place. Lucky players who manage to come across another treasure coin successfully reset the re-spins, further increasing their wins. The total number of coins will eventually determine the size of the wins at the end of the treasure coin feature.

The scatter symbol which is represented by the amulet can also trigger free games when a minimum of three symbols are seen landing during a regular spin. Players can scoop up six free spins with the wild reels. The free spins can also be re-triggered, bringing six additional free games. During the free spins, the Cloud feature and the Wind Wilds will stay locked in position on the second and fourth reels, respectively.

The Treasure Spins feature is similar to the wild reels feature and awards six free games that can trigger an additional treasure coin feature for extra chances to win.
