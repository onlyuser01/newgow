NSYNC Is Back for One Final Performance

Slot aficionados who happened to have a burning passion for the iconic NSYNC band in the 90s have plenty of reasons to rejoice. The Sweden-based company that plans on turning into the leading gaming entertainment supplier has brought back the group for one final mesmerizing performance. This time around, Justin, Chris, Joey, Lance, and JC will introduce players to an action-packed slot with a cool Free Spins feature and a clear goal to expand the reels in the special Encore Spin feature. The reels are filled with Pop Symbols on both the top and the bottom. Each of these can reveal an exciting Mystery symbol. Players must clear the grid during the Free Spins feature and make NSYNC play one more time in the Encore Spin feature. Once all Pop symbols on the additional rows are cleared, players enjoy 2,400 ways to win, while the Encore Spin feature becomes active. During it, Wilds, JC, Justin, Lance, Chris, and Joey symbols are available for landing. The game’s Sticky Wilds will, instead, stay in place.

All five individual artists can be seen on the reels, performing their popular Pop songs in the background while the physical animations are completing the atmosphere with their popping sounds.

A Little Something for Everyone

The company’s head of games Charlotte Miliziano explained their decision to include a pop-themed slot into their already prestigious rock and metal-themed portfolio. She said that Play’n GO is fully dedicated to ensuring its portfolio “has something for everyone.” She added that while their Music IP titles keep being enjoyed by players, the company is also eager to continue its expansion and reach new horizons. Accordingly, she suggested a new ‘80s star may be soon joining Twisted Sister in the near future.

One week ago, the developer released Cat Wilde and the Pyramids of Dead, the sequel to its beloved Book of Dead series. Play’n GO also added Rocco Gallo, an animal-themed slot to its portfolio.
