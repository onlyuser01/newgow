The leading sports technology provider, Genius Sports, expanded its portfolio of partners by signing a new exclusive official data and streaming agreement. On Thursday, the company confirmed it signed a collaboration with the European League of Football (ELF), which represents the American football competition outside North America.

Under the terms of the freshly minted partnership, Genius was designated as the ELF’s official data, technology and broadcast partner. Thanks to the collaboration, the company gained access to exclusive rights for capturing, managing, as well as distributing official live video streams and data from the ELF.

The collaboration will see Genius’ extensive portfolio of partners, including regulated sportsbooks, benefit from official data and streams. Additionally, the deal will see the company support the expansion o of ELF’s sponsorship network around the world. The new season of the League is scheduled to start on June 3, 2023, and will meet 17 teams from 9 different countries.

The Company Continues to Expand Portfolio of Partners

Jonny Katanchian, Genius’ head of sports and broadcast, shared his excitement about the recent announcement. He explained that the company’s leading technology and sports data will expand the reach of official ELF data. Finally, Katanchian noted: “We’re delighted to broaden our world-leading American football offering, providing our partners with year-round exclusive content across multiple time zones.”
