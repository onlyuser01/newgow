The Game Brings Free Spins, Multipliers

The aforementioned Mystery symbols are presented as stars on the reels and reveal symbols that are already matched, granting a chance for big wins via the cluster mechanic. At the same time, the players can benefit from Multiplier Wild symbols that can bring a multiplier of up to 5x “to any win it is a part of whilst substituting for any base game symbols.”

Additionally, the game offers Scatter symbols that can grant players free spins. Landing three or more Scatter symbols unlocks a free spins bonus round with 10 spins. During this round, mystery symbols that land become “sticky,” allowing them to remain in their position which boosts the chance for big payouts.
