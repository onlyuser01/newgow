AffPapa has announced that it has sold out the upcoming iGaming Club Amsterdam event which has a maximum capacity of 300 registrants. iGaming Club has become a focal point of interest for affiliates with AffPapa itself specializing in the affiliate side of the gambling industry and creating formats that are exciting and mutually beneficial for all businesses that area involved in the company’s network.

AffPapa’s iGaming Club Amsterdam 2023 is just around the corner with the latest event not going to be the company’s last opportunity to bring together specialists from the space who are keen to collaborate and advance the industry’s understanding of affiliates.
