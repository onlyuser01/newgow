The Fresh Upgrade Brings Players Closer to Big Wins

The new game preserves most of its predecessor’s mechanics, graphics, and elements that turned it into a popular title soon after its launch last September. The addition of the Bonus Buy upgrade, however, gives players the opportunity to buy 10, 15, or 20 Free Spins, bringing them closer to the big wins.

The Fishing and Dynamite features are once again part of the offering. The two features helped the original game turn into a top performer in Europe in terms of gross gaming revenue and SlotCount in the last few months.

To trigger the Fishing feature in the new game, players must first engage in the Free Spins game. There, they need to come across Wilds and Fish symbols on any location on the grid. All Wild symbols collect all the values of all the Fish symbols found on the reels.

The Wild’s final value will represent the player’s winnings. The latter is added to the rest of the regular winning combos hit by players during the round.

To trigger the Dynamite feature, players need to come at the end of a losing spin with a minimum of one Wild. No Fish symbols must be found on the reels. During this feature, between three and six regular symbols get replaced by Fish symbols.

Players enter the Free Spins round whenever Scatter symbols are visible on any of the reels. Extra Scatter symbols trigger additional Free Spins and boost the winning potential up to x10,000 the original bet.
