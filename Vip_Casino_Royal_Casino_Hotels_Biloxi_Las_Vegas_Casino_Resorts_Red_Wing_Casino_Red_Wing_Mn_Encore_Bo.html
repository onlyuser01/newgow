Kambi to Support BetCity.nl

Kambi will elevate BetCity’s sports betting options through its critically-acclaimed technologies. Furthermore, Kambi’s profound experience with gambling regulations and maintaining integrity will be of invaluable help for BetCity’s launch.

Kambi’s chief executive officer, Kristian Nylén, is happy to work with BetEnt. Nylén spoke on behalf of the whole company when he said that Kambi is “delighted to have been selected as the exclusive online sportsbook provider to BetCity.nl”.

“The Netherlands will be extremely competitive but the combination of Kambi’s technology and sports betting know-how with BetEnt’s sports-first strategy leaves the BetCity.nl sportsbook well placed to succeed,” Nylén optimistically added.

BetEnt’s chief executive, Melvin Bostelaar, is just as happy to work with Kambi. Bostelaar places his full trust in Kambi’s expertise as one of the best sportsbook providers on the market.

“It is exciting to enter the regulated Dutch market with such a first-class partner who fully supports and has an unrivaled track record in our values of compliance and sports integrity,” Bostelaar added.
