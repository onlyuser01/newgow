Mini Slot Machine Bonus and Envelope Bonus

Landing at least three scatter symbols anywhere on the reels will unlock the Mini Slot Machine Bonus. The captivating bonus round counts eight mini-slot machines that will start to spin. While engaged in this round, players must land a minimum of three adjacent dragons on each of the mini-slot machines to receive a respin.

When a mini slot machine does not feature the dragon trio on the reels, players will see it automatically get locked. This will, in turn, prevent it from further spinning.

Players who manage to fill an entire reel with dragon symbols get the Envelope bonus. The latter will reveal an instant jackpot cash prize on each of the mini slot machines that also feature an increasing multiplier. The maximum win that players can reach with the help of the bonus is capped at 8,800x the bet.
