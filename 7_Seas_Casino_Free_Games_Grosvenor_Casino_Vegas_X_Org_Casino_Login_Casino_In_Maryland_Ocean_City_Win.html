BF Games, a developer of premium dynamic iGaming titles, has expanded its reach in Europe with a new deal. The studio inked an agreement with Stanleybet.ro, a leading online operator in Romania. Under the deal, BF Games will supply its new client with a vast range of exciting gaming titles.

BF Games Expands in Romania with Stanleybet

As per the deal, BF Games will power Stanleybet’s content offering with its critically acclaimed games. Some of the titles that will become available to Romanian audiences include hits such as Book of Gates, Buffalo Trail Ultra, Cave of Fortune, Energy Fruits, Hot Sunrise, Stunning Hot Remastered and Wild Jack Remastered, among others.

The current deal continues BF Games’ expansion in Romania, which is a market that the studio has been actively targeting. In September, BF Games agreed to supply games to Superbet, another Romanian operator. Shortly after, the studio minted another deal, this time with Favbet. Both agreements were welcomed by BF Games, which has a profound understanding of what players in the region want and is thrilled to expand its local footprint.

BF Games currently boasts a rich library of thrilling slot titles that is continuously expanding with new additions. The company is ambitious on creating a vast portfolio of games, allowing it to appeal to the needs of gamers all over the world.

Stanleybet, on the other hand, is a seasoned operator with years of experience in Romania’s land-based and iGaming market. The company opened a retail business in the country in 2004 and, more than a decade later, secured a license to operate online gambling as well. In 2018, the company finally launched its Kambi-powered online gambling site, foraying into the digital sector.
