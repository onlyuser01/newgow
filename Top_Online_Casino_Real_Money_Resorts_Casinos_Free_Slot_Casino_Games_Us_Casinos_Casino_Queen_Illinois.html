The former Sony Pictures Entertainment and Starz International executive will fill the role of chief strategy and content officer effective from October 9

The leading provider of gaming solutions, powering the global gambling sector and ASX20 listed company, Aristocrat Leisure Limited, announced a new senior-level appointment. The company has announced it strengthened its team with the addition of Superna Kalle, an expert with extensive experience in the media sector.

Joining Aristocrat, she was appointed to the role of chief strategy and content officer. Although the senior-level appointment was announced recently, the company explained that Kalle will fill in her role effective from October 9, 2023. Currently, she holds a Bachelor of Arts from Barnard College, Columbia University. Additionally, Kalle has an MBA from the NYU Stern School of Business.

In her new role, she will oversee strategic processes related to the company’s growth. As chief strategy and content officer, Kalle will report directly to Aristocrat’s managing director and CEO, Trevor Croker. Sharing his excitement, he revealed that Kalle brings extensive knowledge from the media sector, spanning more than two decades.

Croker spoke about her proven success as a strategy and operational leader. “Superna has a track record of maximizing the strategic value of content across a range of consumer media verticals,” added Aristocrat’s CEO and managing director.

“I am delighted to welcome a global executive of Superna Kalle’s caliber to Aristocrat, and to our leadership team.“ Trevor Croker, managing director and CEO at Aristocrat

Moreover, Croker welcomed Kalle to the company’s growing team of experts and explained that it is a pleasure to have such a seasoned veteran join Aristocrat. He predicted that the new chief strategy and content officer will play a key role in the company’s growth and help it expand significantly its reach. Finally, Croker said: “I look forward to her contribution and congratulate her warmly on her appointment.”

Joining Aristocrat’s C-suite, Kalle brings a wide range of knowledge and experience. Overall, she was a part of Sony Pictures Entertainment for 16 years. Throughout her professional career with the company, she spearheaded important operations across Asia, including in India and Japan. Additionally, Kalle also has experience with Sony’s US-facing networks. Her professional background includes an important role with Starz International. Serving as president, Kalle oversaw the company’s expansion to more than 60 countries around the globe.
