Effectively, this program allows players to use Caesars.com to book a comprehensive offer that includes resort accommodations and other activities. The company is confident that the Travel Bundle is what it describes as the best “Caesars Entertainment gateway” offering a revamped traveling and vacation experience that adds true value and advantage to the customer experience.

We’re so proud to see the Caesars Rewards Travel Bundle come to fruition.
