Aristocrat Strikes Slot Machine Licensing Agreement with NFL

The gaming technology supplier Aristocrat Gaming announced a new agreement with the National Football League (NFL). Under the multi-year slot machine licensing agreement, Aristocrat is granted an exclusive global license for building NFL-themed land-based slot machines.

Moreover, the first of its kind deal for the NFL features non-exclusive rights for virtual sports games. Thanks to this partnership, the NFL has the unique opportunity to boost fan engagement. The new NFL-themed slot machines are expected to be released for the 2023 NFL season.

The unique slots will enable NFL fans to customize their experience by choosing their favorite team. A short promotional video reveals that with the new NFL-themed slot machines “every play is an experience.” For the moment, the gaming technology supplier is yet to release more details regarding the new slot machines.
