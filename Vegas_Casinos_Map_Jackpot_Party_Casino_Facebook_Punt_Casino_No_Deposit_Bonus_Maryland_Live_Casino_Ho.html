Big Bucks Bandits, Yggdrasil and ReelPlay’s latest Megaways slot title, features a thrilling Wild West adventure developed with YG Masters.

117,649 Ways to Win

Yggdrasil and ReelPlay just announced the release of Big Bucks Bandits Megaways, their new action-packed slot game set in the Wild West. The leading iGaming operator’s new title was developed by studio partner ReelPlay via the YG Masters program and offers players a rip-roaring gunslinging adventure in pursuing bounties.

With 117,649 different ways to win and prizes of up to 10,000 times the original bet, Big Bucks Bandits uses the Megaways innovative mechanic, which brings a random reel modifier.

Various features allow players to multiply their chances to win as well as the size of their winnings. The Double Cross Gamble will give slot fans the opportunity to wager their prizes to increase their total winnings by up to five times. Players can immediately withdraw their winnings or take a gamble and move on to the next multiplier.

Triggered with six or more scatter symbols, the Jackpot Heist Respin rewards players with three additional spins. New symbols will remain on the reels, while the respin count will be reset. Once the spins are all spent, each symbol held until there will grant users with jackpots and prizes.

On top of those exciting features, the game features the Trophy Jackpots, which offers different jackpot levels, such as the Platinum pot which multiplies the original bet by 1,000.
