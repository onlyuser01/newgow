Gambling Industry Expert Peter Nolan Passed Away

Nolan had a rich experience in the casino, sports betting, poker and horse racing industries. He was successful in developing innovative products and operations that helped the growth of companies within the gambling industry. Aside from the UK, Nolan participated in successful projects for land-based and online gambling operators around Europe, Brazil and even Africa.

Nolan was the founder and CEO of iGaming Solutions, a leading independent betting and gaming consultancy. Founded back in 2009, the company leveraged extensive experience and offered quality services that helped add value to different companies around the globe operating within the betting and gaming vertical. iGaming Solutions is famous today for its cost-effective way of doing business that helps deliver value on a long-term, short term or interim basis. This provides flexible solutions that fit the needs of different companies within the industry. What’s more, the company helps its clients achieve growth while cutting costs and improving efficiencies.

In addition to iGaming Solutions, Nolan was a part of FoliuMed, a company that focuses on producing cannabis extracts as well as non-smokeable products for medicinal and health uses. During his tenure at the company, he served as CEO of the UK between January 2019 and January 2020. Back in 2016, Nolan was a part of SUAPOSTA, an online betting company licensed in Brazil. He was a part of the company for one year serving as its CEO.

Nolan Will Be Missed

Besides managing director at iGaming Solutions, Nolan was also a part of Digitain, a global award-winning iGaming software solutions provider. Digitain offers a leading casino and sportsbook platform catering to the retail and online gambling industry.

The company holds more than a dozen awards and is a proven specialist in the industry. Back in April 2020, Nolan joined the company and served as its strategic consultant until June this year. He was then appointed to the position of group R&D director.

“Digitain’s staff and entire community deeply mourn the passing of our most beloved and respected Peter Nolan, Group R&D Director and one of the company’s pillars.
