The opportunity to be adding a prominent supplier was welcomed in earnest by Vicca Group National Marketing Director Enrique Amorocho who hailed this opportunity and said that the enterprise was fully focused on promoting the best gaming experience to players in the country.

"With Zitro, we know we can offer players an outstanding product range, as innovation is what differentiates Zitro’s games. We are excited to expand our offering with their titles and further strengthen our partnership," Amorocho added.

Zitro’s Regional Director for Latin America, Alejandra Burato, was similarly pleased with this opportunity and she said that it was great to see Vicca Group entrust Zitro with the delivery of more of the supplier’s gaming machines.

In fact, Burato said that Vicca Group’s brands and their players have already embraced the Zitro slots and were keen to explore new opportunities as well. Burato thanked Vicca Group for the opportunity to continue on this partnership together and continue to deliver top-performing games for Vicca Group and its many brands, across their numerous casino floors.
