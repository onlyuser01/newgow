Colossus: Hold and Win Information

Players are carried into a mesmerizing place, right next to the Colossus of Rhodes, a statue dedicated to Helios, the god of the sun in Ancient Greek mythology. Game Features

The most important and at the same time the most valuable symbol within the game is the Colossus Wild. The symbol will expand once it lands on a winning line and trigger a multiplier of up to 10x.

The Hold & Win Respins can be triggered with at least six gold coins. Initially, a player will receive three respins. Then, the spins will automatically reset every time a minimum of one coin will land on the reels. The visible values will be paid out once the respins are over.

The Colossus Rising Flame will pop at the beginning of each new Hold & Win Respins round, or whenever a new coin lands. In turn, this will cause a flare-up that will multiply the value of every coin on the screen by as much as 10x. This means players can expect to scoop up a juicy payout in the end.

Players who manage to fill up the grid with golden coins during the Hold & Win respins will win a Colossus Spin on the special wheel. This will either engage the Colossus Award, the Minor, or the Major Awards, worth 10,000x, 50x, and 500x their bets.

The game also features multiplier wilds that will assist players with creating more winning combos by substituting missing symbols, except for coins. Once a multiplier wild lands, it will expand and cover the entire surface of the reel, while triggering multipliers ranging from 2X to 10X. The bonus buy feature allows players to pay 100x and instantly activate the bonus game. Colossus: Hold & Win can be played on the go, as it is compatible with all types of mobile devices, including notepads.
