The renowned provider of games for the online gambling industry, Pragmatic Play, announced the launch of a new spooky Halloween-themed slot game. The company confirmed earlier this week the launch of Infective Wild, its newest slot game offering wilds, free spins and a chance for significant prizes.

The latest slot game comes ahead of Halloween, and it features a spooky theme with vibrant colors and thrilling sound and visual effects. The new slot game from Pragmatic Play uses standard card symbols between 10 and Ace but also incorporates high-paying symbols such as a cat, raven and potion, among other symbols that match the distinct Halloween theme.

Pragmatic Play's recent slot game offers 40 paylines and an RTP of 96.09%. Quality animations and engaging options such as auto-play elevate the excitement further, making Infective Wild a perfect addition to the iGaming provider's growing portfolio of thrilling and unique games.

In a statement released Thursday, Pragmatic Play's Chief Operating Officer, Irina Cornides, shared her excitement about the latest slot game. "We set out to create a game that embodies the spooky spirit of Halloween while also offering an unexpected mechanic – Infective Wild with its infectious feature spreading Wilds across the reels does just that and more," she explained.

Cornides added that the base game offers big win potential which is further propelled by Wild wins. "We’re excited to see the reception this Halloween-themed slot has from players and operators alike," she explained in conclusion.

While the base game offers an entertaining experience, players can benefit from Wilds that have the ability to substitute any base game symbols. This unique feature grants a chance for mouth-watering payouts. However, Wilds that land adjacent to standard high-paying symbols help transform them into additional Wilds. This process continues to spread, effectively infecting the reels for staggering payouts.

On the other hand, landing at least three scatters grants the players free spins. Just before the start of the free spins round, the slot game presents eight mini-reels that can award up to three free spins during the bonus round. Wilds that land during free spins stay in their place, granting a chance for the transformation of symbols and more subsequent wins.
