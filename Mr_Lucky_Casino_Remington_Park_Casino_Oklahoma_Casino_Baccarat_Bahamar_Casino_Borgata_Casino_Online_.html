Apparat Gaming to Spread Its Wings Across Europe

In turn, Apparat Gaming will have the opportunity to introduce its gaming products to new audiences and make sure that its product portfolio is known to players from all across Europe and beyond. Apparat Gaming has a Malta Gaming License and has launched on the market in July 2021. Presently, the company employs 15 team members and it offers what it describes as iGaming “with a German accent.”

The developer has unique titles such as Pharaoh Princess, 40 Sevens, Total Eclipse, and Fruit Storm. The company is also planning to introduce its products to a number of established and licensed German operators.
