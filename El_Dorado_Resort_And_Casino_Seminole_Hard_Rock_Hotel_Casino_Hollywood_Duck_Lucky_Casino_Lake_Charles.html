New Product Joins Gaming Corps’ Offer

The game follows the basic layout of the classic version which features a Player and Banker Bets, which are the two possible options to bet on. Baccarat is all about guessing which of the two types of bets will yield better results.

There is no obligation to bet on the Player Bet just because you are a “player.” Rather, you pick one of the two based on who you think would get a better hand.
