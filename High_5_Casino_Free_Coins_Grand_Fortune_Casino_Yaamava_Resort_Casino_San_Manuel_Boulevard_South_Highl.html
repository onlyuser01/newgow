Exacta Systems, the leading provider of technology, powering new and existing Historic Horse Racing (HHR) markets, announced a new collaboration, expanding its US footprint. On Thursday, the company revealed it agreed to supply Derby City Gaming Downtown in Louisville, Kentucky, with its leading solutions. Per the terms of the new collaboration, Exacta Systems confirmed it will deliver its Exacta Connect system, bringing top titles from AGS, Bluberi, Incredible Technologies and Everi for the leading gaming and entertainment venue that is operated and owned by Churchill Downs Incorporated.

The latest deal reaffirms the company's position as a go-to provider for gaming operators. The installation at Derby City Gaming Downtown further expands Exacta Systems' portfolio of partners, allowing its technology to reach new customers and bring new and exceptional experiences.

As a renowned provider of innovative technology, Exacta Systems plays an important role in the development of future and existing HHR markets. Thanks to the company's Exacta Connect system, operators can benefit from unlimited math modeling capabilities. Moreover, the system supports games from different vendors allowing operators to tap into a diverse library of titles. Ultimately, Exacta System promises unrivaled experiences for its partners and their clients.

Garth Williams, Derby City Downtown's general manager, was excited about the recently announced collaboration. "This is certainly an exciting time for our entire team and we couldn't be happier than to have a partner like Exacta alongside us," he explained in a statement. Additionally, Williams said: "Exacta's customer service, reliability and ability to deliver a diversity of game titles from top manufacturers all under one system, make them a partner for success."

Jeff Clifford, Exacta Systems' vice president of sales and revenue, was similarly delighted about the freshly minted partnership. "Exacta Systems is excited and proud to be launching our system in a Kentucky-based Churchill Downs Incorporated property," he said. Finally, Clifford revealed: "Alongside our new partners, we will continue to expand the HHR footprint while also maintaining the highest level of customer support that we're so well known for."

Exacta Systems was founded more than a decade ago in 2012. Headquartered in Boynton Beach, Florida, the company has been continuously growing its footprint, becoming a leading provider of games and systems powering the HHR sector. Besides Florida, Exacta Systems has offices located in Richmond, Virginia, as well as Austin, Texas.
