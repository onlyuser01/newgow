Betway has become the official sports betting partner of Spanish La Liga champions Atlético de Madrid.

The online gaming operator signed a two-year global partnership, excluding in Spain and Asia, with the 11-time Spanish champions to increase its presence in the sporting sphere.

The deal will see Betway’s branding feature pitch-side on LED advertising boards at the club’s Wanda Metropolitano Stadium in domestic and European fixtures. The operator will also have access to Atlético’s players and the ability to produce social and digital content for the football club’s fans.

Betway CEO Anthony Werkman said: "We’re incredibly proud to become the official sports betting partner of the current La Liga champions, Atlético de Madrid.

"We’re excited to have an agreement with oneofthebiggest clubs involved in both La Liga and the Champions League; two of the most viewed sporting competitions around the world."

"With a team full of internationally recognised names, this partnership will gain further exposure for our brand, that has an ambitious global vision."

Betway already boasts an extensive list of sporting sponsorships. It is currently the main shirt sponsor of English Premier League team West Ham United. While in July, the operator became the premium partner of German Bundesliga club VFB Stuttgart and the official betting partner of league rival Eintracht Frankfurt. It sponsors all of the West Indies’ men and women home international cricket matches, and within the last year signed sponsorship deals with the South African rugby and cricket teams.
