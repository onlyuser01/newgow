This week Sportradar, the leading provider of sports data and information, announced their partnership with Major League Baseball /MLB/ expanded further through the launch of virtual baseball in-play for all licensed gaming operators around the world.

Meet Sportradar

Sportradar is the MLB real-time game statistics exclusive distributor, providing data for all international markets except the U.S. to companies from the media sector and the regulated sports betting industry, with the exclusive rights to distribute live standard audio-visual game feeds for any territory outside the U.S. where sports betting is legal.

Virtual Reality That Surpasses Expectations

Being the official supplier of MLB’s real-time betting data feed through the official MLB Statcast data allows Sportradar to utilize the development of technologies in virtual reality and present the baseball fans and sports bettors with an in-play betting solution that almost replicates a real game, as there are all 30 MLB teams, with official kits and player logos, the MLB brand and player avatars animated by state-of-the-art motion capture from professional athletes, to ensure a game of unraveled details and realism.

“As with all of our virtual sports offerings, our aim was to bring the most realistic look, feel, and betting experience to the market” David Lampitt, Managing Director, Sports Partnerships, Sportradar.

Besides giving fans the excitement of watching an almost real game of baseball, the in-play betting solution provides sportsbooks with an additional source of revenue, expanding on the sector of sports betting that has the highest growth in the past few years, in-play betting, offering them a unique product based on historical data that can be accessed any time throughout the year.

The Art of Entertainment
