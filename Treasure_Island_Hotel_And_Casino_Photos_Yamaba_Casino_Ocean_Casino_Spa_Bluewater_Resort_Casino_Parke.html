The Green Knight review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed on Arthurian medieval fantasy.

In an eerie forest, players will take on an epic quest, finding runes, knights, sorceresses, and, of course, the legendary Green Knight himself. In this realm, magic is possible and myth becomes real.

Those excited about the modern film interpretation of The Green Knight will especially enjoy getting lost in this supernatural world.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is an epic fantasy melody that brings to mind the climatic moment of an adventure. This helps add tension and place the player in the action.

The animation has a vivid high quality that makes it easy to get lost in the game world. Players will find a mythical horse, an ornately designed ax, and an array of celtic runes. Each of these objects suggests a world teeming with ethereal magic.

The Green Knight himself is iconic with shimmering armour adorned with plants. His antlers are especially striking and make it clear that he is a creature of myth.

The forest backdrop gives the game a spooky atmosphere and creates the feeling that players are in the Arthurian legend with the characters.

Overall, the front end of this game does a superb job of illustrating its ancient subject matter. Lovers of the original myth or the more recent film will not be disappointed.

Gameplay

The Green Knight is a five reel, three row slot, with 20 win lines.

The gameplay is slick and fast paced so players will have no difficulty staying engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Green Knight and Special Green Knight symbols.

Multiplier Wilds can also land on any spin and during the free spins feature Wilds will always land as Multiplier Wilds.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when 3 or more Green Knight symbols land. The number of free spins players receive goes up depending on how many Green Knight symbols land.

During the Free Spins feature players will receive a progressive multiplier for every spin.

While in the Free Spins feature, players can land 2 or more Special Green Knight symbols to trigger 5 Shield Spins.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.27%, slightly above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.10 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this moderately high RTP, high volatility Arthurian fantasy slot.

The gorgeous animation combined with the high volatility make this game a clearly excellent choice. The bonus options uphold the theme well and add an extra twist to this slot.

Players will enjoy both gameplay and possibility for maximising wins that this slot has to offer.
