Premier management advisory firm SCCG Management has enhanced its Global Advisory Board with the appointment of the seasoned industry veteran Mark Stebbings as chairman. The new appointee boasts decades of leadership experience that will reinforce SCCG’s position as a leading gambling advisory firm.

Prior to joining SCCG Management, Stebbings served as the chief operating officer of Betfred Group. During his three-decade-long tenure at Betfred Stebbings oversaw the company’s expansion in a number of markets, including several notable US states, such as Colorado, Arizona, Ohio, Virginia, Nevada and Maryland.

According to SCCG Management’s announcement, Stebbings’ leadership has been a major factor in Betfred’s growth trajectory.

As the chair of the SCCG Global Advisory Board, Stebbings will play a role in improving the SCCG Managed Services (SCCGMS). The company believes that Stebbings’ appointment will cement its position as a leader in gambling-related advisory services.

For reference, the company’s Managed Services are designed to help tribal operators manage their online casino, sports betting and social gaming operations. To that end, the SCCGMS provides tribal companies with a myriad of solutions, risk management tools and bespoke training programs.

The Managed Services are designed to help SCCG serve a vast network of partners and deliver exceptional, client-focused service.

CEO Crystal Welcomed Stebbings to the Company

Mark Stebbings commented on his new appointment and expressed his delight about the opportunity to work with SCCG Management. He vowed to leverage his decades of experience in the sports betting and online casino gaming sectors to help the company evolve.

I look forward to utilizing my 30 years of sports betting and iGaming experience to assist Stephen and his dedicated team in achieving their goals and reinforcing SCCG’s position as the top consultancy in the gambling industry. Mark Stebbings, chairman, SCCG Global Advisory Board

Stephen Crystal, SCCG Management’s founder and chief executive, welcomed Stebbings to the SCCG family. The CEO praised the new appointee’s “remarkable insights and leadership qualities” that helped Betfred expand its presence in the US.

I am thrilled about the depth and breadth of experience Mark brings to our advisory board and SCCG Managed Services offering. His vision and expertise are exactly what we need to drive our innovative services to new heights and continue setting the gold standard in the gaming industry. Stephen Crystal, founder & CEO, SCCG Management
