Cavs Pick ‘Em Live Game Now Available to Cavaliers Fans

The GameDay+ experience now comes with a free-to-play LIVE predictive game known simply as Cavs Pick ‘Em. The premise is simple – fans receive awards based on whether they have successfully predicted what happens during a game in real-time.

For all intents and purposes, the Cavs Pick ‘Em is a live betting experience with the added benefit of playing for free. According to the official press release, the game will also test fans IQ by seeing if Cavaliers loyalists can successfully predict developments during a game.
