Hot to Burn Extreme review

Theme and Storyline

Hot to Burn Extreme has a classic fruit machine theme with a fiery twist.

Players will enter a familiar fruity world of watermelons, oranges, cherries, and lemons. In this retro realm of floating produce, sizzling flames spice up the scene.

This slot will best suit fans of vintage fruit machine aesthetics.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The fast-paced soundtrack brings to mind retro dance music with plenty of digitally-infused sounds. Upon spins, there is a mechanical clink that suggests the mechanism of an old school slot machine.

The animation quality is moderate, with black outlines contrasting the semi-realistic fruit depictions. Cherries, lemons, plums, and strawberries are all rendered in bright hues that call on classic slot style. This look will appeal most to players who enjoy retro aesthetics.

The dark backdrop contrasts with animations of flames on either side of the reels, giving a nod to the heat of this slot’s name.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack create a vivid world of fruit and flame.

Gameplay

Hot to Burn Extreme is a five reel, four row slot, with 40 win lines.

The gameplay is quite swift and engaging, ensuring players’ attention will be held.

The maximum win for Hot to Burn Extreme is 5,000x players’ total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. The Wild symbol appears on all the reels.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land at least 3 Scatters. The number of free spins awarded depends on how many Scatters triggered the feature as follows:

3 Scatters = 10 free spins

4 Scatters = 15 free spins

5 Scatters = 20 free spins

Players can retrigger the Free Spins feature by landing 3 or more Scatters again, with the number of spins decided by the number of Scatters landed, as above.

Special reels are used for the duration of the Free Spins feature.

Players can buy directly into the Free Spins feature if they wish by paying 100x their current total bet.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.65%, nicely above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.40 up to a maximum of $/£/€240 per spin.

The volatility of Hot to Burn Extreme is medium, which makes it a less ideal match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, medium volatility fruit machine slot.

Though the volatility might be somewhat moderate for mathematically minded players, the robust RTP and brightly hued animation elevate this slot.

For players who are itching for a classic fruit machine, this slot will be especially tasty.
