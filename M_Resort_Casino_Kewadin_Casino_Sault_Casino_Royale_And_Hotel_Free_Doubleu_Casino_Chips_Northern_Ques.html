CT Interactive, a company that develops and supplies games for online casinos, has announced a new distribution agreement with GrooveGaming, an expert iGaming aggregator. As agreed, the former company will supply the latter with its premium iGaming portfolio.

Under the agreement, CT Interactive’s suite of games will become available to GrooveGaming clients. The Bulgarian casino supplier boasts a library of over 200 unique titles, each featuring exciting mechanics, engaging features and excellent math models. CT Interactive’s games have varied themes, helping its content appeal to a wide range of players.

The current deal between CT Interactive and GrooveGaming was finalized during ICE London 2023, the biggest annual gaming industry convention.

GrooveGaming Clients Are Sure to Appreciate CT Interactive’s Diverse Games

The partnership was welcomed by the two companies’ leadership. CT Interactive’s head of business development for the EMEA region, Milena Tsankarska, addressed the deal in a new statement. She lauded GrooveGaming as a reputable company that has cemented itself as a top platform for casino content.

Because of that, Tsankarska is happy to join forces with the aggregator and provide it with the best games CT Interactive has to offer.

We are very happy that our content will become available for more clients worldwide, and we plan to continue supplying the platform with our new games. Milena Tsankarska, head of business development EMEA, CT Interactive

Yahale Meltzer, GrooveGaming’s co-founder and chief operating officer, also commented on the agreement. He praised CT Interactive’s products for their high quality and applauded the company for its diverse content. Meltzer concluded that he is certain GrooveGaming’s customers will appreciate the variety of content that CT Interactive will bring.

We are sure that our customers would appreciate the diversity of their content and enjoy the new games. Yahale Meltzer, COO, GrooveGaming

Other Recent Developments Involving the Two Companies

The agreement follows other developments for CT Interactive. A few days ago, the content provider expanded its presence in Italy thanks to an agreement with the Italian operator Slot Plus.
