The Player versus Player Battles grant options for preconfiguring battle currency and conversion, as well as set entry timings.

Additionally, PVP Battles offer other configuration options such as jackpot boundaries, stake limits and battle types. Powered by innovation, the new feature seeks to boost customer engagement and bring unique incentives for the players. The participants can either create their own battle or play in arenas that are created by other players.

Ultimately, PVP Battles offer an immersive spin game that has a winner.
