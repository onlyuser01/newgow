Play’n GO has announced it has reached close to 250 million daily rounds played on November 1, marking a new record for the company

The pioneer business-to-business online slots provider that recognized the potential of “gaming on the GO” almost two decades ago, Play’n GO, has announced that it has broken its record for the number of rounds played in a day.

The figure marks an all-time high for the studio since it first opened its virtual gates to the public in 2005. The number underlies the “enduring strength of the company” as well as their ongoing expansion across global regulated gaming markets.

At the moment, the company’s 300 premium games are delivered to casino operators in over 25 global jurisdictions.

The CCO took the opportunity to mention the upcoming release of blockbuster titles like Gargantoonz, which is their latest addition to the popular Reactoonz series launched almost a decade ago, and which is also expected to help them finish the year with even more impressive records.

Olsson also expressed his feelings of pride regarding the work of the entire team, calling it “brilliant” to be rewarded with a new all-time high.
