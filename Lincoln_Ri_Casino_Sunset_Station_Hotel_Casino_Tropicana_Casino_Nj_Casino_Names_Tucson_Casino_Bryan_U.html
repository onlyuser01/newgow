Q: You mention Unlimited Spins - is this more of a marketing buzzword or a genuine intent to set yourself apart from other slot companies and introduce something new?

The feature doesn’t end like a normal bonus where you get a set number of spins. The Bonus in Blazinator ends when a single win is the same or higher than the Big Hit level. It’s a pretty great feeling when you get a Big Hit and you think the bonus is over and then BAM! a Blue Fireball is revealed which keeps the bonus going by giving you an extra Big Hit. Red Fireballs will raise the Big Hit level on the side meter – increasing the chance to have an even longer bonus round with great potential. It’s a full-on action-packed bonus.
