Betcris Teams Up with FC Juárez

As per the agreement, Betcris will sponsor FC Juárez, also known as Bravos de Juárez, for at least for years. The deal will see the operator work with the club on various activations as both try to promote each other’s brand.

Through this deal, Betcris will expand its presence in Mexico and grow its brand. Meanwhile, the club will receive crucial sponsorship that will help it to continue its operations and deliver the performances fans love.

Betcris shared an official statement on the matter. The company said that FC Juárez is an ambitious team that is always looking toward the future. Betcris added that it hopes for a long-term relationship with the club as both strive to achieve success on and off the field.

“In sports, the FC Juárez board is working hard making important signings to make this team a protagonist in Liga MX. Betcris welcomes FC Juárez to their family and they look forward to continuing to grow together and achieving their goals both in sports and business.”
