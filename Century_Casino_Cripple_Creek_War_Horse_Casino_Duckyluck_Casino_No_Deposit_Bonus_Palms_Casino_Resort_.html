The thing is that Everton’s roster looked really well last year, but the Toffees finished the season in the #16 position – just two spots above the relegation zone.

The Start of a New Era in Chelsea

This club achieved its greatest moments during the Abramovich era. However, he’s no longer the club owner. Instead, it’s Todd Boehly, an American entrepreneur famous for being the owner of DraftKings. Since the takeover, a big pile of money has been added to Chelsea’s transfer budget. However, we didn’t see the Blues spend too much on signings.

One of their biggest signings of the summer is Kalidou Koulibaly who came from Napoli as a replacement for Antonio Rudiger, after his departure to Real Madrid. Chelsea also signed Raheem Sterling, an England international who played for Manchester City in the past seven seasons.

What the Blues still haven’t done is sign a center forward to fill in the gap left by Romelu Lukaku. They need a striker desperately as Timo Werner has decided to move away from Stamford Bridge by the end of summer. What all this means is that Chelsea is practical without a proper striker, with Kai Havertz expected to be in charge of goal scoring at the start of the season.
