Hard Rock Atlantic City Boosts C-Suite

The news about the latest jackpots come on the heels of senior-level changes announced by Hard Rock Atlantic City. Seeking at expanding its presence in the region and increasing market share, early last month, the company promoted the experts George Goldhoff and Mike Sampson.

Goldhoff joined Hard Rock Hotel and Casino Atlantic City as its new president, effective from January 30, 2023. Previously, he was the president of Hard Rock Casino Cincinnati. On the other hand, Sampson, who previously held the position of interim general manager of Hard Rock Hotel and Casino Atlantic City, was promoted to general manager. His position was also effective as of January 30.

In his new role as general manager, Sampson reports to Goldhoff. As president of Hard Rock Atlantic City, Goldhoff on the other hand reports directly to Hard Rock International’s CEO and chairman, Jim Allen.
