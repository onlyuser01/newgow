ESA Gaming Strikes Another Deal in Italy

Provider of unique and innovative iGaming products, ESA Gaming, has taken a new stab at the Italian market by partnering up with local operator Scommettendo.

Thanks to the link-up, ESA will provide the operator with its EasySwipe portfolio of optimized HTML5 mobile-first games designed to facilitate gamification for all parties, and easily integrated into sportsbooks offers.

ESA’s flagship products in the series include Goal Mine and Fruit Staxx as well as several versions of Blackjack and Roulette, adding inherent value to the gaming experience. In the meantime, ESA is working on other games that will be coming all throughout 2021 and reinforce the company’s existing partners.

As to Scommettendo, the company has over 10 years on the market and has significant presence in Italy’s evolving iGaming landscape. Thanks to the company’s own unique gamification platform, it has positioned itself as one of the leading choices for players of all hues.

Expanding beyond its native Italy, the company has been able to obtain certification from Colombia and Malta and will focus on growing in new jurisdictions in 2021. Commenting on the deal, ESA Gaming director Zorica Smallwood said that the tie-up with Scommettendo was a great start to the year, with the developing benefiting from Scommettendo’s established market presence.

ESA Gaming’s Strong Growth Continues

For its part, ESA Gaming is relying on the popularity of the EasySwipe portfolio which has a proven cross-selling ability, Smallwood noted. Scommettendo COO Donato Nigro shared the company’s company to be teaming up and adding ESA Gaming’s unique content to its product lineup.

“ESA Gaming offers a high-quality online casino experience to players while they are betting on their favourite sport. We look forward to a successful partnership,” Nigro concluded.

Previously, ESA Gaming has sought closer ties with other established Italian operators, including Aresway late in December. ESA tapped into Aresway’s gaming aggregating system, allowing the company to share its EasySwipe portfolio with Italy’s iGaming community.
