One of the first sportsbooks in the world to adopt esports betting markets is now bringing a new dedicated competitive event under its aegis

The Pinnacle Cup, a popular tournament series organized by the namesake sports and esports betting company, will be coming back for a new edition in 2023. The competition focuses on Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, one of the most bet-on video games in the world of esports.

Pinnacle Cup Returns in 2023 with Four New Events

Pinnacle is also expanding the current scope of the competition, adding a Brazilian circuit which reflects both the company’s commitment to some of the most popular regions when it comes to CS:GO and also the fans based in those regions.

The 2023 edition will feature a total of four events. The upcoming events will include a Swiss and Playoffs format and they will have a Play-In stage with a $50,000 prize pool. Two of the big tournaments that are coming will be taking place in Brazil, with Pinnacle giving a much stronger focus on the jurisdiction.
