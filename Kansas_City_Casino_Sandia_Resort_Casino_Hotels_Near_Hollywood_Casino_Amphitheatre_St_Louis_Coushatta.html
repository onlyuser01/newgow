Playson has marked another string of successes so far in 2022 and to celebrate this, the company has launched “Playson & FRIENDS,” an initiative designed to focus on the company’s global network of partner operators. The initiative seeks to appreciate its collaborations with leading operators who continue to use the company’s aggregation platform, promotional tools, and in-house products.

Playson Hosts First “FRIENDS” Event

This initiative comes along with a series of events that will take place in what Playson believes to be key regions. This initiative will include social gatherings which will translate into a “unique and delightful communal experience,” the company informed.

The first such event will take place in Athens, Greece in September at the Stanley Hotel’s Cloud 9 Roof Bar. It’s the right venue to start, as the company was recently awarded the Best Game Provider of the Year distinction.

Playson CCO Tamas Kusztos welcomed the opportunity and said that the company was determined to do something that demonstrates its appreciation for the strong performance driven over the year.

Our network of partner operators has grown exponentially, and we really appreciate their hard work and support. Playson CCO Tamas Kusztos

Kusztos spoke about the first event, arguing that it will allow the company to celebrate the achievements of the past month.

Networking to Celebrate Company’s Successes

Playson senior account manager Christos Zoulianitis was also excited about the upcoming event and said that he was appreciative of the company’s delightful group of partners. He explained:

Therefore, we’ve launched this exciting initiative to thank them for their unrivaled loyalty, giving them an entertaining and social evening. We’re looking forward to welcoming all of our special guests, in what will be a fantastic and pleasant evening. Playson senior account manager Christos Zoulianitis

More “Playson & FRIENDS” events will be coming next, with the company looking to announce more get-togethers over the coming months that will celebrate the company’s success and its partnerships with established operators.
