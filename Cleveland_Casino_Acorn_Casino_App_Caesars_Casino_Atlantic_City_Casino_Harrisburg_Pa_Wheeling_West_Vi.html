Liverpool Still Dreaming of the Title

Twenty-five of the 30 Premier League seasons saw the winner finish with 92 points or more. Unfortunately for Liverpool, finishing with 92 points might not be enough to clinch the 2022 title. It’s because everything is in the hands of Manchester City.

This, however, doesn’t mean the Merseyside Reds aren’t going to try. They’re going to go for a win on Sunday, hoping that it’s going to be enough to leapfrog Man City to the top of the EPL table.

So, can they beat Wolves? Sure they can – they did it in each of their previous six duels.

Still, it probably isn’t going to be an easy game as the visitors are pretty good in the defense.

Wolves to Defend With All They Got?

Liverpool vs Wolves Prediction
