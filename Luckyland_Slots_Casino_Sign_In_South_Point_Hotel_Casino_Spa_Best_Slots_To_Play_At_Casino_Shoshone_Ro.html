Seattle Seahawks Still Believing in Their Playoff Future

The Seattle Seahawks are 6-5 in 2023. This is kind of surprising as their 2023 roster did not appear capable of challenging for a spot in the postseason before the start of the season. They’ve got a chance to grab a wild card ticket to the playoffs, but for that, they need to up their form.

The Seahawks are 1-3 in their last four games, which could be a sign that the initial enthusiasm about Pete Carroll’s team is going to deflate. This isn’t just because of their recent run, but also for the fact that their fixture list looks very tough.

After their game with the Cowboys, they also have to play with the 49ers, Eagles, and a few other teams that seem capable of beating them. To make things even worse, the Cowboys also have a tough schedule ahead. What this means is that they’ll probably want to grab an easy win before things get rough.

Dallas Cowboys Want to Keep Their Good Run Going

The Cowboys are 8-3 overall with three wins in a row, which saw them score a total of 127 points or 42.33 points per game on average.
