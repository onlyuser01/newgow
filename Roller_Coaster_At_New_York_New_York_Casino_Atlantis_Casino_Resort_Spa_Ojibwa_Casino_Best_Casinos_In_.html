Al Fateh Looking for Its First Home Win Over Al Nassr Since 2016

Al Fateh is 1-1-0 in the 2023 Saudi Professional League, while last season, we saw this team finish sixth with a 13-4-13 record. What’s interesting is that only six of those 13 wins happened on this team’s turf.

Speaking of home turf, Al Fateh has won only one game against Al Nassr at Al-Hasa in history. It happened in 2016, and they’re winless at home against this opponent in seven games.

Al Fateh has played one home game in the new season. It happened on August 13 when we saw them draw 1-1 with Al Taawon. Moroccan winger Mourad Batna was brilliant in that game. Actually, he’s been Al Fateh’s best player so far, along with Feras Al Brikan, who was this team’s scoring leader in the previous season.

Al Nassr is Great Offensively, Poor in the Defense

With players like Cristiano Ronaldo, Sadio Mane, and Talisca in the front three, Al Nassr looks capable of scoring against any opponent in the 20232 Saudi Professional League. Plus, this season, this team also has Marcelo Brozovic in the middle of the field, as well as Otavio who’s yet to make his debut after coming from Porto a few days ago.

That said, Al Nassr’s fans have nothing to worry about their boys doing well offensively, but what about their defense? In one word, it’s horrible. The defense is Al Nassr’s Achilles heel in 2023, as evident that they’ve already conceded four goals after two rounds.

The worst thing is that their opponents in those two games were Al Ettifaq and Al Taawon, two teams that aren’t famous for being great offensive powerhouses.

Al Fateh vs. Al Nassr Prediction

Al Nassr will win this game, we have no doubt about that. A third straight loss in the 2023 Saudi Professional League would mean that there’s something seriously wrong with this team. But we don’t think it is. Al Nassr’s defense is leaky for sure, but its offense is good enough to make up for it.
