The latest deal provides a boost for Octavian Lab's aggregation platform while at the same time expands Gaming Corps' reach in Italy

The fast-growing developer of games for the online gambling sector, Gaming Corps, unveiled details regarding a new deal on Monday, revealing that it will continue to expand its presence in Europe. The Swedish games developer confirmed that it joined forces with the leading software house and service provider for the iGaming and betting vertical, Octavian Lab.

Under the terms of the new deal, the company will benefit from Gaming Corps’ unique and engaging content. Per the collaboration, the developer’s content will be integrated into Octavian Lab’s aggregator platform, allowing it to expand within the regulated gambling market in Italy, one of the most important markets on the Old Continent.

As a result, players in Italy will be able to benefit from Gaming Corps’ content portfolio, including Crash, Mine, Table, Slot as well as premium Plinko games. The latest content deal will help further expand Octavian Lab’s aggregator platform, delivering thrilling titles such as Penalty Champion, 777 Jackpot Diamond Hold and Win, Coin Miner and Wild Woof, among other popular fan-favorite games.

At the same time, the latest deal helps further extend the reach of Gaming Corps in an important European market. Thanks to the collaboration, new players in Italy are going to be able to reach Gaming Corps’ titles that stand out with a customer-centric approach and rewarding features.

The Two Companies Remain Committed to Growth

Emanuele Nocentelli, Octavian Lab’s CEO, explained that the company continues to grow and reach new players in the process. He said that this is why it is incredibly important for the company to join forces with reliable and trusted providers of iGaming content, just like Gaming Corps. “I look forward to working with them and I am very pleased our platform will host Gaming Corps’ innovative content,” said Nocentelli in conclusion.

“We are reaching new customers and increasing our footprint like never before, so it’s very important for us to partner with premium games developers like Gaming Corps.“ Emanuele Nocentelli, CEO at Octavian Lab

Currently, Octavian Lab is famous as a leading service and software provider for the gaming and betting vertical within the gambling market in Italy. Besides its aggregation platform, the company is also focused on the development and growth of Accounting Gaming Platform – AGP, its leading proprietary software.

“We’re in a highly active growth phase with feedback on our content being heavily weighted on the positive side.“ Danielle Calafato, CCO at Gaming Corps

Danielle Calafato, Gaming Corps’ CCO, spoke about the company’s ongoing growth and explained that its content continues to reach new customers. According to her, the new collaboration with Octavian Lab complements this goal and allows the company’s games to reach new customers. Finally, Calafato deemed Octavian Lab as the perfect partner for Gaming Corps in Italy, given its strong reputation and experience in content distribution.
