Hacksaw Gaming said that its debut in the Colombian gaming market represents a major milestone for the fast-growing supplier

Hacksaw Gaming, a premium supplier of slots, scratch cards and instant win games to the online gaming industry, has penetrated the Colombian gaming market thanks to a new partnership agreement with Betplay.

The new agreement will see Hacksaw Gaming provide a selection of its best casino games to the operator. As a result, Betplay will secure access to some of the supplier’s most acclaimed hits. This notably includes the provider’s flagship title, Wanted Dead or a Wild, as well as a number of chart-toppers such as Chaos Crew and Stack’em.

Hacksaw Gaming said that its debut in the Colombian gaming market represents a major milestone for the fast-growing supplier. It follows Hacksaw’s recent launch in Lithuania and will further solidify the company’s international presence.

Earlier this month, the company also expanded its presence in Italy through a content agreement with NetBet, expanding the company’s footprint in Europe.

Hacksaw Gaming Is Thrilled to Enter a Major LATAM Market

Marcus Cordes, Hacksaw Gaming’s chief executive officer, commented on the new agreement, expressing his team’s excitement about the tie-up. He said that the supplier is looking forward to working with Betplay in Colombia, which he lauded as “one of the biggest markets in South America.”

Our award-winning content has proven hugely popular in South America and we have no doubt our games will resonate with Colombia’s discerning casino audience thanks to this deal with Betplay. Marcus Cordes, CEO, Hacksaw Gaming

According to Cordes, the new deal is the result of months of hard behind-the-scenes work.
