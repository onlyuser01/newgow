The global gaming brand FBM revealed its plans to celebrate fall 2023 with two exciting promotions. As announced by the company, its Filipino audiences will be able to enjoy the Scratch n’ Boom and Octoberific Prize Fest promotions.

The former promotion will continue to provide the company’s players with more chances to win prizes on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Thursdays. In addition to that, audiences can also play for one of the exclusive extra prizes, which include monetary rewards, smartphones, iPads and brand-new motorcycles.

The promotion is part of the Octoberific Prize Fest promotion, allowing consumers to win while playing on an FBM machine with the Green meter during Champion’s Hour from 2:00 pm to 7:00 pm. Once players activate the Green meter, they will be presented with a scratch card. If players match 3 same logos, they can claim a prize.

The distribution will last until the scratch cards last. Players can turn to site staff or FBM representatives if they have any questions.

About Octoberific Prize Fest

In the meantime, players can also enjoy the Octoberific Prize Fest promo. The promotion provides FBM players with a shot at a Suzuki Skydrive Sport Motorcycle. One lucky champion at each participating bingo hall venue will grab such a vehicle.
