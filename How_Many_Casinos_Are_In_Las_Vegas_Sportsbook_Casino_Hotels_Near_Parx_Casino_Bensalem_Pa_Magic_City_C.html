OddsMatrix Sports Data Feed, an EveryMatrix-powered sports betting solution, has formed a new partnership with Kiron Interactive, a leading virtual sports specialist. Kiron Powers OddsMatrix with Virtual Sports Content

OddsMatrix is EveryMatrix’s odds and data subsidiary, known for delivering coverage for over 100,000+ live events at peak. The odds specialist covers all major sports and esports events providing cutting-edge odds for betting companies. Following the agreement with the software expert Kiron Interactive, OddsMatrix will also be able to enhance its offering with coverage for more than ten virtual sports.

Kiron Interactive is known for developing and supplying top-quality virtual sports and numbers games. The company has an impressive suite of best-in-class virtual games, such as Basketball Final Hoops, Dog Racing Platinum Hounds, Football Goal, Harness Racing Charging Chariots, Horse Racing Dashing Derby, Hockey WHL, Steeple Chase Jumps, Stock Car Racing Maxcar and Table Tennis Pro Pong.

As a company seeking to provide sportsbooks with everything they might need to engage their fans, OddsMatrix will greatly benefit from this agreement. As a result of the new agreement, the EveryMatrix subsidiary will be able to expand its portfolio and provide its partners with data for virtual sports events, helping them improve profitability.

OddsMatrix Is Happy to Boost Its Portfolio

Kiron Interactive’s co-chief executive, Steven Spartinos, commented on the new agreement. He said that his team is glad to be teaming up with a trusted and valued brand such as OddsMatrix. He praised the supplier, adding that its popularity will put Kiron’s content at the forefront of the industry. Spartinos concluded that his team will continue providing the gambling sector with the best virtual sports experiences and, to that end, will continue seeking reliable partners like OddsMatrix.

He noted that virtual sports have exploded in popularity in recent years, making Kiron’s virtual sports content a great addition to OddsMatrix’s sports data offering.
