Kambi Group plc has entered into a strategic partnership to provide its retail and online sportsbook solutions to Affinity Interactive, an omni-channel gaming and betting industry leader. As a result, AI will now benefit from Kambi’s products and power its own retail, digital, online sports betting and DFS initiatives, all of which are in the pipeline, an official press release stated.

AI stands to benefit from an array of Kambi’s proven software and tools, including but not limited to best-in-class betting solutions, compliance, and odds tools, as well as customer intelligence and risk management, which are increasingly important aspects of the sports betting experience in highly regulated markets such as the United States.

Breaking Ground in Regulated US Markets Together

The partnership between the company specifically focuses on regulated states, making it a multi-pronged approach to the evolving US betting market. Kambi CEO Kristian Nylén said that he was thrilled to be able to provide Kambi’s software to the talented team at Affinity Interactive:

“This agreement is an exciting opportunity to showcase the strength of our product and leverage our experience to succeed in the competitive sports betting market. AI Fully Prepared for the Digital Age
