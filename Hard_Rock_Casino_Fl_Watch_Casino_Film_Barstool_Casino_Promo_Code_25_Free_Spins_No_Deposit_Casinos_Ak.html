Octoplay, an innovative up-and-coming content creator for the iGaming sector founded in 2022, has achieved a major milestone by deploying its cutting-edge titles with Flutter Entertainment-owned companies Paddy Power and Betfair.

This is the first time that players in the United Kingdom will have access to Octoplay’s titles, which include a special category of titles known as Smash Games.

"They are an impressive game developer led by a talented, experienced management team and with a portfolio of slots that players want to play," Kerr explained. "Paddy Power and Betfair are exactly the calibre of operators that we want to provide our games to, and these partnerships are a testament to the incredible work that the team puts into each game that leaves our production line," Ejlertsson added.
