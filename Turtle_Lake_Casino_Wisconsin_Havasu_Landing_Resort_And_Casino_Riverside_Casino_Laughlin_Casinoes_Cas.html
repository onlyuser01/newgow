The addition of content from CT Interactive will support MD shop’s goal of solidifying itself as a leading gambling company in Bosnia and Herzegovina

CT Interactive, a provider of online gambling content and solutions, has unveiled a new partnership with MD shop, a gaming firm based in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Under the deal, the former company will provide its new ally with games.

Thanks to the partnership, players in Bosnia and Herzegovina will get to experience some of the best games CT Interactive has to offer. This includes hits such as Aztec Gold 20, Champagne and Fruits, Fire Egg, Hit the Hot, 20 Mega Slot, Lizard Blizzard, Purple Fruits, Shining Treasures and Coffee Magic, among others.

MD shop’s visitors will also get to experience Win Storm – a spectacular 5×3 slot that won the Online Game of the Year Award at the BEGE Awards 2022.

CT Interactive’s products have been highly sought-after as attested by multiple recent deals with various operators. In mid-August, the company penned a partnership arrangement with NetBet Italy, agreeing to supply the operator with games.

Before that, CT Interactive also signed a Switzerland-facing deal with Jackpot.ch, rolling out its content through Gamanza. Several days earlier, the fast-growing supplier also formed a monumental partnership with Sazka-Allwyn International.

The Deal Will Help MD Shop Cement Itself as a Local Powerhouse

Representatives expressed their thoughts on the new deal. Filipović Sretan, MD shop’s head of operations, said that his company seeks to be among the best gambling companies in the country. The addition of content from CT Interactive will support MD shop’s goal of solidifying itself as a leading gambling company in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Their [CT Interactive’s] award-winning products are just what we need to fulfill our expansion plans. Filipović Sretan, head of operations, MD shop

Meanwhile, CT Interactive’s head of business development, Milena Tsankarska, said that her team is excited to add content to MD shop’s platform. She said that her team trusts MD shop’s potential and will continue to provide it with best-in-class products.

CT Interactive is proud to announce that its content will become available on the platform of MD shop, Bosnia and Herzegovina. We see a lot of potential in the brand, and we will continue to follow its development and provide it with our latest games. Milena Tsankarska, head of business development, CT Interactive
