Gameplay

When you load up Zeus vs Hades: Gods of War, you will be presented with the choice of which game mode to play. This will only impact the volatility of the game, with the base game remaining the same regardless of which route you take.

The slot uses a 5x5 grid with 15 paylines that are active on each spin. Winning combinations are formed across paylines on consecutive reels starting from the leftmost reel.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Like the base game, the bonus features are not impacted by which game mode you choose to play in.

The first feature you will probably encounter is the Expanding Wilds. Whenever a Wild lands, it will expand to fill the entire reel. A quick battle between Zeus and Hades is played out, but the outcome is irrelevant. It will then add a multiplier of between x2 and x100 to the Wild. This will then be applied to any wins it is a part of.

The other bonus is the Zeus vs Hades: Gods of War Free Spins round. You will need to land three Scatters to trigger 10 spins. During the round, any Expanding Wilds that land will become sticky and stay in place till the end of the bonus.

Depending on your location, you may also have access to a Bonus Buy feature that allows you to buy your way into the Free Spins.
