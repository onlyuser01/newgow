Wayne Gretsky to Tell BetMGM’s Story

BetMGM has secured a multi-year deal with hockey icon and former NHL legend, Wayne Gretzky, making him its official brand ambassador. The four-time Stanley Cup champion will join BetMGM’s hockey and marketing efforts to promote the company on the North American Market.

BetMGM chief revenue officer, Matt Prevost, said: “Wayne is an exceptional talent who transcends the sports world, and we’re proud to welcome him to the BetMGM team.”

Gretzky stated that BetMGM is a great company, which stands at the forefront of the dynamic changes in the sports industry. He said that he is excited to become a part of the company’s plans for expansion into new uncharted territories.

Prevost said that the company is looking for a potential expansion in Canada and across the US. Gretzky will participate in that venture, incorporating his unique ability to tell BetMGM’s story.

Gretzky The Great One

With 61 unique NHL records, Hall of Famer Wayne Gretzky, known as “The Great One,” is highly regarded as one of the best hockey players of all time. No one in the history of the NHL has accomplished more than him.

The Canadian superstar is the all-time leading champion in goals and assists with 2,857 points in 21 seasons, with the New York Rangers, Edmonton, Los Angeles, and St. Louis. He won 9 Hart trophies and is considered NHL’s Most Valuable Player.
