FanDuel upped the stakes as the sportsbook is set to host its inaugural Super Bowl live stream. “Watch & Win” Will Be Streamed on the YouTube Channel of McAfee

The event will be hosted by Pat McAfee and Lisa Kerney from Los Angeles’ Magic Box and is scheduled to take place the night before the Cincinnati Bengals and Los Angeles Rams clash at Super Bowl LVI. The hosts will be joined by quite a lot of celebrities.

Some of them include Emmitt Smith, Anthony Anderson, Wiz Khalifa, Joe Montana, Brian Urlacher, Ludacris, Steve Smith Sr, Brian Urlacher, Ed Reed, Terrell Owens and Michael Irvin. Ludacris and Wiz Khalifa are set to perform live during the event.

FanDuel reminded those who wish to attend the event in person that they need to be over 21 years of age and have proof of vaccination or a negative COVID-19 test not older than 48 hours before attending the show. Those that cannot attend the event will be able to stream it via McAfee’s official YouTube channel.

All of the aforementioned guests will participate in numerous tail-gate games such as darts, mini-golf, and axe-throwing while guests will be able to wager on the winners via FanDuel Fantasy and FanDuel Sportsbook.

In a statement, FanDuel said that as an official sportsbook partner of the NFL, it will provide sports fans with quite a unique experience with the Watch & Win event.
