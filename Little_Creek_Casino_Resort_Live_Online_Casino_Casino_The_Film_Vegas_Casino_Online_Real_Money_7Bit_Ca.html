Tampa Bay Buccaneers Just Hoping to Avoid Embarrassment

Obviously, there’s something wrong with their offense when they failed to score more than 20 points in total in their last two home games. Actually, their season average is 17.2, which is one of the worst numbers in the league. In comparison, the Bills are averaging 28.3 points per game.
