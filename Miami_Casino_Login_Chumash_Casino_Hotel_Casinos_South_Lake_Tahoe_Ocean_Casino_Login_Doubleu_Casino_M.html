Free Spins and Sticky Wild Symbols

The game hosts two special symbols: the wild symbol and the scatter. Both can only be seen on the second, third, fourth, and fifth reels. The wild is represented by a red and gold marquis with the word WILD written on it. Some wilds also feature stars and other symbols above the word WILD, which turns them into sticky wilds.

When a pair of stars show up on top of the wild, the wild will stay there for the following two spins. When just one star is seen on top of the wild, players are only given one sticky wild spin. Wilds are important as they replace the rest of the symbols with the exception of the scatters, even though there are some wilds that feature scatters on top which allows them to successfully replace the scatters.

The scatter symbols are represented by the image of a classic slot machine with three red 7s.

Landing a trio consisting of red seven machine symbols means collecting 10 free spins. Four scatter symbols reward players with ten free spins with multiplier wilds that can go as high as 3x.

While engaged in the free spins round, all wilds will continue to be sticky for the entirety of the feature.

Randomized Bonus Features

The game also comes with two bonus features that can be randomly activated after a spin that has not generated a win.

Wild Boost

Here, players get additional wild symbols for their upcoming spin once they see a gold frame showing around the edge of the reels to prove the feature is activated. While engaged in the base game, players can still be rewarded two or three wilds during a spin. Once the Wild Boost gesture is engaged, players receive five, six, or seven wilds simultaneously.

Stick and Re-spin

When this feature is engaged, it randomly selects a special symbol to turn it into a sticky symbol until the feature ends. Next, the reels are spun again until players earn a payout with the help of the respective special symbols. Big payouts are possible since sticky symbols can be seen lining up on the pay lines.
