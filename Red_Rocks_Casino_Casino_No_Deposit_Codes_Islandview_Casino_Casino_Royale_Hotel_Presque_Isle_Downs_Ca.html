LV Raiders Inked a Partnership Agreement with Aristocrat Technologies

The Las Vegas Raiders announced a new partnership with the global games leader Aristocrat Technologies. Under the new partnership, Aristocrat Technologies has becomes an Official Partner of the Raiders and Allegiant Stadium.

The Raiders home arena, Allegiant Stadium, will display year-round signage by Aristocrat. Furthermore, the operator will benefit from in-game and in-stadium branding.

As a part of the newly inked deal, the Owners East Club at Allegiant Stadium will be renamed to Aristocrat Club. The location will offer an ultra-exclusive experience with a full-service premium bar.

Sports fans are also going to enjoy a luxury lounge as well as upgraded stadium seats for game viewing. But that’s not all. The new deal also makes Aristocrat a presenting sponsor of the Raiders 50/50 Raffle.

With this initiative, Aristocrat and the Raiders are joining forces in giving back to the community.

An Exciting New Opportunity

In a statement, Hector Fernandez, president of Aristocrat Americas said: “We are thrilled to welcome the Raiders to Las Vegas and to score an exclusive casino gaming supplier partnership with the team.”
