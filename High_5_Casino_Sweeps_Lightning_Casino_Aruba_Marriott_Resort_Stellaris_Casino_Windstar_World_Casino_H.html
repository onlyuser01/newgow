Hacksaw Gaming was just provided a transactional waiver to operate in the state of New Jersey. Its entry into its second US jurisdiction was made possible by its partnership with the American gambling giant DraftKings.

Under the deal with DraftKings, Hacksaw Gaming will inject ten of its best games into the operator’s platform. DraftKings will be the exclusive distributor of these games in the state for the first two weeks of Hacksaw’s operation in the Garden State.

Hacksaw’s launch in New Jersey is expected to go live by the end of the year.

The agreement with DraftKings is a cornerstone of Hacksaw’s growth in the United States as the latter company remains bullish on growing its footprint in the highly lucrative North American region.

The opportunity to exclusively offer some of Hacksaw’s best games, on the other hand, will channel players toward DraftKings’ platform, helping it to solidify itself as a leading American operator.

Fans will be able to try out some of Hacksaw’s most exciting titles and see what the fuss is all about. Slots enthusiasts will get to try out hits such as Chaos Crew, OmNom, Mines Dare2Win and Boxes Dare2Win, among others.
