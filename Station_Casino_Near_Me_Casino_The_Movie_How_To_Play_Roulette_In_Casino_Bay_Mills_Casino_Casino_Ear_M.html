MGA Games is taking its portfolio of iGaming titles a step further with a stronger focus on the African market.

The new partnership formed with Goat Interactive, and its gaming brand Premier Bet, will target the region and help local players experience MGA Games’ unique products, tailored to audiences of all backgrounds.

MGA Games Begins Expansion Throughout Africa

The agreement, says the company, is just the beginning of a bigger expansion on the continent and one which will bring strong dividends for all participants and stakeholders.

Africa has long been underappreciated as a continent where sports betting and iGaming are actually long-overdue for the arrival of major brands and products. 888 Holdings and Betway have been among some of the companies to move in quickly.

Suppliers are also following suit with the arrival of MGA Games’ distinguishable products, such as GT World Challenge and Lucky Dragon at first. More games will be added as the partnership matures, though, the company explained in a press release.

Premier Bet players will further have the opportunity to explore games such as Buffalo Bingo, Sweet Home, Bingo Sea, Magician, Roma Bingo, Calaca Party, and so much more. MGA Games already has a plan about how far it can penetrate the African continent and its nascent iGaming market.

Sales director Javier Lanfranchi noted that the company’s products were already present in a number of jurisdictions across the region, including Cameroon, Congo, DR Congo, Nigeria, Senegal, Tchad, Zambia, and Ghana. He added:
