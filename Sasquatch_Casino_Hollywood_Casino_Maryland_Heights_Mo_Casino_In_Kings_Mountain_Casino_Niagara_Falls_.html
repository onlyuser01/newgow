Wazdan, a provider of innovative casino titles, announced that it will attend this year’s Canadian Gaming Summit, a prestigious gaming industry event.

The summit is scheduled to run from June 13 to 15 at the Metro Toronto Convention Center. It will welcome some of the most esteemed Canadian gaming industry leaders, experts and stakeholders and will highlight the latest developments in the sector.

Wazdan’s participation reflects its commitment to North American markets. It will present the provider with an opportunity to promote its products and showcase its cutting-edge technologies while sharing insights and experience with other industry specialists.

In addition, Wazdan is a premium sponsor of the pre-registration party that will be held prior to the convention on June 13 at the Urban Waterfall Garden Toronto.

Where to Find Wazdan?

Visitors can expect to find Wazdan at stand G142, where they can speak with the company’s team and learn more about its best games. Attendees will be able to discuss all-time favorites such as the company’s popular 9 Coins and Hot Slot Series.

In addition, they can learn more about the Mystery Drop promotional tool, which saw Wazdan secure the Game Feature of the Year 2023 award during the recent CasinoBeats Game Developer Awards.

Notable Wazdan executives will attend the event and will be at the stand to answer any questions participants might have. In addition, they will be prepared to discuss future cooperation with other gaming companies.

The Wazdan team at the event will include the following:

Michal Imiolek – CEO

Andrzej Hyla – CCO

Izabela Slodkowska-Popiel – head of account management NA and LATAM

Marcin Trafialek – account manager

Wazdan will welcome delegates to its stand and will showcase its extensive portfolio of games to existing and potential customers.
