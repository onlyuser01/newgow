The leading business-to-business (B2B) provider of technology, hardware, services and gaming content, Inspired Entertainment, announced a new groundbreaking partnership with the National Basketball Association (NBA). Details regarding the new partnership emerged Monday and see Inspired Entertainment secure rights from the NBA for the development of new Virtual Sports games. The leading gaming content and technology supplier explained that those games will focus on archived footage from the world's premier professional basketball league.

According to a statement released by Inspired Entertainment, its new Virtual Sports products, featuring a distinct NBA theme, will provide sports fans and bettors alike plenty of fast-paced action. Similar to a standard sportsbook, the new Virtual Sports games will offer options for wagers such as money line, over/under total score, and more.

Thanks to the partnership and the secured rights, Inspired Entertainment's products will feature a wide range of NBA imagery. Such will include archived footage of the NBA's 30 team logos, along with NBA Finals, NBA Playoffs and NBA All-Star imagery.
