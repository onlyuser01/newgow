How online craps works

Traditionally, craps was a game where you simply bet on the outcomes of two six-sided dice.

Players take it in turns to roll, and the game is divided into two parts. The first, known as the 'come-out', involves the player (or 'shooter') rolling the dice, the outcome of which determines whether the game continues into a second part, known as 'the point'.

Online craps is just a digital version of the classic game, but there are several variations from the original. Some of these include bonus games and features which involve extra challenges, while others are just a virtual graphic of a player throwing two dice from a cup.

Many versions also feature a classic Craps table that players use in real-life casinos. Like roulette, this has different betting areas for players to place their chips when betting. There's also a long section where the 'shooter' can roll the dice.
