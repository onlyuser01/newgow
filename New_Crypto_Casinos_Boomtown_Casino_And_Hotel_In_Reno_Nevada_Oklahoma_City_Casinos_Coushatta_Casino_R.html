Honey Honey Honey review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around honey and adorable bees.

Set in a charming cartoon forest, this game has symbols dripping with golden honey. Players will collect flowers, honey pots, and zealous fuzzy bees as they try to grab as many sweet rewards as possible.

The atmosphere is sunny and friendly in this enchanted forest. Players will love kicking back and chilling in this colourful world.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The ambient background sounds are soothing with gentle chimes, bird song, and humming bees. Players will feel like they are in a magical forest between spins.

The music that plays during spins, however, is more like a cheesy carnival soundtrack. Players may feel slightly more like they’ve climbed aboard an old-fashioned ferris wheel. It can be avoided by clicking the mute option if players aren’t in the mood.

Large trees arch over the reels and a classic beehive sits in one oozing honey. Flowers sway on the ground and glinting sparkles float through the air.

As players get acquainted with the world they may think of classic Disney cartoons such as The Many Adventures of Winnie the Pooh. The wholesome good cheer is a pleasant diversion.

The symbols glow upon wins, reinforcing the fantasy mood. The Wild Bee symbol is especially cute during wins as his wings flutter and antenna bounce.

Overall, the front end of this game is bright and welcoming.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

The Bonus symbol will only appear on reels 1, 3, and 5. When players land 3 Bonus symbols the Free Spins feature will begin.

At the start of the Free Spins feature, players will have a pair of options:

Sticky Streak Free Spins : Player receives 8 free spins and can enjoy an expanded Wild symbol that remains on reel 3. If players land a winning combination with regular symbols, those winning symbols will remain in place and a respin will begin. Symbols that match the remaining symbols will also remain on the reels and another respin begins. All winning combinations are paid when the respins end.
