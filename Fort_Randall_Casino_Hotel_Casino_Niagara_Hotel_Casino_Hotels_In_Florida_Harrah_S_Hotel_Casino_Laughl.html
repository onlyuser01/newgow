Saudi Soccer Presents Exciting and Novel Content

With DAZN now holding the broadcast rights, UK viewers will enjoy live coverage of all matches in the league, providing them unprecedented access to the action and drama of Saudi Arabian soccer.

While DAZN does not yet possess the rights to the Premier League, the streaming platform hopes to entice viewers with unique content from abroad.
