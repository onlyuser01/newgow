The game introduces players to a series of special features, including the Free Spins Bonus, Aladdin’s Wish, and the Tap feature. Here is all you need to know about them:

Players get free spins once they manage to land a minimum of three lamp symbols anywhere on the reels. During this special feature, players can also see the respin round and the Aladdin’s Wish feature be engaged.

Aladdin’s Wish Feature

To trigger the Aladdin’s Wish bonus feature, players need to come across a minimum of six Lamp or Prize symbols on any location on the grid. Once the bonus round is engaged, the empty spot on the reel where the symbols were found turns into an independent one that awards players with a re-spin.

Right before the start of the respin round, players are allowed to open the lamp symbols and watch the award cash prizes come to them. When all the lamp symbols are opened, the respin round is initiated. Players get an extra respin provided they find an additional prize symbol during this round. The action is repeated until there are no more prize symbols to be collected.
