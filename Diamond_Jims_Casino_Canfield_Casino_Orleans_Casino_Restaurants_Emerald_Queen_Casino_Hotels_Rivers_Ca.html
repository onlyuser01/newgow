Cash Bandits 2: Game Symbols and Payouts

The game’s highest-paying symbol is represented by the angry policeman dressed in a standard blue uniform. Players who come across five cop symbols at a time scoop up 2,000 coins. The bank teller with an alarmed look on her face can reward players with a maximum payout of 1,500 coins for five-of-a-kind. Even the bank has a symbol of its own on the paytable, paying 1,000 coins for five identical symbols.

The safe key and the police badge are the mid-paying symbols that bring players 600 coins for five matching symbols. The burglar alarm, the Wanted poster, and the stack of cash pay 250 coins when landing five symbols. The low-paying symbols are represented by the bags of money, handcuffs, and coffee cup and doughnut combos that pay 100 for five-of-a-kind. There are no classic playing cards as low-paying symbols, which means all symbols are perfectly aligned with the theme.

The game also counts a wild symbol marked through the Cash Bandit 2 icon which pays 5,000 coins for coming across five symbols at a time, as well as a scatter symbol in the form of the bank robber. The latter pays 50 coins for five matching symbols.

Cash Bandits 2 Gameplay

The playing field with 15 symbols on the 5X3 grid lets players freely test out a few combinations of lines and betting range values until they find the best ones for them. Once the game is launched, the standard settings of the bet value are set at $1,00. All 25 available pay lines are engaged and players can easily set the desired line number to a value ranging between 1 and 25. Also, the bet size can go from $0,25 to $25, allowing players to enjoy the flexibility they need for a chance to crack open the five safes.

It is also possible to use Autoplay mode or alter the speed of the game for faster-paced action on the reels. The paytable and the game rules are detailed in the dedicated Help & Rules section which can be accessed by clicking or tapping the right-hand bank pillar.

Cash Bandits 2: Vault Feature & Jackpots

Vault Feature

As soon as the wild symbol is seen landing on a random reel, it will act as a substitute for the rest of the symbols, except for the robber symbol which is the scatter. Matching three scatters will automatically engage the lock-picking mini-game feature. By default, the feature gives players five free spins. However, successfully unlocking a safe will trigger up to 190 free spins along with a multiplier that can go as high as 17x. To open a safe, players must use a random code made of one, two, three, four, or five digits. Players can select five, six, or eight numbers from a keypad counting 10 digits when three to five scatters are triggered. Provided one of the chosen numbers is seen in any of the codes, it will proceed to replace the corresponding placeholders on the vault’s code screens. Players who manage to complete a five-digit combination on the vault are granted 100 free games.

The Vault Feature can be retriggered. Every time this happens, five new free games are added to the current feature while the multiplier is maintained in its position. The free games are played at the bet and lines used during the triggering game.

Progressive Jackpots

Players can trigger the Minor or Major jackpot or both at any given moment during the base game. The wins from the progressive jackpots are automatically added to the rest of the wins. The contribution of the random jackpot is 1.04% of the total Return to Player rate of the game.
