Bon Bomb Luxpots Megaways review

Theme and Storyline

Bon Bomb Luxpots Megaways is themed around a world of candy and dessert.

Players will float into a fantasy realm filled with rainbows, ice cream, and a variety of colorful sweets. As they explore the hillsides of donuts, players will fill their pockets with as many treats as possible.

This slot is a good fit for fans of candy themes, or even fruit machine enthusiasts looking for a new flavor.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The ethereal soundtrack is filled with delicate piano notes that add to the dreamy atmosphere. Players will likely enjoy the tune, but for those in a less whimsical mood, the mute button is at hand.

The reels are filled with edible symbols animated in a vivid, realistic style. Translucent green gummy bears, ice cream, and plenty of brightly colored candy create a plentiful aesthetic.

Beyond the reels, players will see a scene adorned with sweets and desserts, with a rainbow arching above. This fanciful landscape will let players channel their inner child as they imagine roaming through hills of donuts.

The reels themselves are built into the side of a wholesome food wagon. The exact logic of a food wagon in a land of sweets is unclear, but it’s too much fun to question.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Bon Bomb Luxpots Megaways create a festive world imagination.

Gameplay

Bon Bomb Luxpots Megaways uses the Megaways system, varying the number and size of symbols in each reel for each spin. There is a set of six reels for the base game and above them, four additional reels spin independently.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, regardless of their size.

The Cascade feature means that symbols in winning combinations will vanish, making way for new symbols to fall into their place.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Candy Bomb symbol functions as a Scatter symbol and appear on all the reels. If players land 3 or more, a prize is awarded, and if they land 13, the top prize is awarded.

The Candy Bomb symbols are accumulated until the conclusion of all Cascades and then paid out.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land 3, 4, or 6 Scatter symbols. The number of Scatters triggering the feature determines the number of free spins awarded as follows:

4 Scatters = 12 free spins

5 Scatters = 17 free spins

6 Scatters = 22 free spins

During the Free Spins feature, Candy Bomb symbols remain stuck on the reels between spins, providing new Candy Bomb symbols land.

Throughout the Free Spins feature, Candy Bombs may have a multiplier associated with them that can be activated when at least 3 Candy Bomb symbols are present. The multipliers can be x2, x3, x4, or x5.

If multiple Candy Bomb multipliers appear during the same round, they will be added together before being applied.

If players land an additional Free Games Scatter symbol, an extra spin will be awarded.

Players can buy directly into the bonus if they choose by selecting ‘Bonus Buy.’
