The 3rd Annual All American Sports Betting Summit (AASBS) is well on its way with the event taking place in San Diego on June 27-29, 2023. The venue has been chosen specifically to reflect how California is changing, with the organizers describing the state as a "goldmine for sportsbook and gaming companies."

There are also big hopes pinned on the market, which is yet to undergo any significant and meaningful change. Attendees of the upcoming event will have the opportunity to find out just about what these opportunities are at AASBS 2023. The event will start with a discussion of what industry people and consumers may expect in 2023 in terms of "big moves" and developments. The speaker lineup consists of impressive and well-represented stakeholders, including:

A big part of the event will also discuss safe gaming measures which are definitely going to dominate the discourse in the United States before long.
