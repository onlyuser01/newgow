XLMedia’s director of partnership Cody Darwick commented on the new deal, describing it as a chance to tap into Newsweek’s “massive national footprint” in the context of the expanded legalization of sports betting in the country. Darwick called the expansion a “valuable” one for both parties while expressing eagerness to begin the collaboration and start adding their “insightful” sports betting content to Newsweek’s top digital property.

XLMedia’s brands currently operate across multiple fields including gaming, personal finance, and sports. They effectively “reach passionate audiences” while building close between their brands and the interested communities through innovative processes, expert crews, and creative storytelling. The company takes great pride in the way it manages to reach “the right consumers with the right content at the right time”.
