UK-based sports betting giant William Hill announced today its US subsidiary opened up a retail sports book in Illinois, the operator’s first in the state. In partnership with Caesars Entertainment and its Grand Victoria Casino Elgin, the new sports book marked another milestone for William Hill’s strategy for expansion into regulated markets.

William Hill Enters Illinois

The William Hill-branded sports book at the Grand Victoria Casino Elgin opening also expands on the long-term partnership between the sports betting company and the casino operator. Both parties recently opened 6 betting kiosks in Isle Casino Hotel and Lady Luck Casino in Black Hawk, Colorado.

“Grand Victoria Casino is located just outside of Chicago – where some of the most passionate sports fans in the country live. I’m looking forward to seeing the community come together to watch their favorite teams as we welcome back the return of our favorite professional sports.” David Grolman, President, Retail Operations, William Hill

The retail sports book features three full-service ticket windows, out of which only two will be open to comply with physical distance requirements. Besides the service windows, the venue has multiple televisions for prime sports viewing, while seated in the comfort provided by the seating options near the high-top tables. As the health and safety of employees and guests is a top priority for the operator, the newly-opened sports book will follow a thorough protocol for cleaning.

Adjacent to the table games and slot machines in the casino, the William Hill sports book is expected to turn into a multi-stop for local sports fans willing to place a bet, while having a drink from the bar on the casino floor, located just steps away from the sports book.

The opening of the retail sports book is just the first step regarding the sports betting market in Illinois, as William Hill intends on offering mobile sports betting to fans across the state, as soon as it receives the necessary regulatory approval.
