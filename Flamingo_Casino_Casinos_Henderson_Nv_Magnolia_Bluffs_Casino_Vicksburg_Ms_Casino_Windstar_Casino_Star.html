Stardust: Game Symbols and Payouts

As previously mentioned, the game only focuses on 12 symbols that are perfectly aligned with the theme. The all-ways-pay structure of the slot boasting 243 different ways to win and minimum bets of 40 with coin values set at 0.01 are just a few of the main highlights of the game with medium volatility.

To score a win, players need to come across three to five identical on the reels. The combinations pay only when they are seen in adjacent positions from the utmost left reel to the right. Scatter symbols pay in any random position on the grid.

The studio has selected berries, crystals, hove, and toadstools are the low-paying symbols. They pay out between 3x and 75x the line bet. The medium-paying symbols pay between 5x and 100x and they are represented by the ladybugs and the butterflies. To grab wins of 125x, 250x, and 500x, players need at least three glass balls, unicorns, or golden sprite symbols.

The green fairies represent the wild symbols in the game, replacing all the other symbols except for the scatter symbols. They can turn into blue fairies before awarding payouts to players. The blue fairies are also wilds and they substitute the rest of the symbols as well, granting a 4x win when helping form a win.

The game’s scatter is represented by the willow, a symbol that pays up to 125x the total bet for landing five symbols. The same symbols can also unlock a series of bonus games.
