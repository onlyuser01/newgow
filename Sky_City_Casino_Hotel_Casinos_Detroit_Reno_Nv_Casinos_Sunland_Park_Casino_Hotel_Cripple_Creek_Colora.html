FSB’s SSBTs Go to BetXS Stores in Ireland

FSB, a leading sportsbook and iGaming solutions provider, has partnered up with BetXS, an Irish retail betting operator, delivering its next-generation self-service betting terminals (SSBTs) across BetXS stores in the country.

FSB Chief Technical Officer Sam Lawrence made the company’s self-service betting terminals, adding several unique features, which would allow betting operators to control their retail environment. These next-generation terminals have a new back-end management tool, giving operators 100% visibility over their real-time liabilities. The product features a suite of reports to allow operators to gain total control and customization over the front-end, as the multi-layered back-end offers them a chance to differentiate their offer. The terminals include full SIS integration, tutorial and Create Bet videos, and ante-post betting.
