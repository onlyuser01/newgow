Stakelogic Live has strengthened its executive team with the appointment of Dejan Loncar as the company’s new Head of Live Casino. The supplier is looking to actively scale its presence in the vertical and create fresh experiences that are worthwhile and exciting with Loncar now in charge of spearheading the company’s forays in this particular vertical.

Loncar is also a company veteran, which will make his transition to the new role not only effortless but also impactful as he previously worked as Head of Live Operations and is intimately familiar with the division’s ambitions and goals, and its long-term strategy which he himself has helped shape.

In his new role, he will be directly involved in executing this strategy and continue to help Stakelogic Live deliver products that pay attention to detail and build solutions that meet the dynamic nature of the segment. According to Stakelogic’s official press release, Loncar is an individual with a proven track record in the company and someone who has been able to navigate challenges and spur new business and growth opportunities.

The new appointee also brings clear excitement for the vertical he is involved with, making his choice even more worthwhile. He also arrives in his position at a time when Richard Walker, the outgoing incumbent, is set to take on new challenges beyond the company.

Commenting on the recent shake-up, Stakelogic Live CEO Stephan van den Oetelaar praised Loncar’s promotion and his "natural passion" for his role.

"His outstanding leadership and innovative mindset have greatly impacted our live operations," the executive added praising Loncar’s professionalism. It’s therefore that Stakelogic has full confidence that Loncar will lead the live casino offering into the future and take it to new frontiers.

Loncar himself was very pleased with the opportunity to expand his presence within Stakelogic and the Live Casino division and welcomed the chance to strengthen the division by bringing even more excitement and innovation to the segment.

"I am honored by this opportunity and excited to further contribute to Stakelogic Live’s growth. I believe in pushing boundaries and creating experiences that stand out.," Loncar concluded. In the meantime, the company has been building up its list of partners, most recently launching its slot and live content with Coolbet, a prominent iGaming operator.

Bally’s also recently welcomed Stakelogic Live’s products, indicative of the successful outreach the supplier has been able to deliver over the past months.
