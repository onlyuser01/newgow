Available at the Casino Floor and Sports Bar

The We-Ko-Pa Casino is now featuring eight self-service sports betting kiosks that allow players to wager on select sports markets, including pre-match, in-play and futures, utilizing straight bets, parlay cards, teasers and more. Sports bettors can even place additional bets with their winning tickets.

We are now one step closer to offering Arizona sports bettors a great betting experience with the new self-service kiosks at We-Ko-Pa Casino Resort. We look forward to opening the complete Betfred Sportsbook early next year.” Bryan Bennett, COO, Betfred Sports

Reliable Retail Solution

“We’re delighted to have opened these betting kiosks and look forward to working alongside OpenBet and the Fort McDowell Yavapai Nation over the coming months to launch our complete sportsbook at the casino,” Bennett concluded.
