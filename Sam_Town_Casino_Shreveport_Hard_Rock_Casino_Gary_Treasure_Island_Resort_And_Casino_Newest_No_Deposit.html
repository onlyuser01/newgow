100 Zombies review

Theme and Storyline

100 Zombies is set in a horrifying future city, over which the undead revenants of a biological disaster rule. A plucky group of heroes must fight for survival. With your help, they might just make it to dawn.

If zombies are your jam, then this slot might be for you. Just remember, it isn’t just your coins that are on the line, your braaaaaains are too.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The graphics of 100 Zombies are one of its major selling points. The fun cartoonish horrors and comic book style heroes make for an engaging set of characters. Meanwhile, the green smog of the background lends the game some real atmosphere.

Each of the characters are animated when part of a winning combination, each animation emphasising the character and helping sell the player on the world that Endorphina has built.

The 100 Zombies soundtrack is made to echo the electro-Western soundtrack of The Walking Dead, and is augmented with the odd zombie moan or groan. There are a pleasing variety of sounds and sound effects for the win and spins noises so the slot's loops manage to avoid going stale.

Gameplay

100 Zombies is a five reel slot with four rows and a whopping 100 win lines (hence the name).

Gameplay is well paced and very straightforward.

There is a high-low gamble feature that can allow the player to run their wins up and increase the volatility of the game. Unlike most games, where a straightforward double or nothing coin flip is available, the high/low format means that not every draw is a 50-50 prospect. If the dealer’s card is low, the odds are better for the player.

There is a free spin bonus (details below). So, more or less everything you’d expect from a standard issue slot, but not a thing more.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

Scatters appear only on reels 2, 3, and 4. Hitting three Scatters anywhere will trigger the free spin bonus. This comes with 10 free spins, and can be retriggered.

Conclusion
