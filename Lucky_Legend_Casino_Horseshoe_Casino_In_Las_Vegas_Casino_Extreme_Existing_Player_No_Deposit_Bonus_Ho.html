Regal Streak review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed loosely around a classic card deck, with a side dish of fruit machine.

With a baize green background, the setting seems to be a felt cards table. The gold framed reel adds a royal ambiance to the mood.

The concept is not especially original, but players looking for that good old-fashioned casino vibe will be right at home in this game.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is a series of repetitive notes that do not take long to grow tiresome. Many players will likely opt for the mute option. It feels like a missed opportunity to have used a darker party tune that would have upheld the casino mood.

The graphics are straight forward with symbols such as green clover-like clubs and glossy cherries. Upon wins the symbols are framed in glowing gold and a spurt of gold coins appears.

The overall aesthetic is festive, but not particularly cohesive or striking. It feels a little like a few classic slot images were assembled together all in one game.

Gameplay

Regal Streak is a five reel, three row slot.

Players are awarded wins whenever matching symbols land anywhere on the same win line.

Players can be awarded larger multipliers for higher paying symbols (see further details below).

The gameplay has a familiar pace and style that players will be comfortable with.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

There is no Wild symbol in this game.

When it appears three times in a win line, the Regal Spins symbol triggers the 15 Free Spins. Each additional Regal Spins symbol triggers 5 more free spins.

A Multiplier Symbol is chosen at random before the free spins begin.

The Multiplier Bar gets closer to the opportunity for a bonus every time the player wins with the Multiplier Symbol.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 94.7%, decisively below our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€10.00. per spin.

The volatility of this game is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We do not recommend this low RTP, high volatility card and fruit-themed slot.

The unoriginal theme and mediocre atmosphere do not make up for this game’s poor strategy stats. Players looking for a captivating front end, or maximized wins will want to keep moving and try a different slot.

Perhaps some players will be craving the truly classic aesthetic of this game, but they’ll be sacrificing their own odds to indulge.
