Swedish casino game provider with a strong focus on creating casual games with lifetime performance Lady Luck Games has released a fresh slot for ComeOn! players. The new game is called Astro Anna and it incorporates the studio’s proprietary Pipe Mechanic. The 5-reel, 5-row slot features a Free Spins bonus round, multiples up to 20x, and the Buy Bonus option. Astro Anna will be available for players in Canada, Norway, and Sweden.

Explore Space With Courageous Anna the Astronaut!

Players with a fascination for outer space and space exploration will be delighted to discover Lucky Lady Games’ freshest release. Astro Anna features excellent space-themed graphics with carefully designed symbols representative of a pipe that can bring together between two and four sides of a square it lands on, connecting to a different pipe in nearby squares. When the pipes manage to spin in a full connection to the sides of the grid, players receive a reward. The slot also features random multipliers. When these multipliers are seen landing on the pipes, they automatically boost the win provided they reach the connected pipe.

Up to 50x Sticky Multiplier during Bonus Game

Astro Anna comes with a Free Spins bonus round that can be engaged when four, five, or six prizes are connected in a single round. This brings players 10, 15, or 20 Free Spins. Triggering seven, eight, nine, or ten prizes during a single spin also attracts a multiplier of up to 20x. The sticky multiplier is added on top of the original multiplier seen on the counter when accessing the Free Spins Round. The multiplier can go as high as 50x during the bonus game.
