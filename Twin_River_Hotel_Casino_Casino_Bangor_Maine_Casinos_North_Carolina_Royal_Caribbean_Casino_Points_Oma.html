Bonuses for New and Existing William Hill Customers

Further to the announcement, William Hill launched a new promotion “Watch the Fights on Us“. The promotion will be applicable for both new and existing customers of William Hill Nevada Mobile Sports.

Any new clients that sign up on Friday and Saturday will receive a bonus of $65 provided that they have placed at least $100 in bets on UFC 249. Returning customers of William Hill will also receive a $65 bonus if they wager at least $249 on UFC 249.

The fans that are not yet registered can download the William Hill Nevada Mobile Sports app from the official web site. Upon registering, it is required to provide a photo of a valid ID which is issued by the State of Nevada. Users complete the registration by taking a “selfie” via the app upon arriving at one of the drive-through sites.
