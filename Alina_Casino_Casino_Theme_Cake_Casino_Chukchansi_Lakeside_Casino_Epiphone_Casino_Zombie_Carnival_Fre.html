Zombie Carnival review

Theme and Storyline

Zombie Carnival is themed around a ghoulish troupe of entertainers.

Players will step into a colorful carnival filled with lights, candy, and rides. However, things are not quite as they seem in this fantasy world, and soon the undead are running the show.

Fans of horror will appreciate the macabre vibe of this slot, and those looking for a cheerful atmosphere will not be disappointed either.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The eerie soundtrack is filled with haunting bird calls and tension-inducing notes. Players will feel immediately drawn into the world of this creepy carnival.

The whimsically unsettling animation may remind players of Tim Burton films. The carnies are all zombies with bulging red eyes and devious smiles. A green-haired clown is especially striking as he nearly bursts out of his reel.

Letters also appear among the symbols in shades of blue, green, and red. Each letter nods to a carnival game with balls, mallets, and canons.

Beyond the reels, players will see a brightly-hued carnival scene with striped tents and fireworks exploding above. It’s fun to imagine how shocked the fair-goers would be to enter this contradictory realm of death and fun.

Together, the soundtrack and graphics create a creative combination of dark and lighthearted aesthetics.

Gameplay

Zombie Carnival is a six-reel, four-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel and going towards the rightmost.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Scatter symbol appears on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5. When players land 2 or more Scatter symbols, the Free Spins feature begins, starting with 6 free spins. Special reels are used for this feature.

When the Free Spins feature begins, the Scatters that triggered the feature transform into Wilds and remain on the reels for the duration of the feature.

Whenever additional Scatters land during the feature, players will be awarded an additional free spin. The Scatters will then be engulfed by the Wilds and contribute to the Wilds’ multiplier.

If more than one Wild is included in a win, their multipliers are added together before being applied to the winnings.

The Wild symbol substitutes for all regular symbols and only appears during the Free Spins feature.

There is also a Mystery symbol which transforms into a randomly selected symbol whenever they appear during the base game and the Free Spins feature.
