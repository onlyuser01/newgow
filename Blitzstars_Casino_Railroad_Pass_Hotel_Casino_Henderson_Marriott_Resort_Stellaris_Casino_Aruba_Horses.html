Digitain, a supplier of iGaming products, has appointed Armine Sirunyan as its new Group COO. Sirunyan is stepping into this position, moving from her current duties as deputy COO and director of analytics for the company. She has eight years under her belt working in Digitain and helping steer the company towards success.

Sirunyan Is a Trusted Veteran and Digitain Asset

As a permanent C-level member of the company, Sirunyan is very well prepared to address the needs of the supplier and help it realize its full business potential. Sirunyan welcomed this new appointment and outlined its significance for herself and what it means for the company as well. She explained:

I’m delighted to take up this exciting new role and to continue my journey with Digitain. We are a renowned firm with a widely acclaimed range of iGaming products and I’m immensely proud to be appointed as their COO. Digitain Group COO Armine Sirunyan

Sirunyan will use her new position to consolidate the company’s success and achievements to date and will continue to work on bringing around innovative solutions within the company so that it can thrive in the next years. Digitain founder Vardges Vardanyan has welcomed the opportunity to work with an accomplished professional like Sirunyan in her new position. He praised her qualities and contributions to the company so far:

Armine has been an exceptional colleague and I’m very pleased to appoint our new Group COO from within the Digitain family. I am sure Armine will continue to help drive our business forward to even greater success. Digitain founder Vardges Vardanyan

Digitain has been pivoting towards the Kazakhstan market where the company is looking to expand further. Other than that, Digitain also brought in Peter Nolan as its new research and development director in mid-2022.
