Evoplay Entertainment Inks Agreement with EGT Digital

The leading game development studio, Evoplay Entertainment announced today that it inked a new agreement with the online gambling services provider EGT Digital. Under the agreement, EGT Digital will integrate into its platform 30 highly popular games by Evoplay Entertainment.

Consequently, the games will go live for partners of EGT Digital in Romania, Bulgaria, and Italy. Furthermore, according to the announcement by Evoplay Entertainment, more game titles will be launched with the platform provider for multiple markets in Latin America.

Evoplay and EGT Share the Same Bold Ambitions

In a statement, Vladimir Malakchi, CBDO at Evoplay Entertainment revealed that both Evoplay and EGT Digital have matching bold ambitions “to supply top operators in Europe and beyond“. Furthermore, he deemed the agreement with EGT Digital as “fantastic“. In conclusion, Malakchi stressed that the two companies share an exciting vision and that Evoplay looks forward to cultivating a successful relationship with its new partner.

Undoubtedly, Evoplay Entertainment is focused on growth since the new partnership comes only a week after the company revealed another key partnership with the platform provider Playzido!. Under the agreement, Evoplay increased its market reach across the UK and MGA markets by providing immersive fan-favorite titles for Playzido clients and partner networks.
