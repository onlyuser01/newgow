Supplier of gaming technology Scientific Games announced today it entered into an exclusive multi-year agreement with games developer Pixiu Gaming to distribute its titles via the OpenGaming ecosystem in North American regulated markets.

Pixiu Offers Diversification Away from Slots

The deal which makes Scientific Games’ content aggregation OpenGaming platform the exclusive distributor of Pixiu games, significantly enhances its offering and helps affirm its growing appeal among lottery operators in North America.

“We are thrilled to be extending our partnership with Scientific Games into the growing U.S. market. With a focus on high quality, intuitive games, often customised with our partners’ logos, Scientific Games is a natural partner for us and we look forward to launching more exciting titles through the year.” Tony Plaskow, Founder, Pixiu Gaming

Developer of bespoke games Pixiu is the creator of a number of player-favorite, market-leading Keno, Baccarat and Reactor games supplied to some of the major lottery clients such as Lotto Quebec, British Columbia Lottery Corporation (BCLC) and Atlantic Lottery Corporation (ALC).

“Our deal with Pixiu reflects the reach and power of the OGS. By continuously adding more quality content from a wide range of providers such as Pixiu, we’re generating strong momentum within the North American market.” Dylan Slaney, SVP Gaming, Digital, Scientific Games
