Despite its hurdles in Curacao, 1xBet continues to collect accolades and grow its global business. The international operator now announced that it has reached the international EGR Operator Awards 2023 final and will compete with its competitors in six categories.

The categories 1xBet has been nominated for include the following:

Sports betting operator

Affiliate program

Crypto operator

Esports operator

Football betting operator

Mobile sports product.

1xBet hopes to achieve victory in one or more of these categories and further solidify itself as a leading global company.

Winners of the event will be determined by an authoritative jury and will be announced on October 26. An awards ceremony will take place at the JW Marriott Grosvenor House London.

The EGR Operator Awards are presented by eGaming Review Magazine, a prominent media outlet that covers the gambling and sports betting industries. Its awards are a sign of prestige and are a firm testament to the quality of a company’s products.

1xBet Hopes to Maintain Its Momentum

A 1xBet representative commented on the matter. They remarked that this is not the first time the company has ranked among the leaders in the betting industry. Boasting previous participations in the EGR Operator Awards, 1xBet hopes to maintain its edge and continue to sit at the forefront of gaming and betting.

It [1xBet’s participation in the finals] is a confirmation of 1xBet consistent progress: it is very pleasant that our success impresses not only customers but also recognized authorities in the industry. 1xBet representative

Participation in the EGR Operator Awards final is not 1xBet’s only achievement in 2023. Earlier this year, the company became a laureate of the SiGMA Awards Americas 2023 and was named Innovator of the Year at the International Gaming Awards.

1xBet Faces Some Trouble

Unfortunately, not everything has been rosy for the company as earlier this year, 1xBet’s co-founder Sergey Karshkov passed away in a Swiss hospital. Despite being described as very healthy, the Russian businessman suffered a devastating allergic reaction that caused his passing.

In addition to that, 1xBet is still dealing with financial trouble in certain regions and legal issues in Russia. The brand is notably banned in the country because of allegations of illegal operations and money laundering.
