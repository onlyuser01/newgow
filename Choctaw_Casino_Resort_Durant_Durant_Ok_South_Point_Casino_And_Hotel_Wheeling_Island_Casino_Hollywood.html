KWCH reported that the opening of the new DraftKings retail sportsbook at the Boot Hill Casino & Resort in Dodge City, Kansas, marks a significant milestone in the state’s sports betting industry.

New Sportsbook at Boot Hill Casino Offers Top-Notch Features for Sports Bettors

The Boot Hill Casino’s new sportsbook is a partnership between DraftKings and the casino, and it offers an array of features that make it a top destination for sports bettors. The facility boasts a 17-foot LED video wall, several high-definition televisions, two cashier stations, four betting kiosks, a sportsbook lounge seating area, and food and beverage options. The sportsbook offers a comfortable and entertaining atmosphere that is certain to attract sports fans with its features for sports betting and viewing their favorite sports games.

The ribbon-cutting ceremony for the new sportsbook took place on March 10, 2023, and was attended by sports fans, industry insiders, and even Kansas City Chiefs player Neil Smith.

Overall, the opening of the new DraftKings retail sportsbook at the Boot Hill Casino & Resort is a positive development for sports betting in Kansas. With its top-notch features and commitment to customer service, the sportsbook is sure to attract a wide range of sports fans and bettors, further solidifying Kansas’ position as a major player in the rapidly growing sports betting industry.

DraftKings and Boot Hill Casino Executives Celebrate Opening of New Retail Sportsbook

Speaking at the event, Diane Giardine, general manager for Boot Hill Casino & Resort, expressed her excitement about the new sportsbook:

We’re thrilled to announce the opening of the new DraftKings retail sportsbook. This is the place to be for sports fans, where you can watch your favorite games while enjoying a comfortable and entertaining atmosphere. We look forward to welcoming sports fans and creating unforgettable experiences for them. Diane Giardine

Michael Kibort, who is the senior director of retail sportsbooks at DraftKings, expressed similar thoughts. He stated that it was a thrilling experience to witness their joint vision of developing DraftKings’ innovative mobile app being brought to fruition by providing an in-person experience at their new DraftKings retail sportsbook situated at Boot Hill Casino & Resort.

He also mentioned that the sportsbook is part of DraftKings’ continuous dedication to delivering high-quality sports betting experiences to customers nationwide.

Chris Reedy, Butler National Corporation COO, also weighed in on the new sportsbook, thanking the Kansas legislators, administration, and regulators for making sports betting possible in the state. He noted that the DraftKings retail sportsbook is poised to promote tourism and travel to Western Kansas, consistent with the company’s commitment to the lottery.
