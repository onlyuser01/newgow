Internet Vikings Appoints Andreas Soll as Commercial Unit Leader

The premium cloud hosting, domain management, and marketing solutions service provider, Internet Vikings announced that it recruited Andreas Soll on Wednesday this week. In his new position, Mr. Soll will be leading the commercial unit of the company’s Domain Management department.

In a statement, Internet Vikings’ CEO, Peter Ekmark, outlined that Mr. Soll will bring both knowledge and skills which the company was missing to scale up its Domain Names Management business area. Furthermore, he stressed that Mr. Soll is the right person that can help the company increase and maintain its growth.

Although 2020 has been a difficult year for many businesses, this is not the case with Internet Vikings. In fact, this year, the company nearly doubled its staff and keeps hiring professionals. The latest move only complements the ongoing momentum which Internet Vikings has gained as the company will continue to attract talented and skillful professionals.

Insight on Andreas’ Professional Background

Mr. Soll has been a part of the domain business since 1999. At his previous job, he was responsible for managing the Corporate Domain Business at Key-Systems GmbH since 2007. By joining Internet Vikings’ Domain Management Department as a Sales Manager, Mr. Soll brings fundamental knowledge in a wide range of IT-solution and communication areas.

“Internet Vikings is well-known in the domain management and services sector.” Andreas Soll

Mr. Soll shared his excitement by saying that Internet Vikings is uniquely positioned on the market with the help of its incredible product portfolio combined with an ecosystem of know-how and technology. In conclusion, Mr. Soll outlined that he believes that the new collaboration will bring a lot of value for the customers.

Rickard Vikstrom, the CCO of Internet Vikings, revealed that the broad customer experience and deep understanding of the product, market trends, and dynamics, makes Mr. Soll a perfect addition to the company.
