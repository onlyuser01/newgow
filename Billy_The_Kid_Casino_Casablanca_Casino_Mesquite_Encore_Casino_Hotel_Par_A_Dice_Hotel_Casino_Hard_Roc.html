The Strategy Organization (TSO), a company that specializes in offering actionable advice to gaming and hospitality firms, is bringing Seth Young as its new managing partner. Young will be an important member of the team owing to his 15 years of related experience within the interactive gaming and technology sectors.

Bringing a Seasoned Veteran on the Job

Commenting on his appointment, Young said he was happy to be joining the team behind one of the most successful industry consultancies. TSO can deliver credible services that translate into results for clients. Young is confident that TSO can deliver on the needs of all clients who work in both the hospitality and gaming markets.

“Most importantly, the group is extremely entrepreneurial, highly collaborative, and has an aligned vision of the future of the market.” TSO managing partner Seth Young
