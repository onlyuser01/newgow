888.ru to Utilize EveryMatrix’s Turnkey Sportsbook Solution

Leading iGaming software provider EveryMatrix entered the Russian regulated gaming market after it launched its turnkey sportsbook solution for the sports bookmaker 888.ru. As a result, 888.ru will utilize the company’s turnkey platform as well as a suite of products including the fully managed OddsMatrix Sportsbook with BonusEngine.

Furthermore, 888.ru will benefit from using EveryMatrix’s MoneyMatrix platform used for payment processing and ID verification. All of those solutions were adapted to fit the gaming regulations in Russia.

The high-tech online bookmaker 888.ru has been operating in Russia since 2017. The company that manages the bookmaker has more than 25 years of experience in the gaming industry. Currently, 888.ru is a strong local brand in the Russian regulated market. Furthermore, according to EveryMatrix, more than 100,000 users were migrated to the new platform as a part of the solution launch.
