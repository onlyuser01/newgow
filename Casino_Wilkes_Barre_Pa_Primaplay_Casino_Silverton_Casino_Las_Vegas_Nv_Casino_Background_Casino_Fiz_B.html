The New Game Brings Respins and Jackpot Features

Online players that trigger the Cash Mesh feature in Burning Slots Cash Mesh, can see “any position containing a coin symbol being held and all other positions spinning individually,” explains BF Games.

This bonus mode starts with three spins initially, but if a new symbol lands, that count is reset. This continues until the respins are finished or all positions have a coin that fills them in. In case a player hits 15 coins during this bonus mode, they win a Full House Multiplier, which may award them an x2 multiplier of the total value of the coin symbols.

What’s more, the players can also benefit from jackpots. If a player lands a Jackpot or Bonus symbol while in Cash Mesh bonus mode, they can be awarded a Mini/Minor Bonus or a Major/Grand Jackpot. To further boost the engagement and thrill, the Grand, as well as Major jackpots, grow progressively with each spin, building up potential for huge payouts.
