Jammin' Jars 2 review

Theme and Storyline

Jammin' Jars 2 is themed around a disco party filled with tropical fruit and jam.

Players will arrive at a neon-lit club and discover an array of tempting produce. As the palm trees sway, they’ll get their groove on with a jar wearing sunglasses.

This whimsical world of jam is a good fit for fans of fruit machine themes and those looking for a lighthearted atmosphere.

Gameplay

Jammin' Jars 2 has eight rows and eight reels. Wins are awarded when players land a minimum of 5 matching symbols that connect horizontally or vertically.

Due to the Cascade feature, symbols that form winning combinations vanish, allowing new symbols to fall into the new positions.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols.

If a Wild is part of a winning combination, it will jump into open, adjacent positions before the Cascade begins. The Wild then remains in place during the Cascade, increasing its chances of becoming part of another winning combination.

Each Wild that is part of a winning cluster adds its current multiplier to the total win, including the Instant Prize wins

The multipliers that accompany Wild symbols increase each time Wilds are part of a winning combination. These multipliers begin at 1x and increase by +1 for each win.

Wild symbols are also Scatter symbols, triggering the Free Spins feature if players land 3 or more Scatters. The Free Spins feature begins with 6 free spins.

When players hit a minimum of 5 Instant Prize symbols in a cluster, all the prizes are added up and awarded to the player.

The Fruit Blast feature is triggered at random and triggers a minimum of one Giant Fruit symbol to appear on the reels (and/or a Giant Instant Prize symbol). The positions covered by the Giant Fruit symbol or symbols are transformed into matching symbols. All symbols covered by the Giant Instant Prize symbol are transformed into Instant Prize symbols.

Win with Wild symbols in the Free Spins feature

The Gold Vinyl symbol is collected by the player and leaves a randomly selected paying symbol in its place. If players fill the Giga Jar (which sits beside the reels) entirely with Gold Vinyl symbols, they may trigger the Giga Jar feature.

When the Giga Jar feature begins, any symbols on the reels are taken away, and Wild symbols are added. The Giga Jar is then added to the reels, creating an Instant Prize symbol block.

The Giga Jar feature starts with 8 spins, and each position spins with a chance to hit an Instant Prize symbol. Further spins can be triggered by landing an Instant Prize symbol.
