According to Frankel, the law has been lenient with private businesses allowing them to figure out the benefits of non-smoking for themselves. Failing that, Frankel is now prepared to enact the necessary legislation:

“If we cannot count on private businesses to do the right thing, even when it’s the financially smart thing to do, then it’s time for political leaders to step in and insist on the protection of the health of our constituents.” Keystone State Rep. Dan Frankel
