Special Slot Features

The game also has special features like Chance x2, Buy Bonus, and Refilling features. For example, during the Refilling feature, a player may come across numerous consecutive wins on each new spin by paying out all matching combinations and eliminating all winning symbols after a spin. The empty cells are then filled with endless symbols dropping from above until no additional winning combinations show up.

Hitting anywhere between four and six golden-haired Medusa scatters on any location on the grid will trigger the Free Spins feature that will pay 10, 20, or 30 free spins. Players who are lucky enough to hit more than three scatters while the Free Spins feature is engaged receive five extra free spins.

During the same Free Spins round, hitting a random Multiplier symbol from x2 to x100 will considerably boost the game’s winning potential. The multipliers will remain in their positions until the end of the refilling sequence. Finally, their values will be summarized in order to multiply the total win.

It is also possible to engage the Free Spins round via the available Buy Bonus feature for a price that is automatically adjusted whenever the player changes the bet. This feature is not active when the Chance x2 feature is triggered.
