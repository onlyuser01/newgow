Gaming solutions provider Yggdrasil announced today it entered into a deal with Finland’s state-owned gaming operator Veikkaus, signing a Game IP Licensing Agreement.

Slot Games for Veikkaus’ VLTs

Under the terms of the deal which relates to the retail segment of Veikkaus, Yggdrasil will work in collaboration with the state-owned gaming company to repurpose its slot portfolio and provide slot games for the retail estate of the operator, which operates under Veikkaus’ proprietary video lottery terminals (VLTs), extending the game content Yggdrasil already supplies for its online channel.

“We are delighted to extend provision of gaming content to Veikkaus in their retail environment. Expansion of partnership into Retail fits perfectly into our own and Veikkaus omnichannel strategy, w[h]ere we effectively can scale business operations, drive improved total player value, and content familiarization.” Bjorn Krantz, Chief of Global Market Operations, Yggdrasil

After the game content supplier provided an early proof of concept, it is expected to supply Veikkas’ retail estate with the first games fully compliant with the requirements of the gaming regulator in Finnland by mid-2021.

“It has been a thorough selection process by Veikkaus, and I am very proud of the Teams involved driving the successful process. We really look forward to a long and mutually beneficial retail relationship with Veikkaus.” Bjorn Krantz, Chief of Global Market Operations, Yggdrasil
