PressEnter and Swintt have entered into a content partnership that will see the former benefit from the supplier’s extensive range of games. PressEnter currently operates a number of prominent iGaming brands, including Nitro Casino, Neon Vegas, and XLbet, all of which will be able to benefit from Swintt’s advanced gaming solutions.

PressEnter’s online casinos will be able to host a variety of Swintt’s outstanding slots, including present and future releases. The games stand out by virtue of their excellent graphics and underpinning mechanics. Among the games that PressEnter will be able to offer its players are titles such as Aloha Spirit XtraLock, Mystic Bear XtraHold, and not least, Book of Shai, which is an upcoming and highly-anticipated release.

PressEnter brands will benefit from dedicated lobbies for some of Swintt’s most popular and best-received games, such as Master of Books Unlimited, Secret Spellbook Respin, and Takutiki, among others.

Swintt Chief Executive Officer David Mann welcomed the opportunity to see his company’s SwinttGames and SwinttPremium releases roll out with PressEnter’s network of partner brands and operators, and noted the benefits that his own company is going to enjoy from the collaboration:

"By teaming up with PressEnter, we’ll be able to introduce our slots to a wide new audience while also helping the company’s sites continue to deliver the outstanding casino content that sets them apart from the competition."

PressEnter Chief Product Officer Hampus Eriksson was similarly pleased with the new content collaboration. Eriksson explained that this was yet another milestone the company had been able to achieve since it launched in 2018. He further hailed Swintt as one of the leading and most in-demand suppliers of iGaming content.

The executive was confident that adding these games to PressEnter’s brands’ lobbies would result in higher engagement and uptake from players and deliver audiences the fresh experiences they deserve.

Swintt has had several successful content tie-ups recently. The company launched its Premium and Select lineups with Jokerstar earlier in March. In February, the company similarly launched with Spins.lv, adding its extensive content to the operator’s library.
