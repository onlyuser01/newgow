The action takes place in the iconic castle of Camelot where players will meet a plethora of knightly figures, royal rules, and wizards. They all represent the main symbols on the board, along with the golden chalice that is representative of the game’s wild symbol.

Thanks to ReelPlay’s 10K WAYS mechanic, all winning combos are taken off the reels, leaving room for fresh sets of icons to tumble down and fill in the empty positions.

The cascading wins allow players to enjoy huge big win possibilities since every new win will set off another chain reaction of potentially new winnings.

As expected, the sword of Excalibur has been given a special makeover, as it is two symbols high and takes the role of the scatter in the game.

Free Spins and Unlimited Win Multipliers

Landing at least three of these symbols will trigger the free spins round. During this round, players can unlock unlimited win multipliers.

While the free spins are active, a spin counter is seen resetting once a new scatter shows up in full anywhere on the grid. At the end of each spin, provided the scatter is fully in view, it will bring players up to 100x the bet which will also be boosted by the win multiplier.

Commenting on the release, Yggdrasil’s chief gaming officer, Mark McGinley, explained the slot “sees the return of ReelPlay’s popular mechanic” while carrying players on a mesmerizing journey through British mythology to a realm where wizards and knights live.

McGinley further commented on the unlimited multiplier and massive cash rewards waiting for players as soon as they hit the play button.

ReelPlay’s chief executive officer, David Johnson, praised the game’s “incredible art and sound” as well as the pioneering use of double-sized symbols in a 10K WAY game.
