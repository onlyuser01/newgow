Photography Feature and Free Spins Bonus Round

The game randomly selects eight up to 10 different positions on the reels and marks them using a special photography frame. This means they are turned into Special Positions on the reels. A minimum of one Special Position will be on at least one reel at any time. Once the spinning is over, all animals occupying the Special Position become Wild symbols. Players are given the opportunity to grab some important wins.

Coming across a minimum of three Scatters triggers the Free Spins bonus round where players are awarded 15 free spins. To get 20 or 25 free spins, players must hit four or five Scatter symbols. Any Scatters showing up during the Free Spins round are turned into Special Positions.

In some countries, Wild Shot players will be allowed to easily buy their access straight into the Free Spins round. The same feature is up for deactivation provided operators make the requirement.
