As per the deal, the supplier will leverage its rich portfolio of iGaming titles to bolster the content offering of the VoodooDreams, NYspins and Duelz brands. As a result, SuprNation’s players will get to experience a rich selection of crash games, table games, slots and Plinko.

Some of the most notable titles that will go live with the three brands include games such as Coin Miner, Jet Lucky 2, Paddy’s Payout and Wild Woof.

SuprNation holds licenses in Sweden, Malta, the United Kingdom and the Isle of Man. By partnering with the operator, Gaming Corps will be able to greatly expand its European footprint and reach more players than ever before.

The agreement follows an aggregation arrangement between Gaming Corps and Infingame. Prior to that, the supplier penned a similar deal with Uplatform.
