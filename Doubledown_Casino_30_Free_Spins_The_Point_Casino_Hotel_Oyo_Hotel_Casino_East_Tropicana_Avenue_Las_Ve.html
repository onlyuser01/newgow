FSB Takes to the Skies with SPRIBE’s Aviator

Among the games available to FSB and its partner operators is Aviator, an acclaimed and highly innovative game that has completely innovated the way players experience iGaming products. Aviator is based on the crash game concept whereby players can decide when to leave a round.

The longer they stick around, the higher the multiplier becomes, but there is also a chance for the animated aircraft in the game to crash, and for the players to lose their original stake and any multipliers thereof.

The crash occurs at a random point, without any particular predetermination, much like the Random Number Generator of a traditional slot game.
