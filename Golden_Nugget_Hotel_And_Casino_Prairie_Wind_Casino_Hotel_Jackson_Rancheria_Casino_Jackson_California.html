BetMGM, one of the biggest sports betting and iGaming operators and brands in the United States, has recapped the year 2023 in jackpots. "The performance of our exclusive, in-house-produced games shows these titles are resonating with players and solidifies BetMGM as the destination for progressive jackpots," Nisbet added.

In total, BetMGM paid out the biggest jackpots in Michigan, even though New Jersey players turned out to be luckier.
