Matt Stafford to Make an MVP Claim?

The Rams’ quarterback is currently the #4 in the list of top MVP favorites. At DraftKings, the odds of him winning the award are +1200.

They might get shorter after Week 7, when he’ll play against his former teammates. If there’s someone who knows their strengths and weaknesses, it’s Stafford.

Even if it wasn’t the case, everyone would still expect to see a lot from the QB. After all, he’s been in really great form in the past few weeks.

Last weekend, for instance, we saw Stafford throwing for 251 yards against the New York Giants. The fact that that was the least number of yards per game he’s made this season shows just how phenomenal he’s been so far.

He didn’t cover as many yards as usual, but he did make plenty of crucial passes in that game. In fact, four of his throws resulted in a touchdown.

Jared Goff Coming Home

Goff’s return to Los Angeles probably isn’t going to be great. The quarterback is having one of the worst seasons of his career, with 1,505 passing yards in six games, seven touchdowns, and four interceptions.

Although he’s not a bad QB, his new teammates just aren’t good enough to provide him with decent support.

Evidence of this is that the Detroit Lions is the only NFL team with a 0-6 record in the 2021 season. What’s worse is that their schedule hasn’t been as bad as one might assume based on their stats.

In fact, their toughest challenge of the season comes this Sunday. The Rams are better than them in all aspects of the game, which is why it’s no surprise the bookies have set the spread to 15 points. Will the Rams cover it? Probably.
