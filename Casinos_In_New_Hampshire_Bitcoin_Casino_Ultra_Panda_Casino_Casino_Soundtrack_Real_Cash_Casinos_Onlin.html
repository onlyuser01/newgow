The Game Sports Many Innovative Mechanics

The Rollover Respins feature will see a Wild Genie landing in a win with five or more of the same symbols trigger respins on non-sticky positions. Depending on the results, new symbols can extend the respins. Thanks to that, players can make better combos and win more money.

Multiple Sticky Symbol Activations can stack up respins, providing many winning opportunities. This feature is available both in the base game and during Free Spins mode.

In addition to that, the Genie’s Magic Multipliers feature will activate a variety of multipliers when certain conditions are met, allowing players to further increase the potential of their bet.

If players want to jump straight to the action, they can use the Buy Bonus feature to purchase free spins. Finally, players that desire more dynamic experiences can activate Turbo Mode to skip repetitive animations.

The new game will be added to Armadillo Studios’ impressive content lineup. The company is famous for its high-quality slots which include titles such as Fairy Fantasy Exotic Wilds, Wild Willy’s Gold Rush, Hot Hot Honey and Mythological Mayhem Supreme Streaks.
