FSB, a global B2B sports betting and iGaming supplier, continues to strengthen its positions in the Latin American market with new offices in Colombia. The company is setting up a technology hub in the country to help it push and expand in the region. FSB is equipping its newly onboarded local technology team with the necessary means to sustain the company’s ambitions in South America and beyond.

FSB Doubles Down on Latin America and Poises for Stronger NA Push

Besides focusing on Latin America, FSB is also going to leverage the Colombia team’s expertise to help with growth in North America. The 30-people team is going to assist with making stronger inroads in markets that are of immediate interest to the company and the new squad will work and coordinate with FSB’s global teams to continue delivering on a strong presence and products that make a difference.

FSB CEO Dave McDowell welcomed the opportunity and said that this is a significant move in expanding the company’s presence in South America and growing its global footprint. He added:

Opening a new hub in the Americas has become essential due to the rapid growth of our North American client portfolio over the last 12 months as well as our strong ambitions in the LATAM region. FSB CEO Dave McDowell

McDowell assured that these new headquarters will give FSB the means to expand both in Latin America and North America as it seeks to secure the strongest partnerships and deliver the best possible products. The company has been making changes to its executive lineup over the past months.

FSB appointed Kenneth Paterson as its new global delivery director in July. Later that month, the company promoted Lisa Waterfield to director of sales for the North American region. Most recently in August, FSB broke ground in South Dakota where the company obtained a supplier license.
