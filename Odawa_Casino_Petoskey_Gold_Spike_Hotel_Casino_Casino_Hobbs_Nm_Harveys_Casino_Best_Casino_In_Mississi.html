Spin Sorceress review

Spin Sorceress is a video slot coming from NextGen Gaming. The game has a modern 243 ways to win design and belongs to the Superbet family of NextGen slots.

Take advantage of this option to the maximum and you can end up with multipliers of up to x50 attached to the Wild symbol. Thanks to this feature all it takes is one great spin to get a big win on the reels of Spin Sorceress.

Theme and Visual Details

As you can already guess from the slot's name the game has a fantasy magic theme. The reels of the game are set high in the mountains with a beautiful snowy landscape in the background.

The designers really put in a lot of work into the visual details of the game, using modern animations and high symbol details. The end result is a visually attractive video slot which will surely catch your attention.

Gameplay

The gameplay is very exciting thanks to the random Sorceress Wild Reels feature. If you are playing at the maximum Superbet level the Sorceress can award Wild symbols on reels 2, 3 and 4 with the maximum x50 multiplier, often resulting in a big payout. You never know when the feature can be triggered, which makes every spin exciting.

Bet size, payouts and RTP

The bet size in Spin Sorceress can range anywhere from $0.25 to $25 per spin, but this bet size is without the Superbet option active. When you play with the Superbet on level 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5, the minimum bet size will increase from $0.25 to $0.50, $1.25, $2, $3 or $5 per spin accordingly.

Game has simple paytable compared to other games

Increasing the Superbet level also affects the overall RTP value of the game. With the Superbet off the RTP is 94.617%, with the Superbet on level 1 it’s 95.084%, on level 2 it’s 95.638%, on level 3 it’s 96.063%, on level 4 it’s 96.582% and on level 5 it’s 97.136%. As you can see the higher the Superbet level, the higher the RTP value of the game actually is.

Bonus features

The Wild symbol in Spin Sorceress can land only on reels 2, 3 and 4. It will substitute for all other symbols in the game except for the Scatter symbol. The first feature you will notice in the game is the Superbet option. Using this feature has a direct impact on the Wild symbol. With the Superbet off the Wild doesn’t have a multiplier attached.

With the Superbet on level 1 the Wild multiplier is x2, x3 or x5, on level 2 it’s x5, x8 or x12, on level 3 it’s x10, x15 or x20, on level 4 it’s x15, x20 or x25 and and finally level 5 offers the Wild multiplier of x20, x30 or x50.

Super Bet's Multipliers

On any given spin the Sorceress Wild Reels feature can be triggered. In this feature the Sorceress is guaranteed to add a Wild symbol to reel 2, 3 or 4. You can also get a Wild symbol on two or even on all 3 reels. The Wild symbols will come with a multiplier in correspondence with the Superbet level.

Players can also trigger the free spins bonus in Spin Sorceress by landing 3 or more Scatters anywhere on the reels. When this happens the player is awarded with 10 free spins, which can be re-triggered by landing another trio of scatters on the screen. Each of these bonus spins has guaranteed at least one Wild to land on any of the reels.
