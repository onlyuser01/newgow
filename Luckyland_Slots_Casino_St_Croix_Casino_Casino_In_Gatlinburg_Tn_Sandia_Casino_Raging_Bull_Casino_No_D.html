Enter the Fantasy Realm and Let the Magical Creatures Decide Your Fate

Luck & Magic invites players to set foot into a fascinating fantasy realm and let their fate be decided by magical creatures based on some of the most popular characters and themes in iconic video games.

The game features a one-of-a-kind face-down card system used to replace standard reels present in most slots. Once players hit the Spin button, all cards turn face-up to uncover all the symbols and values underneath them. This means players no longer have to worry about checking the game’s paytable.

The innovative feature is accompanied by a Bonus game based on Bonus symbols which add even more mystery to the action on the grid. The wise wizard represents the Bonus symbol and is seen watching over the reels.

Landing at least three Bonus symbols on the first, second, or third reels will initiate the Bonus Game. There, players are asked to select one of five available cards that, in return, receive a multiplier won prior to the rest of the cards starting to flip to reveal their values.

Players can also opt for the Bonus Buy feature and instantly enter the Bonus game at any random moment.
