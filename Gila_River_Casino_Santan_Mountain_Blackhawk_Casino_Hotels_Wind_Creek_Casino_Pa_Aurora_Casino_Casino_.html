"Bầu – cua – tôm – cá " ( also known as "gourd-crab-shrimp-fish") is a Vietnamese gambling game using three dice.

Bầu Cua Tôm Cá is essentially the Vietnamese variation of Hoo Hey How (Fish-Prawn-Crab) played in China, the dice game Crown and Anchor played by British sailors, or chuck-a-luck played in America. Gour – crab – shrimp – fish is often played at Tết (Vietnamese New Year).
