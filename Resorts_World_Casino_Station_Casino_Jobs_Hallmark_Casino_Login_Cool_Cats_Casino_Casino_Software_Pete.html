The appointments come at a time when the gaming studio continues to grow and prepares to further expand the reach of its content to existing and emerging markets

The fast-growing iGaming content developer delivering engaging games that stand out with high quality and creativity, Peter & Sons, announced two strategic hires. On Tuesday, the company confirmed it hired Christina Mirnaia and Maor Nutkevitch to help lead the expansion of the game studio. The appointments come after the studio secured a major investment from SkillOnNet, the famous global entertainment brand.

Now, Mirnaia will join Peter & Sons and spearhead the company’s account management department. As a part of the leadership team, she will play a key role in the company’s relationships with Yggdrasil, Relax Gaming, as well as Bragg Gaming. At the same time, Mirnaia is expected to help expand its reach and engagement with Peter & Sons’ RGS platform.

On the other hand, Nutkevitch, who currently serves as SVP of corporate development at SkillOnNet will remain in this role and at the same time join Peter & Sons as the company’s CEO. In his new position, he will spearhead Peter & Sons as the game studio prepares to expand further its presence in strategic markets around the world.

The Appointments Come at an Important Time

Yann Bautista, Peter & Sons’ founder and commercial director, welcomed Mirnaia and Nutkevitch to the company’s team. He pointed out that Peter & Sons is excited to see the two experts join. Finally, Bautista acknowledged that the appointments mark an important milestone for the company as it seeks to expand further its presence and become a go-to provider for the iGaming sector globally.

“I’d officially like to welcome Maor and Christina to the team and say how thrilled we are to have you both on board.“ Yann Bautista, founder and commercial director at Peter & Sons
