Gerard's Gambit is the latest online slot from Play'n GO that resembles a visual novel, telling the story of an adventurer called Gerard

The provider of entertaining and unique games for the iGaming vertical, Play’n GO, announced it added a new title to its popular Wilde franchise on Thursday. The company confirmed the release of Gerard’s Gambit, a new and unique “educative online slot” game.

The new game marks a fine addition to Play’n GO’s growing portfolio of Book of Dead titles. Such games include the popular Rich Wilde and the Tome of Madness, as well as Cat Wilde and the Pyramids of Dead. Expanding further its Wilde Franchise, the famous developer delivers a new and engaging slot that features unique gameplay and grants online players a chance to win big prizes.

The new slot from Play’n GO elevates the player’s experience as it takes them to an adventure that unfolds in front of their eyes. Besides quality graphics, Gerard’s Gambit offers crisp sound effects. The innovative slot game features plenty of instructions while combining engaging and rewarding features.

A Truly Unique Addition to Play’n GO’s Growing Portfolio

George Olekszy, Play’n GO’s head of game retention, shared his excitement about the recently released game. He acknowledged that Gerard’s Gambit provides unique experiences as it takes the players to an all-new thrilling adventure. Olekszy explained: “With the Book of Dead series being one of our most popular IPs, it made sense to uncover a brand-new route for fans to take and expand upon the lore of the Wilde family.”

“In Gerard’s Gambit, not only will fans be introduced to the rugged and mysterious Gerard Wilde, but they’ll also get to try out a great new way to play slot games and develop a deeper understanding of the traditional mechanics of these games.“ George Olekszy, head of game retention at Play’n GO

Play’n GO’s head of game retention said that besides telling the story of Gerard Wilde, the innovative slot game helps the players better understand the mechanics of slot games. Finally, Olekszy noted that the expanding reels functionality of the game delivers exciting experiences for new players while at the same time, catering to seasoned slots fans too.

Without any doubt, Gerard’s Gambit offers a unique experience for the players. The adventure-themed slot helps teach the players about the mechanics of traditional slots. This allows the players to gradually progress from simple to more complex mechanics. Not unexpectedly, the process involves expanding the reels and incorporating mechanics that are more complex, granting a chance for bigger wins. Such include respins, multipliers, scatters and more. Overall, Gerard’s Gambit features ten different levels that offer unique features and themes.
