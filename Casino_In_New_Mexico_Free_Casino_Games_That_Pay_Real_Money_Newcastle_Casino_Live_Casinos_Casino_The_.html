Mexican Lottery: A Cultural and Economic Treasure

Lotería, commonly abbreviated as 'Mexican Lottery', is a traditional card game rooted in Mexican culture. Unlike traditional lottery systems, lottery is a game reminiscent of bingo, but uses cards decorated with images instead of numbers. It is a game that has transcended generations and is played at gatherings, parties and festivals. Each card has a unique image, from 'El Catrín' to 'La Sirena', and these images have become iconic symbols of Mexican popular culture.

This game not only offers the opportunity to win, but also vividly reflects the history, traditions and daily life of Mexico.
