BETER Doubles Down on LatAm Expansion

The Latin American-pivot is important for both companies as it enables them to position themselves in a market that has long-term implications and holds a lucrative promise. Through this partnership, BETER will directly feed live sports and esports feeds to the company in all regulated markets where both entities are covered.

BETER has already established an impressive footprint in the region on its own with market access to Brazil, Peru, Paraguay, Argentina, and Ecuador. Much of this is owing to the company’s immense variety of gaming markets being offered.
