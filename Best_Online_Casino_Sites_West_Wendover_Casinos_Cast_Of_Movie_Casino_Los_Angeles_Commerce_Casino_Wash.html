Slingo Packed with Cool New Features

There is a trail game featured in the core gameplay that will see you roll dice to move the Lucky Joker protagonist in the second-screen game and get instant cash prizes if the character ends up stepping on certain parts of the trail.

For each Slingo that players manage to produce on the reels, players will contribute to the prize ladder and get a chance to win even better rewards. The free spins round is awarded after four Slingos have been created.

In the 5×3-reel bonus you will get lucky horseshoes, green hats, pots of gold, and other cool symbols that contribute to the overall experience. Slingo Lucky Joker contains 20 paylines to enjoy, with a Wild symbol there to help you boost your win potential even further.

The wheel can lead to instant cash prizes, more free spins, and even an upgrade to the wheel to get your hands on juicier prizes. Slingo Lucky Joker is an original instalment in the acclaimed iGaming series by Gaming Realms and remains an important part of the gaming landscape through and through.
