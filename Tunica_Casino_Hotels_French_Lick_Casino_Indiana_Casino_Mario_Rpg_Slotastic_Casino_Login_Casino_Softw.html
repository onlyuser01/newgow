The Power of the GigaBlox Mechanic and Bonus Respins

The GigaBlox mechanic brings players the guarantee that a minimum of one symbol measuring between 2×2 and 4×4 is going to be seen landing on the reels during each new spin. This includes scatter and wild symbols also.

The Bonus Respins feature can be engaged when a minimum of six coin scatter symbols. When this occurs, they award three respins. Each new coin appearing on the reels thereafter is worth between 1x to 1000x.

Whenever the coins land in a square formation of at least 2×2 while the Bonus Respins feature is triggered, they are mixed to form a Mega Coin. They also feature a multiplier worth twice the sum of the multiplier values of all of the combining symbols.

The game also provides players with the Buy Bonus alternative feature that will send them right into the Bonus Respins feature where the Mega Coin will already be engaged.
