Theme and Storyline

This game meshes a few classic slot themes including fruit, gems, and bells.

Players will see familiar symbols from cherries to sparkling diamonds. They’ll embrace a vintage aesthetic as they snag as many goodies as possible.

The game is a great fit for players in the mood for a retro slot machine experience, with a modern sensibility.

The festive soundtrack makes it clear players are entering a roaring party with a bit of a ‘90s feel. This music feels in line with the slot’s classic theme.

Though the symbols reference classic slot themes, their animation feels fresh, high quality and slick. The diamonds are luminous and self-aware of their art cartoon style while the cherries seem to glow red.

The reels flash with tiny light bulbs that add extra drama to wins and create the reel of playing on a reel machine.

Overall, the animation and music of this game live up to its theme and create a retro atmosphere.

Gameplay

This game uses the Megaways system, which randomises the amount of symbols on the reels with each spin.

Wins are awarded for matching symbols on adjacent reels from left to right.

This slot also uses a cascade feature, meaning that symbols in winning combinations vanish upon payouts. This leaves their positions open for new symbols to fall in.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. The Wild symbol will only appear on reels 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6.

In the Free Spins feature, Wild symbols may have multipliers of x2 or x3.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when 5 symbols land anywhere. Players begin the feature with 15 free spins and can receive 15 additional free spins for landing 5 Scatters again.

If players land more than 5 Scatters, the additional Scatters provide 1 extra free spin each.
