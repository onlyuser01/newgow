The latest collaboration sees the company expand the reach of its games to Betway's customers in Mexico and the UK

The leading provider of games, powering online gambling operators, Push Gaming, announced it signed a new groundbreaking deal with the leading global online betting and gaming company, Betway. Under the terms of the freshly minted agreement, the iGaming developer agreed to supply its complete portfolio of games, powering Betway’s operations.

Details regarding the new partnership emerged Tuesday and see the betting and gaming company deliver thrilling titles from Push Gaming to its customers in the United Kingdom, as well as Mexico. As a result, Push Gaming’s entire portfolio of games will become available for the operator’s customers in the two jurisdictions.

The new landmark agreement significantly expands the reach of Push Gaming’s thrilling portfolio. Thanks to the partnership, Betway’s clients will be able to access recent releases from the iGaming developer, such as Razor Returns, as well as popular games from its Fat series. Customers of the operator will also be able to enjoy hits like Jammin’ Jars and Big Bamboo, among others.

Anthony Werkman, Betway Group’s CEO, was excited about the latest content agreement with Push Gaming. He said that the company is thrilled to add engaging titles from the famous iGaming content supplier for players within the two markets. Finally, Werkman pointed out: “At Betway, we strive to bring our customers the finest and most innovative gaming content, and partnering with Push Gaming aligns perfectly with our goal of giving our players more exciting games to enjoy.”

“We are proud to announce the addition of Push Gaming’s portfolio across several of our key markets.“ Anthony Werkman, CEO of Betway Group
