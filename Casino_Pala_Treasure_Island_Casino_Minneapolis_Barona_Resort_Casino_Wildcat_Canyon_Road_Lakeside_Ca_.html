Ragnawolves WildEnergy includes Nordic rune-inspired symbols along with special character symbols of the two wolves, as well as symbols resembling the Goddess of the Sun and God of the Moon. Additional special symbols include the sacred tree, Yggdrasil, along with WildEnergy symbols.

As with many new slot games, Yggdrasil’s slot offers a bet buy option via Golden Bet. The functionality enables the players to pay extra for their wager in exchange for “10x their bet to guarantee a wild symbol on each spin, and players can buy 10 or 13 free spins for 100x or 500x their bet respectively.”

Players that land WildEnergy symbols are granted four lives. According to Yggdrasil, if a “WildEnergy symbol is present on a winning payline, respins will be triggered.” This symbol remains on the reel for life’s duration and decreases with every respin. This effect goes on until the slot player has no more lives left.

On the other hand, if a player lands three or more scatter symbols on the reels, they trigger ten free spins. Wilds can also improve this gameplay by expanding the number of free spins, just like in the base game.
