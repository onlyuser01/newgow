Gameplay

Kluster Krystals Megaclusters is a five reel, five row slot.

Wins are awarded for clusters of at least 8 crystals.

Symbols take different forms (Singles, Doubles, Triples, and Quads), depending on the number of crystals they are made of.

This game employs a cascade feature, meaning that winning symbols will vanish to make room for fresh symbols to drop down.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

There is no Wild symbol in this game.

The Multiplier Feature means that Multipliers with values of x1, x2, x3, x4, x5, and x10 may land.

When they land next to a winning cluster, multipliers are upgraded. The upgrade increase corresponds to the base multiplier. Multipliers may be upgraded three times.

Once the round is complete, the multiplier/sum of multipliers is applied to a player’s total win.

There are three random mini features which are triggered after a series of 6 cascades:

Krystal Vanish : one colour of symbol is removed from the reels.

The Free Spins Feature is triggered by upgrading the bonus symbol entirely. This occurs when the bonus symbol is beside a winning cluster.

Players re-trigger the Free Spins Feature if they upgrade a bonus symbol fully during free spins. This awards the player four additional free spins.
