Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol and the Money symbol. The Wild symbol appears on all the reels while players are in the Free Spins round.

The Scatter symbol appears on all the reels.

When players land 3 or more Scatter symbols, the Free Spin feature is triggered. The greater the number of Scatters, the greater the number of free spins. For example:

3 Scatters: 10 free spins

4 Scatters: 15 free spins

5 Scatters: 20 free spins

While in the Free Spins feature, every Wild symbol gathers value from Fish Money symbols.

Whenever Wilds land during the Free Spins Feature, they are collected and saved to the end of the round. Every fourth Wild gathered retriggers the Free Spins feature and increases the Money symbol multiplier. The feature can be retriggered up to 3 times.

Wild symbol collects fish symbol prizes

The maximum win is limited to x5000 the player’s total bet, and, if player’s achieve this, the round will end immediately.

The Money symbol is assigned a random value during every spin from x1 to x20. It appears only on the middle reel.

When 3 Money symbols land, this prompts the Hold & Spin Bonus feature. During this feature, the middle reel symbols are held in place and the other reels vanish.

Hold and Spin bonus feature

The player begins with 3 respins and any Money symbols that land will reset the respins back to 3. The value of all the middle reel’s Money symbols are added to the player's total win.

The Diamond Money symbol appears on reels 1, 2, 4, and 5 while players are in the Hold & Spin feature. When this symbol lands, it is randomly assigned a value between x100 and x4993.

If player doesn't reach the guaranteed win in the Hold & Spin bonus feature, respins again to 3. Guaranteed win of this feature is player’s total bet x20.
