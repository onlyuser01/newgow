Tumbling Game Mechanic

Coming across a matching combo does not offer players an instant payout. Instead, it triggers the reel-tumbling events. For example, a winning combo made of three symbols makes the rest of the symbols vanish from the grid, causing fresh symbols to tumble down. Provided extra matching symbols land near the winning combo, they create a cluster. Players receive their payouts once there are no other matching icons available to fall on the grid.
