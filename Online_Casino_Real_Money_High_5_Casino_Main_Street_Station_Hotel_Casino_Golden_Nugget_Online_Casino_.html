The Giants are a Step Away from the Playoffs

The Giants’ chance of making the playoffs is close to 90%. If things don’t go horribly wrong for them, they’re going to make the postseason for the first time since 2016.

As they’re playing in NFC East – which is one of the toughest divisions in the league – their only chance to get to the playoffs is via Wild Card.

To book a Wild Card berth, they might only need one more win. That certainly sounds good, but the problem is that their schedule in the remainder of the regular season looks very hard. After the game with the in-form Vikings, they’re taking on the Indianapolis Colts, while in the final gameweek, their opponent is the Philadelphia Eagles.
