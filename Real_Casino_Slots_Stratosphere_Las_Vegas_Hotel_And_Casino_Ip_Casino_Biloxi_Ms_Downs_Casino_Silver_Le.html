Reinventing IGT’s Classic in Slingo’s Image

The high-performing original game will find a new lease on life and will benefit from the popularity of the Slingo mechanic with players. Players are able to climb through a prize ladder that holds incrementally increasing rewards.

Slingo Cleopatra also comes with a new accumulation mechanic which will come with more free spins as players climb up the ladder. This will help players with triggering the Bonus Spins as well as accessing better payouts, the company details in a press release.

Gaming Realms has also deployed wild features and symbols to help drive player interaction and reward players as they progress through the game. Free spin symbols also award free spins as you continue to progress upward on the prize ladder.
