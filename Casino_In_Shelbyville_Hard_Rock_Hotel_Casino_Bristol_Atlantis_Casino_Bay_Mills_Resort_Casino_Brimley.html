The market leader in game development, R. Franco Group, announced that R. Franco Technologies SLU, secured an important compliance certification from the gaming industry's leading testing and certification services provider, Gaming Laboratories International (GLI). The company that belongs to R. Franco Group was recognized for IRIS, its Platform Account Management platform that covers Event Wagering Systems.

The certification comes after a rigorous evaluation process. To secure the certification, the company has undergone an extensive review of its framework, features and management system. As a result of the process, R. Franco Technologies secured the GLI-33 v1.1 Event Wagering Systems Standard.

The aforementioned certification marks a milestone moment as it recognizes the company's commitment to adhering to the highest quality standards within the gambling sector. Additionally, GLI's certification recognized R. Franco's innovative technology and capabilities.

Karen Sierra-Hughes, GLI's Vice President of Latin America, Caribbean and Spain, shared her excitement about the latest certification in a statement. "We are grateful to R. Franco for trusting GLI for this important milestone in their plans for international expansion," she explained. Moreover, Sierra-Hughes pointed out: "We celebrate the company’s commitment to integral compliance and the dedication their technical team demonstrated to comply with the GLI-33 standard diligently, thus putting themselves a step ahead to enter any jurisdiction with little to no time to market."

Javier Sacristán, R Franco Digital's International Business Director, added that the company is excited to secure the prestigious GLI certification for its leading platform. "It recognizes the hard work and expertise of our product and development teams, and it will help us to achieve our strategic objective to reach new markets in the near future," he explained in conclusion.

The company predicted that the new certification from GLI would strengthen its position within the gambling sector. According to R. Franco, the achievement will help propel the company "towards new frontiers in international markets." Finally, the leading provider of technology for the sector pointed out: "We extend our gratitude to GLI for being a part of this significant milestone in our journey towards global expansion."

Founded more than three decades ago, GLI is currently a leader in testing and assessment services for the iGaming, lottery and land-based gambling sectors. The company has established its presence on six continents and holds international and US accreditation for compliance with ISO/IEC 17025, 17020, and 17065 standards for the betting, lottery and gaming industries.
