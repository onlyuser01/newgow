Philadelphia Eagles Coming to Seattle to Get a Win

The Eagles are 10-3 overall with two of their three losses happening in the last two games. It sounds bad, but there’s an explanation – they lost to the Dallas Cowboys and San Francisco 49ers who have been in top shape throughout the 2023 season.

Before that, we saw the Eagles beat a number of heavyweights, including the Buffalo Bills, Kansas City Chiefs, Miami Dolphins, and even the Cowboys.

Given the circumstances, that is, a very ugly schedule, these guys have been fantastic in 2023. This goes particularly for their offensive team which is sixth-best in the league with 26.3 PPG. With QB Jalen Hurts in top shape, it’s no surprise it’s been like that.

This guy is 10th in passing yards and tied 12th in passing touchdowns. He’s also been great when it comes to running the ball, scoring 12 rushing TDs already. The reason why these stats are important is that Philly’s next opponent isn’t great at defending against runs. In fact, the Seahawks are the NFL’s fourth-worst defense in rushing TDs conceded.

Will Seattle Seahawks Finally Lose to Eagles?

The Seattle Seahawks have won every single of their last seven duels with the Philadelphia Eagles. However, they haven’t played against them since 2020. Fast-forward to December 18, 2023, and neither team looks the same as before.

The Eagles are now a team with Super Bowl ambitions while the Seahawks are struggling to challenge for a Wild Card ticket to the postseason. Pete Carroll’s boys are 6-7 in 2023, which isn’t bad at all. However, they did lose each of their last four games.

But there’s a caveat. Two of those games were against the 49ers, one against the Cowboys, and one against the Rams. Plus, the loss to the LA-based team was by just one point.
