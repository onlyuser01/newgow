Gameplay

Using the Megaways system, this slot randomises the symbols in each reel for each spin.

A horizontal tracker above the reels will add additional symbols to reels 2, 3, 4, and 5.

This slot awards wins for matching symbols in adjacent reels from left to right.

With the cascade feature, symbols that form winning combinations will vanish to make way for new symbols.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

When players land 4 Bonus symbols, they are awarded 12 free spins. Landing additional Bonus symbols increases the number of free spins by +5 for each additional symbol, up to a maximum of 27.

Players can also buy directly into the Free Spin feature.

Mystery symbols will transform into matching symbols whenever they land together, increasing the opportunities for wins.

Mystery symbols may land at random and transform into matching regular symbols.
