Hotel Yeti-way review

Hotel Yeti-way is themed around a charming Yeti on a tropical holiday.

Players will dip their toes in warm ocean water as they join a mythical mountain ape for some fun in the sun. As they surf, swim, and bungee jump together, they’ll get into the swing of vacation life.

The lighthearted atmosphere of this slot makes it an ideal choice for those looking for an upbeat experience.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The animation style feels modern, with bright colors and a welcoming aesthetic. The reels are packed with vacation accessories, including sunscreen, sandals, and sunglasses.

The reels are framed by a surf hut with plenty of palm leaves swaying nearby. It’s fun to imagine sauntering up to the hut to rent a surfboard only to find a Yeti running the show.

The Yeti character is a charming fellow who is up for adventure. He seems to be spending his time in the tropics engaging in many daring holiday activities and not missing his mountain home one bit.

A friendly seabird stands on the reels pecking for food, ruffling his feathers, and being generally quite adorable.

Lazy ukulele strumming ensures that the soundtrack has a Hawaiian feel. Gentle bird calls give life to the seabird character and help establish the beachside setting.

Together, the graphics and soundtrack establish a cheerful holiday vibe.

Gameplay

Hotel Yeti-way is a six-reel, four-row slot.

Players are awarded wins for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Free Spins feature is triggered if players land 3 or more Scatter symbols, with the number of free spins awarded depending on the triggering Scatters as follows:

6 Scatters award 20 free spins

5 Scatters award 16 free spins

4 Scatters award 12 free spins

3 Scatters award 8 free spins

Three special features are available to be selected during the Free Spins feature:

Scuba Diving: Additional respins are prompted by sticky Sharks.

Bungee Jumping: Enhanced chances of stacked Wilds appearing.

Surfing: Each time the Yeti surfs, Wilds are added to the reels, up to a maximum of 8.

During the Free Spins feature, one or more reels is selected to have a multiplier added to it. This can affect up to 6 win lines at a time.

The Wild symbol substitutes for every symbol with the exclusion of the Scatter symbol.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.19%, about our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.10 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of Hotel Yeti-way is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this average RTP, high volatility tropical vacation-themed slot.

The surprise of finding the Yeti character in the Hawaiian setting adds whimsy to this engaging slot.

Meanwhile, the decent RTP and robust volatility make Hotel Yeti-way a nice choice for mathematically-oriented players.
