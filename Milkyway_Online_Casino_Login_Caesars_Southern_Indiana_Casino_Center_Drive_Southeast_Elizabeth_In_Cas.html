Games Global, a supplier of iGaming content to online gambling operators, has unveiled Games Global PLUS, its new content distribution network. Thanks to the new solution, the company will be able to better promote the games of its partnering studios.

Games Global PLUS Goes Live

Games Global PLUS is the newest addition to Games Global’s arsenal. The new aggregation platform will help the supplier distribute gaming titles from some of the best studios in the iGaming sector. The company has existing agreements with a whopping 25 top-tier studios, allowing it to supply its partnering operators with best-in-class games.

Operators that pick Games Global as their partner can now access many exciting games from some of the best studios, such as Hacksaw Gaming, Stakelogic and Gaming Realms, to name a few. The new Games Global PLUS network will provide partnering companies with greater flexibility and choice than ever before.

The new platform is very intuitive and easy to use, providing operators not only with games but with a huge selection of iGaming tools as well. By leveraging its rich portfolio of solutions, Games Global allows iGaming companies to engage customers with unforgettable experiences.

The New Solution Will Benefit Games Global Partners

Games Global’s chief product officer, Andrew Booth, spoke about the launch of the new platform. He called its release an important move towards the company’s goal of becoming one of the best suppliers in the world.

I am thrilled to announce the launch of the latest Games Global product and to offer our customers a simplified approach to aggregated content. This is a significant step in Games Global’s journey to becoming the industry’s number one content supplier. Andrew Booth, CPO, Games Global

Operators that are part of Games Global’s distribution platform can immediately benefit from Games Global PLUS, the aggregator announced. This will allow them to access a diverse suite of games, containing hundreds of titles.

The release of Games Global PLUS further boosts Games Global’s existing offering and strengthens its focus on studio development. Thanks to the new aggregation network, the company will be able to provide its partnering operators with the freedom, platform, tools and distribution network they need to “fully explore their creativity and inspire revolutionary new developments.”

Games Global emphasized that the new solution will help companies enhance the experience of their users. The aggregator hopes that its new platform will help the industry grow and explore “previously uncharted territories.”
