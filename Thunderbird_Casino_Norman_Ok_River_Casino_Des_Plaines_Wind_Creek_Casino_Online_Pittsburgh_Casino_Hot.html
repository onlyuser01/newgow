Scarab Free Spins, Bonus Boost & DoubleMax Features

When players land a minimum of three scatters on any position on the reels, they automatically engage the Scarab Free Spins feature. The latter awards players seven Free Spins along with an initial gem boost that will not cause the DoubleMax multiplier to reset.

The Bonus Boost feature can add 25% extra to players’ total bets, thus doubling the odds of activating the Scarab Free Spins. Players who are in a hurry to start enjoying the free spins can opt for the Bonus Buy as long as they are willing to pay 100x the bet. The Bonus Buy option is only available in certain jurisdictions.

The DoubleMax feature works by doubling the value of a win multiplier after each new winning spin. The winning symbols are then seen disappearing from the reels, with a wild symbol replacing them to trigger fresh wins.

To engage the Pharaoh’s Gaze Wilds feature, players need to wait for the landing symbols on the reels to also reveal a gem shortly after. When players collect a minimum of three gems, the special feature is triggered. Next, they are rewarded with one up to three extra Wild symbols for the upcoming three spins or cascades.
