Starting in New Jersey

Further Expansion on the Cards

With licenses to provide its games in New Jersey, Michigan, West Virginia, Pennsylvania, and Connecticut, NeoGames is planning to expand Pariplay’s presence with DraftKings in these jurisdictions in the future, pending agreement, licensure and regulatory approval.
