Pittsburgh Steelers Hoping for Impossible

The Steelers are coming to Orchard Park as 10-point underdogs, which makes them the team with the least chance of winning of all the teams playing in the 2024 NFL Wild Card Round. There are several reasons for this, starting with the fact that they haven’t been too impressive throughout this entire campaign.

They finished the regular season with a 10-7 record, which seems pretty good, but they need to count themselves luck. For instance, they played two games with the Cincinnati Bengals after Joe Burrow suffered a season-ending injury, so it wasn’t too difficult to beat them.

They also defeated the mighty Baltimore Ravens in the final round of the regular season, when it was already clear that the Ravens were going to finish as the No. 1 seed.

The Bills have been brilliant this season, finishing with an 11-6 record and winning the AFC East. They won their last five games and are undefeated since November.

Further, each time they lost this season, they lost by a relatively small margin. In fact, six points is the biggest margin by which they lost in 2023. It happened against the Cincinnati Bengals in Cincy. It happened in early November when Joe Burrow was still healthy.

Many of them believe this is the year when the Bills will get to the Super Bowl for the first time since 1993.

But first things first – they need to defeat the Steelers on Saturday. Will they pull it off? Well, they did it in three of their last four meetings.
