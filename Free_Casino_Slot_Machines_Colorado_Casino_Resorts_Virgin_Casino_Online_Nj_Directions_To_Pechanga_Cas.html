Martin Truex Jr. Needs to be Taken Seriously (Again)

After two disappointing finishes, Martin Truex Jr. got himself on the podium again. He won Crayon 301 on Monday, which was his third win in the last three months. The result is that he remains top of the NASCAR Drivers’ championship standings.

Oddsmakers are confident he’s going to continue to drive well, the evidence being that he’s seen as one of the co-shared for the Pocono race.
