Incentive Games and Bet Lion Extend Partnership

Incentive Games, a leading Free-to-Play and virtual sports games provider extends its partnership with Bet Lion, a popular sports betting provider in Africa, for another 12 months. Users in Africa will enjoy even more Play-to-Play and Free-to-Play sports games.

Incentive Games CEO John Gordon stated that the company is happy to extend the successful partnership with Bet Lion and continue the collaborative efforts by launching more games with the brand. He also said that Incentive’s offerings have been specifically designed for markets like Zambia and Kenya but play exceptionally well on mobile devices like smartphones and tablets.

Incentive Games continues to extend its reach. With a strong foothold on 5 continents, Incentive Games recently signed a new partnership with FSB Technology, as the latter will promote its array of games to more potential customers.

New Incentive Games Content on Bet Lion

Under the new agreement, Bet Lion will launch more games in Zambia and Kenya, including Incentive Game’s hit titles such as Pick 5, upgraded Pick 6, and Dream Team games, which attract thousands of players per week.

With Pick 6, users could predict the correct scores in 6 soccer games to win the cash prize. Pick 5 is a new concept that combines score predictor and virtual sports in a Free-to-Play format, meaning users predict the scores with virtual machines instead of real-life matches.

Incentive Games’ Dream Team has been the players’ favorite for a long time. It allows users to choose their fantasy team of 5 players from 1 soccer game to compete with other users and have a chance to win the cash prize. The 5 players either earn or lose points for the actions they take in the real-life game.
