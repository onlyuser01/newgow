Hall of Fame NFL quarterback Dan Marino has signed an exclusive partnership with Pixiu Gaming, an established North American developer of iGaming and Keno experiences. The news was shared by GameOn, a marketing agency.

He spent 17 seasons with the team and made history before retiring in 2000. Now, the man is back in Pixiu’s upcoming range of slots which will seek to introduce players to fresh and innovative features and hit markets across North America in the second quarter of 2023. Pixiu is readying up to launch the products in both Canada and the United States.

The profits realized by the slots will also go towards helping the Dan Marino Foundation, a not-for-profit the NFL legend helped set up in order to help individuals with developmental challenges and autism and help them achieve their full potential.

All that remains is for the launch that will happen around mid-2023.
