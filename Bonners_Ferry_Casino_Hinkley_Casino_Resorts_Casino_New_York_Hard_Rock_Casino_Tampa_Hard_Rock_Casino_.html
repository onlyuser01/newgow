BetRivers and the United Soccer League (USL) championship team Indy Eleven announced today a multi-year corporate partnership to make the sports betting and online gaming brand of Rush Street Interactive (RSI) Official Sports Betting Partner of the Indiana-based soccer franchise.

“BetRivers.com is excited to be an official sports betting partner of Indy Eleven.” Mattias Stetz, COO, RSI

Third Pillar Partner

Under the terms of the agreement, BetRivers gains exclusivity in the “Sports Betting” category and becomes the team’s third “Pillar Partner”, alongside Central Indiana Honda Dealers and Community Health Network.

“Since the legalization of sports betting in Indiana in late 2019, Indy Eleven has been extremely focused on finding a true partner to maximize what has become a pivotal area for growth in all corners of the sports industry.” Greg Stremlaw, President and CEO, Indy Eleven

The long-term agreement was achieved with the support of Swiss-based sports data provider Sportradar, a key partner to Indy Eleven and a developer of the business relationship in the wagering category.

“I want to thank BetRivers for the tremendous commitment they have shown to our club. We look forward to working with them in a myriad of ways beginning with the upcoming 2021 season.” Greg Stremlaw, President and CEO, Indy Eleven

The deal will see 2021 jerseys of Indy Eleven feature BetRivers sleeve logo placement to signify the sports betting brand as an official partner of the soccer team, signage and digital asset integration, as well as other initiatives, including sponsorship of the post-game show following all local television broadcasts and sponsorship of the opening segment of the Saturday soccer radio show The Fan.

“This partnership combines two organizations that share an unwavering commitment to excellence in providing sports fans with unparalleled experiences.” Mattias Stetz, COO, RSI

Indy Eleven and BetRivers will also hold a Presenting Partner night in conjunction with a home game from the 2021 season.

Sport Partnerships Trend Continues

The partnership between BetRivers and Indy Eleven is in line with the recent trend of collaboration between professional sports franchises and sports betting and online gaming operators as most industry players reposition themselves looking to fully engage with sports fans in expectation of the gathering speed sports betting market nationwide.

In January, BetRivers entered into a similar agreement with the National Basketball Association (NBA) franchise Detroit Pistons to raise brand awareness among fans of professional basketball. The deal included tailored fan experiences such as betting contests with special prizes in exchange for access to intellectual properties of the Pistons including logos and marks.
