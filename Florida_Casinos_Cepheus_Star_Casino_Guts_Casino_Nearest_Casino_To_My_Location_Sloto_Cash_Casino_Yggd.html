Power of Love Is Packed with Features

The slot keeps players engaged with a series of valuable features, including Love is in the Air, Love is Popping, and Love Grows. While playing the base game, the Power of Love symbol represents the Wild that increases players’ chances to create winning paylines.

When two halves of a heart are seen landing on consecutive reels, the Love Meter fills up. Once it reaches its full height, the special features become active on the selection screen.

The three features mentioned earlier can also be used in stacks and work together to generate massive win opportunities.

The Love is Popping feature will trigger between two and 20 Wild symbols to land on the reels while all the heart symbols will be turned into Wilds.

Love is in the Air is a symbol improvement feature that turns all selected symbols into matching symbols that are more valuable. The same feature also triggers two Wild symbols to land on the reels.

Love Grows is an expanding reel feature that will change the height of the reels and enable the grid to reach 5×8 in size while doubling the number of active paylines.

When three bonus symbols are seen landing during a spin, players receive seven Free Spins that can stack all three features for the remainder of the bonus round.
