Holding an MGA license allows the company to deliver its fan-favorite and popular slot machine games to new partners. By tapping into the company’s remote gaming server, the gambling operators can expand their offering, gaining access to a range of slot games and thrilling titles, such as Bullet Bonanza, Lolicat and Pearly Shores, among others.
