OnAir Adapts a Famous Slot into a Live Casino Game

The so-called 9 Pots of Gold StreamIcon is an innovative offering that will diversify OnAir’s live casino portfolio. The game takes inspiration from Gameburger’s original slot and turns it into an engaging live experience. The game will now have a live presenter and other innovative features that will debut on the live leprechaun-themed title.

9 Pots of Gold StreamIcon will allow players to place a bet per spin and decide how many spins they wish to bet on. The host will then press a large physical button that triggers a spin. This novel idea will spark a new life into the beloved slot game and engage customers in a new way. In addition, the StreamIcon edition will come with spin and mega multiplier features. Other additions are free spins and a pop-up wheel offer that provides players with an extra chance to win prizes.

The game will be streamed from OnAir’s studio. The live casino will add a social element to the title. To further emphasize that, OnAir Entertainment will also introduce custom chat stickers, allowing players to interact and have fun. The game will debut in September, with OnAir planning to release other games within the next few months.
