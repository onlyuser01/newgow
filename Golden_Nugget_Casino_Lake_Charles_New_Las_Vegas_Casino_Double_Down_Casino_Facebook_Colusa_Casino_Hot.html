Air Dice Reinforces Its Leadership

Nikunen is an iGaming professional with years of experience within the gaming industry. He has worked for some notable local companies, including Paf, one of the oldest gaming companies in Finland and one of the first companies to digitalize its offerings. As a member of Paf, Nikunen worked as a customer relations manager and later as a player intelligence manager.

In 2013, Nikunen joined Finnplay, another local gambling leader. At first, he served as a vice president of sales before being promoted to chief commercial officer two years later. In 2018, Finnplay named Nikunen as chief marketing officer – a position he maintained for over three years.

Nikunen is also one of the founding partners of Fortuity Holdings, the brand behind LuckyCasino.com.

As chief business development officer of Air Dice, Nikunen will be responsible for helping the brand grow and cement itself as a leading force in the Finnish market.
