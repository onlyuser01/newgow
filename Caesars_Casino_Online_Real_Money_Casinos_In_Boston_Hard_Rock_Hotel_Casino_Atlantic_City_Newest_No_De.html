The leading supplier of games for the iGaming industry, Pragmatic Play, announced the launch of its new sportsbook product. Under the terms of the deal, the iGaming content and games provider agreed to deliver its full-service iGaming solution. This means that the operator would not only benefit from Pragmatic Play’s sports betting solution but gain access to its portfolio of games, including live casino, virtual sports and slot titles.

The integration into DAZN BET was completed via Pragmatic Play’s Player Account Management platform technology delivered by Pragmatic Solutions. This innovative solution served as the core technology for DAZN BET since its official launch. The integration of Pragmatic Play Sportsbook enables the company to significantly expand its footprint within the vertical and makes it available for all Pragmatic Solutions’ platform partners.

Recently, Pragmatic Play announced an important deal with bet365. Under the terms of that collaboration, the company expanded the reach of its games for customers of the operator in two important European markets – Sweden and Spain.
