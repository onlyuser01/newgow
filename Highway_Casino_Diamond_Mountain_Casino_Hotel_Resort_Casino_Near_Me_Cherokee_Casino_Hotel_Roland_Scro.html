CT Interactive, the provider of quality games for the online gambling industry, continues to grow its European presence. The company confirmed recently that a portfolio of its exclusive games, as well as jackpot functionality, secured certification for Republika Srpska. Lauded as a milestone moment, the achievement enables CT Interactive to continue its expansion in Europe and the Balkans in particular.

The announcement comes after the company secured certification from the Institute for Testing and Certification. As a result, 50 of CT Interactive's fan-favorite games, including Champagne and Fruits, Fire Egg, Hit the Hot, Lizard Blizzard, 20 Mega Slot, Shining Treasures, Purple Fruits, Coffee Magic, Win Storm, and Aztec Gold 20, among others, secured the certification.

The aforementioned titles are already recognized for their innovative, thrilling and engaging features and themes. In fact, Win Storm recently secured the Online Game of the Year Award from the BEGE Awards 2022, a prestigious event for the gambling sector in the region.

In addition to the 50 exclusive games, CT Interactive secured certification for its Hot Luck Jackpot. The innovative and engaging 3-level mystery interactive jackpot allows providers to set bet limits and adjustable hits. At the same time, Hot Luck Jackpot offers additional awards for online players.

Lachezar Petrov, CT Interactive's CEO, shared his excitement about the company's expansion in a statement. He pointed out: "We are so happy that the players from Republika Srpska will have the chance to enjoy such an exceptional portfolio of high-quality products." Last but not least, Petrov noted: "This process is part of our policy to grow our European presence."

Late last month, CT Interactive confirmed its plans to showcase products at the upcoming 2023 SBC Summit Barcelona 2023, the renowned event for the global gambling industry. The company revealed that it will be at stand CG 112 and showcase two of its jackpots, as well as some of its latest slot releases.

Before that, earlier last month, CT Interactive forged a new collaboration with NetBet Italy. Under the terms of that deal, the company agreed to supply its portfolio of games, powering the leading operator in the country. As a result, CT Interactive expanded further the reach of its cutting-edge titles to new audiences.
