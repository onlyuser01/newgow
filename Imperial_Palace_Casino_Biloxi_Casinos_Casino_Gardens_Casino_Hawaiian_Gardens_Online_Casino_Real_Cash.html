Ocean Casino Resort in Atlantic City just started building its High-Limit Slot Experience, coming soon.

High-Limit Slot Experience Coming Soon

Atlantic City’s Ocean Casino Resort started construction on a new slots area this week. It features 140 slot machines and private restrooms, as well as upgraded beverage services and a cage reserved for guests playing in the parlor.

The High-Limit Slot Experience will offer new gaming products including Lightning Cash, Dragon Cash, Dollar Storm, and Cash Link. Classic games will also be featured.

Ocean Casino Resort CEO Terry Glebocki shared the company’s excitement over the new launch, as players have been asking for more enhanced gaming options.

In November, the casino will be the first gaming facility to feature Konami Gaming’s All Aboard slot machines. The six brand-new games will include base games Dynamite Dash and Piggy Pennies.

Ocean Casino Resort Aids NJ Families

Ocean started the season by “giving back” to the community: the resort donated $10,000 to The Community Food Bank of New Jersey to aid families in need during the holiday season.

On November 11, Ocean will welcome US Veterans and Active Military on a red-carpet check-in line at the hotel and will offer a discounted accommodation rate. The Ocean Casino Resorts logo and the iconic ball at the top of the tower will be lit up in blue, white, and red.

Atlantic City’s Best Casino Award

The Ocean Casino Resort is the tallest building in Atlantic City, New Jersey, and spans twenty beachfront acres on the Atlantic City Boardwalk. The tower features 1,399 rooms and suites.

AC Ocean Walk’s Ocean Casino offers nearly 2,000 slot machines, 125 table games, and a sportsbook with partner William Hill. With Ocean Rewards Club, the resort’s exclusive loyalty program, members have the luxury of experiencing premium incentives and rewards for visiting. The resort also offers 15 locations for world-class dining, as well as a spa and bathhouse, three swimming pools, a golf court, nightclubs, and retail stores.

Ocean Casino Resort was recently awarded Best Casino by the Press of Atlantic City’s Best Press Awards.
