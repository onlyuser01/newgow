As a Table Games consultant, the former chairman and CEO of Shuffle Master, will play an important role in the development of the company's products

The leading cross-platform games and entertainment company, formerly known as Scientific Games Corporation, Light & Wonder, confirmed it engaged in a new collaboration, strengthening its C-suite with the addition of an important expert. On Thursday, the company revealed it selected Dr Mark Yoseloff as its new consultant. Joining Light & Wonder for this position the former CEO and chairman of Shuffle Master is expected to play a key role in the development of the company’s Global Table Games business.

He will provide strategic advice and guidance, helping to brainstorm new ideas. Moreover, Yoseloff will play an important role in the development of new products, design features and strategic initiatives that seek to propel the growth of Light & Wonder. One of his ongoing focuses would be the development of cross-platform content as well as Electronic Table Games and Felt-Based Table Games.

According to Light & Wonder, a new game developed with vital guidance from Yoseloff is expected to debut at the upcoming 2023 Global Gaming Expo. The prestigious and much-anticipated event for the gambling industry is scheduled to take place in Las Vegas between October 10 and October 13, 2023. The upcoming title would feature DIAMOND MILLIONS, a new progressive theme that grants players a chance to secure a multi-million-dollar jackpot with a wager as small as $5.

The Former Shuffle Master Executive Is Delighted to Team up with Light & Wonder

Sharing his excitement, Yoseloff said that he is delighted to collaborate with Light & Wonder for the development of its products. He explained that the company is focused on constantly improving the experiences of its players through innovation. Finally, Yoseloff said he is looking forward to the success of the new games and solutions from Light & Wonder.

“I have been impressed by Light & Wonder’s management team and am thrilled to work with the team and contribute to the Company’s legacy of innovation within the gaming industry and the shuffler and utility space.“ Dr Mark Yoseloff, consultant for Light & Wonder

Mike McKiski, Light & Wonder’s SVP of Global Table Games, was similarly excited regarding the new collaboration. He acknowledged Yoseloff’s extensive experience and track record. Last but not least, McKiski predicted that the new consultant will play a vital role in the development of the company’s products that are going to elevate the experience for the players.
