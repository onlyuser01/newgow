BETER, a company that focuses on the creation of content and tech for the gaming and betting verticals has appointed Maria Mashchenko as its new general manager for the BETER Sports business unit, the company shared in a press release. This is a de facto promotion as Mashchenko spent the past two years as the company’s chief operating officer and has been responsible for the development of in-house, outsourced, and franchised sports and esports tournaments.

BETER Sports Gets New Iron Lady at the Helm

During his stint, Mashchenko has helped BETER create a new generation of events and opportunities by working with esteemed esports and sports organizations.
