Game Features

The game addresses players who appreciate both traditional and modern-day game elements with innovative twists to them. The base game has been designed in such a way that it continues to be appealing to players even when no other features are activated. When the wild and scatter symbols start popping, joined by other special symbols and features, the game instantly goes to the next level.

Hit ‘n’ Roll Feature

Players who manage to land three Hit ‘n’ Roll symbols will get to trigger a wheel. The wheel will either reward them with an instant cash prize, one of the three available free spins variants (Scatter Split, Expand ‘n’ Lock, Paired Reels), or one of the six Cashpots.

Extra Free Spins

This feature will trigger three different types of free spins games to enjoy. Players can also grab an additional Free Spin by hitting the Gold ‘+1’ symbol on any location on the reels while playing the Free Spins game.

Hyper Bonus Feature

This special feature will allow players to reach the Free Spins or the Hit ‘n’ Roll feature straight away, without worrying about landing the right symbols.

Paired Reels Feature

This feature will automatically and randomly group the reels into several possible combinations that could generate massive wins on each new spin.

Expand ‘n Lock Feature

This will trigger the reels to expand and lock a randomly chosen symbol. The symbol will be stacked on top, allowing players to enjoy even more ways to win.

Scatter Split Feature

This will allow for a randomly chosen symbol on the reels to instantly split in two or three and generate a payment no matter what its position might be. The new symbols will be counted as two or three matches.
