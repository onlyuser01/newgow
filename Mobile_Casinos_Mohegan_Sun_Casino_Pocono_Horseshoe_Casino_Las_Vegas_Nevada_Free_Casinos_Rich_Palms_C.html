Frank's Farm review

Theme and Storyline

Frank's Farm is themed around a farm of adorable animals.

Players will find themselves on a sunny farm where they’ll meet irresistibly cute chickens, pigs, and cows. If they can take care of these sweet critters, players will thrive in this pastoral world.

Frank's Farm will appeal to players with a soft spot for animals.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The relaxing soundtrack has a subtle country vibe and clanging notes that bring to note an old-fashioned dinner bell. Occasionally, the snorting of pigs and the cries of sheep add to the farm atmosphere.

The owner of the farm, Frank himself, is a friendly sloth who sports a straw hat and a laid-back smile. This kindly farmer immediately welcomes players to his idyllic property.

The reels are filled with animal symbols, each one animated in an ultra-cute cartoon style. Bubble bees, fish, cows, and chickens all appear with large eyes and tiny smiles. Players will be left with an urge to cuddle these charming characters.

In the background, players will see the farm from above with green grass, a cozy cabin, and a bouncing tractor nearby. Sharp-eyed players will notice a couple of baby chicks jumping around the yard as well.

Overall, the soundtrack and animation of Frank's Farm creates a charming countryside fantasy.

Gameplay

Frank's Farm is a six-reel, six-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing at least 8 matching symbols anywhere on the reels.

Players can buy directly into the Bonus by selecting the Buy Bonus option.

The maximum possible win for this slot is 5,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Market Scatter symbols have the special ability to multiply all wins when they are on the reels. When more than one Market Scatter is present, their values are multiplied together before being applied to wins. The possible Market Scatter multiplier values range from 2x to 10x the player’s total bet.

The Order Rush Bonus is triggered when players land 3 Free Spins (FS) symbols. This feature begins with 10 free spins. If players land FS symbols during the Order Rush Bonus, they will be awarded 1 additional free spin for each FS symbol.

The premise of the Order Rush Bonus is that players are given orders to complete. The orders are represented by 1-3 symbol sets, and each symbol set is made up of 10-24 matching symbols. With each successful order, the level of difficulty increases.

When players complete an order, the minimum value of the Market Scatter multiplier increases by 1x.

The Diner Dash Bonus game begins when players land 4 FS symbols. The feature begins with 10 free spins, with additional FS symbols awarding extra spins as in the Order Rush Bonus. During the Diner Dash Bonus, a special Diner symbol can appear and remain sticky on the reels throughout the feature.

Every win that occurs during the Diner Dash Bonus is collected by the Diner symbols and awarded at the end of each spin.
