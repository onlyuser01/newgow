Juventus Determined to Improve Their Serie A Form

Juventus are sitting in the No. 7 position right now, which does not lead to either of Euro competitions. The reason behind this isn’t that they’ve been bad in the league this season. On the contrary, their record is 18-5-7, which is better than all teams other than Napoli and Lazio.

The reason why they have just 44 points is that the league deducted some points from them as a fine for a breach of financial regulations.

What this means is that they need to get into a winning mode in Serie A to get themselves into the Euro zone. The bad news is that their recent form isn’t good. They’re coming off back-to-back losses. The good news is that they definitely have the manpower to get to the Top 6.

Napoli are Very Close to Securing the Title

With 14 points more than the No. 2 team, Napoli are almost certainly going to win the Serie A title. Their recent run isn’t that impressive, but that’s probably because their focus was set on the Champions League in the last couple of weeks. But that dream is over – AC Milan knocked them out of the UCL earlier this week.

What this means is that their only focus for the rest of the season is going to be the Italian domestic league. With only eight games left to be played, they need only a few more wins to secure the trophy. So, can they get a win on Sunday?

Juventus vs Napoli Prediction
