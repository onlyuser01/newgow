The new collaboration will boost Gamingtec's offering while helping Thunderkick engage with new audiences

The provider of innovative content for the iGaming industry, Thunderkick, announced it forged a new partnership with the leading business-to-business solutions provider, Gamingtec. The terms of the freshly minted partnership enable the fast-growing provider of games for the online vertical to reach new markets and audiences thanks to Gamingtec.

As a result, thanks to the deal, Thunderkick’s portfolio of engaging slots will become available for the B2B solutions provider. Such games include fan-favorite hits like the Esqueleto Explosivo series as well as Dionysus Golden Feast. Gamingtec’s partners will also have the opportunity to tap into the recently launched sequel of Sword of Khans, Sword of Shoguns.

Besides the existing portfolio of content, Gamingtec will benefit from upcoming releases from Thunderkick. This will help strengthen further the offering of the B2B solutions provider by bringing exciting new games that are expected to be released by the end of the year.

“Thunderkick is a very exciting studio with an exemplary portfolio of games, so we’re honored to assist in elevating them to new heights.“ Andrei Beu, commercial director at Gamingtec

Andrei Beu, Gamingtec’s commercial director, was delighted about the new collaboration with Thunderkick. He explained that the company offers engaging and exciting games which is why Gamingtec will be delighted to help them expand their reach.

Beu pointed out that Gamingtec strives to offer the best iGaming content and solutions for the online gambling vertical and help its clients grow. Finally, he said that the company is looking forward to the success the new collaboration with Thunderkick will bring.

The new partnership with Gamingtec comes on the heels of another important collaboration announced by Thunderkick. Only recently, the company joined forces with the fast-growing B2B aggregation platform, REEVO. Similarly to the deal with REEVO, the partnership with Gamingtec helps Thunderkick further expand its footprint within the iGaming vertical.
