Carolina Panthers Need to Up Their Defensive Game

The Carolina Panthers are 1-7 in the 2023 NFL season, winning their only game two weeks ago against the Houston Texans with 15-13 on the scoreline. You would think that 15 points isn’t much, but this season, they’ve scored fewer points in another three games.

The highest number of points they’ve scored so far in the 2023 season is 27 in the game with the Seattle Seahawks. These guys are averaging 17.5 points per game, which makes their offense the 6th-worst in the NFL.

If you thought Carolina’s offense was bad, wait to learn about this team’s defense. With 28.3 points conceded per game, the Panthers are in the shared-bottom place when opponents’ points are concerned.

The Panthers’ defense is even worse when it comes to points conceded on the road. They’re allowing 36.3 points on average in away games.

The Chicago Bears are 2-7 this season, but they’re on a two-loss skid. Both of those games, however, were played on the road. The last time the Bears played at Soldier Field, they defeated the Las Vegas Raiders 30-12.
