Gigantoonz review

Theme and Storyline

Gigantoonz is the latest in the Play'NGo series that is themed around delightfully cute aliens. Other entries in the series include Dr Toonz and the two Reactoonz slots.

As with previous installments, players will enter the heart of a spaceship and meet a variety of colorful extraterrestrials. Each creature has a unique personality, and often an unusual number of eyes.

This slot is a great pick for players in the mood for a cheerful sci-fi theme and a whimsical atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The upbeat soundtrack is filled with tinkling notes and occasional nonsense interjections from the aliens. Their voices are undeniably adorable and they add a lot of personality to the little extraterrestrials.

Each of the aliens is animated in a cartoon style in vivid hues of blue, pink, yellow, and green. Most smile cutely out at the player, showing off their unique features, such as tiny horns, or single eyeballs.

The Gargantoon alien, a much larger character, sits beside the reels with three eyes and a speckling of spots. He definitely seems a little fiercer than the tiny aliens on the reels.

Behind the reels, players will see a dreamy spacescape of drifting purple and blue galaxies and shooting stars. This helps confirm the game’s sci-fi theme and creates the sense of flying through space.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Gigantoonz combine for a lighthearted, sci-fi vibe.

Gameplay

Gigantoonz is an eight reel, eight row slot.

Players are awarded wins by landing at least 6 matching symbols in a cluster. These symbols must be connected horizontally and/or vertically.

The Cascade feature means that symbols that form winning clusters will vanish and new symbols will fall from above to take their place.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Quantum Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols. Between 5 and 10 Quantum Wilds will be added to the reels at random during non-winning spins.

Gargantuan Wilds only appear via the Quantumeter beside the reels and also substitute for all symbols.

The Quantumeter charges through wins and provides bonuses at four designated charge levels which trigger in turn every 25 winning symbols collected. These bonuses include:

Removal of selected symbols (25 winning symbols)

Transformation of symbols (50 winning symbols)

Addition of Mega symbols (75 winning symbols)

Addition of Mega Gargantuan Wild symbols (max charge, 100 winning symbols)

Mega symbols take up a greater number of positions than regular symbols and can appear in sizes 2x2 to 7x7. For cluster wins, Mega symbols count as a single symbol, however, they have a randomized value that is used to calculate the prize given.
