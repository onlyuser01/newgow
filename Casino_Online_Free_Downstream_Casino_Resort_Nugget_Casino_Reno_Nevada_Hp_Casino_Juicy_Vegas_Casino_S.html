SBC Events is proud to present the latest major stop on the gambling industry’s calendar with SBC Summit Rio now confirmed for March 5-7, 2024, and to be held at the Windsor Barra Hotel, a five-star venue worthy of the industry gathering.

Brazil has a population of more than 217 million people and it is now in the midst of rapid reregulation that has enabled many companies to pursue growth in the jurisdiction. The potential remains significant, and SBC Events is hosting the Rio de Janeiro event to help steer and guide stakeholders.
