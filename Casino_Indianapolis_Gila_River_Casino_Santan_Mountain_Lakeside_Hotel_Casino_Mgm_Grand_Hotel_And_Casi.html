Tireless Work, Unique Solutions

Chief executive officer and company founder Richard Vikström spoke about the tireless work of the team that has generated “solutions that are far different from other hosting companies”. He also praised their innovative solutions that can cater to the one-of-a-kind needs of individual clients. Speaking about the expansion on the Louisiana market, Vikström said he was “glad” to operate in the new state. He also expressed the company’s intention to maintain its “steady growth”, expand its database of customers, and reach fresh markets in the US.

The company’s chief technology officer and founder Victor Jerlin called the new Louisiana development “a great achievement”, mentioning the state’s “wealth of opportunities.” Jerlin also spoke about the important growth in the “lucrative” gaming sector here. The fact that Internet Vikings will be “at the forefront” will enable them to provide a vast array of solutions to a variety of different businesses, concluded Jerlin.

Internet Vikings currently has a staff of more than 70 specialists working from five different countries. Their portfolio of solutions features cloud hosting services across important jurisdictions worldwide, along with search engine optimization hosting, hosting colocation, and managed hosting.
