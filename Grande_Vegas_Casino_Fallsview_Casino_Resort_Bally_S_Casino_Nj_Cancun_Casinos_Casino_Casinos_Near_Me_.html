Gaming studio offering value-driven iGaming experiences for a variety of global regulated markets Wizard Games has released Towers of Olympus. The new slot has a Greek gods theme and it is packed with Free Spins, Super Spins, and other cool features waiting to be discovered. NeoGames’s subsidiary players are invited to discover the intriguing story of Zeus while spinning the reels on the 5×4 grid and saying hello to other legendary Greek deities.

Trigger Super Spins with Blue Mystery Towers

The game’s graphics have been designed with the Greek mythology theme in mind, with beautiful details, players will come across a variety of symbols ranging from low-paying symbols like card suits paying up to 1x the initial bet for a full combo, mid-paying symbols including helmets, shields, and horns paying up to 2x the bet for coming across five identical symbols, and high-paying symbols like Hera and Zeus. The two aforementioned gods pay 10x the initial bet for a full combination.

Players who manage to get a minimum of one or up to two Blue Mystery Towers symbols land on any of the reels will see them turn into random symbols, mix with the rest of the symbols, and trigger winning combos. When Blue Mystery Towers pop on the reels, they change into Golden Mystery Towers and engage the Super Spins round.

During it, Golden Mystery Towers are seen nudging in order to perfectly match the size of the highest symbol. Players receive a number of Super Spins based on the actual number of rows on the reel. With each new Super Spin, the Golden Mystery Towers shrink in size, engaging a mystery symbol.

Scatters Engage Free Spins Mode

The new game that “is attractively presented” according to Wizard Games’ managing director Joey Hurtado also features a special Free Spins mode. With between three and five scatters, players are rewarded with eight, 12, or 15 free spins, along with a Blue Mystery Tower that is guaranteed to pop on every new spin. Lucky players can also trigger Super Spins during the Free Spins

According to Hurtado, the slot’s Boost option gives players extra chances to enter the Free Spins mode. The Boost feature increases the initial bet by 50% and places more scatters on the reels. Players in some jurisdictions worldwide will have the chance to jump straight into Free Spins mode via a dedicated Buy Bonus option.
