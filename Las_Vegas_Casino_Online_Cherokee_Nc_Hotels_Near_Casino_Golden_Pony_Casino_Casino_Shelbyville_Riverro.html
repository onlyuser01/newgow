Boyd Launches Cashless Table Game Trial with Aristocrat

Aristocrat Gaming, part of the global games leader Aristocrat Leisure, together with Boyd Gaming announced on Wednesday the successful launch of a cashless payment solution for table games. The new solution, Boyd Pay Wallet, is powered by Aristocrat’s digital wallet technology and the field trial is being conducted in partnership with Genesis Gaming.

The field trial of Boyd Pay Wallet is taking place at Boyd Gaming’s Aliante Casino Hotel and Spa in Las Vegas, Nevada. By integrating into Boyd Gaming’s B Connected app, Boyd Pay Wallet will allow players to use their mobile devices to fund table games without the need to withdraw cash, redeem chips or tickets at the cages.

The Cashless Solution Offers Unique Functionalities, Reduces Risk

Once a table game player logs securely into the B Connected app, they can use the Boyd Pay Wallet to buy into the game and use traditional casino chips to play. Once the player decides to end their play, they can either collect the casino chips or use the app to transfer their funds directly to the cashless Boyd Pay Wallet and there’s no need for the player to visit a cage.

Moreover, Oasis 360 casino management system with TrackIT that is powered by Genesis enables players to wager using a QR code generated by Boyd Pay whenever a gaming session is started. This system also allows player redemption by using Boyd Pay Wallet which further enhances the cashless experience.

By seamlessly integrating TrackIT, Boyd’s digital player club card, Boyd’s mobile app and Boyd’s digital wallet, the operator can view the player’s activity. At the same time, this unique integration reduces risk, as well as costs and supports responsible gambling.
