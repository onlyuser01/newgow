5 Frozen Charms Megaways review

Theme and Style

5 Frozen Charms Megaways has a festive leprechaun theme.

Players will explore a snowy Irish realm of fantasy where they will meet a mischievous leprechaun. If players have the luck of the Irish, they might just find some frosty beers and four-leaf clovers.

This slot will suit players who enjoy Irish mythology themes.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack of 5 Frozen Charms Megaways brings to mind a music box with its jovial high notes. However, the loop of this tune is quite short which may prompt some players to reach for the mute button.

The setting of this slot is a snowy landscape with a hint of greenery in the foreground. A smiling leprechaun poses on one side of the reels holding a snowball in his hand. His classic green suit is embellished with tiny golden four-leaf clovers and his hat is dusted with snow.

On the reels, players will see pink mushrooms, beer steins, and letters and numbers decorated with Celtic designs. The bright color palette of the animation gives this slot an eye-catching look and a cheerful vibe.

Overall, the soundtrack and animation create an entertainingly chilly twist on a classic Irish mythology theme.

Gameplay

5 Frozen Charms Megaways uses the Megaways system. This means that the size and number of symbols on the reel vary for each spin. As many as seven symbols can appear on reels 1 to 6.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting with the leftmost reel.

The Tumble feature means that winning symbols will disappear, allowing new symbols to fall in from above. Tumbles continue until no more winning combinations are possible.

The Ante Bet feature allows players to add a bet multiplier that can alter the way the game behaves. If a multiplier of 25x is selected, the player’s odds of triggering the Free Spins feature are naturally doubled. This is due to a higher number of Bonus symbols appearing on average. However, a bet multiplier of 20x will result in normal play.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Appearing on reels 2-5, the Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

If a Wild symbol forms part of a winning combination a randomly selected multiplier is applied to the winnings. Multiplier values range from 1x to 40x.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land 3 or more Bonus symbols anywhere on the reels. Bonus symbols do not disappear during Tumble sequences.

Prior to the start of the Free Spins feature players must choose one of seven versions of the feature. The options are as follows:

25 free spins + 2x, 3x, 5x Wild multiplier

20 free spins + 3x, 5x, 8x Wild multiplier

15 free spins + 5x, 8x, 10x Wild multiplier

13 free spins + 8x, 10x, 15x Wild multiplier

10 free spins + 10x, 15x, 30x Wild multiplier

6 free spins + 15x, 30x, 40x Wild multiplier

Mystery number of free spins + Mystery multiplier range

Throughout the Free Spins feature, a random multiplier from the selected range is applied to winnings that include a Wild symbol.

If players land 3 additional Bonus symbols during the Free Spins feature, the feature is retriggered. The number of free spins awarded will match the original round.

Special reels are used during the Free Spins feature.
