SCCG to Invest in Ronin Sport and Help it Grow in the US

SCCG Venture Fund, an alternative investment fund that focuses on increasing value and distributing profit through investing in gaming opportunities, has opted to invest in Ronin Sport (also known as roninsport.io).

SCCG Will Support Ronin Sport

Ronin Sport is a platform that collects information and compiles it into high-quality TV guides for live sports. The platform first joined forces with SCCG in April, when the two inked a partnership deal. As per the deal, SCCG agreed to help Ronin expand its presence in the North American region.

Stephen Crystal, the chief executive officer and founder of SCCG Management, now confirmed that his company has decided to deepen its ties with Ronin. SCCG Management’s SCCG Venture Fund will, therefore, provide the data company with a large investment. SCCG is a specialist when it comes to investing in gambling companies and will provide a critical help to Ronin.

As a recipient of SCCG Venture Fund, Ronin will also receive SCCG’s expertise on how to expand its business. SCCG Management is a consultancy that specializes in all kinds of gambling companies and will provide the sports data company with crucial insights and advice.

In an official statement, SCCG promised to support Ronin Venture’s growth. The consultancy vowed to use every tool in its arsenal to help Ronin become a powerhouse in the North American market.

SCCG will provide insights, business development and advisory services to grow the company within different North American sports market segments. SCCG Management statement

SCCG – A Seasoned Veteran in Gambling

Ronin is a fairly new name in sports data and sports betting. The company was founded in January 2020 and offers live TV streaming data for many major sports events.

SCCG, on the other hand, is a seasoned veteran when it comes to investing in the gambling sector. The company has worked together with many partners. According to its description, it specializes in sports betting, iGaming, sports marketing, affiliate marketing, technology, intellectual property protection, product commercialization, esports, capital formation, M&A, joint ventures, casino management, and governmental and legal affairs for the casino and iGaming industry.

CEO Stephen Crystal is an experienced industry heavyweight with over two decades of experience with gambling companies and gambling-related businesses. He has previously headed numerous companies in the sector and has provided advice to even more.

In November last year, SCCG teamed up with another sports data brand, namely Data Sports Group. Upon its entry into the US market, the data company appointed SCCG Management as its official marketing partner.
