Cai Hong is a 2018 RTG release with an interesting Oriental theme at the center. The 5 x 3 game relies on the idea that rainbows bring good luck and fortune to those who come across them. Cai Hong: Game Symbols and Payouts

The reels count 30 fixed pay lines. This means all of the lines are simultaneously active. When a minimum of three identical symbols run in an unbroken sequence across a line starting with the utmost left, players score a win.

The low-paying symbols are represented by the standard playing card symbols (As, Ks, Qs, Jx, 10s, and 9s). The top wins are scored when the animals populating the reels are seen landing across a line together with gold coins. For example, five tiger symbols pay 750 while the same five tiger symbols together with the gold coins symbol pay 1,500. The golden ingot is the highest-paying symbol during the base game.

The wild is represented by the God of Wealth. It works by replacing all of the other symbols while doubling the value of all win-lines generated by it. While the wild does not come with a specific value, it helps players complete extra combinations, especially since it is a stacked wild symbol. This means it has the potential of filling any random reel he is seen landing on.

The free games will be played at the chosen lines and bet corresponding to the triggering game.

Bonus Picks

Prior to the free games feature, two, three, or four bonus picks will be handed in exchange for three, four, or five triggering scatter symbols.
