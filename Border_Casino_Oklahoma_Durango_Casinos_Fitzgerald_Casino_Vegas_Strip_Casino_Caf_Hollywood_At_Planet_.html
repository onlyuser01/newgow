Arsenal Need to Take This Game Seriously

Although the rivalry between these two isn’t the fiercest possible, this is a London Derby and such, it’s bound to be exciting to watch. All of the recent Arsenal vs West Ham duels were very fun, even though only one team (Arsenal) dominated most of the games.

The Premier League leaders are expected to win again, but there are a couple of things that need to worry Mikel Arteta and his boys ahead of Monday’s game. First of all, they got some problems with injuries, with their most notable absentee being Gabriel Jesus, one of their key strikers.

Further, a number of Arsenal players have only recently returned to London after spending several weeks in Qatar with their national teams.

West Ham Desperate for Points

Last season, the Hammers finished in the seventh spot in the Premier League. This season, they’re nowhere near that spot. They’re currently sitting in the no.14 position with just one point more than the nearest team from the relegation zone.

They’re not doing great, but they do have a very good roster in 2022. They’re strong in literally all positions on the pitch, they just need to start showing it. Winning points against Arsenal won’t be easy, but they will certainly give their best to do it.

Arsenal vs West Ham Predictions

We can smell a surprise – favored Arsenal aren’t going to beat West Ham. But are they going to lose? We don’t think that either. Instead, the best the Hammers can get from this game is a draw.
