NetBet, a company that focuses on operating the eponymous online casino brand, has expanded its content library through a well-positioned partnership with BGaming, a globally acclaimed supplier of iGaming content.

Commenting on this opportunity, BGaming Head of Sales Olga Levshina said that the company was excited to be helping NetBet add to what is an outstanding library of slots, crash games, card games, and casual experiences for its players.

"And all games undergo testing by real players during the development process, which helps us better understand players and create the best gaming experience. Thanks to this partnership, even more players will be able to enjoy our products," Levshina concluded.

NetBet PR Manager Claudia Georgevici was similarly pleased with the development and welcomed BGaming’s range of titles and games that are now adding to the operator’s continuously increasing portfolio of titles and opportunities.

NetBet has been too on a tear, with the company securing a number of prominent partnerships with a range of studios and developers, including Spinomenal, Spadegaming, and CT Interactive.
