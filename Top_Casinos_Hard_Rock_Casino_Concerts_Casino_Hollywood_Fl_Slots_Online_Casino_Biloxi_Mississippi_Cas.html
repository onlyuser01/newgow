BetVictor announced that it is currently looking for several executives for its new brand. According to the operator, talkSPORT Bet is in need of a brand head, a display executive, an acquisition executive and an engagement executive, among others.

Emma Heyde, BetVictor’s talent partner, added that the company is also in need of an affiliate manager. Heyde concluded that talkSPORT BET will be launching just in time for the highly-anticipated soccer tournament. According to her, the new brand will seek to become synonymous with the World Cup.

TalkSPORT Bet Is an Exciting Joint Venture

TalkSPORT Bet is a joint venture between BetVictor and the renowned radio station talkSPORT. The two companies first joined forces two months ago when they agreed to ink a white-label agreement. The new brand will leverage BetVictor’s betting capabilities and expertise and talkSPORT’s knowledge of the soccer field to create new engaging experiences for sports bettors. As announced by BetVictor, the new brand will combine “the great things about talkSPORT radio” with the pristine sports betting experience” the operator is known for.

Brent Almedia, chief commercial officer of BetVictor, further elaborated that the new brand will allow players to enjoy sports in more ways than one and engage with their favorite pro tournaments. He added that talkSPORT Bet would also retain BetVictor’s famous commitment to safer gambling – something that remains an increasingly high priority within the industry.

The new white-label partnership is yet another expansion of BetVictor’s footprint. The latter company already has similar agreements through which it operates the Parimatch platform in the UK, together with Heart Bingo and BildBet.
