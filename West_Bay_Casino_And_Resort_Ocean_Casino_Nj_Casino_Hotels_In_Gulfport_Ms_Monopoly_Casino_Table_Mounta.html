The new game captures the spooky spirit of the season while providing many ways to win

Armadillo Studios has launched another atmospheric game called Franksylvania. Coming ahead of Halloween, the new slot will guide players through a vampire’s haunted mansion.

Franksylvania Metrics

Rows: 4

Reels: 5

Paylines: 25

Volatility: High

RTP: 96%/94.17%

Min/Max Bet: 0.25/100

The Game Is Packed Full of Features

Reflecting the game’s theme, Frank the vampire can shapeshift into a bat and transform random reels into wild symbols. Players can get up to four wilds, boosting their potential to win.

The host will also amaze guests with his Colossal Reels powers that allow him to fuse adjacent reels to create 2×2, 3×3 or 4×4 symbols. In addition, he can upgrade lower-paying symbols to higher-paying ones.

This is not all the game offers, however, as collecting four Blood Vial symbols will trigger Bloody Party spin, showering players with bonuses.

An additional feature to look out for is the Scary Bonus symbols. Landing on three of those triggers Trail Bonus mode, during which players will be taken to a haunted graveyard packed with bonuses.

Armadillo Studios Continues to Innovate

The launch of the new game follows the launches of other exciting slots. Previously, Armadillo Studios released Joker Classic, Tiki Time Exotic Wilds, Fairy Fantasy Exotic Wilds, Aladdin’s Rollover Respins, Mythological Mayhem Supreme Streaks and Hot Hot Honey, among others games.

The arrival of Franksylvania comes just in time for the spooky season. David Stoveld, the studio’s chief operating officer, explained that the game is Armadillo Studios’ own twist on the Dracula story. The game, according to him, seeks to provide paranormal thrills to players while providing them with chances to win money.

With Frank the vampire, or the bat, as their guide, players experience the magic of the paranormal and unlock a world of mysteries and potential big wins, coupled with best-in-class visuals and sounds. Our team is thrilled to present this supernatural journey that offers an adventure like no other to our worldwide customers and their players. David Stoveld, COO, Armadillo Studios
