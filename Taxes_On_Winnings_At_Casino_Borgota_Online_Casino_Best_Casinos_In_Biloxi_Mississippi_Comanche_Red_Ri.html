German Champions Putting Their Hopes in Thomas Tuchel

Thomas Tuchel is the new coach of Bayern Munich. He made his debut in the dugout of the German champion on the first day of April when we saw his boys beat Dortmund 4-2 in Der Klassiker. The win has given a massive boost of confidence to Bayern’s fans who now believe their boys can with the Champions League.

Another reason behind their optimism is that Tuchel is a manager who’s already led one team to the title. It happened two years ago when he was in charge of Chelsea. The Tuchel-led Blues won the trophy after defeating Manchester City in the final.
