Help the Mischievous Cats Protect Their Treasures

The main action is focused on Mafdet, Sekhmet, and Bastet, the cute-looking cats that were named after their Ancient Egypt deity ancestors. The lovely kittens are, as expected, equally mischievous, so watching their adventures on the reels is anything but dull. Join the lovely creatures with godly powers in their attempt to protect their beloved treasures from Apep the serpent. Watch them turn from kittens and adult cats with the help of the Expanding Wilds feature within the game.

The designers of the game spent their time creating a series of excellent visual elements. The action takes place on the grid that has an Ancient Egypt tomb as a backdrop, with pillars that are covered in hieroglyphics with various cat symbols.

The three kittens are seen relaxing on top of the grid, letting players easily keep a close eye on their progress. This allows players to anticipate the number of kittens that will soon be turned into the much-anticipated Expanding Wilds. The way the small cats turn into adult cats and interact with Apep is another proof of the gaming studio’s creed to focus on “entertainment, fun, and quality,“ which it calls “its DNA.“ When a player manages to cover all available five reels with Expanding Wilds, the rewards are even more generous.

The Free Spins Round Brings More Action to the Table

Once the Free Spins are activated, players will watch the reels expanding from the standard 5×3 grind to a new 5×4 grid. A Multiplier will also be applied to the grid, as a means of opening a locked treasure chest packed with mouth-watering wins.

Play’n GO’s games ambassador George Olekszy called the new game “a really fun game to play” with crystal clear goals and the chance for players to watch their progress in real-time.
