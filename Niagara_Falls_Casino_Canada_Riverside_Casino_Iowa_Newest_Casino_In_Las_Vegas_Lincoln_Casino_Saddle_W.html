The new collaboration brings premium Prospect Gaming content to QTech Games' platform, allowing the company to expand its reach

The leading Asian-facing game distributor, QTech Games, announced a new collaboration, providing a boost to its offering with new and engaging premium content. Confirmation about the new deal came Monday and sees QTech Games integrate more engaging content from 1X2 Network’s leading iGaming brand, Prospect Gaming.

Per the new collaboration with the latest dedicated iGaming studio, QTech Games will deliver new content to existing and emerging markets. Prospect Gaming’s content stands out by combining classic slot retail feel with innovative mechanics. This unique technique helps deliver engaging and thrilling experiences for online customers.

Known as an iGaming arm of the leading 1X2 Network, Prospect Gaming delivers an extensive range of online gambling games. It complements other studios from the company such as Iron Dog Studio, as well as 1X2gaming, reaffirming the potential of 1X2 Network’s wide range of content.

Alex Ratcliffe, 1X2 Network’s CPO, explained that Prospect Gaming delivers engaging titles that stand out with unique features. “The Prospector Wilds series has hit the ground ‘digging’ and illustrates the benchmark quality that operators have come to expect from the 1X2Network,” explained Ratcliffe in conclusion.

“With Prospect Gaming, we were looking to round-out the multi-studio offering that is the 1X2Network with a core bricks-and-mortar-to-digitally-focused studio.“ Alex Ratcliffe, CPO at 1X2 Network

While the collaboration with QTech Games represents an important step for the iGaming studio, the company remains focused on delivering immersive and engaging experiences by developing unique products for the online gambling vertical. Talented developers, designers and artists with significant experience within the iGaming vertical back the team at Prospect Gaming.

Prospect Gaming Titles Deliver Unique Experiences

Daniel Long, QTech Games’ CCO, spoke about the unique games Prospect Games delivers by combining classic retail slots with new and engaging features. “Together, we can continue to raise the bar and shape a localized experience for global players across varied cultures,” outlined QTech Games’ CCO. Finally, he said that the company is looking forward to seeing how games from the studio will be received, while Prospect Gaming and 1X2 Network continue to grow their global footprint.

“Prospect Gaming offers something old and something new, with some fresh twists on old retail classics, as eloquently exemplified by the release of their debut title, Prospector Wilds.“ Daniel Long, CCO at QTech Games

Joining forces with QTech Games will enable Prospect Gaming to reach new audiences. At the same time, the collaboration strengthens QTech’s platform, expanding its offering.
