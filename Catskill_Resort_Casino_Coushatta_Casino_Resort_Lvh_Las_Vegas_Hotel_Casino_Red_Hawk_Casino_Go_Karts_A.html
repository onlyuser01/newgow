Evolution has revealed a new Live Casino game inspired by MONOPOLY. MONOPOLY Big Baller is a fresh live game show-style bingo game that is now available to company partners. The game comes with both a live game host and automated ball-drawing.

All Aboard with MONOPOLY Big Baller

Once again, MR MONOPOLY will be present in the game thanks to augmented reality and a dedicated 3D environment where the mustachioed gent makes his entry. Evolution will feature multipliers that can influence gameplay and significantly increase the bottom-line payout to players as well.

MONOPOLY Big Baller is reminiscent of the base MONOPOLY Live Casino game developed by Evolution. It features dice rolls, Free Space, Chance and Go to Jail spaces, and so much more. In Big Baller, though, players come aboard a riverboat cruise where a ball drawing machine will be the one enlivening the gameplay and action.

Players will then have to make successful combinations of numbers in lines – the more the better. There will be all sorts of bonuses interwoven into the gameplay to make the surprise element felt across sessions and make people feel more involved and entertained.

A dedicated Bonus Game will evoke MR MONOPOLY in augmented reality with the man walking across a 3D virtual MONOPOLY board. All in all, Big Baller reflects Evolution’s ambition to continue improving the casino experience by finding new and creative ways to build new products. Commenting on this launch, Evolution CPO Todd Haushalter said:

Mr. MONOPOLY is back big time! We have a sizeable segment of players who love bingo- and lottery-style bouncing ball games. Our Mega Ball game, for example, is extremely popular. Evolution CPO Todd Haushalter

Universally Recognized and Loved Franchise

The new game is thematically and visually very different, which makes it stand apart. The Bonus Round is something to definitely look for. Part of the game’s success is also predicated on the prominence of the MONOPOLY brand. The premise of the board is well understood and loved the world over, Haushalter added.

That is why the new game is bound to appeal to both MONOPOLY and bingo lovers. Presently, the MONOPOLY brand and intellectual property are owned by Hasbro which licenses it to Evolution.
