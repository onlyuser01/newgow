The new game has arrived at the perfect time for some bonus summer fun, Armadillo Studios’ COO said

Armadillo Studios has launched a refreshing new slot game called Tiki Time Exotic Wilds. The game is the fourth installment in the studio’s famous Exotic Wild series and an engaging new way to enjoy gaming.

A fairly traditional-looking slot with fruit symbols, Tiki Time Exotic Wilds nevertheless features a number of innovative features to dazzle players.

Collect Tiki to Win Big

Armadillo Studios’ latest release features 243 paylines. In addition to that, the game sports the titular Exotic Wilds mechanic that offers three types of exotic wilds represented by Tiki symbols.

The Violet Tiki will yield a multiplier from 2x to 5x. The Green Tiki, meanwhile, can yield a multiplier of between 3x and 7x. Finally, the Super Tiki (a mix of green and violet) carries a multiplier of between 5x and 10x. Once the player triggers a Tiki, it will expand to cover the entire reel, helping to score winning combinations.

By landing on three or more Free Spins symbols, players can also enter the bonus round where they can earn huge prizes. During free spins mode, Special Symbols will provide an additional spin while enhancing all active multipliers by up to 20x.

Before the free spins round, players will be asked to select a volatility level. The Multiplier Boost mode is the medium volatility version of the round that offers 12 spins and enhanced multipliers. The Extra Exotic mode, meanwhile, is a high-volatility version where players will have 10 spins. During this iteration of the bonus game, players can collect ice cubes and umbrellas to increase their chances of triggering wild symbols and prizes. Finally, the Extreme Exotic mode offers, as the name suggests, 8 extreme volatility spins.

Players can also buy free spins in markets where this is permitted.

Armadillo Studios’ Exotic Wilds Series Grows

The new release is a part of the Exotic Wilds series and follows the releases of Fairy Fantasy Exotic Wilds, Fortune Dragon Queen Exotic Wilds and Queen of Egypt Exotic Wilds. The new game is an exciting addition to Armadillo Studios’ portfolio that will join the supplier’s lineup of high-quality games.

Armadillo Studios’ chief operating officer, David Stoveld, commented on the timely release of the title, saying that it perfectly encapsulates the magic of the summer season.
