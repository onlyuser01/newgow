The Communitas Awards represent an international effort to recognize individuals, organizations and businesses with outstandingly unselfish business practices

Scientific Games, a leading provider of lottery products and solutions, has announced that it has won two 2023 Communitas Awards for excellence in social responsibility. The awards are a testament to the company’s dedication to environmental and ethical responsibility, as well as to its commitment to protecting players from harm.

The first award Scientific Games earned is the 2023 Communitas Leadership in Ethical and Environmental Responsibility Award which cements the provider as a company with high ethical standards in its social, economic and environmental activities. These activities, Scientific Games noted, include stringent game security protocols to inclusive hiring practices and sustainable production methods.

The second award Scientific Games grabbed is the 2023 Communitas Award for Excellence in Corporate Social Responsibility for Responsible Gaming. The second award is a recognition of the supplier’s Healthy Play program and 2022 National Responsible Gaming Study.

Scientific Games is notably the first lottery provider to earn certifications in Quality Management, Information Security and Anti-Bribery from the International Standards Organization, cementing it as a company with a strong ethical sense that seeks to develop a sustainable business in the 130 jurisdictions it serves. The company also boasts a Responsible Gaming Supplier Certification from the World Lottery Association.

The Communitas Awards represent an international effort to recognize individuals, organizations and businesses with outstandingly unselfish business practices.

Scientific Games Is Proud That Its Efforts Got Recognized

Scientific Games representatives commented on the two new awards with Stephen Richardson, the company’s chief administrative and compliance officer, expressing his excitement. Richardson pointed out that the company’s daily operational activities are “built upon a bedrock of ethical, environmental and social responsibility.”

Scientific Games upholds the highest standards of corporate governance in everything we do, so our lottery customers can feel confident using our games and technologies to fund their good causes across the world. Stephen Richardson, chief administrative and compliance officer, Scientific Games

Dena Rosenzweig, Scientific Games’ chief legal officer, also shared her thoughts on the matter, saying that the company has “woven stringent environmental, social and governance protocols into its daily practices in many areas,” thus shaping a gold standard for the lottery industry.

Rosenzweig concluded: “Now as we celebrate our 50th anniversary this year, we are truly honored to receive recognition for our work.”
