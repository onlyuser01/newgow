The solution allows clients to easily access sports streaming. Integration of iGameMedia’s ONEPlatform solution is seamless, intuitive and easy, requiring no lengthy timelines and change management. The innovative solution empowers the currently popular inplay betting product used by many companies today.

The integrated solution powers clients with a “content gateway” to a plethora of sports content. Furthermore, it allows the industry to tackle a variety of challenges, such as the mapping of IDs from different API sources.

To that end, iGameMedia leverages iGame ONEMap, a product that utilizes a single API instance that exposes the respective IDs of all third-party vendors associated with a live event. Thanks to the clever use of AI and ML tech, the solution is fully automated and highly accurate.

The mapping tool also tackles a challenge such as non-standard team naming conventions. Usually, typical machine processing fails to identify PSG and Paris Saint Germain as the same team, sometimes causing unmapped events that require manual intervention.
