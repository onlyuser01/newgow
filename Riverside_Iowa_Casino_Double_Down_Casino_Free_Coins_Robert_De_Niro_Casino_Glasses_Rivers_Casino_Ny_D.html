The freshly inked partnership will see True Geordie participate in a GGPoker-exclusive tournament and represent the brand for the WSOP in London

The leading poker room, GGPoker, announced a new partnership with one of the UK’s most popular influencers and YouTube stars, Brian ‘True Geordie’ Davis. Details regarding the recent tie-up emerged Wednesday and will see True Geordie collaborate with GGPoker for exciting initiatives.

Lauded as a “significant milestone for both parties,” the deal will kick off on this Thursday, June 8, 2023, with a weekly poker tournament. True Geordie invites its growing fanbase to join him for this virtual poker tournament where they are expected to benefit from unique and unforgettable experiences.

Starting this Thursday, the exclusive GGPoker tournament will run every Thursday at 6:00 PM BST. Under the terms of the new collaboration, the tournament will run through August 31, 2023. Players that tap into the tournament will not only face unique experiences but have a shot at winning huge prizes and can even try to claim a bounty for True Geordie’s head.

Besides the aforementioned tournament, the new deal will also see True Geordie represent GGPoker for an upcoming World Series of Poker (WSOP) event planned for London. At the event scheduled to start on July 27, 2023, True Geordie will appear as GGPoker’s ambassador, allowing him to enter games with opponents that can include poker fans as well as seasoned pros.

The Deal Marks an Exciting Moment for True Geordie, GGPoker

Angela Martin, GGPoker’s head of marketing UK & Ireland, was delighted to see the YouTube sensation and podcaster join the company’s growing team. She welcomed True Geordie to the company and acknowledged that he has a unique personality and exceptional talent.

Martin did not miss saying that True Geordie will help deliver unique experiences for GGPoker players and poker fans alike. Finally, she said: “We can’t wait to see him engage with our vibrant community and bring his unique style to the tables.”

“We are delighted to welcome Brian ‘True Geordie’ Davis to the GGPoker family.“ Angela Martin, head of marketing UK & Ireland at GGPoker

GGPoker’s latest deal once again reaffirms the platform’s commitment to delivering unparalleled experiences for its growing customer base. At the same time, the poker network will benefit from True Geordie’s strong presence in social media, helping it reach new audiences.

“We are absolutely delighted to work with the number-one poker brand in the world!“ Brian ‘True Geordie’ Davis

Commenting on the topic, Davis was similarly delighted to team up with GGPoker. He explained that his experience will play an important role in the new partnership. Finally, Davis noted that he is looking forward to engaging with poker fans with the help of GGPoker’s ground-breaking online poker technology.
