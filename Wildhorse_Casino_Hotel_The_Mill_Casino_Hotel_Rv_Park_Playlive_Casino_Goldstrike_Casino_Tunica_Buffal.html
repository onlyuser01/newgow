Free Spins and Wheel of Fortune

When a minimum of three book symbols are seen landing on any position on the reels, players receive 10 free spins. Prior to the bonus round being initiated, players must spin a wheel of fortune. The landing symbol on the wheel will turn into a special symbol during the free spins round. During the bonus round, the special symbol will automatically expand and cover all three positions on the reel provided enough symbols can activate a win.

When at least three book symbols are seen popping up anywhere on the reels while the free spins feature is still in play, the bonus feature is retriggered for an endless number of free spins and greater prizes.
