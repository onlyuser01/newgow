Provider of casino gaming products and player loyalty solutions Everi Holdings announced it has signed a new agreement with the British Columbia Lottery Corporation (BCLC) to extend their partnership into anti-money laundering (AML).

‘Adapting to Customer Needs’

Under the new arrangement, Everi Holdings will provide the BCLC with its Everi Compliance AML solution to support the gaming and online operations of the Canadian operator and ensure compliance with the AML laws in the country.

Darren Simmons, executive vice president and FinTech Business Leader at Everi Holdings, outlined the supplier’s commitment “to adapting to customer needs” and creating “exciting new opportunities” in line with “industry changes.”

“BCLC has prioritized high levels of compliance as part of the continued expansion of their fast-growing gaming operations in casinos and online platforms across British Columbia,” Simmons continued, delighted that the BCLC has chosen Everi and its RegTech solutions that “are designed for a wide variety of applications” to support its critical AML program.

Everi Holdings will provide support for BCLC’s operations across lottery, casino, bingo, and online gaming as the corporation offers British Columbians gaming on behalf of the province. Providing AML services to over 600 gaming properties, Everi’s software is the most widely used AML solution available for the gaming industry.
