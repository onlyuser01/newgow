The leading entertainment, gaming, dining and shopping destination in the US, Mohegan Sun, announced its plans for a Super Bowl LVII viewing party

The viewing party, labeled “The Big Game,” will take place at Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook and starts on Sunday, February 12, 2023, the date planned for the kickoff of the Super Bowl LVII. The game is set to start at 6:30 pm, although the two teams that will be competing are yet to be confirmed.

Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook will offer plenty of amenities for the visitors, such as a Big Game Buffet offering a drinks menu and exciting pre-game and live betting options. Moreover, visitors to the outlet will be able to enjoy the football action on a 140-foot-wide video wall.

The organizer of the event confirmed that reservations for The Big Game have already started. Visitors that want to reserve a spot can either visit Mohegan Sun’s website or call the venue to save a seat at Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook for the date of the game. According to the company, every visitor of the exclusive viewing party will receive a “free commemorative gift.”

Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook Offers Plenty of Amenities

Overall, Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook uses an area of 11,000 square feet. The betting location offers more than 220 seats, live-teller windows, as well as 39 self-betting kiosks. Additional amenities include a full dining menu and drinks. Beverage lovers can enjoy imported, domestic and local beers, as well as cocktails such as “Ricky Vaughn” which includes Lime Sour, Cointreau, Cazadores Blanco tequila, Fresh Jalapeño and a Tajin rim.

Just ahead of the Super Bowl, Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook announced a special promotion. The promotion will be valid between February 6 and February 12, which is the gameday. Sports fans and bettors who place NFL Same Game Parlay (SGP) wager of $50 or more at any sportsbook kiosk of 5/1 odds or longer, will receive a unique gift which can be a BBQ grill set, Bluetooth speaker, cooler or a different type of prize.

Besides the Super Bowl, fans of MMA can also enjoy a good time at the Mohegan Sun FanDuel Sportsbook. Those fans can visit the venue on February 11 and enjoy the long-anticipated fight between Islam Makhachev and Alexander Volkanovski for the Light heavyweight belt.
