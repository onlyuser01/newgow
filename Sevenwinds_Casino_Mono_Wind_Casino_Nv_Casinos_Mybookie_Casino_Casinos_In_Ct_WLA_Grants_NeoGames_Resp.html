NeoGames Meets WLA RG Standards

Ultimately, the Association’s RG certification seeks to reduce the impact and risks of problem gambling. To complete the RG certification, the iLottery operations from NeoGames went through an assessment. The process was completed by an independent monitor who sought to determine whether the company’s operations comply fully with the WLA standards for responsible gambling.

The assessment was completed within multiple different verticals including the evaluation of advertising and marketing communications, employee programs and research. Finally, the independent evaluation concluded that NeoGames’ operations and platform comply fully with the RG framework set by the WLA.
