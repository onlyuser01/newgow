Reels of Fortune Slot Review

Reels of Fortune online slot is a Woohoo Games game that is inspired by classic slot mechanics but has put a nice polish on the traditional fruit experience. With a highly polished one-line and three-reel grid, the game packs a surprising amount of fun in one neat package. Players will enjoy multipliers, a Bonus Wheel Feature, and hourly, daily, and Super Hot Drop Jackpot that will be available to you once you get started.

The game sports a nice user interface and a modern look, even if the main game mechanics are a throwback to simpler times. With a 500x multiplier to scoop up a juicy prize and flexible betting limits, you will definitely be in for a treat with this one! Play it for free or for real money, but before you do – make sure to read our full Reels of Fortune slot review.

Theme, Storyline, and Graphics: Simple Yet Captivating

Reels of Fortune has built a solid game that is based on a three-reel and one-line gameplay. The slot is designed as a luxurious one-arm bandit machine with cogwheels discreetly spinning in the background. The payable is neatly displayed on the left-hand side making it easier for players to know what to expect.

Reels of Fortunes is about a quaint and transparent experience. The jackpots, denominated as Mini, Minor, Major, Mega, and Vegas, will be displayed in real-time with their actual sums at the time of playing. The grid is filled with highly-stylized symbols of traditional classic slots, such as cherries, sevens, bars, and diamonds that serve as a wild.

This is correct, although a classic game, Reels of Fortune has not shied away from integrating a wild symbol to help boost your potential to win from the game. The reels spin with a pleasant soft thud and there is parlor music playing in the background adding to the immersiveness of the game.

Reels of Fortune Game Symbols and Payouts

Reels of Fortune features a simple payable and will pay for combinations of two or three symbols. The symbols you can expect to find here include multi-tier bars, a single-tier bar, cherries, sevens, and of course, the wild diamond-shaped symbol. You will notably get the biggest value out of getting three wilds on the payline for a $500 payout.

There is also a special Bonus Symbol that is shaped like a golden coin with the words Bonus across it. This symbol only appears in the third reel, and it will unlock the Bonus Wheel. The Bonus Wheel will offer multipliers between 5x and 500x, but you may also trigger a second Bonus Wheel game which features jackpots, ranging from Mini to Vegas.

All payouts are awarded from left to right with the central row being the one where the winning combination is formed. Simple and easy to follow, Reels of Fortune’s gameplay and symbols will guarantee that you are having a fun time with one of Wohoo Games’ best slot machines.

Sounds and Animations

In terms of visuals and audio, Reels of Fortune is attentive. Reels of Fortune Gameplay

The gameplay here is designed for accessibility. The paytable is already displayed when you load the game, adding to the ability to quickly find what combinations are worthwhile and what to avoid. Although it all comes down to chance, it’s still a nice addition.

The play buttons are visible and easy to navigate. You can click on spin to send the reels for a round. Players can also choose the Autoplay feature that has an unlimited number of rounds, but we recommend picking one of the pre-configured values instead. You can select the value of the bet adjusting it to be anything from 0.20 to 100 per bet.

Other than that, there is more information that you can find about the game in the upper left corner of the screen where the game features, payouts, and symbols are explained in further detail.
