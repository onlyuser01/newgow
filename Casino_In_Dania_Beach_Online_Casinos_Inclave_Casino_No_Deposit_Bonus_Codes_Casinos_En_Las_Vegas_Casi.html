Inspired by Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde’s Story

Clearly inspired by the popular Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde story, the new slot carries gamers on a mysterious journey through the beautiful and mysterious cobbled streets in London. The graphics are excellent and the atmosphere is maintained with the help of the nicely designed symbols including science kits, serum bottles, books, and, of course, the Hyde and Jekyll symbols.

As expected, the game features cascading symbols that pop up after every Megaways win, leading up to some mouthwatering potential wins thanks to the HYDE Super Spin with high volatility. The feature can be triggered on any random spin, where it acts by removing symbols, boosting the Multipliers, and increasing the minimum Megaways during a single super spin.

Players who manage to come across a minimum of four bonus symbols are sent straight into the Free Spins bonus round. There, they can expect to see extra HYDE Super Spins being triggered along with a growing Multiplier for a potentially huge win potential.
