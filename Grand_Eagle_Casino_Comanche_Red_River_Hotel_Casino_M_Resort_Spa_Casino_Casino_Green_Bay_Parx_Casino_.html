Breakthrough Partnership with Gaming Giant Intralot

Free-to-play games provider Incentive Games has inked a 36-month deal with Intralot to make its Margin Selector, Dream Team, and Bracket games available for NBA and NFL contests. The breakthrough partnership has started with the NCAA’s March Madness 2021 tournament.

“We are pleased to partner with Incentive Games to provide players in Montana and Washington, DC added value through the addition of Free-To-Play games on our sports wagering platforms.”
