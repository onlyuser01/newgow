This partnership will give the lottery players access to the provider’s slots, table games, and instant win games for the first time. Recent releases like Battle Maidens™ and 1Million Megaways™ BC will be included with the all-time favorites such as Rainbow Wilds™, Blood Queen™ also making the list.

The games are equipped with sophisticated mechanics and smart math to offer an immersive experience to the players and a great chance of winning. 1X2 Network aims to create high-quality products. The company’s games are famous all over the world for its authentic sound and compelling visuals. The developer provides products for various markets and ensures that the games meet their regulatory requirements.

Kevin Reid, chief commercial officer at 1X2 Network, shared his enthusiasm and elaborated more about the company’s entry in Canada. In his opinion, 1X2’s titles can become a hit on the Canadian market, as they quickly have already gained worldwide popularity.

Canadian consumers would have the chance to play the existing table games and slots but also become the first to receive access to the company’s newest additions. Reid shared his delight in entering the Canadian market with Loto-Québec. He stated that this is one of the most prestigious operators in the country.
