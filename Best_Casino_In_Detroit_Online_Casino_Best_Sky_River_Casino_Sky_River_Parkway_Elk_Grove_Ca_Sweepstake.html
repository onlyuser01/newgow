Is It Time for Underdogs to Upset the Odds?

Most of the world’s best golfers have decided to skip past this event, meaning that some lower-ranked players will have a chance to get ahold of the title, as well as $1.4 million.

On the contrary, the 31-year-old hasn’t missed a cut in his previous 18 starts.

His most recent tournament was the Saudi International, where we saw him finish in tied eighth position. It’s not crazy to assume he’ll do at least equally well at the Honda Classic, and with the odds of +2000, Fleetwood does look like a decent betting option.
