Skyrocket Studios has officially launched its newest branded slot game, American Gods. The release is the result of collaboration between Bang Bang Games and Skyrocket with the title now available on Playzido and Entain as of Tuesday, December 21.

American Gods Slots Now Available

The release marks the successful negotiation of international gaming rights carried out by Skyrocket Entertainment earlier this year, with the company securing the rights from Fremantle and Amazon Prime Video for the franchise based on Neil Gaiman’s novels.

Bang Bang Games added their expertise to the development process, having worked on a number of high-profile games in the past, including Deal or No Deal, Dumb and Dumber, and Austin Powers to name a few.

The game, which is also available to play as a demo, sets itself the ambitious goal of transforming the iGaming experience.

The title follows multiple narratives, similar to how it does in the original TV series, and the companies have been sure to imbue their game with a range of features, including promo features, reel sync, high symbol transforms, random and wild reels, a worshipper bonus feature, Old Gods Free Spins, New Gods Free Spins, a Mega Bonus, and many other twists of the experience. The slot is set at a 95.79% return-to-player (RTP) rate.

Skyrocket CEO Sean Kelly has been a big fan of the American Gods franchise. Back in November, when Skyrocket Entertainment gained the rights to the franchise, he said: “American Gods is loved by audiences worldwide and we’re thrilled to be opening this up to new audiences through the gaming market. The first of many more super brands to come.”
