IGT has been supplying solutions to the lottery for many years

International Game Technology (IGT), a global leader in gaming tech and solutions, has secured an agreement extension with Lotrie Nationale Belgium (LNB), the country’s national lottery. As a result, IGT will continue to supply the operator for four more years.

Under the extended agreement, IGT’s subsidiary, IGT Global Services, will continue to provide its partner with lottery and iLottery central systems technology and other related lottery products.

IGT is a global leader in lottery technology and boasts of being the primary supplier of 8 of the 10 biggest lotteries in the world. The company also supplies solutions to 16 of the top 25 lotteries.

IGT believes in the provision of quality and safe products that allow players to enjoy gambling without risking gambling harm. The company serves many gambling segments, including iGaming, lottery, retail and digital slots and sports betting.

IGT has an extensive global presence thanks to its agreements with operators, regulators and governments in over 100 regulated markets. The supplier fields around 10,500 employees and is committed to the highest possible standards of service, integrity and responsibility.

IGT Is a Valuable Ally to LNB

Over the course of our longtime partnership, IGT has worked closely with LNB to successfully stay ahead of ever-evolving lottery trends and innovations. IGT’s flexible core lottery and iLottery systems securely power LNB’s retail, digital and omnichannel sales, while a variety of related components support the Lottery in its efforts to offer players responsible and exciting experiences.
