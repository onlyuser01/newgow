Cash Out, Chance Level, and Grand Jackpot Features

Commenting on the fresh release, the company’s chief commercial officer, Andrzej Hyla, has expressed their excitement about their ability to add another leading title to their iconic Extremely Light collection.

Hyla believes that by preserving the initial mechanics of the original slot that has already recorded high rates of success among players, the new version is “sure to go down a treat” with global slot aficionados.

The CCO also spoke about the continuous need to turn the iGaming world into a “more accessible” one while positioning themselves at the forefront with its premium releases.

Hot Slot: 777 Cash Out Extremely Light includes the Cash Out feature that allows players to accumulate and pay out wins based on the Cash and Jackpot symbols found in the base game. Regular cash prize symbols feature values ranging from 1x to 10x the stake. Mini, Major, and Mega jackpot symbols are worth 20x, 50x, and 100x the stake.

In the lighter variant of the game, the Cash Out symbol has been designed to resemble a golden safe. Players receive the 1,500x Grand Jackpot provided they manage to exclusively fill up the entire grid with cash prizes and/or jackpot symbols.

The Chance Level feature has also been brought back. The feature lets players double their base bets and boost the odds of landing Jackpot symbols.

The same feature increases players’ potential of coming across the Grand Jackpot by covering all 15 reel positions with Jackpot symbols. The Chance Level ante bet will not influence the values of the paytable symbols.
