Filippo Ferri has been selected to join Delasport’s leadership, becoming the iGaming software solutions provider’s new chief compliance officer. Ferri will use his experience with the gaming industry and compliance to push the company forward.

Ferri Brings a Decade and a Half Worth of Experience

Delasport’s new chief compliance officer has years of experience in the field. He has previously worked for various gaming companies and has an understanding of compliance thanks to his year-long tenure as Maxima Compliance’s chief commercial officer.

Before joining the full-service compliance consultancy, Ferri worked as director of iGaming strategy and new products at the private independent gaming certification lab BMM Testlabs for three years and a half. Prior to this, he has held various positions in other gaming companies such as Evolution Gaming, Inspired Gaming Group, GLI Europe, and Gambling Compliance.

Ferri’s total experience amounts to 16 years in the industry. The numerous jobs and positions have provided him with critical experience and a deep understanding of the field. He will carry this experience to Delasport and will use it for the company’s better future.

The New CCO is Looking Forward to Working with Delasport

As a chief compliance officer, Ferri will make sure that Delasport remains compliant within all of the markets it operates in. The new hire will check all the licensing requirements make sure that Delasport doesn’t break any of the rules and carefully check whether its sports betting and casino operations correspond to the expectations of local regulators.

Ferri spoke about his new position in Delasport. He revealed that he is very happy to be able to work with the company.

“I am thrilled to join Delasport as its outstanding proprietary sports betting platform, built by true veterans of the iGaming industry, pursues rapid expansion across more regulated markets,” Ferri said.

Ferri added that he feels honored to become part of Delasport’s team. He believes that the talented workers which the company has surrounded itself with will help it advance on the market. The new chief compliance officer is looking forward to working with the compliance team and, in his words, “working tirelessly to make sure compliance will be an instrumental component” of the company’s growth.

“We are very pleased with Filippo joining the management team. His vast experience in compliance and regulatory affairs is a great addition to propel Delasport’s strategy to rapidly expand its global reach.” Delasport CEO Oren Cohen Shwartz
