Novomatic’s Greentube Launches More Titles for Romanian Players

Romanian operator Baumbet teamed up with Greentube, a company specializing in creating quality iGaming software and games. The announcement came today, May 22. With this agreement, Greentube is enhancing its footprint in the European gaming market. As a result, Greentube’s leading casino games will be added to Baumbet’s growing portfolio of titles for the first time. The integration will be facilitated by the Pariplay platform.

Greentube’s Novomatic division titles include highly popular picks Sizzling Hot deluxe, Book of Ra deluxe, Lucky Lady’s Charm deluxe as well as Dolphin’s Pearl deluxe. Local players’ favorite game Plenty on Twenty has already gone live and many more of Novomatic’s games customized for the Romanian market have been integrated.

In Romania, Baumbet is a well-known brand with experience of over 25 years in the industry. Ever since Romania became a legal gambling market in 2018, Baumbet has been among the top operators in the country. With this recent agreement and integration, Greentube is showing rapid growth and expands further in the Eastern European region.

Baumbet COO, Daniel Cordos commented on the recent deal: “Greentube has more than lived up to its reputation for creating quality interactive games that stand the test of time both in the land-based market and online.” Cordos continued by saying that with the variety of games that Greentube is offering, Baumbet will get closer to its goal, which is : “providing an online casino experience that outperforms the market.”

Greentube CFO/CGO, Michael Bauer had to say: “Launching with such an established operator further demonstrates the high demand for our portfolio among local players. We expect our games will be an instant hit with Baumbet’s customers.” Bauer concluded by saying that Romania quickly proved to be a strong market for Greentube games. He further noted that regardless if it’s classic titles or new ones, Greentube content is highly-popular among players.

Meanwhile, Greentube has been prolific in signing up pivotal partnerships with market leaders, including Playtech. The pair signed a content deal, allowing Greentube to list titles on the Playtech Games Marketplace, including the all-time favorite Book of Ra Deluxe and Lady’s Charm Deluxe.
