GAMOMAT, a German iGaming supplier dedicated to providing an “outstanding gaming experience for slot fans,” has made a foray into Italy thanks to its agreement with Bragg Gaming Group. This became possible after Bragg Gaming’s local partner was given the green light in the country.

The distributor’s local partner, Microgame, just received approval from the Agenzia delle Dogane e dei Monopoli to launch in the country. As a result, Bragg Gaming can now supply the local brand with games developed by GAMOMAT.

Under the agreement, gamblers in Italy will be able to experience some of the German studio’s best titles. These include games such as La Dolce Vita, Book of Romeo and Julia, Ramses Book and 5 Flaring Fruits. To play any of these games, Italian customers simply have to visit Microgame’s local platform.

With Italy being one of the top markets in Europe, GAMOMAT will be able to expand its presence and reach many new players. The company considers the country to be a key growth market for its business.
