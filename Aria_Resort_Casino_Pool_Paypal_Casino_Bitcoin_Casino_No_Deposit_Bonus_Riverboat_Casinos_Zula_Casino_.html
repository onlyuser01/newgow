Sporting and Media Career

Besides the Mets, Valentine has spells as a manager for the Texas Rangers, the Boston Red Sox, and the Chiba Lotte Marines in Japan on the international stage. As a player, he was featured with the jerseys of four Major League Baseball (MLB) teams, the Los Angeles Dodgers, California Angels, New York Mets, and Seattle Mariners.

His professional career also includes eight years serving as the executive director of athletics at Sacred Heart University and was an All-State football player in his home state three times.

Outside of the playing field, Valentine performed as an analyst and announcer for Sunday Night Baseball on ESPN and NBC Sports, as well as a contributor to Sirius.
