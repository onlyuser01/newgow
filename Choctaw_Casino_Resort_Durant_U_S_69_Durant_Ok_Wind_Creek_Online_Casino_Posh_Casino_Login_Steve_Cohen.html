Mermaids Offer Unique Enchantments

Online players that tap into Enchanted Waters will be able to see four mermaid symbols that can appear on the fifth reel. Each of those mermaids delivers a different enchantment that results in a respin and is automatically activated.

Landing a blue mermaid can result in a multiplier of up to x25, while a red mermaid elevates the thrill and excitement by removing all low-paying symbols which can result in big payouts. Players that land a yellow mermaid will experience wild symbols transform into expanding wilds, while the green mermaid activates an avalanche feature.

Players can also use the game’s Golden Bet feature by engaging in a 15x stake in exchange for a guaranteed mermaid symbol on the fifth reel. “Alternatively, the free spins mode can be triggered at a cost of 200x the bet. For 1000x the stake the bonus will start with all mermaid symbols active at once,” explains Yggdrasil.
