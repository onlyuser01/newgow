The leading gaming industry technology provider, Playtech, joined forces with PokerMatch, the largest poker room in Eastern Europe.

Playtech and PokerMatch Unveil New Agreement

The exciting new collaboration was announced Wednesday and will see PokerMatch join Playtech’s popular international network of poker rooms, iPoker. The network, famous as one of the longest-running networks in the poker industry features leading software that helps it comply with regulations within different jurisdictions around the globe. At the same time, Playtech explained iPoker “offers an excellent liquidity pool” and the network is within the top 5 leading poker platforms globally.

“We are really excited to welcome PokerMatch to the iPoker network.“ Marat Koss, Playtech’s VP of interactive gaming

Marat Koss, Playtech’s VP of interactive gaming, said that the company is excited to team up with PokerMatch. He revealed that PokerMatch was able to expand its presence significantly in Europe, becoming one of the leading Eastern European operators in only six years since it was founded. In conclusion, Koss said that Playtech is looking forward to the success the collaboration with PokerMatch will bring, while at the same time helping the poker room grow further.

“We look forward to a long and successful partnership with PokerMatch as it continues to grow and break new records,“ added Koss

PokerMatch Grew Significantly since 2016
