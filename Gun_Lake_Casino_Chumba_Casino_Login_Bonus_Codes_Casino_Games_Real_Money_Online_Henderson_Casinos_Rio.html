Safari Sam 2 review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around an African Safari.

Set on the African savanna, this game is hosted by an eccentric ranger named Sam. With his help, players will explore a world filled with friendly wild animals and scorching heat.

The whimsical mood of this game provides an exotic escape into a sunny world of adventure.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is made up of percussion beats and animal noises. The lion roar in particular provides a safari-like ambiance. A rainstick rustles when players hit 'spin', adding to the sense that this is an African setting.

The characters of this game are animated with a Pixar-like quality. Sam looks from side to side nervously, while the female guide raises an eyebrow with sassy confidence. Players may begin to have a sense that she’s the one really running the show.

The animal symbols are especially memorable, with a grumpy lion and sly-faced zebra stealing the show. They are accompanied by a host of other safari symbols from rugged vehicles to makeshift tents and a acacia tree silhouetted against a sunset.

Whenever the reels spin, they leave a little trail of dust, adding to the sense that players are riding on dry roads.

The backdrop behind the reels is beautifully animated with distant mountains and clear blue skies. The reels themselves seem built into a large welcoming tree.

Overall, the graphics and soundtrack support the theme effectively and help make this game’s world an entertaining one.

Gameplay

Safari Sam 2 is a five reel, three row slot, with 50 win lines.

When 3 matching symbols appear stacked on top of each other, players are awarded a three-of-a-kind prize.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

Every spin, players have a chance to be awarded the Call of the Wild feature, which covers as many as 4 of the reels in Wild symbols.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land at least 3 Scatter symbols.

While in the Free Spins feature, players will also have a greater chance of landing Wilds.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.3%, slightly above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is medium, which makes it a less ideal match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, medium volatility safari-themed slot.

This game overcomes its middling volatility with a fun theme and engaging characters.

Players with their eye on maximising their chances for bigger wins may wish to move on to games with higher volatility, but they’ll still be glad they gave this one a try.
