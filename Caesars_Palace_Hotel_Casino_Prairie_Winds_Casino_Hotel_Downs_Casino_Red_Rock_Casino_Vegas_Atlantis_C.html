As a result, the latter will benefit from a series of unique performance tracking tools that will help it make faster and better-optimized decisions for higher revenue.

EveryMatrix, “Very Pleased” to Welcome DeepCI to the Team

At the moment, DeepCI is using its state-of-the-art proprietary technology to monitor tens of thousands of affiliate sites. Its services are offered to some of the most popular gaming and sports betting games in the industry, including Betsson, Betway, bet365, and 888.com.

The new partnership will now also see DeepCI’s “must-have” tools work their magic on EveryMatrix and its over 200 worldwide customers. The tools work by locating all gaming brands on all the pages of the assessed websites.
