Join the Colorful Slime Party Gooeyness

Fans of colorful slots will appreciate the gooeyness of Angel, Devil. And Wizard, the three special slime party guests inviting you to join them for the sweet, colorful madness of the grid.

With symbols that pay anywhere on the reels, wilds, free spins, and a volatility of 98.10%, the game that aims to put a new spin on a sugary theme has plenty to offer.

The Candy Cane wild symbols are not only helpful at generating matching combinations, but they can also occasionally feature multipliers. In case multiple wilds are seen landing on the grid, the value of each of these symbols will be added together.

When a player lands a minimum of three slime King Scatters, they receive up to 30 Free Spins. During the Free Spins rounds, all wilds with attached multiplier values are accumulated, leading to a massive maximum win capped at 48,900x.

Players can also opt for the Buy Feature and jump straight into the free spins action by purchasing the round.
