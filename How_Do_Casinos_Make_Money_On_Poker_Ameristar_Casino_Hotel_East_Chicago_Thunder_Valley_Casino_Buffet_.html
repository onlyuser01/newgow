OnAir Entertainment, a live casino studio seeking to inspire and engage players with best-in-class games, has unveiled Card Matchup, a brand new sports-themed game. The new title is set to go live in November this year.

Card Matchup Goes Live on November 10

Card Matchup is a new and scalable live table game that is designed to appeal to a broad range of players. The concept is fairly simple – players have to guess whether their team or their opponent’s team will emerge victorious and bet on it. After that, the teams would draw cards and the side with the highest card would win the match.

To make this simple concept more exciting, OnAir Entertainment will provide players with odds that allow them to follow all bets placed, see earlier results and check their winning streak. In addition, Card Matchup boasts a highly-customizable user interface that partnering operators can customize to their own liking. iGaming companies that opt to add Card Matchup to their offerings can implement their logo and set the UI color to one that represents their brand.

The best thing about Card Matchup is that an unlimited number of players can join and bet on the outcomes while a live host flips the cards. The new game is a great addition to OnAir Entertainment’s roster of engaging live casino games, which includes both classic and innovative titles.
