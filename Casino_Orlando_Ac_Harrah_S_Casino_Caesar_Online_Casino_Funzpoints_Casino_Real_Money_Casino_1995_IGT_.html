‘Extensive Library of Premium iLottery Content’

IGT Global Corporation, a subsidiary of IGT, signed an agreement with the Michigan Lottery to provide the state lottery operator with its engaging iLottery content delivered via IGT’s remote gaming server (RGS).

Jay Gendron, chief operating officer at IGT, outlined the benefits for the Michigan Lottery the integration with IGT’s advanced RGS solution would bring, offering players an “extensive library of premium iLottery content” while helping the Lottery diversify its product portfolio with popular “eInstants and omnichannel themes.”

“As a leader in this space, the Michigan Lottery has built a robust iLottery offering from the ground up, and IGT is thrilled that we can contribute to its continued success through the launch of our games,” Gendron concluded.
