Arizona Cardinals Putting Their Hopes into Kyler Murray

Arizona’s crucial player Kyler Murray is expected to make his first appearance of the 2023 NFL season this coming Sunday. Without him to call the signals, the Cardinals have been dreadful offensively, scoring just 16.8 points per game on average.

As if that wasn’t bad enough, their average in the last three games is just 11.3. But those days might be over after Murray makes his comeback. Still, there’s a big question mark over whether the long-term hiatus has taken its toll on him.

He might not turn Arizona into a world-beater straight away, but our Falcons vs. Cardinals prediction is that Murray will up his team’s offensive numbers. Will that be enough for a win? It all depends on how Arizona’s defense performs on Sunday.

These guys have been horrible in 2023, allowing 26.7 points per game on average. They conceded 20 or more points in eight of the nine games they’ve played so far.
