The leading developer of original iGaming content, Gaming Corps, announced it expanded the reach of its products via a new partnership with the leading iGaming platform and sportsbook solutions provider for the gambling industry, Gaming Innovation Group (GiG)

The new partnership, announced Monday, sees Gaming Corps expand its distribution network by joining forces with GiG. As a result, leading titles from the company including Tikiz N Juice, Coin Miner, Penalty Champion, Stormy Witch, Cat Ching, and Skyliner among other popular titles will reach new audiences and markets.

GiG Teams up with Gaming Corps

Founded in 2014, Gaming Corps quickly developed within the ever-changing iGaming vertical. By now, the company is famous for its premium casino games that combine innovative features and engaging gameplay. Besides slots, Gaming Corps offers table games, mine games, multiplier games and other leading modern titles. Considering its attention to detail and quality of content, the company’s titles are now the preferred choice of many operators in the industry.

Martin Collins, GiG’s director of sales and business development, explained that joining forces with Gaming Corps doesn’t come unexpectedly. He outlined that the company offers quality content which makes it the perfect addition to GiG’s partner list. Finally, Collins welcomed Gaming Corps to the company’s portfolio of partners.

“We’re always looking to make quality additions to our platform, ensuring we remain a provider of choice for the industry and we’re delighted to be able to count Gaming Corps amongst that select group of partners.“ Martin Collins, director of sales and business development at GiG

Danielle Calafato, Gaming Corps’ head of commercial, added that 2022 brought plenty of success for the company. Still, she explained that the partnership with GiG represents an important achievement for the company as its content will enter new markets.

“Throughout 2022 we have seen a rapid expansion at Gaming Corps and securing a partner as prestigious as GiG means we will be able to offer more games in more markets, thus expanding our reach further.“ Danielle Calafato, head of commercial at Gaming Corps

Calafato acknowledged that GiG has a strong presence in the gaming industry with its large roster of brands. Finally, she said that the collaboration will help connect Gaming Corps with new audiences thus creating new growth opportunities for the company.
