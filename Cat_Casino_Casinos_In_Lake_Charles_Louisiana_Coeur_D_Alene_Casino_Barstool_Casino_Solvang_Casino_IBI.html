The International Betting Integrity Association (IBIA), the leading global voice on integrity for the licensed betting industry, has welcomed esports betting solutions provider Oddin.gg as its latest member. Oddin, which provides partners with an end-to-end esports betting ecosystem, now joins a family of approximately 50 companies and more than 125 leading sports betting brands.

Oddin.gg boasts licenses in several North American jurisdictions, including Colorado, New Jersey and the Canadian province of Ontario. The company is bullish on growing its US footprint and is actively seeking opportunities in additional US states.

As a part of the IBIA, Oddin will cement the integrity organization’s position as the world’s leading operator-run integrity monitoring body.

For reference, the IBIA is a not-for-profit organization that leverages a global monitoring network to prevent corruption in sports and sports betting. It seeks to identify and report suspicious activity in regulated betting markets, allowing relevant authorities to react.

Oddin.gg Is Committed to High Integrity Standards

Oddin.gg’s chief executive officer, Vlastimil Venclik, said that his team is excited to join the IBIA. He said that this move highlights his team’s commitment to maintaining the “highest standards of integrity in esports betting.” In addition, Oddin is now aligned with a globally recognized leader in betting integrity, Venclik added.

This collaboration underscores our dedication to providing a safe and fair betting environment for our partners and their customers. Vlastimil Venclik, CEO, Oddin.gg

Khalid Ali, IBIA’s chief executive officer, also expressed his thoughts on the matter, welcoming Oddin.gg as an IBIA member. Ali praised the company as a “leading global player in the esports betting environment” and lauded Oddin’s desire to tap into the “best integrity protection” in the world.

Through our world-leading betting integrity network, we will work in close collaboration to protect the esports betting products and services offered by Oddin.gg to its sportsbook partners. Khalid Ali, CEO, IBIA
