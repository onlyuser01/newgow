Charlotte FC Playing Great at Home

Charlotte FC is 6-2-9 (20 points) overall, currently sitting in the #9 position in the Eastern Conference. The reason why this team isn’t higher in the standings is that its away performances are nothing short of disappointing. At home, however, it’s a whole different story.

So far, Charlotte FC has won 18 points at home, which is second only to New York City in the conference. These guys have won six games so far, while losing two. At this point, they’re still without a single draw played at Bank of America Stadium.

Charlotte FC won their previous two home games, beating New York Red Bulls 2-0 and the Vancouver Whitecaps 2-1. Are we going to see them add a third win to the list?

Austin FC Playing Well on the Road

Home turf has huge importance in the MLS, with teams winning most of their points on their turf. On the road, there aren’t many teams that have been too impressive.

Austin FC, which is one of the best away teams in the Western Conference, has won just 13 points on the road so far. After playing eight away games, Austin FC scored a total of 12 goals, while allowing 13.

Nevertheless, their away form is pretty decent. In their previous five games on the road, they collected nine points, beating such MLS heavyweights as Houston Dynamo, Los Angeles FC, and CF Montreal.
