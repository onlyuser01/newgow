‘Essential Piece Going Forward’

‘Forward-Thinking Approach to the Industry’

Brian Napoli, vice president of Corporate Partnerships for the Philadelphia Eagles was excited to see the Unibet partnership extended by another three years, outlining Unibet’s “forward-thinking approach” as key to empowering the franchise to connect with its fans in “new and interactive ways.”

“Through our continued partnership, we are excited to offer Eagles fans even more engagement opportunities, starting with the new Unibet Landing at Lincoln Financial Field this season,” Napoli concluded.
