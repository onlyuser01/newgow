Global esports betting powerhouse GG.BET has renewed its existing partnership agreement with Team Vitality, an international esports club. Now extended through 2024, the deal will see the bookmaker continue its support of the club’s Counter-Strike team.

GG.BET first penned a partnership agreement with Team Vitality’s CS roster back in 2022 and has supported it ever since. Throughout their partnership, the two parties participated in a variety of activations, both online and real-life.

Some of the previous activations, for example, saw GG.BET and Team Vitality promote V.Hive, a mobile blockading application that has so far been downloaded by more than 80,000 Team Vitality fans.

In addition, Team Vitality participated in GG.BET’s Match of LeGGends, a spectacular event, during which they clashed with NAVI, another professional esports club. The match was seen by over a million fans across the world.

Vitality, which is currently considered the top CS team in the world, won its first major title at the BLAST Paris Major 2023.

The Two Parties Are Looking Forward to Participating in Joint Activations

GG.BET commented on the deal, reaffirming its commitment to supporting Team Vitality. The company pointed out that its 2023 projects reached more than 150 million fans on social media, inspiring countless esports enthusiasts across the world.

In 2024, we’re setting ourselves new goals, and we’ll be taking the strategic partnership between GG.BET and Team Vitality to new heights! GG.BET statement

Team Vitality CEO Nicolas Maurer weighed in on the matter, saying that his team is likewise pleased to extend this partnership. He lauded GG.BET as a leading esports company and said that this cooperation has always been about “having fun and creating unmatched experiences” for esports fans.

Like our meet & greet event in Rio, which gathered over 3.5 million reach, and the unforgettable show match against NAVI. We’re looking forward to unveiling the exciting projects we’ll be putting in place over the next two years, to make the CS community shine and grow. Nicolas Maurer, CEO, Team Vitality

GG.BET teased that it will continue to organize joint online initiatives alongside the team, as well as large-scale real-life events for true fans of esports.
