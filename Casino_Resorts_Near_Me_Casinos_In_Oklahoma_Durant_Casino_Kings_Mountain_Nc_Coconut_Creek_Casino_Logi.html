The integration of Amplifier AI into Betsson Group’s brands will ensure 1-to-1 personalization, helping the operator bolster its profitability and player retention. In addition, the solution has built-in monitoring tools that will help Betsson prevent harmful gambling.

Betsson is a company that is firmly committed to protecting players from gambling harm, cementing it as the company of choice of many players across the markets where it operates. What sets the company apart from its competitors is its tendency to act during the moments that matter and react immediately in key touchpoints.

Future Anthem’s founder and chief executive officer, Leigh Nissim, commented on the agreement, saying that his team really enjoys working with progressive clients and teams, such as Betsson Group. We’re delighted that our all-encompassing personalization product, Amplifier AI, is becoming an integral part of their [Betsson’s] journey. Leigh Nissim, CEO, Future Anthem
