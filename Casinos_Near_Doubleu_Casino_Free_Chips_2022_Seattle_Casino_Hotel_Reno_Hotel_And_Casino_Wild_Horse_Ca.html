Responsible gaming organization Conscious Gaming announced the appointment of gaming industry specialist Blair Aronson as its new Operations Director.

Second Full-Time Employee

New Operations Director Aronson, who joins Conscious Gaming having spent the past 3 years as Director of Customer Success – Americas at Scientific Games (SG), will be working with gaming operators, state regulatory bodies, sports leagues, and platform providers, as she would be seeking to manage, scale, and optimize the organization’s responsible gaming tool PlayPause.

“As we continue to make progress with PlayPause, it was important to add someone with Blair’s skillset to ensure proper implementation of the technology for our clients. She has a wealth of experience, knowledge and relationships that as we continue to grow, will help us deliver on our promise to protect consumers.” Anna Sainsbury, Trustee, Conscious Gaming

Blair Aronson spent 5 years performing as Director of Operations at NYX Gaming Group, prior to working with SG Digital, which acquired NYX Gaming in 2018.

“I am thrilled Anna has offered me the opportunity to work alongside her in an organization with such an important mission.” Blair Aronson, Operations Director, Conscious Gaming

Customer-Facing Teams Responsibilities

At Conscious Gaming, she will also serve as Chief of Staff within the service delivery and operations teams, to make sure structure and process within customer-facing teams continue to improve in sync with the company’s expansion.

“The online gaming business is growing rapidly and the need to modernize and strengthen responsible gaming will be critical to its success. I look forward to continuing the relationships I have built throughout my career to help roll out the PlayPause technology.” Blair Aronson, Operations Director, Conscious Gaming

Founded by geolocation and compliance specialist company GeoComply, the independent nonprofit organization Conscious Gaming utilizes the latest technology developments to provide social responsibility solutions. Recently, the organization received a generous donation from its parent company to fund the further development of its self-exclusion tool PlayPause.

Seeking to ensure better customer protection, responsible gaming, and integrity in sports, Conscious Gaming collaborates with industry stakeholders, regulators, gaming advocacy groups, academics, and gambling treatment experts to introduce a holistic approach to consumer protection by bringing together each state’s self-exclusion database into a national repository.
