Gaming Innovation Group and Betsson Group are onto a good thing and they are nowhere near ready to throw in the towel just yet. The companies are extending their long-term partnership yet again that will cover platform and managed services across multiple jurisdictions and aspects of the industry.

Commenting on the current extension of what has been a successful collaboration so far, GiG said that the alliance will carry on for at least another three years through the end of 2025.

GiG to Continue Powering Betsson Brands

As a result, Betsson will benefit from GiG’s advanced products for some of its flagship brands, including Rizk, Guts, Kaboo, and Thrills, along with ancillary brands. GiG is prepared to support Betsson’s growth across new markets where the potential for securing a sizeable market share is significant. GiG’s press release said that the company was capable of scaling partners’ brands in important targeted markets.

GiG CEO Richard Brown commented on the development and partnership between the companies so far, as well as what to expect ahead:

“The partnership will support and help to drive the two businesses’ growth in existing and new markets, increasing the diversification and market opportunities for both Betsson and GiG.” GiG CEO Richard Brown

GiG is currently undergoing a transition that is pivoting towards a pure B2B focus, Brown added. The news was similarly welcomed by Zecure Gaming’s commercial director, Andrew Valenzia. Zecure is owned by Betsson:

“We are delighted to have extended our agreement with GiG for an additional period of three years.
