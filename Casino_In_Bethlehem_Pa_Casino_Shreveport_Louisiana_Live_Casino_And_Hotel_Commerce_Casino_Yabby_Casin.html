Premier League side Southampton announced today its support for a brand new mobile app aimed at providing discrete support for at-risk gamblers. The RecoverMe app which uses cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) to treat problem gambling would have the Saints’ financial support for further development and growth.

Developed by 3 junior doctors and in consultation with psychiatrists and psychologists, as well as people who struggle with gambling addiction, the RecoverMe app “has been made with problem gamblers for problem gamblers”.

Responsibility to Raise Awareness for Safer Gambling

The team of doctors conducted an acceptability study in 2019, over 60 hours of interviews with ex and current problem gamblers, looking for app features that would need improving and what else they could offer, and based on their findings, they iterated the therapy, design and feature list into the app.

“I am delighted to announce the club’s support for RecoverMe. The club takes its responsibility to promoting responsible gambling extremely seriously and it is important that we are proactive in exploring new opportunities to implement our commitment to this cause.” David Thomas, Chief Commercial Officer, Southampton FC

Gambling harm has been a central issue in the UK recently, and Southampton FC outlined that while gambling can be part of the excitement football fans look for during games, the club strongly believes that it is part of its responsibility to raise awareness of the support available for those who need it. The support for the app is the club’s latest step to promote responsible gambling.

Mindfulness Sessions, Emergency Helpline, Self-Help Exercises

The app allows its users to manage addictions by reflecting on gambling habits, challenging thought processes and feelings around gambling, as well as providing access to mindfulness sessions and an emergency helpline and self-help exercises.

“We wholeheartedly believe RecoverMe will provide a lifeline to those suffering in silence. It has been incredible to see how motivated Southampton are in creating this meaningful change and we are extremely excited to be working with them to do this and we hope this is just the beginning!” Minal Jain, Co-Founder, RecoverMe

Southampton FC is adamant building strong relationships with organizations positioned at the forefront to help people struggling with gambling addiction is the way forward, reiterating its commitment to promote a safer gambling environment, and enhance and refine practices in collaboration with experts from the industry.

“We’re proud to support the launch of the RecoverMe app and I am excited by the chance to work with an organisation so closely aligned to our values.” David Thomas, Chief Commercial Officer, Southampton FC

Southampton will work together with the RecoverMe app team to expand the awareness to people struggling to keep in check their gambling habits that the app can provide a viable solution to their problems, as well as extend the app’s reach to as many users as possible.
