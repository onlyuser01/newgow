The leading distributor and provider of iGaming content, 3 Oaks Gaming, expanded its portfolio of games with the addition of Crystal Scarabs: Hold and Win, a new 5x4 slot game that offers engaging features and exciting gameplay. As the name of the new game suggests, Crystal Scarabs: Hold and Win offers 3 Oaks Gaming's popular Hold and Win mechanic. The new slot game represents the company's 15th installment within the popular series for this year alone, reaffirming its dedication to continue expanding its offering.

The latest slot from the leading iGaming content provider features the evergreen ancient Egyptian theme. Players of Crystal Scarabs: Hold and Win can expect to see standard card symbols between Jack and Ace, along with other symbols that match its theme. Quality animations, sound effects and an engaging soundtrack ensure unrivaled experiences, further boosted by the game's features that grant a chance for mouth-watering payouts.

Yuriy Muratov, 3 Oaks Gaming's Chief Business Development Officer, explained in a statement that Crystal Scarabs: Hold and Win players can expect a thrilling adventure. "This Egypt-themed sensation promises a gaming experience like no other, providing a range of in-game extras that are set to ignite the excitement of players," he added. Finally, Muratov said: "With three more games set to captivate audiences before the year concludes, stay tuned for more immersive gameplay that defines 3 Oaks Gaming."

Players that tap into 3 Oaks Gaming's new slot game should watch out for its Bonus Gold Symbols that are portrayed as Gold Scarabs. This game feature grants them a chance to secure significant prizes, given that they land two, three, four or five of those symbols. Once this happens, players have a chance to win a Mini, Minor, Major or Grand Jackpot with a prize of x5,000.

Additionally, Crystal Scarabs: Hold and Win allows players to tap into the Hold and Win Bonus Game by gathering Scarab Symbols via its Bonus Accum mechanic.
