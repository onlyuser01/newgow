Money Train review

Theme and Storyline

Players will get a ride into the Wild West with this slot.

Set in a rugged town in the blazing sun, Money Train has an immediately adventurous mood.

The characters lurking in the reels have a mysterious look about them, as if they have secrets to share with the player. Some of the characters lean into stereotypes that belong firmly in the world of fiction, however.

Overall, players will enjoy getting a ticket for this journey into the Old West.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The reels arrive dramatically on a rattling train car. The engine and other cars are animated with intriguing detail. Players will likely keep noticing new elements of the design each time they play.

As the game begins the shutters of the train car clatter up. Surrounded by rusty machinery, the reels seem part of a game players might find at a Wild West carnival.

A windmill slowly turns in the background, adding depth to the scene. The delicacy of the metalwork on the train enhances the realism world as well.

The soundtrack has a bouncing rhythm befitting a train journey. However, the jolly tone wears after some time and players may wish to leave it off.

The front end of this game impressively captures fun details that players will love.

Gameplay

Money Train is a five reel, four row slot, with forty win lines.

Gameplay is smooth and has a pleasant flow. Players looking to kick up the action may be inclined to use the Buy Feature to initiate the Money Cart bonus (see below).

Buy-in option for the bonus feature

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols in a winning combination.

The Money Cart bonus round is triggered when players get a Bonus symbol on reels 1, 3, and 5. Those triggering symbols then unveil a value which is displayed as a multiplication of the bet, then 3 spins are awarded.
