In Search of the Riches of the Three Kingdoms

As they delve into the fascinating Chinese era and explore the artifacts and relics of the Three Kingdoms, players are invited to create winning combinations on any random spin and cross their fingers to land at least three Scatter symbols to enter the Free Spins round.

Here, they are granted a random number of free spins selected by a spinning Wheel. On each Free Spin they receive, players are guaranteed Expanding Symbols that can trigger more winning opportunities.

Players who land two additional Scatter symbols while engaged in the Free Spins round will engage the Nested Spins feature. The latter will award another random number of free spins while locking the Expanding Symbols in place until the end of the round.
