ICE London is, without a doubt, one of the most important conferences in the gambling industry. As expected, several gaming operators and gambling companies are already gearing up to showcase the products and offerings that they have been working on. There are very many exciting exhibitions in the line up from leading gaming brands which sets the event up to be one of the most exciting ones of 2020.

Microgaming’s New Slots

Last year, Microgaming announced that it was shutting down its online poker network but it assured its customers that it had so much in store for them. At the upcoming ICE London, the company will be showcasing some titles in its ever-expanding portfolio of gaming content.

The company reportedly has plans to unveil over 100 exclusive games as well as over 200 partner games to its highly-praised content aggregation platform. That is definitely going to be a huge development for the company especially as it readies to completely do away with its online poker network.

Pragmatic Play Readies New Range of Virtual Sports

The renowned gaming content and solutions provider has several new additions to its robust multi-product portfolio lined up. These will all be released in the course of 2020 and gamers will have the chance to get a glimpse of them at ICE London.
