Unibet Becomes Official Sports Partner for the Pittsburgh Steelers

Ahead of the football season, Unibet announced that it inked a partnership with the Pittsburgh Steelers. The latest partnership announced on Thursday this week marks the second National Football League partner for Kindred Group’s sportsbook brand.

Unibet revealed that the partnership will feature collaboration within digital and social media channels. Additionally, the recent tie-up will bring sweepstakes for fans. Under the newly inked agreement, Unibet and Pittsburgh Steelers will support local communities.

The collaboration will boost fan engagement on game days and fans will have the opportunity to collaborate with Steelers Legends at the newly created Unibet Tailgate zone outside of the Heinz Field stadium. Last but not least, the collaboration will see the launch of a Steelers-themed casino game. It is expected the new game to be available later this year exclusively at Unibet PA casino.

The NFL Team Is Excited to Partner with Unibet

“We are excited to welcome Unibet to the Steelers family.” Ryan Huzjak, VP of sales and marketing, Pittsburgh Steelers

Founded in 1933, the Pittsburgh Steelers is the fifth oldest NFL franchise. So far, the team has won six Super Bowl titles.
