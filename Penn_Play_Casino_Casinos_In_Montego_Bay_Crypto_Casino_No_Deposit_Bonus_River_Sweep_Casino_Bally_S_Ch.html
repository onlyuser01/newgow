The provider of online software, offering more than 200 games for the iGaming vertical, CT Interactive, unveiled details regarding a new partnership that helped it expand further the reach of its titles. Earlier this week, the company confirmed it joined forces with Sbbet.me, a leading online betting and gambling operator within the market of Montenegro.

Teaming up with the company, CT Interactive agreed to supply some of its most popular and successful games. Per the new deal, Sbbet.me customers will be able to tap into games that have proven to hit around the world from the popular developer.

As a result, customers of the gambling operator will be able to enjoy titles such as Champagne and Fruits, Dice Valhalla, Dice Quest, as well as Bloody Princess. Moreover, Sbbet.me players will be able to experience hit games, including Hyper Cuber, Fruit Galaxy the Wheel, Doctor Winstein, Fire Egg, as well as Lord of Luck the Wheel. Additional titles CT Interactive agreed to supply include Monkey Kingdom, Queen of Flames the Wheel, Treasure Chase, The Golden Duck, Fruits and Bells, Fruitball Heroes, as well as Aztec Gold 20.

Aleksandar Spadijer, Sbbet.me's CEO, shared his excitement about the new collaboration with CT Interactive in a statement, saying: "We're delighted to team up with CT Interactive." Finally, the CEO pointed out: "Their portfolio is outstanding, and we are sure that through it we will provide our clients with a world-class gaming experience."

The new collaboration will help expand the offering of Sbbet.me. As the first betting operator in Montenegro, the company launched its services back in 1997. Since then, Sbbet.me has grown exponentially and by now offers online betting options as well as a plethora of online gambling games, including casino, virtual and live game titles.

Milena Tsankarska, CT Interactive's Head of Business Development EMEA, was excited to see the company join forces with Sbbet.me. "We are so happy that our presence is growing so fast in the Montenegrin territory," she said. Tsankarska outlined that the new collaboration comes after earlier this year, CT Interactive secured licensing for a package of its products in Montenegro.

"Since then, we have helped a lot of local operators increase their clientele and better their players' experience," she added. Last but not least, the company's Head of Business Development EMEA, said: "For us, it is a great pleasure to partner with Sbbet.me."

The recent collaboration comes after late last month, CT Interactive expanded further its European-facing presence. At the time, the company joined forces with The Ear in Italy, bolstering its presence in this important market on the Old Continent. Under the terms of the deal, CT Interactive agreed to deliver a selection of its games, powering the platform's operations in the country.
