The advanced platform provider that meets multi-state regulatory requirements across the United States and in Canada, Strive Gaming, announced an important senior-level appointment. Earlier this week, the company confirmed it strengthened its compliance and legal teams with the addition of Tommaso Di Chio. Joining the company, the seasoned expert with significant experience within the regulatory vertical was appointed to the role of chief legal and compliance officer.

In his new role with Strive, Di Chio will oversee strategic legal matters for the company across North America. He will also play a pivotal role in such matters on a global scale, providing strategic guidance and bringing his extensive skillset.

Leveraging his extensive experience, Di Chio is expected to help strengthen Strive’s compliance and legal teams. With strategic knowledge, guidance and expert advice, he is expected to play an important role at the leading platform provider, helping them navigate future challenges across North America and around the world. Ultimately, Di Chio’s efforts are expected to unlock new synergies, enabling the company to continue growing.

“I’m thrilled to be joining Strive Gaming on its journey; a journey that we are in together, every step of the way.“ Tommaso Di Chio, chief legal and compliance officer at Strive Gaming

Sharing his excitement, Di Chio spoke about the rapid changes within the gaming sector, explaining that growth can be achieved through the balance of responsibility and new ideas. “I’m all about pulling together the smarts from regulators, lawmakers and folks in the industry to make gaming not just a money-maker but also something we can be proud of,” he added. Finally, Di Chio pointed out that it is thrilling to become a part of Strive’s C-suite at such a pivotal moment in its growth and provide his strategic guidance to help continue this process.

A Perfect Addition to Strive’s C-Suite

Max Meltzer, Strive’s CEO, spoke about the constantly evolving regulatory and legal landscape in North America, explaining that it is important for the company to have experts who can help navigate it through different challenges. He added: “In Tommaso, we have the perfect person to oversee all legal and compliance activity at Strive Gaming and to lead and grow our teams.” Finally, Meltzer deemed the latest appointment important, outlining that it strengthens the company’s role as a leading PAM provider for North America and across the globe.

“This is an important hire for us and will help us to continue to strengthen our position as the leading PAM provider in North America and beyond.“ Max Meltzer, CEO at Strive Gaming

Undoubtedly, Di Chio brings extensive experience to his new role with Strive. He most recently was a part of the leading B2B sports betting platform provider, Kambi. Overall, he was a part of the company for nearly a decade, holding different senior compliance and legal roles.
