Huddle Tech Inc. has strengthened its presence in the United States through a new alliance with Odds on Compliance. The partnership will be focused on catering to the needs of iGaming operators, which is Huddle’s bread and butter.

Huddle Tech Seeks Advise on US Gambling Regulation

The company uses advanced and proprietary data-driven solutions to help with navigating the regulatory landscape in the United States. Odds On Compliance delivers information in real-time by tapping into disparate data sets and organizing them to create cost-reducing efficiencies. Through this partnership with Odds On Compliance, Huddle will be able to achieve better regulatory compliance as it seeks to enter more states and jurisdictions in North America.

Huddle will rely on Odds On Compliance’s recently released Playbook solution which offers a one-stop-shop for all regulatory matters in the United States. The solution uses AI to ensure it stays on top of the latest development while relying on expert human review.

The solution is readily and easily available to partners who wish to quickly catch themselves up on any regulator changes in the United States and uses natural language to conduct searches.

Huddle co-founder and Huddle Tech Inc. CEO Francesco Borgosano welcomed the opportunity to be teaming with Odds On Compliance and what he described as their “game-changing” product, the Playbook. Thanks to this repository, companies such as Huddle can quickly catch themselves on the regulatory landscape and step confidently into new markets and jurisdictions.
