This marks yet another increase when compared to the $609.9 million for the full year 2020. The Company’s Q4 2021 Results Look Good

It comes as no surprise that Boyd Gaming enjoyed a successful fourth quarter in 2021. The company revealed that its Q4 2021 revenues hit $879.8 million. This marks a staggering 34% increase when compared to the $635.9 million in revenue reported for Q4 2020. Additionally, in Q4, 2021, Boyd Gaming’s net income hit $109.8 million. For the fourth quarter of 2021, the total adjusted EBITDAR hit $347.3 million, representing a 65% increase when compared to the $210.5 million for the same period a year ago.
