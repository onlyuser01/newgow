Wealth Inn review

Theme and Storyline

Wealth Inn has a Chinese luxury tavern theme.

Players will ride up to a mysterious inn in a dense forest of bamboo. Weary from their travels, they’ll duck in to find luminous red lanterns and an enchanting atmosphere.

Weath Inn is a good choice for fans of Eastern themes and cheerful aesthetics. It leans into some cartoonish stereotypes, but this is part of the fairytale vibe.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The laidback traditional Chinese soundtrack is filled with zither notes and regal ambiance. The reels spin with a delicate beep that seems to be from a much more modern century. Wins are celebrated with a gentle gong sound and chiming.

The reels are filled with precious Chinese artifacts and tantalizing fruits. Ornate knot symbols in gold, red, and green are especially eye-catching.

The reels themselves are glossy red and framed by golden designs. Behind the reels, players will see an inviting open-air room decorated in vivid red and lit by swaying lanterns.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Wealth Inn create a warm and exotic atmosphere.

Gameplay

Wealth Inn is a three reel, three row slot with 8 win lines.

Winning combinations are calculated from the leftmost reel towards the rightmost. Only the highest win on each line pays.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols. There is a special symbol that transforms into a Wild and expands to fill its reel whenever it is part of a winning combination.

This slot is quite sparse when it comes to further bonus features, and players won’t find many fan favourites here.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.64%, above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.08 up to a maximum of $/£/€80.00 per spin.

The volatility of this game is low, which makes it a poorer match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We do not recommend this high RTP, low volatility Chinese tavern-themed slot.

With a colorful frontend and respectable RTP, Wealth Inn is on the right track, but due to its decisively low volatility, it does not merit a strong recommendation. Players with a mathematically inclined mindset will do better to seek out slots with a more robust volatility.

Given the popularity of East Asian themes, it is more than possible to find a similarly themed slot with more strategy-friendly volatility.

Wealth Inn also offers quite minimal bonus features, making it somewhat less engaging than it could have been.
