Yokozuna Clash review

Quick verdict Beautiful design, a brilliant bonus game, and a good RTP (96.30%) make this slot a lot of fun to play.

Theme and Storyline

Yokozuna is the highest possible rank a sumo wrestler can achieve in the Japanese sport. In this sumo themed slot, it goes even further, with the highest level wrestling opponent being not just a sumo pro, but an actual demon.

The cartoonish style of the setting makes the sport come alive as an epic, one on one wrestling match for the ultimate title.

Gameplay

Yggdrasil Gaming also invest in interesting gameplay. Here, the main innovation is in the bonus (see below).

The base game, however, is pretty straightforward, with five reels, three rows, and an any way wins system in place that gives up to 243 ways to win in both directions.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

Two Bonus symbols on reels one and five trigger the Sumo Bonus Feature. In this bonus feature, the player gets free spins that continue until their player is pushed out of the ring. This occurs in the following way. When a player wins from left to right, their sumo pushes the other sumo back a step. When the player wins from right to left, the player’s sumo is pushed back. There are seven steps in either direction.

Throwing the enemy sumo out of the ring puts the player through to round two of the free spin bonus.

The sumos are randomly assigned and the sumos that are in the action each round determine the mix of wilds and multipliers that are applied to the wins. This very cool, interactive bonus is the major selling point for this unique and strange slot.
