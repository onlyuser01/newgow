The agreement with NSoft will facilitate Oddin's footprint expansion in the esports industry

Esports wagering solutions supplier Oddin.gg announced it has signed a new strategic partnership with platform supplier NSoft to further extend its footprint in the esports industry.

‘Reach New Heights of Success’

The new B2B partnership is set to combine Oddin’s expertise in providing esports betting solutions with NSoft’s extensive knowledge of the esports market and bring benefits to existing betting operator customers and future brand partners.

Marek Suchar, managing director and co-founder of Oddin.gg, highlighted NSoft’s credentials as “a well-known platform and content supplier,” confident that the partnership would help Oddin to “reach new heights of success” and bring its services to a wider audience.

“We share the same vision of innovation, expansion, and product quality, and we’re looking forward to a solid partnership,” Suchar concluded.

The new agreement will allow NSoft’s business clients access to the esports betting experience provided only by titles such as CS:GO, DOTA2, Valorant, and others, offering their players an immersive and engaging experience while Oddin provides them with a comprehensive range of solutions, including iFrame, widgets, 24/7 content, and marketing services to maximize their profits.

Teaming Only with the Best

An esports enthusiast himself, Dario Jurcic, chief executive officer of NSoft, commented on the importance of the ecosystem in the esports industry, adamant that NSoft is always on the lookout for the best esports providers for its client portfolio.

“All this led us to the partnership with Oddin.gg,” Jurcic said, looking forward to bringing Oddin’s “exciting esports content” to NSoft players and “seeing how the future will shape esports in general and especially” in the esports betting industry.

The addition of Oddin’s ecosystem to NSoft’s comprehensive product suite comprising an omnichannel, turnkey betting solution, virtual draw-based games, lotteries, virtual sports betting, and casino content is expected to boost NSoft’s product offering at its core markets and cement its position as a leading platform provider.

NSoft joined a prominent group of Oddin.gg’s partners, including Betway, Yolo Group, LOOT.BET, Aspire Global, Altenar Softswiss, and other sportsbooks and platform providers worldwide benefiting from Oddin’s solutions that combine machine learning algorithms with advanced data science.

Last month, Oddin.gg entered the esports market in the Balkans via an agreement with MaxBet by providing its iFrame solution to the online casino and betting operator active in Montenegro, Serbia and Republika Srpska, one of the two entities comprising Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Earlier this month, Oddin.gg signed an agreement with QTech Games to provide its full product portfolio to the gaming distributor active in Asia.
