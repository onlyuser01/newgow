Ezugi Partners with Sisal for the Italian Market

Ezugi, the live dealer gaming provider, increased its presence in the Italian market after inking a partnership with Sisal today. By partnering with the gaming and lottery giant, Ezugi launched its live roulette offering for Sisal customers last week. As a result, Ezugi’s live roulette content is now available on Sisal’s online casino and sports betting portal. Furthermore, the company revealed that it plans to launch blackjack and punto banco on Sisal’s platform soon.

“This is a significant strategic deal for Ezugi and one that underlines our commitment to the vibrant Italian market.” Rais Busom, territory manager, Ezugi

Rais Busom, Ezugi’s territory manager, said that the partnership is a significant strategic deal for the company. Furthermore, he acknowledged that the deal reaffirms Ezugi’s commitment to the Italian market. Busom stressed that Ezugi is honored to partner with a major brand like Sisal. In conclusion, he said that the recent deal underlines the company’s strategy of “seeking out blue-chip partnerships in exciting growth markets“.
