Playson and Blox Limited Ink a New Partnership

The leading igaming software developer, Playson inked a new partnership with the Italian-based platform provider, Blox Limited earlier this week. Announced on December 1, the agreement strengthens the presence of Playson in the Italian market. Furthermore, with this move, Playson is extending its player reach within the regulated market of Italy where the company has already inked multiple key partnerships.

Under the deal, Blox, which is a part of Blue Ocean Gaming, will be able to distribute hit games by Playson across its operator network. The games include Buffalo Power: Hold and Win, Zeus: Thunder Reels as well as the recently launched Solar King.

Furthermore, as a part of the deal, the Italian-licensed platform provider can deploy Playson’s integration-free engagement tools which include Network Tournaments, Jackpots, and Free Spins. In fact, those tools have already proven to increase player engagement.
