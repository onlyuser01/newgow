The fast-growing developer of games for the online gambling industry, Play’n GO, announced the release of a new cluster pays slot game. On Thursday, the company unveiled details regarding Honey Rush 100, a sequel to its popular Honey Rush game from 2019.

The latest slot invites the players on a sweet adventure and features a hexagonal grid. As a cluster pays slot, to secure a win, players need to clear five or more symbols, which can be flowers, honey or diamonds, among others. Elevating the experience for the players, Honey Rush 100 brings them engaging features that can boost the payout further thanks to wilds and multipliers.

The release of Honey Rush 100 comes on the heels of the latest installment of Hugo, celebrating the company’s iconic series. Earlier this month, Play’n GO released Hugo the Legacy, a new game commemorating Hugo’s 30th anniversary.

The New Slot Offers Engaging Features

The freshly released Honey Rush 100 features vibrant colors and invites the players on an adventure taking place in a forest. The hexagon grid is displayed at the center of the screen while a multiplier, presented as a beehive can be found on the left. Just like other modern slots, Honey Rush 100 offers auto-play options and allows the player to set limits.

George Olekszy, Play’n GO’s head of game retention, shared his excitement about the sequel of the popular slot game. “This slot builds on everything that made the original slot great and amps everything up to 100 in terms of both presentation and features,” he explained. Additionally, Olekszy praised the slot’s Drone Colony options and other engaging features that grant a chance for huge payouts.

“Honey Rush 100 represents what a true sequel should be.“ George Olekszy, head of game retention at Play’n GO

There are plenty of thrilling features that Honey Rush 100 brings. For example, the slot game offers Sticky Wilds that remain in place if they are part of a winning cluster. They can move down, left or right once the winning cluster is removed and can only be removed “when part of a winning cluster in any of the bottom seven spaces, after which they will drop into the Honey Pot,” explains Play’n GO. Once this happens, the game’s multiplier is increased by 1.

Additionally, Honey Rush 100 offers an Overcharge Meter. Players can fill up a gauge which then crystalizes to become the Meter. Then, they can collect up to 15 symbols to upgrade it. Ultimately this feature grants a chance for mouth-watering prizes.
