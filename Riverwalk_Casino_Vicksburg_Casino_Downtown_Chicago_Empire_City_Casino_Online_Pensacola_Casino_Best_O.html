Mississippi Gaming Commission Releases September Results

The regulator in charge of gambling activities including sports betting, the Mississippi Gaming Commission, has announced the latest results covering the performance of sports betting operators in September. Overall, the sports betting handle reported by licensed operators in the state last month hit $43.1 million. A month earlier, in August, the sports betting handle improved slightly when compared to July and hit $21.2 million. When the result in September is compared to the betting handle in August, an increase of 103% is observed.

Despite the increase when compared to August, the betting handle last month decreased when compared to the result from September 2021. Last year in September, the sports betting handle hit $54.8 million, which means that the result from last month marked a 21.3% decrease. Still, after multiple months with decreasing betting handle, the results in August and September improved.
