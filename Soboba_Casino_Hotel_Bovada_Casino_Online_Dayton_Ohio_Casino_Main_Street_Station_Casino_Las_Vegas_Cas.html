A Fin-tastic iGaming Experience

The game comes from Inspired’s Astra studio and was described as a “fin-tastic” online casino experience. The game sports a classic 5×3 look with 10 paylines and innovative mechanics.

Catch of the Day Reeling ‘Em In sports vibrant colors and a lighthearted feeling, allowing players to relax while playing for exciting prizes. During the game, players will collect various symbols representing letters, fishes of various sizes, fishing cups and fishing equipment.

The game has 10 paylines, allowing players to make various combinations to win.

Lucky players that land on three scatter symbols will be taken to a free spins bonus mode where they can earn extra prizes. For each additional scatter symbol they get, they will get 5 extra free spins for a total maximum of 20.

During free spins, gamblers can land on a wild fisherman symbol that automatically claims all fish symbol prizes currently on the reels. Each time players earn a fisherman symbol, they will move a position on the Fisherman Trail. Positions 4, 8 and 12 will award players with 10 additional free spins and a number of respins.

Players can buy free spins in markets where this is allowed.

Players Can Gamble for Multipliers or Free Spins

A gambling feature can be turned on if players desire to risk it all for bigger prizes. Failed gambles will nullify the win but winning ones can double, triple or quadruple the win, or award free spins.

The gambling stage is represented by a wheel with a red part and a green part. Once the feature is triggered an arrow will spin around the wheel. If it lands in the green area, the gamble is successful and the wager will be multiplied. If not, the player will lose their winnings and will be returned to the main game.

Players who win their gamble can continue to gamble until they decide to stop, until they win 2,000x their bet or until they fail. With each consecutive spin, the risks increase but so do the rewards.
