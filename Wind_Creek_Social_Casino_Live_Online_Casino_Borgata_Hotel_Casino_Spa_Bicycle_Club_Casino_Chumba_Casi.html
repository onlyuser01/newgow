iGaming content developer and supplier Spinomenal has secured an entry into the UK market through a new B2B license granted to the company by the UK Gambling Commission. This will effectively enable Spinomenal to pursue partnership opportunities in the highly lucrative market which is undergoing a major shift in regulation.

Spinomenal Secures UK Gambling License

Spinomenal will now be able to deliver on a number of slot games that will be available to licensed UK operators, including Lucky Jack – Lost Jungle, Demi Gods III, Wolf Fang, and many others. This marks the latest expansion by Spinomenal, which has already received approvals from numerous established licensing authorities around Europe.

Spinomenal has been cleared for launches by the Malta Gaming Authority, the Kansspelautoriteit in the Netherlands, and the Spelinspektionen in Sweden. Spinomenal’s head of partnerships, Olga Sirokha, welcomed the opportunity to enter the UK market.

She noted that Spinomenal and its classic slots studio, Retro Gaming, will now be able to deliver quality content to businesses in the United Kingdom, and said:

Our Retro Gaming project is gaining momentum all the time and we expect this to accelerate now we’re able to target UK-licensed operators with its range of classic entertainment products. Spinomenal’s head of partnerships, Olga Sirokha
