A Fruit-Themed Slot with Innovative Features

The Swedish slot developer’s latest title is colorful and combines quality animations with good sound effects. The slot reminds of the classic fruit-themed slots but brings unique functionalities and features including auto spin with customization options.

Considering the famous Megaways mechanics, the new title offers multiple ways for players to win. The game offers six reels and each reel can hold between 2 to 7 symbols. The possible option for a win is in the range of 64 to 117,649, depending on what number of symbols fall on each reel, a famous feature of the Megaways mechanics.

With each spin, players need to land Coins and Coin collect symbols. If there is at least one Coin collect symbol on the reels, all coins are collected and the player is paid. In case there is more than one Coin collect symbol, the prize is multiplied.

Buy Bonus and Free Spins

Players of SpinJoy Society Megaways can trigger a Free Spins bonus round by landing at least 3 Free Spins symbols on the reels. As a result, they can benefit from 10 Free Spins by landing the first three Free Spin symbols. For extra Free Spin symbols, players benefit from 5 additional Free Spins.

The maximum number of Free Spin symbols players can land with a single spin is 42. This would award them a total of 205 Free Spins. With that in mind, players can also benefit from a multiplier that may appear during the bonus game. This multiplier can increase the wins further. According to Lady Luck Games, the multiplier “starts at x1 and has no upper limit” and at the same time applies for Coins and symbols. A unique trait offered by the game is a Buy Bonus feature. Online players that use this feature can start the Free Spins Bonus round at any time.
