How Good is Germany at Basketball?

Oddsmakers don’t have faith in the German national basketball team. They see the Germans as +1200 underdogs for the title, which is understandable knowing that the 2022 EuroBasket is incredibly competitive.

However, knowing that two German cities are going to host most of the EuroBasket games, these guys might stand a chance to get far. Cologne is the place where several matches of the group phase will be played, including France vs Germany game. Berlin is the place where all games of the knockout stage will take place.

Germany will have fans on their side, but the question is can they turn their players into world-winners? Well, the German roster definitely isn’t the best in Europe, but there are some pretty decent players on this team. Dennis Schröder (Houston Rockets) is probably their most famous player, while Johannes Voigtmann (CSKA Moscow) is a big name in Europe.

Then, there are several players from Bayern Munich and Alba Berlin, teams that play in the EuroLeague. That said, this team is probably much stronger than what most basketball fans think. Still, we don’t think they’re strong enough to beat France in the first round of the EuroBasket.
