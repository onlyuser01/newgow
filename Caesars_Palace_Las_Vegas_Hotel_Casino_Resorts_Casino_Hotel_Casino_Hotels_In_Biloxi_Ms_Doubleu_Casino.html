The leading compliance provider group for the iGaming industry, ComplianceOne Group, announced multiple senior-level appointments, expanding its team of experts in an effort to further solidify its position as a leading provider of compliance solutions for the gambling vertical.

Confirmation about the latest appointments came Tuesday and see the addition of Robert Yuile, Martin Hodges, as well as David Schubert, a statement reveals. Each of the experts delivers extensive experience within different verticals, making them a perfect fit for ComplianceOne Group's growing team of seasoned iGaming industry veterans and experts.

Joining the company, Yuile was appointed to a dual role. On one hand, he will serve as the Managing Director of DataVault ONE, while on the other, he will be the COO of ComplianceOne Group. This won't be a challenge for Yuile, considering his wide experience within senior leadership within different globally-recognized businesses. Without any doubt, he brings valuable knowledge and insight, making him a perfect addition to the company's senior management team.

Schubert was appointed to the role of Sales Director. Overall, he brings more than two decades of experience in technology sales, including AI, cloud computing and other related verticals. Last but not least, Hodges joins ComplianceOne Group as its new Director of Marketing. His appointment marks a strategic move for the company as he will spearhead its marketing efforts. Hodges brings a wealth of experience after being a part of iGaming and compliance providers in his 14-year career.

Currently, ComplianceOne Group incorporates some of the online gambling industry's most trusted and recognized compliance brands. Such include the single technical compliance intelligence database company for the iGaming vertical, Complitech, as well as the reliable, advanced and safe solution, DataVault ONE.

Additionally, the Group combines the globally-recognized technical compliance consulting company, Maxima Compliance, as well as the compliance recruitment company for the online gambling sector, RecruitCompliance, and the operational and regulatory compliance company, Diligence in Gaming. Ultimately, this makes ComplianceOne Group a one-stop-shop for operators, as well as suppliers that need assistance with iGaming compliance.

Antonio Zanghi, ComplianceOne Group's CEO, shared his excitement about the latest appointments. "It's a pleasure to welcome Robert, Martin and David to ComplianceOne Group," he said. Additionally, Zanghi pointed out: "Between them, they bring a wealth of experience to the team, and put us in an even stronger position to deliver the full suite of compliance products and services our partners have come to rely upon." Last but not least, ComplianceOne Group's CEO acknowledged that the experts mark a fine addition to the company's team and will help it continue to grow by delivering compliance solutions.
