Leading Swedish game studio Lady Luck Games announced a new deal strengthening its presence in Latin America. The company said Friday it joined forces with Logrand Entertainment Group.

Logrand Entertainment and Lady Luck Games Ink New Deal

Under the terms of the new deal with Logrand Entertainment Group, Lady Luck Games agreed to provide its leading gaming content for Strendus.mx. Logrand was founded back in 2005 and by now it is a leading operator in Mexico. The company currently operates multiple land-based casinos, as well as Strendus Casino, which is its online brand that was launched back in 2018.

The new deal will bring popular high-quality games for Strendus customers in Mexico including the Ruler of Egypt, Mr. Alchemister, as well as Weight of the Gun. What’s more, the new collaboration is expected to further expand Strendus’ offering by adding more leading games from the popular studio. Those titles are expected to include Beetle Bailey, The Treasures of Tizoc and Valholl Wild Hammers. According to Lady Luck Games, the titles will be available for Strendus via Slot Matrix.

“We are glad to welcome Lady Luck Games on board.“ Eduardo Pelaez, senior product manager at Logrand Entertainment Group

Eduardo Pelaez, Logrand Entertainment’s senior product manager, revealed that the company is delighted to join forces with Lady Luck Games and expand its offering. He added that thanks to the latest deal, Logrand Entertainment will deliver exciting new experiences for its customers.

Lady Luck Continues Its Global Expansion

LL Lucky Games AB’s co-founder and CEO, Mads Jørgensen, shared his excitement saying that the company continues to grow its presence in Latin America. He acknowledged that the market holds significant potential which makes it significantly important for gaming companies. Jørgensen added that the company is excited to bring its leading gaming titles to customers of Logrand Entertainment in Mexico. Last but not least, he pointed out that Lady Luck is looking forward to growing further and strengthening this collaboration.

“We are thrilled to be able to deliver our gaming content to Logrand Entertainment Group, and we look forward to strengthening our collaboration in the future.“ Mads Jørgensen, co-founder and CEO of LL Lucky Games AB

The latest deal with Logrand Entertainment comes after only recently, Lady Luck launched SpinJoy Society Megaways slot for Novibet customers in Greece. The announcement came earlier this month and marked the first Megaways title for Greek customers of Novibet.
