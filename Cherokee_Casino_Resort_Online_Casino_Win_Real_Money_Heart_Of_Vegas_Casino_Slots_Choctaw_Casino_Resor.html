Rocket, Tumble, and Free Spins Features

The new slot introduces players to a series of intriguing features that guarantee a more lucrative experience. Among them, we can mention the Rocket feature that will cause all symbols underneath its point of landing on the reels to turn into wild symbols.

In turn, the wilds will lower their position after each new tumble for greater chances of scoring a win.

The Tumble feature removes all winning combos from play and replaces them with a fresh set of symbols from above. The tumbling action will go on until there are no more wins possible, finally awarding all payouts to players.

Landing at least four Scatter symbols on any random spin will engage the Free Spins feature, awarding a minimum of 10 free games for starters. Players will be given the opportunity to either gamble or collect their amount of free games prior to the beginning of the round for a chance to scoop up five extra free spins.
