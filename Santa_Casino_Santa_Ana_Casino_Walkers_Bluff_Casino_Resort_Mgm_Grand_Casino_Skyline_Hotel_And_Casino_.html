AffPapa is strengthening the lineup of partners for the upcoming iGaming Awards 2023, the second edition of the event, which will see the company distinguish the very best companies in the industry over the past 12 months.

CasinoCanada steps in as the event’s Diamond Sponsor, owing this to its established track record in providing trustworthy and safe affiliate services to Canadian consumers over the past 20 years. CasinoCanada focuses on detailed reviews and analyses of the country’s best online casinos and is largely seen as an authority on all things iGaming in Canada, says AffPapa in a press release.

Therefore, the company is very happy to see CasinoCanada join as the Diamond Sponsor for the event and further contribute to the success of AffPapa iGaming Awards 2023. The event will reunite various stakeholders from the industry and allow them to compete across a number of categories in which they will be able to showcase their achievements.

Commenting on the opportunity to work closely with AffPapa, CasinoCanada Head of Content Dmitry Rogalchuk said that his company was happy to participate in the event which strives to celebrate the achievements of the online gambling industry.

"We want to say thank you to the people who do affiliate marketing for their hard work and dedication, so we’re happy to be sponsoring this year’s AffPapa iGaming Awards. We believe that it’s because of these people and their love for what they do that this industry is successful and always changing," Rogalchuk added.

The opportunity was welcomed by AffPapa Head of Commercials Yeva Avagyan who was similarly excited to see CasinoCanada choose to join AffPapa iGaming Awards as the gathering’s Diamond Sponsor.

"The affiliate website’s expertise and reputation in the Canadian market is unparalleled, and the team’s commitment to celebrating the online gambling industry’s brightest stars is truly inspirational," Avagyan said, welcoming CasinoCanada and saying that the entire team at AffPapa is looking forward to working with the affiliate firm.

The AffPapa iGaming Awards 2023 are coming and for those interested in attending, the time to get a ticket is now. The event is taking place on June 20, 2023, in Valletta, Malta.
