Resorts World Las Vegas to Bring Unrivaled Entertainment with Zouk Group

On Thursday this week, Resorts World Las Vegas and Zouk Group entered into a key partnership. Under the partnership, Zouk Group will bring an ecosystem of immersive entertainment and lifestyle concepts to the integrated resort. The $4.3 billion resort is being developed by the Malaysian corporation Genting Berhad and is planned to open for visitors next summer.

The new agreement brings multiple experiences within one entertainment complex which will feature both a nightclub and beach club, an innovative gaming bar as well as a dining venue influenced by the Eastern culture. The lifestyle and entertainment spaces will spread across approximately 100,000 square feet of space and will feature four distinctive environments:

Zouk Nightclub

AYU Dayclub

RedTail gaming bar

FUHU dining venue

According to the announcement by Resorts World Las Vegas, the innovative Zouk Nightclub will be the most technologically advanced nightclub in Las Vegas. Furthermore, the outdoor AYU Dayclub will represent an “oasis inspired by the beauty and harmonious atmosphere of Southeast Asia’s idyllic islands“.

Visitors of the new RedTail gaming bar will be able to enjoy premium beverages and shared plates while at the same time playing games such as beer pool, darts, and beer pong. Furthermore, guests who want to enjoy a world-class dining experience can visit the FUHU which is a “high-energy, experiential dining venue“.

Zouk Group to Debut for the U.S. at Resorts World Las Vegas

According to Scott Sibella, President of Resorts World Las Vegas, the company aimed at “reinventing the traditional integrated hospitality experience in Las Vegas across every sector, including nightlife“. He outlined that in partnership with Zouk Group, Resorts World will be able to offer a world-class entertainment complex that features multiple unmatched nightlife experiences available for guests and visitors.

“The partnership between Zouk Group and Resorts World Las Vegas will bring entertainment and lifestyle brands together on a property destined to breathe new life into the Las Vegas market.” Andrew Li, Chief Executive Officer of Zouk Group

Andrew Li, Chief Executive Officer of Zouk Group outlined that the company is proud to make its debut in the United States at Resorts World Las Vegas. With that in mind, Zouk Group has over 29 years of experience in the nightlife industry.

The leading lifestyle and entertainment company is from Singapore and owns establishments spanning across nightlife entertainment as well as food and beverage sectors. Further details regarding the concept of each entertainment space as well as a schedule for future performances are expected to be released in the next few months.
