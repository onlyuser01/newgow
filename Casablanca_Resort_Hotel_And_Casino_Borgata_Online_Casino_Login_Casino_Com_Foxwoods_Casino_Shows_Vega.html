Developer of online casino slot games PopOK Gaming announced today that its iGaming offering has successfully passed the certification process in Romania and will soon become available to players in the country.

Portfolio of Engaging Slots

Commenting on the certification, Tsovinar Elchyan, gaming product manager at PopOK Gaming, could not hide his excitement that the firm’s games would soon become available to players in the region, hailing the news as a great start for the business that is seeking to build strong relationships with local online gaming operators and reach new heights together with its partners.

The certification granted by the national gambling regulator in Romania gives local players the needed level of assurance that the in-house developed game titles that PopOK Gaming would soon deploy in the market would provide a secure gaming environment for their online gaming experience.

For operators, the certificate acquisition means that they can partner with the game developer and offer PopOK Gaming’s high-quality gaming solutions to its customers.
