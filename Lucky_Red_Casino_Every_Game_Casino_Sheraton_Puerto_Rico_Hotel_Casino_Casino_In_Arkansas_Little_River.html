The latest collaboration will help the leading development studio reach new audiences in different markets around the world

BF Games, the leading development studio offering dynamic games for the online gambling industry, announced a new strategic content agreement, allowing it to significantly expand its reach. The company revealed Wednesday it joined forces with the iGaming behemoth, SOFTSWISS. Per the freshly minted deal, BF Games agreed to supply its portfolio of games for SOFTSWISS, delivering its unique titles to new audiences across the globe.

Currently, BF Games offers a growing portfolio of more than 80 unique slot games. Thanks to the new collaboration, those games will become available via SOFTSWISS’ Game Aggregator, the company’s leading iGaming content hub. The new content deal will enable clients in new markets to experience BF Games’ thrilling portfolio.

Under the terms of the new agreement, SOFTSWISS’ partners and their clients will tap into top-performing and fan-favorite titles from the iGaming developer. Such include the famous Chicken Madness Ultra, Stunning Cash, as well as Horizon Hunters, among others.

Famous as a provider of dynamic slots that stand out with engaging features, BF Games already has a strong presence in strategic markets such as Latin America, North America, as well as Europe. Now, thanks to the collaboration with SOFTSWISS, the company will expand further its global footprint, bringing its unique games to new customers.

Late last month, BF Games joined forces with SkillOnNet for a new content deal. That collaboration boosted further the iGaming developer’s presence within the iGaming vertical while at the same time expanding SkillOnNet’s offering.

Focusing once again on the latest deal, by teaming up with BF Games, SOFTSWISS expands the offering of its Game Aggregator. The company’s partners can now benefit from more than 16,000 games available within the aggregator platform and that portfolio continues to grow.
