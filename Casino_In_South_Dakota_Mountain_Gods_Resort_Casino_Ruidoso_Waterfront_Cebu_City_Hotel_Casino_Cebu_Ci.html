Gambling tech supplier International Game Technology (IGT) announced today its entire cashless gaming solution is now approved for deployment in Nevada following the regulatory approval gained for its Resort Wallet module of the ADVANTAGETM casino management system.

Green Light for Statewide Deployment

The announcement that IGT’s Resort Wallet completed the final stages of the regulatory approval process in Nevada further strengthened IGT’s market position as a leader in the cashless space, offering casino operators in the state a turnkey, fully integrated cashless gaming solution with external funding.

“IGT earning Nevada regulatory approval for our award-winning fully integrated cashless solution is a perfect bookend for a year marked with tremendous momentum for our Resort Wallet and IGTPay cashless gaming modules of the IGT ADVANTAGE system.” Ryan Reddy, SVP Global PM VLT, Systems, Payments & Poker, IGT

“This milestone achievement will allow for players and operators throughout the State to enjoy the many benefits of cashless gaming and reinforces Nevada’s reputation as a future-forward gaming jurisdiction that is committed to advancing the player experience.” Ryan Reddy, SVP Global PM VLT, Systems, Payments & Poker, IGT

IGTPay utilizes a wide range of external payment sources, including bank accounts, credit and debit cards, and Sightline Play+ prepaid accounts.
