Caesars Sportsbook Introduces Micro-Betting with New Partnership

Forbes reported that Caesars Entertainment has signed a partnership agreement with Simplebet, which will see Caesars Sportsbook enriched with real-money micro-betting.

This means that Caesars Sportsbook clients in all 17 US states where its mobile application is available will have the opportunity to place bets on all micro-events happening during matches from the NFL, NBA, NHL, college football, and basketball from December 15, while micro-bets on the MLB will be available once the season starts next year.

Caesars Sportsbook is the third major partner of Simplebet introducing micro-betting with the help of Simplebet’s products. The company has previously signed partnerships with DraftKings and bet365.

With the help of machine learning and automated processes, Simplebet is able to offer micro-betting, which turns every minute of any sports event into a betting opportunity.

What is Micro-Betting?

Micro-betting is the latest trend in sports betting which is gaining more and more traction in the gambling industry with companies such as Simplebet and Betr revolutionizing the market.

Micro-betting allows bettors to place live-action in-game wagers on every event during a sports match, which is not necessarily connected to the final result. This could be the outcome of the next pitch in baseball or the outcome of the next free throw in basketball. The possibilities are practically limitless.

What is the most characteristic feature of micro-betting is that it is quick. The events bettors can place wagers on happen very fast so the bettors themselves have to be quick in placing their wagers. The results also come quickly making this form of sports betting intense and very exciting.
