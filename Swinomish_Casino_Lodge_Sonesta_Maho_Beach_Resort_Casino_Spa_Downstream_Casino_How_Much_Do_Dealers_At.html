Manchester City Going for the Treble in 2023

Pep Guardiola’s men have reached the final of the FA Cup, as well as the semifinal of the Champions League. They also have a very good chance to win the Premier League again. So, can they win all three of those?

They will surely try. Their recent form is brilliant, which gives them the confidence they can do the treble this year. It’s not going to be easy, but they do have an excellent roster. Practically all of their key men are fit and ready for the battle, which explains why bookies see them as the favorites in the next game.

Another reason why they’re expected to win on Wednesday is that the game is played on their turf. At Etihad Stadium, the Cityzens are 10-0 in their last ten games in all competitions and are without a single loss since November when they slipped against Brentford.

Alarm Bells Sounding Off in Arsenal’s Locker Room

At the beginning of the month, the Gunners had an 11-point lead over the Cityzens. Right now, that number is only 5, and the worst thing is that they’ve already played two games more than Man City. What this practically means is that if they lose on Wednesday, they will probably drop out of the EPL summit.

What needs to worry them ahead of their trip to the North of England is that their recent form is not great. In fact, they played a draw in each of their three league games.

Another worry is that they have some bad experiences from their trips to Manchester. They lost each of the last seven games played at Etihad Stadium. Further, the last time they won at this stadium was in 2015.

Manchester City vs Arsenal Prediction
