Kanter will be tasked with leading Continent 8’s advisory practice and will support new gaming and betting clients

Continent 8 Technologies, an award-winning provider of managed hosting, connectivity, cloud and security solutions, has reinforced its team with a major new appointment. As announced, the company will welcome the seasoned regulatory veteran Jeremie Kanter to its team, putting him in charge of Continent 8’s compliance-related matters.

As a member of Continent 8, Kanter will serve as the company’s director of regulatory affairs. In this new position, the hire will be tasked with overseeing the firm’s new C8 Comply division.

In addition to that, Kanter will be tasked with leading Continent 8’s advisory practice and will support new gaming and betting clients who seek to navigate the complex regulatory landscape.

Continent 8 described its new director of regulatory affairs as an experienced individual, listing some of his previous positions. Prior to his current role, Kanter held a variety of senior management and consulting positions at leading gambling companies, such as William Hill, PokerStars, SBTech, Playtech and Stations Casino, among others.

According to Continent 8, the appointment comes at a very important time for the company, which continues to seek international expansion in key regulated markets. The company seeks to leverage its knowledge and robust solutions to cement itself as the go-to infrastructure provider for operators around the globe.

Kanter Is Looking Forward to Working with the Company

Continent 8 Technologies’ founder and CEO, Michael Tobin, welcomed the new hire on board, praising him as “one of the most experienced and skilled regulatory and compliance specialists in the industry.” Because of that, he would be an incredibly valued asset to the Continent 8 team, the CEO added.

Regulation and compliance are the foundation of Continent 8’s business and bringing Jeremie on board as we continue to grow aggressively across regulated markets will be fundamental to our success in these jurisdictions, as well as those where we currently support our customers. Michael Tobin, founder & CEO, Continent 8 Technologies

Jeremie Kanter also commented on his new appointment, expressing his excitement to join “one of the most established and reputable companies in the sector.” He praised Continent 8 for having supported its customers in regulated markets for 25 years now.

Kanter concluded: “I know the team well and I am excited to be joining such a reputed company, and looking forward to leveraging my industry experience and skillset to help Continent 8 achieve its ambitious goals.”
