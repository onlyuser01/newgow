The Popular Franchise Gets a Powerful Feature Boost

In a way, Win Ways resembles the Megaways mechanic but with its own twist, of course. Thanks to the addition of the Win Ways feature into Book of Ra Deluxe, the game will now come with up to six reels and 117,648 paylines.
