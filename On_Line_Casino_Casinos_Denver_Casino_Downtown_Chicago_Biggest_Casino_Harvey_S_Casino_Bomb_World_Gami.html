FunFair Technologies’ Purser to Outline Future of Blockchain Gaming

FunFair COO Lloyd Purser will attend the World Gaming Executive Summit (WGES) representing the company, which will be both a sponsor as well as a participant in various panels hosted during the expo.

Scheduled for December 8 through December 9, the virtual industry meet-up will reunite top talent and insiders form the world of iGaming. For his part, Mr Purser will discuss the significance of blockchain gaming and the adoption of cryptocurrencies in the iGaming sector.

In its entirety, WGES will hold a number of live presentations, panel debates, roundtables and conversations broaching various topics from the world of gaming. Apart from sponsoring the event, FunFair intends to make as much impact on the industry by providing valuable insights into the adoption and significance of blockchain technology.

Mr. Purser will host a fireside chat with Mattias Wedar, LeoVegas CPTO, and Tobias Svensen, CEO at CasinoGrounds, and together the trio will discuss the importance of attracting Gen Y and Gen Z audiences.

He will further talk about the things that new entrants in the blockchain space should be careful about when get involved in the blockchain business and provide further insight as part of a discussion with Gregory Kirstein from PaySafe touching on the topic of iGaming payments.

FunFair’s Year of Successes and an Exciting 2021

FunFair is about to start 2021 on a strong note with the company’s first multiplayer game launching. The title, aptly called ‘Rocketeers, To The Moon’, is FunFair’s first attempt at a fundamentally different gaming model that should elevate the blockchain and iGaming experience in Q1 2020.

The product emulates ‘crash’ style games and the end result is targeted at younger generations of consumers. Sharing his opinion about the upcoming event, Mr Purser had this to add: “GES is well-known as one of the most exclusive events on the industry calendar and one that always offers an agenda of thought-provoking panels and high-level presentations from sector executives.”

He added that FunFair is excited to play a key role in the upcoming virtual meeting and that the company was excited about the opportunity to share its expertise with others as well as exchange know-how.
