The leading provider of software, powering the growing online gambling and betting industry, NuxGame, announced it secured an important certification. On Wednesday, the company revealed that it was awarded a GLI-19 certification. Securing the certification represents an important milestone for NuxGame, a statement released by the company reveals.

The iGaming and betting software supplier confirmed that the GLI-19 certification recognizes the hard work and dedication of its team of experts. Additionally, NuxGame confirmed the certification reaffirms its commitment to delivering exceptional, innovative and secure software for the growing online gambling sector.

The certification was awarded by the gaming industry's leading testing and certification services provider, Gaming Laboratories International (GLI). The successful GLI-19 certification follows a rigorous assessment and testing process. Throughout this extensive process, strategic policies as well as solutions for NuxGame have undergone evaluation.

Denis Kosinsky, NuxGame's Chief Operating Officer, commented on the topic: "The GLI-19 certification is a globally recognized integrity standard that paves the way for NuxGame to enter new markets as well as demonstrating our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement." Finally, he pointed out: "NuxGame prides itself in being the trustworthy and secure platform provider to the iGaming industry and our latest accreditation underscores just this."

According to NuxGame, GLI-19 is a "coveted certification focusing exclusively on interactive gaming systems." As such, it recognizes some of the best standards and practices for the global online gambling sector. Additionally, the company explained that this prestigious certification complements its commitment to meeting the highest industry standards for security and quality.

Securing the certification, NuxGame anticipates further growth and expansion within the iGaming vertical. The company said that the recognition will help it reach new partners across the globe that will benefit from its innovative and reliable services.

The latest achievement comes on the heels of another important announcement by NuxGame. Last year, the company confirmed it expanded its list of supported cryptocurrency and blockchain options.

At the time, NuxGame revealed it added Metamask, Phantom and Aptos crypto wallets to its platform. This strategic announcement further diversified the range of crypto payment options the company provides for its partners.
