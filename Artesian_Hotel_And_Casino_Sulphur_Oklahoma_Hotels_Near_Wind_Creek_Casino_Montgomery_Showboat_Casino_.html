Enteractive to Help Coolbet Reactive Consumers in Latin America

Enteractive will implement a number of its reactivation and retention tools with Coolbet and help it to ensure that there are fewer registered non-funded player accounts, as well as conduct targeted reactivation campaigns designed to connect with lapsed players.

Enteractive puts a great focus on a personalized approach that allows it to elicit strong results and responses from players.
