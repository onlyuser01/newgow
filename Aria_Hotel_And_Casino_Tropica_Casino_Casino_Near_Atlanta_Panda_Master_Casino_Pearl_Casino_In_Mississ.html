The leading global online poker room, GGPoker, has announced details regarding new online micro tournaments called MicroMillion$.

MicroMillion$ Poker Tournament Series Start June 19

The new tournament series will feature multiple events and are planned to launch on June 19 and run through July 3. GGPoker unveiled that overall, there will be $5 million guaranteed in prize money. The poker room said in a statement that MicroMillion$ is a perfect fit for anyone bored with the old tournament series. Moreover, the poker room said that the new series will bring exciting and fun experiences for all poker fans.

If you’re sick of the same old tournament series, then the new MicroMILLION$ is for you. We’re aiming to put the fun and excitement back into poker, MicroMILLION$ tournaments are for everyone to enjoy, reads a statement released by GGPoker

The buy-ins for the MicroMillion$ tournament series start from as low as $1.05. On the other hand, the MicroMillion$ Main Event has a $25 buy-in and features a guaranteed $1,000,000.

Other events part of the series that are worth mentioning include Mini Global Million$. The $5 buy-in event features a $250,000 guaranteed prize pool. Poker fans can also benefit from the Mystery Bounty $10.50 buy-in event that has a $1 million guaranteed prize pool.

Additionally, poker fans can also join the Sunday Super Storm event. The event features a $101,000 guaranteed prize pool, with a buy-in set for $11. Last but not least, GGPoker revealed that there will be thousands of free MicroMillion$ tickets that will be given away.

Besides the new tournament series, GGPoker announced it selected Jeff Gross and Ali Nejad as Brand Ambassadors earlier this week. The new collaboration will see Nejad join GGPoker as Media Ambassador, while Gross will become the Voice of GGPoker.
