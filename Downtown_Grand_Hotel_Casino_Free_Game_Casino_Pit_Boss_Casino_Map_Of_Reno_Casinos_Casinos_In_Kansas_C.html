Gates of Olympus review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed on Greek mythology.

Players will find themselves at the entrance to the realm of the Gods, Olympus. An ominous Zeus hovers at the gate, daring those who approach to be worthy.

Up against reels of precious gems and mysterious artifacts, players will face a trial to enter the heavenly kingdom. The violet sky seems temptingly just beyond the gates.

This slot has an epic mood and lots of mythical drama.

Gates of Olympus Gameplay

Gates of Olympus is a six reel, five row slot.

Gates of Olympus has cascade system instead of usual win lines

Wins are awarded for landing certain numbers of matching symbols anywhere on the reels, with increasing payouts for larger numbers. This effectively turns every symbol into a Scatter symbol.

The cascade feature takes place after every spin as winning combinations pay out and then the winning symbols vanish. The symbols that are left drop to the bottom of the screen and then the empty positions are filled with fresh symbols. Players may receive unlimited cascades.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins in Gates of Olympus

There is no Wild symbol in this slot.

The Scatter symbol may appear on any reel and pays on any position.

There are four Multiplier symbols that may be present on all reels. Whenever a player lands a Multiplier symbol, they receive a random multiplier value from x2 to x500.

After the end of a cascade feature sequence, all the Multiplier symbols are added together and the total win of the sequence is multiplied by the final value.

Players are awarded the Free Spins feature when four or more Scatter symbols land anywhere. Players begin the round starting with 15 free spins. Whenever Multiplier symbols land during the Free Spins feature, the Multiplier value is added to the total multiplier.

If players land three or more Scatter symbols while in the Free Spins feature, they win five extra free spins.
