3 Buzzing Wilds review

3 Buzzing Wilds is a bee-themed online slot game developed by Pragmatic Play. The slot focuses on a hive of friendly bees as they gather honey.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The 3 Buzzing Wilds slot sticks closely to the blueprint laid out by similarly themed titles. The Wild Swarm slot game from Push Gaming would be the closest comparison, with both games putting a cheery spin on the bee genre.

Mega win in the 3 Buzzing Wilds slot machine

The creatures you encounter in the 3 Buzzing Wilds slot feature a cheery bee, a beautiful butterfly, and a charming caterpillar. The reels are placed in the middle of a brightly colored forest, with a glowing light illuminating the screen and helping to bring the action to life.

Overall, it’s a simple design, but it sets a fun tone for the game’s action.

Players can choose out of 3 options for the bonus round

Gameplay

3 Buzzing Wilds keeps things simple with its base game action. The slot has five reels, each of which displays four symbols. There are 20 fixed paylines. Players will win when they land matching combinations of three or more across a payline, starting from the leftmost reel.

If you want to let the bees do the hard work for you, you can use the autospin option, which is found under the spin button on the control panel beneath the reels.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

All of the action in 3 Buzzing Wilds is built around Wild symbols. There are three different Wild modifiers that can land.

The first is the Random Wilds feature. This does as its name suggests, with random Wild symbols being added to the grid. Next is the Expanding Wild. This will land and then expand to fill its reel. Finally, there is the Surrounding Wilds. When this lands, it will turn the symbols around it into Wilds.

Random Wild symbols are one of the bonus features of the game

Landing three or more Scatters will trigger the game’s Free Spins round. At the start of the round, players get to choose from one of three options. They can have ten spins with the Random Wild feature, eight with the Expanding Wilds, or six with the Surrounding Wilds. During the bonus spins, whichever ones the players choose, the Wild symbols are sticky.

Depending on your location, you may also have the chance to buy into the Free Spins bonus for 100x your stake.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

3 Buzzing Wilds uses a maths model found in a lot of Pragmatic Play slots. The maximum win of the game is a solid 5,000x your stake.

It has been given a full 5/5 volatility rating by the developer, making it the ideal slot to play, according to our online slot strategy guide. The RTP comes in at 96.03%, putting it around average.

The stake is adjusted using the +/- buttons on either side of the spin icon. The minimum bet is $0.20, and the maximum is $100, giving this a solid betting range.
