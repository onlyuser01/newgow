Piggy Bjorn review

Theme and Storyline

Piggy Bjorn is themed around a fantasy world of viking pigs.

Players will ride into a realm of ice, nordic magic, and warrior swine. As they learn to wield a variety of elegant weapons, they’ll begin a hunt for mythical treasure.

This slot is a good fit for players who are in the mood for a viking theme with a creative twist.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The epic soundtrack is infused with ethereal flute notes and a steady drum beat. The ambient sounds of horses whinnying and distant rumbles add a visceral feel to the setting.

On the reels, players will see glowing swords, bows and arrows, shields, and a host of ruins carved into stone. This reinforces the sense that tradition and battle skills are crucial in this unforgiving world.

The reels themselves are crafted from wood and iron, with a mountain landscape visible in the distance. The snowy peaks are tinged with pink light suggesting sunrise.

The pig characters are animated in a cartoon style with fierce expressions and elaborate viking ensembles. Colorful face paint, horned helmets, and punk-rock hairstyle give these warrior hogs an intimidating aesthetic.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Piggy Bjorn create a unique fantasy world that fans of Norse culture will have fun with.

Gameplay

Piggy Bjorn is a five reel, four row slot with 50 win lines.

Wins are calculated from left to right, and only the highest win on each payline is awarded.

The maximum win for a session is x50,000 players’ total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter, Mega Scatter, Piggy Loot, and Piggy Collect symbols.

The Piggy Loot symbol can appear on reels 1, 2, 3, and 4 during the base game, and it carries a randomized multiplier between x1 and x20.

The Piggy Collect symbol lands only on reel 5 during the base game. If at least one Piggy Collect symbol lands along with a Piggy Loot symbol, the values of the Piggy Loot symbols are combined and paid out.

When players land at least 3 Scatters, the Raid Bonus begins, as each Scatter starts the bonus with a random feature. Players will begin with up to 5 free spins, one for each triggering Scatter, with the spin count resetting each time a new symbol lands. During this feature, the reels can expand a maximum of two times if you fill them entirely.

If all the reels are filled with symbols in every possible position, the Raid Bonus will conclude, and players will be awarded a x500 Full Reel Bonus.

Mega Scatter symbols will award players the Fury Taxes symbol during the Raid Bonus. This symbol adds all visible values to its own.

A great variety of additional special symbols with distinct special features and multipliers are available. Some consume the values of other symbols, while others contribute their value to other symbols.

Players can buy directly into the bonus by selecting the Buy Bonus button.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.44%, above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%. This can be increased to 97.94% during the special features.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.50 up to a maximum of $/£/€40.00 per spin.

The volatility of Piggy Bjorn is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. We suggest a strategy of targeting the highest possible volatility.
