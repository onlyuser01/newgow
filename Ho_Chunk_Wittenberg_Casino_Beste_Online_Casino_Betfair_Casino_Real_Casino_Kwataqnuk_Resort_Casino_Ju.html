Juventus Looking for a Way to Salvage the Season

After a shocking 3-0 home loss to Villarreal two weeks ago, Juventus got knocked out of the Champions League. As per some reports, the defeat caused a real uproar in Juve’s locker room, with some players reportedly asking to be traded.

Nevertheless, things settled a few days after the loss, as evident from the fact that Juventus defeated Salernitana 2-0 in Serie A. Sure, beating the bottom-placed team isn’t much of an excitement, but the win did probably serve as a confidence boost to Juve ahead of this week’s challenge.

It’s not going to be easy for them on Sunday, but the black-and-whites need to go for a win. A victory would mean leapfrogging Inter in the #3 position in Serie A standings, as well as getting closer to AC Milan and Napoli.

Injury Troubles for Inter’s Offense

Inter scored a goal in each of their previous three games. Two of those were Serie A games, in which the reigning champion was seen as the betting favorite. Unfortunately for Il Nerazzurri, they drew 1-1 both with Torino and Fiorentina.

As a result, they dropped to the #3 place in the Serie A table. On the bright side, they’ve played one game fewer than the two teams currently sitting above them. The bad news is that taking advantage of that fact isn’t going to be easy. Why? Because in order to win games, Inter players need to start scoring more.

The trouble is that a couple of Inter’s key players are sidelined. Lautaro Martinez, who’s their top scorer in Serie A is out with an illness. Further, Marcelo Brozovic is injured, the same as Matias Vecino.

Juventus vs Inter Prediction
