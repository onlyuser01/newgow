The deal will expand Push Gaming’s presence in the Nordics while reinforcing Chanz as a leading local brand

Push Gaming, a business-to-business provider of gaming content, has signed a multi-market deal with the gambling operator Chanz. As agreed, the former company will supply content to its new partner in several markets.

The partnership will see Push Gaming integrate its entire portfolio of casino games into Chanz’s platform, thus expanding its reach in Denmark, Estonia and Sweden. As a result, players in those markets will get to play some of Push Gaming’s best titles, including hits such as Fat Rabbit and Jammin’ Jars.

What makes Chanz different from other casinos is the social element it adds to the casino experience. Thanks to that, the operator has quickly solidified its position as a fast-growing brand in multiple regulated markets.

The deal will expand Push Gaming’s overall footprint, helping it increase its presence in the Nordics. The arrangement is the latest in a string of agreements with notable operators. Some of the supplier’s latest moves include deals with companies such as Pokerstars, Sky Bet and ComeOn Group.

Push Gaming continues to expand its global presence thanks to its focused commercial development.
