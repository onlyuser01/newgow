The fast-growing business-to-business (B2B) aggregation platform, REEVO, announced a new deal with the global B2B provider of live lottery, live casino games and retail solutions for gambling operators, Ezugi. Per the terms of the new deal, REEVO will benefit from Ezugi's current content which will expand its platform. Besides the existing portfolio of games, the new collaboration encompasses future releases, allowing REEVO to continue to expand its aggregation platform with upcoming games from the popular provider.

Ultimately, the partnership serves a dual purpose as it will help solidify the position of REEVO as a leading aggregator on a global scale. This deal will help the company bring new games to its growing portfolio of partners. On the other hand, the partnership will enable Ezugi's content to reach new audiences around the globe, expanding the reach of its innovative and engaging games.
