Preparing to Launch Sports Betting

‘Imperative’ to Utilize Technology

Ray Pineault, president and chief executive officer of Mohegan, commented that for Mohegan, “it is imperative [to] align strategically with brands” that will bring innovation and provide its guests with “state-of-the-art technology to enhance their experience.”

“We are pleased to partner with SUZOHAPP and to introduce their sports betting products to our properties in Niagara,” he added.
