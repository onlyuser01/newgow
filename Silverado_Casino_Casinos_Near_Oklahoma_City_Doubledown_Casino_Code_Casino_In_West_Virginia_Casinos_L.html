The SuperDraft Free SportsBook aims at creating new ways to engage with casual sport fans by offering them a chance to utilize their knowledge in sports and win cash prizes, and thus complement SuperDraft’s fan-friendly DFS offering.

In addition, the app is looking to provide fun for sports fans while competing against their friends and among themselves and leverage social gaming experience by letting them make big bets on their favorite sports with no financial risk.

Exciting Prize Pool

Cash, gift cards, consumer electronic gadgets, tickets for sports and concert events, as well as experiences from Caesars Rewards are part of the prize pool for the free-to-play app.

“The SuperDraft Free SportsBook is unlike anything fans have ever seen. We can’t wait to reward players with exclusive Caesars experiences and big-ticket prizes.” Steve Wang, CEO & Founder, SuperDraft

The SuperDraft Free SportsBook, developed by Nate Hunter, chief technology officer at SuperDraft, in partnership with Sportnco, will offer free-to-play sports betting on all major leagues in the US and some global markets, by utilizing straight and parlay bets, round-robin and live in-game bets, with daily, weekly and monthly leaderboards displaying who is eligible for a prize by the end of the period.

A social gaming currency, SuperCoin, is used in the app as a token currency to place bets and to redeem prizes from the in-app store, besides the Caesars Rewards credits players can use for vacation packages and other experiences across Caesars properties.
