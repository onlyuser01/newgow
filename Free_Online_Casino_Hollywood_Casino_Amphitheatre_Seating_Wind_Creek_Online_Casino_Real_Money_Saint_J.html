GG.BET, an online esports betting brand has made a foray into the United Kingdom after Rednines Gaming secured approval to use the trademark in the country. As a result, British players will be able to place exciting wagers on their favorite esports disciplines.

The company reassured customers that their current accounts and active balances will be saved. Old Dr.Bet customers will thus be able to use their credentials to log in and play with the rebranded operator.

With GG.BET now available in the UK, local players will get to experience intense sports ad esports betting experiences. The site will offer a huge selection of wagering markets, appealing to many sports and esports fans.

In addition to that, GG.BET will also offer casino slots, as well as a generous bonus program for new and existing users. Finally, the company will offer reliable customer support, helping its clients find resolutions to all their queries.
