Nashville Predators Name Bally’s Official Sports Betting Partner

The new deal between Bally’s and Predators is the first partnership with an NHL franchise for the global gaming giant. Moreover, the new collaboration marks the company’s first deal with a professional sports team in Tennessee. Under the deal, Bally’s became an Official Sports Betting Partner for the Predators. Additionally, Bally’s is now the Official Free-to-Play Partner of the franchise. Under the deal which is set to run through 2025, Bally’s gained access to Nashville Predators intellectual property rights. Moreover, the company is granted access to Predators’ logos, marks as well as other content rights.

A New Free-To-Play Game Will Commemorate the Deal

Commemorating the partnership, Bally’s will launch a new fan-engaging free-to-play game dubbed Smashville Selector. The players will be asked to guess the correct answers for five questions related to upcoming Predators games.

Then, the top competitor after each game will receive a prize. Moreover, at the end of the season, grand prizes will be distributed to Predators’ fans that had the highest scores. The launch of Smashville Selector continues Bally’s line of dynamic mobile sports apps.
