Kyiv will host the second Ukrainian Gaming Week on June 22 and 23. This summer, Ukraine’s major gambling exhibition will again bring together gambling industry representatives from across the world at the International Exhibition Center.

Tickets are now available! Join us to obtain access to market insights, talk to industry leaders, share experience and establish useful business contacts.

UGW 2022 Program

The second Ukrainian Gaming Week will be even greater with a more comprehensive program. So, how will the summer exhibition impress guests?

Participation from Top Gaming Companies

At UGW 2022, leading Ukrainian and international companies will introduce the best gambling solutions: slot machines, casino equipment, furniture and games, betting software, iGaming platforms, payment systems, as well as a wide range of services for successful business operations.

Two-day conference

This year’s program will consist of six blocks:

Regulation

Social responsibility and customer focus

Business processes and resource management

Betting

Innovations in Gaming

Esports

Speakers: Representatives of gambling operators and betting providers, heads of specialized associations, software developers, marketing experts, lawyers, analysts, arbitrage and other specialists with long-term gambling experience.

They will talk about the latest changes in the regulated market, betting investment generation, aspects of promoting gambling brands. Experts will focus on the role of sports organizations in the sportsbook ecosystem and the state-of-the-art technologies for gambling business optimization. Specialists will also reveal taxation issues and give industry development forecasts.

In addition, the conference will include three hot panel discussions on the following topics:

Responsible gaming compliance in Ukraine: Taxes, gentlemen's agreement and prevention of illegal activity

Immersive tech: Is 2022 a breakthrough year?

Transformation of the gambling market: 1.5 years after the legalization. Business ramifications, first results and projections for 2022

Job Board

The exhibition will install a Job Board, allowing employers to post open vacancies and job seekers their CVs. Thus, business owners will be able to find the competent staff for their teams and various professionals to get highly paid jobs at promising Ukrainian and international companies.

Negotiations Room

For two days, the exhibition will feature a Negotiations Room, allowing guests and participants to carry out business meetings in a comfortable environment. You can book the place and time of negotiations in advance.

Follow announcements in the News section, on the Telegram channel and social media of the event in order to keep up with the program updates.
