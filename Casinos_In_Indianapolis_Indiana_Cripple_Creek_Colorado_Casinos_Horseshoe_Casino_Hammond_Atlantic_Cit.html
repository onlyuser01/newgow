The newly signed partnership enables the provider of real-time data and digital content to expand its coverage for Czech handball and bring sports fans unique insights and experiences

The leading provider of real-time data and digital content, FeedConstruct, revealed it extended its existing partnership with the Czech Handball Men’s Extraleague. The latest collaboration, announced Monday, sees the provider significantly expand its content offering, enabling handball fans across the globe to benefit from enhanced experiences.

Per the terms of the freshly minted partnership, FeedConstruct will cover the Czech Handball Men’s Cup, as well as Women’s Cup matches. This extended coverage comes in addition to its coverage of the Men’s Extraleague. The collaboration starts from the quarter-final stage of the famous competitions, FeedConstruct confirmed.

Ultimately, the latest agreement is expected to significantly elevate the thrill and excitement for handball fans across the globe. Thanks to the partnership, FeedConstruct will bring unique insights and enriched experiences for fans of the sport. While the collaboration is expected to boost customer engagement, it is also anticipated to help new fans connect with the sport.

“FeedConstruct, a noted real-time data and digital content provider, is excited to announce the extension of its partnership with the Czech Handball Men’s Extraleague. This updated partnership comes with a significant expansion of content offerings, enriching the handball experience for fans worldwide,“ reveals a statement released by FeedConstruct

An Action-Packed Calendar Awaits Handball Fans

Thanks to the extended partnership, FeedConstruct will hold video and data rights for the Czech Handball Men’s Cup and Women’s Cup. Additionally, the collaboration will see the leading provider of real-time data and digital content hold exclusive data coverage rights for the Czech Handball Men’s Extraleague.

Overall, the upcoming season will offer a strong lineup of more than 200 matches. Notably, there will be 14 really exciting matches within the Women’s and Men’s Cups as well. After the quarter-final round, the Men’s and Women’s Cups will feature eight teams each. On the other hand, 13 teams will compete for the Men’s Extraleague.

The Men’s Extraleague kicked off on August 31 and is scheduled to run through May. The schedule for the Czech Handball Men’s Cup and Women’s Cup is slightly different as matches start in September and are scheduled to run through March.

Undoubtedly, thanks to the new collaboration, handball fans and bettors alike are in for a treat, considering the extended coverage and unique insights FeedConstruct will bring through its latest partnership.
