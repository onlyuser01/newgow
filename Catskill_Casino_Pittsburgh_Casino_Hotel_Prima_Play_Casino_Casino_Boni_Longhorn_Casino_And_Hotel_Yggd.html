Bold Graphics and Lucrative Gameplay

Like the rest of the games part of Yggdrasil’s YG Masters portfolio created by the talented team at Jelly, the new fishing-themed slot has fantastic graphics on display. The amazing details and colorful elements truly make the theme pop up. The game’s innovative fishing mechanic can be activated by a pair of wild fishermen symbols landing on the first two reels. Next, the fisherman starts capturing all fish found on the third reel. They can move across all five reels on the grid and each various multiplier value attached to each fish symbol. The fifth reel, which is the highest paying reel, can reach up to 100x.

Provided three bonus symbols reach the third, fourth or fifth reel, players get to enter free spins mode. The free spins can also be triggered whenever wild symbols pop on the first and second reel, and a scatter symbol reaches the third reel. While still in Free Spins mode, the last three reels can be seen expanding up high with up to six symbols, carrying the potential winning potential of the game to new levels.
