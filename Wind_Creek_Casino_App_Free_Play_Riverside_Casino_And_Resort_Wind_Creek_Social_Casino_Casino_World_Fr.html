The leading investment firm within the iGaming vertical, HappyHour.io, announced it strengthened its C-suite thanks to a new appointment. The company revealed it expanded its list of experts by adding Ben Clemes to the role of investment portfolio partner.

To his new role, he brings extensive experience of nearly two decades within business development, user experience and casino operations. In addition, Clemes has a track record in spearheading the growth of brands and companies within the online gambling vertical.

As an investment portfolio partner with HappyHour, he will play a key role in the company’s future growth. Moreover, he will oversee HappyHour’s global expansion and investment growth. In his new role at the company, Clemes will also contribute toward expanding HappyHour’s ties with different startups within the gambling vertical. He will be based in San Francisco and play an important role in the company’s relations with different North American-based investors, partners and startups.

Clemes joins a growing team of experts at HappyHour. As a part of the company’s C-suite, he will collaborate with its managing partner, Robin Reed. This isn’t the first time the duo join forces. Reed and Clemes co-founded the gaming industry giant, Gaming Innovation Group (GiG).

“Working with Ben again is immensely gratifying, given his remarkable track record of growing startups into robust and prosperous businesses.“ Robin Reed, managing partner at HappyHour

Reed shared his excitement about the latest appointment, acknowledging that it is delighting to team up with Clemes once again. He spoke about Clemes’ track record, extensive experience and deep understanding of the gambling vertical. Finally, Reed deemed Clemes as the perfect fit for HappyHour.
