Continuing its global expansion, Pariplay appointed Jorge Morales as director of business development. In his new position, he is going to be responsible for the growth within the LatAm and Caribbean region.

Pariplay Keeps Focus On LatAm and Caribbean Region Expansion

Pariplay, the leading aggregator and content provider, announced on Thursday that it appointed Jorge Morales as director of business development for the LatAm and Caribbean region. With this move, Aspire Global’s leading aggregator and content provider continues its global expansion to new regulated markets.

Focusing on its reach and growth in the region, Pariplay tasks Morales with overseeing the company’s expansion strategy within local, regulated markets such as Colombia, Argentina, Mexico and Peru. Additionally, in his new position, he will also be monitoring closely the developments in emerging markets such as Brazil and Chile.

Pariplay has already established its presence in Latin America. The aggregator has inked multiple deals with operators and suppliers within Colombia, Mexico, Peru and Ecuador. Its content is well received in the Latin American market and further growth is expected to be boosted by Morales.

Before joining Pariplay, Morales has more than 15 years of experience within the industry. Previously, he led Continent 8 Technologies as a sales director, but he has also served key roles as a part of Microgaming, Bally Technologies and Playtech. Not unexpectedly, his previous experience is related to development within the LatAm and the Caribbean regions.

Morales Is Thrilled to Join Pariplay

Morales shared his excitement in a statement saying that he is thrilled to accept this new opportunity. Moreover, he said that making Pariplay a must-have aggregator in the LatAm and Caribbean region will be his main focus. Acknowledging that Pariplay is fairly new to the LatAm market, Morales stressed that it holds incredible potential. In conclusion, he said that he is looking forward to “supporting local partners with a premium solution that will help further their business and reach with the help of our technologically advanced Fusion platform.”

I’m thrilled to take up this new opportunity with Pariplay and I have my goal set on making us the must-have aggregator in the region.” Jorge Morales, Director of Business Development for LatAm and Caribbean at Pariplay

Pariplay’s managing director, Adrian Bailey, said that Morales has a true passion for the industry. Moreover, he acknowledged that Morales has strong relationships complemented by significant experience within the region, which will help Pariplay continue to expand. In conclusion, Bailey stressed that Morales is a great addition to Pariplay and that the company is excited to have him aboard while it transforms into a leading supplier worldwide.
