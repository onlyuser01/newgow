The fast-growing aggregation platform and business-to-business (B2B) content provider, REEVO, announced a new partnership. On Thursday, the company confirmed it joined forces with the provider of classic online slots, 1spin4win. Under the terms of the freshly minted agreement, REEVO agreed to add the iGaming content provider's games to its aggregation platform, propelling further its offering.

Ultimately, the collaboration with REEVO will help 1spin4win's games reach new audiences across the globe. Currently, the company is renowned for its quality classic online slots that stand out with engaging mechanics, rewarding features and unique themes.

Back in May 2021, 1spin4win launched its first title. To date, the company offers more than 100 thrilling slot games. Besides delivering unrivaled experiences, the company's games stand out with transparent mechanics and high Return to Player (RTP) percentages, making them an instant hit among online gamblers.

Volha Hlukovskaya, 1spin4win's Head of Partnership, shared her excitement about the new partnership with REEVO in a statement. "At 1spin4win, we’re thrilled to join forces with REEVO, leveraging their robust platform to amplify our reach and enhance our offerings," she explained. Last but not least, Hlukovskaya pointed out: "This partnership is a testament to our commitment to providing top-notch entertainment by making our beloved classic slots available to an even broader audience through REEVO’s extensive network."

Petra Maria Poola, REEVO's Head of Sales, shared the excitement of Hlukovskaya. She explained that the company is delighted to expand its portfolio of partners with the addition of 1spin4win. "The addition of their classic and engaging slots perfectly complements our diverse portfolio, ensuring that players have access to an even wider range of gaming options," added Poola. Finally, REEVO's Head of sales explained: "This partnership represents a significant step towards our goal of driving differentiation and delivering the best gaming experiences to our partners and their customers."

The partnership with 1spin4win comes after recently, REEVO announced another important deal. Earlier this month, the company confirmed it integrated content from ESA Gaming, allowing the company to significantly expand its reach and engage with new audiences across the globe.

This year was a busy period for REEVO. The company announced many important partnerships, allowing it to significantly expand its aggregation platform offering. By now, REEVO's B2B aggregation platform boasts more than 8,000 games from some of the most popular iGaming content suppliers. Throughout the rest of the year, the company said that it plans to join forces with nearly two dozen more iGaming providers, continuing the expansion of its aggregation platform.
