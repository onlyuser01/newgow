Prophet to Launch Services in New Jersey, Indiana

New Jersey-based sports betting exchange, Prophet announced Monday that it entered into a multi-year market access agreement with Caesars Entertainment. Under the agreement, Caesars will see Prophet launch its peer-to-peer sports betting exchange platform Betprophet in New Jersey just in time for the 2021 NFL and college football season. After that, Prophet will launch its innovative platform in Indiana next year. The company’s services will be available on the web, as well as Android and iOS devices via their public API.

An Innovative Sports Exchange Platform

Prophet was founded in 2018 by Dean Sisun, who is currently acting as CEO, and Jake Benzaquen, who is currently COO. Formerly licensed in the UK, Prophet brought its innovative peer-to-peer sports betting exchange platform and technology to the US market, one of the most lucrative markets concentrated on sports wagering.

Prophet’s innovative exchange technology offers players many benefits compared to traditional sportsbooks. The platform allows sports fans to bet on spread and totals at even (+100) odds, breaking from tradition. Furthermore, users of the innovative platform can benefit from lower vig on money line markets and no limits. Last but not least, users who are interested in trading quickly or in bulk can benefit from the ability to market-make with public API access.
