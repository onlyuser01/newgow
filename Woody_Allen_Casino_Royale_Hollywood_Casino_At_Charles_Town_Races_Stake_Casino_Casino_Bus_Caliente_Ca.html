Pragmatic Play, One Bit Tech Forge a New Deal

Pragmatic Play, the leading content provider to the gaming industry, announced on Monday that it grew its Latin American footprint after joining forces with One Bit Tech. Under the terms of the deal, the platform provider will now be able to offer Pragmatic Play’s leading live casino and slots products to its partners in Latin America.

“It marks further expansion for Pragmatic Play in Latin America, following its recent commercial success in the region across multiple verticals,” reads a statement released by Pragmatic Play

The new collaboration is going to bring Pragmatic Play’s leading products such as Sweet Bonanza Candyland, Wolf Gold and award-winning slot Gates of Olympus for One Bit Tech’s partners in Latin America. Moreover, the new deal complements Pragmatic’s ongoing expansion in the region.

“We have prioritized growth in emerging regulated markets in Latin America and are delighted to broaden our footprint in the region by partnering with One Bit Tech.” Victor Arias, VP of Latin American operations, Pragmatic Play
