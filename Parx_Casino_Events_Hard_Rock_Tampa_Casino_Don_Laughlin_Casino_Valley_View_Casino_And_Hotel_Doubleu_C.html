Kyle Larson or Denny Hamlin – Oddsmakers See These Two As Top Favorites

Kyle Larson is the top favorite to win the Darlington race on Sunday with odds of +500. If he does it, it’s going to be the third win of the season for the former champion. This guy’s been in very good form lately and was very close to winning last weekend’s race.

He was the one to lead the most laps in AdventHealth 400, but it was Denny Hamlin who got the win in the end. The Joe Gibbs Racing driver is expected to do great this weekend as well. He’s the No. 2 favorite, priced at +550.

Hamlin loves the Darlington Racetrack, the evidence being that he’s already won a couple of events on this racetrack. In fact, he’s got four wins in races held at this place.

Goodyear 400 NASCAR 2023 Prediction

Bookies are right to think of Larson and Hamlin as the two top favorites. We’re almost certain that at least one of them will end up on the podium on Sunday. The big question, however, is whether it will be the No. 1 spot.

The thing is that surprises do happen in this race. Last year, for example, Joey Logano was a +1500 underdog before the race, but he ended up winning eventually. So, which underdog stands a good chance of winning in the 2023 event?

If we had to pick one, we’d go with Chase Elliott at +1200. After all, he’s one of the greatest NASCAR drivers of the generation.
