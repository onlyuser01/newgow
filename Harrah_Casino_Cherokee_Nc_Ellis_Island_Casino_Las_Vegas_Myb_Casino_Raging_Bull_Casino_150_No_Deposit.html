Fight for Good or Evil

During a game of Torii Tumble, players will collect various Japan-themed symbols, including stylized letters and samurai-themed symbols.

The new game offers players a huge win potential with the maximum bet being 20,000 times the stake. Players can enhance their wagers thanks to a variety of engaging mechanics. For example, the game features a famous tumble mechanic, a multiplier ladder, as well as multiplier boosters and spin savers.

Players can grab wild symbols which greatly improve their chance to score winning combinations and win big. In addition, players can land on bonus symbols represented by a gong icon. Should they grab three such symbols, players will trigger the bonus round.

The bonus round is where Torii Tumble really breaks ground. Once players trigger free spins mode, they will be presented with a choice, allowing them to select multiplier mode, spin save mode or mixed mode.

Multiplier mode will see the players side with the notorious oni for an increased chance of triggering high multipliers. Spin saver mode, on the other hand, will ally players with the honorable samurai for an increased chance of landing on spin saver symbols. Mixed mode, as the title suggests, is the perfect solution for those who want to have the best of both worlds.

Once the bonus round finishes, players can use a gamble feature for a chance to win even more spins.
