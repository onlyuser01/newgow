One of the world’s most reputable online sportsbooks, bet365, has announced that it is working on a new Sports and Betting News website which will be launched in the coming months and it will be a platform where customers can read the latest developments in the world of sports.

The News Website Will Be Available in Several Languages

James Clark, the manager of Sports and Betting News, shared his insights into the new bet365 department. He stated that his colleagues are working hard to make sure that the new website will meet the latest standards.

He also added that it will be available in several languages and the platform will be launched “in the coming months.”

In terms of the content, Clark stated that customers will be able to read the latest betting and sports news. Not only that, but the platform, which will be user-friendly, will feature previews, reviews, tailored video content, bespoke imagery, exclusive interviews, and betting tipsters.

Expansion of the team is also on the horizon as Clark noted that bet365 will be recruiting several new roles that will work in the division. The jobs will vary between content producers, analysts, management positions, etc.

The main goal of the new Sports and Betting News site will be to achieve high-quality content. Clark stated that the company wants its customers to feel informed and engaged.

bet365 Was Also Named PGA Tour’s Official Partner

At the end of June, bet365 became the first international OBO partner of the PGA Tour outside of North America. Some of the new territories that were included in the partnership were the UK, Australia, and Ireland. The deal will last throughout 2024.

Thanks to the new partnership, bet365 will be the official odds generator on the leaderboard and player pages at the official website of the PGA Tour. Additionally, the operator gained rights to use PGA Tour’s marks, as well as rights to advertise within the Tour partner and Tour media platforms.

A bet365 spokesperson stated that it is truly a pleasure to be working with one of the “most iconic brands” in the global sports industry. The spokesperson added that thanks to this partnership, bet365 will continue to deliver a unique in-play experience to golf customers.

Apart from the PGA Tour partnership, bet365 also signed an agreement with Sports Information Services (SIS), thanks to which the operator will be able to stream 49’s Original Live Draw on its platform. Most recently, bet365 strengthened its PA Betting Services partnership and thanks to that development, the latter is set to receive a major boost in its soccer data. Eugene Delaney, the director at PA Betting Services, stated that both parties have been working relentlessly to deliver quick and accurate data and that their partnership embodies their agility to work so effectively in such a short time space.
