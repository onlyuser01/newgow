Power of Sun: Svarog review

Theme and Style

Power of Sun: Svarog transports players to a world of myth and fantasy.

On an unforgiving landscape, players will begin a quest to learn the secrets of the fiery sun, god Svarog. Precious gems and iron weapons await on this adventure.

Fans of ancient mythology will especially appreciate this slot.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack of Power of Sun: Svarog has an ominous melody blended with a dramatic beat. Grunting vocalizations seem to come from Svarog himself, adding tension to the gameplay.

The reels are framed in iron with a desolate mountain scene visible in the background. The grey sky and snow-dusted mountains suggest particularly chilly weather.

On the reels, players will notice Iron letters studded with brightly-hued gems. Blue sapphires, green emeralds, and purple amethysts all make an appearance.

Ornate, cryptic emblems associated with the god Svarog can also be found among the symbols. The most striking symbol is Svarog himself illustrated in gold with tendrils of hair surrounding him like rays of sunlight.

Overall, the soundtrack and animation of this slot create an engaging world of ancient gods.

Gameplay

Power of Sun: Svarog is a five-reel, three-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel. In total, there are 243 ways to win.

The maximum win for this slot is 5,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all regular symbols.

Cash Infinity symbols can appear at random during the base game, remaining sticky on the reels until the Bonus concludes. Cash Infinity symbols award prizes ranging from 1x to 15x the player’s total bet which can be won during the Bonus round.

Mystery symbols and Svarog Mystery symbols with the Sticky to Infinity feature can appear during both the base game and the Bonus round. They remain on the reels until the end of the Bonus.

The Hold the Jackpot Bonus is triggered when 6 or more Bonus symbols appear. During this feature, every Bonus symbol remains sticky on the reels and 3 Respins are awarded. Whenever a new Bonus symbol appears, the Respin count is reset to 3.

During the Hold the Jackpot Bonus prizes ranging from 1x to 15x the player’s total bet can be awarded. Minor and Major Jackpot symbols can appear at random, awarding special prizes.

Meanwhile, the Svarog Jackpot is only available to be drawn on the Svarog Mystery symbol. Players can win multiple Jackpots of the same kind during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus.

Collector symbols can appear during the Hold the Jackpot Bonus, accumulating the values from Cash and Cash Infinity symbols and multiplying them.

If players collect all 15 Bonus symbols of any type, the Grand Jackpot of 5,000x the player’s total bet is awarded.

The Hold the Jackpot Bonus continues until no more Respins remain.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.15%, just about our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€10,000 per spin.

The volatility of Power of Sun: Svarog is adjustable which means players can tailor their experience to their preferences. We suggest opting for the highest volatility as this aligns with our preferred slot machine strategies.

With high-quality animation, a dramatic atmosphere, and a variety of bonus features, Power of Sun: Svarog is worth checking out.
