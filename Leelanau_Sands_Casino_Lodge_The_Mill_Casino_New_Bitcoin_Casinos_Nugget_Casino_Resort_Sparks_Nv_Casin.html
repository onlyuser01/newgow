AffPapa has announced that the registrations for the upcoming AffPapa iGaming Awards 2023 are now open. The event, which is expected to reunite industry leaders and distinguish the most innovative companies in the industry, will be taking place on June 20 in Valletta, Malta.

AffPapa is proud to present an event that will seek to do justice to every entity involved in the industry. This is why the company is moving forward with a variety of categories that are going to focus on individual distinctions by the companies that compete for various accolades.

There are categories for affiliates and operators, B2B companies, and more. AffPapa iGaming Awards will seek to recognize the brightest performance across all gambling. Some of the categories include Best Industry Newcomer, Best Affiliate Program, and more.

Newcomers and veterans are very welcome to submit their bids and demonstrate why they deserve to grab the silverware. The winners in each of the categories set out by AffPapa iGaming Awards will be selected by industry companies which have an established track record and are positioned to reasonably determine which entities deserve to grab the title in each category.

Commenting on this, AffPapa CEO Levon Nikoghosyan said: "The registration for the 2nd annual AffPapa iGaming Awards is now officially live! AffPapa is hosting a special gala night in Malta to celebrate the industry’s tremendous success, and we can’t wait to see all the entries come in!"

In the meantime, the registration deadline for AffPapa iGaming Awards 2023 has been set for May 16. All submissions must be finalized and sent by that cut-off period. To do so, any participant must submit their bids through the official website.
