Relax Gaming has announced the release of Lure of Fortune, a fresh slot with three volatility modes, a max win of 50,000x, and Cascading Wins

The iGaming aggregator founded in 2010 by Patrik Österåker and Jani Tekoniemi has launched Lure of Fortune, a fresh 5×5 fishing-themed slot with three available volatility modes, a massive maximum win potential of 50,000x the bet, and winning clusters that trigger Cascading Wins.

In September, Relax Gaming released Joker Split, a medieval-themed game with free spins, high volatility, and a maximum win of 20,000x.

Lure of Fortune Boasts Innovative Mechanics

As explained by Relax Gaming’s director of casino products, Shelley Hannah, the fresh release boasts an “innovative mechanic” that enables players to choose their favorite level of volatility out of three available options.

This means the needs of all types of players will be well catered to by the new game which also packs a plethora of popular features that have already proven their success. Together, both the old and the new features are expected to “go down a treat” with the studio’s audiences.

Hannah also took the opportunity to mention the increased worldwide popularity of the selected fishing theme while expressing delight for the studio once again producing a unique “slot mechanic which stands apart from anything else available” in the iGaming industry at the moment.

Next to a handful of companions including the Persistent Hammer Octopus, the Persistent Spawner Fish, and the Persistent Payer Worm, players are on a mission to fish as close to the boat as they can.

Generating a winning cluster with at least four identical symbols next to one another on the vertical or the horizontal automatically triggers the Cascading Win feature.

All symbols that were previously used to form a win will vanish from the reels, allowing new symbols to fall down and cover the positions that are now empty. The action will go on as long as fresh wins can be generated.

Multiplier Upgrade and Bonus Game

The game also features a Multiplier Upgrade where all symbol types with attached multipliers starting at 1x are doubled whenever the specific symbol types are included in the win.

Depending on the chosen volatility mode (medium, high, extremely high), players can enjoy a maximum potential multiplier of 128x for a symbol type.

Players who manage to land a minimum of three Scatters trigger the Bonus Game. While engaged in the Bonus Game, five symbols featuring multipliers are placed at the bottom of the reels marked from one to five. The sixth reel will feature a multiplier ladder applied to symbols located on the same row at the end of the game.

Players can also buy the Bonus Game in exchange for 50x, 100x, or 150x the bet on the selected medium, high, or very highly volatile modes.
