Dr. Toonz review

Theme and Storyline

Dr. Toonz is themed around a lab filled with whimsical creatures.

In a secret underwater facility, players will meet an eccentric researcher. Together, they’ll study a variety of mysterious and adorable life forms.

This slot is a great choice for fans of sci-fi, or players looking for a cheerful atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The reels are crafted from large test tubes, and inside players will see smiling alien-like characters. These cute creatures come in blue, orange, green, pink, and brown. Some peer out from their reel with just one eye, while others sport tiny horns.

Beside the reels, Dr Toonz himself slumps in his seat, often snoozing. With a white lab coat and a large mustache, he brings to mind quintessential scientist characters such as Doc from Back to the Future.

In the background, the lab is walled by glass, revealing green liquid on the other side. This suggests that this is no ordinary operation or setting.

Together, the music and animation create a quirky sci-fi world.

Gameplay

Dr. Toonz is a six reel, four row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels. These combinations are calculated from left to right.

The Cascade feature means that winning symbols vanish, making way for new symbols to fall into their positions.

The maximum win is 22,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

There is a meter beside the reels called the Quantumeter that begins at 0 and charges during winning Cascades.

Once fully charged, the Quantumeter activates once the winning combinations are paid out. There are a variety of fully charged features offered by the Quantumeter:

Quantum Wilds: 2-6 Wilds are added to the reels.

Metamorphosis: 5-8 symbols are changed into another symbol, excluding Wilds and Scatters.

Disintegration: All lower-paying symbols are removed from the reels and 2 Wilds are added.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land at least 3 Scatters. The number of free spins awarded depends on the number of triggering Scatters as follows:

3 Scatters = 10 free spins

4 Scatters = 15 free spins

5 Scatters = 20 free spins

6 Scatters = 25 free spins

The Quantumeter begins the Free Spins feature with 1-2 charges.

At least one reel is chosen to have a 2x multiplier during the Free Spins feature. As many as six multipliers may be active at the same time.

The Free Spins feature cannot be retriggered.
