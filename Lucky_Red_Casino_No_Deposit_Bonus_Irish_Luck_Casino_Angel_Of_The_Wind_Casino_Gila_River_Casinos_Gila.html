Mega Cash Stacks Symbols and Gameplay

The game is excellent for players who prefer conventional gameplay with one-of-a-kind features. The base game is still entertaining enough even when zero features are enabled. When the scatter symbols, wilds, and the rest of the special symbols come up on the grid, the action truly starts to pick up. The slot has the money theme at the center. Players win when landing a minimum of three identical symbols on a line.

All the symbols in the game are stacked on top of one another during the base game. The same happens when the bonus features are activated. This way, players get to boost their cash stacks.

The Progressive Spins mechanic is triggered when a player land at least three symbols on any location on the game reels. A player can win up to 15 free spins via the wheel of fortune mini-game. Each of the free spins pays out a cash prize that is greater than the previous free spin.

Players can also choose to gamble their winnings during the mini-game. Players must either choose the left-hand stack of coins for the opportunity to boost their cash winnings by one-third. They can also double or even triple their winnings. On the other hand, the right-hand cash stack of coins gives players the opportunity to win up to 15 extra progressive free spins.

Golden Bet – 50% Increase in Bet

Mega Cash Stacks lets players enable the bonus for 100x the stake or use a Golden Bet to increase their bet by 50%. This will double their chances of activating the Progressive Free Spins bonus.
