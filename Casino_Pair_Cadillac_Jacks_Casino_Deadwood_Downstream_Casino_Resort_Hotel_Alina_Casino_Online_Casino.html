Triggy, an innovative develop of betting technology, expands its U.S. market offerings by adding American football to the LeoAlerts live score app by LeoVegas.

A Whole New Betting Experience with Triggy

Triggy launches a new offering for the U.S. sports betting market, adding American football to LeoAlerts live score app to provide relevant information based on intelligently filtered data, distributed to the players. LeoAlerts users will have push notifications for their favorite teams and games, and relevant content from NextBets with three betting suggestions with every notification sent.

Triggy’s co-founder and CEO Björn Nilsson has expressed last year that the U.S. market is the most important one for them and that the company will release American football first. As the company expands on the American market so will the offering. Triggy also plans to add basketball, baseball, hockey, and soccer in the nearest future.

NextBets’ betting experience will be much more in-play and advanced thanks to the ‘Trigger’ functionality which will allow users to set alerts based on their preferences for score updates, odds, and time.

Martina Åkerlund, CEO of Triggy AB said that the release of the American Football in their live score apps is a big success for Triggy and it is also a part of their strategy to offer multi-sport coverage. She also stated that more sports will be added in 2021 to build up their offerings on the U.S. market. Many ambitious pioneering sports betting operators have shown interest in the full in-play betting experience.

Triggy Ambitiously Strengthens Its Presence in the U.S.

Triggy’s goal is to become a hub for the development of interactive betting products that will give players the ability to stay ahead of the game.

Although created a bit over three years ago Triggy has managed to create products that are innovative and embraced by SB Live, LeoAlerts, and Pinnacle Live Soccer Scores.

In an interview last year, Nilsson has described Triggy’s products as innovative products with smart data filtering that deliver relevant content to the players and allows them to make educated decisions.
