Aztec Gold Megaways review

Theme and Storyline

Aztec Gold Megaways borrows its imagery from South American civilisations that existed before the Columbian Exchange. But it turns that imagery into a fantasy setting with some Mike Mignola style art and a god-like emperor-priest.

It puts one in mind of the Hellboy comics or Disney’s Atlantis, but with real Inca flavour.

Can you decipher the ancient myth, and find the City of Gold? That’s the story to unravel in this slot.

Three main features of this slot are Megaways system, cascade win system and any-way win system

All in all, this is a slot with wonderful visual design and atmospheric sound.

Gameplay

Aztec Gold Megaways uses the Megaways system, which randomises the number of symbols on each reel every spin. Here, the six reels can have up to seven symbols on them and a minimum of two. This makes for a range of win ways from 64 to 117,649.

Winning combinations are removed from the reels and new symbols drop in from above. Any new winning combinations from this cascade count towards the player's winnings for the spin.

There are a set of jackpots and a bonus game, which keep things interesting, though no gamble feature.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

Five or more Scatters on the reel trigger Aztec Gold Cash Respin Bonus. Scatters come with a multiplier number on them or the word Mini, Major, or Mega.

During the Respin Bonus, all Scatters remain locked in place. The player gets three respins. Everytime a new Scatter lands on the reels, the number of remaining spins resets to three. At the end of the respins, the multiplier numbers on the Scatters will be added up and applied to the player’s bet per spin.

The Mini, Major, and Mega Scatters are worth a x30, x100, and x1,000 multiplier respectively.
