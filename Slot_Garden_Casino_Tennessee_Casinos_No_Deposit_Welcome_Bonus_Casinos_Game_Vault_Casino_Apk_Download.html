How to Play Tap Tap Splat

Players can expect to charge themselves with plenty of positive vibes while whacking cute and colorful jelly monsters ready to pop out their holes. Before the beginning of each new round, players are supposed to bet on their skills and ability to whack the jumping monsters. After the bet is placed, a new 60-second round is initiated. During it, the player must prove his skills and hit as many monsters as possible. The more hits they have, the greater their rewards. Tap Tap Splat has four different types of monsters: blue, red, purple, and green. Each color corresponds to a certain number of points.

The game becomes even more interesting when the player hits a bomb and his number of reward points drops by 150. Once a player reaches the 500 points mark, the bonus game is engaged. During the bonus game, players enjoy higher winnings without having to worry about any exploding bombs.
