Less than a day remains before one of the biggest industry event discussing the sports betting market in the United States and North America gets underway. The 3rd Annual Edition of All American Sports Betting Summit 2023 or AASBS 2023 for short is taking place in San Diego, California playing host to the prominent event.

The AASBS 2023 has picked BillPascrell III, from Princeton Public Affairs Group, as the Chairperson of the summit. Pascrell III has more than 25 years of experience in the gambling industry not just regionally but globally, and he is a well-known champion of the industry, having worked on New Jersey’s Online Gaming Act and its successful campaign.

As an industry specialist, Pascrell III brings a powerful voice for the industry at the event and is capable of addressing the immediate challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. The event itself is really well-thought-out, and while it continues in the tradition of the previous two events, it has been expanding and bringing new names.

The speaker lineup is heavy with prominent experts who will lean in on various aspects of the sports betting industry in the United States. The first panel that will take place is "Industry’s next big move," which will understandably talk about the challenges and opportunities all stakeholders face. The panel will be moderated by DraftKings representative and content specialist Tanner Kern.

Another highlight panel is the "Playing it safe" discussion which will take place on the second day of the expo, and as the name suggests, will focus on responsible gambling and business sustainability. Pascrell III will be moderating this panel and discuss with stakeholders what strategies and initiatives can lead to better player safety and contribute to the overall success of the industry.

On the third day of the event, delegates will be able to attend the "Identifying the market gap" which is a panel dedicated to the opportunities yet to be realized in the market. The panel will be moderated by Sparket – The Social Betwork CEO Aaron Basch and it will touch on how technology can be put to good use to ensure that brands remain competitive in what is a very dynamic vertical.

AASBS 2023 is hours away with the event hosted out of Paradise Point Resort & Spa in San Diego, California, the United States. You can find more details about the event on the official website.
