Olybet and Real Madrid CF Player Luka Modrić Forge New Partnership

Olybet, the Baltics sports betting brand, has announced a new collaboration with the football star Luka Modrić. The new regional Baltics partnership between the company and the professional soccer player from Croatia will come with local media campaigns. Moreover, the new collaboration will offer sports fans unique fan engagement opportunities. Fans will be able to engage with the football star via social media, as well as in person.

Corey Plummer, Olybet Group chairman and CEO, said that just like Modrić, the company was born “in very similar circumstances.” He acknowledged that the company has big ambitions, as well as a highly competitive spirit. According to Plummer, Modrić “represents a lot of these values” just like the brand does. Plummer added that besides being competitive, Modrić has lots of passion for sports and excellence.

Modrić Is Excited to Join Forces with Olybet

In an interview for Olybet TV, Modrić revealed that he decided to partner with the company because just like him, they are very ambitious. Moreover, he acknowledged that he enjoys working with companies that set “high goals” and “strive for excellence.”

“I decided to partner with Olybet because they are a very ambitious brand all over Europe and I am also ambitious as a person.” Luka Modrić

Currently, Modrić is a part of Real Madrid CF as a midfielder. But the soccer player also serves as a captain of the Croatian national team. Throughout his professional career, Modrić has won the Ballon d’Or back in 2018. Moreover, he has also won the prestigious UEFA award Men’s Player of the Year for 2017-2018.
