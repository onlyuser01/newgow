Campeón Gaming has stepped up the game, launching two of its latest brands with Delasport’s modern sportsbook. The brands in question are Foggybet and 21.Red, which are a fairly new addition to the operator’s portfolio.

Campeón Gaming’s Foggybet and 21.Red brands boast licenses from Malta’s Gaming Authority and offer a rich selection of video slots, live casino games and multiple online sports betting markets.

Delasport is a Gibraltar-headquartered company that provides sportsbook and iGaming turnkey software solutions to gambling operators all over the world. The company boasts one a cutting-edge sportsbook that incorporates many modern features that allow betting companies to stay ahead of the curve.

The award-winning supplier has many notable clients all over the world. In April, for example, JNS Gaming re-launched its LynxBet brand with Delasport.

Delasport’s capabilities will offer Foggybet and 21.Red superior personalization and engagement tools that will help the Campeón Gaming brands differentiate themselves from their competitors.

Campeón Gaming, on the other hand, is a fast-growing B2B and B2C company that has over 30 brands and a team of more than 125 employees across four offices. It was recently listed among the Best Workplaces Hellas 2023, by Great Place to Work Hellas.
