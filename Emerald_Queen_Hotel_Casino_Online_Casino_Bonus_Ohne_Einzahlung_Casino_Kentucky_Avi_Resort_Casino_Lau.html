Inspired Entertainment, a leading business-to-business provider of gaming, content and solutions, has unveiled a major partnership agreement with the National Basketball Association (NBA). Under this deal with one of America’s biggest sports leagues, the provider will be able to develop an NBA-inspired Virtual Sports game.

Based on archived footage, Inspired Entertainment’s new digital product will allow fans to wager on fast-paced matchups. The Virtual Sports game will provide multiple wagering opportunities, offering familiar sports betting markets, such as money line bets, over/under total score and more.

Under the agreement with the association, Inspired Entertainment will be able to create a product that leverages archived footage to simulate a genuine basketball experience. The new product will also include all 30 team logos, further enhancing the basketball atmosphere.

The agreement with the NBA is a huge milestone for Inspired Entertainment, which will now be able to capitalize on the global popularity of the league and its 30 teams.

The NBA Is Sure Inspired Entertainment Will Create an Exciting Product

Inspired Entertainment’s president and chief executive officer, Brooks Pierce, commented on the new partnership. He expressed his excitement to provide basketball fans across the world with the one-of-a-kind experience of a Virtual Sports NBA game.

Pierce added that the use of archived NBA footage will allow his team to produce an offering that is truly unique. He promised fans fresh gaming experiences that would allow them to stay connected with the world of basketball, even during the off-season.

Our partnership with the NBA allows us to provide an incredible offering that elevates our customers’ brands and expands their reach to millions of basketball enthusiasts worldwide. Brooks Pierce, CEO, Inspired Entertainment
