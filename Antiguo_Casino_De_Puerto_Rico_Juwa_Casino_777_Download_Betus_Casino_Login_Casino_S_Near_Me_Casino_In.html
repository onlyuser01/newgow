What are live dealer casinos?

The concept of a live casino is simple, and we can sum it up in one sentence:

A live dealer casino is a gambling site that streams live casino games, run by a real person, to people playing at home.

The dealer responds to each game as it happens, and you can hear the sounds of the casino, such as the clink of roulette wheel or slap of the cards on the table, as you play. The real-life items are usually supplemented by virtual items, like chips or cards, for you to move around the screen.

Live dealer online casinos have been around since the mid-2000s, but it was only until a decade or so later that they really started becoming popular. Improved internet speeds led to smoother gameplay and fewer interruptions.

These advances led to more companies dedicated solely to live-dealer games. Some even built their own physical casinos with several studios for players to choose from; players could do more during the game, like use special features and interact with the dealer.

Different types of live casino games

If you're wondering which live casino games are on offer, then there might be more than you expect. Live dealer casinos are becoming more adventurous in their game offerings as technology improves. Here are some of the most popular.

Live roulette

The famous green table and silver wheel come to life in these real-time games, where a live dealer guides you through each spin. There are usually digital chips that you place on either a virtual board or the table itself, and you must place your bet within the live time limit, just like you would in a real casino.

The quick and simple nature of live roulette makes live roulette casinos very popular among players, and the game itself is probably the most common type of live dealer casino game.

Live blackjack

More complicated than roulette, it's a good idea to learn the basic strategy of blackjack before playing live as you only get a limited time to make a decision.

That said, the live blackjack experience is more rewarding than playing against a computer: you can interact with the dealer and see him or her deal the cards, as well as take in the atmosphere of a live setting.

Live poker

Live poker games are different to the video poker you normally come across in an online casino. You play against a live dealer instead of the computer and they give you your cards.

Also, improving technology allows for more interaction between players and dealer, which means the live casino experience is often close to the convivial mood that in-person poker is famous for.

Live Bingo

Bingo thrives in a live setting: a human drawing the numbers generates more tension than numbers simply appearing on a screen.

Bingo halls have long used a telephone connection to communicate with other halls for national games, but the new era of live bingo takes it up another level so that players all over the world can play at the same time.

Live Baccarat

Baccarat isn't as popular as some other casino games, but there's still a sizeable number of players who enjoy live baccarat with its human dealer and real-life table. Like with other online versions, the live-dealer game involves betting on the player or dealer to win, or a tie.
