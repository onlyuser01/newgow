About HiPay

HiPay started trading in 2015, when it became an independent entity from the HiMedia Group. Its focus was on the online market where it began to offer an agile e-wallet and payment service to customers, and a ready-to-use platform for internet merchants.

By 2017, it was processing €2.2 billion in transactions per year, a figure that rose to almost €4 billion just two years later. Such rapid growth reflected a solid commercial strategy within the company and led to it being recognized as a major payment provider in Europe. It was also the result of an expansion to other countries: along with its headquarters in Paris, HiPay now has offices in Portugal, Spain, Belgium, and Italy, as well as the United States and Brazil.

HiPay's products are compatible with many big card brands. These include Visa and Mastercard, and its international presence means local options, such as Multibanco in Portugal and Belgium's Belfius, are also included.

Like many e-wallets, HiPay is useful for customers in that it provides them with one place to handle multiple online transactions. You can pay for internet shopping, book flights and, of course, make online casino deposits by using this service – all while keeping your sensitive banking info safe.
