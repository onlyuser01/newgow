About Trustly

Trustly was first started under its previous name InstantBank in 2008, in Stockholm, Sweden. It currently has offices in multiple European countries, with the main one in Malta. Trustly is authorized and supervised by the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority, offering its services to customers in tens of European countries, covering the majority of the continent.

The idea behind Trustly is to make online bank payments instant, or at least as quick as they can be. Traditionally issued bank transfers usually take several days to process, while payments made using Trustly are instant in most cases. This is definitely at least one of the reasons behind Trustly’s popularity.

How Trustly works in online casinos

In the next step, you need to enter the same information you enter when logging into your bank’s internet banking system and subsequently select the account you want to make the payment from. This allows you to make a bank transfer without ever leaving the casino’s website.

Withdrawals are similarly quick and easy and follow. They do take a bit longer than deposits, which are basically instant, but they are still quite fast when compared to other payment methods available on the market, especially credit cards and regular bank transfers.

Concerns about safety

Some people have voiced their concerns about the safety of Trustly, as they don’t feel confident entering their banking information anywhere else but directly on their bank’s website. This is, of course, a completely understandable concern.

That being said, Trustly is an officially licensed payment institution that has to follow strict rules regarding privacy, safety, and other qualities that are needed to operate a trusted financial service.

Credentials entered when making a payment are, according to Trustly’s Terms and Conditions, forwarded to the corresponding bank through a secure connection, and are never stored by Trustly.

No account casinos and their use with Trustly

Trustly is often used in so-called no-account casinos, in which players can play without being required to create a player account. This modern online casino phenomenon exists because of the ability to safely identify people using identification information connected to their bank accounts (BankID).

Trustly is one of the main payment methods used for this purpose, thanks to the connection to bank accounts, and the ability to make fast and reliable deposits and withdrawals, which would usually take more time to proceed when using "normal" bank transfers.

No account casinos are only available in selected countries so far (Germany, Sweden and Finland at the time of writing this article), but they are already a popular addition to the online gambling world. One of the reasons behind their popularity is the fact that they save time and allow players to skip the annoying verification procedures that are present in regular online casinos. The "accounts" are verified using BankID, so there is no need for further verification.

Of course, no accounts casinos are not for everyone. Besides not being available in most countries , some people still prefer the experience of creating a casino account in the usual way.
