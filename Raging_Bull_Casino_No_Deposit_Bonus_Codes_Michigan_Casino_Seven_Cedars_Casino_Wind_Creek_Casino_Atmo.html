SiS Signs Exclusive Deal With Codere

The leading multi-channel supplier of 24/7 live betting services, Sports Information Services (SiS) announced today that it inked an exclusive deal with Codere. Under the agreement, SiS expanded its international racing rights portfolio for the broadcasting of every horse race from the Hipódromo de las Américas racetrack in Mexico City.

Although some territory restrictions may apply, all of the remaining 76 thoroughbred fixtures of the current season from the Mexican race track are available for SiS customers around the world via both retail and online services. Furthermore, the company provides a full fixed-odds service, featuring on-screen graphics with betting triggers, data, derivatives, and prices.

The exclusive deal complements the company’s ongoing expansion of a market-leading portfolio of international horse races. Currently, SiS features races from Latin America, the US, Saudi Arabia, Spain, Germany, Hungary, the Czech Republic, the UK and Ireland, and Dubai. In other words, the company solidifies its global position as a market leader in 24/7 live betting services.
