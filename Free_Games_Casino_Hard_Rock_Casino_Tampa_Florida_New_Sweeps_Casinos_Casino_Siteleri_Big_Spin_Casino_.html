The Drake Curse Came Back to Haunt the Rapper

Last week, Drake once again picked up Stake.com to place a wager. The rapper, who is also the operator’s brand ambassador, placed two big bets on the match between Kamaru Usman and Leon Edwards during the UFC 278 event.
