BetMGM Signs First-of-Its-Kind Deal with Borussia Dortmund

Bundesliga top contender Borussia Dortmund has formed a partnership with BetMGM that grants the sportsbook rights to player imagery and its logos for marketing purposes. The deal will also include social media promotions, ritzy signup offers for supporters, and co-branded content on Dortmund’s English social media channel, as well as special contests by BetMGM with team-branded prizes.

Borussia Dortmund’s head of international and commercial business Benedikt Scholz, added that BetMGM’s app would also stream the club’s matches for fans to watch on the go. He expressed his content about the deal with the US operator, saying it was “wonderful news” that US-based fans will now be able to follow games through the Entain-MGM joint-venture app.

“Together, we will broaden our reach and further develop our presence in the US market,” Scholz added.

Matt Prevost, BetMGM’s chief revenue officer, explained that given the increasing popularity of the Bundesliga in the US, such a partnership was a logical next step. He went on to add that BetMGM’s ground-breaking partnership with a German soccer club was another first for the company.
