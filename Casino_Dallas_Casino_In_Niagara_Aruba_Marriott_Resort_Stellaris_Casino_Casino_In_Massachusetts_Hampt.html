Martin Clarke has an extensive track record and the experience needed to be a vital part of OddsMatrix’s growth journey

OddsMatrix, the leading sportsbook arm of EveryMatrix, has expanded its leadership team with a new appointment. The division will be joined by the experienced sports betting strategy, design and development innovator Martin Clarke, who will serve as its chief product officer.

As shared by the company, its new hire is an acclaimed sports product veteran whose experience stems from the many years he spent with Betfair. During his time with the competitor company, Clarke helped it deliver many cutting-edge products, such as Cash Out, Fast Markets, Odds Boost and BetBuilder.

Furthermore, Clarke boasts rebuilding Metric Gaming’s business-to-business platform, as well as its in-house racing, soccer and US sports offerings.

In addition to his experience in gaming and betting, OddsMatrix’s newly-designated CPO also has “considerable trading and risk experience.” In his new position, Clarke will be responsible for multiple areas within the sports business unit.

The areas in question include product strategy, the development and communication of a clear product roadmap, upgrades to existing products, the prioritization of stakeholder demands, quality assurance and project management, among others.

Clarke will work directly under Tor Skeie, OddsMatrix’s new chief executive officer.

CEO Groes Welcomed Clarke to the Team

The chief executive of EveryMatrix, Ebbe Groes, welcomed Clarke on board, praising the hire as a “sports product superstar. Groes noted that Clarke has an impressive track record and will be a great addition to the team.

The CEO furthermore said that the new appointment is in line with the company’s efforts to bolster its growth and success.

Martin’s arrival proves we are on the right path to accelerate our growth and success as we continue to attract major tier-1 operators, providing them with industry-leading software and services that take their brands to the next dimension. Ebbe Groes, CEO, EveryMatrix

Clarke himself commented on his new position, saying that he is glad to join the EveryMatrix family. He applauded OddsMatrix for its exceptional platform, product and services, saying that they are making a massive difference to some of the biggest global gaming brands.

I’m really looking forward to working with Tor and the team and supporting them with some exciting plans in 2023 and beyond. Martin Clarke, CPO, EveryMatrix

OddsMatrix continues to steadily grow. According to the division’s annual results, its gross profits grew by 28% in 2022. The unit now serves major companies in the gaming sector, including Bet-at-home, which beat 25 competitors to win the tender for the Hungarian lottery thanks to EveryMatrix’s technology.
