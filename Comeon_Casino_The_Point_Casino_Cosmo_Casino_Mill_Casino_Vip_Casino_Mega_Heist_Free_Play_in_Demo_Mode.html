Gameplay

Mega Heist starts out on a fairly standard 5x4 game grid. It has 178 ways to win that are active on every spin.

You will find the control panel for the game under the reels. Here, you can hit the spin button, change the stake, or use the autospin option if you want to let the criminals do the hard work for you.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

All of the bonuses in this slot are based on trying to bust the convicts out of prison. The first bonus is the Escape Bonus, which launches when you land two Bonus symbols.

Random feature of the game - the Escape Bonus round

When this triggers, up to four prisoners will attempt to escape. Prisoners will be placed on a separate grid where they need to move across five spaces to escape. Each prisoner will carry either a prize or a bonus symbol. They will move one position each time, and then police officers will appear above random positions. If they are caught, then they are removed from the grid. If they get to the other end with a prize, then you will be paid the prize. If they get to the other end with a Bonus symbol, then you will launch the Mega Heist Bonus.

You can also trigger this bonus by landing three or more Bonus symbols. Three, four, or five symbols will place 10, 15, or 30 prisoners on the grid, respectively. This operates in a similar way to the standard Escape bonus, only with much bigger prizes and a lot more elements.

Players can advance through three different phases, each with a higher total prize than the last. You will advance once you have got your prisoners to the end zone with all of the prize money. If all of your prisoners are caught, then the round will end.
