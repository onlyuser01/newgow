BGaming, a fast-growing provider of iGaming content, has announced a new partnership with Betmexico.mx, a Mexican iGaming operator. Under the agreement, the latter company will onboard many of the supplier’s slot games.

As per the deal, BGaming will leverage its market-leading portfolio, which encompasses over a hundred unique games, to power Betmexico with content. As a result, Mexican players who pick Betmexico as their operator of choice will be able to try out famous slots such as Joker Queen, Fruit Million and Wild Cash.

In addition to slots, BGaming will also supply its new partner with a selection of casual and table games, further strengthening its offering.

BGaming’s content has proven to be in high demand in Mexico, with the current deal reaffirming the provider’s appeal among some of the biggest Latin American online casino operators. The agreement will help the company expand its regional foothold, reaching more players with its acclaimed content.
