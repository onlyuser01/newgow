Greentube Grows Portfolio with Ineor

The company acquired 80% of the shares in the Slovenia-based company, and will now leverage its critical software development capabilities and resources to boost its own offer globally.

Ineor has an established track record in both computer programming and the development of dedicated gaming software. The company already provides its solutions in the digital sector for a number of multinational organizations.

Greentube stands to benefit from this deal by incorporating this expertise into its own offer, following on a number of similar integrations of late. The company acquired a 65% stake in Alteatec recently, and it now participates in Flamingocatz, an up-and-coming studio and developer of iGaming content with an exclusive focus on the North American market.

Previously, Greentube also acquired Present Creative, another prominent US-based studio dedicated to building strong and distinguishable iLottery and iGaming products. Greentube has been able to leverage its strong momentum from previous years to quickly boost its production capacity and ensure that it snaps up assets that contribute to the company’s further expansion moves in global markets.

Greentube also announced the Greentube Mynt solution designed to significantly boost the company’s capabilities in producing and delivering new products, all of which are brought forward with the help of a Remote Gaming Server. This acquisition is a fantastic one as we continue to bolster our technology platform in what will be a very big year for us strategically. Ineor has been a highly valued partner for a number of years and it’s great to be able to harness its resources in-house. Greentube CFO/CGO Michael Bauer

Challenging Ineor to Be Better

Ineor founder Dejan Spasovski was equally thrilled to see his company become a member of the Greentube and NOVOMATIC’s family.
