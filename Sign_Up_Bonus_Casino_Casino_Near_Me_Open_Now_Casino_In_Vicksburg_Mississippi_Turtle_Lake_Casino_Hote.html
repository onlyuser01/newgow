Peggy Sweets review

Theme and Storyline

Peggy Sweets is themed around a colorful 1950s-style dessert shop.

As players step into Peggy Sweets, they’ll be offered a range of tasty treats including pancakes, donuts, and milkshakes. Each dish is served by a whimsical waitress gliding on rollerblades.

Players with a sweet tooth may especially appreciate this slot.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack of Peggy Sweets is an upbeat tune with a dance-worthy beat. Wins prompt ethereal chimes that add plenty of excitement to the moment.

Behind the reels, players will see the entrance to a dreamy dessert café. With cheerful arrows pointing towards the establishment as well as the word ‘sweets’ decorated with lights, players will feel immediately welcome to grab the nearest seat.

On the reels, a variety of desserts can be found, from Jello topped with whipped cream to pancakes dripping with cherry syrup. Pink donuts and strawberry ice cream sundaes are also among the symbols.

The café is staffed by a number of quirky waitresses, each with a unique personality and hair color. One has pink hair and a demure expression, while another sports sapphire locks and a sassy attitude, and a third has golden locks and wise eyes.

The soundtrack and animation combine to create a dreamy world of sugary fun.

Gameplay

Peggy Sweets is a five-reel, five-row slot.

Wins are awarded when players land 8 or more matching symbols anywhere on the reels. The prize is proportional to the number of symbols and the value of the symbol.

After symbols have formed a winning combination, they will vanish, making way for new symbols to fall into the reels from above. This creates the possibility for further winning combinations.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

This slot’s waitresses, Pink Girl, Blue Girl, and Yellow Girl, have an associated special feature that can be triggered when their portrait is displayed in the top right corner of the screen.

Special features can be triggered randomly in any spin

Pink Girl’s associated special feature is Make It Large, in which she swaps all instances of one symbol type for another.

Blue Girl’s special feature is Get One Free, in which she selects a symbol type and doubles it. Symbols of this type will still be the same size, but will be counted as double symbols.

Yellow Girl has the special ability to award multipliers of 2x, 3x, 4x, and 5x for wins that take place in the respin feature. The multipliers awarded by Yellow Girl are stacked up to a maximum of 30x.

The Free Spins feature begins with 8 free spins when players land 3 Scatters. During this feature, Pink Girl, Blue Girl, and Yellow Girl’s special features are randomly triggered.
