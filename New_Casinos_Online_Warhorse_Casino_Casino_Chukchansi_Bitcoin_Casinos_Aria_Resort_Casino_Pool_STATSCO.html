A month after enhancing its live coverage with cutting-edge ads, STATSCORE, a leading provider of reliable sports data, live sports statistics and software, announced that it has expanded its soccer portfolio with the addition of betting data for certain Asian leagues. According to the company, the expansion will cover leagues with great growth potential.

STATSCORE explained that the new additions come amid a talent drain that has seen multiple soccer legends migrate to Saudi Arabia’s top-flight soccer scene. This includes notable athletes such as Christiano Ronaldo, Karim Benzema and Sadio Mané, the data company explained.

As a result, STATSCORE decided to tap into some of Asia’s best soccer leagues and competitions, expanding its soccer portfolio with the addition of many beloved Asian leagues and competitions.

STATSCORE noted that the new products will be available for ordering by its partners via the ScoutsFeed product and the company’s live data widgets, like LivematchPro. The company emphasized that ScoutsFeed offers customizable coverage options that allow its partners to only include the matches it and its fans are interested in.
