MaxBet and Oddin.gg Sign New Deal

Those jurisdictions represent strategic markets where MaxBet has already established its casino and online betting services. At the same time, the company plans to further expand into Africa, which is a market that holds significant potential.

Although currently, MaxBet has an esports presence, the new deal with Oddin.gg is expected to improve those operations. On the other hand, as noted, Oddin.gg will benefit from expanding the reach of its leading solutions and entering strategic markets in the Balkans.
