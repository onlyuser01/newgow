Dr Eyal Loz, RubyPlay’s CPO, revealed that the company is delighted to join forces with YSI for this exciting deal. “This partnership not only marks our first entry into the US, but also underscores our commitment to delivering innovative, responsible and engaging gaming experiences,” he added. Finally, Dr Loz said that RubyPlay was focused on adapting its content to the social vertical and it is looking forward to providing engaging social casino experiences.
