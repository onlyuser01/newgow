Second Sports Partnership in the UK

The sports deal Tebwin signed with the Warriors comes only months after the brand operated by King J. Entertainment partnered with English Football League (EFL) soccer club Queens Park Rangers FC for the 2021-22 season in the Championship.

“Delighted to join the Tebwin sports portfolio and work hand-in-glove” with the sports betting brand, Warriors commercial director Adam Palfrey outlined the opportunities “to develop additional excitement around the Warriors brand.”

“Our partnership with Tebwin.com will help Worcester Warriors to engage fans in responsible and innovative ways this season as the sports betting landscape continues to offer amusement and fun as well as enhancing the fans experience.” Adam Palfrey, Commercial Director, Worchester Warriors
