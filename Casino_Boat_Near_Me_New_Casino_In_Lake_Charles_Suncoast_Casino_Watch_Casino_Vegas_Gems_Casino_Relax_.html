The Money Cart Bonus Is Back

Another classic game feature that has made a return in Money Train 3 is the Money Cart Bonus Round. The feature was incredibly popular among players of the previous game in the series and it is expected to trigger similar results in the new release.

At the beginning of the round, the symbols used to trigger the round will give layers three free spins, while showcasing a value displayed as a multiplication of the initial bet.
