Sakura Fortune review

Theme and Storyline

Sakura Fortune has a romantic East Asian theme.

Players will wander down an old path to find an idyllic world of cherry blossoms and greenery. Here, a hero and heroine will guide them on a quest to gather beautiful relics.

Players in the mood for a fantasy escape, and lovers of East Asian storytelling, will immediately get sucked into this game.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

A calming classically East Asian tune plays, filled with flutes, and delicate zither notes help establish the exotic atmosphere. A breathy flute note adds tension to spins, ensuring the moment is exciting.

On the reels, players will find jade lions, carved dragons, and plenty of Chinese coins. A sword wielding hero and a beautiful heroine with jewel-adorned hair also make an appearance. It is easy to imagine the pair of them in a fantasy story.

The most striking symbol is a princess entirely clad in red, her hair caught by the breeze. With her hand on her sword, she peers down at the players with an unmistakably royal air.

The setting behind the reels is filled with wild greenery and bright pink blossoms. The greenish light seems to be filtered through a forest canopy, adding a mysterious vibe.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack create an alluring world of Asian fantasy.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols and also functions as a Scatter symbol. It appears at a 4x1 size and only lands on reels 2, 3, and 4 during the base game as well as the Free Spins feature.

The Bonus Scatter symbol only lands during the base game, and when players land three or more Bonus Scatters consecutively, the Free Spins feature begins.

3 or more Scatter and Wild Scatter symbols award Free Spins bonus

During the Free Spins feature, any Wilds that land on the reels expand to cover their entire reel and award players an additional free spin. The Wild remains stuck on the reels until the Free Spins feature concludes.

The Sakura Fortune Respin feature is triggered when players land 2 full stacks of Wilds. The number of respins awarded is decided by the number of Wilds on the reels when the feature is triggered.
