WynnBET Launches Sports Betting Services in Tennessee

The premier casino and sports betting application, WynnBET, launched its mobile sportsbook services in Tennessee last week. The launch came after the board of directors of the Sports Wagering Committee of the Tennessee Education Lottery gave its approval. Consequently, WynnBET launched its services, making Tennessee the sixth state where the application has launched in less than nine months. Currently, the application is available for download anywhere in Tennessee, on both Android and Apple devices.

World-famous hospitality and casino company, Wynn Resorts, launched WynnBET last year. Since the launch, the company has already entered the markets in Indiana, Virginia, Colorado, Michigan, and New Jersey. Besides the markets where WynnBET is already live, the company has revealed that it has market access opportunities in Ohio, Nevada, Pennsylvania, Illinois, Iowa, and Massachusetts, subject to legalization and regulatory approvals from the relevant authorities in each jurisdiction.
