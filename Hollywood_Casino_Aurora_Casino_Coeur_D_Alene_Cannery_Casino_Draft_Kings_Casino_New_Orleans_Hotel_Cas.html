However, Fish 'n' Nudge comes packed with engaging and rewarding features, perfectly fusing a classic feel with modern functionality.

This 5x4 reel slot game from Push Gaming offers medium volatility, variable RTPs such as 96.40% or 94.38% and a maximum win of 10,000x. The engaging slot uses symbols that match its distinct fishing theme such as lures, reels and rods, among others.

Bringing thrilling and rewarding features, Fish 'n' Nudge is the perfect addition to the growing portfolio of Push Gaming. The recently released slot game follows other hit titles from the company, such as Razor Returns, Rat King and Goat Getter.
