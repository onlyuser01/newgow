Huddle, a next-gen iGaming tech provider, has reinforced its leadership team with the addition of Dylan Mitchard as vice president of business development. Mitchard has years of experience in the technology and iGaming industries, which he will leverage to speed up Huddle’s growth.

Dylan Mitchard Joins Huddle

As mentioned, Mitchard has previously worked for notable companies in the tech and iGaming sectors. His career started with a tenure at GlobalData where he worked as a business development executive. Thanks to his hard work, he was quickly promoted to global business development manager.

After that, Mitchard left GlobalData and joined the global sports data specialist Genius Sports. He served the company as a business development manager for three years. Mitchard next had a short stint as Inspired Entertainment’s head of business development of virtual sports.

As VP of business development, the new appointee will be in charge of forging key new business partnerships, helping Huddle grow and expand its reach. The company was recently supported by the casino juggernaut Las Vegas Sands and earlier underwent a merger with DeckPrism Sports, a US-based sports odds specialist.

Huddle Is happy to Have the New VP on Board

Leo Gaspar, Huddle’s chief business development officer, had a few words about the new employee. He said that the entire Huddle team is delighted to have Mitchard join the company as VP of business development. Gaspar lauded the hire’s industry experience and profound understanding of the entire iGaming ecosystem.

In addition, Gaspar believes that Mitchard is a great fit for the company. The business development officer is looking forward to working with the new VP on strengthening Huddle’s business.

More importantly, we see him as a great cultural fit. I am honored that he will be working alongside me in building our business development vertical from the ground up. We wish Dylan all the best in his new role. Leo Gaspar, chief business development officer, Huddle

Huddle is a fresh company that was launched in 2020. It uses a microservices model to build products. The company offers various sports betting solutions, such as trading, odds feeds and risk tech.

The company’s software leverages artificial intelligence and machine learning to analyze data. This helps Huddle’s partnering operators boost their efficiencies across the numerous jurisdictions they operate in, helping them offer content that is tailored to the needs of local audiences. In addition, AI-powered tools help operators optimize risk.

All and all, Huddle is an innovative supplier that seeks to contribute to the industry’s growth by building advanced data-driven technology solutions.
