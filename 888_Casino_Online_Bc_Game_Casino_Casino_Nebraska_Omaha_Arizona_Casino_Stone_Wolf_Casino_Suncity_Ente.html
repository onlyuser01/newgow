The largest Asian junket operator, Suncity Group, is heading into a Vietnam to manage an integrated resort, the group has announced.

Suncity Group Joins the Vietnamese Market

Suncity Group Holdings Ltd has revealed plans to join the Hoiana integrated resort through a subsidiary, SGMC (Hoi An). The announcement came on Monday, May 13, with Suncity Group inking a deal that would allow it to cater and provide casino properties with food and beverages.

Suncity has a stake in the developing Hoiana project, which is a joint-venture owned together with Chow Tai Fook Enterprises and VinaCapital, a Vietnam-based real estate company. Suncity is currently one of the owners of the Hoiana project with a 34-per-cent stake.

Bent on investing in its future, Suncity’s subsidiary will be the main caterer for the IR project for a period of 10 years, with an option to extend the partnership by another 10 years. SGMC will have to consult with other stake holders to establish proper rates as well as to get the go-ahead to acquire the necessary equipment to service the casinos.

A Slice of Heaven for VIP Customers
