The fast-growing provider of software for iGaming and sportsbook operators, Altenar, signed a new deal, expanding further its Latin American presence. Under the terms of a new collaboration with the experienced and popular sports betting operator GanaPlay, the company agreed to deliver its full multi-channel software.

Announced Monday, the deal will see the operator tap into the extensive software solutions and offering by Altenar that are specifically designed for the LatAm vertical. Per the new collaboration, GanaPlay will access sports betting on virtual sports, as well as lottery and quinielas. The new content deal will also see the operator gain access to international sports betting options such as soccer.

“We are thrilled to announce a deal with Altenar to offer our award-winning product to the LatAm markets.“ Andres Felipe Clavijo, CEO at Ganaplay

Andres Felipe Clavijo, GanaPlay’s CEO, said that the company is delighted to forge a new deal with Altenar and leverage the company’s products to boost its LatAm growth. He predicted that Altear’s Partial Cashout and Betbuilder solutions will contribute toward GanaPlay’s growth in the region. Finally, Clavijo said: “We’re excited to get started.”

The Deal Is Expected to Help GanaPlay Continue to Grow

GanaPlay has been a part of the market in Central and Latin America for more than 15 years. During that time, the operator accumulated trust and became popular with its offering.
