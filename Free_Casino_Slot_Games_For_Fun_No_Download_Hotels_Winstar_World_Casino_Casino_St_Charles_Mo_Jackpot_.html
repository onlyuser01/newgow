Play’n GO has launched Scroll of Seth, a new 5x3 slot with 50 paylines, free spins, and wild multipliers

With the Ancient Egypt god of disaster, storms, and deserts at the center, precious relics ready to be uncovered, and a valuable scroll that can turn everything into gold, Play’n GO’s latest slot, Scroll of Seth, has a lot of exciting features to offer.

The Scandinavian studio that creates content that stands the test of time has released many other games packing the interesting Ancient Egypt/powerful deity theme, including Dawn of Egypt in 2020, Ankh of Anubis and Doom of Egypt in 2019, and Legacy of Egypt in 2018.

At the start of the month, Play’n GO announced that its most iconic slot, Book of Dead, will be available for retail players with William Hill in the UK. Grab the Chaotic Power and Dig for the Treasures

In Scroll of Seth, players are invited to start the hunt for “the unimaginably powerful artifact” while crossing their fingers for the special game features to be activated for even more excitement and greater win potential.

Horizontal Reel Feature

Players who come across Seth’s magic scroll on the third reel automatically trigger the dormant horizontal reel located on top. The dormant reel will come to life and start spinning with wild symbols on it. The wild symbols can potentially fill any base game reel they appear above, for some even higher wins.

Free Spins

When at least three Golden Ankh scatter symbols are seen showing up on the grid, they generate a varying number of free spins (between eight and 15 free spins). Players who land another Scroll of Seth symbol during a Free Spins round will manage to unlock a second tier for the Scroll of Seth bonus.

This will allow the Scroll to join the horizontal reel. In case the same symbol is seen landing on this reel after it starts spinning, more wild multipliers are added. They can expand onto the reels, attracting an even juicier treasure trove of riches.

Betting starts at $0.10 and goes all the way up to $100 while the top jackpot is capped at 5,000x the stake, which is a common value for high volatility slots.
