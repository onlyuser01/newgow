Relax and Greentube Partner up for European Superiority

Leading iGaming aggregator Relax Gaming has entered a new deal with Greentube, Novomatic’s interactive division, to offer exclusive gaming content across regulated European markets.

To start the partnership a selection of proprietary and third-party games from Relax will be deployed with the Estonian online casino brand Fenixbet, the online brand recently launched by land-based operator Novolotto.

In the next few months, the agreement with Greentube is scheduled to spread to other markets like Germany, when iGaming becomes officially legal in the summer of 2021. A further expansion is expected for Relax in some of Europe’s most important markets.

“To be partnering with Greentube is a feather in our cap and one that will further our reach into core European markets,” said Daniel Eskola, Relax Gaming CCO in a statement.

Eskola also commented that Greentube’s strong relationships with Germany will be a major benefit to the Relax’s own growth plans when the regulated online gaming industry launches next year.

“The diversity and quality of the games provided through Relax’s aggregation platform will significantly boost Fenixbet’s offering and we are confident they will be a hit with its growing customer base,” stated Marcantonio Bottaro, Director of Brand Operations RMG at Greentube.

More Games For the Best Gaming Experience
