Unibet Launches PA Sportsbook, Second in the USA

Unibet, a Kindred-owned sports betting, casino and poker brand, has launched its second sportsbook in the United States. The new betting facility is located in Pennsylvania, one of the latest states to formally give its approval to sports betting post-May 2018 and launch online gambling and poker as of July 2019.

Unibet will unfold its offer in the state with the assistance of Mohegan Sun Pocono. The company is looking to establish a footprint in every major state across the US, after earlier, Unibet teamed up with the Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Atlantic City in New Jersey, just at the beginning of September.

While Mohegan Sun Pocono will initially focus on running retail operators, the property will gradually make its way into the digital space and online betting options will roll out before 2019 is out.

Unibet will rely on Kambi, one of the leading sports betting vendors, to provide the technology necessary for bettors to place their wagers in comfort and without running a risk of misfires. This is a particularly auspicious moment for Unibet to be expanding, as the NFL and NHL Leagues are back in full blast. Not least of all, the NBA games are also already running.

Everything Goes According to Plan

This launch marks several months of preparation after Kindred signed up the deal with Mohegan Sun Pocono earlier this year in January. Commenting on recent events, Kindred US SVP Manuel Stan:

“Introduction of the Unibet offering to the market is a great positive for Mohegan Sun, as well as the State of Pennsylvania.”
