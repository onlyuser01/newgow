Pariplay Adds New Upcoming Supplier to Ignite

Pariplay has earned a name for itself through the addition of a number of top-tier suppliers which now shape its motley family of products with Ignite. In turn, the company is capable of delivering the product throughout a global distribution network.

Caleta Gaming is still an emerging studio but one that has been deemed to offer a solid technological stack and creativity to be featured as part of the growing Ignite network.
