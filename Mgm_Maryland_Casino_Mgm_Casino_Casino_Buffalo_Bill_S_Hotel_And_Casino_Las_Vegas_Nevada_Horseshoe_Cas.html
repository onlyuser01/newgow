The partnership will see the supplier and developer of casino information and management systems deploy its CasinoTrac system, which is a dedicated Casino Management System. Golden Buffalo Casino & Motel General Manager Quintin HT McGhee welcomed this opportunity and said:

We are excited for the installation and future launch of Casino Trac’s integrated slot accounting and customer reward programs. Golden Buffalo Casino & Motel General Manager Quintin HT McGhee

He said that Casino Trac’s system will be capable of delivering the necessary experiences for guests and help optimize the facility’s own operations and results. One of the things that McGhee is looking forward is to leveraging the CasinoTrac system to make sure that the property builds up loyalty with existing customer, and also help drive customer growth.
