The award-winning infrastructure provider for the global gambling industry, Continent 8 Technologies, announced a senior-level appointment by hiring Dave Phillips, a former Google executive with extensive experience. Joining the company's C-suite, he was appointed to the role of Vice President, Network. Continent 8 Technologies' latest senior-level appointment comes at an important moment for the company as it continues to expand rapidly around the world.

As Vice President, Network, Phillips will oversee a team of network specialists across the globe. He will be responsible for experts located across the USA, Canada, the UK, Europe, as well as India and Singapore. Phillips' efforts will be focused on ensuring that Continent 8 Technologies retains its position as a leading provider of secure services, powering the global online gambling sector.

Currently, the company's global network has more than 100 locations on four continents. Continent 8 Technologies has established a strong presence across the United States, considering it operates in 30 different states.

In a statement, Phillips revealed that it is thrilling to join the company and praised its extensive global network, comparing it to the backbone of the online gambling industry. Finally, he said: "I'm excited by the extraordinary growth facing Continent 8, further developing our innovative product set and strengthening our position as the infrastructure partner of choice for many of the biggest operators and suppliers in the global gaming industry."

Edward O’Connor, Continent 8 Technologies' Chief Technology Officer, spoke about Phillips' extensive experience, knowledge and deep understanding of the internet sector. He added that the former Google executive brings a wide range of skills, making him a perfect fit for the role at Continent 8 Technologies. O'Connor outlined: "For over 25 years our industry’s biggest brands have relied on the Continent 8 network and hiring Dave to lead our global team will help to ensure that we continue to innovate on behalf of customers for the next 25 years."

Last but not least, Continent 8 Technologies' Chief Technology Officer said: "Dave is joining a fantastic team – with his experience and the immense creativity of our group, I’m certain that we’re going to invent great ways to help our customers in 2024 and beyond."

Overall, Phillips brings 15 years of experience after holding a number of roles with Google. During his tenure, he was in charge of different teams, including Product, Delivery, Engineering and Operations, allowing him to gain invaluable knowledge.
