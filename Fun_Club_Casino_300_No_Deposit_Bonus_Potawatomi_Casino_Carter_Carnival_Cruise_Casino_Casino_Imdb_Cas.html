Sports betting technology supplier Kambi Group announced today its sportsbook partnership with the owner of Parx Casino and betPARX, Greenwood Gaming and Entertainment, has been extended.

Powering Growth for Its Partner

Kambi and the owner of the largest casino complex in Pennsylvania initially partnered in 2018 in a multi-year sportsbook agreement which provided Greenwood Gaming with Kambi’s cutting-edge sports betting platform, range of managed services and best-in-class land-based wagering experience to Parx Casino and later to a Parx-branded online sportsbook.

Under the extension deal, Kambi will continue to provide Greenwood Gaming with its best-in-class technology and services, including its award-winning BetBuilder product, across multiple US states and Kambi’s chief executive officer and co-founder Kristian Nylén was more than delighted to have secured the contract extension with the “valued partner.”

“Since we first partnered in 2018, it has been fantastic to watch betPARX grow from a local operator in the Philadelphia area to a nationally recognized brand, and I am pleased that Kambi will continue to support the operator on its journey as it expands further across the United States.” Kristian Nylén, CEO and Co-Founder, Kambi Group

Greenwood Gaming is currently present in Pennsylvania via Parx Casino where it is powered by Kambi and with retail partners Gun Lake Casino in Michigan and Freehold Raceway in New Jersey. Its wholly-owned interactive and mobile gaming subsidiary betPARX is live in the same three states and Ohio and is planning to expand into additional states in the future.

Matthew Cullen, senior vice president of Interactive Gaming and Sports at betPARX was thrilled to continue working with Kambi and continue to grow the brand nationally powered by Kambi’s “award-winning technology.”
