The latest addition to the company's Premium portfolio delivers a Quadspin mechanic which grants a chance to further boost the winnings of the players

The award-winning provider of games for the online gambling vertical, Swintt, confirmed the release of a new Chinese culture-inspired slot game called Lucky Fortune Door. The engaging game, part of the company’s Premium portfolio, invites the players to the ancient Chinese realm, offering a chance for high payouts and providing unique and engaging gameplay.

To further elevate the experience for the players online, Lucky Fortune Door delivers the innovative Quadspin mechanic. The mechanic grants players a chance to win even bigger prizes, elevating the excitement and thrill of the new Premium slot game from Swintt.

The New Slot Delivers Engaging Experience

Swintt’s new title brings symbols from the Far East, including yin and yang pendants, lucky coins and red dragons. At the same time, Lucky Fortune Door uses the popular card symbols between 10 and ace. The game features quality sound and graphic effects, as well as the popular auto spin option.

David Mann, Swintt’s Chief Executive Officer (CEO), shared his excitement about the recently released slot game. He explained that an ongoing goal for the company is to reinvent the classic land-based format to the online vertical. According to Mann, Lucky Fortune Door achieves precisely this by offering the engaging Quadspins mechanic.

“At Swintt, we’re always dreaming up new and innovative ways to reinvent the classic land-based slot machine format – and in our latest Premium release, Lucky Fortune Door, we’ve managed to do just that by introducing the Quadspins mechanic.“ David Mann, CEO at Swintt

The company’s CEO pointed out that the Quadspins mechanic grants the players four separate chances to significantly boost their base game prize. Finally, Mann predicted that “Quadspins will prove a welcome gameplay addition that can seriously crank up the excitement after each spin and we hope to be able to roll it out across more slots soon.”

Quadspins Grant a Chance for Big Payouts

The 5×3 slot game offers 10 fixed paylines, as well as an RTP of 96.02%. Besides combinations within the standard paylines, Lucky Fortune Door offers options for special wins, via Quadspins. According to the game developer, to trigger the unique mechanic, players need to win at least four times their bet within the base game. Once this happens, they are granted a choice to either claim their prize or use part of it for a special Quadspin round.

If the online players engage in a Quadspin round, “the gameboard will be quartered and the subsequent spins will take place over four separate reel sets, potentially quadrupling the prizes on offer,” explained Swintt. Moreover, during Quadspins, the players can benefit from a mystery symbol that can unlock big payouts.
