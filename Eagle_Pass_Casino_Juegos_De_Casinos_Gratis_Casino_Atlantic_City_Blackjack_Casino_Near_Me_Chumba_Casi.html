The Slot Brings Golden Pearls, Free Spins

Players of Swintt’s newest game that land enough golden pearls can benefit from an engaging bonus round. Landing six or more such symbols during a single spin anywhere on the reel takes the players to a new 5×3 reel that features triggering pearls. “Initially, each of these symbols will spin to reveal a cash prize and players will receive a starting set of three free spins,” explains Swintt.

During this bonus round, a free spins counter indicates how many are remaining. If players land a new pearl symbol, they reset the counter. The special bonus round can continue until the counter reaches zero or there are no free spaces available on the reels. Elevating the excitement further, some pearls can offer a bonus modifier, granting a potential for mouth-watering prizes.

Lucky players can land special chest icons. Once this happens, “a countdown of ten will be displayed to indicate the number of spins needed before the chest will open,” reveals Swintt, explaining that once this number hits zero, players will be awarded randomly a Minor, Major, Maxi or Mega Pot.
