Gameplay

Fat Santa is a five reel, five row slot, with fifty win lines.

The gameplay moves at a cheerful pace and the reels move satisfyingly fast.

The Buy Feature allows players to immediately activate the Free Games Feature (see below for details). This costs 80 times the player's current bet, which the player confirms prior to going forward.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Fat Santa Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols.

Meanwhile, the Christmas Pie and Wild Christmas Pie Symbol also serve as Wild symbols. They do not substitute for the Fat Santa symbol.

The Free Games Feature is triggered when a Santa symbol and a Wild Christmas Pie symbol land anywhere on the reels at the same time.

Fat Santa Wild symbol travels around and eats up any Wild Christmas Pie symbols between spins

While in the Free Games Feature, players will see the Santa symbol go to the Christmas Pie symbols and eat them up. These consumed Christmas Pie symbols are added to the Christmas Pie meter. When the Christmas Pie meter is full, the Fat Santa symbol increases in size, covering more squares on the reels. The more pies, the bigger your wins as the number of winning combos increases.

Each 5 eaten Wild Christmas Pie symbols add more free spins and size of Fat Santa Wild symbol

At random, the Santa’s Sleigh Feature begins, flying over the reels. Santa’s sleigh leaves a trail of Christmas Pie symbols in randomized positions. These symbols then act as Wild symbols.
