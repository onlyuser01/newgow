Gameplay

Aztec Gems Deluxe is a three reel slot machine. Each reel has three rows. The game offers nine win lines that are non-adjustable.

The excitement really begins with the bonus features (see below for details).

Overall, this slot is well paced, with each spin seamlessly leading to the next.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Money symbol.

Appearing on all the reels, the Money symbol takes on a random value each spin. It may have Mini or Minor Jackpot values.

The Money Respin Feature begins once other win lines have paid. As the regular symbols disappear, the Money symbols remain and special reels replace the regular ones. These special reels only contain Money symbols and blank spots.

Main bonus feature of the game, the Money Respin feature

Players are initially awarded three Respins. Every Money symbol that lands after each respin will remain in place until the end of the feature.

When players land at least one Money symbol, their Respins reset to 3. The players continue the Respin round until either the positions are filled or they run out of Respins.

When the feature is complete, players receive the values of all the Money symbols. This total is added to the total winnings.
