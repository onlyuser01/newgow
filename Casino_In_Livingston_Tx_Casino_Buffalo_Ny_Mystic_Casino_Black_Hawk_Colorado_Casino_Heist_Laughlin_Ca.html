Commenting on the partnership, Aardvark Technologies’ chief executive officer Simas Denisovas outlined the need for any business operating in the competitive iGaming sector to acquire new customers while “keeping hold of its existing ones.”

Ibex.Ai was exactly what we’ve been looking for as they have developed a system with a different approach to allow the maximum possible personalization and an extremely high level of automation which we think is crucial for our partners. Simas Denisovas, CEO, Aardvark Technologies

With a system built to meet the needs of a modern online gaming operator, Ibex.ai provides for increased customer retention and customer lifetime value (LTV) rates, utilizing 100% personalization and a high level of automation-based machine learning algorithms.

Using unique monetization-based calculations to maximize profitability, the system targets each customer with the most profitable offer at the right time via the correct channels to ensure a high level of monetization in a responsible way.

Shared Passion for ‘Cutting-Edge’ Technology
