Bally’s Corporation, a U.S. gaming company, has submitted a proposal to Richmond, Virginia, to build a $650 million World Class Resort and Casino.

Bally’s Richmond Casino Resort Could Attract 3.7 Million Visitors

According to the proposal, “Bally’s Richmond Casino Resort” would consist of a 4-star hotel, superb dining amenities, pool and spa, state-of-the-art casino, and sportsbook, and a place for live entertainment conferences. The resort and casino could provide job opportunities to 2000 employees, full-time, annually attract 3.7 million visitors, and generate a profit of $415 million per year.

The proposed project would spread over 1.6 million sq. feet on a 61-acre parcel located on the west side of Richmond, Virginia. With direct access to the highway and proximity to the downtown area, the location promises to become a popular destination without disturbing the communities around. The project would take approximately 18 months to build and could open in 2024. The construction would cost $650 million, including the one-time payment of $100 million to the City of Richmond.

Last year Bally’s Corporation entered a long-term partnership with Elite Casino Resorts and secured its positions in Iowa. With this collaboration, Bally’s extended its reach over 11 states, making this the first state on which it doesn’t operate through its land-based casino.

Soo Kim, Chairman of Bally’s Corporation’s Board of Directors, stated that he’s interested in the city’s prosperity. He said that he is grateful for the opportunity and emphasized what developing this project could mean for the city and the thousands of jobs it could create. Kim stated that Bally’s is committed to the local businesses and communities and wants to promote diversity.
