Fast Track CEO and co-founder Simon Lidzén commented on the partnership explaining that teaming up with innovative brands such as Dunder felt rewarding to the company’s inhouse team.

He said that adding accomplished operators allows Fast Track to see how far the tool can go and also obtain valuable feedback about future prospects and improvements:

“We anticipate that Dunder will be producing truly innovative player engagements once we are live.” Fast Track CEO Simon Lidzén
