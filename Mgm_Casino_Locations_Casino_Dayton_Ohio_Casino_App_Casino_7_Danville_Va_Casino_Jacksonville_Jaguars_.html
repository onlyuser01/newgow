Atmosphere is Great in the Jacksonville Jaguars Locker Room

The Jacksonville Jaguars beat the Dallas Cowboys 40-34 in overtime after 34-34 in regulation. Even though they were four-point underdogs before the game, they ended up winning thanks to an inspired performance in the second half.

In fact, it was 34-31 for the Cowboys with under two minutes left on the clock. Lawrence run away from his opponents but then got the ball knocked from his arms in the crucial moment.

Luckily for him, the Jags defense saved the day. They stopped the Cowboys offense deep in their half, getting the ball back to Lawrence. This time, the Jags did well, with Riley Patterson scoring a three-point field goal to take the game to overtime.

Then, it was Jacksonville’s defense that got in the spotlight once again – safety Rayshawn Jenkins made a game-winning pick-six for the Jaguars’ sixth win of the season.
