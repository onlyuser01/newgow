Odds On Compliance has named a new president to lead its European division and replicate the company’s US success

Odds On Compliance, a technology and consultancy firm providing gambling firms with crucial regulatory advice, has announced a new addition to its team. The company has reinforced its European division, naming the experienced compliance specialist Cheryl Jones as president of Odds On Compliance Europe.

Odds On Compliance Europe Designated Jones as President

Jones is a gambling industry veteran with intimate knowledge of the sector. Her expertise was cultivated over years in the gaming sector, during which she occupied compliance-related roles at several notable companies.

Jones’ career in gaming began when she joined NMi Metrology and Gaming, a provider of testing and auditing services to the gambling industry. She served as a regulatory specialist and corporate legal specialist, helping her develop her skills. The company would later be acquired by Gaming Laboratories International where Jones would occupy a variety of compliance-related positions.

Between September 2021 and December 2021, Jones had a short stint as the chief legal and compliance officer of g.games. After that, she joined Lady Luck Games, a fast-growing developer of casino games. During her time at Lady Luck, Jones worked as the provider’s chief legal and compliance officer.

Jones has previously worked with international regulators and has offered guidance on responsible gambling, Odds On Compliance emphasized. Her profound experience is sure to help Odds On Compliance replicate its US success in Europe.

Jones Is Thrilled to Grow Odds On Compliance in Europe

Jones shared her thoughts on joining Odds On Compliance. She praised the company for being the go-to provider of compliance-related advisory services in the United States and said that she is excited to work for the company’s European arm. Jones is looking forward to bringing Odds On Compliance’s regulatory knowledge to Europe and helping the company’s clients navigate the regulatory landscape.

Eric Frank, Odds On Compliance’s chief executive officer and co-founder, welcomed Jones on board. He lauded the new hire as a highly committed person who is capable of leveraging tech and innovation to improve compliance. This, he said, makes her a great fit for the company.

Cheryl’s experience, determination, and leadership will strengthen our position as the global leader in the compliance space. Eric Frank, CEO, Odds On Compliance

Frank briefly addressed Odds On Compliance’s foray into Europe, calling it an “exciting moment” in the company’s history.

Cheryl Jones was designated as one of the Most Influential Women in Gaming in 2022 and would be a valuable addition to Odds On Compliance’s team.
