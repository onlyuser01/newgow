Digitain, a global leader in the provision of award-winning sports betting and gaming platform technology, has added a true sports star to its ambassador team. The renowned soccer legend Luís Figo will be joining the company as a brand ambassador.

Figo Is a Legend in Soccer

This is a historic moment for Digitain because Figo is acclaimed as one of the most famous Portuguese players in the world. He was among the top players during his prime and played for some of the best-known teams in the world. Figo served as a winger under the banner of Barcelona, Inter Milan and Real Madrid, to name a few.

Not only did Figo play for some of the powerhouses in soccer, but he also won numerous trophies during his career. Some of his most notable feats include a UEFA Cup Winner’s Cup, a UEFA Champions League title, four Series A titles and four La Liga titles. In addition, he was named FIFA World Player for 2001 and received the Ballon d’OR in 2000.

Figo’s feats and the popularity of his name make him a perfect partner for Digitain, which remains ambitious in maintaining its leadership position in iGaming.

Because of those qualities, Vardanyan is happy to have the soccer legend represent Digitain and its offerings.
