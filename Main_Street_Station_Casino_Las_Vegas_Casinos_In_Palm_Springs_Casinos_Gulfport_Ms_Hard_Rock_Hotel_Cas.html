Take the Bank: Game Symbols and Payouts

The highest-paying symbols in the game are the diamonds, the gold bars, and the cash stacks. Landing two, three, four, or five diamond symbols will bring players payouts of 0.10x, 0.40x, 1.20x, and 6x. The mid-paying symbols are represented by the gold bullions paying 0.30x, 0.60x, and 2x for landing three, four, or five symbols, and stashes of cash symbols paying 0.30x, 0.50x, and 1.80x for three, four, or five matching symbols respectively.

The low-paying symbols are represented by the safe door, the red bomb, and the crowbar symbols each paying 0.20x, 0.40x, and 1.20x for three, four, or five identical symbols showing up on the reels. On the lower end, players will discover the ‘A’ that reward players with payouts of 0.20x, 0.40x, and 1.20x and ‘K’, ‘Q’, ‘J’, ’10’, and ‘9’ triggering wins of 0.10x, 0.20x, and 0.60x each.

The game also has the crafty robber as the wild. The police cars represent the scatter. Matching three scatters at a time unlocks the free spins feature we are going to speak more about below. The highest payout can reach up to $4,500 which represents 225x the initial wager.

Take the Bank Gameplay

The slot has simple gameplay on the five reels and 75 pay lines. New players need to start by setting the value of the minimum bet amount per payline. The value ranges from $0.20 to $20 on a single spin. Next, players will need to use the “+” and “-” icon located at the bottom of the screen to toggle and adjust their best according to their specific needs. The pay lines are fixed and they do not require adjusting.

The gameplay can be customized using the Autoplay feature that lets players select the desired number of automated spins they would like to initiate together with the total cost of each spin. There is also a sped-up Turboplay alternative as well as the possibility to adjust the sound and background effects.

Finally, on the home screen, players can click the spin button to get started and keep an eye on the bomb-shaped countdown timer that will gradually decrease from 10 down to 0. Once the counter hits zero, the robber symbols turn into wilds and scatters and show the payout.

There is also a double-up feature available for standard wins where players can predict the result of a coin flip and possibly double their wins. There is also a buy feature that lets players purchase 15 free spins and engage in play using five, seven, or 10 wild symbols.

Take the Bank: Heist Spins & Free Spins

Heist Spins

To engage in a one-of-a-kind progression system, players must keep an eye on the bomb counter every time they are ready to spin the reels again. The robber symbol will turn into bombs. At the end of 10 free spins, the bombs will turn into wild symbols.

Free Spins

To enter the bonus game, players need to land a minimum of three police car symbols. In the bonus game, players get five, seven, or 10 epic wild symbols spread all across the reels. Each new spin will relocate the wilds to a brand new location on the grid, paying out huge wins.
