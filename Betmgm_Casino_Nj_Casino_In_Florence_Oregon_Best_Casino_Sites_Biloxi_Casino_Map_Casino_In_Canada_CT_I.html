The fast-growing software provider of games for iGaming operators, CT Interactive, confirmed it signed a new deal that will help it expand the reach of its products. The company revealed it joined forces with the innovative game provider SpinOro. Per the terms of the partnership, a selection of CT Interactive content will be added to SpinOro's aggregation platform.

As a result, the company will leverage this exclusive set of games to expand its global footprint. At the same time, the deal will bring benefits for CT Interactive as well, by helping the company to significantly expand the reach of its games and delivering them to new audiences around the world.

CT Interactive is a leading provider of games for the iGaming vertical with more than a decade of experience, considering it was founded back in 2012 in Sofia, Bulgaria. By now, the company has launched more than 200 HTML 5 games. CT Interactive's content is certified in 18 different markets but the company is constantly looking forward to expanding this number, a statement reveals.

Milena Tsankarska, CT Interactive's Head of Business Development EMEA, commented on the topic saying: "At the end of the last month, we announced that an additional mix of our products has been certified for four markets." She referred to the certification the company secured for more games in markets that include Montenegro, Serbia, Bulgaria and the Czech Republic.

Finally, Tsankarska noted: "As our brand continues to add more and more products to its portfolio, we are actively working to conclude the certification processes as quickly as possible so that our partners can always have newer and better products. We are sure that SpinOro will benefit from our latest certifications."

Lachezar Petrov, CT Interactive's CEO, was similarly delighted about the partnership signed with SpinOro. "We are always very happy when we receive an opportunity to collaborate with another renowned company, such as SpinOro," he said.

Petrov acknowledged that SpinOro is a fast-growing brand that delivers unique and engaging products for its growing customer base. Finally, CT Interactive's CEO added: "By providing them with our games to operate on all the regulated markets they work on, we achieve one of our goals to increase visibility on those markets."

Jeff Letlat, SpinOro's COO, acknowledged that the company seeks to offer unrivaled gaming experiences by teaming up with leading developers and providers. "CT Interactive's portfolio is an incredibly valuable instrument in the process of satisfying our client's needs, and we are confident that incorporating it into our 3rd party aggregation platform will enhance our reach," he added.
