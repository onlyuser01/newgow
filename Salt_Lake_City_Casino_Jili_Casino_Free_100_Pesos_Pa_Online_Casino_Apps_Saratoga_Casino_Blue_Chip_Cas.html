NeoGames: a leading iLotto provider with grand plans

NeoGames started trading in 2005, setting its sights on the online lottery industry. Back then, internet lotto games were very basic, and NeoGames aimed to develop modern solutions that would tempt more people to play online.

The company's product range included its NeoSphere platform, which it supplied to online operators, and a selection of instant lotto-style games.

In 2014, after a string of successful partnerships, NeoPollard was formed – a product of an alliance between NeoGames and Canadian lottery firm Pollard BankNote. Described by experts as the most successful iLotto vendor in the United States, it has won several EGR awards and was responsible for around 70% of total US iLottery revenue in 2020.

Note: This success prompted NeoGames to raise $94 million in a 2020 share offering, which included publicly trading on Nasdaq under the symbol NGMS. By this point, it had already struck a 'strategic partnership deal' with major gambling firm William Hill, which bought just under 30% of NeoGames as part of the agreement.

Since then, NeoGames has shown no signs of slowing down. The company announced partnerships that enabled it to expand into the Austrian and Turkish markets in 2021, and it harbors plans to increase its size, making a $420 million bid to acquire Aspire Global.

With its considerable size, solid reputation, and licenses from several gambling authorities (including the MGA), we consider NeoGames to be a secure gaming provider, and one to watch for the future.

NeoGames games and software

NeoGames is an iLottery specialist, and all of its games have lotto-based elements to them, where the player's success comes down to fortune, rather than any level of skill.

The company divides its gaming stock into three categories, which we could label as variants of Lotto games:

eInstant

eScratch

Keno

eInstant

As the name suggests, these games are based on the idea of instant wins (or losses). As the player, you're given a grid of random numbers: your prize is then based on how many of those match the winning numbers. Often these games will include some nice features, like in Frost and Flame, you can choose whether you play on an icy or fiery setting; plus, you can land bonus prizes.

eScratch

A variation on eInstants where you uncover or 'scratch' each section of the grid to reveal the symbol or number beneath. It adds an element of intrigue compared to standard eInstant games, and NeoGames offers some stylish eScratch themes, like the slick design of VIP Ultra.

Keno

While not the most popular casino choice, Keno games appeal to some players because of their quick, uncomplicated gameplay. The difference here, though, is that you get to choose your numbers, which may add a feeling of control although, in reality, it's still a pure game of chance.

NeoGames' Keno offerings include Instant Keno Multiplier, Top Shot Keno, and Instant Keno.
