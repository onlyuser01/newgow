BlitzBet Parent Company Secures Majority Stake in LivePools

I3 Interactive is shifting its focus on the Indian iGaming platform market with a majority stake in LivePools, a self-billed “egaming platform” which will pave the way for I3 into one of the most populous gaming markets in the world.

I3 Interactive, which owns BlitzBet, a brand endorsed by Dan Bilzerian, will secure 51% of LivePools’ shares for the sum total of $7.5 million and will finalize the deal across four separate tranches.

As per the agreement, I3 may still seek to add the rest of the stock, or 49%, for another $7.5 million. However, any additional purchase of stock must be exercised by February 1, 2022. The deal is an important additional entry for I3 in the Indian market, with LivePools focusing on fantasy cricket and soccer, two of arguably the most popular sports in the country.

Thanks to the special status of daily fantasy sports, the activity is safer to carry out from a legal standpoint, too. LivePools also has products as online rummy, and the company expects to keep adding to its gaming portfolio in India as well as globally.
