Aztec Gems review

Theme and Storyline

This Aztec-themed slot is jam packed full of jewels.

Set against a dark and eerie jungle, the golden reels seem to glow. Each symbol is a different vividly hued gem. Players will feel like explorers facing a mythical trial every spin. They’ll want to walk away with every treasure.

The atmosphere is haunting, as if players have found themselves in a magical location. The atmosphere brings to mind classic adventure films such as Indiana Jones or Romancing the Stone.

The rendering of Aztec symbols definitely has a cinematic quality rather than an archeological one. However, players will find themselves entranced by this South American world created by Pragmatic Play.

The soundtrack sets an epic tone with drumbeats and birdcalls. Players may enjoy the increasing tension the music adds.

Gameplay

Aztec Gems is a three reel, three row slot, with five win lines.

This game also has a fourth reel, in which one item is randomly selected each spin. The item chosen serves as the win Multiplier for that spin, and applies to any win lines that the player lands.
