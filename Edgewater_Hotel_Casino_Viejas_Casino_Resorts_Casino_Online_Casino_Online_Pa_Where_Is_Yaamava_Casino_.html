80 Events During PokerStars Winter Series Offer $50M in Prizes

For the next three weeks, players at PokerStars will be able to enjoy all sorts of exciting online poker action as the Winter Series kicks of and will last until January 13th. Over 80 events will be available for players across the web, offering up over $50 million in guarantees.

As is the case with most major PokerStars events, the Winter Series will be split up into three tiers. Low, medium and high level buy-ins are available, starting at $5.50 going all the way up to $10,300.
