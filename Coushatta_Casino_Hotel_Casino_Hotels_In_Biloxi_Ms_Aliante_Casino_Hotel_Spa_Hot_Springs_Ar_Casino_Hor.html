Red Tiger Gaming Expands Nordics Influence

Sweden, being one of the fastest-growing betting jurisdictions in Europe, has been highly appealing to numerous betting companies. The country’s online punters have substantially increased in the past few years and the Swedish gambling industry has flourished.

ATG has a special place in the Swedish betting market. After it lost its monopoly after one of the SGA’s numerous regulation changes, the operator sought to expand its business by including more sports betting and iGaming offerings to its platform.
