Super Wilds and Scatters

The sharpshooting archer who is the main character of the new game never misses hitting wild symbols which are depicted as targets. Once such a symbol appears, the archer shoots it with an arrow, then the wilds “substitute all other symbols except the free spin-awarding scatter symbols and aid players in unlocking wins,” explains the game developer.

Additionally, Fire Archer players can also benefit from free spins. Hitting three scatters grants the slot players five free spins. During that round, super wild symbols can also be upgraded. What’s more, during the bonus round, players can also trigger additional bonuses granting them extra spins.
