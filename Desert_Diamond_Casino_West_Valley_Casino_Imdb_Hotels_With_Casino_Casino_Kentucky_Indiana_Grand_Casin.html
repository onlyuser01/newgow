Join Caesar in His Wild March

The new title will see players collect a variety of symbols, including gems, Roman coins, swords, goblets, harps, caligae, centurions and Caesar himself. Caesar and the Roman soldiers serve as wild symbols, substituting all regular symbols and providing opportunities for huge wins.

In addition to that, Caesar’s multiplier symbol triggers Caesar’s Wild March, a special mechanic that will see the symbol remain on the reels and move one position to the left with each successive spin. Caesar’s multiplier will increase by 2x with each move until he exits the grid.

The game’s titular mechanic is triggered by any win that includes a wild symbol. All reels involved in the winning combination are then nudged one position. The nudges continue until no more wins are possible. This mechanic greatly boosts the chances of triggering a bonus round.

Win Big with Free Spins

Roamin’ Romans UltraNudge also boasts a free spins mode that is triggered when players land on three or more golden Roman eagles. Players will be awarded 10 spins that they can gamble to increase the multiplier of Caesar’s wild symbol during the bonus round.

During free spins mode, Caesar’s multiplier will not reset. This means that huge opportunities wait each time Caesar appears on the reels and triggers his Wild March.

Players can skip straight to the action and buy free spins for 100x or 300x the bet. Alternatively, they can use the bonus boost feature to increase their chances of triggering free spins mode.
