Wanted Dead or a Wild review

Theme and Storyline

Wanted Dead or a Wild has a dark Western theme.

An orange sunset burns in the distance and players face a spooky Western landscape. In this gritty world, they’ll need whiskey, bullets, and a little luck to get by.

This slot is a good pick for Western lovers who are ready to walk a more mysterious road. Cowboys and outlaws alike will need to be up for adventure on this dark frontier.

The slow-burn soundtrack brings to mind a cowboy riding out to meet a villain. The music manages to be unobtrusive, even as it adds tension, though the loop is quite short.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbols and the VS symbols.

VS symbols will expand to cover the reel whenever their expansion will lead to a win. Players will then see two outlaws with different multipliers. This pair will duel, and the reel will also transform into Wilds and the winner’s multiplier will be applied to the reel. The outlaw’s multipliers will be between x2 and x100.

Should players land one VS symbol on each reel, then all the reels will become fully Wild.

Lucky player may win a lot if multiple Duel symbols land in one spin

When players land 3 or more of the Duel symbols, the Duel at Dawn Bonus feature will begin, starting with 10 free spins.

The Great Train Robbery feature is triggered when players land 3 or more Train Robbery symbols. During this feature players will have 10 free spins and all Wild symbols will be sticky.

The Dead Man’s Hand feature is triggered when players land 3 or more Dead symbols. During this, players accumulate Wilds and multipliers to be later applied to winnings.
