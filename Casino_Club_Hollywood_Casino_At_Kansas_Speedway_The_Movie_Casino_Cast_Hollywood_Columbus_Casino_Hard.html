One-log-in-to-all mobile sports app developer Oddsium has announced it will launch an innovative aggregator app in New Jersey by the end of March. The app will be used to offer smarter, smoother, and simplified sports betting.

Oddsium Is Eager to Join the American Market

Founded in 2012 by Krister Malm and several investors in Stockholm, Oddsium is a sports betting app that offers a unified user interface that allows bettors to log in a single time and access all available sports betting operators. The app that can be downloaded from the App Store provides direct odds betting with multiple companies, live scores, and transparent overviews of all betting outcomes.

The Oddsium app launch that is scheduled for the upcoming weeks will help the company enter the US market and tap into its incredible potential for growth in the context of more states legalizing mobile sports apps.

Last September, sports betting in New Jersey reached the $1.01 billion mark. Co-founder and group CEO Krister Malm expressed the company’s excitement regarding the penetration of the American market, while CEO Todd Kobrin emphasized that sports bettors should be able to enjoy convenient betting experiences when placing their bets using several different sportsbooks.

What Should Sports Bettors in New Jersey Expect From Oddsium Launch?

The Oddsium app will provide fully customizable experiences via advanced machine learning solutions. This means each bettor will be able to use a single login button to consult ultra-fast live scores in real-time, place their sports bets instantly using a plethora of different sportsbooks, and know the exact status of all of their bet and game results on a 24/7 basis.

Plus, bettors from New Jersey that will use the innovative Oddsium app will also receive unlimited access to their preferred sports teams and leagues while enjoying the cool design and simplified user interface of the app. In other words, bettors will benefit from all the options and information necessary before, during, and after placing a bet using Oddsium’s advanced technology.

The app is expected to completely change the way sportsbooks work in the future and the company has also announced its further plans to expand in Colorado, Indiana, and Pennsylvania by the end of 2022.
