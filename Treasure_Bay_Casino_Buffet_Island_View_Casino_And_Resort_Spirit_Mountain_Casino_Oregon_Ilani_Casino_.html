Timed Jackpots Offer a Novel Way to Enjoy Games

Red Tiger’s cutting-edge progressive jackpot mechanic has already proven its merits in the North American market after successful launches in Connecticut, Ontario, and Quebec. The Evolution-owned supplier boasts 48 titles that support the feature, allowing operators greater control over jackpots while remaining fair and engaging for players.

Games with the feature have two progressive jackpots configurable by the operator that users are guaranteed to hit within a specified time frame. A basic Mini Jackpot, usually set to $100, helps keep players engaged. The Daily Jackpot commonly sits at $1,000 and can be configured to go off between preset hours.
