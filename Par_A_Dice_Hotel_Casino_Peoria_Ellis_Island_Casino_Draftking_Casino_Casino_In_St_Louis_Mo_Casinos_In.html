Dionysus: Golden Feast review

Theme and Storyline

Dionysus: Golden Feast is themed around Greek mythology, particularly the god of wine Dionysus.

Players will step into an idyllic garden where they’ll find lush grapes ripening. The jolly god Dionysus will guide them through his charming world, offering plenty of tempting refreshments along the way.

Win in the Dionysus: Golden Feast slot

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack of Dionysus: Golden Feast is a relaxing melody featuring ethereal flute notes. Bird calls can be heard in the distance, reinforcing the sense that players are in an exotic garden.

On the reels, plump purple grapes appear, glinting in the sunlight. Harps, theater masks, embellished urns, and cornucopias overflowing with fruit are also among the symbols.

Dionysus himself is the most memorable symbol with his welcoming smile, lush beard, and crown of leaves and grapes. He seems suitably joyful for a god of wine and pleasure.

Behind the reels, a garden paradise can be seen with vibrant greenery and a sparkling fountain. A marble arch and a majestic statue give a distinctly ancient Greek feel to the setting.

The reels themselves are framed in ornately carved gold, adding to the lavish feel of this deity’s garden.

Together, the soundtrack and animation create a dreamy mythical world.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbols.

Every winning combination is multiplied by the Wild multiplier, based on this table

The Sticky Respin feature is triggered when the Golden Scatter symbol transforms into a randomly selected sticky symbol. Throughout this feature, the sticky symbols remain on the reels and when any matching symbols land, they will also remain on the reels.

The Sticky Respin feature continues until no more symbols become sticky, or until all positions on the reels are filled with sticky symbols.

The Free Spins feature begins when the meter beside the reels is filled with 3 Scatters. The feature begins with 8 free spins and can be retriggered with 4 additional free spins if 3 Scatters fill the meter again. The meter is reset between each spin.

At the start of every free spin, a Gold Scatter symbol is placed at random on the reels.
