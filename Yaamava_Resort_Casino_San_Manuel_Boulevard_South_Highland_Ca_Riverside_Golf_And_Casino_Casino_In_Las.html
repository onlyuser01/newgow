Lion Gaming Group continues to focus on its iGaming portfolio with the latest acquisition of 1Click Games, a prominent studio in the industry

The acquisition will considerably enhance Lion Gaming Group’s expertise in the delivery of online products. The Group is focused on the development of a technology platform that brings around white-label solutions for casino and sportsbook operators.

1Click Games Joins the Lion Gaming Group’s Family

The turnkey solutions made available by Lion Gaming Group and its software are available to both digital and land-based partners. That includes powerful aggregation platforms. Moving forward, all of this will be bolstered by the addition of 1Click Games’ unique products. Those include lottery, aggregation, land-based, crypto, and other high-performing solutions.

1Click Games has been delivering outstanding products for the iGaming industry since it was founded back in 2014. The company intends to continue doing so under its new parent company. Commenting on the latest deal, Lion Gaming Group president & CEO Duncan McIntyre welcomed the opportunity to further bolster the company’s iGaming lineup, saying:

As Lion Gaming Group continues to explore the ways we can enhance our product offering, we’re very excited to add 1Click Games to our portfolio of companies. Lion Gaming Group president & CEO Duncan McIntyre

The acquisition allows Lion Gaming Group to expand with what McIntyre says are 40 online casino brands, giving a strong boost to its operations. He further praised the team behind the 1Click Games brand. McIntyre noted that they are accomplished specialists in various spheres, such as payments, compliance, customer service, development, engineering, and more.

A Marriage Meant to Enhance Global Expansion

The acquisition was similarly praised by 1Click Games CEO Maksims Terehovics. The executive said that he was very happy to see the company join the Lion Gaming Group family. The collaboration will further consolidate the brands’ global expansion efforts and growth ambitions.

“We can offer a range of premier products to both regular and crypto businesses through the combination of technologies and products. It allows us to create a truly unique proposition for the market,” Terehovics explained.

All in all, the executive sees the tie-up as a stepping-stone for further success in the gambling industry, one that both 1Click Games and Lion Gaming Group will now strive to notch up as part of the same gaming group.
