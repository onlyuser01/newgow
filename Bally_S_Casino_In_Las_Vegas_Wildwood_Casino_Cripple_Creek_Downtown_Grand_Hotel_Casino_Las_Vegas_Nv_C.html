The leading iGaming content and solutions developer, Light & Wonder, announced it expanded its collaboration with the globally-recognized Warner Bros. Discovery Global Themed Entertainment (WBDGTE). Details regarding the new collaboration emerged Wednesday and will see Light & Wonder develop new thrilling online games.

Under the new multi-year global licensing deal with Warner Bros, the iGaming developer will be able to expand its portfolio of offering by delivering titles such as Willy Wonka: World of Wonka, an iconic game that has been a fan-favorite for decades within the land-based gambling sector. Now, thanks to the collaboration, Light & Wonder will be able to extend this popularity and deliver an online equivalent of the game that is expected to cater to new audiences.

Although Willy Wonka: World of Wonka will be the first such game, the collaboration will enable Light & Wonder to deliver more thrilling titles. Such will include the online counterpart of the land-based hit game, The Wizard of Oz: Road to Emerald City. Moreover, the new strategic deal will bring more branded games from Warner Bros, allowing Light & Wonder to tap into new audiences and bring thrilling experiences. Besides the aforementioned titles, the iGaming developer and aggregator confirmed that it plans further releases into 2024 and beyond.
