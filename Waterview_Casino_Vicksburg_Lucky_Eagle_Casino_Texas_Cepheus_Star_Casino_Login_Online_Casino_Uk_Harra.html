King Carrot review

Theme and Storyline

King Carrot has a fruit and vegetable theme.

Players find themselves playing in a sunny park filled with friendly grocery produce. Smiling pears, cheerful carrots, and sour-faced lemons all peer out from the reels, waiting to be discovered.

This slot may especially appeal to players who enjoy fruit machine themes and a healthy dose of cuteness.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is filled with a beat of percussion instruments and the tiny, unintelligible voices of the fruit and veggies. The high-pitched chatter of the little produce creatures adds a sweet touch of character.

The fruit and veggie symbols are animated in a whimsical cartoon style, each with a cute face. Players will see apples, strawberries, and ambitious carrots among the party.

When ominous shadows of flying birds pass over the reels, the fruit and veggies look adorably terrified of being gobbled up.

Behind the reels an idyllic park setting gives the game a picnic vibe. Every now and then a leaf drifts down from above, suggesting a calm breeze.

Overall, the soundtrack and animation create a welcoming world of colorful produce.

Gameplay

King Carrot is played on a 7x7 reel grid.

Payouts are awarded for landing at least 5 matching connected symbols anywhere on the reels.

The gameplay is engaging with a fast pace and plenty of action on screen.

Players can buy directly into the bonus feature if they wish (see feature details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all high and low paying symbols.

When players land a King Carrot symbol, a low-paying symbol is chosen at random to be replaced by Carrot symbols and a respin is triggered.

King Carrot symbol eats a randomly picked symbol and replaces it with Carrot symbols

If players land more than one King Carrot symbol, then more than one symbol will be replaced by the Carrot symbols.

During the replacement of symbols, the Epic King Carrot symbols have the chance to replace a Carrot symbol.

The Epic King Carrot symbol also adds a multiplier to a random Carrot symbol. The multipliers start at x2 and go as high as x100. This multiplier is applied to the winning combination of Carrot symbols its symbol is within.

Lucky players may get their winnings multiplied

When players land at least 3 Free Spins symbols (called FS symbols), the Free Spins feature begins, starting with 10 free spins. During the Free Spins feature, up to 8 symbols can be replaced by a King Carrot at random. One type of symbol is then replaced with the basic Carrot symbol for each King Carrot, potentially filling the entire reel with one winning combo.

The Bird Attack feature begins when players see the shadows of flying birds pass over the reels. A bird removes at least one reel and/or row of symbols, allowing fresh symbols to drop in to replace them.

During Bird Attacks, FS symbols and Wild symbols are left on the reels dropping with the other symbols.

King Carrot symbols and Epic King Carrot symbols can substitute for Carrot symbols when assessing winning combinations.

The maximum payout is x10,000 the player’s bet.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.3%, above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%. Buying directly into the bonus increases this RTP slightly to 96.41%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

Coming in with medium volatility, King Carrot is a less optimal fit for our preferred slot machine strategies. We recommend seeking the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, medium volatility fruit and veggie-themed slot.

Though its medium volatility is not ideal, King Carrot wins us over with its solid RTP and whimsically fun gameplay.

The adorable produce characters and action-packed bonus features elevate this game and make it well-worth checking out.
