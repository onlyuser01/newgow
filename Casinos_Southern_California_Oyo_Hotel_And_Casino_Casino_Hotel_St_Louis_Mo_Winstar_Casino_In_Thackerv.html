Sports Bars as Alternative Sportsbooks

Betcris and Buffalo Wild Wings (BWW) sports bar brand announced a new partnership in Mexico, in which both parties would join forces to bring sports betting to the restaurant chain. So far, the agreement allows Betcris to enter in 10 restaurants under the BWW brand, but there are provisions for more locations to be added in the future.

Betcris will launch the new offering with modules located at Mundo E and Paseo Interlomas in Mexico State, Acora Delta in Mexico City, Plaza Paseo Quintana and Juriquilla in Santiago De Querétaro, Midtown Jalisco in Guadalajara, Distrito 1 in Chihuahua, Centro Comercial Las Misiones in Ciudad Juárez, Plaza Gran Vía in Mexicali, and Baja California Norte and Plaza Colosio in Aguascalientes.

Restaurant guests will also have the opportunity to take advantage of the Betcris Arena loyalty program, the operator’s Affiliate Program, instant payment options and others. Besides the deposit and withdrawal options at customers’ disposal at all times, Betcris modules will allow bettors to collect immediately their winnings.
