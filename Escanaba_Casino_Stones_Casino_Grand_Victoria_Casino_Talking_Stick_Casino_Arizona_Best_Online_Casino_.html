Dim the Lights and Break the Curse of the Mummies

The game will reach Silver Bullet markets that belong to the Relax Gaming network. The list includes jurisdictions like Malta, the UK, Gibraltar, Denmark, Sweden, Germany, Romania, Netherlands, Latvia, Estonia, the Isle of Man, and Ontario.

Addressing the needs of players passionate about games with tomb raiders and ancient civilizations at the center, Blue Guru Games’ latest slot displays all the hallmarks of the gaming studio determined to revolutionize the industry with solid storytelling and fully-immersive visuals and audio.

The new slot features excellent graphics that create a spooky atmosphere, with the image of a detailed, dimly-lit tomb and a sinister evil-laugh soundtrack to accompany the frightening cinematics. The slot also incorporates a series of additional animations and it is built with the help of ten regular symbols. The list includes scorpions, snakes, mummies, jewelry, and crucifixes. The new slot also carries a wild symbol used to replace all regular symbols on the winning lines, and two non-payable scatter symbols. The Super Scatter symbol triggers a higher multiplier during the bonus round.

The score a win, players must land at least three identical symbols on consecutive reels starting with the leftmost position. The free spins bonus round can be triggered with the help of a multiplier. Players can also choose to buy free spins for a fixed price whenever they are in a hurry to enter the bonus round. The multipliers can be retriggered during the bonus round.
