The leading gaming tech company’s new Chief Financial Officer brings a wealth of experience and a strong track record in financial leadership

Karin den Dunnen succeeds previous CFO Jos van den Borne, taking over the position’s substantial responsibilities. Her significant leadership and financial expertise should be perfect for her new role as she helps propel Novomatic’s Netherlands division to new heights. The new CFO’s industry insights will be instrumental in maintaining the Novomatic brand’s enduring success and future growth.

Wide-Ranging Leadership Experience Will Prove Invaluable

Den Dunnen joins Novomatic Netherlands after operating in several diverse industries, holding CFO positions at high-profile companies like Zelfstroom, Findependent, and The Rent Company. Her leadership experience extends to the sustainable energy, secondary education, digital investment, and retail printing industries, fostering unparalleled flexibility and adaptability. This wealth of experience will be invaluable in her new position as she joins Novomatic’s leadership team.

As CFO, den Dunnen will oversee the finance team’s day-to-day operations, ensuring the company’s financial health and compliance with regulatory requirements. Additionally, she will play a key role in preparing her teams for internal audits, contributing to maintaining a transparent and accountable financial structure within the organization.

With Karin den Dunnen, we have gained a first-class financial expert with years of experience in the gaming industry. Peter Eder, Novomatic Netherlands Supervisory Board Member
