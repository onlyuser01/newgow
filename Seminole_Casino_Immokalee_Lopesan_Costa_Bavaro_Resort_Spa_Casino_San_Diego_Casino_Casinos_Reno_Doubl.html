Fortune Tiger review

Fortune Tiger transports players to a Chinese realm where tigers rule.

Surrounded by luxurious shades of gold and red, players will meet enchanted creatures including frogs, fish, and, of course, tigers. With the help of these animals, they might discover a golden coin or two.

Big win in the Fortune Tiger slot machine

Fans of Chinese cultural imagery will particularly enjoy this slot.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Traditional red lanterns adorn each side of the reels with a pot of golden coins balanced at the top. Sharp-eyed players will notice two golden tiger figurines above the reels as well.

Among the symbols, there are phoenixes, fish and frogs. Each animal can appear in gold, or in regular hues. The tiger is the most striking animal, animated in a realistic style in shades of green, blue, purple, and golden orange. The realistic style of the tiger ensures that this majestic predator is an intimidating presence.

Behind the reels, players will glimpse the silhouettes of trees and bridges. Glowing fireworks appear in the sky, adding to the festive feel of this slot.

The soundtrack of Fortune Tiger has a theatrical vibe with lingering string notes and a classical Chinese influence. The reels spin with a beep and clicking sound, and occasionally a gong for emphasis.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Fortune Tiger create a compelling world of animals and East Asian imagery.

Gameplay

Fortune Tiger is a five-reel, three-row slot.

There are 243 ways to win with wins awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels.

Gameplay video

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol has the special ability to award a displayed Jackpot. In cases when multiple Jackpots are on display, the highest is paid out.

The number of "golden" symbols can be set by the player, with each symbol having a regular version and a "golden" version with a higher value.

When 12 coins appear, the Jackpot game begins. Players must select coins until they unveil 3 matching Jackpots. This Jackpot is then awarded.

When 3 or more Scatter symbols land on the reels, the Free Spins round begins, starting with 10 free spins. During this feature, low-paying symbols do not land on the reels.
