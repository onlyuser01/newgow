The newest casino destination in Colorado, Chamonix Casino Hotel, confirmed that it started accepting hotel reservations. Last week, the future premium entertainment and hospitality venue confirmed that it started to accept hotel reservations ahead of its opening scheduled for December 26, 2023. Chamonix Casino Hotel is currently being constructed by Full House Resorts, a company that focuses on leasing, operating and developing gaming properties across the United States.

Once completed, Chamonix Casino Hotel is expected to deliver unrivaled entertainment and thrilling casino experiences. The venue is going to offer luxury guest rooms and VIP suites, a meeting space, along with different food and beverage outlets. Visitors to the Chamonix Casino Hotel can expect supreme casino entertainment thanks to its brand-new gaming area.

Baxter Lee, Chamonix Casino Hotel's General Manager, shared his excitement on the topic in a statement explaining that the venue is going to deliver unforgettable experiences to its visitors. "Cripple Creek is an area with a rich history that we wanted to honor with this new property," he added. Additionally, Lee predicted that the hotel and casino are going to benefit the residents of the town. "I'm excited to officially open our doors in December and show off all our hard work. Reserve your room today; this will be a special place you will want to visit," explained Lee in conclusion.

Overall, the venue is planned to offer approximately 300 luxurious guest rooms as well as VIP suites. Amenities that the visitors can enjoy include soaking tubs, fireplaces and balconies offering unique sights of The Rockies. Moreover, Chamonix Casino Hotel is going to offer a 5,100-square-foot ballroom, a high-end spa, a rooftop pool and a space for meetings or entertainment.

Further amenities include a restaurant operated by the famous chef with leading steakhouses across Las Vegas, Barry Dakake. Ultimately, Chamonix Casino Hotel is expected to offer an experience that fuses perfectly European elegance with the charm and comfort of Colorado.

The surrounding areas are perfect for visitors who are interested in exploring the wilderness. There's a wide range of options for mountain biking, rock climbing, hiking and fishing. Additionally, Cripple Creek visitors can explore historic railways and gold-mining operations, among other experiences available in the region.
