PSV Players Love Playing on Their Turf

PSV have lost just three competitive games on their turf in 2022. All those defeats happened against pretty tough opponents, namely, Leicester City (Europa Conference League), AZ Alkmaar (Dutch Eredivisie), and Ajax (Dutch Eredivisie).

In their most recent game, they defeated Monaco in the previous round of the Champions League qualifiers. It wasn’t an easy win, however. After 2-2 in the 90 minutes of the regular time, the game went to extra time where Luuk de Jong scored the winner for PSV.

This season, their ambitions are even higher. They want to do well in the Champions League. However, first they need to qualify for that competition.

To do that, they need to beat PSV in the Netherlands. The good news for them is that they usually play good soccer on the road, which isn’t surprising knowing that their faithful fans tend to follow them everywhere they go.
