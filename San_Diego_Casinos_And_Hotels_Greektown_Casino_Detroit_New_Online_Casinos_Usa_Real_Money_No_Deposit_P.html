This slot is hosted by a gleeful joker who has been crowned king. The theme is a fruit machine with a deck of cards twist.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. When it lands, another paying symbol is chosen at random to transform into a Wild as well.

Each Wild symbol turns other paying symbols into Wilds to maximize winning potential

Scatter symbols will have random multipliers (x2, x3, x5, x10, x10, or x25). This multiplier is applied to players’ total win after a spin. Multiple Scatter symbols will have their multiplier values added together.

If a player lands 3 or more scatters, they are awarded x1 their total bet.

Free Spin rounds are awarded when players land 3 or more Scatter symbols. The number of free spins received goes up depending on how many Scatters landed:

3 Scattes – 8 free spins

4 Scatters – 10 free spins

5 Scatters – 15 free spins

6 Scatters – 20 free spins

The maximum wins is limited to x5,000 the player’s bet. The round will conclude if this amount is reached.
