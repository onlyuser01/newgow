Manchester United Taking Europa League Very Seriously

Did you know that Man United has won the Europa League only once? Admittedly, it’s mostly because they played in the Champions League on a regular basis in the past. However, we did see them take part in this competition a couple of times in the post-Alex Ferguson era.

They want to win it this year as it would mean booking a ticket to next season’s Champions League. They also have a good chance of getting there by finishing in the top 4 in the Prem, but they surely want to keep all options open.

Even if that wasn’t the case, they’d still want to win the Europa League. After all, the Red Devils are the strongest team left in the competition. Although the road to the trophy is long, online bookmakers do think they’re going to pull it off. Man United is the top favorite to win the competition with the odds of +150. In comparison, Sevilla’s odds are +1200 at the moment.

Can Sevilla Shock the World on Thursday?

The short answer is – no. Sevilla just doesn’t look like a team that can do much this season. The main reason is that their key players just haven’t been great this season in all competitions. In the Spanish domestic league, they’re sitting in the 13th position with just 5 points more than the closest team from the relegation zone.

Another huge problem is that they’re horrible on the road. They did win their most recent game on the road, but before that, we saw them go 0-2-5 in away games. There is, however, one thing that’s going in their favor ahead of their trip to Manchester. Sevilla has a very good record against the Red Devils. The Spanish team is 2-1-0 against Man United in competitive games.
