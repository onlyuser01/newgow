The company continues its expansion across the US by agreeing to power a new retail sportsbook located at Santa Claran Casino Resort in New Mexico

The interactive gaming and sports betting technology company, Elys Game Technology, confirmed it expanded its presence in the United States via the launch of a new retail sportsbook. The company unveiled Wednesday that a new sportsbook located at Santa Claran Casino Resort in Española Valley, New Mexico, opened its doors to visitors.

Elys Game Technology confirmed that its subsidiary, Elys Gameboard Technologies, agreed to deliver the betting and bookmaking technology for the new Sports Book at Santa Claran. The new retail location complements Elys’ ongoing growth and expansion into regulated betting markets across the US. At the same time, the new sportsbook location represents one of the first branding initiatives for Elys America.

Visitors of the new sportsbook, located at 460 N Riverside Dr, in Española, can enjoy thrilling betting options for MLB, NBA, NFL, as well as college basketball. The retail outlet offers in-person ticket counters as well as self-service kiosks positioned around the Sportsbook Bar and Grill.

Santa Claran is a premium entertainment destination located in northern New Mexico. The family-friendly gaming resort offers a 27,000-square-foot gaming floor, as well as 122 guest rooms. Moreover, the resort features different entertainment options while at the same time offering spectacular views of the Sangre de Cristo Mountains. As a premium casino and entertainment destination in New Mexico, Santa Claran is positioned closely to the Black Mesa Golf Club, a premium location for fans of golf. In fact, the Club is recognized by Golf Magazine as one of the top 50 golf locations in America.

Earlier this year, Elys announced plans for another retail sportsbook located at The Ugly Mug restaurant and bar in Washington, DC. At the time of the announcement, that was the company’s eighth such location and now, judging by the latest news, Elys continues to expand the reach of its innovative technology, powering new sportsbooks.

The recent launch complements the growth of Elys in the US. Currently, the company delivers innovative omnichannel gaming services and products, powering the operations of online casinos, sports betting and virtual sports operators around the country. In addition to having a presence in five US states, the company has established its role as a leading provider for the regulated gambling market in Italy.
