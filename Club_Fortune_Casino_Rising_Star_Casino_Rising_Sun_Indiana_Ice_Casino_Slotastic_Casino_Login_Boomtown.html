The leading integrated resort planned to open in Incheon, South Korea, Mohegan INSPIRE, strengthened its C-suite with two strategic appointments. On Monday, the resort revealed that it promoted the seasoned executive, Chen Si, to the role of President of Mohegan INSPIRE. Further strengthening its senior-level team, Wade Howk was appointed to the role of Chief Operating Officer (COO). The two new C-suite appointments underline Mohegan INSPIRE's dedication to growth and expansion, while at the same time seeking to reaffirm its reputation as a leading integrated resort, a statement reveals.

In his new role as President, Si will leverage his extensive knowledgeand oversee strategic planning and operational results at Mohegan INSPIRE. His responsibilities will include business planning and budgeting. Additionally, Si will play a pivotal role in ensuring the completion of the resort's performance targets and efforts for improvement. He will also spearhead Mohegan INSPIRE's relationships with business partners and the government sector, serving as its Representative Director.

The promotion of Si as a President at Mohegan INSPIRE doesn't come as a surprise, considering his extensive experience within the gambling sector. Before joining Mohegan, he was a part of globally recognized and trusted companies within the sector, including Las Vegas Sands and McKinsey. Throughout his professional career, Si was able to accumulate significant experience and a wealth of knowledge, making him the perfect fit for the new role at Mohegan INSPIRE. What's more, he has a deep understanding and knowledge of international marketing and strategic development, making him the perfect figure to spearhead the company's growth.

On the other hand, Howk will be the second in command after Si and assist him in his new role. As COO, Howk will oversee operational processes, create reports and ensure smooth operations that are ultimately expected to deliver unique experiences for the guests. Leveraging his extensive experience, the new COO will help uphold the values and positive culture at Mohegan INSPIRE. He will contribute toward successful collaborations with stakeholders, government institutions and other important relationships for the resort.

Prior to joining Mohegan INSPIRE, Howk was a part of Hard Rock Casino Cincinnati where he served as Chief Financial Officer and interim President. His professional background includes a Corporate Vice President of Strategy role with SeaWorld Parks & Entertainment as well.

Howk commented on the topic by saying: "I am excited to bring these values to new heights in my new role, ensuring that the brand remains synonymous with the excellence and visionary leadership that our guests have come to know and expect at our properties across the globe."

On the other hand, Si was similarly excited and pointed out: "I am honored to be stepping into this role as President of Mohegan INSPIRE." The integrated resort's new President added: "As we begin this next venture for the Mohegan brand in South Korea, I am motivated by the promise of innovation, rich cultural exchange and strengthened partnerships."

Ray Pineault, Mohegan's President and Chief Executive Officer, shared the excitement of the duo and added: "With Mohegan INSPIRE, we are expanding our horizons beyond what we once thought possible." He predicted further growth and expansion for the company. Finally, Pineault praised the experience Howk and Si bring and predicted they will help Mohegan INSPIRE reach a new chapter of growth.
