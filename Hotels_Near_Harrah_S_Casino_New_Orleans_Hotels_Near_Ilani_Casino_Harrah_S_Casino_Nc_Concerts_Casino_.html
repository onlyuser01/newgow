The leading sports-tech entertainment company, FanDuel Group, announced the launch of two new features, strengthening significantly its basketball offerings. The company said Thursday that it introduced its new product innovation called “The Pulse” along with a new feature called NBA League Pass.

The new NBA League Pass feature builds upon FanDuel’s successful partnership with the National Basketball Association (NBA). Previously, the sportsbook was named the official sports betting partner of the Association. Ultimately, the new features seek to elevate the experiences for basketball fans and bettors alike, offering options for wagering on out-of-market games as well as betting on key moments in real-time.

Between October 19 and October 26, new users will be able to access three complimentary months of NBA League Pass as well as $200 in Bonus Bets as long as they register and place a $5 wager. NBA League Pass can also be accessed by existing customers of the sportsbook that wager $5 on the NBA. Basketball fans who are currently a part of the NBA’s global membership program, NBA ID, can tap into exciting unique benefits, FanDuel explained. The latest announcement designated FanDuel as the only US betting operator that offers NBA League Pass.
