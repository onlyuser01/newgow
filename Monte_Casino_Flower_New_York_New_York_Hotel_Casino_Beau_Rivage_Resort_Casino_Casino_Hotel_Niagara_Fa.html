Freemarket, a company that specializes in cross-border B2B payments, has added to its team of experts with two new hires

The appointments include those of Kiran Ranu, who will now serve as the company’s business development manager, and Martin White, who is stepping in as business development director.

Freemarket Strengthens Team of Experts

Both additions have significant experience working in the gaming and gambling industries. Their appointments come shortly after the selection of Annie Osborne, a former Xace chief executive, who joined the company as business development director last week.

White himself has more than 30 years of relevant work experience, with 23 years of working in the payments vertical. He will now move forward with helping Freemarket strengthen its positions in the gaming and gambling industry which are fast becoming important points of interest for Freemarket.

Ranu has worked with TrueLayer, Trustpilot, and AAT, and she has a lot to offer as well, bringing a diverse set of skills that has to do with coaching, leadership, recruitment, account management, and more.

Freemarket VP of sales Richard Harrison has welcomed the additions of Kiran and Martin and said that the duo added to the talent momentum the company has been enjoying recently, pointing out Osborne’s hiring. Harrison added:

Both bring fantastic energy to the business, as well as the drive and tenacity that is very much central to our company. Their experience and network bolster the gambling team and I am very excited to see the huge impact that they will undoubtedly have. Freemarket VP of sales Richard Harrison

Company Ready to Offer Gaming Sector What It Needs

Meanwhile, both White and Ranu welcomed their appointments. Ranu said that she was confident that she will be able to build the company’s presence in the gambling industry and take it to the next level.

As to White, he is similarly thrilled to be joining the company and bringing his know-how and expertise. He argued that the gambling industry needed cross-border payments and currency exchange solutions that Freemarket is now fully capable of bringing.
