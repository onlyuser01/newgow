The extension sees STATSCORE become an official Statistics Partner of the league through the 2023-2024 season

The leading company that supports organizations, fans and media outlets by delivering reliable live data, as well as in-depth statistics and analysis, STATSCORE, has announced it extended its agreement with the second tier of football in Poland, Fortuna 1 Liga. Under the terms of the extended agreement, Fortuna 1 Liga designated STATSCORE as its official Statistics Partner. Per the extended agreement, the provider of live data will remain in the aforementioned role through the end of the 2024 season.

Under the freshly extended deal, STATSCORE will continue to provide extensive and verified sports data from Fortuna 1 Liga’s games. Additionally, the company will deliver statistical insights and continue to support the pay-per-view (PPV) model for different matches.

The initial agreement between the duo dates back to 2020. Since then, representatives of STATSCORE, called scouts, have been collecting and delivering live match statistics and data. Such collected information would then undergo verification and deliver unique insights regarding different aspects of the game to football fans.

The Two Organizations Are Delighted to Continue to Collaborate

Dariusz Łęczyński, STATSCORE’s CEO, said that the company is delighted to be selected as the official Statistics Partner of Fortuna 1 Liga. He explained that the extended collaboration reaffirms the trust of the league in STATSCORE’s data.

Additionally, Łęczyński pointed out that STATSCORE is excited to have the opportunity to further improve the experiences for football fans by delivering valuable data and insights. “The collaboration with Fortuna 1 Liga reinforces our commitment to advancing the sport and ensuring fans have the best possible experience,” said STATSCORE’s CEO in conclusion.

“We are proud to be named the Fortuna 1 Liga’s Statistics Partner.“ Dariusz Łęczyński, CEO of STATSCORE

Marcin Janicki, the president of Pierwsza Liga Piłkarska, the association of clubs playing in Fortuna 1 Liga, shared the excitement of STATSCORE’s boss. He explained that in the last few years, the league developed a strong relationship with STATSCORE.

Moreover, Janicki said that the league is delighted to extend its collaboration with STATSCORE through the 2023-2024 season. According to him, the provision of data live data from the company is an important part of the league’s fan engagement strategy. Finally, Janicki spoke about the importance of STATSCORE’s technical support for the development of PPV solutions, catering to football fans.
