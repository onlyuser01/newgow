Sports Betting and MMA

Speaking about his new role, Ariel Helwani, who has previously worked with major sports outlets like ESPN and SB Nation, outlined the close link between sports betting and MMA, and that this relationship is more likely to deepen as more states legalize wagering on sports.

Helwani will serve as BetMGM brand ambassador while continuing to honor his obligations at The MMA Host for Vox media, as well as The Ringer MMA Show podcast, bringing its invaluable insight from the latest fights across MMA, boxing and pro wrestling.
