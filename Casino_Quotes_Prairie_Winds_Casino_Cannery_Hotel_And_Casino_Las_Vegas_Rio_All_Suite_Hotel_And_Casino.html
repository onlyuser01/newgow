RAW iGaming has reported that its content aggregation platform, RAW Arena, has already signed a trio of tier-one operators, which will allow the company to demonstrate its capabilities and provide partners with outstanding and more importantly - innovative content, the company said in a press release shared via email.

RAW Arena was created with the idea of only aggregating content from companies that are truly committed to building products that break the industry mold and cast brand-new gameplay features and scenarios. The partnership with PAF.comOlyBet.com and Twin.com marks an important step forward for the aggregation platform.

RAW Arena tries to buck the trend which the parent company says is an industry that is too saturated with similar content.
