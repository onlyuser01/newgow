The 7×7 game ushers players into a spectacular realm with free spins bonus rounds, tumbling wins, and special expanding symbols.

Free Spins, Special Expanding Symbol, and Tumble

Players ready to join the god of mischief Loki in a spectacular “realm of the trickster” and help him find the hidden treasures can start by focusing on landing at least five adjacent hammer, ring, or shield symbols, among other Norse-themed symbols.

The adjacent symbols must be connected either vertically or horizontally in order to create winning clusters.

Next, the respective symbols are taken off the grid which will in turn cause new icons to tumble down and land on the board for more potential clusters and consecutive prizes.

The low-paying symbols in the game are the blue, green, orange, and red gems. Players who land at least 25 identical symbol types in a cluster are paid between 50x and 150x.

The slot’s high-paying symbols are the green shield, Loki’s hammer, the golden red ring, and the golden Loki symbol. Landing 25+ of the same symbol type in a cluster pays between 300x and 10,000x.

At any moment while engaged in the base game, a paying symbol can be randomly chosen as a special expanding symbol. The sticky symbol will stay in the same position until all tumbles on the irrespective spin are completed.

This will then cause the expansion of the symbol into a 2×2 cluster of matched symbols for an even potentially higher win potential.

Players who land at least four scatter symbols (purple book with a question mark on it) in the base game receive 10 free spins.

At the beginning of a new free spins bonus, a paying symbol will be picked randomly and used as a special expanding symbol for the remainder of the round while being engaged on each new spin.

In case a minimum of four scatter symbols are seen landing on any free spin, the same bonus is triggered once again.
