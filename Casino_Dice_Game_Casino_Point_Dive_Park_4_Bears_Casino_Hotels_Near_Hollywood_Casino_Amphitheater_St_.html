Oddin.gg has secured a new partnership which will see the prominent supplier of esports odds launch with a new brand in the Balkan market

Aptly called Balkan Bet, the operator is going to benefit from Oddin.gg’s outstanding product solutions dedicated to the esports betting experience and market. Specifically, Balkan Bet will access Oddin’s iFrame solution.

Balkan Bet Taps into Oddin.gg’s iFrame Solution

Balkan Bet is one of the most prominent operators in the region, and it’s mostly focused on its home market in Serbia, offering more than 85 retail venues, and a dedicated online platform. The brand has been building up its proposition rapidly over the past years and will now be expanding to try and cater to esports fans.

Esports has become an indelible part of the betting experience and many operators are beginning to actively seek ways to engage with these communities in a meaningful and sustainable way. Oddin.gg co-founder and managing director Marek Suchar has welcomed the news and said:

We are excited and honored to provide Balkan Bet with our iFrame solution. We are sure this collaboration will expand their esports presence, bring more engagement and attract new bettors in their target markets. Oddin.gg’s Marek Suchar

Oddin.gg brings a dedicated 360-degree esports betting experience that is designed to elevate the vertical to new levels and engage with the target audience. Oddin is determined to drive profitability and volume around-the-clock for its partners, including Balkan Bet.

Players have access to numerous esports betting markets, including popular choices such as Dota 2, Valorant, League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and more.

Operators Committed to Grow in the Esports Vertical

Balkan Bet head of sportsbook Mirza Capra was excited to welcome Oddin.gg as the operator’s latest partner and elevate the bookmaker’s own esports betting potential through the iFrame Solution.

“This collaboration aligns perfectly with our commitment to delivering top-notch services to our clients and solidifying our position as the leading player in the competitive Balkan market,” Capra said. Balkan Bet head of sportsbook Mirza Capra

Oddin.gg has been actively looking to expand its footprint, quickly shaping itself up as one of the most trusted and reliable esports betting partners and providers of the necessary infrastructure and technology. The company recently teamed up with Olimpbet to spearhead the operator’s esports betting offer in Kazakhstan.

Meanwhile, Oddin.gg has also been building up its presence in the Balkans, with the company previously announcing a similar partnership with MaxBet.
