Sports betting monitoring company US Integrity has become the partner of one more casino in the state of Washington. After a partnership with Silver Reef Casino, US Integrity is now going to provide monitoring services to the upcoming sportsbook of 7 Cedars Casino, owned by the Jamestown S’Klallam Tribe.

US Integrity Will Ensure a Safe Sports Betting Environment at 7 Cedars

US Integrity has signed a new partnership with the Jamestown S’Klallam Tribe casino 7 Cedars located in Washington state as the casino plans to launch their own in-person sportsbook this autumn ahead of the NFL season.

7 Cedars will be joining 19 other tribal casinos out of the 29 in Washington state in launching sports betting as part of their product offering. All of these casinos already have received their licenses but not all of them have launched their sportsbooks.

Under the new partnership, US Integrity is to use its proprietary dashboard in order to offer monitoring services to the casino. The way this dashboard works is through analyzing a large amount of various data sets, which helps to discover any suspicious activities and irregular betting patterns.

US Integrity CEO Matthew Holt commented on the new collaboration: “We are looking forward to working with the exceptional team at 7 Cedars Casino. Their commitment to instilling the highest integrity standards align with U.S. Integrity’s initiative to ensure that sports wagering remains fair and safe.”

Bill Matsko, who is 7 Cedars Casino’s new Sports Wagering Manager, expressed his excitement regarding the comprehensive partnership with US Integrity. Matsko commented that the services of US Integrity will help 7 Cedars to detect sports betting irregularities at all times. US Integrity will allow the casino to identify and thwart any dubious betting activities right away, which will ensure a secure and enjoyable experience for all gamblers coming to the 7 Cedars Casino.

7 Cedars Casino’s New In-Person Sportsbook

The 7 Cedars Casino has repurposed part of its space in order to house the new Cedar Sports sportsbook. The redevelopment cost the Jamestown S’Klallam Tribe $800,000. An existing stage has been made into a sportsbook theater with big TV screens. The 7 Cedars property will also house self-service kiosk dedicated to sports betting, which will be scattered around the casino and hotel.

7 Cedars Casino has been operational since 1995. It is located in Washington state about two hours away from Seattle between the Olympic Mountains and the Strait of Juan de Fuca. Besides the casino, which sports various table games, bingo and 600 slot machines, the Jamestown S’Klallam Tribe property offers a wide range of facilities including a hotel with guestrooms and suites, numerous restaurants and its very own golf course.
