One of the leading sportsbooks and betting apps has been awarded with the opportunity to be the official provider of gambling options at the Arnold Sports Festival

The largest multi-sport fan festival in the United States will see Tipico as the exclusive partner for the event this year, held at the Great Columbus Convention Center, Ohio. Fans will have the opportunity to connect with athletes and fitness enthusiasts, and more importantly – get to know Tipico Sportsbook in the process, the company said in a press release.

Bringing Together Sports Fans and Athletes

Festivalgoers will also get to benefit from a $200 bonus that will be awarded in bet credit along with a $50 deposit and a chance to take part in the Arnold giveaway. The Arnold Expo is set to be held on March 2-5 and it will also make available Tipico’s on-site “Pull Up, Cash In” game. This particular challenge will be about endurance which will be measured by gripping and hanging from a pull-up bar, just like Arnold would.

Through this innovative new alliance, Tipico will expand its arc of hyperlocal partnerships that have steadily culminated statewide. Tipico Chief Commercial Officer David Paschkes

The upcoming event is the 35th year edition of the event and Tipico will have the opportunity to celebrate its recent launch in Ohio by bringing more meaningful and excellent betting options to local sports fans. The sportsbook will be presenting an award during the March 2-5 event.
