The move market-entry marks the end of the company’s transformative journey after the company had to miss the initial launch date in November and returns with renewed strengths today.

Moving forward, Kindred Group will be authorized to service the Dutch market and provide gambling products as per its license under the Remote Gambling Act. This marks an important milestone for the company as it has been able to ensure entry into its 20th local market, an important overall step for the company.

"The Netherlands is a large and important European market and one that we look forward to operating in with a local license," commented Kindred Group Chief Executive Officer Henrik Tjärnström.

Tjärnström continued by adding how important entering individual markets have become for the company. Kindred Group worked hard to make this launch a reality, the executive explained. He also added that moving forward, the company will be entirely focusing on providing a fair and sustainable experience.

Dutch regulators have been particularly demanding from local licensees, asking them to comply with a ream of regulatory measures and norms.
