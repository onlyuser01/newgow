Pariplay, a leading iGaming content provider and aggregator platform, has enriched its dedicated Fusion platform by adding new content by TopSpin Games. As a result, Pariplay players will have access to a brand new variety of games that have been produced in-house and developed with popular mechanics, player preferences, and timeless Indian-culture themes in mind.

TopSpin Adds Cultural Enrichment to Pariplay’s Aggregator

TopSpin Games has been created in a way that seeks to appeal to the broader iGaming community and create memorable experiences enhanced by high-quality graphics and a particular focus on diverse subtropical continent folklore as the games’ main theme across its numerous games. As a result, Fusion partners will be able to quickly access prominent titles by TopSpin Games and incorporate them into their own content portfolios.

The games provided by TopSpin include slots, bingo, and table games, and they are available in eight Indian languages which are spoken across 85% of the country. Some of the most prominent titles by the company include Jihandi Munda, 3 Patti, and others. TopSpin has focused on a mobile-first approach for all of its games, seeking to consolidate its standing with player bases on the go.

Pariplay director of partnerships Callum Harris has welcomed the exciting opportunity to extend the existing aggregation platform’s offering and said:

We’re always on the lookout to get interesting, unique content, and TopSpin Games certainly supplies that! With its artfully designed coterie of Indian games, their titles are a point of the real difference in the industry and we’re very happy to add them to our Fusion platform. Pariplay director of partnerships Callum Harris

TopSpin Games CEO Ankur Gupta said has also welcomed the opportunity between the companies. Gupta said that Pariplay’s qualities as a leading aggregation platform were well-known and the company made a fitting partner for TopSpin’s offer.

“Our games appeal to a diverse demographic, as we take inspiration from Indian stories to bring them to the iGaming world,” Gupta assured.
