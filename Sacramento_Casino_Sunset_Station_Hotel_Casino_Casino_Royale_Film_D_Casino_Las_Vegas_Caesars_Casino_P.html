Join Hercules on a Legendary Adventure

The 5×5 slot will see players accompany the famous mythological hero on his journeys. Players will join the demigod as he tackles his famous labors and slays deadly creatures. Symbols include well-known monsters such as Cerberus, the Nemean Lion, the Hydra and the Cretan Bull.

A Jackpot Spin may be triggered randomly before a normal spin and includes only blank symbols and DD symbols on the reels. If players land on 5DD symbols, they will trigger the Dream Drop Bonus, allowing them to grab the RAPID, MIDI, MAXI, MAJOR or MEGA jackpots.

In addition to that, players can activate the Beast Slayer Bonus by landing on three or more bonus symbols. The bonus round expands the size of the game, upgrades the symbols and provides players with lucrative multipliers.

The bonus game also launches a re-spin feature that starts with three spins. The number of respins decreases on every spin but resets every time a winning symbol lands.
