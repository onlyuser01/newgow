Ballin review

Theme and Style

Ballin takes players to a world of lavishly wealthy wild cats.

From fast boats to designer clothes, the felines of Ballin love to live large. As players join them for an epic party, they’ll learn the secrets of the rich and famous.

Players who dream of the high life will have fun joining this gang.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Ballin has a soundtrack that brings to mind a chilled out party. The reels spin with a swish that adds excitement, along with a voice over that occasionally quips "Ballin!"

This slot is set in a shimmering city animated in shades of orange and gold that suggest a sunset. The reels are built into a multi-story house, and sports cars speed by on either side. Players will be able to easily imagine they are heading to a party in Miami or LA.

Together, the soundtrack and animation create a whimsical fantasy of wealth and luxury, with a catty twist.

Gameplay

Ballin has a 3-4-4-4-3 reel structure with 20 win lines.

The maximum win for Ballin is 50,000x the player’s total bet.

Players may buy directly into the Bonus if they wish (see further details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Wild symbols have associated multipliers of 2x or 3x and appear on reels 2-4. They substitute for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

Super Star Wild symbols appear on reels 2-4 and reveal Wild Multipliers in their positions as well as upwards on the reel.

When players land 2 Bonus symbols on reels 1 and 3 plus s Big Balls or Super Star symbol on reel five, the Bonus is triggered. The bonus that is triggered is determined by which symbol lands on reel 5:

Big Balls symbol trigger Big Balls Free Spins, in which payers are awarded 10 free spins and any Wild Multipliers that land on reels 2-4 are Sticky for the duration of the feature. If players activate a full reel of Wilds, 4 additional free spins are awarded. Super Star symbol triggers Super Star Free Spins in which players are awarded 6 free spins and any Wild Multipliers that land on reels 2-4 are Sticky for the duration of the feature. Super Star Wild symbols are active and any that land will reveal Wild Multipliers in their positions and upwards in the reel. If players activate a full reel of Wilds, 2 additional free spins are awarded.
