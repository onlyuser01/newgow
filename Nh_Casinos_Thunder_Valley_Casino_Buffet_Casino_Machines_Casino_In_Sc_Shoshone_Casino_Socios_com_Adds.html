Every club that joins Socios.com generates a supply of Fan Tokens, fungible or tradable assets in laymen’s terms. These tokens grant fans rights to participate in club matters, whether it is choosing participating players, strategies and more.

Each club and organization that signs up for Socios.com may only have a limited supply of tokens that is available for distribution, making for an exclusive experience.
