This tie-up is a second partnership for the company in the Italian market, as it seeks to expand its regional reach and pull with prominent local players.

Flows Makes Another Move in Italian Gaming Market

Nazionale Elettronica will benefit from this new collaboration by “supercharging” its CRM. The slot provider will also be able to boost automation and personalization.

Flows is a particularly successful and innovative company at what it does.
