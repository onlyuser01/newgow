Wheel of Fortune Slots theme place will be established on a 2,900 square feet space in the core of the gaming zone of Plaza Hotel and Casino in Downtown Las Vegas. Plaza Hotel teams up with International Game Technology to create this incredible gaming experience for Las Vegas players.

Las Vegas Will Have Its Very Own Wheel of Fortune Zone

The entire 2,900 sq. ft. place, located at the heart of the Las Vegas’ Plaza Hotel and Casino, will be dedicated to the Wheel of Fortune game with its new décor and slots. Casey Adras, the Plaza Hotel and Casino director of slot operations, stated that when guests go to the Slots Zone they will be immersed into the world of the legendary Wheel of Fortune by IGT.

There will be 40 Wheel of Fortune slot games with classic and new themes. The game formats will vary in mechanical reel and video reel. The themed zone will include all of the Wheel of Fortune slots that have given legendary jackpots and are considered very high in demand.

Wheel of Fortune by IGT Gave $3bn in Jackpots in 25 Years

Wheel of fortune is referred to as “the theme of the most successful slot of all time”. IGT launched their Wheel of Fortune games 25 years ago and since then the slot machines have paid out in jackpots a total of $3bn. The company has made over 250 variations of the games which have become a legend in the industry. Las Vegas, which is the world’s most emblematic gaming place in the world, is the best home for the Wheel of Fortune life-changing slots. It is truly exciting to hear that the Plaza Hotel and Casino will have a place dedicated to the Wheel of Fortune.

The Zone for the legendary slots at the Plaza Casino will be exclusive for Las Vegas and it promises to bring a life-changing experience for players as it will quickly immerse them into the world of the Wheel of Fortune with new IGT technology and give them the chance to win the famous fortune jackpots.

IGT has recently spearheaded numerous initiatives tying into the iGaming space. The company garnered regulatory approval to launch a cashless technology in Nevada and IGT further diversified the payment experience by adding a Bitcoin option for slot payments.
