Piggy Factor Respins, Free Spins, and Free Games

Players who are eager to try Payday Pig are invited to find their way to the farm and start exploring a fun adventure by joining their pink piggy friends in their search for big wins.

The slot counts nine active paylines and six different symbols, with three low-paying symbols (single, double, and triple BAR symbols) and three high-paying symbols (bags of gold, leprechaun hats, and pigs).

The grid is also packed with Wilds, Piggy Bonus symbols, and Piggy Factor symbols. The Wild symbols replace the rest of the symbols and help create winning combinations. Piggy Bonus symbols and Piggy Factor symbols trigger the exciting bonus features.

Players need to land at least one X symbol anywhere on the reels to be granted a Piggy Factor Respin. When this happens, the second and third reels will start to spin again with digits ranging from 0 to 9 to generate Multipliers leading to instant wins.

Three Scatter symbols landing on the reels will trigger 10 Free Spins while adding one more reel for even higher wins while the Piggy Factor Respin feature is active. During the Respin round, the second, third, and fourth reels will be filled with new digits.

At the end of each Free Game, the values on each line with an X will be added together, multiplying players’ stakes even further.
