The Resort Ranked among the Best in Multiple Categories

Organized by the country’s Tourism Board, The Singapore Tourism Awards distinguishes the leading individuals and organizations with the best practices in the sector. The ceremony presents up to 30 awards in several categories, representing all facets of Singapore’s diverse tourism and hospitality industry. Winning businesses exemplify the country’s drive to remain among the most popular destinations in the region.

Resorts World Sentosa (RWS) ranked among the top four in Outstanding Achievement in Sustainability and Outstanding Event Venue Experience. The luxury integrated resort also won the Outstanding Employer distinction, owing to its continued commitment to the well-being of its employees. These achievements place Resorts World among the leading venues in the country and an example for the wider industry.

The prestigious award directly results from RWS’ ongoing mission to upskill and re-skill its employees, preparing for Singapore’s ongoing post-pandemic recovery and rebounding tourism sector. In 2022, RWS employees undertook approximately 300,000 hours of training, working closely with industry partners to offer workers valuable job-redesign initiatives and career conversion programs.
