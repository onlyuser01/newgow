Leadstar Media Is Now Licensed in Virginia

Besides Virginia, the company is licensed to offer its services legally in other key markets such as Colorado, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey, to name a few. Moreover, this year Leadstar sought to increase its presence in the US by developing new and existing products for the market. The company had backed up its US expansion plan by creating a strong team as well.

“Leadstar Media is set to acquire licenses in further states that are already regulated.” Leadstar Media
