The Game Brings Respins and Free Spins

Players of Going Wild in Vegas WildFight can expect to see multiple characters on the reels. The game displays the reel at the center of the screen while a vibrant Las Vegas theme serves as a background. The new slot game offers colorful graphics while engaging music can be heard in the background.

The action-packed gameplay of the new slot game brings Free Spins and Respins mode. To engage in Respins mode, players need to land eight or more diamonds on the reel, triggering three spins. Landing diamonds leaves a “border on the reels” which may lead to a boost in the payout, Yggdrasil reveals.

On the other hand, landing three Scatter symbols will enable the players to access Free Spins mode. Once this happens, all low-paying symbols are removed, which may boost the WildFight mechanic further and result in mouth-watering payouts.
