This is the latest move by an esports-focused operator who is moving into other verticals.

Building Past the Esports Experience

Aspire CEO Tsachi Maimon spoke highly of the esports segment:

“Esports is one of the most exciting segments in igaming and we are determined to lead the development in this important area. We look forward to working with WIN Group to create the world’s premier gaming experience in esports.” Aspire Global CEO Tsachi Maimon

Aspire has welcomed WIN Group’s approach which is multi-pronged and focuses on both delivering esports media coverage as well as building a dedicated esports betting platform.
