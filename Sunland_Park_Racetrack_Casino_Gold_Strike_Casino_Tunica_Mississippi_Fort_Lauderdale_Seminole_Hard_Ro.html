Spinomenal, a leading provider of iGaming content, has launched its full portfolio of products on the UK market through a new partnership

Thanks to a new tie-up with PokerStars, the company is bringing a strong variety of products that will be available to players in the market and help drive engagement for the Flutter Group-owned brand’s online casino segment.

Spinomenal Secures Important Partner in Face of PokerStars

Among the games available to players right now are Demi Gods II, Wolf Fang, Book of Rampage, and Majestic King to name a few. These games are being integrated with the help of Light & Wonder’s OpenGaming platform, making for quick onboarding and launch, and further maintenance of the products simple.

Spinomenal’s products are a strong iGaming product that has long appealed to international players. PokerStars and Flutter appreciate the unique approach the company has developed through the introduction of the “shared universe concept” which enables the supplier to bring characters from its most popular games together.

Meanwhile, PokerStars and Spinomenal are not stopping at the United Kingdom only. They will move on to other markets seeking to expand in jurisdictions covered by a license from the Malta Gaming Authority. Commenting on this opportunity, Spinomenal CEO Lior Shvartz said:

Our first UK operator launch is a major milestone for Spinomenal and what a way to begin by signing with PokerStars. Getting to this stage has been a massive team effort from both PokerStars and Light & Wonder, now we’ll see the positive impact our games will bring to a new audience. Spinomenal CEO Lior Shvartz

Spinomenal’s partnership with PokerStars in the United Kingdom follows four months after the company acquired a license from the UK Gambling Commission in August 2022. The event was hailed as a major milestone by the supplier at the time.

The company also started the year strongly by obtaining a license from the Hellenic Gaming Commission to become a certified supplier in Greece just last week.
