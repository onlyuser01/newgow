Pariplay Boosts Its Footing In the United States with the GAN Agreement

After negotiating a deal to supply its proprietary games portfolio to platform provider GAN, Pariplay Ltd, the leading aggregator and content provider of iGaming products, has dramatically increased its presence in the United States.

Pariplay Boosts GAN iGaming Portfolio in US

Pariplay has a large game library of more than 120 high-quality games. The content includes a combination of classic and new slots with a variety of themes, features, and volatility to cater to a wide range of players.

Stallion Fortunes, Mystery Fox, and Dragons of the North are among the best-selling games.

With GAN’s plug-and-play aggregator platform open to operators in three states – New Jersey, Pennsylvania, and Michigan – Pariplay will broaden its presence in the fast-growing US market.

GAN currently provides online solutions to about 20 US Real Money and Social Casinos, including Penn Interactive, Borgata, Seneca, and Twin River Casino. GAN also makes its platform available to Italian operators.

Pariplay, which launched in New Jersey with Rush Street in February this year, sees a lot of promise in the growing US online gaming industry.

Chief commercial officer at Pariplay, Christine Lewis, said: “Establishing a presence and building our brand in the US market is a priority for us this year. The US is growing at an impressive rate with more states currently considering legalizing online gaming, and our objective is to be at the forefront of the developments.”

Lewis continued by saying that Pariplay will get their games in front of a significant audience across multiple states by working with GAN, a well-established supplier in the market with prominent operator partners, and the company is looking forward to growing their business in the US together.

Pariplay’s Continues to Grow Business Through Partnerships

GAN’s current offering will be greatly enhanced by the inclusion of Pariplay’s games, which will provide their partners with premium titles that the company is confident slot fans will enjoy.
