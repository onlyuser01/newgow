The membership marks a milestone moment for the brand as it was one of the first to launch its services within the regulated market in Portugal. In addition to joining forces with the Association, the brand may collaborate with other companies operating within the region that include Casino Portugal, Bwin, Betano and Betclic, among others.

João Saraiva, PokerStars’ planning and strategy manager for Portugal, shared his excitement about the freshly minted collaboration with APAJO. Finally, Saraiva said: “We have high ambitions for this market, and we can’t wait to start working together with APAJO.”

“We are delighted to join APAJO and look forward to sharing our expertise from other licensed jurisdictions to support APAJO in promoting a responsible and safe play environment.“ João Saraiva, planning and strategy manager for Portugal at PokerStars
