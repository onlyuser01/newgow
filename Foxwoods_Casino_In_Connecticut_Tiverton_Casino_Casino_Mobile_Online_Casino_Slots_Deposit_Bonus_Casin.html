Galaxy Gaming Inc. is focusing on expanding in the iGaming vertical through its newly-launched arm, Galaxy Gaming Digital. Taking on the iGaming Market with New Division

Commenting on this partnership, Galaxy Gaming CEO and president Todd Cravens commented on this branching into the online gaming space, after previously focusing exclusively on the land-based sector. Cravens assured that the same appreciation for detail would be applied to the iGaming solutions similar to the land-based developments:

The new division will significantly increase Galaxy Gaming’s capabilities to expand into the online space and deliver excellent titles adapted for the digital age.

Galaxy already has a track record for developing both online and land-based table games, but the arrival of the dedicated division is a more determined step in embracing iGaming.
