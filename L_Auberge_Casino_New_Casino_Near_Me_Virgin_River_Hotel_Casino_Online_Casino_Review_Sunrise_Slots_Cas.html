Evoplay’s Passion for Soccer Themes

Instead of taking action to one of the glamorous stadiums of the region, however, Evoplay has picked the streets of Salvador as the backdrop for its latest game. Players will get to play a national team of their choice and try to score against a goalkeeper on a concrete pitch.
