How Can Sports Fans Participate in the Bracket Challenge?

Sports fans that are interested in participating in the new promotion need to be 21 or older and verify their identity for the operator. Players from the US must register or use an already registered account with Hillside LLC (New Jersey) to participate in the challenge.

It is important to mention that bet365 allows only one entry per eligible person. The operator explained that it will monitor the use of multiple accounts by the same individual or group. In case bet365 finds reasonable grounds to suspect multiple entries by the same player or group those entries will be voided.

Many Prizes Are up for Grabs

To win the $5,000,000 prize, a player would need to guess the perfect bracket. In other words, they would need to correctly predict the outcome of all 63 games. However, bet365 features prizes even if no one guesses the perfect bracket.

By participating and guessing correct predictions, each player receives points. The player that has accumulated the most correct predictions that brought him the highest number of points will win $100,000. Moreover, the player that takes the second position will collect $25,000, while the player with the third position will go home with $10,000.

The prize for the player that takes the fourth position based on the earned points will collect $5,000, while the prize for the fifth position is $1,000. Overall, there will be additional prizes for players up to the 250th position.
