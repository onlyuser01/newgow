Sportradar, a company that offers leading sports technology, announced the release of an Athlete Wellbeing educational video. The new video reaffirms the company's commitment to the integrity of sports and acknowledges that the well-being of professional athletes is the main priority in this process.

The new educational video that seeks to help raise awareness of the impact of sports betting. The video is a part of the company's Athlete Wellbeing program that seeks to provide support and resources for its partners in terms of education on betting harm amid the growing popularity of sports betting.

The free video is available for the company's Integrity Services league as well as governing body partners and reveals how legal sports betting can impact professional athletes' mental health. What's more, the video reaffirms "Sportradar's commitment to providing solutions and supporting stakeholders within the growing sports betting ecosystem," the company explained in a statement.

The company acknowledged that sports betting can be a form of entertainment for many people. However, Sportradar said that the activity can have a significant impact on the mental health and well-being of professional athletes. This is one of the prime reasons why the company offers an education that includes data about the potential impact of sports betting on athletes.

Sportradar's education also helps sports organizations identify unhealthy behaviors and reveals why it is important for athletes to receive help. Additionally, the company provides education on how to better protect athletes from the impact of sports betting.

The recently released educational video includes mental health and responsible gambling experts that are partners of the company's Athlete Wellbeing program. Additionally, the video features the professional basketball player Garrett Whitlock and Lindsay Tarpley Snow, a former professional soccer player and a two-time gold medalist. What's more, the video includes Connor McCaffery, currently a college basketball player. Each of the participants in the new educational video shares their story about the impact of wagering.

Besides the free educational video, Sportradar revealed that it offers additional services and products as a part of its Athlete Wellbeing program. Those include workshops, training via in-depth digital platforms as well as consultancy services.

Seeking to ensure the success of its program, Sportradar leverages engaging training elements and backs its educational materials with case studies. Ultimately, those services and solutions seek to help the athletes and ensure their well-being.
