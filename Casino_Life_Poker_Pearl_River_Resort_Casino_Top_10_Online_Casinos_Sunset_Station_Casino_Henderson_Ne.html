PandaScore, a French provider of esports data and odds, has penned a new partnership agreement with the global betting operator Betfred. Per the deal between the two companies, the latter will bolster its esports betting product by integrating the supplier’s full esports betting portfolio.

PandaScore’s product offering currently includes 308 unique esports betting markets and more than 32,000 competitive events a year. The supplier, furthermore, boasts an average year-on-year turnover increase of 33% for operators.

PandaScore is fully committed to growing esports and the esports betting vertical while driving innovation in the sector. The company is backed by an experienced and professional trading team and is further powered by extensive player props markets, customer engagement widgets and an esports-oriented BetBuilder solution.

The provider’s BetBuilder product is the richest of its kind in the entire esports betting industry, the company claims.

The partnership between PandaScore and Betfred is set to launch later in 2023. It will help Betfred expands its esports betting portfolio and attract more esports punters in the United Kingdom. Betfred is a leading operator that has a strong presence in the United Kingdom’s retail and online sectors.
