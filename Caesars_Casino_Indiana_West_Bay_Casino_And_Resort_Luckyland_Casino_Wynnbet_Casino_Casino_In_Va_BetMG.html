BetMGM Launches a New Sports Betting Venue

BetMGM expects the strategic location to play a huge role in the success of the retail sportsbook as it will provide local punters with an easily-accessible place to sit down, watch games, and place bets.

The 4,000 square feet state-of-the-art venue will be able to accommodate 187 people at once. It is equipped with 40 big high-quality TV screens where fans of sports betting can closely follow the latest sports developments, results, and odds. There will be a total of six cashier desks in order to avoid the forming of long queues and allow everyone to place their bets on time.

Furthermore, BetMGM’s new venue will provide visitors with a plethora of drinks and food so that they can enjoy themselves while following their favorite sports events on one of the screens.
