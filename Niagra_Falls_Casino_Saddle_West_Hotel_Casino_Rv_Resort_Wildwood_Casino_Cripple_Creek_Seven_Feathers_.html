The casino company NetBet Italy has inked a partnership deal with Spribe, a provider of engaging and innovative next-gen iGaming titles. The agreement will see NetBet’s portfolio reinforced with a selection of the best games Spribe has to offer.

NetBet Italy Will Add Spribe’s Exceptional Titles

As per the deal, Spribe will roll out its top titles on NetBet Italy’s platform, allowing the operator to better engage its audience. The critically-acclaimed supplier will power NetBet’s offerings with games such as Aviator – a title that took the industry by storm a year ago.

Aviator is an online multiplayer gambling game where players have to bet strategically and cash out before a plane flies away. The inspiration behind this title is the cryptocurrency industry where investors should carefully follow the tendencies and cash out before a price drop.

Aviator is marketed toward Millennials and is tailored to suit their tastes. Because of this, it is a mobile-first title with simple UI, social elements, multiplayer, and fast game sessions. Aviator attests to Spribe’s commitment to disrupting the market and its willingness to appeal to the various betting demographics.
