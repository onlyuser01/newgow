FashionTV Highlife Will Carry Players to the Core of the FashionTV Universe

As a licensor for FashionTV’s megabrand of iGaming products, FashionTV Gaming Group features dedicated content aimed at fashion, trends, and an overall luxurious lifestyle, all important elements that characterize the TV channel that broadcasts under the same name.

FashionTV Gaming Group’s chief operating officer considered the collaboration with EveryMatrix and Spearhead “an absolute pleasure”. The end result is an innovative game slot ready the join the much-anticipated line of luxury games that includes FashionTV Jackpot Baccarat, the group’s first release of the year. Spearhead Studios, Delighted to Work Together With FashionTV
