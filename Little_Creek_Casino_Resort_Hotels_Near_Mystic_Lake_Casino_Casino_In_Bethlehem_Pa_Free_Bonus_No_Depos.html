Wild Yield review

Theme and Style

Wild Yield is themed around a wholesome farm.

Players find themselves out in a field of fresh produce with a sleepy scarecrow for company. As the clouds drift through the sky, players will need to harvest as many fruits and vegetables as they can.

Players who enjoy farming and fruit machine themes may also appreciate Wild Yield.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The animation of Wild Yield has a cartoon aesthetic with plenty of bright, cheerful hues. The reels are made from rustic sticks tied together with rope, accented with some flowering vines. In the background, players will see rolling green hills and a windmill turning slowly in the breeze.

Among the symbols players will find colorful letters, along with a range of personified veggies. From earnest tomatoes to smug pumpkins to nervous lettuce, each little veggie is a unique character.

Beside the reels, a lanky scarecrow alternates sleep and waking. With his tattered straw hat and baggy overalls, he looks as if he has had a long day at his post out in the field.

The drawl of a country melody reinforces Wild Yield’s farming theme. Meanwhile, gentle bird calls and rooster crows help to create an outdoorsy atmosphere.

Gameplay

Wild Yield is a five-reel, five-row slot with 259 ways to win.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel.

The maximum win for Wild Yield is 50,000x the player’s total bet.

If players wish, they can buy directly into the Free Spins Bonus (see further details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols when calculating wins.

Weather symbols have the special ability to change a different number of symbols into Wild symbols based on different patterns. The location of symbols transformed into Wilds depends on the type of Weather symbol ranging from Tornado to Rain to Lightning to Earthquake to Frost to Fire. Fire symbols will only appear during the Free Spins feature.

When players collect 3 Bonus symbols, the Free Spins feature begins, starting with 6 free spins. Every additional Bonus symbol awards +1 additional free spins.

Players will also have the option to purchase an Extra Free Spin at the end of the Free Spins feature in the event that fewer than 10 Weather symbols have appeared.
