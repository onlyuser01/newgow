Sharp App, the leading app that enables bettors to make smarter bets, won the top prize on 2 Minute Drill, David Meltzer's famous "pitch only" competition show. Jason Mezrahi, Sharp App's co-founder and COO participated in the famous show and pitched the app to a panel of notable celebrity entrepreneurs.

Participants in the show are given two minutes to deliver the most convincing pitch in front of the leading entrepreneurs. After participating in the famous show, Mezrahi won the top prize granting $50,000 in cash and prizes. The prestigious achievement was completed during Episode 2 of Season 4 of 2 Minute Drill.

Meltzer, the famous investor, best-selling author and legendary entrepreneur hosted the show along with a team of celebrity entrepreneurs that included Devin Johnson, Kennected's CEO, Joe Camberato, the CEO and founder of National Business Capital, as well as Shawne Merriman, the former NFL star and CEO of Lights Out Brand.

Key functionalities that made Sharp App stand out are the app's AI-powered tools, as well as educational programming and analytics. Thanks to those unique functions, the app delivers unrivaled experiences for sports bettors and empowers them to make better decisions when wagering.

It was back in October when Sharp App was announced as a part of the HPL Digital Sport and Cardinal Sports Capital Accelerator Program. With that in mind, Sharp App has seen a significant month-over-month premium subscriber retention rate of 80% since 2021.

At the same time, Sharp App continued to scale up its content offering trends, betting information and news via its Game Center. What's more, Sharp App's Sharp Academy offered a multimedia masterclass led by the sports betting industry expert John Alessia. The innovative masterclass offers to teach bettors different aspects of wagering and provides invaluable insight.

Upon winning the award Mezrahi shared his excitement in a press release received by CasinoGuru. He revealed that it is incredibly exciting to be recognized by a panel of such notable entrepreneurs.

Moreover, Mezrahi said: "While our tech driven tools and services are second-to-none in the sports betting market, what really separated Sharp App from the other contestants was our commitment to sustainable profitability through our lean operation and meticulous approach to user acquisition and retention." Finally, he explained that Sharp App will use the funds to help deliver further innovations and continue to excite sports fans and bettors.
