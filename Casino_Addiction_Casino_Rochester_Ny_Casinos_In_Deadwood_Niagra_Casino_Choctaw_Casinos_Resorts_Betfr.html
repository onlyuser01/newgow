The leading bookmaker Betfred continues its US-facing expansion by announcing a new deal in Iowa. The company said Wednesday that it inked a new sponsorship deal with Iowa Wild, the famous American Hockey League (AHL) team.

Iowa Wild and Betfred Ink Multi-Year Deal

Thanks to the new multi-year sponsorship, Betfred will benefit from various marketing opportunities such as a digital and print presence. Moreover, the brand will benefit from TV and radio exposure as well as game-day activations at Wells Fargo Arena.

The new partnership will bring unique experiences for Iowa Wild fans as well. According to Betfred, the marketing collaboration will bring exciting giveaways that include suite sweepstakes, as well as exclusive merchandise and ticket giveaways, starting with the 2022-2023 season.

“We are excited to announce Betfred as our exclusive partner in the Sportsbook category. We look forward to the tremendous addition Betfred will bring to our Iowa Wild fan experience.” Brent Arnold, senior director of sales for the Iowa Wild

Brent Arnold, the Iowa Wild’s senior director of sales, acknowledged that the team is excited to join forces with the leading betting operator Betfred. He explained that the Iowa Wild is looking forward to the unique experiences the new collaboration will bring to the team’s fans.
