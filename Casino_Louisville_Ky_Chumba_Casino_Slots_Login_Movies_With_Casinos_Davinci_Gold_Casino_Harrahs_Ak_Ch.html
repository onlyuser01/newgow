International online gambling operator Casumo will reinforce its Spain-facing business with the addition of content from MGA Games, a major supplier of games. Under the deal, the latter company will provide its new client with multiple new titles.

As agreed, MGA Games will inject 18 new games into Casumo’s product offering in Spain. These include many fan-favorite titles that will help the operator engage its customers. The full list of games that will become available to Spanish players contains the following hits:

The first of the aforementioned titles is one of MGA Games’ latest releases which hit the market this May.

All of MGA Games’ titles are hailed for their high quality, innovative mechanics and engaging features. The provider is trusted by many gambling companies thanks to its constant quality and commitment to great gaming experiences. MGA Games’ titles are also known as a great driver of profitability for gambling operators, making the integration very important to Casumo’s Spanish business.

The deal will see more games integrated into Casumo’s Spanish platforms at a later date.

Representatives commented on the matter with MGA Games’ sales director, Javier Lanfranchi, saying that the deal will benefit both companies. He noted that the partnership will allow his team to solidify its position as a leading provider of content in Spain.

In addition, the partnership will allow MGA Games to take advantage of its synergies with Casumo and move closer to its international goals, Lanfranchi said.

Mark Busuttil, Casumo’s chief product officer, also commented on the matter. He noted that his company acknowledges the importance of this partnership to Casumo’s growth in Spain.

At Casumo, we are thrilled to announce our collaboration with MGA Games. This alliance demonstrates our commitment to providing an exceptional gaming experience for our players in Spain. Mark Busuttil, CPO, Casumo

Busuttil reaffirmed his team’s commitment to innovation in iGaming. He believes that the alliance with MGA Games will allow his team to bring a more diverse and entertaining experience to its players.
