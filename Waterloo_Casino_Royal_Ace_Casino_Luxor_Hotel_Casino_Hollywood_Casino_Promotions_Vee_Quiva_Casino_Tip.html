The sports betting operator will celebrate the agreement with a new promotion for customers in Colorado, Iowa, New Jersey and Ohio

Tipico Sportsbook, Tipico’s sports betting division, has penned a deal with Front Row Motorsports, agreeing to become the sponsor of the No. 38 Ford F-150 in the NASCAR CRAFTSMAN Truck Series race.

As a result of the agreement, Tipico will launch a special promotion for new customers in Colorado, Iowa, New Jersey and Ohio. The promotion is called Ride with Zane and will allow customers who deposit at least $38 to have their names featured on Zane Smith’s truck at the Mid-Ohio Sports Car Course. The offer is limited to the first 300 sign-ups.

In addition to that, newly-registered Tipico users who make the deposit will earn $150 in Bet Credits. To top it off, the five new users who make the largest deposits will get free VIP passes to the Mid-Ohio NCTS race or another 2023 Truck Series race of their choosing.

The NASCAR CRAFTSMAN Truck Series race is scheduled for July 8.

Tipico Remains Committed to Delivering Top Experiences to Fans

David Paschkes, the chief commercial officer of Tipico NA, commented on the new sponsorship agreement. He noted that the operator is happy to join forces with the Front Row Motorsports racing team.

Tipico is thrilled to be partnering with the Front Row Motorsports racing team, a group that has set a remarkable pace in the NASCAR circuit with their impressive strides and monumental wins achieved in under two decades of racing. David Paschkes, CCO, Tipico NA

Paschkes said that Tipico recognizes the importance of such teams in growing the popularity of the sport as a whole. He added that Tipico would like to expand its presence across the NASCAR community, especially in Colorado, Iowa, New Jersey and Ohio.

The CCO noted that Tipico remains committed to providing best-in-class betting experiences to all NASCAR aficionados.

Other Recent Tipico News

The new deal is the latest in a string of deals the sports betting operator has been securing. Last month, Tipico unveiled a new partnership with the Dayton Dragons, the high-A Minor League Baseball affiliate team of the Cincinnati Reds.

Under that deal, Tipico agreed to become the exclusive fireworks series sponsor of the MiLB team for the season. Tipico also agreed to sponsor a section of Day Air Ballpark that was to be named after the operator.

The Ride with Zane promotion isn’t Tipico’s first deal for the year either as in March, the operator launched a special promotion for March Madness promotion, promising to award the winner with a year of free weekly $100 bets.
