In her new senior-level role, Baumgardt brings extensive experience within the iGaming vertical. As VP of Sales and Customer Development EMEA, she will oversee the development and growth of the aforementioned teams. Additionally, Baumgardt will play a key role in the future expansion of Future Anthem and help the company reach its next phase of growth. The experienced executive will also closely collaborate with Matt Nichols, Further Anthem's General Manager of the Americas, for the global growth of the company.

Future Anthem's CEO, Leigh Nissim, shared his excitement about the latest hire in a statement. "Ann shares our vision and is excited to contribute to our collective success," he said. Finally, Nissim predicted: "She will be instrumental in helping us develop the opportunities within our fantastic customer base as well as expanding our business to new clients who want to personalize their player experiences in real-time, with sustainability at the heart of their player journeys."

Over the last year, Future Anthem has significantly expanded its workforce which grew by an impressive 152%. Recently, the company hired experts, including Jessica Wu and Laura Bird to the roles of Head of Marketing and Chief Financial Officer respectively.

Throughout her extensive career, Baumgardt accumulated significant knowledge within the online gambling sector. Future Anthem's new VP of Sales and Customer Development EMEA added: "I have spent many years working within this industry, and I know exactly what it takes to lead a strong commercial team capable of maximizing revenues and developing new and existing customer relationships." Finally, she said that she is looking forward to collaborating with the growing team of experts at Future Anthem.
