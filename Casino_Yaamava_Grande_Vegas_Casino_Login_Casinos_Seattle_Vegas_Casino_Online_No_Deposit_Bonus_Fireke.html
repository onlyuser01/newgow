Pitch ICE, a unique event that provides start-ups with a priceless opportunity to connect with investors started accepting applications on November 24 this year. Entrepreneurs that are interested in participating in the event and showing off their innovative solutions and technology will have until December 8 to submit a 2-minute video via Pitch ICE’s website and answer a form containing different questions. After public voting and approval from an independent jury, 14 pitches will make it to the Pitch ICE event planned for February next year.

Startup Companies Can Still Submit Applications for Pitch ICE

The deadline for startup companies that are interested in participating in Pitch ICE is getting near. Once the submission deadline passes, on December 9, 2022, voting will begin which will run until December 16, 2022. During the voting period, members of the public will have 10 votes each to support startup companies they want to see at the Pitch ICE.

Upon completion of the voting process, an independent panel of industry experts will select up to 14 companies that will participate in the event itself. The lucky winners that receive approval from the independent panel of experts will be “offered a free-of-charge pod to participate at ICE London on the Pitch ICE Pavilion for the full three days of the show,” the event organizers explained. This will grant the companies a unique opportunity to meet with potential investors on February 7 or February 8 on the Pitch ICE stage.

Set between February 7 and February 9, 2023, ICE London is one of the biggest and most-anticipated business-to-business events for the growing gambling industry. Besides startup companies, operators, distributors, regulators and game developers are going to participate in the prestigious event.

The Event Recognizes Exceptional Industry Talent

Andy Ventris, ICE London’s event director, said about the upcoming event: “Clarion Gaming has a proud track record of promoting start-up businesses by using the influence of its brands to help make the connections that all businesses need.” Moreover, he explained that the event helped many companies connect with strategic partners and investors ever since the first edition which dates back to 2009.

“Pitch ICE is about recognizing the extraordinary entrepreneurial talent that exists within this industry and helping them on their journey.“ Andy Ventris. event director of ICE London

According to Ventris, every year companies benefitted from joining forces with leading partners and investors, which was a result of their pitch presentations. According to him, the event represents a unique opportunity for companies that have a product that is ready to be presented and can be upscaled. Finally, Ventris said that Pitch ICE picks exceptionally talented people and helps them grow further in the industry.
