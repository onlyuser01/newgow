The premium gaming and entertainment destination in Minnesota, Treasure Island Resort & Casino, announced the launch of a new lucrative promotion that features big prizes and giveaways. Earlier this week, the venue unveiled details regarding its Instant Millionaire promotion. Treasure Island Resort & Casino confirmed in a statement that the promotion kicked off this week and will run through November 24, 2023.

Active between 10 AM and 8 PM daily, visitors to the casino will be granted a chance to win a wide range of prizes. Such include hotel stays, new cars, as well as cash prizes. Additionally, Treasure Island Resort & Casino visitors can win Pepsi products, concert tickets and free slot games.

Lucky visitors will have a chance to win prizes every day, while four lucky guests will be able to benefit from a guaranteed prize of $10,000 or $5,000. What's more, the premium casino entertainment location revealed that top prizes as a part of the Instant Millionaire promotion include large cash sums between $100,000 and the staggering $1,000,000.

Kevin McNair, Treasure Island's Director of Casino Marketing, shared his excitement: "This is the first time a casino promotion of this caliber has taken place in the state of Minnesota." Additionally, he pointed out: "We're expecting hundreds of guaranteed daily winners, one of which we hope will be for a million dollars!"

McNair spoke about the engaging casino games at the venue that enable the visitors to participate in the Instant Millionaire giveaway for a chance at big prizes. "We are looking forward to celebrating winning swipes all day long at the Instant Millionaire kiosk station," he explained in conclusion.

To participate in the exciting giveaway, visitors must be Island Passport Club members. Membership for the Island Passport Club is free and available for visitors 18 years or older. According to Treasure Island Resort & Casino, the promotion is not eligible for employees and visitors must be present on-site to secure prizes.

Upon visiting the casino resort, guests can participate in the promotion via the Instant Millionaire kiosk and swipe their Island Passport Club card. The kiosk is located close to the Parlay Lounge, explains Treasure Island Resort & Casino. Earning swipes is based on the visitor's daily play, as well as card level. The promotion is limited to a maximum of five swipes daily.

Treasure Island Resort & Casino offers 44 table games, as well as 1,800 video poker, video keno and slot machines. The venue features a 500-seat high-stakes bingo hall, as well as a poker room and hotel accommodation with more than 780 rooms, making it the second-largest hotel in Minnesota.
