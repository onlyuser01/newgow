Stakelogic: Novomatic's former interactive division that's now an independent entity

Stakelogic entered the iGaming industry as a division of Greentube – Novomatic's interactive games arm – in 2015. With Novomatic keen to keep its status as a leading provider, Stakelogic's mission was to produce innovative multi-channel slot games for iGaming websites.

It was only three years later, however, that Novomatic sold Stakelogic to outside investors, and a new chapter of the company's history began. The company immediately started to release new slot games as an independent entity, and struck deals with other providers and major gaming sites through its partner program Greenlogic. It also added the Gambling Commission (GC) license to its armory, a respected authority that holds providers to the highest quality standards.

Stakelogic also expanded geographically. In 2020, it opened a new headquarters in Malta, possibly to make it easier to obtain the MGA license, and it also started to operate in Portugal, Italy, and Greece. Megaways slots became part of Stakelogic's collection, too – you can play some of these for free on our Stakelogic Games page – while the company also won several awards from organizations such as SIG.

Apart from its extensive slots collection, Stakelogic operates a live casino game program that offers blackjack, roulette, and baccarat games, allowing players to experience the feeling of being in a live casino setting while at home.
