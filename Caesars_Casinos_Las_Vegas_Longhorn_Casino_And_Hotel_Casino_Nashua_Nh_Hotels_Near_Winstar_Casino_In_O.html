Sportsbook Tipico Strengthens Positions in Germany with Bayern Partnership

Tipico will continue to pursue stronger ties with local sports franchises in Germany as the company has renewed its partnership with Bundesliga’s FC Bayern Munich. Through the partnership, Tipico will be able to continue collaborating with the soccer club on various marketing opportunities and create tailored content for sports fans.

This deal is a continuation of a five-year deal originally signed back in 2015 and the period of the new partnership puts the companies together as partners well through 2025.

The collaboration was welcomed by Tipico CCO Marlon van der Goes who argued that by continuing to build on this relationship, the company was demonstrating its unfaltering dedication to improving itself.

A Big Player in a Big Market
