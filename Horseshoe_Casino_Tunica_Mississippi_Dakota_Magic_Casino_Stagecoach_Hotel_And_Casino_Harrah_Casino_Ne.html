Make You Rich Slot Review

Make You Rich is an Asian-themed 3×3 video slot released by the Dragon Gaming studio. Introducing Caishen, Ancient China’s god of wealth and good fortune as the main character in the game and its storyline, Make You Rich invites players to spin the reels and wait for the mighty god to bestow his many blessings upon them in the form of riches.

The game is engaging to play and has many interesting features and graphic design elements that help it stick its head out of the crowd. but its balance is not entirely clear to us. While some players might consider it a little too expensive to play when using medium to large stakes, it is still a game worth checking out thanks to the special win-win bonus round, and bonus buy feature.

After carefully testing and reviewing the game and comparing it with other similar titles from top providers that have embraced the same theme and genre, our experts have issued their own conclusions regarding Dragon Gaming’s slot released in June 2022. If you are ready to read about their discoveries, check out the rest of this online slot review dedicated to Make You Rich.

Theme, Storyline, and Graphics: Receive Your Blessing from the Caishen

Caishen is ready to descend from the skies and shows players who are destined to be rich their very own path to some unfathomable prizes. Of course, they need an ounce of luck to start collecting their wins, and this is where the almighty god comes to their help.

The background looks excellent with a grid located against a vividly colored celestial, gold-plated temple located up high in the mountains, with picturesque columns set at the foot. The columns are nicely wrapped around by Chinese dragons that resemble snakes that are seen moving continuously, helping set the mysterious mood and atmosphere created by the game itself.

Caishen, the Abacus, the Key, the ingot that resembles Aladdin’s lamp more than an ingot, and the Lantern are all traditional Chinese symbols complementing the theme and making game exploration more enjoyable and interesting for players.

Sounds and Animations

At first glance, the game is expected to feature a classic Oriental soundtrack with specific music accompaniment to complement the graphics and overall theme. However, the developer picked a playful type of music that is more engaging and danceable and less calming and soothing than one would expect. Make You Rich Gameplay

Similar to the rest of the Dragon Gaming slots, Make You Rich has simple gameplay rules that are easy to follow by both experienced players and newbies who are just getting familiarized with the world of online slots. The game is initiated once the slot machine has finished loading, players need to plate their bets. This can range between 0.05 and 100. Next, they need to click or tap the “Start” button to initiate the spinning of the reels. The five available pay lines on the grid are active nonstop. Provided multiple pay lines are seen landing on the grid, players are paid according to the largest amount.

The control panel is easy to explore and “+” next locate to the “Start” button will help players enter the setting menu where they can set up their bets, opt for auto-spins, or control turbo mode by turning it on and off.

Make You Rich: Ingot Collector, Mega Spins & Buy Feature

The game does not introduce players to any of the standard bonus features that are normally found with most video slots. This means that while there aren’t any free spins and expanding wilds to be looking for, players can still enjoy an extra Ingot Collector round. They are also given the choice to use the Bonus Buy Feature and jump straight into the middle of the action.

Ingot Collector Feature

When the prize combination is seen landing on the grid, the left-handed counter will proceed to replenish with an extra Ingot symbol. In order to engage the Mega Spins round, players must come across a minimum of three identical symbols, and their string of winnings should not suffer any interruptions.

The bonus consists of a series of five games with a 5x bet multiplier. All spins guarantee a win while the reels will spin with the bet that the player has chosen to place prior to the start of the bonus session. The Mega Spins round is, therefore, played using the same rules as the ines sued to play the base game. It also consists of the same symbols, with the difference that these symbols are now upgraded and they are worth five times the original value. The bonus feature will come to an end once the five winning spins are over.

Bonus Buy Feature

Players who would like to start taking advantage of the Ingot feature straight away, with zero delays, can take advantage of the available bonus buy feature to do it. They are given the opportunity to either buy all three ingots simultaneously or limit their purchase to the ingots ones that they are currently missing so they can fill out the collector for 82x max bet.
