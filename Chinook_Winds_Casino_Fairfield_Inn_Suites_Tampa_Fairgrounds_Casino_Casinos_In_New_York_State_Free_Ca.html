More about Amatic and the company's history

Amatic (full name Amatic Industries) is an Austrian supplier of gaming products established in 1993. It has been operating as a designer, manufacturer, and distributor of gaming technology for land-based casinos ever since, so it's fair to say that the company has a long history. And it's a successful one, too.

It was founded by Reinhold Bauer, who has already had a lot of experience in the gambling industry. He decided to utilize his knowledge and started a new company, which was then known as Amatic Trading. The company specialized in producing slot machine games and cabinets for land-based casinos, which is still one of the cores of its business today.

The name of the company was later changed to Amatic Industries, and it kept on growing. Amatic developed a collection of roulette games for brick-and-mortar casinos and kept improving their line of slot machines and other products designed for land-based gambling.

Apart from regular standalone cabinets, Amatic also developed their own video lottery terminals (VLT). VLTs might look the same to players from the outside, but they function differently to normal cabinets. VLTs are connected to a central game server, which is where the game logic is stored. The VLT cabinets only display the results of the game to the player, but all operations are performed outside of them. This allows controlling multiple locations at once and managing them with a central management system.

Thanks to the constant improvement, Amatic remains one of the top suppliers of games, slot machine cabinets, video lottery terminals and other solutions for brick-and-mortar gambling establishments, especially popular in European countries.
