The vital role of sounds

Music and sound effects in slots are significantly important as they are proven to have an impact on the players and their engagement. Usually, slots tend to highlight wins and bonuses, such as free spins. Many slot games also play a sound for wins, even if the payout is lower than the actual wager, an activity known as losses disguised as wins (LDWs). It is important to note that whenever a bet is not a winning one, slots rarely play any sound effects. This, in turn, emphasizes the wins, thus creating thrill and excitement even if a majority of the bets are not winning ones.

According to a study into the impact of slot sounds, such effects can have a major influence on the players. The study uncovered that slot sounds play a strategic role when it comes to the positive reinforcement of the players. To conduct the study, researchers enabled 96 gamblers to engage in playing a slot machine simulator, one with sound and one without sound effects.

Research data showed that the "sound influenced the arousal of participants both psychophysically and psychologically." The researchers said that a majority of the players preferred to play slot machines that are backed by winning sound effects. What's more, the study outlined: "The sounds also caused players to significantly overestimate the number of times they won while playing the slot machine."
