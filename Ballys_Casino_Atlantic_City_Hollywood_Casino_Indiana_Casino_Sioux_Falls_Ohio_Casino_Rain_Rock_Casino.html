UK gaming operator Entain and US partner BetMGM are backing a new responsible gaming app to be distributed by Entain Foundation in the US. The Gamble Responsibly America App will be an educational resource to assist problem gamblers.

Accessing the App

Developed in partnership with Epic Risk Management and RG24/7, the app will be available for free across all mobile devices. The unbranded app has been designed to provide no commercial benefits for Entain, BetMGM, or MGM Resorts. The sole focus of the app is to give players access to resources and strategies to develop healthy betting habits. Consumers will be able to access information directly without having to register.

Promoting Responsible Betting in a booming Sports Betting Market

With legalized sports betting set to continue to expand across the US, the Entain Foundation is continuing to promote responsible gaming initiatives for US consumers. The foundation has established itself as a key advocate for responsible gaming through funding several key responsible gambling programs. The new Gamble Responsibly America App will give consumers direct access to advice in English and Spanish.

Key Features

Some of the key features of the app include:

Limits and Tools: The app informs people how they can regulate playing habits with functions like limiting deposits on a weekly basis or allotting a time-out, where logging into accounts will be restricted until a set time is complete. Most operators offer such tools, which effectively help people to control their levels of play.
