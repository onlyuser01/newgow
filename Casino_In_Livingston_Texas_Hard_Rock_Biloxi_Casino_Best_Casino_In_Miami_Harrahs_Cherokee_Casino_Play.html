Multipliers up to 10x and Free Spins Bonus Rounds

Twilight Princess is a 5×5 slot that ushers players into the same mesmerizing universe as the one created for the player-favorite Starlight Princess slot. The same appealing flowers, crowns, and the beautiful princess herself have been used to decorate the grid where players must land in clusters of five or more symbols to trigger wins.

Players who manage to hit a wild orb on a random spin during the base game will also enjoy the perks of multipliers of between 2x and 10x. The multipliers will automatically apply to any winning combination they belong to.

Provided more than a wild symbol participates in the same win, the respective multipliers will be added for an even greater winning potential.

Scatters, represented by golden orbs, can also be seen landing on any reel, engaging the Free Spins bonus round when a minimum of three of these symbols are hit while playing the base game. While the bonus round is triggered, all wild symbols landing on the grid will stay in their positions until the end of the round.

Players who are eager to play the free spins can get them on the spot by purchasing them for 100x their bet in those markets where the bonus buy option is available. Players who opt for this feature will receive up to five scatters on the triggering spin.
