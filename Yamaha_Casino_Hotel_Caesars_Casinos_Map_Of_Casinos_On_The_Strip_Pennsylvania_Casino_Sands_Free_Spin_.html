Award-winning iGaming platform and sportsbook solutions provider Gaming Innovation Group (GiG) announced a new partnership with Strike Games.

Strike Games to Leverage GiG’s Platform

The terms of the new deal announced Thursday see GiG provide Strike Games access to its leading platform solution. The company’s platform is developed in a way to help operators grow on a global scale and capture emerging opportunities. Leveraging GiG’s platform, Strike Games will be able to deliver new experiences for its growing customer base. At the same time, the company will deliver different customization options for its clients as well.

The new partnership is signed for an initial term of five years. During this time, GiG agreed to provide its leading player account management (PAM) platform, as well as its front-end and content management system (CMS). Those innovative solutions, combined with Strike Games’ marketing experience and growing presence in the industry are expected to deliver growth for the company.

“Centered on personalization and unique player experiences, Strike Games will initially focus operations in several international and emerging markets, utilizing GiG’s innovative technology and experience to leverage its own high growth potential,“ reads a statement released by Gaming Innovation Group

The Agreement Brings Excitement to the Duo

Richard Brown, GiG’s CEO, revealed that the collaboration with Strike Games brings excitement to the company. He acknowledged that GiG offers leading solutions and this collaboration further expands the company’s global reach. In conclusion, Brown said: “I am particularly pleased with the potential of brands such as Strike, combining with our technology, as we continue to push innovation and drive opportunity for the brands we power.”

“Strike games is incredibly excited to commence a partnership with GiG.“ James Pound, director at Strike Games

James Pound, Strike Games’ director, added that the company is delighted to join forces with GiG and forge this new partnership. The company’s director revealed that GiG was identified as the company that will be able to deliver innovative technology that will ultimately bring success for Strike Games within the growing market. Pound added that the new collaboration will enhance the customers’ experience and deliver better services. Finally, he said: “We are looking forward to developing something amazing and launching a truly new age iGaming platform.”

The latest announcement comes after earlier this month, GiG announced a deal with SpinAway. The new partnership sought to facilitate the launch of SpinAway into the regulated gambling market in Ontario, Canada. In addition to helping the company go live in one of the most important markets in North America, the deal expanded GiG’s portfolio of partners.
