Bally’s Corp Signs Long-term Partnership Deal with NBA

Bally’s Corp. has inked a long-term partnership with NBA to become its authorized sports betting operator. Under the agreement, Bally’s will get rights to use official NBA logos, official league data, and the NBA marks on its online sports wagering products portfolio. The company will also work hand-in-hand with the NBA to help protect the integrity of the game.

This partnership is the gaming provider’s second sports betting deal with a professional league after it collaborated with the NHL. Bally’s Corp. stated that the idea behind the collaboration with the association is to transform the way fans engage with teams. With two of the biggest professional sports leagues, Bally’s goal is to become a leader in iGaming and sports wagering.

President and chief executive officer of Bally’s, George Papanier, said that this partnership is part of the company’s increasing momentum in collaborating with professional leagues. He stated that it is a very important deal that will allow the company to provide NBA’s fans with entertaining sports wagering products.
