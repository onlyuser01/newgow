Pick A Crab

On any of the base game spins, the fisherman can pop up and bring players the Pick A Crab feature. Opting for one of the three crabs will automatically trigger one of the four available modifiers: Bonus Add, Let’s Catch Crabs, Win Spin, or Stacked Wilds. The Bonus Add modifier will be responsible for adding scatter symbols to the reels, thus boosting a player’s chance of enabling the bonus game. The Let’s Catch Crabs modifier will reward a player with three or four crab symbols, by landing the wild symbol on the reels. The Win Spin modifier will be responsible for adding a series of mystery symbols to the reels. The Stacked Wilds modifier will display at least a couple of super stacked wild symbols.

Free Spins Frenzy

Landing three scatter symbols on the first, third, or fifth reel will enable the Free Spins Frenzy feature. This means the player will be given eight free spins with two to five reels spinning in sync. With each new spin, a different and completely random number of synced reels will be used.

Hold & Win Respins

A player who manages to land at least five crabs on a single spin will also get to trigger the special Let’s Go Crabbin’ Respins feature. This feature will reward them with a Hold & Win type of bonus featuring three respins. The respins will become active as soon as a new crab will land on the reels.
