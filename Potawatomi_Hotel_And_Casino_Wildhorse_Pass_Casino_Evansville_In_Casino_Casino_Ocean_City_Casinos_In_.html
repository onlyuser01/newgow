Fruit Warp review

Theme and Storyline

Without winlines you can win with up to 3 sets of fruits

Fruit Warp’s not your average slot. It does away entirely with the whole reel or grid structure, it has no cutesy characters or complex bonus games.

Instead, it features an eerie, but strangely beautiful void from which 9 pieces of fruit emerge as if flung out by the eldritch greengrocer of the abyss.

What bizarre mind constructed this, we don’t know. But it is hard not to fall immediately for its simple and elegant mechanics and visuals.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The game is 3D modelled in a way that allows the fruit to come arcing up at you in a lazy parabola. Once they arrive in front of you they spin slowly in place until warped out of existence by the next spin.

The sound design is basic, with a slightly annoying marimba track you might want to mute if you’ll be playing for a while.

Gameplay

As described above the gameplay the game throws a random selection of nine fruits at you rather than dealing with reels and symbols on a grid.

If you hit three or more of the same fruit the game pays out.

Explanation how bonus works

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

If you manage to hit four of the same kind of fruit a portal zaps the other fruit out of existence and triggers a respin of those fruit.

Hit five or more and you trigger one of the fruit mode bonuses. Each fruit mode is a slightly different set of bonuses and multipliers that are triggered when you hit more of the same kind of specific fruit. The game continues until a spin happens without the fruit mode’s specific fruit appearing.

If you hit 4 of the same fruits you get the respin feature which gives you big chance for a Fruit game

In each fruit mode every time you hit one of the mode’s fruit you earn a combination of lives, which allow you to ignore a spin where there are none of the specific fruit, and multipliers which are applied to your total win at the end of the bonus feature.

Each type of fruit provides a different combination of lives and multipliers with the rarest fruit giving the best rewards during bonuses.

Hitting the special symbol of a bonus game raises the multiplier for your bonus winnings
