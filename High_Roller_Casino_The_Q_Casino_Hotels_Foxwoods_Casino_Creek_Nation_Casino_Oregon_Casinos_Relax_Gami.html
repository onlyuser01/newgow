Players of the new slot game from Relax Gaming can form wins when matching symbols land from left to right. In case they land a wild symbol, nearby symbols are transformed into blockers that remain on the grid until there are no more wins. Once this happens, those symbols turn into the same normal symbol or a coin that grants a chance for a prize with a multiplier of the player's bet between 1x and 1,000x.

Players who destroy all blocker symbols during the base game can access six free spins. During this special bonus round, there are no blocker symbols. "If a wild lands, normal symbols are turned into mystery symbols which either turn into matching symbols, an additional free spin, or coin symbols which are worth their weight in gold," explains Relax Gaming.
