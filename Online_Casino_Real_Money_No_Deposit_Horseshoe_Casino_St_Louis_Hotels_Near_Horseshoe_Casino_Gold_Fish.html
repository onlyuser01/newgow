The esports betting provider is bullish on growing its presence in the Americas

Oddin.gg, a leading business-to-business provider of esports betting products, has secured a temporary license in Colorado. Having been greenlit by the Colorado Division of Gaming, the esports specialist will be able to expand its overall presence in the Americas.

According to Oddin.gg’s official announcement, the license attests to the compliant nature of its products and the company’s desire to play by the rules. Fully in line with the gambling laws in the Centennial State, the provider will now grow its US footprint.

Already boasting licenses in New Jersey and the Canadian province of Ontario, Oddin.gg remains bullish on growing its presence. The company hopes to firmly cement itself as a leader in the provision of esports betting solutions.

Oddin.gg’s products allow betting operators to tap into esports, engaging younger and more dynamic audiences. Its end-to-end esports betting system includes multi-award winning esports odds feed, risk management, iFrame solution, widgets, 24/7 content and marketing services.

Oddin.gg currently partners with multiple notable sports betting operators all over the world. Some of its latest agreements saw it join forces with Olimpbet and Lion Gaming and provide the two operators with solutions.

Oddin.gg Seeks to Supply Forward-Thinking Companies with Esports Products

Oddin.gg’s co-founder and chief executive officer, Vlastimil Venclik, commented on the development, expressing his team’s excitement about the temporary license in Colorado. According to Venclik, the license represents “another important milestone” for the company and is in line with Oddin.gg’s North American expansion strategy.

The CEO teased that the esports specialist is currently working on securing more licenses in US jurisdictions.

Venclik added that experts expect esports to experience exponential growth in North America. Analysts believe that the esports betting volume will reach $10 billion by 2025, providing a “vast expanse for heightened bettor engagement and an enhanced esports viewing experience.”

According to Venclik, the fast-growing esports betting industry “paves the way” for innovative bookmakers to embrace esports.

At Oddin.gg, we’re poised to empower such companies with our award-winning tools and services, driving their success and concurrently elevating the stature of esports. Vlastimil Venclik, co-founder & CEO, Oddin.gg

Oddin.gg concluded that it would continue to provide resources to the global esports ecosystem, helping it reach “unprecedented heights.”
