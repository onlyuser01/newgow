A Modern Twist of a Classic Game

The new title is a fruit-themed slot featuring many familiar symbols, such as Lucky 7s, bonus symbols and, of course, fruit.

The game sports the DoubleMax feature that removes any winning combination from the reels, with a multiplier doubling with every winning outcome during a single spin. Thanks to this pop-and-drop feature, players can win huge prizes with a single wager. The multiplier resets only after a losing cascade.

In addition, after every winning spin, a wild with a possible modifier will appear on the reels, allowing players to make additional combinations. Modifiers can include Super Wilds, Boost Wilds, Each Way Wilds and regular Wilds.

Player excitement is further boosted by a Free Spins mode that is triggered when gamblers land on three or more bonus symbols. Three symbols award 7 spins, four symbols provide 10 and five give a whopping 13 free spins. During the bonus, the multiplier starts at 1x and builds up without resetting after every spin.

A gamble feature allows players to stake their free spins for a chance to win more. However, if their wager is unsuccessful, they will lose all spins.

If players want to skip straight to the action, they can buy 7 spins with the price depending on their bet. Alternatively, they can use the Golden Bet feature which increases their stake by 25% while doubling their chance to naturally trigger free spins.

The new game is a familiar-looking title that is nevertheless packed with innovative features that players are going to adore.
