Stakelogic has expanded its content reach in the regulated online gambling market in the Netherlands. The company, which focuses on the development of iGaming products, has signed a new partnership with BetNation, a local operator. This partnership should allow Stakelogic to press on with both its slots and live dealer content portfolios in the country.

Stakelogic Boosts Content Offering for BetNation

The partnership was facilitated by Oryx Gaming, which is the official aggregator platform for Stakelogic. As a result, BetNation players may now explore the full range of Stakelogic titles across classic and modern slots. Stakelogic offers a good mix of classic titles and feature-rich options that come with Wilds, Free Spins and Respins.

There are numerous games to explore, including El Cowboy Megaways, Wild Moon Saloon, and Hola Frutas, all of which come with some pretty exciting gameplay on the core level. Stakelogic Sales Manager Neil Tanti welcomed the opportunity to be teaming up with BetNation. Tanti added:

BetNation needs no introduction. It is a power player operator, and we are thrilled to see our classic slots, video slots and live dealer games land in its game lobby and for Dutch players to experience the insane excitement our content offers. Stakelogic Sales Manager Neil Tanti

Tanti is confident that Stakelogic’s portfolio of games is one of the world’s leading options for players and that BetNation’s game offering will receive a significant boost as a result. Smart Gaming B.V. Director Robert T. Schouten also joined the conversation and said that BetNation fans will definitely be excited to have a crack at Stakelogic’s titles.

The Netherlands Continues to Elicit Strong Interest from Stakeholders

“Its content really is next-level entertainment and will undoubtedly be a big hit with our players,” Schouten concluded. The Netherlands has been a focal point for the efforts put in by a number of companies from the sector recently. Swintt announced that it is expanding its own footprint with Joi Gaming earlier this week.

Stakelogic has also been pushing its content in the Netherlands for a while now. Back in September, the company teamed up with Unibet. Overall, the Dutch iGaming market remains vibrant and highly competitive, and a good example of how tight regulations and industry sustainability can go hand-in-hand.
