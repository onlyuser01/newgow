Dragon Tiger review

Theme and Storyline

Dragon Tiger has an East Asian theme, focusing on dragons and tigers in particular.

Players will be transported to a world infused with Chinese flavor and inhabited by mysterious creatures. These animals will guide players on a quest for alluring treasure.

This slot is a good fit for fans of Asian themes and fantasy animals.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The relaxing soundtrack is filled with flute notes that give it a subtle Eastern vibe. The reels spin with a click, and wins are celebrated with festive chiming.

On the reels, players will immediately notice the variety of golden and jewel encrusted animals. Lucky toads, leaping koi fish, lumbering turtles, and elegant rabbits all make an appearance.

The most striking symbols are the white tiger and the enigmatic golden dragon. Each of these majestic beasts seems ethereal and wise as they gaze out from their reels.

The reels themselves are framed in gold, with a fierce dragon crawling up each side. Beyond the reels, a soft red and golden glow adds further enchantment to the aesthetic.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Dragon Tiger create a magical Eastern atmosphere.

Gameplay

Dragon Tiger is a five reel, four row slot.

Players are awarded wins for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel.

The swift pace of the gameplay ensures players will stay engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol. It appears on reels 2, 3, and 4.

The Free Spins feature is triggered if players land at least 3 Bonus symbols. The number of free spins awarded depends on the number of Bonus symbols that trigger the feature as follows:

3 Bonus symbols = 8 free spins

4 Bonus symbols = 15 free spins

5 Bonus symbols = 20 free spins

Throughout the Free Spins feature, Wild symbols have a multiplier selected at random. These multipliers can be x2, x3, or x5. If there are multiple multipliers on the reels, they are combined and then applied to players’ winnings.

Players can obtain additional free spins during the Free Spins feature by landing at least 2 Bonus symbols. The number of free spins awarded depends on the number of Bonus symbols landed as follows:

2 Bonus symbols = 5 free spins

3 Bonus symbols = 8 free spins

4 Bonus symbols = 15 free spins

5 Bonus symbols = 20 free spins

During the Free spins feature, special reels are used.
