Crazy Mix review

Theme and Storyline

Crazy Mix has a festive Australian theme.

Players will arrive in the Australian wilderness, accompanied by some rugged guides. A host of animals including turtles and koalas greet players with friendly smiles. Exotic fruits are also on offer in temptingly bright colours.

This game is a good fit for players looking for a whimsical exotic holiday vibe.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is a chilled out tune that feels intended for holiday fun, but also has a pace that escalates pressure. This may prompt some players to opt to click mute.

The colourful symbols include classic Australian animals such as koalas and kangaroos. These creatures are animated in a cheerful cartoon smile that will make players want to befriend them.

The exotic fruit symbols are animated with a slightly more realistic style, showcasing their unique colours and textures.

The backdrop behind the reels is a fanciful interpretation of the Australian wilderness. Whimsical baobab trees are topped with pink leaves and the iconic red road is lined with spiky foliage.

The game’s hosts Chuck and Aneka are dressed for a chill outdoor party. Chuck sports sunglasses and swim trunks, while Aneka’s hair is adorned with flowers.

Overall, the animation and music combine for a festive, distinctively Australian atmosphere.

Gameplay

Crazy Mix is a five reel, three row slot with 15 win lines.

Winning combinations are created from left to right with a minimum of 3 symbols in a row for a winning combination.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols. The Wild symbols come in two forms, Chuck’s face and Aneka’s face, with the Chuck Wild also triggering respins and collecting Fruit symbols.

During the Boomerang Respins feature, players are awarded a respin, a collectable Fruit symbol, as well as a +1 multiplier whenever the Chuck symbol lands.

The Cocktail Free Spins feature is awarded when players gather 4 or more Fruit symbols. They begin the feature with 5 free spins. Depending on the combination of Fruits symbols, different bonus features will be in effect. These bonuses can include: random Wilds, bet multipliers, and additional free spins.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.35%, above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.15 up to a maximum of $/£/€45 per spin.

The volatility of this game is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, high volatility Australian-themed slot.

With an excellent strategy profile, this Crazy Mix is a great pick for players looking to maximise their wins.
