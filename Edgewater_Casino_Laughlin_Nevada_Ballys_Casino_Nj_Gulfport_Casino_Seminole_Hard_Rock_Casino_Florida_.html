WynnBET Is Focusing on Boosting Brand Visibility

After his retirement from professional football, Johnson became an influential figure in social media and quickly gaining popularity again when he agreed to participate in an exhibition boxing match in June. “This is all about connecting directly with sports betting fans to provide great entertainment and conversation through social media and in-person events,” Johnson explained in a brief statement.

Wynn is also interested in giving back, and the company will assist Johnson with his charitable initiatives, many of which are based in his hometown in Miami, Florida.
