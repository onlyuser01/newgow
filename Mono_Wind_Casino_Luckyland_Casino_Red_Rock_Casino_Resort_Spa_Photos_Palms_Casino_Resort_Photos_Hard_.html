Resorts World Las Vegas revealed that it seeks to hire some 6,000 workers for its resort which is planned to open next year. The company accepts job applications for multiple positions from entry-level positions to managerial level positions.

Resorts World Accepts Job Applications

In a statement released this Monday, Resorts World Las Vegas announced that it is now accepting online job applications for multiple positions at the resort. In the coming months, residents that want to apply can submit their application to the company’s Career Website as the resort looks to fill open positions in the coming months until the opening in the summer of next year.

Approximately 6,000 new jobs are vacant and the jobs include various positions from entry-level to management-level positions. Applicants can apply for food and beverage positions, hotel operations, nightlife, casino, finance, and more.

In a statement, Scott Sibella, President of Resorts World Las Vegas said that the company is excited to launch the Career Website and initiate the required efforts for creating approximately 6,000 new workplaces in Las Vegas. With that in mind, Resorts World is not only creating a world-class resort experience but it is also “cultivating a talented team of hospitality professionals” who share the company’s core values such as honesty, loyalty, safety, hard work, and compassion, added Sibella.

Further Details Are Available Online

The operator said that residents who are interested in applying can create an account at the Resorts World’s Career Website. Applicants who apply will have all of the tools and resources needed to complete their application and hiring process. Furthermore, Resorts World outlined that information such as frequently asked questions, workplace environment details, and additional details about the company can also be found on their Career Website.

Registered applicants also have access to an online chat with a Resorts World recruiter via their account dashboard for a selection of available live information sessions. Within those 30-minute live information sessions, applicants can ask questions about their application and hiring process in real-time. According to Resorts World, the sessions will commence on Monday 30 with a first-come, first-serve reservation.

With that in mind, recently Resorts World announced that it partnered with Zouk Group. Under the fresh partnership, the global lifestyle company Zouk will debut in the United States, bringing unrivaled entertainment to the resort which is planned to reopen in summer 2021.
