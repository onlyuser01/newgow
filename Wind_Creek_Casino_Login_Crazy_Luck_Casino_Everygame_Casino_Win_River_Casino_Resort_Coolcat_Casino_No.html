Walk Into the Fairy Forest Filled With Magic

Habanero has chosen the magic unicorn theme, designing a slot filled with interesting, cartoon-like creatures, breathtaking and vibrant visual elements. The carefully chosen background music perfectly completes the magical and highly nostalgic atmosphere. Habanero’s head of corporate communications Toni Karapetrov expressed the company’s delight to be able to release yet another “captivating title which features a beautiful blend of audio-visuals and unique features” while offering a fully immersive experience.

Game Symbols, Free Spins & Special Features

Golden Unicorn Deluxe features the classic low-paying card symbols along with specially designed fairy symbols, which are the high-paying symbols within the game. Players will also have the pleasure of coming across a series of wild and scatter symbols together with bonus symbols that can instantly trigger the Chest Exploration feature. The developer made sure to add a Unicorn Shop for players eager to immediately jump into the exciting bonus level filled with free spins and cash prizes inside the mysterious chests.

Up to 100 Free Spins

Players must collect a mix of castle symbols in order to trigger as many as 100 free spins. The good news is that this option can be retriggered times and times again, which makes the new slot even more interesting.

Find the Secret Boxes With the Chest Feature

Players can trigger the Chest Feature and play a game of secret boxes that allows them to collect hidden gems, cash prizes, and higher game multipliers.

The Golden Unicorn Wild Symbol

During the base game, players need to keep an eye on the magical unicorn and try to catch it. This will double their winnings. The more Wild symbols they collect, the bigger the winnings. Collecting as many as five wild symbols brings players a 32x multiplier.
