The company confirmed it joined forces with the leading brand operated by Kaizen Gaming, Stoiximan. The new partnership marks the debut of the iGaming supplier's games in the European country. Per the new agreement, Stoiximan's Greek customers will benefit from engaging EGT Digital games, including Clover Chance, Single Progressive Jackpot, Bell Link and High Cash.
