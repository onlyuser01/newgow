The Swedish-founded company with a strong presence in the online gambling sector, Play’n GO, signed a new deal with the famous betting and gaming company in the United States, Rush Street Interactive, Inc. Details regarding the new collaboration emerged Thursday and see Play’n GO agree to supply games, powering RSI’s BetRivers platform.

Under the terms of the content agreement, BetRivers will be able to roll out games from the renowned casino entertainment provider to its customers in strategic markets such as New Jersey and Michigan. Per the new deal, the brand’s customers in the two states will be able to enjoy thrilling Play’n GO games, including Rise of Merlin, Fire Joker and KISS: Reels of Rock.

Judging by a statement, RSI plans to expand further its offering and deliver more games from Play’n GO soon. The collaboration represents an important deal for the brand as it enables it to engage with its growing customer base by delivering thrilling and unique titles from the famous iGaming content developer.

At the same time, the deal complements Play’n GO’s North American growth, allowing its engaging games to reach new audiences in Michigan and New Jersey. The iGaming content provider deemed the new collaboration “technical,” as its content will be integrated into RSI’s platform directly.

Coinciding with its US-facing content deal, Play’n GO announced the release of Honey Rush 100, the sequel of its popular Honey Rush game from 2019. The latest slot game delivers thrilling experiences with its hexagonal grid and rewarding features.
