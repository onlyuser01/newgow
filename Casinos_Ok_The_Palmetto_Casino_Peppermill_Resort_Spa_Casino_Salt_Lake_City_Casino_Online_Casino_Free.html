The new fall promotion marks the first out of three planned throughout the end of the year, featuring a total prize pool of €2.5 million

The provider of innovative games for the iGaming industry, Wazdan, celebrated the start of the fall with the launch of its Mystery Fall promotion. The engaging network promotion marks the first out of three such activations the company planned throughout the rest of the year. The Mystery Fall network promotion launches on Friday, September 15, 2023, and it is scheduled to run through October 15, 2023.

Players familiar with Wazdan’s portfolio of games would find the promotion quite appealing, considering that it will be featured throughout its entire portfolio. As a result, players can benefit from the thrilling activation while enjoying popular hits like Mighty Wild: Panther, Fortune Reels, Burning Sun, Magic Spins and 9 Coins: Grand Platinum Edition, among other hit games from Wazdan.

The recently launched activation, along with the upcoming network promotions will be available to all partners of the company. Accessing the promotions is free of charge and is expected to elevate the excitement and thrill for online players. To ensure a seamless process, Wazdan confirmed that its partners will be able to benefit from a dedicated account manager and will deliver the necessary ready-to-use marketing materials and graphics.

Finally, Hyla said: “Kicking off with the Mystery Fall drop, we will be helping operators maximize growth and boost engagement with an impressive €2,500,000 prize pool to keep players enticed.”

After the Mystery Fall promotion, players can expect the launch of two more activations with HalloWIN Drop being the first one.
