The game joins other popular Greek Mythology games released by Play’n GO, including Rise of Olympus, Gates of Troy, and Athena Ascending.

Discover the Mystical Forces

By adding its own twist to the mythological tale, Play’n GO has created a fascinating game with Mystery Symbols, Scatters and elusive Spirit of Hope symbols seen mixing the game’s frightening theatrics with the captivating storyline.

The fascinating narrative speaks about Zeus who created Pandora and gave her a magical box that she was forbidden to open. However, her curiosity prevailed. Pandora eventually went over Zeus’ word, opened the box, and freed a series of mystical forces from the nether realm.

This is where Play’n GO’s genius and creativity come into the picture. Mystery Wilds, Multipliers, the spirits of Mania, Malady, Discord, and Avarice are all found in Pandora’s box of evil, either causing chaos and bringing misfortune or attracting big wins. The catch, of course, is that players cannot foretell whether they will unleash a monster or a great fortune before spinning the reels and opening an evil box.

For example, landing an Evil Box symbol anywhere on the reels will reveal a Mystery Symbol. Provided Zeus manages to hit the evil box two times before the box is opened, the box is transformed into a Mystery Wild symbol instead of simply opening with the regular x2, x3, x4, and x5 Multipliers.

When Spirit of Hope symbols are seen landing on the grid, the Re-Spin of Hope feature is engaged. When the spin starts, all mystery symbols are seen closing. At the end of the spin, all Mystery Symbol, Scatter, and Mystery Wild symbols are locked.
