The leading provider of engaging games for the online gambling industry, Lady Luck Games, announced it expanded its presence in a strategic European market. The company confirmed Wednesday that it secured a gambling license from Spelinspektionen, the Swedish Gambling Authority (SGA), a statement reveals.

Describing the achievement, Lady Luck Games said that the Swedish gambling license represents a milestone moment. Additionally, the iGaming developer pointed out that the new license from the SGA allows it to expand its presence in Sweden by delivering its thrilling games to new customers.

Thanks to the recently secured license, Lady Luck Games can now deliver its engaging portfolio of games to players in Sweden. At the same time, the license reaffirms the iGaming developer's dedication to meeting regulatory compliance, adhering to responsible gambling and offering safe and secure gambling entertainment for players in the European country.

Securing the license from Spelinspektionen enables Lady Luck Games to deliver cutting-edge slot titles, including Astro Anna, Spinjoy Megaways and Popeye for Swedish clients. Additionally, the approval grants customers in Sweden access to popular hits like Treasures of Tizoc, among other titles featuring unique and engaging themes, as well as thrilling and awarding mechanics. The company predicted that its games will be well-received by players in the country, delivering elevated while at the same time responsible gambling experiences.

Julian Borg-Barthet, Lady Luck Games' CCO, shared his excitement about the latest achievement and pointed out: "We look forward to serving our customers in Sweden and extending our warmest welcome to all players who are fans of Lady Luck Games."

Borg-Barthet explained that the license in Sweden creates new growth opportunities for Lady Luck Games and at the same time, allows the company to expand the reach of its thrilling games. "We are dedicated to continuously enhancing our games and providing innovative experiences that captivate and thrill our players," explained Lady Luck Games' CCO in conclusion.

Mads Jørgensen, Lady Luck Games' Chief Executive Officer, was similarly excited about the license from the SGA. "We are thrilled to receive our Swedish gambling license, enabling us to bring our innovative and immersive casino games to the Swedish market," he said. Finally, Jørgensen pointed out: "This license is a testament to our dedication to compliance, responsible gaming, and delivering top-notch entertainment experiences to our valued players."
