GGPoker, the leading online poker room, announced Tuesday that it expanded its brand ambassador team. The announcement saw Ali Nejad and Jeff Gross join GGPoker as brand ambassadors.

The Leading Poker Room, GGPoker, Selects New Brand Ambassadors

Under the collaboration, Nejad will serve as GGPoker Media Ambassador, while Gross will serve as Voice of GGPoker. Moreover, the duo will represent the online poker room during online as well as live events and will collaborate with other ambassadors such as Daniel Negreanu, who is currently a global ambassador. Nejad and Gross will also join forces with Fedor Holz, Jason Koon and Bertrand “ElkY” Grospellier. The main goal for the collaboration between GGPoker and the duo is to create new experiences for poker players and help expand the reach of poker globally.

“Adding two of poker’s best contest-creating personalities to the GGTeam means we’ll better entertain our existing customers while broadening the game to reach new ones.” Daniel Negreanu, global ambassador for GGPoker

Commenting on the topic, Negreanu revealed that GGPoker is the biggest online poker room in the world. However, according to him, this doesn’t mean that GGPoker isn’t planning further growth. Negreanu explained that by adding the duo as brand ambassadors, GGPoker will offer even more entertaining and exciting experiences for its customers. In conclusion, he pointed out that by joining forces with Gross and Nejad, the poker room will help attract new customers.

The Two Brand Ambassadors Have Significant Experience within the Poker Industry

Gross shared his excitement by saying that it is thrilling to join GGPoker. He said that he is looking forward to being a part of the GG team as a brand ambassador and hosting the best tournament on the internet, the weekly $10,300 Super MILLION$.

“I am thrilled to be joining GGPoker. It’s an amazing time right now for poker and GG is leading the way.” Jeff Gross, Voice of GGPoker

Overall, Gross has more than 10 years of poker experience. It was back when he was 14 when he first played poker. According to The Hendon Mob, by now, Gross’ total earnings from poker have surpassed $3.5 million. Besides being a poker player, Gross is also famous for being a streamer and podcaster with a huge fan base on social media.

On the other hand, Nejad is famous for participating as a poker broadcaster in NBC’s Poker After Dark and National Heads-Up Poker Championship, The Ultimate Poker Challenge TV series, ESPN’s World Series of Poker and World Poker Tour broadcasts. Additionally, Nejad recently commented on the live-action and interviewed players that participated in the Triton Super High Roller tournament.
