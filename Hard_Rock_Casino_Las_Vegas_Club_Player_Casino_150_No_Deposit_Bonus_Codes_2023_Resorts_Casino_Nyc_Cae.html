Yggdrasil, the award-winning gaming software supplier “built on passion, creativity, and great teamwork” has added a fresh title to its Game Engagement Mechanic portfolio. The studio has released its pioneer DuoMax creation in the form of a dystopian city-themed slot called Florageddon! DuoMax. The new game was released just in time for Halloween and it has plenty to offer.

Enter the Dystopian City

The theme of the new game is centered around a dystopian city with flesh-eating mutant plants threatening to end all humanity. Your job is to enter the city, fight the mutants, and regain control of Florageddon once again. Victorious players land huge wins, especially when the dropdown wins cause all winning symbols to vanish from the reels, triggering even more cascades. Whenever dropdown wins are engaged, a new Wild symbol gets placed on the reels.

The game gives high-energy gaming vibes thanks to its eye-catching, detailed visuals and animation options. The audio is spot on while the carefully-chosen symbols represent the apocalyptic carnivore plants in the form of orange, green, pink, and blue triffids, purple street lamps, fire hydrants, clock towers, and sign posts. The game also gestures the classic A, K, Q and J icons as low-paying symbols. To score a win in the base game, players must match between three and five symbols.

Free Spins, Free Spins Gamble & Special Rewards

Players can collect between seven and 11 free spins by gaining three consecutive Dropdowns. While using free spins, scoring three more consecutive Dropdown wins will engage the Special Reward. Players can also choose to gamble between seven and 16 free spins to see the bonus round move to the next level. Triggering four Cascades in the base game generates a Special Reward in the form of a Double Wild, a Wild Stack, an Extra Life, or a 10x Multiplier Boost.
