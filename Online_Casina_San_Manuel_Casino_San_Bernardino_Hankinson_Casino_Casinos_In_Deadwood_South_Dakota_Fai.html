Caesars Entertainment congratulated the player on his spectacular victory, sharing news of his achievement on its social media channels.

In May, Rivers had a short stint as a free agent at the Arizona Cardinals, which quickly cut him. The player later moved to the Seattle Seahawks, where he stayed but for a few weeks.
