Scatter Monsters review

Theme and Style

Scatter Monsters takes players to a ghoulish world of demons, vampires, and zombies.

The flames of hell are close at hand as players navigate an underworld of adorably horrible monsters. In this perilous realm, players will need to keep their wits about them to escape the wrath of supernatural creatures.

Fans of macabre imagery and players looking for a whimsical vibe will especially appreciate this slot.

Win in the Scatter Monsters slot machine

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Players enter the slot through the mouth of a skull with fangs that suggest it belonged to a monster. The setting is a cartoon vision of hell, with catlike demons perched on mounds of brimstone.

Among the symbols there is a range of tiny supernatural beings. Child-like vampires with purple skin, sleepy zombies, and fiery-mouthed demons are all among the cast. Most of these monstrous characters are more cute than terrifying.

Upon wins, the color drains from the little monster symbols as their souls are drained. In the foreground, players may notice the tails and heads of horned demons watching the gameplay.

Scatter Monsters offers a moody, rock soundtrack that brings to mind bands of the ‘70s. The reels land with a gentle echo, upholding the sense of cavernous space.

Together, the animation and soundtrack create a lighthearted take on eternal torment.

Gameplay

Scatter Monsters is a six-reel, six-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols anywhere on the reels. The size of the prize is determined by the type of symbol and the number that have landed.

The swift gameplay ensures that this slot will hold the player’s attention.

Conclusion
