Revolutionary mobile game developer and creator of the best mobile Baccarat game on the planet OneTouch has launched Queens of Glory Legacy. The game is a much-anticipated sequel to the already successful Queens of Glory slot released in April 2020. The slot that offers a glimpse into the fascinating world of royalty has three Free Game modes and a Feature Buy option.

Royalty Theme & Cool Features

The game features the classic 5-reel, 3-row grid with 10 winlines designed with the royalty theme in mind. The action revolves around three queens looking to share their glory with players and generously reward them with free spins. The background introduces players to an opulent palace with lots of golden elements, jewelry, and other precious items. The accompanying audio is suitable for the theme.

While the game has medium volatility, the jackpot is rather small at 735x the initial bet. Nonetheless, with a hit frequency set at around 22.50%, players can win almost once every four spins. The minimum amount accepted for play is set at 0.40. High rollers can wager as much as 100 per spin.

The game’s pay symbols are spread into low paying symbols including the regular card ranks, and high paying symbols such as rings, torches, amulets, torches, vases, harps, or chalices. Low-paying symbols have the potential of paying up to 10x the bet for hitting a five-card matching combo. Harps are the highest paying symbol. They can pay as much as 300x the bet for a complete combination.

The game also comes with a series of special symbols including three different types of Wild symbols and the Temple Scatter symbols. Each of the three queens in the game features a Wild symbol. The Wilds always double as 2x win multipliers during payouts in the base game as well as during regular free spins modes. Temple Scatters can engage regular free spins.

Three Free Game Modes

Each of the available Free Game modes is associated with one of the three queens: Ruby, Emerald and Rose. To trigger any of the free spins modes, players must collect a minimum of ten gems of matching color. This will bring players ten rounds of the said Free Games, each with its own functionalities for more gameplay versatility.

During the Ruby Free Game, the initial spin triggers additional Wilds that stick to the screen for the remainder of the round. To win in Emerald mode, players must get a Wild multiplied by a random multiplier. During the Rose Free Games mode, players are rewarded with Sticky Wilds.

Fresh Feature Buy Option

The sequel also brings a Feature Buy option as a fresh game element. This mode allows players to get quicker access to a variety of features while smoothening their way towards cool rewards.

The company’s head of product Madis Raus called the three Free Game modes “a delightful opportunity” for players looking for innovative gaming experiences.
