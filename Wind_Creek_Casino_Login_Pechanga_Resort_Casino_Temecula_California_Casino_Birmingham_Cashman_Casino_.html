The leading gaming solutions provider, Intralot, announced that its US subsidiary, Intralot Inc., extended its existing contract with the Wyoming Lottery Corporation.

Intralot and Wyoming Lottery Extend Their Partnership

Under the terms of the 5-year partnership extension, Intralot Inc. will continue to supply its services and lottery operating system for the Wyoming Lottery. The duo’s collaboration dates back to the launch of WyoLotto by the Wyoming Lottery Corporation back in 2014.

Since then, the collaboration between Intralot Inc. and the Wyoming Lottery has been continuously growing. Only recently, back in March, the duo announced the launch of the newest draw game called 2by2. Moreover, in just five years, the collaboration between Intralot and the Wyoming Lottery boosted the state with $25 million.

The Partnership Extension Marks an Exciting Moment

Founded in 1992, Intralot is proven a world leader in supplying gaming solutions for operators globally. By now, the company is active in more than 41 regulated markets around the world. While offering innovative and quality services for the gaming and lottery industries, the company supports operators in raising money for good causes.

“We are grateful to the Wyoming Lottery for their trust and long-standing partnership, and we look forward to continuing to create value and drive their growth through our state-of-the-art technology and advanced quality of services.” Byron Boothe, CEO at Intralot Inc.

Intralot Inc.’s CEO, Byron Boothe, said that the company is grateful to continue its collaboration with the Wyoming Lottery. He outlined that Intralot is looking forward to taking the collaboration to a new level while at the same time generating revenue for Wyoming.

“We take our responsibilities seriously at the Wyoming Lottery Corporation. Our selection of a gaming vendor is of extreme importance and is at the very core of our operations.” Jon Clontz, CEO of the Wyoming Lottery Corporation

The Wyoming Lottery Corporation’s CEO, Jon Clontz, revealed that selecting a suitable gaming partner is incredibly important for the Lottery. He explained that the Wyoming Lottery is honored to extend its deal with Intralot and “continue to rely on our mutually beneficial, trusting, and high-performing partnership.”
