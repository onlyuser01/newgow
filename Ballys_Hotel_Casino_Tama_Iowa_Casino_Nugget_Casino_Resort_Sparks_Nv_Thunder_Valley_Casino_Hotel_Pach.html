Blueprint Gaming, a leading British gaming studio, has agreed to supply DAZN’s gambling division, DAZN Bet, with content. The new partnership will see the latter company onboard some of Blueprint Gaming’s best slot games.

The content will enhance DAZN Bet’s offering in the UK and Spain. The provider’s titles are already live with DAZN Bet in Britain, allowing customers to enjoy some best-in-class titles. The operator will promptly include the games into its Spanish offering as well and, as agreed with Blueprint Gaming, more market launches will follow in the following months.

The games that will make it to DAZN Bet’s platform include proprietary titles such as Eye of Horus, Fishin’ Frenzy and Genie Jackpots. In addition, Blueprint Gaming will power the operator with a comprehensive collection of hit licensed titles, including The Goonies and ted.

The agreement with the UK-based supplier will provide DAZN Bet with a further boost, helping the casino brand expand its global presence. DAZN Bet has cemented its place as a fast-growing iGaming brand and is bullish on further growing its presence. At the same time, the new deal will allow Blueprint Gaming to reach new markets, further consolidating its European footprint.

Blueprint Gaming Praised DAZN Bet’s Growth

The partnership was welcomed by executives from the two companies. DAZN Bet’s chief executive officer, Mark Kemp, shared his thoughts on the new agreement. He praised Blueprint as a studio whose name has become synonymous with online slots entertainment. According to the CEO, adding Blueprint’s content to the DAZN Bet library is an important step for his company.

Kemp added that DAZN Bet is keen on growing beyond the United Kingdom and Spain and will be happy to do so alongside Blueprint Gaming.

Our international market growth ambitions beyond the UK and Spain are high and Blueprint is a high-quality and much-respected gaming content partner to support DAZN Bet’s international growth. Mark Kemp, CEO, DAZN Bet
