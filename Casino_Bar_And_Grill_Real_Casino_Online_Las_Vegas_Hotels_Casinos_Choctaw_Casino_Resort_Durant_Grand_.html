One of the leading European and global operators of online and sports betting content, NetBet, has significantly strengthened its iGaming library by signing a partnership with EveryMatrix. The focus of this new collaboration is the latter company’s B2B aggregator, SlotMatrix, which distributes games from select suppliers and strengthens their distribution capabilities while adding value to partner operators’ libraries and content variety.

Both NetBet and EveryMatrix stand to benefit as they will be able to deploy more worthwhile gaming experiences with players all over the world and strengthen their respective offers in the industry. SlotMatrix is bringing a range of select and worthwhile games to NetBet, among which are proven and player-favorite titles such as Sweet Gems, Cleopatra II, Wild Gold, Queen of Egypt Exotic Wilds, and Book of Soul among others.

All of the games bring a unique engagement opportunity for NetBet and its audiences, with SlotMatrix constantly providing its partner brands with exclusive titles from the industry’s most accomplished developers. Commenting on this opportunity, NetBet PR Manager Claudia Georgevici said that her company was delighted to be bringing even more thrilling titles to its audiences.

"Our collaboration represents a significant step forward in our ongoing commitment to delivering the best gaming experience for our players," Georgevici noted, and added that the introduction of SlotMatrix content to NetBet’s library would undoubtedly lead to more enjoyable moments for players all over the world.

EveryMatrix CEO of Games Razvan Haiduc was similarly pleased to see a global brand such as NetBet become one of the latest partners to EveryMatrix and SlotMatrix. Haiduc said that EveryMatrix was happy to bring a diverse range of innovative and compelling games through SlotMatrix, which is also a powerful way to inject content quickly and efficiently and meet leading operators' needs for varied content.

NetBet has been significantly doubling down on its content game, with the company recently signing several content alliances with individual suppliers and companies. Among those is an arrangement with ESA Gaming directed at the brand’s Italian operations and another one with WorldMatch Games also covering the Italian market.

NetBet struck another pivotal opportunity with Swintt, with the two companies negotiating to collaborate on content distribution in jurisdictions covered by a Malta Gaming Authority license, and Germany in yet another major move for the operator. NetBet Italy similarly signed another relevant content partnership with Gaming Realms, once again strengthening its local footprint in the country.
