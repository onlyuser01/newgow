Caribbean and South America Next for FanDuel

The CAGE Companies will assist the United States’ leading sportsbook, FanDuel, to expand in new jurisdictions across South America and the Caribbean, the company announced on Wednesday.

To deliver on this new opportunity, CAGE Companies is passing the baton to CAGE Sports BV, a subsidiary that will spearhead FanDuel’s drive in the Latin American markets and the Caribbean.

The partnership offers mutual benefits to both companies, as CAGE will leverage its familiarity with local markets, and FanDuel will guarantee a premier sports betting experience in the target markets.

Commenting on the partnership, Robert L. Johnson, who is CAGE Companies majority owner, this is an exciting opportunity for the company to seek closer ties with FanDuel and expand its presence in the sports betting business.

Thanks to FanDuel’s advanced software solutions, Johnson believes that lead to significant expansion opportunities for business and create new jobs as well as generate recurring, sustainable revenues for governments.

FanDuel to Deliver Retail and Online Solutions in New Markets
