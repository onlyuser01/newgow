Nashville SC Hoping to Improve Home Record

Nashville SC is 3-3-3 in the 2023 MLS, while its home record is 2-2-1. That’s not too bad, but these guys haven’t won a game on their turf since March 11. They did win one on the road since then. It happened against Orlando City in a game that saw Nashville SC score twice while keeping a clean sheet.

Speaking of which, the boys from Nashville have kept a clean sheet in five of the nine MLS games they’ve played this season. In the other four, they conceded a total of five goals. That’s the lowest number of all the teams in the league, which makes Nashville SC the best defensive side in the 2023 MLS.

The most impressive fact is that they did well at the back despite playing against some really tough opponents, including Los Angeles FC, New York City FC, and FC Cincinnati.

Atlanta United’s Ambitions Keep Rising

Atlanta United is 5-3-1 in the 2023 MLS, and with 18 points, the boys from Georgia are sitting in the No. 3 position in the Eastern Conference. They’re just two points behind the New England Revolution and FC Cincinnati, each of which has 20 points.

Atlanta suffered a humiliating 6-1 loss to Columbus Crew in their last game of March. However, the loss had a sobering effect on them – they then went on to win two and draw two games in April.

What’s really impressive about Atlanta’s record this month is that it’s had to face some really nasty rivals. But they still managed to do very well, beating NY Red Bulls and Chicago Fire, and drawing with NYCFC and Toronto FC.
