MultiMax Multipliers, Golden Ticket Free Spins & Bonus Bets

The game introduces cluster pays, a mechanic that requires players to land a minimum of five matching symbols to score a win.

Each new winning cluster is removed from the reels, allowing fresh icons to drop down in a cascading movement, accompanied by a wild symbol to keep the party going.

Next, the multiplier on top of the grid will start going up, triggering the iconic MultiMax Game Engagement Mechanic to swiftly crank up cascades in volume and turn them into juicy wins.

The cascades can also combine once the cluster straddles the reels, revealing even more generous rewards.

To enter the Free Spins round, players must land a minimum of three Golden Ticket Free Spins icons. In return, players get at least seven spins. During the round, the multiplier on top of each of the reels will not reset.

This means the multipliers can rapidly pile up with each fresh winning combination. Players can also gamble the Free Spins for a shot to grab even more wins.

The Present symbol can randomly fall on any spin, rewarding players with up to 100x, four extra wild symbols, or 11 free spins. If the Present symbol is seen landing during the Free Spins round, players receive an additional spin or an increased multiplier.
