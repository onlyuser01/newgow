Withdrawing the funds is easy and users can simply transfer them to their own account. A request for the withdrawal can be made via the Bally Bet app or the website. Still, if there are customers that fail to transfer their funds, Bally’s confirmed that it will pay out their balance via a check. The betting and gaming company said that there is no minimum amount required for the check and that it will be sent to the addresses players listed on their accounts, ensuring users receive their funds back.

“Simply log in to your Bally Bet account to see if you have any remaining funds to withdraw. You can personally withdraw these until June 30th. Any funds remaining in your account after this date will be mailed to you at the address listed on your account,“ explains a statement via Bally’s

Bally’s explained that there is no need for the customers to deactivate their accounts. Once June 30 comes, the company will automatically deactivate all customer accounts and process the payout of funds for users that have not withdrawn their funds.
