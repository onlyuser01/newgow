Sports wagering monitoring operator US Integrity announced today it has entered into a comprehensive partnership with Desert Diamond Casinos & Entertainment to provide integrity services to the company’s mobile sportsbook.

‘Ensuring the Safest Mobile Experience’

The agreement between Desert Diamond Casinos & Entertainment and US Integrity will see the integrity firm deliver monitoring services via its proprietary dashboard to the brand operating mobile sports betting and retail sportsbooks.

Commenting on the new partnership, Tohono O’odham Gaming Enterprise’s chief executive officer Mike Bean outlined the excitement to work with US Integrity while the company’s mobile sports betting platform continues to grow locally.

“Having US Integrity as a partner furthers our commitment to ensuring the safest mobile experience for our guests,” added Bean, stressing the capabilities of US Integrity’s system to “identify potentially suspicious wagering activity in real time.”

Matthew Holt, chief executive officer and co-founder of US Integrity, also expressed his excitement to partner with Desert Diamond Casinos and help the brand protect the integrity of its sports betting operation.

“US Integrity’s mission is to provide conflict-free, best-in-class insights and compliance solutions to our clients,” Holt continued, looking forward to collaborating with the team of the brand that aligns with US Integrity’s standards in its “commitment to fair and safe sports wagering.”

Better Quality of Life for All People in Arizona

Leveraging partnerships with sportsbook operators to gain access to real-time data, US Integrity analyses dozens of data sets to uncover irregular wagering patterns that may suggest an event is being influenced either on a contest level or via officiating. The company recently received financial backing from the world’s largest operator of integrated casino resorts Las Vegas Sands.

Desert Diamond Casinos & Entertainment, the business brand operated by the business entity of the Tohono O’odham Nation, launched its mobile sports betting operation in February this year and is also currently operating three retail sportsbooks across the tribe’s four properties in Arizona.

Tohono O’odham Nation’s casinos near Glendale, Tucson, Sahuarita, and Why seek to provide the means for a better quality of life not only for the tribe but for all people in Arizona. Desert Diamond Casinos & Entertainment is among the largest tribal casino operators in the Grand Canyon State.

In February, US Integrity was selected by another tribal property after the Suquamish Clearwater Casino Resort teamed with the provider to benefit from its integrity services. The casino resort operated by the business arm of the Suquamish tribe, Port Madison Enterprises, hired US Integrity’s bet monitoring services to ensure the integrity of its sports betting operations.
