ComeOn Group has promoted from within, naming its director of customer engagement, Sherwin Jarvand, as its new chief data officer

ComeOn Group has strengthened its leadership team with the addition of Sherwin Jarvand as chief data officer. In his new position, Jarvand will lead the company’s data strategy.

ComeOn Promotes from Within

ComeOn Group expanded its C-suite with the promotion of Jarvand to chief data officer. The worker is already well-familiar with the company’s team, having worked with ComeOn for the last two years.

Jarvand first joined as head of customer relations management, before being promoted to director of customer engagement. Jarvand also has years of experience as an analyst and consultant and will be a great addition to ComeOn’s team. His past experiences include remits at various companies, such as Addison Global, William Hill and Axa Travel Insurance, among others.

As chief data officer, Jarvand will oversee the company’s data strategy, helping the iGaming company grow its market presence. He will serve in the new role in addition to his existing position as director of customer engagement. Jarvand will work from ComeOn’s main office in Malta and will work closely with Juergen Reutter, the company’s chief executive officer.

Jarvand Vowed to Use Data to Help the Company Grow

Speaking of Reutter, the CEO spoke on the matter, welcoming the worker to the company’s executive team. Reutter said that he is glad to have Jarvand promoted to chief data officer and praised his “outstanding track record” in the industry.

The CEO is certain that Jarvand has the needed skill set to further boost the company’s efforts centered on personalization and player engagement. The chief data officer is expected to help ComeOn Group continue delivering exciting and safe experiences to its clients.

To add, I am also very proud that we have promoted talent from within, which is something we always encourage from a people strategy perspective here at ComeOn. Juergen Reutter, CEO, ComeOn Group

Jarvand also shared his thoughts on his promotion. He says that he feels very privileged by the opportunity to lead ComeOn Group’s data strategy and work with the company’s executive team. He vowed to use his knowledge of data to accelerate the company’s growth.

For us, data has always played a crucial part of our business strategy and our vision to personalize our products and services, enable smarter decision making and ultimately enhancing the experience of our players. Sherwin Jarvand, chief data officer, ComeOn Group

ComeOn Group remains focused on providing its customers with seamless tech paired with personalized content and unparalleled real-time CRM solutions. The company wants to provide end-users with experiences akin to Netflix.

In November, the company once again reinforced its team with the addition of two new directors.
