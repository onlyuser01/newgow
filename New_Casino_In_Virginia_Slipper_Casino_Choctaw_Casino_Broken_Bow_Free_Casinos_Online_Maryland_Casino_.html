The globally recognized betting and gaming operator, Flutter Entertainment, released its latest trading update, uncovering details regarding the company’s performance for the fourth quarter of 2023. The new unaudited report emerged Thursday and highlights strong growth in terms of revenue and the number of average monthly players (AMPs) for the latest trading period.

Flutter posted a total revenue of £2.67 billion ($3.38 billion) for the fourth quarter of 2023, a result that marked an increase of 11% when compared to the figure posted for the same period in 2022. Notably, in Q4 2023, the company’s AMPs increased by 12% year-over-year to 13.6 million.

A breakdown of the £2.67 billion ($3.38 billion) in revenue reveals strong growth in terms of sports betting as well as gaming revenue. In total, Flutter’s sports revenue in Q4 2023 hit £1.66 billion ($2.1 billion), up by 8% when compared to the £1.53 billion ($1.94 billion) result from the corresponding period the previous year.

Besides the Q4 results, the company’s new report revealed its fiscal year results. For the fiscal year ended December 31, 2023, Flutter reported a 20% year-over-year increase in AMPs. Complementing this result, the company’s revenue for the period hit £9.5 billion ($12 billion). When compared to the corresponding period in 2022, the revenue soared by 24%.
