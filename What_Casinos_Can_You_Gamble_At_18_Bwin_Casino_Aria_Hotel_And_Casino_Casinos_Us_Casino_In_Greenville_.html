What Does the Relaunched Slot Bring New to the Table?

The upgraded mystical-themed game is a 6×6 reel slot that will carry players to a plush location located in the heart of the desert. The new game will reunite revolutionary game mechanics and an impressive list of bonus features meant to make players’ gameplay more exciting than ever. The game features up to 46 656 ways to win, with a maximum win of x20,000 the bet up for grabs.

Medallion Megaways is a highly volatile game with an RTG of 96.13% and a minimum bet set at €0.20. The maximum bet is limited to €50. Here is a quick breakdown of the game’s most important elements.

Game Symbols

The original game introduced players to the popular and rather mysterious tarot theme, expressed through high symbols part of the greater arcana: the Sun, the Moon, the Star, and the World. The relaunched version does no longer includes the Sun symbol. The medallion symbol looks like a spinning coin and it can suddenly pop up at the end of any reel spin during the base game, enabling or disabling stone symbols and their features. A stone symbol plays the role of an expanding wild symbol, taking over the entire surface of the reel once it lands, while also rewarding the player with a multiplier of up to x6.

Game Features

Void Respins, Expanding Wilds, avalanches, the free spins mode, and progressive multipliers are the hottest attractions part of the revamped version of Medallion Megaways. The Void Respin and the Expanding Wild features can be triggered out of the blue, at any given moment while engaged in the base game. The Void Respin grabs ten random symbols and proceeds to respin them. Next, the symbols are taken off the reels, and an avalanche of extra symbols takes their place for even more winning combos.

The free spins bonus round is the main star feature of the new slot game. The all-seeing eye symbol announces 20 upcoming spins tied to the progressive multiplier part of expanding wilds. In free spins mode, the multiplier goes up with each new avalanche, and not just with consecutive avalanches.
