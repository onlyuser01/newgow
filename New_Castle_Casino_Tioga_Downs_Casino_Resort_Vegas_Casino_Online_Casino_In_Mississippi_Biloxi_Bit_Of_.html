BETBY, an innovative sportsbook supplier, has expanded its leadership with Stefanos Karakidis as senior business development manager. The new appointee will be a welcome addition to the company’s senior sales team, further strengthening the company’s global expansion efforts.

Karakidis is a decorated sports betting industry veteran who has worked with a variety of gambling companies and tier 1 betting operators over the last thirteen years. His impressive track record includes a variety of roles related to trading, marketing and commerce, making him a perfect pick for BETBY.

The new hire will leverage his expertise to research new markets for BETBY, as well as to help the supplier form robust relationships with companies in said regions. Furthermore, Karakidis will be tasked with making sales projections and forecasting revenues – things that will help BETBY enhance its business.

The addition of Karakidis comes several months after BETBY welcomed the experienced Sergey Tsukanov to its team. Tsukanov joined the company as chief product officer, helping the supplier enhance its pipeline while overseeing the risk management and trading teams.

Karakidis Is Looking Forward to Growing BETBY

Stefanos Karakidis commented on his new position at BETBY, saying that he is very happy to have the opportunity to join such an esteemed supplier. He praised the company for its rapid growth and said that he is looking forward to being a part of its journey.

The company is continuing to grow at a strong pace, and as we expand our footprint around the world, I can’t wait to contribute to BETBY’s ongoing success. Stefanos Karakidis, senior business development manager, BETBY

Chris Nikolopoulos, BETBY’s chief commercial officer, welcomed Karakidis to the company, saying that the entire team is happy to have him on board. The CCO said that Karakidis’ experience will be key to expanding the company.

His expertise is sure to prove beneficial to our processes as we aim to partner with more leading operators and supply our wonderful cross-vertical offering. Chris Nikolopoulos, CCO, BETBY
