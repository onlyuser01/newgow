Might of Ra review

Theme and Storyline

Might of Ra is themed around Egyptian mythology, especially the iconic sun god Ra.

Players will begin a journey across the hot sands of the Egyptian desert, a storm rumbling in the distance. As they pass the pyramids, they’ll encounter the majestic god Ra and accept his call to a quest.

This slot is a great find for fans of ancient Egypt and those looking for a magical atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Flute notes add a mysterious tone to the soundtrack that will heighten players' sense of adventure. The Middle Eastern feel of the tune also helps establish the Egyptian setting.

The reels are filled with intimidating animals including fierce birds of prey, fiery-eyed horses, black scorpions, and snarling snakes. Each of these creatures has a supernatural quality that upholds the mythical tone of the slot.

Beside the reels, the god Ra looks on, an Ankh symbol hovering in his hand. His elaborate ensemble and ripped abs make his status as a deity clear, however his gentle swaying makes him seem somewhat like a videogame character waiting to be commanded.

Beyond the reels, a desert is visible with pyramids in the distance. Though the golden sand is bathed in sunshine, distant grey clouds are illuminated by occasional flashes of lightning. This suggests that even in this dazzling world, conflict is not far off.

Overall, the animation and soundtrack of Might of Ra create an adventurous Egyptian fantasy.

Gameplay

Might of Ra is a six reel, four row slot, with 50 win lines.

The gameplay is smooth and quick, ensuring players will stay engaged.

The maximum win for Might of Ra is x22,500 players’ total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol appears on all the reels and substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol. Whenever they land, Wilds may randomly transform their entire reel to Wild symbols. An assigned multiplier is also applied to any wins that contain a Wild.

Bonus symbols appear on reels 2, 3, 4, and 5 and when players land 3 or more Bonus symbols, the Free Spins feature is triggered. Players are awarded a prize of x2 their total bet if they land 3 Bonus symbols, and x5 their toal bet if they land 4 Bonus symbols.

The Free Spins feature begins with 15 free spins and the addition of an Ankh special symbol. For the duration of the Free Spins, players gather Ankh symbols which offer different levels with upgrades to the number of Wilds.

If players land 2 or more Bonus symbols, the Free Spins feature is retriggered. Players are awarded a greater number of free spins if a greater number of Bonus symbols retriggers the feature.
