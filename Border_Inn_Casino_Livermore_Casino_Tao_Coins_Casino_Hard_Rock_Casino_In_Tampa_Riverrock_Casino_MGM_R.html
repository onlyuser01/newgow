Aristocrat and MGM Resorts International have negotiated an exclusive partnership that features the opening of a now-available Buffalo Zone at the Beau Rivage Resort & Casino owned by MGM. The 1,500-square-foot gaming parlor will focus on Buffalo-themed slot games featured and developed by Aristocrat.

Buffalo-themed Slots for Everyone in Beau Rivage

The space will be enough to accommodate 50 titles and offer some of the most promising games in the series, including Buffalo Chief, Buffalo Max, Buffalo Gold, Buffalo Gold Revolution, Buffalo Grand, Buffalo Link, and more Buffalo-themed slot games that will be now available on Beau Rivage’s gaming floors.

Commenting on this opportunity, Beau Rivage president and COO Brandon Dardeau welcomed the opportunity to further improve the gaming floor and enhance the experience through Aristocrat’s sophisticated and highly-entertaining products. He added:

“Buffalo games are extremely popular among our players and having the opportunity to create a special gaming section allows us to further enhance our guests’ gaming experience.” Beau Rivage president and COO Brandon Dardeau

The news was welcomed with equal zest and gusto by Aristocrat SVP of commercial strategy and business analytics Jon Hanlin who said that the company was thrilled to be teaming up with Beau Rivage to make this venture a possibility.

Great Partnership Bound to Succeed

This is effectively Mississippi’s second dedicated area for Buffalo-themed slots, he explained and said the previous efforts to bring this product online in the state have been a success so far:

“We’ve had a great response since opening the first Buffalo Zone last summer at Beau Rivage’s sister resort Gold Strike Casino Resort in Tunica and expect a similar reaction on the Mississippi Gulf Coast.”

Hanlin spoke highly of the partnerships formed between Beau Rivage and Aristocrat and argued that the new Buffalo Zone would allow players to enjoy the company’s range of themed games while benefiting from the comforts of the Beau Rivage Casino & Resort.
