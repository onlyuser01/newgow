Mattress Mack Backs the Tide

One of the most prominent names in sports betting placed large bets on Monday night’s game, hoping Bama would win the College Football Playoff Championship.

Legally known as Jim McIngvale, he initially placed a $1.2-million wager on the University of Alabama winning the national championship over the University of Georgia. The moneyline odds were +125 and the bet paid out $1.5million for an Alabama win.

Mattress Mack increased his wager for Alabama winning the championship by placing another $1.5 million on the moneyline, with odds of +115. Caesars Sportsbook was his target for those bets, but he also sent some to Wynn Resorts. In total, he put up over $6 million, according to the sportsbooks.
