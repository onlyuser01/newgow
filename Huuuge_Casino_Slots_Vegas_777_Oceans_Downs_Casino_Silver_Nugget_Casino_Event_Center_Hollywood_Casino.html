As announced by FansUnite Entertainment Inc, BestOdds.com’s operator Stram Entertainment has signed a strategic deal to acquire BetPrep. The decision was made as a result of FansUnite’s strategy to optimize its operations, focus on profit, and accelerate its revenue growth.

The international entertainment and sports company that offers technology for regulated online gaming had previously expressed its plans to make its business more streamlined. Accordingly, FansUnite announced it would shift the focus on its Chameleon business-to-business technology platform as well as a number of additional business units that would generate higher revenue values and higher gross margins.

BestOdds to Pay 30% Revenue Share for Three Years

According to the terms of the new deal, BestOdds has agreed to pay FansUnite a revenue share of 30% over the course of the upcoming three years. The respective revenue share will be subject to a minimum monthly guarantee, once the previously agreed ramp-up period will come to an end on September 1, 2023.

BetPrep, the leading affiliate brand in the sports betting and iGaming industries with a focus on North America, enables bettors to try out various wagering strategies. Unlocking Fresh Synergies

FansUnite believes the acquisition agreement will help it cut its operating costs without harming its planned growth while unlocking fresh synergies expected to generate revenue.

Speaking about the acquisition news, FansUnite’s chief executive officer Scott Burton said the new deal would allow them to keep benefitting from BetPrep’s revenue upside without having to tackle the operational costs. Burton also spoke about BestOdds’s good positioning on the market with a clear potential to turn BetPrep into a “successful product within their existing properties”.

Finally, he expressed their excitement regarding the new door that the partnership would open for FansUnite and BestOdds to keep working together.

BestOdd’s co-founder Will Armitage expressed similar feelings of excitement regarding the news of the acquisition. He added that the company was eager to collaborate with the FansUnite crew in the upcoming years while reaping their shared rewards.
