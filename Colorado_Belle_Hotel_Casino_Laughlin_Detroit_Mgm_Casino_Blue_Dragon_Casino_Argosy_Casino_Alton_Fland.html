AvatarUX, an award-winning game studio with an international reach, has doubled down on its global expansion through a new partnership with BetConstruct, a platform dedicated to providing operators with robust overall software for iGaming businesses.

The partnership is important for AvatarUX, which will be able to give its award-winning and player-favorite games CherryPop, TikiPop, PixiePop, Kitty POPpins a significant boost and make them available to operators that are utilizing BetConstruct’s array of solutions.

Commenting on this opportunity AvatarUX CEO Niki Longmuir said that the company was delighted to be teaming up with BetConstruct, which is an established and recognizable name in the iGaming industry. She further hailed the opportunity to see her own company’s portfolio grow rapidly across the globe.

"We look forward to working closely with the BetConstruct team," AvatarUX said. BetConstruct Head of Gaming Ruzanna Elchyan was similarly excited about this opportunity and welcomed the chance to add the supplier’s portfolio of titles to its library and thus enable partners in its network to benefit from some of the most exciting games in the industry right now.

"With an exciting roadmap ahead and proven concepts, we’re confident AvatarUX will become a must-have provider in any operator’s arsenal," Elchyan explained. This is not the first time that AvatarUX has sought to increase its international presence through a deal with aggregation and platform services.

Over the past months, this has been one of the preferred ways of doing business for the company, which established a similar partnership with Light & Wonder in February this year. More of this followed in the months after, with the supplier also teaming up with REEVO andRelax Gaming and joining their respective brands.

AvatarUX has been busy hammering out more worthwhile games in the meantime with the company presenting some exciting opportunities to players with such titles as Zombie aPOPalypse, HipHopPop, KokeshiPop, and others.

AvatarUX’s mechanics have been innovating the sector and bringing excellent experiences to players that can now be reached through BetConstruct’s network of partners. BetConstruct just launched its Bet Easy app, which is advertised as a solution designed to improve the gambling app creation process for partners.
