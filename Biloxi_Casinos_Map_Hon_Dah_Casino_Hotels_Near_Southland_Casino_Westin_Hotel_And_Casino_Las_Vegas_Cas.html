Fred's Food Truck review

Theme and Style

The Fred's Food Truck slot is themed, as its name might suggest, around a food truck placed in the middle of a bustling city owned by a mustachioed chef named Fred.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Hacksaw Gaming slots come in a number of different packages, with titles like Hand of Anubis or Cursed Seas having a dark, ominous tone, and games like Frank’s Farm and Pug Life coming in a much more quirky and fun package. Fred’s Food Truck falls firmly into the second category.

The game has a really charming design, with the titular food truck housing the reels in the center of a busy city street. Fred himself stands to the side of the reels and is brought to life with the kind of fun energy we have come to expect from a Hacksaw Gaming slot.

This is probably not a slot you want to take for a spin if you are on a diet, with all of the symbols being a selection of delicious-looking takeaway food.

Gameplay

The Fred’s Food Truck slot game uses a 5x5 grid, with 15 fixed paylines on every spin. The slot uses a cascading wins mechanic, with symbols from winning combinations being removed from the grid and replaced with new ones.

The stake is adjusted below the reel set, and you will also find the autospin option there. A burger menu provides access to the paytable and other technical information.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Whenever a Green Chilli symbol lands, players will notice that it will display a Multiplier amount. One chilli can display up to x5, two can display up to x20, and three can be up to x100. If these land with a winning combination, then they are added to the Global Multiplier displayed to the left of the reels and paid out.

If you land three FS Scatter symbols, you will trigger the Free Spins bonus with ten spins. If you land four Scatters, the Big Menu Free Spins are rewarded, which rewards 15 spins. During the spins, all Green Chillies that land throughout are automatically added to the Global Multiplier for the round's duration.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The Fred's Food Truck RTP has a 96.33%, which, according to our slot strategy guide, is comfortably above average and well worth a spin. However, it is worth noting that there are multiple versions out there with lower RTPs, so be sure to check the game information before you play.

The slot’s maximum win is 10,000x your stake, and the game has been rated as medium on the volatility scale by Hacksaw Gaming.

In the version of the game we reviewed, the slot’s betting range was $0.10 up to $100. This could vary between casinos.

Conclusion

Fred's Food Truck is a delightful meal of a slot. While it may not be as volatile or as feature-packed as some other Hacksaw Gaming slots, it is still nicely designed, offers a solid 10,000x maximum win, and has a good free spins bonus that makes great use of the theme and some multipliers.
