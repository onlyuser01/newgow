Sparky and Shortz review

Theme and Style

Sparky and Shortz is themed around a sci-fi world where machines rule.

Players will travel through space and time to join a robot team in conducting mysterious experiments. Intergalactic minerals and exotic aliens gleam, ready for players to analyze them.

This slot is a good pick for players looking for a futuristic atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The quirky soundtrack features plenty of digital beeps, as well as metallic clanging that reminds players that this is a world of mechanical devices.

On the reels, players will see a number of unique stones and gems unlike any found on Earth. Each one is animated in a vibrant color that seems to glow, emphasizing their magical quality.

A variety of adorable alien creatures also appear preserved in jars, though they have the ability to transform into much larger creatures. Players may be struck by a longing to let these cute little critters free, even though this could create unpredictable results.

One of the most intriguing symbols is the tiny galaxy captured in a jar. This potent preserve seems both exciting and perilous to open up.

The reels themselves are an advanced machine with a friendly robot hovering nearby for support. The setting seems to be a lab in a complex spaceship with no shortage of secrets to discover.

Together, the soundtrack and animation create an imaginative fantasy world.

Gameplay

Sparky and Shortz is a five-reel, three-row slot with 10 win lines.

All wins are calculated from left to right.

The maximum win for Sparky and Shortz is 10,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

This slot features a win multiplier that increases whenever players land a Stacked reel.

Beside the reels, players will find the Boost Meter from which a multiplier value can be chosen at random. Landing Stacked reels increases the value of the Boost Meter.

If players land 3 Scatter symbols, the Free Spins feature begins. Players can gain an additional 5 free spins if they land 3 Scatter symbols again. The feature may continue to be retriggered up to a maximum of 100 free spins.
