HappyBet Becomes Official Regional Partner of Inter Milan

FC Internazionale Milano, the soccer club also known as Inter Milan, announced a new partnership with HappyBet. The deal was revealed last week and sees the brand become an Official Regional Partner for the team.

Through the collaboration, the team will display the HappyBet brand on the Virtual Cam Carpets, as well as LED boards around the field during home games of the team at Stadio San Siro. This will be possible thanks to the innovative geolocation technology known as Digital Overlay that is used by the club. It is important to mention that only select countries in Europe will view the HappyBet brand on the Virtual Cam Carpets.

Inter Milano revealed that the new partnership will also bring exclusive initiatives. Those initiatives will further promote the HappyBet brand to the large fan base throughout the season.
