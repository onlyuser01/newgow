Ancient Eclipse review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around Aztec aesthetics and a mythical eclipse.

In a dark jungle, players will come upon an ancient temple filled with mysterious carved emblems. Gods and animals all peer out from this set of reels, ready to share the magical secrets of an impending eclipse.

This slot draws on a fantasy notion of South American history, rather than an archeological one. However, the result is a rollicking adventure fit for Indiana Jones.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is an ominous track with tension-inducing flutes. Players will feel as though they have reached a climactic moment in a jungle adventure.

The eerie shadowy jungle behind the reels suggests that the sun may already be eclipsed. Among the wild foliage, sharp-eyed players may notice spears guarding the path. The perilous nature of this world is made clear and players will feel the thrill of danger.

The symbols are impressively animated with ornate designs. Some portray animals such as crocodiles and leopards, while others display stone carvings of classic card suits.

Overall, the animation and music work well to create a dark world of Aztec myth.

Gameplay

Ancient Eclipse is a five reel, four row slot, with 50 win lines.

The gameplay is fast and smooth, ensuring that players stay engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all regular symbols.

The Moon symbol may land on any spin, leaving a gold Sun frame stuck to the position they landed on.

If a Moon symbol lands on one of these framed positions, then the Wild Eclipse feature will be triggered. This means that all Sun Frames will transform into Wild symbols.

The Sticky Sun Free Spins feature is triggered when players land Scatter symbols on reels 2, 3, and 4.

The Free Spins feature begins with 10 free spins and on any free spin that does not initiate a Wild Eclipse, any Moon symbols will give the player an additional free spin.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.64%, above our average yardstick of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is on the high end of medium, which makes it a moderate match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, high-medium volatility Aztec-themed slot.

The detailed animation adds to the appeal of this game along with its ample RTP and reasonable volatility.

The creatively-themed bonus features add extra interest to this engaging slot.
