Irish gambling company BoyleSports has reinforced its leadership team with the addition of an industry veteran as its new head of customer engagement. The one up to the task is Michail Koutsoukos, an individual with years of experience in leadership positions within the gaming sector.

Koutsoukos announced that he will be joining BoyleSports on his LinkedIn account, expressing his excitement for continuing his “gaming journey.”

This includes Kindred Group, which he joined in January 2017 as head of customer communications. Koutsoukos served Kindred from its office in London a acquired valuable iGaming-related skills.
