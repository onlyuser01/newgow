Chaos Crew review

Get ready to dive into a murky underworld with Chaos Crew.

Guided by a vicious cartoon cat and an equally terrifying skull, players will explore a realm of dark symbols and ‘90s alternative aesthetics.

Fans of Nirvana might particularly appreciate some of this slot's nods to grunge culture.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

Chaos combines moody shades of black and brown with bright pink, blue, and green for a striking visual effect. The graffiti-adorned streets behind the reels appear in monochrome hues, while the reels stand out in the neon colors.

The symbols include a variety of spooky emblems, including an inked skull, a smiley face with exes for eyes, and a mysterious moth. Each of these images has a trippy quality that feels right on theme for Chaos Crew.

The classical piano soundtrack is at odds with every aspect of Chaos Crew’s visual aesthetics. The slow and calm tune would be better suited to a romantic scene in a historical film than this trippy slot’s world. However, the mute button is readily at hand for those who find the music clashes with the vibe.

Though the soundtrack is a little off theme, the bold animation does all the walking to create a rebellious grunge world.

Gameplay

Chaos Crew is a five-reel, five-row slot with 15 win lines.

The maximum win for this slot is 10,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Landing 3 Free Spins symbols triggers the Free Spins feature, starting with 3 free spins.

Wherever players land a win during the Free Spins feature, the number of free spins is reset to 3, as monitored on a meter. This means that the Free Spins feature continues until players complete 3 non-winning spins in sequence.
