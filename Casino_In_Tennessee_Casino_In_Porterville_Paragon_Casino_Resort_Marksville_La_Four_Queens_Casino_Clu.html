BetRivers players in Ontario can now access Hacksaw Gaming’s titles thanks to the newly inked deal between the supplier and Rush Street Interactive

By partnering with BetRivers, one of the leading gaming brands in Canada’s only province that currently offers fully regulated online gaming services, Hacksaw will significantly strengthen its position in the region.

The company’s chief commercial officer, Gabrielle Star, expressed delight about being able to add the “top-tier operator” to their ranks while also describing Rush Street Interactive as a highly reputable and trustworthy operator with a wide global reach.

Further commenting on the new deal, Star spoke about the “great strides” that the provider has been making in the Canadian province in the last months. The CCO also acknowledged that the market will continue to be a strategic one for them as they carry on with their plans to expand in more regulated and highly competitive iGaming markets worldwide.

At the moment, Hacksaw Gaming has inked agreements with over 300 partners in more than 20 regulated global markets.
