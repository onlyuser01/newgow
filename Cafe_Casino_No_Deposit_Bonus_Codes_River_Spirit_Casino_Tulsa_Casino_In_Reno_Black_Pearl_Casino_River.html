QTech Games has entered into a new partnership with Oddin.gg, a prominent B2B supplier of esports betting solutions, the companies shared in an email press release. Through this collaboration, QTech Games will strengthen its own exposure in the esports wagering market and help its own partners have access to advanced solutions in this particular vertical.

QTech hopes to establish itself as a go-to option for iGaming operators thanks to the continuous betterment of its product portfolio in which Oddin.gg will now play a key role. Oddin.gg’s entire portfolio of solutions will be integrated with QTech Games and any third-party client.

This way, the esports betting feeds and iFrame feature, risk management, and other relevant products will be available to operators in licensed jurisdictions. Oddin.gg also offers solid coverage of events and gaming titles, among which the most bet on esports video games, including Counter-Strike, Dota 2, and not least, League of Legends.

At the same time, Oddin.gg is continuously improving on its offer, hoping to expand its coverage and technologically-led solutions to feature more esports in the future. There is around ten high-grossing esports right now that the company would be interested in covering eventually.

Commenting on the existing partnership, QTech Games CCO Daniel Long hailed the opportunity to team up with Oddin.gg, and said that the company’s brand shared the same core values as QTech.

"Significantly, since Oddin.gg boasts the highest industry uptime and sharpest odds, its solutions will also have a positive impact on our partners’ operating margins," Long added, noting that QTech’s own analyst team was impressed by the quality of the lines delivered by Oddin.gg.

The partnership was welcomed on similarly amicable terms by Oddin.gg Managing Director and Co-Founder Marek Suchar said that operators were fast adapting to the realities of a changed world where bettors had different preferences than those of previous generations.

Suchar said that his company remains committed to helping operators and partners remain profitable and build a robust product that is future-proof. Esports is part of this effort. "Oddin.gg is the standout leader in the esports betting service vertical with cornerstone philosophies and solutions around risk management, integrity, and official data," the executive added.

Previously, Oddin.gg assisted Sports Information Services to integrate Counter-Strike esports betting markets into its offer and deliver them to its own network of partners.
