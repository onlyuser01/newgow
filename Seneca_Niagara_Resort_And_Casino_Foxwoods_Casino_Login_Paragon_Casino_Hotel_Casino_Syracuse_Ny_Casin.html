The New Slot, Part of the YG Masters Portfolio

Created with the help of Yggdrasil’s GATI technology that allowed ReelPlay to use a variety of preconfigured, standard toolkits to generate the cutting-edge content of the slot, Hercules 10K WAYS is the freshest ReelPlay release that is now part of the prestigious YG Masters portfolio.

The game closely follows equally interesting titles, including Going Wild in Vegas WildFight and Hot Rise’s Nightmares VS GigaBlox by Boomerang.

The exploration of the world-famous Greek heroes is done with the help of a plethora of “fearsome and richly rendered 3D beasts”, all defeated in the labors part of the well-known mythological tales. The list includes figures like Hydras, Cerberus, the Nemean Lion, or the Erymanthian Boar.

Hercules represents the wild symbol in the game. Once a win is scored in the base game, the winning symbols are taken off the reels, making way for fresh symbols seen dropping as part of the cascading wins feature.

Hercules Battles Spins and Victory Prizes

To activate the multi-level mode Hercules Battles Spins feature, players need to land at least five Golden Apples. During the multi-level mode, players who defeat all beasts receive a bonus Victory Prize.

Originally, players are entitled to three respins prior to the epic duel that will be carried between Hercules and several beasts. The corresponding symbols of the beasts will feature both prize and health values.

The feature will go on throughout multiple levels until Hercule no longer has any health left, or until he exits the battle victoriously.

At the same time, ReelPlay’s chief executive officer, David Johnson, called the new slot one that “features titanic tussles and ferocious fights” in an adventure filled with action that is meant to take their successful 10K WAYS mechanic one step further.
