Morgana Megaways review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is ruled by the seductive sorceress Morgana.

Loosely themed on Arthurian legend, this world is packed with dragons, magic, and cryptic symbols. The game is set in a firelit hall straight out of a rollicking medieval fantasy.

Morgana stares down players with the force of a confident dominatrix. Her body-sculpting armour bodice and black cape do not detract from this effect. There is no doubt she’s the one in charge and players will want to stay on her good side.

The atmosphere is fun, though the style is more reminiscent of a children’s movie than high-budget fantasy. Morgana’s sex appeal, however, makes it clear this is a world for adults.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The background music brings to mind the Game of Thrones with its mystical tone. It substantially adds to the feeling of being in a magical place.

The animation style definitely seems like something from a Saturday morning cartoon special. It’s lively, but does not ask to be taken too seriously.

The bright colour palette is eye-catching, and dragon banners softly fluttering beside the reels are gorgeous. Morgana herself is the highlight with her flame-hued hair and intense gaze.

Gameplay

Morgana Megaways is a six reel, five row slot. This slot uses the Megaways System, an any way wins system, meaning that matching symbols in anywhere adjacent reels result in wins.

The Megaways System also randomises the number of symbols on each reel (between 2 and 7). As a result, there are up to 117,649 ways to win each spin.

Max Megaways can be triggered on any paid spin, forcing every reel to have the maximum symbols.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol. The Wild only appears on reels 2,3, 4, and 5.

When three Scatter symbols appear, this triggers Free Spins.

Players can collect symbols to award a multiplier prize, additional Free Spins, and boost the minimum Megaways.
