3 Oaks Gaming has announced a new appointment, welcoming Yuriy Muratov as its new chief business development officer. The iGaming content distributor has tasked its new hire with bolstering the company’s business and overseeing the development of its future strategy

Muratov, 3 Oaks Gaming said, is an experienced industry veteran who has worked in a variety of business development, product, marketing, finance and analysis roles. His abilities made him a perfect candidate for his new position.

As 3 Oaks’ new chief business development officer, Muratov will further the supplier’s business capabilities and will be responsible for developing new business strategies. He will also support the executive team in driving the company’s strategic agenda and oversee the development of new products and services. Finally, Muratov will manage various strategic projects and initiatives.

In his new position, Muratov will be a part of 3 Oaks Gaming’s senior management team. He will work with the other senior managers to help the provider cement itself as a best-in-class distributor of iGaming content.

MD Damian Welcomed Muratov and Praised His Abilities

Sebastian Damian, 3 Oaks Gaming’s managing director, commented on the new appointment, welcoming Muratov to the company. He called the new hire a “fantastic addition to the 3 Oaks Gaming team” and lauded him as a person with incomparable industry knowledge and drive.

Someone who I have worked closely with in the past, his industry knowledge and drive is second to none, and we’re really confident that his addition will further add to our growing momentum. Sebastian Damian, MD, 3 Oaks Gaming

Muratov himself spoke on his new position, saying that he cannot wait to work with 3 Oaks Gaming. He said that he is joining the developer at a very exciting time and is thrilled by the opportunity to push it to the next level.

I’m delighted to be given this opportunity to step into a leading role in a company as dynamic and forward-thinking as 3 Oaks Gaming. Yuriy Muratov, CBDO, 3 Oaks Gaming

The appointment of Muratov follows the addition of Victoria Vashukevich and Krasimir Pankovski who recently joined the company in high-ranking management positions.
