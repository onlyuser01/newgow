Wagering and entertainment business operating out of Australia, Sky Racing World (SRW), announced a new addition to its ever-growing portfolio of horse racing content with the launch of Thoroughbred racing from Abu Dhabi.

Simulcast Abu Dhabi Racing to North America

SRW stated that Thoroughbred racing from the United Arab Emirates (UAE) launched on December 4 with the HH Sheik Mansoor Bin Zayed Al Nahyan Racing Festival in the capital which saw First Classs win the Group 1 Jewel Crown.

Throughout the event, SRW will distribute the simulcast of the track across North America and facilitate the commingling of international wagering B-pools via another subsidiary of Tabcorp, PGI.

It’s with great pleasure that we announce the simulcast of Abu Dhabi Thoroughbred racing to North American punters. With this exciting addition, SRW is continuing their goal of offering users a wide variety of quality International horse racing product to experience at all times of the day David Haslett, chief executive officer of SRW

The next race from Abu Dhabi is scheduled to take place on December 8, while additional races are to be held on December 15 and December 22. The season will continue into 2023, featuring races on January 12 and January 19, February 2, February 9 and February 16, March 2, March 9, March 16 and March 30.

Streaming on Mobile, Online and ADW Platforms

All races from Abu Dhabi will be streamed live on the new Sky World Racing App, on the operator’s website and across major advance depositing wagering (ADW) platforms, while the company’s website will also provide fans of Thoroughbred racing with free access to past performances and picks.

The addition of Thoroughbred racing from Abu Dhabi expanded SRW’s global racing portfolio, adding UAE to the likes of Australia, New Zealand, Korea, Japan, Singapore, South Africa, and Argentina.

Less than two months before the launch of Thoroughbred racing from Abu Dhabi, Australia’s biggest wagering company announced it has expanded its racing content with Malaysian Thoroughbred.

North American punters have a variety of wagering options on the races from Abu Dhabi, including Win, Place, Exacta, Daily Double, Trifecta, Superfecta, Pick 3 and Pick 4, while the commingling of wagering pools will provide a more attractive proposition for them due to the greater liquidity and stability of the odds.

A subsidiary of Tabcorp, SRW retains the exclusive rights to distribute live International Thoroughbred racing, Harness racing and Greyhound racing across the Americas and the Caribbean.
