The new game marks the debut of Yggdrasil's 10th game engagement mechanic, TopHit, delivering thrilling and rewarding experiences for the online players

The leading provider of games for the online gambling industry, Yggdrasil, announced the release of a new slot game, marking the debut of its newest Game Engagement Mechanic (GEM). The company announced on Tuesday it released Starfire Fortunes TopHit, a new interstellar-themed slot game that offers engaging and rewarding features.

As the name suggests, Yggdrasil’s new slot features TopHit, its newest GEM. TopHit marks the developer’s 10th GEM, expanding further its engaging offering by delivering thrilling new experiences for online players. Similar to other games from Yggdrasil, Starfire Fortunes TopHit stands out with quality sound effects and animations. Recently, the developer announced the release of Diamond Symphony DoubleMax in collaboration with Bulletproof Games.

The Game Developer Is Excited to Debut Its 10th GEM

Mark McGinley, Yggdrasil’s chief gaming officer, shared his excitement about the newly released Starfire Fortunes TopHit game. He explained that the launch of the company’s 10th GEM marks an exciting moment.

McGinley explained that the TopHit mechanic “means any spin can lead to multiple wilds landing on the reels, while this takes a whole new dynamic in the bonus round with seriously lucrative wins up for grabs.” According to Yggdrasil’s chief gaming officer, the latest game brings an easy-to-understand mechanic that creates a thrilling experience and grants a chance for huge payouts.

“Our 10th GEM has been launched, and we couldn’t be happier.“ Mark McGinley, chief gaming officer at Yggdrasil

The 5×3 slot game presents the reel at the center of the screen against a colorful background. In line with its name, the new game features symbols inspired by stars, planets and constellations. Besides the standard card symbols between 10 and Ace, the game uses special high-paying symbols too.

The Latest Slot Brings TopHit, Buy Bonus and Golden Bet Features

The new slot from Yggdrasil brings the popular Golden Bet and Buy Bonus features. While Golden Bet grants a guarantee that a nudge can occur, the Buy Bonus feature allows the players to hit the bonus round. Still, it’s important to mention that Buy Bonus is subject to availability in certain jurisdictions.

TopHit, Yggdrasil’s new GEM is the highlight of Starfire Fortunes TopHit. The feature brings an extra reel above the main game reel that spins together with it. “If a high-paying symbol lands fully in view, it will transform into a wild as well as turning any matching icons on the reels into wilds,” explains the game developer. On the other hand, if players land multiple same symbols on the top reel, they will have the chance to benefit from a multiplier of up to 5x.
