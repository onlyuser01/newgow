What is BankID?

At the same time, the institutions or banks looking to confirm your identity will have less trouble too, which makes the tool a perfect fit for the iGaming sector where identity verification is important, not to mention examining the provenance of money gamers use to play.

The simplicity of the system is also to be lauded. Any Swedish citizen or permanent resident with a personal identification number can apply and obtain a BankID. LeoVegas was founded in Sweden in 2011, so becoming a fully-legitimate iGaming service in its country of origin is not surprising.
