NetEnt and Whow Games Ink a Gaming Content Agreement

Premium supplier of digitally distributed gaming systems, NetEnt, inked a new partnership with Whow Games, European Gaming reported earlier today. The partnership facilitates access to the entire portfolio of games by NetEnt for Whow Games’ brands Jackpot.de or myJackpot.com.

In fact, with this move, the leading developer and publisher of online free-to-play social games, Whow Games, becomes Europe’s first social operator to offer the entire portfolio by NetEnt. Among the titles available for Whow Games we find the highly popular “Gonzo’s Quest” or “Starburst” brands. Those games as well as many other world-famous NetEnt games are going to be available free-to-play on Whow Games’ social casino platform.

Whow Games Strengthens Its Offering and International Appeal

In a statement, Philip Reisberger, CEO of Whow Games, revealed that the company is extremely happy to partner with a leading casino games provider such as NetEnt.
