The leading supplier of gaming solutions and content for the gambling sector, Aruze Gaming Global, operating under Empire Technological Group, announced a new senior-level appointment. On Wednesday this week, the company revealed it strengthened its C-suite by hiring the gaming industry expert, Betty Zhao. Joining the company, the seasoned executive was appointed for the role of Senior Vice President of International Operations.

Lauded as a strategic senior-level appointment, Aruze Gaming sought to propel its international growth with the help of Zhao. She brings extensive experience of nearly two decades within the gaming vertical. Throughout her extensive career, Zhao has held important senior-level roles, allowing her to accumulate unique skills and knowledge. Moreover, she is a proven leader with a track record.

Currently, Aruze Gaming Global powers the operations of gambling operators, delivering Class III and Class II electronic gaming machines, as well as table games. The company also specializes in table game technologies that stand out with reliability, innovation and speed. Ultimately, the technology offered by Aruze Gaming seeks to propel the growth of gaming operators across the globe while ensuring unrivaled experiences for their customers.

As the newly appointed Senior Vice President of International Operations shared her excitement in a statement. "I am truly honored to be part of Aruze Gaming Global," she said. Additionally, Zhao pointed out: "It is an exhilarating time in the gaming industry, and I look forward to contributing to the team that will shape its future worldwide."

Kelcey Allison, Aruze Gaming Global's Global Chief Operating Officer, was similarly delighted to see Zhao join the company's C-suite and said that the company is thrilled to welcome her to its growing team. "Her proven track record and history of accomplishments will play a pivotal role in executing Aruze Gaming Global’s International strategic plan," she added.

Finally, Allison said: "Betty’s focus will be to establish Aruze Gaming Global’s international segment as the premier provider of electronic gaming machines throughout the global gaming industry."

Before joining Aruze Gaming, Zhao was a part of LT Game. She first joined the company back in 2007 and most recently held the role of Chief Operating Officer. During her tenure at LT Game, Zhao was in charge of strategic operations and helped with the development of new products, while overseeing sales and marketing.

Her knowledge and experience made her an important asset for LT Game. Under her strategic guidance, the company grew to become a leading gaming supplier in Macau. Before LT Game, Zhao was a part of Johnson & Johnson Medical where she served as Product Specialist.
