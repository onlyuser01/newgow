2023 YellaWood 500 NASCAR Preview

Even though he’s not seen as the main favorite, William Byron has confidence on his side. He’s coming off a win in the Autotrader EchoPark Automotive 400, the result of which is that he’s now at the very top of the standings.

Speaking of standings, Denny Hamlin and Chris Buescher are just behind them, but they too aren’t seen as top favorites either. Instead, the role of the main Talladega favorite is shared by Brad Keselowski and Ryan Blaney.

The latter has a pretty good record on this track, even winning it once in 2019. Still, the man with the most success at Talladega in recent years is Joey Logano, who’s won this event twice in the last eight years.

Then, there’s last year’s winner, Chase Elliott, who’s still looking for his first win of 2023. He didn’t make it into the Round of 12, but the reason for that is that he missed seven races in the regular season.

YellaWood 500 Prediction

Anything can happen on October 1, but our vote goes to Denny Hamlin. The fact is that he’s one of the best drivers in NASCAR in 2023, if not the very best one. He’s also in the Round of 12, with title ambitions. Currently, he’s second in the standings but a win at Talladega would bring him to the top. We think that’s good enough motivation for him to go all-in on Sunday.

Plus, he seems to love driving at this racetrack, the fact being that he won it three years ago. Further, in last year’s race, Hamlin finished fifth.

Pick: Denny Hamlin to win the 2023 YellaWood race.
