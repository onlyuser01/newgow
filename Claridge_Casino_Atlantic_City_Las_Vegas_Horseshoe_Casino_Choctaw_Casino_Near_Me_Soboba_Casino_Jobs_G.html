Baltimore Ravens Believing in Christmas Miracles

The Ravens are 11-3 in the 2023 season with the best defense in the league, conceding just 16.1 points per game. This explains why John Harbaugh’s men have been able to defeat some of the league’s most productive offenses – Detroit Lions, Indianapolis Colts, etc.

When it comes to their losses, two of those happened against teams that are famously very good when it comes to defending – Pittsburgh Steelers and Cleveland Browns.

Their third loss was in their first game against the Colts, but you can think of it as a glitch. Not only did it happen early in the season, but the Colts beat them by a field goal after overtime.

Further, back then, Baltimore’s quarterback Lamar Jackson wasn’t playing as well as he’s been playing over the last couple of weeks. Although he did get an interception in each of his last two games, the 27-year-old quarterback recorded a total of four TD passes in those games.

Reasons Why NFL Odds are Favoring San Francisco 49ers

The Baltimore at San Francisco odds suggest the home team will have a relatively easy job on Christmas. That’s no surprise given that these guys have been fantastic throughout the 2023 season, winning 11 games already and losing just three.
