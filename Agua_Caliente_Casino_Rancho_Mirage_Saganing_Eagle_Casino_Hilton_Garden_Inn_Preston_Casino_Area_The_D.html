CT Interactive said that Hot Luck Jackpot is a best-in-class jackpot solution that is sure to engage players in Italy

CT Interactive, а major provider of gaming content, has announced that its games have been re-certified in Italy, allowing it to offer more of its products in the local market. The biggest development is that all of CT Interactive’s games linked to the Hot Luck Jackpot solution will now be available to local operators.

Hot Luck Jackpot is a three-level jackpot that provides consumers with extra chances to win money. The proprietary solution adds a Hot Luck scale on the right side of a slot game. The scale rises with each bet, until it reaches the top or one of the mystery jackpot levels (Minor, Major or Mega) is hit.

The solution, according to CT Interactive, is a great addition to operators’ portfolios and an efficient engagement tool. It allows gambling companies to set adjustable hit limits for each level, as well as adjustable bet limits. The solution is designed for online casinos and, according to CT Interactive, is sure to appeal to Italian customers.

The Supplier Is Certain Fans Will Welcome Hot Luck Jackpot

CT Interactive representatives released statements on the launch of the new tool in Italy, with Milena Tsankarska, head of business development EMEA, saying:

The Hot Luck Jackpot enables operators to deliver thrilling, personalized experiences that align with their customer retention strategies. The Jackpot mechanics give players the chance to win massive acquisitions while having a great time. Milena Tsankarska, head of business development EMEA, CT Interactive

Tsankarska added that her team is proud to have the opportunity to provide thrilling gaming experiences to iGaming enthusiasts in Italy. She called Hot Luck Jackpot “one of the best jackpot concepts” in the market and said that the solution has proven to be a hit among customers.

Meanwhile, Silvia Hristova, head of CRM at CT Interactive, said that her team aims to provide its partners with gaming experiences that will engage their fans. She added that Italy is a core market for the provider.

Our priority as a company is to enrich, improve, and deliver new and existing content to our partners around the world. We have set a goal for the future to keep expanding our footprints in Italy. Silvia Hristova, head of CRM, CT Interactive

True to Hristova’s words, CT Interactive recently grew its Italian footprint after launching with NetBet in Italy. In addition to that, the company penned deals with other prominent European operators, including Pascual Gaming in Belgium.
