“We look forward to expanding our relationship with the Bears and their dedicated fan base, and offering innovative products and promotions as we remain committed to delivering a first-rate customer experience. The agreement between the Bears and BetRivers comes less than a year after the NFL franchise signed a deal to designate another sportsbook operator, Australian-based PointsBet, as the club’s first sports betting sponsor. Also in April this year, BetRivers reached an agreement for a corporate partnership with Indy Eleven, a soccer franchise from Indiana participating in the United Soccer League (UCL), to become its official sports betting partner.
