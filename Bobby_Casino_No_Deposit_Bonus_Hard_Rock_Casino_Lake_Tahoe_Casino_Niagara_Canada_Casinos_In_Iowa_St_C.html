Flaming Skull Scatters, Free Games & Mask Wilds

As players keep going up the towering peaks, they will come across the flaming Skull Scatter symbols that will trigger 10 Free Spins for a minimum of three matching symbols. Provided the Free Games feature is engaged, players receive 10 more Free Spins.

In case a winning combination is revealed while playing the Free Spins round, players are rewarded with a Respin round. During this round, the winning symbols will stay in their original positions throughout the round.

Provided players score a new win during the Respins, the winning symbols are once again stuck to the reels together with the previously landed symbols.

Besides replacing the standard game symbols, Mask Wilds multiply any winning combination they are a part of by up to 16x. The game also lets players instantly engage in the Free Spins game using the dedicated Buy Feature that costs 70x the base bet for 10 Free Games.
