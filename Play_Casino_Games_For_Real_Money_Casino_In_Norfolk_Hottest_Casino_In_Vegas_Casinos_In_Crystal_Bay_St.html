Nedda Kaltcheva Joins Pronet Gaming as CTO

The leading global iGaming sportsbook solutions provider Pronet Gaming announced on Wednesday that it appointed Nedda Kaltcheva as its new Chief Technology Officer (CTO). The company is planning to strengthen its industry-leading technologies by significantly increasing the number of developers across all locations.

Moreover, the new appointment complements the opening of Pronet Gaming’s office in Sofia, Bulgaria. In her new position, Kaltcheva will be based primarily in that office. The company revealed that as a part of her duties, she will oversee Pronet’s ongoing expansion. Additionally, Pronet will benefit from Kaltcheva’s extensive experience as the company plans to remain at the forefront of the industry.

Before joining Pronet, Kaltcheva worked as a chief product and technology officer of international at William Hill for nearly a year. She was also employed by The Stars Group for nearly one year. Her professional experience includes a position as senior software engineer and consultant at Turner Broadcasting back in 2007. Kaltcheva has also worked as a senior software developer and consultant for companies such as VerizonWireless, BMW and ADP.
