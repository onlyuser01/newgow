Sapphire Gaming Joins RAW iGaming’s Family of Brands

Sapphire Gaming is already an author of innovative and immersive gaming experiences, namely the SuperSlice series which the studio developed. The company is also in the process of unveiling a new gaming engine that will boost the high added value of the gaming experience.

RAW iGaming CEO Tom Wood welcomed the opportunity to be expanding the company’s production capabilities by securing a partnership with a highly innovative albeit private studio. Wood, though, had no doubt about the reputation and expertise at Sapphire Gaming:

“I am so happy and proud to be able to work with one of the smartest, hidden, minds in the iGaming industry, Alec Horley, including his brilliant Sapphire team, which will continue their work in our new office based in the UK.” RAW iGaming CEO Tom Wood

RAW iGaming is keen to keep the Sapphire team intact and allow Alec Horley, the company’s current CEO, to continue to develop and operate on their own terms while benefiting from RAW’s added expertise where appropriate. The companies will be looking to develop new game mechanics and technologies as well as introduce the new aggregation platform by RAW to new customers.
