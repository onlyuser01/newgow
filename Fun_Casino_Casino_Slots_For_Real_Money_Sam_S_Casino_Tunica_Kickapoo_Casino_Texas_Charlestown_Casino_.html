Gates of Hell Free Spins Feature

The Gates of Hell Free Spins feature brings players seven Free Spins. During the spins, one of the symbols is revealed as the Sticky Wild for the remainder of the Free Spins round. The respective symbol then turns into a Sticky Wild on either the second, third, fourth, or fifth reels, substituting the rest of the symbols during the Free Spins. Coming across a minimum of four Sticky Wilds on the same reel rewards players with three more Free Spins.

High Voltage Free Spins

Players get 15 Free Spins for a chance to see the High Voltage Wild symbol pop on the second, third, fourth, or fifth reel. Once the symbol appears, it acts as a multiplier for adjacent wins between x11 and x66. Any Free Spin wins obtained with the High Voltage Wild are then multiplied by the multiplier. Landing at least three scatters while engaged in the High Voltage Free Spins feature brings players 15 Free Spins.
