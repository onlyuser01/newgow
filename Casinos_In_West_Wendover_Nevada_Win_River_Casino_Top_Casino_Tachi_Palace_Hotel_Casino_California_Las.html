The leading iGaming technology and content provider, Bragg Gaming, announced a new partnership with First Look Games, a leading platform that helps connect game developers with iGaming affiliates and publishers by delivering information and content

Details of the new collaboration emerged Monday and see Bragg Gaming become the latest partner of the leading platform. The new partnership will see Bragg benefit from the B2C promotion of its partner and in-house studios via affiliates.

Under the terms of the collaboration, First Look Games, which was founded back in 2018, will help the iGaming provider by distributing information and assets. As a result, in-house studios of the company such as Indigo Magic, Atomic Slot Lab and different partners powered by Bragg Gaming will be able to access more than 800 approved publishers and affiliates.

Per the terms of the new deal Bragg gaming will be able to upload videos, images, logos, as well as demo games to an online library. Then, First Look Games affiliates that are approved will be able to tap into this information and download individual details or bulk packages. This will help by developing traffic-generating content for the studios and helping expand the reach of their content. Ultimately, this data can reach an audience of more than 20 million players around the globe.

He explained that thanks to the deal with First Look Games, the company’s in-house and partner studios will expand the reach of its content and reach new audiences.

Finally, Mravlje said: “Competition for player attention is incredibly fierce among studios, and First Look Games will help us to be heard above the noise being made by our rivals and to drive awareness of the Bragg name, our collection of studios and the thrilling titles that we produce.”
