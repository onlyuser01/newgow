Riches in the Rough: Game Symbols and Payouts

The low-paying symbols in the game are represented by the standard cards marked from 9 to A. As expected, the high-paying symbols are represented by the different precious stones. Among them, the lowest-paying symbol is the yellow gemstone, followed by the emerald stone. To trigger a win, players must match at least three yellow gems. The rest of the gems will trigger a higher payout when matching at least a pair of symbols. An interesting thing about forming winning combinations in the game is the fact that those symbols that participate in a win will begin to glow, while the other symbols will become grey.

Sapphire symbols can also prove to be extremely lucrative when a minimum of five matching icons are seen landing simultaneously, bringing players 250x the bet line. Coming across five ruby gems at a time will generate a win of 300x the line bet.

The game also features a wild symbol used for generating even more thrilling winning combos and lucrative prizes. The wild symbols are represented by a rock symbol with two pickaxes behind it and the world WILD written on the icon using the gems’ colors. The wilds can be used to replace all symbols on the grid with the exception of the scatter symbols. The latter is represented by a mix of all four of the colored gems and they can pay in all positions. Landing five scatter symbols at a time will bring players a massive payout of 800x the line bet. Landing three scatters anywhere on the reels will trigger a prize consisting of eight up to 20 free spins.
