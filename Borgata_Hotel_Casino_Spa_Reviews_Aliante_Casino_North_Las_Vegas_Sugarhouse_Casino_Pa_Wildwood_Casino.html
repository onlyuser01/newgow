BetMakers to Offer Victorian Live Horse Content

According to the terms of the new deal, BetMakers will be allowed to include a feed consisting of Victorian horse races into its platform. The company’s chief executive officer Todd Buckingham announced BetMakers was delighted to sign the distribution deal with VTRIMB for Racing.com’s live horse racing vision. In exchange, he explained, this would help BetMakers make its state-of-the-art content available to a larger number of consumers while increasing interest in Victorian horse racing.
