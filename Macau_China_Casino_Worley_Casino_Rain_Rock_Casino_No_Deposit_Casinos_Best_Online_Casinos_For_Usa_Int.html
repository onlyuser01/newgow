Intelitics, a provider of scalable customer acquisition solutions for iGaming brands will expand its reach through a deal with the leading African sports and digital entertainment platform KingMakers.

Intelitics Will Boost BetKing’s Customer Acquisition

The agreement will see Intelitics use its critically-acclaimed customer acquisition program to support KingMakers’ wagering brand BetKing. The solutions provider will use its data-driven products and platform to help BetKing manage its affiliates and paid media activity. Furthermore, the latter will be able to closely track its players and follow its media campaigns in real-time.

BetKing’s affiliates will also benefit from the deal as they will receive access to real-time data and analytics which will help them to self-regulate their marketing campaigns.

This deal is crucial to Intelitics as it marks its foray into the African market. Previously, the solutions provider has mostly stuck to Europe and North America but is now eyeing the growth opportunities that await in the budding betting markets outside of the Western world. Intelitics has reliable partners and a strong foundation in its native markets, thanks to which it is well-poised for exploring new regions.

The Provider Is Excited by What Awaits in Africa

Neil Bothma, the chief executive officer of BetKing Nigeria, welcomed Intelitics, saying that as a platform provider capable of delivering insights in real-time, it will be an invaluable asset to the operator’s goals of maximizing customer acquisition. “This is a major deal for us, and we look forward to working closely with the Intelitics team,” Bothma explained
