Free Spins Bonus & Dream Drop Bonus

To initiate the Free Spin Bonus, players must land a minimum of three bonus symbols on random reels. All the diamonds that a player managed to collect up to that point will be used to fill a meter. The meter is used to award multiplied prizes up to 1,000x the bet during the Free Spins Bonus feature.

During the same feature, all extra bonus symbols landing on the reels will beef up the Diamond Collection prizes up to 10x, while offering an extra free spin. All the diamonds that a player scoops up during free spins are maintained between spins to trigger even bigger wins.

When at least five Dream Drop symbols are seen landing after a Jackpot Spin, players are sent into the Dream Drop Bonus. During this bonus round, players need to do their best to fill the rows with Dream Drop symbols. This will activate one of the five available jackpots in the game: Rapid, Midi, Maxi, Major, and Mega Jackpot. Their values start at €1 for the Rapid, €5 for the Midi, €100 for the Maxi, €25,000 for the Major, and €500,000 for the Mega jackpot.
