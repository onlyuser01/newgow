Aloha! Cluster Pays Reinvents Itself for the Christmas Holiday

NetEnt is giving its leading Aloha! Cluster Pays slot a Christmas makeover to vibe with the holiday season and give the well-established Hawaiian slot a new lease of life.

In the touched-up version, your favorite tropical slot has a distinct holiday theme attached to it, bringing Xmas to all NetEnt’s customers in the warmer climates. Conversely, the game will prove a real ray of iGaming sunshine to those caught in the snowy winter.

Aloha! Christmas, as this new iteration styles itself, is a 6-reel, 6-row video slot which comes with NetEnt’s bespoke Cluster Pays and Sticky Re-spins mechanics. The game will sport other signature moves by NetEnt, including Mystery and Multiplier Wild symbols, adding to the excitement of the game.

Holiday Season Celebrated in Fashion

To celebrate the holiday season, NetEnt is powering up the experience with Tiki Bar Free Spins and Tiki Bar Max Free Spins, which come as a source of extra rewards this holiday season. The game will offer 96% return-to-player (RTP) rate players can tap into and enjoy for a chance to escape the cold and warm themselves to some neat payouts!

Commenting on the product, Games Director at NetEnt Carl Ejlertsson has said that combining the well-loved features of the original game with some “twinkling twists” leads to a product that will appeal to players in new and exciting ways.

Aloha! Christmas is a top choice for players who are looking to have something fun to this holiday, Ejlertsson added.

Familiar Gameplay, Low Volatility

Aloha! Christmas is a laidback slot with low to medium volatility you may play on any budget. The game is optimized to run on HTML5 and any device, and has a free version. The slot retains much of its original look and feel, with Stacked Symbols and Multiplier Wild Symbols always readily available for new and exciting payouts.

The multipliers pack a powerful punch and vary from 2x to 7x, depending on the combinations. The Tiki Bar and Tiki Bar Max will bring you a varying number of free spins to top off the holiday experience.
