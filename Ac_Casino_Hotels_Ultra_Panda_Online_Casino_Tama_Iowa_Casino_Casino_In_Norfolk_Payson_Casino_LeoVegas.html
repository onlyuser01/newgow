LeoVegas’ New Deposit Limits

The personalized deposit limits have been added to LeoVegas’ British brands, which include 21.co.uk, BetUK, LeoVegas.com, and PinkCasino. The deposit limits will be assigned to bettors during their first deposit. They will use third-party data and a proprietary risk prediction model to determine an individually-personalized limit for every player. The limit will be recalculated constantly, keeping track of a user’s situation.

Additionally, bettors in the United Kingdom will continue to be incentivized to set their safe gambling preferences. LeoVegas will keep on using AI-backed messages that provide valuable advice to players who use one of the operator’s sites and stimulate them to make responsible and informed betting decisions.

“Personalized deposit limits and onsite messages are examples of new initiatives using AI and innovative technology to make gambling safer”, the announcement explained.

The AI solutions of LeoVegas don’t end there as the company will also use some such tools to spot bettors at risk of developing harmful gambling tendencies. The official update reads that the AI system will provide enhanced analysis of customers’ behavior and will automatically prevent risk customers from making new deposits.

Once the AI-powered system has marked those players, it will be up to LeoVegas’ professional customer care team to talk with the gamblers in question and help them to learn to control their iGaming behavior.
