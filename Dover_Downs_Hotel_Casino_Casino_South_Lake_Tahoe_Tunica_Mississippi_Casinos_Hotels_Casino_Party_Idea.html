The fast-growing provider of slots based in Berlin, Hölle Games, has announced it joined forces with the ZEAL Network, the leading online gambling company in Germany. The collaboration saw the iGaming provider agree to power ZEAL Network’s two German brands, Tipp24 and LOTTO24. Offering quality products, Hölle Games is currently the main and only supplier of games for the aforementioned online platforms.

Through the milestone agreement, players in Germany can enjoy Hölle Games’ quality titles that stand out with engaging features, crisp graphics and sound effects. Additionally, the developer’s titles cater to the needs of players in the country and are tailored in such a way as to appeal to German audiences.

Robert Lenzhofer, Hölle Games’ CEO, spoke about the strong presence of ZEAL Network in Germany. He explained that more than 1 million clients every month enjoy the services of the operator. Finally, Lenzhofer said that Hölle Games is thrilled to support the growth of ZEAL Network within the online gambling sector and enable more players to experience its popular and engaging games.

“With over 1 Million Monthly Active Users, ZEAL’s presence in Germany is unparalleled and we are proud to support ZEAL on their journey to dominate yet another iGaming-vertical, with our market-leading Slots-offering.“ Robert Lenzhofer, CEO at Hölle Games
