The game features the evergreen wild west theme. In True Wild, the reel is at the center of the screen while a colorful wild west village serves as the background of the new slot. Outlaw Bonus Round Can Bring Huge Prizes

The latest slot game from RubyPlay offers a Sliding Reel functionality. This is triggered whenever a player lands a Sheriff Badge symbol on the top or middle position of the reel. “The star locks and slides down one position for each spin, providing players with an increased opportunity to trigger the Outlaw feature,” explained the game developer.

To activate the Outlaw round, players need to land six or more stars on the reel. Consequently, a special bonus round is activated and players receive three spins. The appearance of new Sheriff Badge symbols resets the respin to three until the reel is filled with those symbols or there are no more free spins.

Players that are lucky to fill the reel with Sheriff Badge symbols can then unlock a special bonus game on its left side. This bonus can unlock multipliers, as well as one out of the four jackpots True Wild offers. The jackpots include Mini, Minor, Major and Grand, elevating further the excitement of the gameplay.
