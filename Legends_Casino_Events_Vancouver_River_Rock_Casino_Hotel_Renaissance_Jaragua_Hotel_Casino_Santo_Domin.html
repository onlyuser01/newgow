Enter the Aztec Temple and Find the Horde of Stolen Treasures

As the game is initiated, players are sent straight deep into the lush jungle hiding an Aztec temple where stolen treasures are waiting to be revealed. The gaming grid is set at the core of this scenery, overlooked by the serpents carved in stone.

Players who land two special coins will set the snake’s eyes on fire while being granted a full Wild Reel. With a minimum of three bonus reels, more fuel is added to the fire, letting players unlock a Free Spins bonus as well as the fixed Wild Reels as soon as the two coins are collected.

Any random spin can trigger jackpot spins. During these spins, only Dream Drop symbols and blanks are seen landing on the grid. Coming across five Dream Drop symbols DD at a time will engage the Dream Drop Bonus.

At the end of each new spin, the block symbols are swept off the grid and the Dream Drop symbols are dropped to the bottom, staying in play. The spins will go on until the Dream Drop symbols completely fill up one of the reels. In turn, this will reward players with one of the five available jackpot prizes: Mega, Major, Maxi, Midi, and Rapid.

As explained by Relax Gaming’s chief product officer, Shelley Hannah, similar to their other Dream Drop titles, the new release is expected to be “yet another fantastic addition” to their rich portfolio consisting of over 4,000 casino games.
