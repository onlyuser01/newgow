Ontario: a Key Market in Blueprint Gaming’s Growth

According to the new deal, the company that owns and operates NorthStar Bets will see Blueprint eventually launch its entire portfolio of slots for its players in Ontario. Currently, gamers can enjoy a series of popular games from the studio, including The Goonies, Fishing Frenzy: The Big Catch, Ted, Eye of Horus Megaways, and Wolf Legend Megaways.

Other games part of the current offering include Deal or No Deal, a gameshow title that has already attracted a large audience in the region since its launch, together with another industry-leading title, Jackpot King. The latter is a progressive jackpot system that has been rebranded as Jackpot Royale and is fully customized to match the requirements of the Ontario market. The Ontario-based brand will this way continue to provide its players with a one-of-a-kind, local, top-tier user experience on its casino platform while reaching a more diversified audience eager to get access to content tailored to the Canadian market.

Blueprint Gaming’s director of key accounts and marketing UK Jo Purvis called Ontario a key market that has proven to be critical for their “growth across regulated markets in 2022”. Purvis added that the new partnership with NorthStar Bets represents an essential factor in their expansion across the North American region as it would help them place their slots portfolio into the hands of an “impressive swathe of new players”.
