“The Plaza is continually reinventing the hotel and gaming experience in downtown Las Vegas.“ Sue Ascanio, general manager at Plaza Hotel and Casino

The New General Manager Brings Decades of Industry Experience

Ascanio graduated from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. She holds a Bachelor of Science in hotel administration. This solid education undoubtedly helped her in her career which spans more than 30 years within the gaming and hospitality industry. In addition to licensed commercial gaming, Ascanio has experience within the tribal gaming sector. As an expert in the field, she has a proven track record in delivering success for operators and leading large teams.

Before joining the Plaza Hotel and Casino, Ascanio was a part of River Rock Casino in California. During her two-year tenure with the operator, she held the position of general manager. For nearly eight years until October 2018, Ascanio was also a part of Pinnacle Entertainment where she held a VP and general manager position. Her professional background includes two years as VP and assistant general manager at Penn National Gaming’s Hollywood Casino Lawrenceburg. Moreover, Ascanio was a part of Station Casinos for nearly 12 years where she held different leadership roles.
