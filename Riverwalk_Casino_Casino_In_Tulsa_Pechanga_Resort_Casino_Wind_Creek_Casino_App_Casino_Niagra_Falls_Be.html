Real Money vs Play Money Poker

Before starting to play online poker with friends it is important to determine whether you would like to create a friendly online tournament or play a cash game.

Organizing a friendly online poker tournament can be very exciting, especially if you have more friends who like the competitive side of the game. Depending on the platform which is picked, either real money or play money can be used within the tournament.

Whoever creates the tournament can also set up a starting date, time, buy-in as well as the number of seats. Most of these things you will discuss beforehand as a group before moving in on the real action.

Although a tournament can be fun, players who don’t have a lot of poker experience may find it difficult to level up, or even bust early.

This brings us to the next option – cash games. Cash games may be more appealing for inexperienced players simply because they can buy in whenever they are bust.

This applies especially to play-money games, because, if a player runs out of money, they can easily re-enter the game and the fun continues. Playing online poker cash games with friends may also be good for players who are still learning. If play money is involved in the cash game, inexperienced players can learn strategies, or even try bluffing without the fear of losing a dime.
