PlayStar Adopts the Xpoint Verify Solution

Xpoint agreed to power one of its partners, the iGaming supplier PlayStar, with the Xpoint Verify brand. This will provide PlayStar with a robust geolocation solution and will simultaneously expand Xpoint’s presence in the US market.

Xpoint, which is PlayStar’s official geolocation partner, will continue helping the iGaming tech provider remain compliant with local regulations while providing its customers with safe betting experiences.

As described by Xpoint, Xpoint Verify is a geolocation validator software that allows the company’s partners to validate GPS position, connections and device information at lightning-fast speeds. In addition, Xpoint Verify helps operators comply with local regulations by detecting and preventing fraud. Lastly, the solution’s insights help partner companies devise new growth strategies.

Hellberg believes that PlayStar and Xpoint share a lot of similarities. He described the two companies as young disruptors that are about to enter a kind of a David versus Goliath battle against their respective competitors.
