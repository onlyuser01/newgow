Phoenix Forge review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around the mythical Phoenix as well as a host of treasures.

Players will come upon a secret hall of jewels and gold. As they explore the world of priceless artifacts, a phoenix will be their guide, her feathers glowing with flame.

Fantasy lovers will adore this colourful game and feel as though they have joined an epic quest. With a little luck, they'll manage to snag their share of the precious gems.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack creates tension with climactic notes and drums. Players will feel as though they are adventurers who have reached the live-or-die point of a story.

The gems are animated with impressive detail from lush sapphires to luminous rubies. Each stone seems to be expertly cut and framed with an ornately-engraved setting.

The phoenix symbol is animated creatively with flame-like feathers and piercing blue eyes. She seems more than worthy of being the game’s namesake.

Behind the reels, players will see heaps of gold coins and chests of treasure. This creates the perfect atmosphere as players explore the hidden world.

Overall, the soundtrack and graphics work together to create an engaging fantasy vibe.

Gameplay

Phoenix Forge is a five reel, three row slot, with 20 win lines.

The Cascade feature means that winning symbols vanish after payouts, creating open positions for new symbols to fall in.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

The Bonus symbol can appear on any reel and when players land 3 or more, this triggers the Free Spin feature with 10 free spins. Landing 4 Bonus symbols awards 15 free spins, and 5 Bonus symbols secures 20 free spins.

Bonus symbols also pay in any position as follows:

3 Bonus symbols = x2 total bet

4 Bonus symbols = x5 total bet

5 Bonus symbols = x20 total bet

The Hot Spot Multiplier feature means that after every win and cascade, a cumulative multiplier is added to the wins.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.51%, above our average yardstick of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is high, which makes it a great match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.
