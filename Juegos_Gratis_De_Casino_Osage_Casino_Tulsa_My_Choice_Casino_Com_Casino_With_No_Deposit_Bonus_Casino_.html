Konami Gaming, Konami’s gambling supplier arm, has minted a new deal with Pariplay, one of the world’s biggest content providers and aggregators. Under the agreement, Konami Gaming will provide content to Pariplay’s renowned Pariplay Fusion platform.

Pariplay Will Distribute Konami Content

Konami is a big name when it comes to both video games and gambling content. The company has supplied operators with slot machines and gaming enterprise management systems for the last two decades and is known for its dedication to innovation and quality. Konami is also known for its attention to detail and unparalleled originality when it comes to games.

Per the deal with Pariplay, Konami will provide the aggregator with its online portfolio of popular land-based casino titles. The content will be integrated into Pariplay’s Fusion platform, allowing the aggregator’s partners to access a variety of Konami games. Thanks to this, gambling operators will be able to engage players with Konami’s rich selection of classic and innovative casino games.

The Fusion platform is a leading aggregation solution that is currently used by over 150 operators in the industry. It offers more than 14,000 games from over 80 renowned gaming suppliers. In addition, Pariplay Fusion offers a complete selection of player retention tools that allow companies to keep their customers.

Thanks to the agreement with Pariplay, Konami will be able to greatly expand its global footprint and launch its games with various operators across dozens of markets.

The Agreement will Help Konami Reach More Players

Konami Gaming’s president and chief executive officer, Steve Sutherland, commented on the new agreement. He praised the Pariplay Fusion platform for its expansive reach and said that the current deal would be a great way for Konami to “get its content in front of broader player audiences.”
