Push Gaming, a leading B2B gaming supplier, has announced an expansion of its partnership with Kindred Group plc, one of the top-tier operators in the industry.

Push Gaming’s Crystal Catcher Launches Exclusively with Kindred in Dutch Market

This partnership marks a significant commercial step forward for Push Gaming in the Dutch market, where its entire portfolio of games will now be available to Kindred’s customers. Popular titles like Giga Jar, Retro Tapes, Big Bamboo, Jammin’ Jars, and Razor Shark are just a few of the games that will be available to Dutch players.

Crystal Catcher, Push Gaming’s newest slot game, will be available exclusively to Kindred customers across all markets for a period of three months. The game incorporates a unique feature of a moving wild multiplier that increases wins during the base game and activates the free spins round. During the free spins round, multipliers remain in place, elevating the chances of winning big. After the three-month exclusivity period with Kindred Group, Crystal Catcher will become available to the wider network from June 20.

The exclusivity of Crystal Catcher will boost Push Gaming’s prominence across Kindred brands globally while also supporting its growth in the key Dutch market. The deal exemplifies Push Gaming’s continued expansion across regulated markets with additional partnerships scheduled in the coming months.
