BetMGM players in Michigan will be able to try out Atlantic Digital’s The Godfather-themed slot

Online gambling powerhouse BetMGM has teamed up with Atlantic Digital, a game studio based in London. Under the agreement between the two parties, BetMGM will pioneer the supplier’s games in the United States.

The deal will see BetMGM as the exclusive home of Atlantic Digital’s portfolio of cutting-edge games. These include the highly popular The Godfather slot, which, as the name suggests, is based on Coppola’s classic.

As a result of BetMGM’s tie-up with Atlantic Digital, players in Michigan will be able to try out the exciting game. Launches in more states are planned for later dates.

The Godfather is one of the all-time classics in the movie industry. Its slot adaptation reflects the atmosphere of the original movie, allowing fans to remember what sets the original story apart from many other movies. Enjoyment is further enhanced by Atlantic Digital’s exciting engagement mechanics that allow operators to keep players on the edge of their seats.

The Iconic Slot Will Engage Players in Michigan

Atlantic Digital’s chief executive officer, Sam Hobcraft, said that his team is proud to join forces with BetMGM and launch games in the US market. He teased that the launch of The Godfather in Michigan is just the start of what Atlantic Digital has planned.

We at Atlantic Digital are at the forefront of this movement, and, working alongside BetMGM, we will be bringing the highest quality IP online to the US. We couldn’t be more excited about the potential of this partnership and what it could mean for US customers for many years to come. Sam Hobcraft, CEO, Atlantic Digital

BetMGM’s director of gaming, Oliver Bartlett, noted that The Godfather is one of the most iconic movies of all time. He is certain that his company’s customers in the US will enjoy the new game. Speaking about the partnership, Bartlett said that BetMGM is delighted to form a partnership with Atlantic Digital.

We are always looking to partner with brands our customers love and game studios that are creating new and engaging content. Oliver Bartlett, director of gaming, BetMGM
