Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all regular symbols and has the special property of contributing a multiplier to winning combinations. When a greater number of Wilds are part of the winning combination, the multiplier increases as follows:

2 Wilds per winning combination = 4x multiplier.

3 Wilds per winning combination = 8x multiplier.

4 Wilds per winning combination = 16x multiplier.

5 Wilds per winning combination = 32x multiplier.

Players are awarded a spin on a Multiplier Wheel when they land matching symbols lined up on a reel. The Multiplier Wheel awards multipliers between 2x and 10x which are applied to the win that triggered the Multiplier Wheel spin.

Multiplier wheel is awarded for lined up symbols on reels

The Stacked Reel Respin feature is triggered when players land matching symbols on reels 1 and 2. The first two reels then remain in position as the remaining reels respin.
