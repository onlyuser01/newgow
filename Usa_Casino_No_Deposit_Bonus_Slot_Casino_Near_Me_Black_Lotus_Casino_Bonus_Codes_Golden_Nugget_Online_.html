The Accreditation Was Acquired on a Voluntary Basis

The RG Check accreditation certifies online gaming sites that have the necessary tools to address and prevent harm related to gambling. Although this accreditation is not mandatory in the US, where the majority of Barstool’s operations take place, the company chose to voluntarily participate in the process to demonstrate its commitment to social responsibility.

Shelley White, CEO of RGC, extended her congratulations to PENN Interactive for attaining the RG Check accreditation for its US-based sites. According to White, this achievement reinforces the company’s dedication to protecting players and making responsible gambling a priority in its operations.

White continued by expressing that the RGC is satisfied to continue supporting PENN Interactive’s efforts to minimize harm across different jurisdictions.

The Responsible Gambling Council developed the RG Check program in collaboration with policymakers, global gambling providers, players, and individuals who have experienced harm from gambling.
