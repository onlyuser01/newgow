Xace Names Annie Osborne as CCO

Fast-growing account and payment provider, Xace announced today that it has appointed its new chief commercial officer. The position is going to be occupied by the industry veteran Annie Osborne. In her position as CCO, Osborne is going to be responsible for shaping and executing the company’s commercial sales and marketing strategies. Furthermore, she will be in charge of product development and customer experience. Osborne is going to report directly to David Hodkinson, CEO at Xace.

“Xace provides a quality solution to a common problem that gaming companies across Europe encounter and this has seen us ride an incredible wave of growth in our first year.” David Hodkinson, Xace CEO

In a statement, Hodkinson shared his excitement by saying that he is delighted to welcome Osborne to Xace’s team. According to him, Osborne’s tremendous knowledge and experience are going to help the company as it continues to expand rapidly.

Furthermore, Hodkinson said that the company has enjoyed incredible success since its launch last year, thanks to the quality solution services it provides. He added that having Osborne as CCO is undoubtedly going to help the company have another successful year.
