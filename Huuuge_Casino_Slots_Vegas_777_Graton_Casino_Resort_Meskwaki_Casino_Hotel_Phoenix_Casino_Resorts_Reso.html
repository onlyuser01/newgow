The NeoGames SA subsidiary has teamed up with the prominent Swedish gaming group, which will benefit from Pariplay’s extensive aggregated and original content

Moving forward Betsson will have access to Pariplay’s original products delivered through Wizard Games, and more than 14,000 other titles integrated as part of the Fusion platform and delivered by dozens of suppliers.

These third-party games will be integrated with Betsson’s existing operations in regulated markets, including in places such as Sweden, Denmark, and Spain, and other places of interest for the group where it has licenses in Europe.

The partnership marks another opportunity for Pariplay as the company seeks to continue growing its footprint in regulated markets. Betsson will provide the company’s full catalog of titles and integrate them along with a number of player engagement solutions designed to have an immediate impact on results and performance.

Pariplay VP of Sales Andrew Maclean said that his company was constantly trying to grow in important markets across Europe, which will now be possible through the alliance with Betsson.

Strengthening Breadth of Content with Fusion
