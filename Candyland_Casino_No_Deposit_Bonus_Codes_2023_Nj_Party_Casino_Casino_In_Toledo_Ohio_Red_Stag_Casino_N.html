Filotimo Casino Opens Third Location in New Hampshire

DraftKings, the sports betting and daily fantasy leader, announced on Monday that it expanded its collaboration with Filotimo Casino and Restaurant in New Hampshire. The new deal builds upon successful collaboration between the duo dating back to last year.

It was back in August 2020 when DraftKings Sportsbook opened at The Brook in Seabrook, while another venue opened doors at Filotimo in Manchester in September. Now, Filotimo announced that it opened another retail location in Dover. The new DraftKings Sportsbook at Filotimo in Dover is located at 887 Central Avenue in Rollinsford. The new venue effectively becomes the third retail sportsbook location in New Hampshire.

The new DraftKings location in Dover will offer sports betting kiosks. Additionally, the venue will feature 11 TVs and visitors can also enjoy food and beverages, as well as a sports bar which undoubtedly boosts the atmosphere. A bowling alley, arcade, games of chance and bingo, as well as charitable gaming, are also available for sports fans that visit the new location.

On weekdays, the new venue will open from 11:30 AM to a half-hour following the last football game. Similarly, on the weekends, the venue will operate from 10:00 AM until a half-hour following the conclusion of the last football game.
