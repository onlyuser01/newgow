The leading sports betting and iGaming operator, BetMGM, announced a new partnership with the Nashville Predators, a professional ice hockey team from Nashville, Tennessee, competing in the National Hockey League (NHL).

Nashville Predators Ink Deal with BetMGM

The new multi-year partnership announced Monday designates BetMGM as an official sports betting partner of the NHL team. Thanks to the collaboration, BetMGM will benefit from prominent signage at the Bridgestone Arena, the Nashville Predators’ home arena. The brand will benefit from exposure via dasher boards and on-ice logo during Nashville Predators games.

But the new collaboration will deliver exciting experiences for sports fans as well, BetMGM revealed. Via the new multi-year agreement, the sportsbook plans to open a branded restaurant, as well as a bar at the Bridgestone Arena’s suite level. Those amenities will further elevate the experience for Predators fans, as well as sports fans.

“We are always looking for ways to enhance SMASHVILLE and bring a better experience to the best fans in sports.“ Sean Henry, president and CEO of the Nashville Predators

Sean Henry, Nashville Predators’ president and CEO, said that the team is continuously looking for new ways to deliver exciting experiences for its fans. He explained that the new partnership with BetMGM builds upon that hype and acknowledged it represents a unique opportunity. Finally, Henry said that the team is looking forward to the success the new collaboration will bring.
