Kalamba Games has been able to enter the Ontario iGaming market through a partnership with Bragg Gaming Group, significantly expanding its presence in important jurisdictions in North America. Thanks to this tie-up, Kalamba is able to deliver a number of award-winning and high-performing games to Canadian customers.

Kalamba Games Takes on Ontario’s iGaming Market

The content will be distributed locally through Bragg’s Remote Games Server (RGS) through ORYX Gaming and Spin Games. Bragg has already been present on the market for several months, providing local operators with third-party games in the province. Bragg obtained its license in April when Ontario launched regulated iGaming officially.

Kalamba is appreciative of the opportunity to be present in the Ontario market as it allows the company to quickly expand its reach and deliver its products to established operators, and through there – to players interested in exploring Kalamba’s original content.

Kalamba is confident that its product offering is becoming one of the best solutions on the market, with the company gaining a significant foothold across key regions. The latest partnership with Bragg Gaming in Ontario is just a continuation of this strategy.

Kalamba also leverages powerful technological solutions such as BullsEye, a dedicated in-house RGS, and Stargazer, an analytics platform. Both are designed to ensure that players get the best possible experience and that operators are able to leverage the content in the best possible way.
