ComeOn Group, the award-winning iGaming operator, announced two new strategic sponsorship deals. On Monday, the company revealed it renewed its sponsor deals with Sliema Wanderers FC and Sliema Aquatic Sports Club (ASC), two Malta-based sports clubs. The latest collaboration further complements the growth of the company in the region that is close to its Malta headquarters.

Under the terms of the new sponsorship agreements, ComeOn Group and the two sports teams will now collaborate for another successful year. The trio agreed to deliver exciting projects, highlighting their successful partnership and bringing community benefits.

As noted, the renewed sponsorships build upon a previous deal announced by ComeOn and the two clubs. That initial deal kicked off last year with an engaging video. Now, under the terms of the latest sponsorships, the company and the two sports teams will support an active lifestyle. Additionally, the trio will focus on boosting wellbeing and mental health, which highlights the importance of the renewed partnerships.

Rupert Perry, Sliema Wanderers FC's Club President, commented on the topic in a statement. "We are immensely grateful for the unwavering support of our valued sponsor," he explained. Moreover, Perry acknowledged the company's commitment to the club's mission. "As we look ahead to the future, we are excited to continue this journey together, creating lasting impact and achieving new heights of excellence," he noted in conclusion.

Frank Testa, Sliema ASC's Club President, added that the club is delighted to renew its partnership with ComeOn Group. "Without the involvement of such organizations it would not be possible for clubs like ours to continue to work towards achieving our targets," he pointed out. Last but not least, Testa explained: "As we move into a new era with our excellent prospects, we are delighted to continue the journey with ComeOn."

Daniela Vella, ComeOn's Chief Operating Officer, revealed that Sliema is a location that is very close to the company's offices. According to her, through partnerships with the sports clubs, the company was able to uncover synergies that are expected to bring success for everyone involved.

In conclusion, Vella said: "Both teams tie in well with our values, especially the Run Together The Group is also focused on giving back to the communities with its CSR strategy being a key, strategic pillar and sports plays a vital role in nurturing teamwork, and leadership skills."

Constantly expanding, ComeOn Group currently has more than 560 employees from 53 different nationalities across the globe. Not unexpectedly, the company's headquarters represent a majority of its employees. Currently, 50% of ComeOn's employees are female.
