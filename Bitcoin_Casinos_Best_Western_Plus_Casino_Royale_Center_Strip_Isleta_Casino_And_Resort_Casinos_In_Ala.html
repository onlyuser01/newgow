Manufacturer of video games and consumer electronics Atari announced today the company entered into partnership with the Litecoin Foundation, to help facilitate the use of Litecoin across its gaming platforms, as well as with other potential ventures, including joint merchandising.

The Litecoin Foundation is a non-profit organization with the main purpose to promote awareness of the decentralized cryptocurrency Litecoin, as well as highlighting its advantages to encourage its wider use. Litecoin is a fully secure peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that enables instant payments with almost zero costs to any location around the world, without needing a third party.
