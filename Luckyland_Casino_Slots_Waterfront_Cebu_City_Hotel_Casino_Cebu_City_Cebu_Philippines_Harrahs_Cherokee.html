Bringing a Quality Product to the World, Complying with Regulation

GATI is a pre-configured solution that allows participating companies to quickly tap into standardized technology solutions and develop or distribute games in the markets covered by the product.

In other words, YG Masters allows partners to reach new audiences, facilitate onboarding and solve any outstanding regulatory wrinkles in a quick and efficient way, boosting revenues in the process.

More importantly, GATI operates under DAN, Yggdrasil’s Decentralized Aggregation Network, opening developers access to every operator in the network who wants to purchase the slots and add them to their own portfolio.
