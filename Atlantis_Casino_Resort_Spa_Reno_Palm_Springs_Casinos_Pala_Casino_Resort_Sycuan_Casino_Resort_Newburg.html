NetBet said that its new brand ambassador’s qualities make him the perfect ambassador in a year full of huge sporting events

Ray Parlour, a former soccer professional and sports radio pundit, has joined the leading operator NetBet as its latest UK brand ambassador. Under the new agreement, Parlour will promote NetBet’s brand throughout the 2024 season.

NetBet pointed out that Parlour is the first soccer ambassador it has partnered with in recent years. The company praised his extensive fanbase across the United Kingdom and lauded him as an expert with a profound understanding of the soccer, horse racing and golf sectors.

The leading operator emphasized that its new brand ambassador’s qualities make him the “perfect ambassador in a year full of huge sporting events.”

Parlour is a former Arsenal player who participated in over 400 appearances for the club and was a part of the Invincibles squad that won the 2002/03 Premier League title without losing a game. Also known as Romford Pele, Parlour is a seasoned veteran who has won three Premier League titles, four FA Cups, a League Cup and a UEFA Cup Winners’ Cup.

As NetBet’s ambassador, Parlour will feature in a variety of activations, promoting the operator’s brand. He will reflect the gambling company’s desire to engage sports fans with fun and interactive, yet responsible gaming experiences.
