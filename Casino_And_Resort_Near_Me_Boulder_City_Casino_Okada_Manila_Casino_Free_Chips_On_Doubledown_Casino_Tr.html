Betegy to Add Value to FTN Network’s Offering

The companies are going to give a flying start to their collaboration by adding Betegy’s expertise to the FTN show, “Betting with Benny and Tino,” which aims to educate sports fans about different sports and introduce them to different value-betting opportunities.

FTN Networks COO Eliot Crist has touted the partnership as an opportunity for the company to leverage Betegy’s extensive technological offer and improve the company’s own media platform. This way, consumers will have betting-oriented fantasy content for a one-of-a-kind experience. Betegy CEO Alex Kornilov added:

“This important partnership with the outstanding team at FTN represents a key building block in our ongoing development of the definitive bridge between raw data and sports viewers, gaming enthusiasts, and bettors.” Betegy CEO Alex Kornilov
