3 Oaks Gaming, a popular distributor of casino content and marketing tools, has minted a content distribution deal with the gambling operator PIN-UP Ukraine

Under the agreement, 3 Oaks Gaming will power the operator with casino games.

3 Oaks to Provide PIN-UP with Games

As announced, the supplier will provide PIN-UP with its rich suite of gaming titles, including both fan-favorite games and new releases. For example, the Ukrainian operator will now be able to engage its fans with hits such as Sticky Piggy, Rio Gems: Hold and Win and Eggs of Gold, among others.

The agreement will help 3 Oaks Gaming further expand its European footprint. The company has been quite busy in recent times, releasing an abundance of new games and striking strategic agreements with operators.

In December, the company released Hit More Gold, a slot exploring the classic theme of gold mining. Earlier that same month, 3 Oaks joined St8.io a Curacao-based aggregator, in a deal that further helped popularize the studio’s content. A week prior, the distributor also penned a content deal with the iGaming platform provider Technamin, providing it with games like Big Heist, Sticky Piggy and The King of Heroes.

As noted by the distributor, the latest agreements attest to its ability to deliver engaging high-quality slots and proven promotional tools to its clients.

This Is the Agreement PIN-UP Had Been Looking for

Sebastian Damian, 3 Oaks Gaming’s managing director, spoke about the agreement with PIN-UP. According to him, the integration with the operator is a great way to kick off the year. The MD noted that the company continues to seek expansion into new markets.

The studio is ambitious on becoming a well-known name in gaming, which is why it has been working hard expand its footprint.

Here at 3 Oaks, there is no doubt that we are becoming more recognized in the industry for the quality of our offering, and this latest deal only strengthens our reputation. Sebastian Damian, MD, 3 Oaks Gaming

Meanwhile, Igot Zotko, PIN-UP’s chief operating officer, said that his company realizes the need to engage players with the best games the iGaming market has to offer. That’s why PIN-UP is happy to expand its portfolio with 3 Oaks’ slots. According to Zotko, this agreement is exactly the partnership his team has been looking for.

This will definitely be a hugely beneficial deal for both parties, and we look forward to fantastic success with 3 Oaks Gaming. Igot Zotko, COO, PIN-UP
