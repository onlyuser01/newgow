Dynamic game provider BGaming has managed to receive a license to operate in the Romanian gambling market. The company believes that this is an important step in the company’s international journey.

BGaming just announced that it has received a Class II license from Romania’s National Gambling Office, the country’s official gambling regulator. As a result, the company will be able to make a foray into this new market and expand the reach of its products.

BGaming is delighted to announce that the studio has been granted a Class II license from Romanian National Gambling Office. It is a significant decision for BGaming, allowing it to boost its international reach and deliver its products to the Romanian market. BGaming statement

According to BGaming, this new license will allow local gambling operators to access the provider’s huge variety of iGaming products. Some of these include hit slot games, including the beloved Elvis Frog franchise, Wild Cash and Wild Cashx9999.

This means that thousands of players in the Eastern European country will be able to have a taste of the provider’s critically-acclaimed titles. This will bring tons of engagement opportunities for local companies and will help BGaming greatly expand its presence in the region.

The Company Is Thrilled to Go Live in Romania

Marina Ostrovtsova, CEO at BGaming spoke about the new license. She said that the company is delighted by the opportunity to resume its business in Romania and capitalize on growth opportunities in the Eastern European region.

Ostrovtsova added that BGaming’s top-tier content matches all of Romania’s standards and added that she is looking forward to seeing the provider’s games launch with the various Romanian operators.

BGaming’s content matches all the country standards, and we’re delighted to provide high-quality games for local operators and thousands of Romanian players. Marina Ostrovtsova, CEO, BGaming
