The Dog House Multihold review

Theme and Storyline

The Dog House Multihold is themed around a neighborhood of whimsical dogs.

Players will wander down a whole street where they’ll meet a variety of canine friends. Each adorable pup has a unique personality and plenty of treats to share.

This slot will naturally appeal to dog-lovers and those in the mood for a cheerful atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The upbeat soundtrack is blended with the sound of friendly dog barking. Players will feel as if they are surrounded by friendly dogs as they play.

The reels themselves are built into a large dog house, giving a nod to the slot’s name. In the little wooden house, there are colorful letter symbols, dog collars, and enticing bones.

Players will also find a range of dog characters among the symbols. The gang includes a Yorkshire Terrier in a pink bow, a pug with a silly smile, and a charming rottweiler with his tongue lolling out of his mouth. Animated in a vibrant cartoon style, each pup has a unique personality.

Behind the reels, there is a sunny suburban scene complete with white picket fences and green lawns. A blue sky and dainty flowers suggest balmy spring weather.

Together, the soundtrack and graphics create a lively world of mischievous canine fun.

Gameplay

The Dog House Multihold is a five-reel, three-row slot with 20 win lines.

The gameplay has a swift pace, ensuring that players will remain engaged.

The maximum win for The Dog House Multihold is 9,000x the player’s total bet.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol appears on reels 2-4 and substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol. The multiplier associated with a Wild applies to any wins that Wild contributes to. If more than one Wild is included in a winning combination, the multipliers are added together and then applied to the win.

The Free Spins feature is triggered when players land 3 Bonus symbols on reels 1, 3, and 5. This feature is played on a set of 4 matrices, starting with the first matrix unlocked. Players can unlock additional matrices by landing 3 Scatter symbols during the feature. Every time a matrix is unlocked, players are awarded a randomly selected number of free spins.

Throughout the Free Spins feature, Wilds remain sticky whenever they land in any matrix. If two matrices are unlocked simultaneously, additional free spins are awarded twice.

Players can buy directly into the Free Spins feature if they wish.
