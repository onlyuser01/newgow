Fulham are Punching Above Their Weight in 2022-23 Premier League

The Cottagers played in the Championship last season and were one of the top candidates for relegation before the start of the 2022-23 season. However, after six rounds of the Prem, Fulham are sitting in the middle of the table with a 2-2-2 record (nine points) and a score differential of 9-9.

What’s interesting is that both of those losses happened on the road, against teams that are way richer and stronger than them. Fulham lost 2-1 both to Arsenal and Tottenham. When it comes to the two games they drew, one was against Wolves on the road; the other one was against Liverpool at home.

Speaking of Fulham’s home results, this team is still without a loss at Craven Cottage. Apart from drawing with Liverpool, they also beat Brentford and Brighton at home.

In each of those games, Aleksandar Mitrovic was their crucial player. The big Serbian has six goals to his name in the Prem so far. Chelsea players know this, which is why we can expect them to be more conservative in Saturday’s games, putting more resources on handling Mitrovic.

Chelsea Needs to Pull Themselves Together ASAP

Since Round 1 when they beat Everton 1-0, Chelsea hasn’t kept a clean sheet. We’re not just talking about the Premier League, but the Champions League as well. Last Tuesday, they lost 1-0 against Dinamo Zagreb, which prompted the Chelsea bosses to sack Tuchel.

The Blues really need to do better in the defense, although their offense has been underachieving as well. However, when it comes to Chelsea’s attack, they had an excuse as until recently, they didn’t have a natural striker on the roster. However, Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang is now on the squad, ready to help the Blues improve their attacking yield.

Fulham vs Chelsea Prediction

Anthony Barry (or whoever gets in Chelsea’s hot seat on Saturday) will probably want to make sure his team doesn’t get embarrassed again. Rather than rushing toward the opposition goal at any cost, the Blues are more likely to play more cautiously.

As a result, they probably won’t concede too many goals to Fulham. However, we probably also won’t see them score a lot. That said, no one should be surprised if the game finishes in a draw.
