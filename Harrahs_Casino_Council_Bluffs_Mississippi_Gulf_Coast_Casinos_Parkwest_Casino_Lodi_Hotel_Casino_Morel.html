Wolf Sistemas is a reliable provider of white-label sports betting platforms that recently expanded into iGaming

Salsa Technology, an expert in iGaming solutions, has signed another content provision agreement, this time with Wolf Sistemas, a Brazilian platform provider. As per the deal, the former company will leverage its Salsa Gator aggregation solution to level up its new partner’s online casino offering.

The agreement will see Salsa Technologies provide Wolf Sistemas with content that meets the expectations of its gaming partners. The Salsa Gator aggregation division sports a rich selection of gaming content from Salsa’s in-house dev team, Salsa Studio, and third-party providers.

Salsa Technologies has existing aggregation deals with over 80 providers of gaming content. The Salsa Gator content library encompasses games from many industry-leading studios, such as Spribe, High5Games, Spinomenal, RedRake, PG Soft, Hacksaw, Evolution and Pragmatic Play, among others.

All of this content is available to Salsa Technology’s partnering operators through a single integration.

Wolf Sistemas was founded 8 years ago and has cemented itself as a reliable provider of white-label sports betting platforms. At first, the company focused on sportsbooks but eventually expanded to include bingo, casino and lottery systems as well.
