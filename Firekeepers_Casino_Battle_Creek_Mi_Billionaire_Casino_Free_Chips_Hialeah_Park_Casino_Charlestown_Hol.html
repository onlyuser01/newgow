The voting for the AffPapa iGaming Awards 2023 has now officially commenced, the organization said in a release sent to the media. Celebrated as one of the most significant events on the iGaming calendar, this year’s edition of the awards will recognize the continuous efforts of affiliates, operators, and B2B providers in the industry.

This is de facto AffPapa iGaming Awards' 2ndedition and it will be featuring a diverse lineup of exceptional companies. Casino Guru has been nominated in four categories for the upcoming awards itself, and you can now vote for the company to be recognized in the four Crypto Affiliate of The Year, Casino Affiliate of The Year, Best Branding of The Year, and News Affiliate of The Year categories.

Other notable nominees include Roobet, Betsson Group Affiliates, Pragmatic Play, Swintt, Endorphina, Better Collective, Mr Gamble, LeoVegas, N1 Partners, Campeón Partners, and many others. The voting will last through June 6, so anyone interested has still time to go and back the companies they want to see recognized for their efforts in diverse categories and tracks within the iGaming industry.

The voting page is already up with AffPapa CEO Levon Nikoghosyan happy with the developments and opportunities that the 2nd edition of the event is bringing around with it. In a statement, Nikoghosyan said:

"This year’s AffPapa iGaming Awards promises to be an unforgettable celebration of the achievements and innovation within the industry. We are proud of the impressive lineup of companies joining us, having also extended nominations to include B2B providers, further enhancing the caliber of this year’s Awards. We look forward to welcoming everyone in Malta for the grand ceremony on June 20th and encourage all to participate in the voting process to help us recognize the outstanding contributions made. Good luck to all nominees!"

The official ceremony announcing the winners will take place on June 20 at Malta’s Mediterranean Conference Center. Anyone interested in contacting AffPapa in the meantime and finding out more about the event can do so through the official website for iGaming Awards 2023.
