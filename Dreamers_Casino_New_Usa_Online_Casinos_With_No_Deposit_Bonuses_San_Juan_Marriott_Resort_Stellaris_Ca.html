FanDuel Group and Port Madison Enterprises have teamed up for a sportsbook project in Washington State. A Bonafide Retail Sports Betting Experience by FanDuel

As a result, sports fans will be able to access various betting opportunities through three live betting windows and 12 self-servicing kiosks. The interior will be decked out with 17 screens, a high-definition video wall, and comfortable seating to make the experience more worthwhile to attendees.

An additional three kiosks will be available at the Beach Rock Music and Sports Lounge in the casino, which will have 11 screens and enough seating for more than 250 people. FanDuel Sportsbook at Suquamish Clearwater Casino will deliver on the same bonafide sports betting experience fans have grown accustomed to all over the United States when it comes to FanDuel’s products.

Port Madison Enterprises CEO Rion Ramirez stated that he was excited to see America’s number one sportsbook team up with the establishment to provide wagering opportunities to visitors. Boosting Revenue Stream and Introducing Responsible Gambling

A similar message was delivered by the chairman of the Suquamish Tribe, Leonard Forsman, who touted the opportunity to add sports wagering to the casino’s facilities as a way to benefit from the legalization of the segment and boost the company’s revenue streams.
