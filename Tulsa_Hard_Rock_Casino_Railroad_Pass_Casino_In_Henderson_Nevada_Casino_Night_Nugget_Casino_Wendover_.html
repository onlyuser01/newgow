The provider of leading innovative technology solutions, products and services, powering the online gambling industry, Gaming Innovation Group (GiG), announced a new senior-level appointment. On Monday, the company announced it selected the experienced iGaming executive, Matthew Saxon, for the role of Chief Technology Officer (CTO) for Platform and Sportsbook. The new appointment marks another strategic hire for GiG as it strengthens its executive team for its Platform and Sportsbook which is guided by CEO Richard Carter.

Overall, Saxon brings more than three decades of experience within the financial and iGaming industries. He is a proven technical leader with a track record. Saxon's strategic guidance helped many companies over the last 30 years and his expertise proved to be vital when developing AI-driven tools, sportsbooks and platforms. Moreover, he played an important role in the creation of different services, as well as products.

As a part of GiG's C-suite, Saxon will oversee the technical side of the company's Platform and Sportsbook. He will be in charge of its engineering strategy and also spearhead the development of new innovative features and solutions. Additionally, Saxon will play an important role in the company's GiG Logic and GiG Data tools.

In a statement, Saxon commented by saying: "I am excited to be joining GiG and leading their technology team at the start of what promises to be an extremely impressive growth phase." Last but not least, the new Chief Technology Officer at GiG pointed out: "The foundations that are in place are an amazing springboard to help us accelerate our partners’ businesses and I’m looking forward to helping GiG bring these great capabilities to them, both new and existing."

Richard Carter, GiG's Platform and Sportsbook CEO, was similarly delighted, explaining that Saxon's appointment will help the business continue to expand. Finally, he explained: "We are continuing a process of bringing together the strongest leadership in sports betting and iGaming and I am confident that his arrival will help further fuel our strategic plans, establishing us as a leader in technological advancement and commerciality across the iGaming industry."

The new senior-level appointment complements GiG's plan to invest in new and innovative products. The company has been constantly expanding its offering, delivering unique products that stand out with technological capabilities, catering to online gambling operators across the globe.

Many operators around the world have already identified the potential of GiG's services, solutions and technology. The company's innovative AI stack currently powers the operations of more than 60 brands worldwide.
