Online sports and esports betting operator GG.BET will continue working with PGL, one of the oldest and most well-known esports event organizers in the world. Thanks to this, GG.BET will serve as the exclusive betting partner of the PGL Major Arlington 2022.

PGL and GG.BET to Join Forces Once Again

The PGL Major Arlington 2022 is the second major event of the 2021/2022 Dota 2 season. As its name suggests, the event is set to take place in Arlington, Texas. The professional tournament is scheduled to begin on August 4 and will run until the grand finals on August 14.

Silviu Stoie, PGL’s chief executive, spoke about choosing GG.BET as its partner. He said that every time his company organizes an event, it seeks to surprise its fans with promotional content – something GG.BET is known to deliver.

In addition, Stroie thanked GG.BET for its unwavering support. He emphasized that the operator has already sponsored many previous events, which is why it is a trusted partner that PGL is happy to be working with.

Dmitry Voshkarin, chief executive of GG.BET, also shared his thoughts on the partnership. He lauded PGL for hosting supreme esports events that “erase the line between the digital and the virtual.

For us, it’s important that we offer not just an incredible esports betting experience, but that we also help people get the most enjoyment possible out of watching the biggest events in the world of esports. Dmitry Voshkarin, CEO, GG.BET

GG.BET Will Offer Various Promotions

Speaking of GG.BET, the company has already prepared a whole list of activities and promotions to celebrate the partnership with PGL. The operator will share promotional PGL Major Arlington content across its social media and will launch vlogs on Dota 2. In addition, the company will release videos where experts analyze the games and provide valuable insights to esports betters.

As for betting, GG.BET will allow punters to stay in touch with the tournament thanks to its live odds. Furthermore, the company will provide various promotions throughout the event. For example, during the first four days of the event, GG.BET will offer a free $15 bet every day if players place at least three bets of $5. Moreover, between August 8 and August 13 players will be able to get insurance for three of their bets when they make a deposit of at least $25. Lastly, during the event’s grand finals, GG.BET will offer a 250% deposit bonus for any deposit of $10 or more.

GG.BET has previously served as the exclusive betting partner of the PGL Major Stockholm in 2021, the PGL Major in Antwerp this year and the PGL RMR event.
