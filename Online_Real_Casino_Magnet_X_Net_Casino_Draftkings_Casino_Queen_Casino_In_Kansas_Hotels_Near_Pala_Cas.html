Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

Landing only on reels 2, 3, and 4, Wilds are assigned a multiplier of either x2 or x3, assigned at random. A Wild’s multiplier is then applied to each win that it helps form.

Multiple Wilds in one win mean that each multiplier is added together and then applied to winnings.

Bonus symbols appear on reels 1, 3, and 5, and the Free Spins feature is triggered when players land one on each of these reels.

The number of free spins players are awarded is selected at random by a special set of reels.

Any Wilds that land during the Free Spins feature remain sticky on the reels for the duration of the Free Spins feature. The multipliers associated with these Wilds remain with them until the conclusion of the feature and are applied to any wins the Wilds form.

Wild symbols are Sticky in the Free Spins feature

Bonus symbols do not appear during the Free Spins feature, and the feature cannot be retriggered.
