A new partnership saw GiG agree to integrate the leading Jackpot Management System provided by Jooba Tech to its platform, powering iGaming operators across the globe

The leading iGaming technology company, offering services, solutions and products for the online gambling industry, Gaming Innovation Group (GiG), has announced a new partnership with Jooba Tech, an industry-leading provider of solutions for the iGaming vertical. Under the terms of the freshly minted agreement, GiG agreed to integrate Jooba’s revolutionary Jackpot Management System into its platform.

The collaboration represents a major achievement for the company as it will enable it to significantly expand the reach of its leading product thanks to GiG’s extensive partner network. The integration into GiG’s platform means that existing and future partners of the company will be able to benefit from the engaging Jackpot Management System.

Jooba’s system enables operators to deliver different jackpots. Thanks to the Jackpot Management System, iGaming operators can differentiate themselves from the competition through tailored jackpots that deliver unique experiences.

Per the latest collaboration, GiG’s future and current partners will benefit from jackpot capabilities that offer a wide range of customization options, enabling them to engage with their customer base. Besides supreme experiences, the Jackpot Management System from Jooba is proven to boost customer retention and acquisition, providing iGaming operators with unique growth opportunities.

The latest announcement comes after GiG confirmed recently a new senior-level appointment. Last week, the company revealed it hired Andrew Cochrane for the role of Platform & Sportsbook chief business officer. Joining GiG’s C-suite, the seasoned expert brings extensive experience and a wide range of knowledge that are undoubtedly going to help him in his new role.

The Partnership Brings Excitement to GiG, Jooba

Rebecca Attard, GiG’s business development manager, was delighted about the recently announced partnership with Jooba. She said that offering jackpots for any product or game helps brands engage with their clients while at the same time differentiating them from the competition. “Jooba Tech’s Jackpot Management System does exactly that and we are excited to see where this can go,” said Attard in conclusion.

“Having jackpots available on any game or product via our platform is key to providing a unique and differentiated experience for our customers’ brands.“ Rebecca Attard, business development manager at GiG

Tom Finlayson, Jooba Tech’s co-founder, also commented on the topic, explaining that the company is thrilled to significantly propel its reach thanks to the collaboration with GiG. He said that it is exciting to team up with such a recognizable brand that offers a leading platform, powering the operations of iGaming companies across the globe. Finally, Finlayson confirmed that Jooba has been continuously investing in its solutions to ensure that they offer engaging experiences for customers of iGaming operators.
