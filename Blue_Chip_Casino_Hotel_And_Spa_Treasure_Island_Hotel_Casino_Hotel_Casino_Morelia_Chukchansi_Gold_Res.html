NeoGames' subsidiary, Pariplay Ltd, a leading aggregator and content provider, announced a new senior leadership appointment. On Thursday, the company revealed that it selected Linda Sliede as its new Head of Account Management. In her new position with Pariplay, Sliede will help the company's ongoing global expansion in key markets such as the one in North America. Before becoming Head of Account Management at Pariplay, she held another key position with the company. Initially, she joined Pariplay back in November 2021 and most recently held the position of Account Manager.

Overall, she has more than five years of experience within the iGaming industry. Prior to joining Pariplay, she was a part of Playtech's Euro Live Technologies. During her tenure, she held different positions such as Quality Assurance Specialist, Product QA Specialist as well as Customer Relations Manager. Sliede's professional background includes five months as a project manager at New Black Latvia, as well as a PR & IC Project Manager position with DYNINNO Group.

In a statement released on October 6, 2022, Pariplay's Chief Commercial Officer, Enrico Bradamante, said: "I'm delighted to have been able to promote Linda as I am a very strong believer in growing and developing people internally." He explained that the company has a strong Account Management team which reaffirms its focus on delivering the best services to clients.

In conclusion, he said: "I look forward to Linda's contributing further to help Pariplay continue to push the boundaries in the industry." As Head of Account Management, Sliede is going to report to Pariplay's COO, Bradamante.

Currently, Pariplay operates in more than 20 regulated gambling markets around the world. The company's Fusion platform boasts more than 14,000 casino game titles offering a wide variety of content to its growing list of partner brands.

Expanding its presence further into the US market, Pariplay recently announced its entry into Connecticut. The company revealed that it received a supplier license in the state, marking its fifth US state entry. Besides Connecticut, Pariplay holds a B2B license in other US jurisdictions such as New Jersey, Michigan, Pennsylvania and West Virginia.

Aside from the market in North America, the company has been growing its presence in Latin America after entering Panama as well as Buenos Aires City. Earlier this week, Pariplay also significantly expanded its presence in Europe after announcing a deal with ATG. By joining forces with ATG, the company expanded its footprint in Sweden.
