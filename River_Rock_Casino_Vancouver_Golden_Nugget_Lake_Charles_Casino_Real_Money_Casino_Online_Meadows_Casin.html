Fantasma Games has partnered with Casinolytics for the launch of Mystic Spells, an advanced game based on AI and player behavior research

The gaming studio that creates “slots beyond gambling” and focuses on constantly bringing gaming into the world of gambling, Fantasma Games, has reunited with Casinolytics for the creation of one of its most exciting games yet, Mystic Spells.

Casinolytics, the leading Artificial Intelligence-based live-streaming data science company in the online gaming market, has provided the complex AI analytics and player behavior research needed to create a unique game intended to take the industry by storm.

At the same time, in the context of the constantly growing market for live streaming, the slot that will be launched on October 10, aims to turn into the go-to game for streamers and record a resounding success in the community.

In the long run, the focus on streaming is also expected to assist operators with maximizing the exposure of the slot along with its lifespan, ultimately generating long-term returns.

Two Innovative Types of Bonuses and the Mysticspins Feature

Based on the data analysis resulting from the study of billions of minutes of live streaming content along with player behavior analysis of more than 10,000 hours of watched live slots streaming content, Mystic Spells incorporates a series of unique features and mechanics.

The innovative mechanics and bonuses have been created based on the features and content that audiences have already shown high levels of appreciation for in the past.

Besides the one-of-a-kind, mesmerizing universe and eye-catching design incorporated in the slot, players will enjoy discovering two spectacular bonuses along with the Mysticspins feature. The stand-alone Feature Spin feature together with the expanding wild multipliers are expected to catch the attention of a massive number of fans of the genre.

A Game for Streamers Who Love Slots

Fantasma Games’ chief executive officer, Fredrik Johansson, expressed delight at being able to release the game together with Casinolytics. The CEO mentioned the mix of their “creative approach” toward game development with the precious live streaming know-how of their partner was the main reason that has allowed them to generate a game that “could be suitable for the market right now.”

Johansson further commented on the unique project and called streaming something “essential to the modern online slot experience”, considering Mystic Spells to be the ideal game for this particular environment.

Similarly, Casinolytics’ co-founder, Johan Sandén, spoke about the foundation of the new slot that revolves around AI-generated data analysis meant to measure the “popularity of mechanics and features.”

Sandén was joined by fellow co-founder Sebastian Khalighy in his comments about the new release. The latter spoke about the massive role played by streamers in enabling certain games to stand out in today’s online gaming market, while also offering an ideal opportunity to “deep-dive into player behaviors and feelings.”

In June 2022, Fantasma boosted its partnership with Light & Wonder by delivering its slot-based content to Quebec’s provincial lotto and iGaming operator, Loto-Québec. The following month of the same year, the studio announced the release of the Cloud Corsairs slot.
