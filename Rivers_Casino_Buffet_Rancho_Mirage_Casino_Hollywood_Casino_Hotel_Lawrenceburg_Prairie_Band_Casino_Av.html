Pariplay, the content distribution platform and a subsidiary of NeoGames SA has added Reloaded Gaming to its Ignite partner program, which will allow the supplier to use the aggregator to reach new audiences in the North American market. Reloaded Gaming Becomes Latest Ignite Partner

Reloaded Gaming’s products will be made available through Fusion, and they bring a host of themes and opportunities to players. The games are built to encompass the full range of the experience, from casual themes to modern slots, and many more impressive titles developed by the studio and now available to operators in the five states where Pariplay is currently licensed to operate in. Reloaded Gaming CEO Robert Skogh welcomed the opportunity:

The Ignite program provides us with a great opportunity to get our games in front of players across the United States, through Pariplay’s partnerships with a host of leading operators. Reloaded Gaming CEO Robert Skogh
