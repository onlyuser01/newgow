The Game Is Simple Yet Engaging

Free Spins Mode Offers Many Chances to Win

In addition to its engaging mechanics and payline-free gameplay, Dice Bonanza has a Free Spins bonus round that can be activated when a number of scatter symbols are collected.

During Free Spins mode, players can also land on multiplier symbols represented by a golden safe. When players land on a safe, it will open, revealing a random multiplier of between x2 and x100.
