The Collaboration Between LexisNexis Regulatory Compliance and Senet Marks Perfect Goals Alignment

LexisNexis Regulatory Compliance and Senet have announced that they have signed a sponsorship partnership in connection with Senet’s landmark course on gambling regulation taking place in March 2023.

The new collaboration is a perfect alignment between the goals of Senet’s training program to provide clarity in terms of the applicable gambling regulations and the tools LexisNexis Regulatory Compliance offers, which help businesses in the sector achieve compliance with the relevant regulations.

Vice President of LexisNexis Regulatory Compliance Global Myfanwy Wallwork expressed his excitement about the partnership noting that LexisNexis Regulatory Compliance is glad to draw attention to its new tools helping companies navigate the complicated compliance requirements in the gambling industry.

Wallwork also added that his company is delighted to work with a wide variety of renowned gambling sector experts in its new role as sponsor to Regulating the Game.

The Training Program Regulating the Game

Regulating the Game is an intensive 5-day-long educational program targeted at stakeholders in the gambling sector, which was started in August 2021 by Australia’s experts on gambling law and regulation – law firm Senet.

The program is designed to enhance the capabilities of gambling companies and individual stakeholders in terms of compliance goals, enabling the participants to tackle regulatory challenges. The program also aims to spark discussion and new ideas for the betterment of regulatory practices and leadership.

The training brings together various experts from the gambling industry that offer guidance and clarifications in connection with gambling regulatory policies and compliance requirements.

There also are discussions on innovations in the sector and on various present or upcoming risks that come with business operations in the evolving gambling industry.

Paul Newson, regulatory and gambling specialist at Senet, has highlighted the importance of the training program: “Regulating the Game has established itself as a must-attend event for the gambling industry and regulatory stakeholders interested in bettering industry practices and outcomes and contributing to public policy thinking and discourse.”

The next edition of the training program will be held from March 6 to March 10, 2023, at the International Convention Centre (ICC) in Sydney.

Gambling industry experts that have already confirmed their participation are Annette Kimmitt, CEO of the Victorian Gambling and Casino Control Commission; Adam Rytenskild, Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer at Tabcorp; Entain General Counsel Carly Richardson; Malta Gaming Authority CEO Carl Brincat; Ian Hughes, Chief Commercial Officer, Gaming Laboratories International, LLC.
