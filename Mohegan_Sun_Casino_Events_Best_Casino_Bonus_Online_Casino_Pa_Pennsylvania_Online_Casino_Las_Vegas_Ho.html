The leading provider of mobile-focused gaming content, Gaming Realms, strengthened its C-suite with a new appointment. On Monday this week, the company revealed that it hired the services of the seasoned expert within the gambling vertical, Suki Sandhu. Joining Gaming Realms' senior-level management team, he was appointed to the role of Chief Product Officer (CPO). This strategic hire signals the company's dedication to growing and expanding its product offering.

In his new role as CPO, Sandhu will oversee the development of Gaming Realms' Slingo content portfolio. His responsibilities will include strengthening the company's position within the online gambling sector through the development of new features and concepts. With Sandhu as a part of its C-suite, Gaming Realms anticipates further expansion and the introduction of new products that would propel its growth in the iGaming vertical.

In a statement, the new CPO of the company shared his excitement by saying: "I am delighted to join Gaming Realms and I am really looking forward to working with the management, our internal and external studios, and product teams to build creative games and mechanics to grow the Slingo brand and explore complimentary innovative concepts which can help further boost the Gaming Realms business."

Sandhu revealed that he shares a passion for the company when it comes to making a "positive impact in the market." He spoke about the growing popularity of games from the Slingo vertical and added that it would be exciting to contribute toward the development of such products. "We have many ideas and a hungry team, and we will now create a shared product vision to ensure we deliver a positive gaming experience for our players, partners and operators," said the new CPO in conclusion.

Mark Segal, Gaming Realms' CEO added that the company is excited to expand its team with the addition of an expert from Sandhu's rank. Welcoming the new CPO, Segal explained that Sandhu brings extensive knowledge and skills. Finally, he predicted that under his strategic guidance, the company would elevate its offering and deliver new experiences.

Before joining Gaming Realms, Sandhu served as Product Director of iGaming at the leading provider of technology, content and services, Inspired Entertainment. His professional background includes more important roles with industry giants such as Gamesys and WynnBet. Throughout his professional career, Sandhu also held C-suit roles with IGT and PartyGaming, a company that is now a part of the leading gaming and entertainment giant, Entain.
