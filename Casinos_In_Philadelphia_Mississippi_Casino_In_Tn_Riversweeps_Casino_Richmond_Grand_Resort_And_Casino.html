Betting and gaming platform NuxGame has struck a new content partnership with Ezugi, a company focusing on live casino products and a member of Evolution Group since its acquisition by the gaming behemoth in 2018. As a result, Ezugi’s distinguished products will be available as part of NuxGame’s platform offering, accessible to businesses and players.

Ezugi Becomes an Option for NuxGame Clients

Ezugi has been able to systematically grow its offer since 2012, producing more than 20 games and signing partnerships with 200+ iGaming operators worldwide. The company has achieved a number of high-profile content partnerships over the past months, including one with QTech Games.

Ezugi has also partnered with Smartico.ai, a CRM automation provider, which has helped the company continue to deliver on its product and portfolio offering in a sustained and intuitive manner. Commenting on the latest partnership, NuxGame head of sales Bar Konson said:

We are delighted to welcome Ezugi to our growing partnership network and couldn’t be more excited to provide our customers with the brand’s high-quality content. NuxGame head of sales Bar Konson

He welcomed the opportunity to be teaming up with Ezugi, arguing that the partnership will add considerable value to NuxGame’s business operations. Lastly, Konson said that the team will be looking forward to the next months of collaboration with Ezugi and gauge results.

We are excited to see how NuxGame’s customers respond to our products and look forward to seeing how this partnership materializes for both parties. Ezugi Business development director Pang Goh
