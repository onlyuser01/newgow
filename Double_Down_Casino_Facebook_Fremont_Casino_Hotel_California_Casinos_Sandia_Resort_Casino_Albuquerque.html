MGM Resorts International teased its intentions to grow the brand beyond the UK

MGM Resorts International, a global casino and hospitality leader, announced the launch of a new BetMGM iGaming and online sports betting brand in the United Kingdom. The launch is in line with the operator’s ambition to turn BetMGM into a leading international gaming brand.

BetMGM’s UK-facing iGaming and online betting brand is set to provide British players with many exciting products and features.
