REEVO lauded Hacksaw Gaming’s unique touch and said that it is excited to onboard its games

REEVO, a fast-growing B2B content and aggregation platform, has further diversified its library thanks to an agreement with Hacksaw Gaming, a leading provider of iGaming products. As per the deal between the two, the latter company’s library of slot games, scratch cards and instant games will be integrated into REEVO.

Hacksaw Gaming was founded in 2018 and is a disruptive provider of cutting-edge content. At first, the company was primarily focused on digital scratch products but eventually expanded its expertise and now produces many hit slot products and instant win games.

Under the agreement, Hacksaw’s existing and future content will become available to REEVO’s partnering operators, allowing them to greatly improve their offerings. Thanks to the partnership, REEVO’s clients will be able to engage their fans with games such as Le Bandit, Wanted Dead or a Wild, R.I.P. City, Dork Unit and Bloodthirst, among many others.
