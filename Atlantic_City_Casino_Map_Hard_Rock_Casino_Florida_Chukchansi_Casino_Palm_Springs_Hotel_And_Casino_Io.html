FSB, a supplier and provider of iGaming and sports betting solutions, announced this morning that it is extending its partnership with Fitzdares, a UK-based award-winning sports betting brand. The pair first started working together in 2019 and Fitzdares is a recognized name in the UK market.

Fitzdares Breaks Ground in Ontario

As a result of their partnership extension, Fitzdares will be able to continue delivering on the unique content and solutions that have helped shape the industry and gain a competitive edge over rivals.

Fitzdares prioritizes customer experience by delivering highly sought-after games that strike home with its audiences. The sportsbook is powered by FSB’s proprietary platform which guarantees smoothness of the experience, with Fitzdares recently expanding in the newly-regulated Ontario gambling market.

Because of the successful launch of the sportsbook overseas, Fitzdares and FSB are interested in continuing to work together and remain long-term partners. On the back of this development, FSB is also actively looking to bolster its presence in central Europe, after the company signed a partnership with the Hungarian National Lottery.
