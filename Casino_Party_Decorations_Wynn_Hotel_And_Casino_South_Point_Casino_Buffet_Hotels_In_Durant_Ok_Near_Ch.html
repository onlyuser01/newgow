Venuetize has partnered up with Triggy in a deal that will help Venuetize boost its engagement with the sports betting industry.

Venuetize to Integrate Triggy’s Innovative Features

Venuetize, a leading mobile technology, and advanced e-commerce sports betting platform provider, has teamed up with Triggy, a leading platform developer that accelerates digitalization in the betting industry.

Under the deal, Venuetize will gain access to Triggy’s advanced platform, which includes alerts, notifications, live score technology for integration into facility apps and franchise, and NextBets – the betting suggestions feature.

With Triggy’s platform and its notification features, Venuetize will add value to sponsorship deals for sportsbook operators.

Bettors Will Engage Better with Their Favorite Sports Teams

Тriggy CEO Martina Akerlund said that the company is excited to offer its advanced personalization platform and integrate it into the Venuetize sports betting module. She said that this collaboration would help bettors follow and engage with their favorite sports teams. Venuetize CEO Jon Romm said: “Gamification tied into our e-commerce platform we believe is a natural fit.”

Venuetize is happy to partner up with Triggy, its newest gaming module member, staying optimistic about what the two parties can accomplish together. Romm added that the company constantly explores new platform solutions that will provide success to its clients through its products and services.

Venuetize Continues to Expand despite Pandemic Challenges

Over the past year, Venuetize has more than doubled its clients from 18 to 40. It signed deals with several teams from the MLB and the NHL, including Tampa Bay Lightning and Tampa Bay Rays. The company began working with pro soccer franchises like FC Cincinnati, Los Angeles Football Club, and the New York Red Bulls.

Recently Venuetize became a part of the St. Louis Blues technology upgrades. The company teamed up with the NHL team to provide mobile ordering for concessions through the NHL app. Blues fans, who have selected the team as their “favorite” on the NHL app, could securely store their credit card details in a mobile wallet and purchase food and beverages as well as STL Authentics merchandise from their phone.

Triggy Continues to Strengthen Its Us Presence

This year Triggy, expanded its presence on the US market by adding football to the LeoAlerts live score app by LeoVegas. The offering includes information based on sophisticated, intelligently filtered data on the live score app, directly distributed to bettors.

LeoAlerts includes push notifications for fan’s favorite teams and games, as well as relevant content from NextBet and 3 betting suggestions for every notification.
