Wild Falls review

Theme and Storyline

Coming some forty years after the California gold rush, the Klondike gold rush had a very different feel. In the mid-1890s, swarms of prospectors ventured into the inhospitable north of the Yukon with equipment to pan the icy rivers for gold.

Wild Falls adapts this setting, placing the reels on a Canadienne waterfall and, perhaps a little abstractly, boxing the water bourne gold into wooden chests which fall onto the reels from the river above.

The theme makes for a pretty setting and is a nice change from the many gold rush style games that take you underground into mines.

Gameplay

Wild Falls has a five reel, three row base game with twenty win lines and four bonus features (see below for details).

Some of the bonuses are integrated into the main gameplay offering a welcome change of pace and keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

While breaking no new ground, Wild Falls is an example of a slot that does all the basics well.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol, substitutes for all symbols and acts as the Scatter symbol. Rather than being a part of the spinning reels, Wild Falls has a gimmick where Wilds – in the form of treasure chests – drift down the river in the background, over the waterfall and come crashing down into place on the reels from above.

Any time one or two Wilds land on the reel, it triggers a Rapid Respin feature. Wilds are held in place while the respins occur. If no new Wilds land, the respins end.

Rapid Respins - base game feature with chests as sticky Wilds

If you get three Wilds on the reels either in one spin or during a respin, the River of Gold feature is triggered. When this occurs, respins are triggered. Instead, the current Wild’s move down one space between each respin until all the Wilds have dropped off the bottom of the reels. New Wilds can still land on the reels extending this bonus.

Free spins bonus - River of Gold

Gold Rush is a random feature which adds multipliers of 2x, 3x or 5x to all chests. Additionally if you trigger the River of Gold with three Gold Rush chests you can trigger the Super River of Gold in which the multipliers remain in place. This is where the really big wins can occur.
