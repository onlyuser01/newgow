Anne de Kerckhove Joins 888 As Senior Independent Director

888 Holdings, a Gibraltar-based online gambling and gaming company, has appointed Anne de Kerckhove as a senior independent director.

De Kerckhove is a marketing, technology, and entertainment industry veteran with over 15 years of experience with some of the fastest-growing companies. She has been a non-executive director and chair of the nominations committee at 888 since November 2017, when she joined the group.

De Kerckhove is currently serving as the acting CEO of both Freespee and the London-based Iron Group. Freespee is a cloud-based collaboration tool that can be used for marketing, sales, and customer support. On the other hand, Iron Group is a technology firm that specializes in developing and investing in the digital subscription space.

She was previously the Managing Director EMEA for Videology, one of the largest online advertising technology platforms globally, where she oversaw the company’s growth into over 16 countries in just under three years.

De Kerckhove also serves on the boards of 7digital, WeMoms, and Snowite and is an angel investor and mentor for early-stage startups and entrepreneurial funds such as Metail, CRE, and Daphni.

Furthermore, de Kerckhove has held senior positions at companies such as Iron Capital, Reed Elsevier, and Influenced Gaming throughout her long and illustrious career.
