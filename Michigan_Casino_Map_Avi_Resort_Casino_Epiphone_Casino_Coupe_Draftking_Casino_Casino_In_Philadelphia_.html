Candy Island Princess review

Theme and Storyline

This game has a Japanese game show theme.

After arriving on a tropical island, players will meet a selection of bodacious anime girls. Each one is a contestant in the Candy Island Princess competition, and none are too shy about showing off their assets.

This slot is filled with pastel colours, adorable candies, and flirtatious winks. This is a great game for players looking for a sugary fantasy, with a saucy dose of sexy appeal.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is a cheerful pop tune that brings to mind a dance party. The reels spin with a cute bubbling sound, and wins are celebrated with the jiggle of coins.

The Princess contestants each have a signature look and personality. With flower-adorned hair in colours including blond, violet and brunette, they are easy to identify.

At first, only the contestants’ cutesy faces can be seen on the reels. However, the visual expands to show the girls’ scantily bikini-clad bodies. The animation style makes no secret of the girls’ curves, showing off a scandalous full body image.

Despite the sexy looks, the girls also have a wholesome sweetness as they hold beach accessories and smile innocently. This feels right on brand for the anime aesthetic.

Also among the symbols are hard candies in pastel hues and friendly shapes, including hearts and flowers. A camera and film also appear on the reels, highlighting beach model competition at hand.

Overall, the music and animation combine to create a balance of sweet and sassy moods.

Gameplay

Candy Island Princess is a three reel, three row slot with five win lines.

The gameplay is fast and players will not have trouble staying engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Players may mix-and-match the contestant symbols in order to complete a payline.

If players land a matching set of 3 contestant symbols, the symbols will fully expand.

Players will trigger the Free Spins feature when they land 3 Ticket symbols. If players land 2 Ticket symbols, they will be awarded a Lucky Re-Spin of the reel missing a Ticket symbol. This will give players another chance to win the Free Spins feature.

The Jelly Jiggle Delight feature is triggered during every Free Spins feature. Players will have the option to choose 1 of 3 jelly desserts which provide the chance to win a x2 multiplier. This can double the winnings from the entire Free Spins feature.

If players land 3 Camera symbols, this triggers the Behind the Scenes feature. Players will instantly be awarded wins between x5 and x200 their bet.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.2%, slightly above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.05 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is low-medium, which makes it a relatively poor match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We do not recommend this relatively high RTP, low-medium volatility anime-themed slot.

Though the flirtatious aesthetics are fun, the game simply cannot overcome its mediocre strategy profile.

Players looking to maximise their big wins would do better to seek out a game that offers higher volatility.
