Las Vegas-headquartered cross-platform global game company Light & Wonder, Inc has successfully launched its full turnkey platform OpenBet with SAZKA a.s. and its sportsbook brand Sazkabet. The operator runs betting markets in the Czech Republic and is designed to service the market locally.

Sazkabet Boosts Betting Offer

Through the arrival of OpenBet, Sazkabet can deliver next-generation mobile and online sports betting solutions that will significantly improve the consumer experience. OpenBet is also a compliance-ready product that is able to launch directly and ensure that the betting experience is safe and responsible, and aligned with local operational demands.

The platform features a scalable betting engine and liability management pricing and trading service to give Sazkabet the opportunity to adjust its markets and fixtures with precision and confidence. SAZKA a.s. is also involved with lottery operations in the Czech Republic and Europe.

The arrival of the OpenBet platform on Sazkabet is an opportunity for the parent companies to continue working together elsewhere and expand their offering in new European jurisdictions together. Welcoming the arrival of the OpenBet platform, SAZKA a.s. iGaming director Jan Horyna said:

Transitioning to OpenBet’s sports betting technology and services is a key move for us to provide our customers with high-quality and responsible betting experiences. With OpenBet’s transformed modern technology, we are delighted to have introduced significant improvements to our overall sportsbook offering and relish the opportunity to plan future collaborations with OpenBet over the coming months. SAZKA a.s. iGaming director Jan Horyna

OpenBet Appreciates Sazkabet

OpenBet group chief product officer Nikos Konstakis welcomed the partnership and said that Sazkabet is a known player in the Czech market. Konstakis added:

We have a strong track record for delivering sports betting solutions to WLA members worldwide and are very much looking forward to driving next-level player experiences for Sazkabet. OpenBet group chief product officer Nikos Konstakis

The executive is confident that the OpenBet platform is fully capable of delivering the desired cutting-edge betting experiences to consumers in the Czech Republic to enjoy.
