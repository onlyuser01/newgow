The experienced Andy McNamara has joined Red Knot as an account director amid the company’s expansion into Canada

Red Knot Communications, a company describing itself as a boutique PR and communications agency serving the sports betting and gaming industry, has expanded its team with a key hire who will support the company’s Canadian operations.

McNamara Joins the Team

The one joining Red Knot is the experienced Andy McNamara, a veteran with a wealth of knowledge in sales and sports media. He will serve the agency as an account director and will lead its business development.

As announced by Red Knot, McNamara will stand at the head of the Toronto office, which is the company’s first HQ in the North American country. From there, the new hire will work to expand the company’s international presence and help it reach more clients.

Red Knot boasts offices in Canada, Dubai, London, Rome and the United States. The agency launched in 2020 and is supported by a knowledgeable team of seasoned media, sports and gaming veterans. The company offers its clients PR support and strategic counsel, helping its partners grow their businesses. Some of the most notable Red Knot partners include Spotlight Sports Group, SimWin Sports, Odds On Compliance, and Tekkorp Capital.

McNamara Is Excited to Get Started

As mentioned, McNamara has a lot of experience in sports media, having previously worked for companies like Ultimate Franchise Fantasy Sports and Sportsnet. During his career, McNamara oversaw partnership deals with many sports betting and fantasy sports companies.

In addition, McNamara has his own podcast called The Sick Podcast with Andy McNamara. He also shares a role as a content manager with Knup Solutions and leads a whole team of sports writers, editors and content creators.

McNamara shared that he is thrilled to join Red Knot Communications and its talented team. He said that he cannot wait to get started and help the company grow in Canada.

I’m excited to be working with this talented international group of professionals at Red Knot Communications and contributing to the growth of its business across the Canadian landscape. Andy McNamara, account director, Red Knot Communications

Red Knot Is Happy to Have McNamara on Board

Red Knot’s founder, Andy Clerkson, welcomed his namesake to the company. He is sure that McNamara has all the experience needed to help Red Knot establish a strong presence in Canada and turn its local plans into a “productive reality.”

We couldn’t be more thrilled about this expansion, enabling our team to grow and position our current clients on a larger international stage. Andy Clerkson, founder, Red Knot Communications

Ashely Dabb, Red Knot’s head of operations for the Americas, reaffirmed Red Knot’s delight in welcoming McNamara on board. She concluded that his expertise will allow the company to bring all of its world-class services to sports and gaming companies in Canada.
