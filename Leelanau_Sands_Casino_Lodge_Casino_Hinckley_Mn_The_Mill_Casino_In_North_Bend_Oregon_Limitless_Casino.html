Peter & Sons Joins Yggdrasil’s YG Masters Program

Monday, October 19, the YG Masters program by Yggdrasil was joined by the growing slots developer Peter & Sons. By joining the program, the company receives access to the innovative Game Adaptation Tools & Interface (GATI) technology by Yggdrasil. With the help of GATI, the developer plans to rapidly accelerate its global growth strategy. The YG Masters program by Yggdrasil was launched specifically to assist studios crate and distribute genuine content with the help of the innovative GATI technology.

GATI technology is a regulation-ready, preconfigured development toolkit which allows studios as well as game developers to use a standardized technology solution to create and distribute games around the globe. Furthermore, GATI technology is used by all YG Masters program participants.

Peter & Sons is an emerging game developer with an entirely fresh vision for slot games. The company’s goal is to create superior and unique gaming experiences for players around the world. Multiple games by Peter & Sons are in the process of development and their launch is expected later this year.

An Important Stepping Stone

In a press release, Yann Bautista, Business Development Manager at Peter & Sons said that the company is excited to partner with Yggdrasil and release its games through the YG Masters program with the help of GATI technology. He added that as an emerging studio with significant ambitions, Peter & Sons is breaking new ground in terms of visual effects, gameplay, and mechanics.

Bautista stressed that the company is ready to showcase its content to players worldwide. In conclusion, he said that the company is confident that the partnership and support by Yggdrasil will be an “important stepping stone” to achieving its ambitions.
