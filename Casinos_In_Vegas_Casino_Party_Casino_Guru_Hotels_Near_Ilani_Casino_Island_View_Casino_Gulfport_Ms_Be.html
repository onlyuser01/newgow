In the Words of FanDuel’s Twitter Account – “There’s NO WAY. There’s Just NO WAY”

FanDuel Sportsbook, which recently became Buffalo Bills’ partner, took to social media to comment on the happening. The social media department of the sportsbook was concise: “There’s NO WAY. There’s just NO WAY. The most INSANE parlay you’ll ever see, guaranteed.”

The NFL bettor wagered $20 and managed to win just over $579,000 on two parlay bets. First, they wagered for the Bengals to win against the Chiefs with a 27-24 score. They also wagered on the exact score of the Rams versus the San Francisco 49ers 20-17.

Here’s what makes this bet such an incredible win. The Bengals managed to rally even though they were 18 down. A 31-yard goal by Evan McPherson in overtime clocked the 27-24 win against the Kansas City Chiefs in the AFC and in doing so, the Bengals reached their first Super Bowl since 1988.

On the other side, the Los Angeles Rams star-studded team managed to help them overcome the 49ers and edge out the victory to reach the Super Bowl. Matthew Stafford made a huge impact as he led three straight fourth-quarter scoring drives to erase the 10-point lead that the 49ers had.
