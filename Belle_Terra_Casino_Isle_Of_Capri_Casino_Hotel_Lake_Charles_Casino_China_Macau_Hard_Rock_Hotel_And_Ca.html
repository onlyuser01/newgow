Paterson’s Valuable Expertise Will Help the Company

Paterson is a seasoned gambling and tech worker with years of experience behind his back. He has previously worked as GVC’s chief enterprise architect for five years. Following that, he joined KiK Interactive as director of architecture where he remained for two years. His most recent experience was as the director of operations and later chief information officer for The New Lottery Company.

As a part of FSB, Paterson will use his experience to coordinate technical procedures and infrastructures. His responsibility will be to make sure everything is executed smoothly and on time.
