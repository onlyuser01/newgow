Bally Offers Chance to Become Millionaire Off March Madness

Bally’s has also entered the sports betting game with a dedicated sportsbook product and has been actively growing its footprint. Now, one way to do so is to use the popularity of March Madness to create awareness about its brand.

While predicting the outcome of 63 games is a fool’s errand, this will probably not stop people from trying.
