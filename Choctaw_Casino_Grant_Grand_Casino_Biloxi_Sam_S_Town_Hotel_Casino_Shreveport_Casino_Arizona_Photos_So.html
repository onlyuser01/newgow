SportsGrid, a provider of live sports news and odds, has reinforced its team with Warren Sharp as its new lead pro football analyst. The NFL specialist will host live reports and keep sports fans updated on the latest developments in the league.

Sharp Joins SportsGrid

Sharp joins the sports news company amid the National Football League preseason. His job will be to provide coverage of the regular NFL season once it kicks in. Sharp will also appear on various panels during the season playoffs, including Ferrall Coast to Coast, In-Game Live All Access and Pro Football Today.

Sharp’s responsibility as SportsGrid’s lead pro football analyst will be to provide coverage of the hottest topics in the NFL, to share his opinions on the latest developments and to provide fans with unique betting insights.

The famed analyst will also appear on promotional social media content and help SportsGrid boost its digital presence. Furthermore, Sharp will help to promote SportsGrid’s new mobile application upon its launch.

SportsGrid Is Happy to Have Sharp on Board

Warren Sharp is a former NBC Sports analyst who is well-versed in the world of professional football. He will be an important asset in SportsGrid’s arsenal of specialists ahead of the new NFL season.

Sharp spoke about his new position as SportsGrid’s new lead pro football analyst. He shared that he believes SportsGrid is a great place where NFL fans and sports bettors can find news about the latest developments in football.

When I look at the future of sports gambling coverage, I see SportsGrid as an outstanding place to reach the fans who want the NFL insight I provide on a regular basis. Warren Sharp, lead pro football analyst, SportsGrid

Sharp added that his new job will provide him with the freedom to keep his personality during his reports. This is a major benefit for him and he believes that this content strategy would resonate with NFL fans and bettors.

Adam Kaplan, SportsGrid’s chief operating officer, also shared his thoughts on the matter. He welcomed Sharp to the company and said that the entire team is glad to have him on board. Kaplan then praised Sharp is known for his deep knowledge of football and his reputation as one of the best analysts in sports.

Warren’s well-known insights and deep analysis of the NFL teams, players and betting markets have developed his reputation as one of the foremost experts in the sports betting industry. Adam Kaplan, COO, SportsGrid
