Pack and Cash review

Theme and Storyline

Pack and Cash is themed around a workday in a warehouse.

Players will become the efficient employees of a bustling warehouse, packing gifts and other products to be shipped out. Though it might not seem like a typical dream job, this particular warehouse is a bright and cheerful place to work at.

This game’s novel theme may appeal to players who are in the mood for upbeat whimsy.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

A jolly tune plays in the background, accompanied by ambient warehouse sounds like garbled PA announcements and shifting machinery.

The conveyor belt reels are filled with colourful letters and parcels in tantalising shapes. Players will spot a heart-shaped parcel that might contain chocolates as well as others that appear to be trophies and ducks.

The playful packages add a warm vibe to the game and bring to mind Santa’s workshop. Every now and then, each of the symbols jiggles a little, as if someone was shaking a package to see what is inside. Upon wins, symbols burst into gold coins, adding excitement to the moment.

Symbols that move along the entire conveyor belt without becoming part of a win fall into a disposal incinerator. This seems a little sad in an otherwise happy-go-lucky atmosphere.

Slightly blurred behind the reels, players will service carts and forklifts busily moving boxes. The warehouse is bright, clean, and everyone seems happily occupied.

Overall, the animation and music create a friendly and welcoming warehouse atmosphere.

Gameplay

Pack and Cash is a five reel, four row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels starting from the leftmost reel and going right.

All symbols are delivered from the right side, and move on conveyor belts towards the left, filling the reels.

The Cascade feature means that winning symbols vanish and new symbols move along to replace them.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol.

After each cascade, a win multiplier increases by +1. This is tracked on a little screen beside the reels. This multiplier resets after cascade sequences conclude.

As Scatter symbols move farther left along the conveyor belt reels, they become more valuable when triggering the Free Spins feature.

When players land 3 or more Scatter symbols, the Free Spins feature begins. During this feature, the win multiplier beside the reels does not reset.

Players will also be awarded additional free spins for every Scatter that lands during the Free Spins feature.

Express Package symbols will all transform into the same symbol, increasing potential for winning combinations.

The Special Delivery feature is triggered at random during non-winning spins. During this feature, Wilds are added after other symbols are removed at random. This feature guarantees a fresh win.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.2%, slightly above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

Pack and Cash has a high volatility, which means it’s a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. We suggest aiming for the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this slightly high RTP, high volatility warehouse-themed slot.

With decent RTP and strategy-friendly volatility, this game is a solid choice for mathematically-minded players.

The unconventional theme turns out to be quite charming. This, combined with the variety of bonus features, ensures that players will stay engaged.
