BGaming, a dynamic game provider that turns gambling into gaming, has reinforced its team with Ulf Norder as its new chief commercial officer. Norder joins the supplier from QTech Games, where he also served as CCO.

The new hire is known for his intimate knowledge of the sector, as well as his strategic approach to business development. Norder is a skilled individual with years of experience in the sector who has helped his former companies form multiple key partnerships with other firms.

BGaming’s new CCO boasts over twenty years of sales experience and has worked for multiple online gambling companies. In 2004, he joined Tain, where he served as a sales manager for over two years. After that, Norder had a short stint as the sales director of Evolution Gaming. In 2007, he returned to Tain for another two years with the company.

Between 2009 and 2011, Norder served as the sales director of Cake Network, after which he joined SHFL Entertainment as a sales executive. According to his LinkedIn, his career also saw him support gaming companies such as Yggdrasil and Vermantia. He has also held positions at NYX and SG Digital.

As CCO of BGaming, Norder will oversee the company’s commercial strategy. He will focus on expanding its global reach and securing its success in local markets.

Norder Will Help BGaming Conquer Asia

Norder trusts in technology and seeks to drive innovation in iGaming. This made him an ideal candidate for joining BGaming’s team since the company is always looking to augment the player experience through high-quality games.

Norder shared his sentiments about joining BGaming, saying that he is looking forward to levering his experience to help BGaming grow. The appointee also talked about BGaming’s priorities and ambitions:

Our priority will be to establish trust and foster strong relationships with the company’s partners. Our goal is to introduce the necessary tools to increase consumer interest, strengthen player retention for businesses, and continue to shape the future of the gambling industry. Ulf Norder, CCO, BGaming

Marina Ostrovtsova, BGaming’s chief executive officer, welcomed Norder to the company. She said that her team is delighted to have someone as experienced as Norder on board. Ostrovtsova noted that BGaming will leverage Norder’s experience with the Asian iGaming market to expand the company’s presence there.

We will benefit from his expertise as we continue growing our business and expanding our reach globally. Asia will be a big focus, utilizing Ulf’s experience there, but so too will regulated markets like Greece, Bulgaria, Georgia, Slovakia, and Spain. Marina Ostrovtsova, CEO, BGaming
