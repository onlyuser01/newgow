Apparat to Power Hub88 with Games

As per the deal, Apparat will power Hub88 with its full slot games portfolio. As of now, Apparat Gaming, which is a fairly new studio, has only released four titles. The studio’s portfolio currently includes 40 Seven, Fruit Storm, Pharaoh Princess and Total Eclipse. In addition, the operator plans to release two more games in the weeks to follow.

Apparat Gaming’s four existing titles have been seamlessly integrated into Hub88’s offerings, thanks to the latter’s lightweight integration platform. As a result, the studio’s content will be able to quickly reach new operators and players. Meanwhile, Yolo Investments, Hub88’s parent group, secured a stake in Apparat Gaming. This suggests that the investor has further plans for working together with the slot developer.

Apparat Gaming jokingly describes itself as a stereotypical German developer with a dedication to quality and no sense of humor. As such, the ambitious company seeks to enrich the iGaming space with slot titles “with a German accent.”

A Natural Progression for the Two Companies

Dandörfer lauded Hub88’s lightning-fast integration process which allowed Apparat Gaming to integrate its games in a blink of an eye.
