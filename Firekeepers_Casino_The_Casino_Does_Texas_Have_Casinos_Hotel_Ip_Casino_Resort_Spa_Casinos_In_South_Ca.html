One of the biggest boxing fights this year will undoubtedly be the World Heavyweight Championship bout between Tyson Fury and Derek Chisora and Betfred is all over it. The British bookmaker has confirmed via a press communication that it will be the main sponsor for the fight due to take place at the Tottenham Hotspur Stadium on December 3.

The Gypsy King will be defending his title against Chisora, a long-time rival, with the event broadcast on BT Box Office in the United Kingdom and Ireland and ESPN+ in the United States. The stadium can accommodate 60,000 fans and will see just as many attend one of the biggest bouts of all time. The WBC belt is at stake here with Fury feeling confident that he can hold onto his title. Fred Done, Betfred’s founder, said:

I am delighted to be title sponsor of this WBC World Heavyweight Championship fight. Fred Done, Betfred’s founder

Done noted that the heavyweight division was the most exciting for sports fans and bettors, and Betfred would be interested to see how the Fury-Chisora rivalry translates into the excitement all across the board for punters and boxing aficionados.

The odds are presently putting Fury in the lead at 1/33 to beat Chisora. Whether the Gypsy King succeeds remains to be seen.
