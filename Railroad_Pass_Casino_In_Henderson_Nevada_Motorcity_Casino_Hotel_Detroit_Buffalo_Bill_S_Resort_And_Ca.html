Phoenix Suns Overview: Strong in NBA Restart

The Phoenix Suns are coming in strong in the game against the Oklahoma City Thunder. They have won all five of their games since the NBA Season restart arrived. Why are the Suns doing so well in the NBA bubble? We can’t really tell. Judging by individual performance, however, the Suns have put in exceptional work with players such as Ricky Rubio, Devin Booker, and not least Deandre Ayton all making great headway this season.

The Suns also didn’t have to worry about unhealthy players, with the teams showing up in force in Florida, all ready to bring their A game and they did. If there is one player we must focus on, that’s certainly Devin Booker who’s been doing an amazing job so far.

Booker averages some 22.5 points this season, and he’s only going to improve, judging by his most recent plays. Booker soared to 35 points in the 119-112 defeat over the Miami Heat on Friday, posting a great record.

Overall? The Suns are looking exceptionally strong this season and we have every reason to believe they will be successful. So far as sports betting opportunities go, the Suns are a great team to bet on.

What about the Oklahoma City Thunder?

The Oklahoma City Thunder have had a little patchier track record. They are still the fifth seeded team in the Western Conference, but have had a few bad turns of luck against the Denver Nuggets.
