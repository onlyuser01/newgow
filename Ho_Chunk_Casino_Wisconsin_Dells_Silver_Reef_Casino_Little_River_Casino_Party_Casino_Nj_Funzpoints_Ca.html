SportsGrid has signed a new agreement with Wynn Resorts’ sports betting arm, WynnBet. Thanks to this tie-up, WynnBet will offer content for the upcoming In-Game Live All Access Saturday edition.

SportsGrid and WynnBet Team up for In-Game Live All Access Saturday

This production focuses on a panel of experts who will introduce audiences to broader sports coverage, including insights into sports betting. Wynne will be specifically involved with the delivery of in-depth analysis and statistics that have been garnered through the work of its trading team.

As a result, SportsGrid will be able to deliver a range of actionable information, featuring money lines, in-play markets, upcoming game previews, and more. SportsGrid is going to cover a range of sports, including MLB, NBA, and NHL along with World Series, college football games, and FIFA World Cup.

SportsGrid VP of business development and marketing Jordan Bayroff welcomed this partnership and said that the company was pleased to be teaming up with WynnBet for this important opportunity:

“WynnBet’s traders have demonstrated a record of providing insightful perspective and reporting across WynnBet’s platforms. The opportunity to integrate the WynnBet trading team of experts as contributors during the In-Game Live All Access weekend coverage enhances the viewer experience, providing informative, insightful and real-time perspective.” SportsGrid VP of Business Development and Marketing Jordan Bayroff

Both WynnBet and SportsGrid will leverage this partnership through their respective digital channels and intensify their social media reach. SportsGrid is hoping that through this sportsbook partnership, it will expand its reach and be able to deliver insight-driven products that strike home with its target audience and bring better value to players.
