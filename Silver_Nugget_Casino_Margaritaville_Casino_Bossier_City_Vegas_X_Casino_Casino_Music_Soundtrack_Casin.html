The Golden Inn: Game Symbols and Payouts

The game allows a minimum bet set at 0.10 per game. Players can also opt for the maximum bet of 20 per spin. At least three matching symbols are needed to create a win. The size of the win depends on the chosen stake as well as the exact number of matching symbols. According to the payments displayed in the paytable, five-of-a-kind with a 0.60 bet per spin will result in 6x payout for five pot of gold symbols, 3x for five leprechaun hats, 1.5x for five mugs of beer symbols, 0.90x for five sword hilts, 0.72x for five matching triple bars or five double bars, and 0.48x for five single bar symbols. The lowest-paying symbols are the diamonds, the hearts, and the spades with a payout of 0.30x for five matching symbols, and the clubs with only 0.24x for five symbols.

The Golden Inn Gameplay

The gameplay is simple, straightforward, and easy to learn and follow even by inexperienced players who are just getting used to the complex scene of online video slots. To get instant access to the paytable, players can click or tap the hamburger menu on the lower left side of the screen. The speaker icon enables fast music muting/unmuting while the “i” tab used by Nucleus Gaming on all of its slots opens a more complex page with additional game options, Autoplay mode, the rules of the game, and, again, the paytable. The sound effects and background music can both be enabled and disabled at any given moment in time. The same goes for Turbo mode which allows players to enjoy the game at a much faster pace. In Autoplay mode, players can select up to 1,000 automatic spins for a minimum cost of $100 up to a maximum of $20,000 for all 1,000 spins.

The game counts 80 fixed paylines and pays left to right. Contiguous pot of gold symbols on one reel stacks in one. To start playing, the left-sided arrow circle must be clicked or tapped. Once this is done, the symbol turns into a square. Players can click it to make the reels stop spinning.

The Golden Inn: Wilds, Scatters, Free Spins & Multipliers

As the Wild symbol in the game, the four-leaf clover is able to substitute all the other symbols with the exception of horseshoe scatters during the base as well as the bonus game.

The scatters are marked through the golden horseshoes. Landing three to five of these will engage the free spins bonus. When engaged in the free spins round, players can benefit from eight, 12, or 20 free games, depending on the initial number of scatters that triggered the bonus round.

During the same free spins games, the clover wild symbols are capable of landing on the grid with the purpose of delivering either 2x or 3x multipliers. It is also possible to land several of these multipliers for a great boost to the potential prize up for grabs.
