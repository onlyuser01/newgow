3 Oaks Gaming, a fast-growing developer of content and marketing tools for the iGaming industry, unveiled the launch of a new jackpot product.

New Integration-Free Jackpot Product Will Boost Customer Engagement

The company revealed Tuesday that it introduced a new integration-free jackpot product that further boosted its offering. The jackpot product was developed as a seamless solution that doesn’t require integrations or setup from the operators. At the same time, the jackpot product helps operators with customer engagement and acquisition. What’s more, the new solution features multi-brand and local prize pools.

Described by 3 Oaks Gaming as a progressive solution, the new tool offers different jackpot types. The classic jackpot model features Mini, Minor, Major and Grand pots. On the other hand, operators can also benefit from leveraging must-drop and timed jackpots. As a customizable solution, the operators will have to option to set the time limits for such jackpot types.
