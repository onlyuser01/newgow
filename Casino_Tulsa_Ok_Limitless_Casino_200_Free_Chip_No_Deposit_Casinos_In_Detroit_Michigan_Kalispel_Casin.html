Start Exploring the Ancient Mayan Temple

The action is set in an antique Mayan temple buried in the deep and lush jungle, designed with symbols that can trigger Wild waterfalls to suddenly emerge. Players need to start collecting orbs for the opportunity to spin the ancient wheel that can engage one of the two Free Spins games available.

As they continue to explore the temple, players are also likely to come across the iconic Moonlight Roulette that will help them scoop up a series of additional rewards.

Players who land orbs while engaged in the base game gradually fill up the four progressive boxes colored differently. When the bonus game is launched, the progress achieved by players will be used during the Ancient Wheel feature.

Players need a minimum of three bonus orb symbols to enter the Ancient Roulette Wheel that can further carry them in one of the two free spins stages. The first stage counts eight free spins that are played on special reels. The Waxing Moon symbol activates one of the four corners that make up the grid.

The second stage is activated once all four corners are engaged with the help of the Full Moon symbol that allows players to spin the Full Moon Wheel with extra free spins, wilds, and bet multipliers up to 20x available.

The waterfall symbol will trigger a waterfall to cover all the reels while changing into a wild.
