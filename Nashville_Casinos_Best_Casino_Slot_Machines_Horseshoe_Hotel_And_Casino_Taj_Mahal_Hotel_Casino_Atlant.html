The leading tech and entertainment company that delivers engaging technology-powered gaming and sports products, Superbet Group, announced a new senior-level appointment, strengthening its Board. The company confirmed it hired Jimmy Maymann, an expert with international experience in helping companies expand on a global scale, to the role of Deputy Chairman of its Board.

The recent appointment follows other senior-level appointments announced by Supergroup. Only recently, the company confirmed the appointment of Hans-Holger Albrecht to the role of Chairman of the Board. Now, the new strategic hire will further expand the Board's expertise by bringing in Maymann.

Johnny Hartnett, Superbet Group's CEO, shared his excitement in a statement. He acknowledged that the company continues to strengthen its leadership team which reaffirms its commitment to sustainable growth on an international level. Moreover, Hartnett explained that Superbet continues to leverage innovation, which in turn, delivers unique experiences for its customer base.

Finally, Superbet's CEO explained: "The appointment of Jimmy Maymann as Deputy Chairman reconfirms our aspiration to transform the company into a global industry leader, by bringing in top executives who have a clear grasp of the ambitious path for the future which we have designed for our organization."

Holger-Albrecht, who recently joined Superbet's Board as Chairman, was similarly excited to see Maymann become a part of the company's C-suite. He acknowledged that Maymann has a solid professional background. Additionally, Holger-Albrecht deemed Maymann the "perfect fit for the mindset and organizational culture which we are nurturing in Superbet."

Last but not least, Holger-Albrecht said: "We are confident that Jimmy will have a strong positive impact on the Group's strategy and business model, while working together to shape an even brighter future for Superbet."

Maymann added that Superbet has a strong potential for expansion into new international markets. He said that the company's management team leverages progressive thinking which will play a pivotal role in its growth. Finally, Maymann said: "I am delighted to take on the role of Deputy Chairman and offer my expertise and experience, working with Superbet's Board to reach the company's ultimate goal of becoming a global leader in the tech and entertainment industry, while fostering an exemplary environment of responsible gaming."

In his new role as Deputy Chairman of the Board at Superbet, Maymann delivers an extensive experience. As a seasoned veteran within the tech vertical, he was a part of different technology and media companies, NGOs as well as telecoms. His strategic guidance led to the transformative growth of those organizations and Maymann played a pivotal role in those processes.
