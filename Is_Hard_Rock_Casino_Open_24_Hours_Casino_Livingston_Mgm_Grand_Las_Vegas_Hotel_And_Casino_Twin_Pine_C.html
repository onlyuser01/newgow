Caesars Windsor made exciting news on Tuesday, revealing a number of sports betting kiosks on its property, a precursor to the facility’s upcoming dedicated sports betting lounge. This builds upon the momentum for legalized sports gambling in the province of Ontario, Canada, and is part of brands to maintain a strong land-based presence which complements the interactive gambling industry that is rapidly taking place.

Caesars Windsor Offers a Taste of Its Sports Betting Product

The kiosks are set to become operational on Thursday, and they are located inside the Poker Room on the main Skybridge entrance floor. Commenting on this, Caesars Windsor president Kevin Laforet welcomed the opportunity:

We’re thrilled to offer sports bettors a chance to get in on the action with all their favorite sports and enjoy the thrill of single event betting as part of our resort experience at Caesars. Caesars Windsor president Kevin Laforet

The kiosks will be offering the full services of the upcoming lounge, Laforet added, as it is currently under construction. The lounge will feature a number of sports betting windows and 22 65-inch TV screens that will help sports fans who come in person follow the sports betting action.

The new lounge will be located right across from Legends Sports Bar and it will be ready early into the new year, and ahead of the Super Bowl, which should give Caesars Windsor another boost in terms of sports betting handle.

There will be excellent dining and betting options all over the place, Laforet assured and said that the Caesars Sportsbook will bring sports fans the experience they have always looked forward to.
