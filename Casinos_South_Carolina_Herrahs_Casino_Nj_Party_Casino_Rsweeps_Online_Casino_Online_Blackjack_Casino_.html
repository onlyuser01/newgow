Dallas Mavericks Need a Miracle on Wednesday Evening

In the 2022 NBA Playoffs, the Mavericks owe everything to one player – Luka Doncic. The 23-year-old Slovenian forward has been nothing short of fabulous, scoring over 20 points in every single game in the postseason.

Actually, in a couple of games, he was even better than that. In fact, in the previous three duels with the Warriors, Doncic scored a total of 112 points.

Even though he was superb on the court, most of his teammates haven’t been too great. At least that was the case in the first three games of the series. However, in Tuesday’s game, a couple of them stepped up, taking their performances to the next level.

In addition to Doncic, who scored 30 points that evening, five other Dallas players recorded double figures.

Golden State Warriors Going for Another Home Win

Only one team in the entire NBA had a better home record in the regular season than the Golden State Warriors. The team in question is the Phoenix Suns, who had one win more than the Warriors. Speaking of the Suns, they got knocked out of the NBA Playoffs by Dallas.

Not only were the Warriors superb at Staple Center during the regular season, but they’ve been even better in home games during the playoffs. In fact, they got all wins in the 2022 postseason.

Two of those wins happened in the opening two games of the Western Conference finals. In Game 1, they destroyed the Mavs 112-87; in Game 2, the score was 126-117 for the team from San Francisco.
