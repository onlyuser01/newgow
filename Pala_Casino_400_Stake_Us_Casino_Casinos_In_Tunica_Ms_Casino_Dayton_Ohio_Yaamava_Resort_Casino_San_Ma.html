The New Game Offers Rewarding Features, Bonuses

Book of Books is a game that offers win multipliers and expanding symbols. Those features can result in a max win of up to 10,000x for the players. The standard play features a 5×3 grid with 10 paylines.

If a player lands three scatter symbols, they can befit from seven free spins during base gameplay. However, if four of five scatter symbols land on the reels, online players can access a 5×4 or 5×5 grid offering 20 or 40 paylines.

In case a player lands two scatter symbols, they help build a free spins bonus, which can then award a random powerup. “Powerups include additional free spins, expanding symbols and expanding win multipliers worth 1x or 2x,” explains Yggdrasil.

Last but not least, Book of Books offers a Golden Bet feature. This unique bonus option helps increase the chance for the players to trigger a bonus feature. For some jurisdictions, a buy bonus feature is also available that can multiply the bet between 120x and 500x.
