The much-anticipated Brazilian iGaming Summit (BiS) 2022 in São Paulo will be held at the end of June, marking one of the most popular events in Latin America’s gaming industry. The three-day event that will take place at the International Plaza building starting June 28 will host a number of conferences and panels with important guests. Among them, are reputable sports betting operators Betcris and Betwinner.

Betcris Will Have Its Own booth

Founded in Mexico’s Ciudad Juárez, Chihuahua in 2006, Betcris expressed its desire to encourage a “unified sports gaming industry” in the Latin America region, while exploring new regulated markets. Betcris is already a common presence in 15 countries spread across three continents. There, the operator provides top-notch games and unparalleled entertainment solutions. The company also provides in-store services in over 100 stores across Latin America. Over the years, the operator sealed a number of important deals with the Ecuador National Team, the National Football League, and Major League Baseball, to name a few.

Betcris will have its own booth at the 2022 Brazilian iGaming Summit. The booth will be managed by the company’s Brazilian team together with the corporate Betcris crew. The Brazilian branch will be represented by customer relationship management manager Rodrigo and content manager Paula Carvalho. Director of business development Fernardo Garita will be representing corporate at the event meant to reunite the most important players in sports betting, lottery, and affiliate industry.

Betcris, which is Atlante FC’s sponsor and the official sportsbook for Club Pachuca and the Mexican National Soccer Team, also recently sealed a four-year deal with Liga MX club FC Juárez. As part of the new partnership, the operator will be the club’s shirt sponsor for the upcoming four years. Brazilian soccer icon Ronaldinho is also one of Betcris’ most reputable ambassadors.

Betwinner Confirmed Its Presence at the BiS

Betwinner is another loyal company to the BiS event that has also confirmed its presence for the July event. Olívia Marion, the company’s project manager spoke about the brand’s ambassador Roberto Carlos as well as their LatAm office opened in 2021, further explaining their expansion of the portfolio of customers and affiliates with special emphasis on Brazil. Marions called BiS “an incredible opportunity” to get in touch with the most important players in the iGaming industry, as well as an excellent means of sealing new deals.

The event that will reach its second edition in July will also hold an awards night on June 28. The event will be called the Brazilian iGaming Awards and it will honor all brands and professionals that recorded impressive results in 2022.
