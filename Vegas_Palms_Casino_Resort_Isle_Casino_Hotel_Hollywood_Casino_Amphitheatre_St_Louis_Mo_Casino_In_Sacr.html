Get Down to the Fairground and Explore a Massive Payout Potential

Plus, you get to enjoy the classic duck shooting range for some amazing wins, the companies assure. But that is not all, because there is also a hammer man, and well… his hammer, who may activate a special strength meter feature.

There is a special award that waits for you if the meter hits the bell. On top of that, there is a Ferris Wheel that appears during the feature selection process and will award you a variety of prizes along with Strongman, Fire Eater or Duck Shoot.

The Duck Shoot will help you along with getting more Wilds and the Fire Eater will add some random symbols and upgrade some of the existing one, to boost your chances of getting a win once again. The Strong Man will of course stretch across the reels and give the reels a firm push towards better potential payouts.

Besides, there is a Free Spins feature that will work with the Ferris Wheels if you get three of these symbols lined up. The free spins then come with a guaranteed minimum win, and there is the opportunity to also apply modifiers to the experience for some added benefits. Triggering large payouts will be purely dependent on luck, but this does not make it any less fun overall!
