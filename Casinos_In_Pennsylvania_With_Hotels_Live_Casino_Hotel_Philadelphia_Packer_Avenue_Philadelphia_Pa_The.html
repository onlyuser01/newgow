Delasport, a global iGaming software solutions provider, has forged a new strategic partnership with sports betting operator FansBet. The supplier will arm the latter company with solutions, allowing it to capitalize on its Maltese business-to-customer license.

The FansBet Group already has a strong presence in many regulated jurisdictions. The company, which has a player-first approach and always strives to provide its fans with exciting betting experiences, will greatly benefit from the agreement.

Under the deal, Delasport will become FansBet’s iGaming platform supplier. As a result, FansBet will receive access to the provider’s acclaimed solutions, helping it boost its offerings. The operator will therefore be able to leverage its Maltese B2C license to bolster its products with Delasport-powered sports betting, PAM and casino solutions. Delasport will also provide FansBet with a range of engagement tools, such as betting tournaments, spinning wheel games, missions and others.

Delasport’s forte is providing operators across the world with an all-in-one white label iGamming solution. Thanks to the efficient technology it offers, the company has cemented its position as a premier sports betting and casino supplier, which is further attested by the White Label Supplier of the Year award it received this September. This is not the only award Delasport received this year as it was also named Online Sportsbook Provider of the Year at Sigma Europe this month.

As a global company, Delasport will be able to power FansBet’s offerings in multiple markets. Both companies share the same passion for sports betting and want to provide end customers with the best wagering experiences possible.
