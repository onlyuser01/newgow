Rush Street Interactive has added the famous sports reporter Natasha Staniszewski as a brand ambassador. In this position, Staniszewski will represent BetRivers, one of Rush Street’s iGaming and sports betting brands.

Staniszewski Knows Sports Well

The agreement will see Staniszewski leverage her years of experience in the sports sector. She will use her knowledge to make promotional content for BetRivers and engage the brand’s fans. In addition, the sports expert will take part in various activations on TV and radio. She will be responsible for promoting BetRivers and popularizing its brand among fans of professional sports. As a Canadian, Staniszewski will take part in various live events across Ontario as well.

Natasha Staniszewski is celebrated across the country as a true sports guru. Her experience as a sports commentator and reporter has helped her build a robust knowledge of the sector. She also has 10 years of experience as a SportsCentre reporter and is currently serving as StampsTV’s host.

Staniszewski Is Happy to Join BetRivers

Staniszewski shared her thoughts about her new position as BetRivers’ brand ambassador. She said that she is delighted to work with the company and is exciting to be a part of Ontario’s sports betting industry. Staniszewski added that she cannot wait to share her knowledge and passion for sports. She teased she will be creating unique content that will appeal to a wide audience of sports fans.

Richard Schwartz, chief executive officer of Rush Street Interactive, also commented on the matter. He welcomed Staniszewski to the BetRivers family and said that he is excited to have her on board. Schwartz added that the sports expert’s unique perspective and vast experience in sports will be “an incredible in BetRivers’ arsenal. He is sure that Staniszewski will help to engage BetRivers’ experience for sports betting enthusiasts in Ontario and beyond.

Other Recent Appointments

Rush Street Interactive is one of the biggest players in the Americas’ iGaming and sports betting market. The company’s various brands have a huge influence in the USA, LATAM and Canada, reaching millions of bettors.

Other than adding a new brand ambassador, RSI recently boosted its team with several new appointments. Two weeks ago, the company named Zachary Salem as vice president of iGaming. Salem is a long-time employee who had been working with the company for several years.

At the end of March, Rush Street appointed the experienced Bruce Caughill as managing director of the company. Caughill’s decades of experience and intimate knowledge of the Canadian ecosystem were the main reasons he was picked by the company.
