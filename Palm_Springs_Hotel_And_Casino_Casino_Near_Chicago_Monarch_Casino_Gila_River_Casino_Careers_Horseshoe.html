SportingRisk.com, an expert in sports betting, forecasting and analytics, has unveiled a new solution. PropFutures is an innovative product for player prop and interval-based markets.

Sporting Risk Innovates Wagering with PropFutures

PropFutures is described as a single supplier of player prop and interval-based wagering markets, backed by key insights that encourage wagering activity. It uses Sporting Risk’s robust database, predictive modeling and trading to provide what Sporting Risk claims to be the most comprehensive player prop offering ever.

A dedicated trading team will oversee the pricings, which are set to react dynamically to team rosters and in-play events. In addition, PropFutures has a built-in risk management infrastructure that powers clients with full operational visibility.

In short, PropFutures allows operators to maximize their margin, boost efficiency and minimize risk. It is included in both Sporting Risk’s API solution and its Managed Trading Solution. Thus, the new product is available in over 1000 betting markets. Operators can seamlessly integrate PropFutures into their offerings thanks to its flexible integration options.

CEO Newman Says There Is No Other Such Product

Henry Newman, Sporting Risk’s chief executive officer commented on the release of the new solution. He noted that PropFutures is the result of the continuous evolution of the company and its expert predictive modeling and market pricing solutions. Newman is sure that the pairing of player prop pricing and data insights that PropFutures supplies to betting operators will help the latter engage their fans with unforgettable sports wagering experiences.

The CEO concluded by claiming PropFutures is an unparalleled best-in-class product.

PropFutures is currently the only product of its kind offering pricing and insights from only one source, making integration for the client as seamless and flexible as it could possibly be. Henry Newman, CEO, Sporting Risk
