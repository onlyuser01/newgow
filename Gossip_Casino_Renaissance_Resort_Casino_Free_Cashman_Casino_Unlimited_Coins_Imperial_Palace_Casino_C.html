Free Spins, Book Bonus, Collect to Infinity & Chance Level

The Collector symbol accumulates value from both Jackpots and Bonus symbols. The Collector icon, which can pop up randomly, works by adding its value to one of the five existing Treasure Chests seen on the board.

The Treasure Chests are paid out at the end of the Free Spins round. The Book of Faith symbols represent the scatters. Whenever at least three of them are seen landing on the grid, players are rewarded with between 10 and 30 Free Spins.

Once a Free Spins round is activated, a single icon is chosen to be the Expanding Bonus Symbol. Once the respective symbol makes an appearance during the Free Spins, it will proceed to cover the entire row and boost players’ wins before turning into a Bonus Cash symbol.

The latter can be gathered by the Collector mechanic. During this round, players can upgrade the Collector by either multiplying the accumulated prizes for a maximum win of 5000x the bet or by boosting the countdown up to infinity via the Collect to Infinity feature.
