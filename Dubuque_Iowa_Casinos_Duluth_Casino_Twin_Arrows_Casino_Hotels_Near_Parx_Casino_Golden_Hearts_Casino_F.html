FanDuel Shapes Connecticut Entry Strategy with Mohegan

FanDuel Group continues to vie for market share in the highly competitive and still developing US sports gambling market. Facing competition from BetMGM and DraftKings, and not least PointsBet, FanDuel is working on new market access deals, with the latest focusing on the state of Connecticut. Through a partnership between the group and Mohegan Gaming & Entertainment (MGE), FanDuel is now on its way to the Constitution State. FanDuel will bring several segments to the state, subject to regulatory approval.

The company is focusing on online sports betting as well as fantasy sports and iGaming products, together with Mohegan Sun in Uncasville, Connecticut. FanDuel is obligated to work with a land-based partner as per the state’s regulatory framework that allows a host of gambling activities to take place on its territory.

Pineault described FanDuel as “one of the leading sports-tech entertainment companies in the US to bring the brand’s innovative sports betting and iGaming solutions to Mohegan Sun Connecticut.”
