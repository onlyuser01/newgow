Latest SBC Summit Event Focuses on Latin America

The conference is the next in a line of events that are designed to bring industry specialists together and follows the SBC Summit Barcelona which reunited 6,000 delegates. While SBC Summit Latinoamérica is expected to be much smaller in scale, bringing in around 100 speakers and 1,000 attendees, the event is equally significant.

The fast-developing Latin American market presents stakeholders with numerous opportunities but also demands from companies to address regulatory concerns, player protection, and more.

The companies attending are some of the biggest names in the sector who have a clear interest in making a splash in the region and have been working actively to understand Latin American audiences and markets.

Among those are companies such as Betsson Group, Playmaker, Betmaster, EGT interactive, LSports, Betcris, and many others. The executives who are invited as part of the panels have extensive experience working in some of the most important markets in the region, including Mexico, Peru, Puerto Ric, Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Costa Rica, and others.

Discovering Latin America with Industry’s Stakeholders

Overall, the Latin American gambling industry is developing quickly and while regulatory challenges persist, stakeholders are happy to attend the event and improve their understanding of the region and what it has to offer.
