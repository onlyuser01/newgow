Red Rock Resorts, the holding company of Station Casinos in Nevada, announced Monday the appointment of Scott Kreeger as President effective February 28, 2022.

Promoted to President

New Red Rock Resorts President Kreeger joined the company in September 2021 when he assumed the role of Senior Vice President of Development, leaving his position as Director of Operations Development, New Resorts at Galaxy Entertainment Group in Macau. Kreeger joined the ranks at Galaxy in January 2018.

“Scott is the right leader for Red Rock Resorts. Scott’s long history with the company, his knowledge of all aspects of our business and his proven leadership sets the Company up for continued and accelerated future growth.” Frank Fertitta III, Chairman and CEO, Red Rock Resorts

Kreeger joined Galaxy Entertainment Group after 3 years as President and Chief Operating Officer at SLS Las Vegas Resort and Casino, a role he assumed in October 2014 and held until December 2017. Before joining the casino operator in Macau, he served as President and Chief Operating Officer at Revel Resort and Casino, a role he assumed in July 2013 and held until the property was sold.

Kreeger initially spent 13 years at Station Casinos, the land-based casino brand owned and managed by Red Rock Resorts, serving in several key senior positions in operations, property management, marketing, technology and Native American gaming since joining in 2000.

Station Casinos

Provider of gaming and entertainment to the residents of Las Vegas, Nevada, Station Casinos operates regional entertainment properties located in the Las Vegas valley and offers various other amenities such as restaurants, entertainment venues, movie theaters, bowling and convention space, besides the traditional casino gaming.
