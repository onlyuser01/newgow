Enteractive, Ready for More Growth

One of the primary reasons for Williams’ appointment was the company’s intention to keep expanding in the US market. This is also the new head of account management’s plan, as expressed in a statement following her appointment. Enteractive is prepared to boost operators’ retention and player loyalty rates with their advanced one-on-one conversation solutions, while maintaining all player data safe, well-protected against vulnerabilities, and fully compliant with GDPR and G4 Responsible Gaming requirements.

Seven Years of Experience

Foster also called Williams’ experience an “extensive” one in the iGaming industry, further explaining that Enteractive’s decision to appoint her to the important position was a smart call. Williams is expected to help support the company’s business growth in the following years while continuing to build bridges between operators and players.

Before joining Enteractive, Williams spent over a year as WKND’s head of VIP in Malta. At the beginning of 2018, Williams started a three-year VIP relations manager job with Paddy Power Betfair. She was also a regional account manager for DMA UK and an account manager for a food and beverage manufacturing company at the start of her career.

Williams commented on her appointment, expressing excitement for joining the company Enteractive on its path to turn into a world leader in reactivation and retention.
