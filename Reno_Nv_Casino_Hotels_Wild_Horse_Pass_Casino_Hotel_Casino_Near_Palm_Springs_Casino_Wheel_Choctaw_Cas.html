The slot invites players to inject themselves with a dose of cuddly pets that will immediately make them feel relaxed and send them into a playful mood. To add more thrill to the action on the reels, the studio opted for three different types of cats and an equal number of cats when designing the symbols, accompanied by imagery representative of their food, including milk bottles and bones, collars, or toy mice. Whether players favor the lovely cats or the adorable dogs, they will most likely end up with some nice payouts and free spins rewards to keep them going during the game that can be easily accessed on both desktop and mobile devices.

Purrfect Pets: Game Symbols and Payouts

As expected, the main symbols in the game are the dogs and the cats along with their accessories and food items. One particular thing that drew our attention was the fact that the game does not feature any of the standard royal card symbols on the reels. In other words, all the available paytable symbols are entirely related to the cats and dogs, without disrupting the action and theme.

Like most of RTG’s titles, Purrfect Pets has a tighter betting structure, which means players who enjoy taking a risk might not find much appeal to the game. Nonetheless, casual players are likely to have a good time exploring the game that lets them choose between 0.25 and 6.25 coins on a spin.

The rich payout table is an element that is expected to attract most players with its wins scored by matching five identical symbols, an action that automatically pays in multipliers of the line bet. For example, landing five collar and milk symbols will trigger a 200x win, while toy symbols will bring players a 250x payout. Five identical food symbols are worth 300x, while golden puppies bring players 750x in wins. The lovely kittens in teacup symbols pay double – 1,500x – for five-of-a-kind, while the husky puppy symbols pay 2,000x. Five tabby kitten symbols pay 3,000x. The wild Cat symbol pays 5,000x and it replaces all the other symbols in the game while the dog scatter symbol pays 200x. Scatter wins are multiplied by the total bet.

Purrfect Pets Gameplay

Once the player has selected the desired bet and active lines, they need to click or tap the “Spin” tab and start spinning the reels. The player interface is user-friendly, even though some players might feel the paytable and game controls are a little harder to come across in the menu and help buttons located in the bottom right corner.

Purrfect Pets: Doggy Dollars Feature, Wild Cat Feature, Cats vs Dogs Feature, Random Riches & Progressive Jackpots

The dog lets players enjoy a series of excellent bonus features with special emphasis on the free spins.

Doggy Dollars Feature. Players must land a minimum of three dog scatter symbols to get 10 free spins with 2x wins. All scatters turn into wilds, with at least three more dog symbols or three additional cat symbols awarding an extra 10 free games.

Wild Cat Feature. At least three cat wild symbols bring players 10 free spins featuring special 3x cat wild wins. The feature cannot be triggered again.

Cats vs Dogs Feature. Finishing a base game without triggering the Doggy Dollars or Wild Cat feature brings players a random bonus. Players are given the chance to choose from three available symbols and reveal their prize: Doggy Dollars, Wild Cat, or the Random Riches feature.

Random Riches. Players are awarded seven, 10, 20, or 50 free games. All prizes are double in size thanks to a 2x multiplier and they feature a wild with a 6x multiplier. During the feature, the scatter symbols turn into extra wild symbols. Players can retrigger 10 more free games provided they come across three cat or dog scatter symbols.

Progressive Jackpots. These jackpots can be randomly engaged while playing the base game. When this occurs, the wins are added to the rest of the prizes while the Random Jackpot contribution is 1.5% of the RTP at most.
