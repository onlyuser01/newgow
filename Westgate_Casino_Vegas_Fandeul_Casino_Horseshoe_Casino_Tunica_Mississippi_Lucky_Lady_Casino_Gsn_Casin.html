Moon Sisters Hold and Win Slot Review

Launched in the spring of 2020 and counting five reels, three rows, 25 pay lines, an x1000 grand jackpot, and a lucrative Bonus Game, Moon Sisters Hold, and Win video slot is an interesting game from Booongo. The game has a popular Asian theme in place introduced to players through the lovey Moon sisters in their beautiful Japanese garden.

If you are ready to learn more about the game that offers well-focused gameplay with a lot of symbolism and mouth-watering features, keep reading.

Theme, Storyline, and Graphics: Explore the Japanese Garden with the Lovely Moon Sisters

The game tells the story of two beautiful sisters, the Pink Moon Sister and the Purple Moon Sister, who spend their time in a Japanese garden, walking up and down wooden bridges while crossing a mesmerizing lake. Their footsteps are constantly guided by the beautiful lanterns placed in the trees and the gorgeous, colorful butterflies flying all around them.

The attractive sisters are also surrounded by wonderful lotus flowers and they are even seen wearing these flowers in their hair, along with kansashi, which are traditional Japanese hairpins and hair decorations. They are dressed in traditional Japanese kimonos and they constantly admire the lovely koi fish in the lake while the shining Moon is protecting them and bringing players lucrative wins.

The action is set at nighttime, with the starry night sky and the trees packed with lanterns as a relaxing background. The graphics are of premium quality, with well-thought designs chosen by Booongo’s team.

Moon Sisters Hold and Win: Game Symbols and Payouts

The game’s paytable encompasses symbols part of the theme, including butterflies, the Pink and Purple Moon Sister symbols, pink blossoms, and the moon symbol, along with the standard Jacks, Aces, Kings, and Queens.

The slot allows players to set minimum bets starting at 0.25 while the maximum bet can go up to 60. Matching five Pink Moon Sistter symbols on a payline triggers an x50 win. The Purple Moon Sister symbol brings players x480 when matching five identical symbols. A combo of five butterflies generates a win of x360, while five pink blossom symbols will trigger an x240 payout. Matching five-of-a-kind standard card symbols bring players a maximum win of x120.

The wild symbol is represented by a pair of koi fish and it works by replacing the rest of the symbols in the game except for the Bonus Symbol. The Mini jackpot Bonus and the Major Jackpot Bonus symbols are also present on the grid and they can pop up the reels while the Bonus Game is activated.

Sounds and Animations

Moon Sisters Hold and Win features a lovely soundtrack created with the help of traditional Japanese music. The soundtrack also comes with lots of gentle Oriental influences and subtle sounds suitable for a relaxing gaming experience that can be enjoyed late at night. Once the wins start to roll, the sounds really start to pick up and the music becomes more fast-paced.

The animations in the game are mostly visible during the Bonus Game when the eye-catching, shiny moon symbols are seen triggering respins and attracting massive wins.

Moon Sisters Hold and Win Gameplay

With the help of the “i” (information) button in the top right corner, new players have a chance to get rapidly acquainted with the available win amounts for each of the possible symbol combinations on the grid, along with additional information regarding the available features and the common rules they need to know to get started.

The dynamic paytable displays all the values of the payout depending on the chosen bets. Winning combos are formed on a single payline on adjacent reels, starting with the utmost left reel.

Autoplay mode is also available by clicking the respective icon underneath the green SPIN button on the right side. Players must use the dedicated plus and minus signs to change the size of their bets. They can also select the stacked coins symbol to choose their preferred amount ranging between 0.25 and 60. The game is initiated once the SPIN button on the bottom right side of the screen is clicked or tapped. The Last Win and Balance amounts are also visible at the bottom.

Moon Sisters Hold and Win: Bonus Game, Jackpots & Mystery Symbols

Moon Sisters Hold and Win encompasses a series of attractive features including the Bonus Game that is specific to many of Booongo’s popular video slots and the mini and major jackpots.

Bonus Game

To scoop up the x1000 jackpot available in the Bonus Game, players must collect all 15 Bonus Symbols there. In order to engage in the Bonus Game, a minimum of six Bonus Symbols represented by the moon symbols need to pop on reels. The moon symbols are sticky symbols which means they will remain on the reels for the entire duration of the Bonus Game.

The potential wins while playing the Bonus Game range from x1 to x10, x14, x16, x18, x20, or x25 the total bet. The same Bonus Game is initiated with a total of three respins. Each fresh moon symbol that is seen showing up on the reels resets the number of respins to three.

The end of the Bonus Game comes when players have no more respins left or when they successfully scoop up all 15 available moon symbols. When this occurs, the value of these symbols is added together so players can learn what is their Bonus Win.

Mini Jackpot, Major, and Grand Jackpots

The sight of the Mini or the Major Jackpot Bonus symbols on any random location on the grid, while engaged in the Bonus Game, will trigger the Mini or the Major Jackpots. The first jackpot brings players x30 the total bet while the second jackpot is worth x150 the total bet.

Provided the grid is filled with all 15 moon symbols, players are rewarded with the Grand jackpot that brings them x1,000x the bet.

Final Thoughts

Moon Sisters Hold and Win has an excellent Oriental theme and design paired with a satisfying series of features including a rewarding Bonus Game and three jackpot options for up to 1000x the bet. If you are searching for a soothing game with beautiful aesthetics, give this Booongo title a go.
