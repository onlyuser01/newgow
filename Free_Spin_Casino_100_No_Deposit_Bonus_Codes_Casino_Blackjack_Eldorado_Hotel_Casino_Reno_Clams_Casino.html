Joining over 40 Other Companies

Simplebet offers micro-betting products and is a globally recognized specialist in its niche. Joining the International Betting Integrity Association (IBIA) means it will be subjected to the same industry-recognized standards for integrity as more than 40 other companies, along with north of 100 sports betting brands part of IBIA, totaling more than $135 billion in combined betting handle annually.

Integrity and trust are non-negotiable at Simplebet, so we are excited to be partnering with the IBIA, who are the world’s leading independent integrity monitor. Melissa Davis, executive vice president of business affairs and general counsel, Simplebet

Joining efforts with IBIA also means joining around 50% of all regulated commercial betting done by current members of IBIA.

IBIA welcomed Simplebet amid its ranks very warmly. “I am very pleased to welcome Simplebet to IBIA; its membership highlights the operator’s commitment to protecting the integrity of betting and related sporting events,” said Khalid Ali – the chief executive of IBIA.

The addition of Simplebet adds to IBIA’s North American presence and continues its expansion into the US especially.
