Super Diamond Wild review

Theme and Storyline

The goal of Super Diamond Wild seems to be simplicity. Three by three grid, classic symbols, with just a few basic tweaks.

This is all presented with a one-armed bandit style set of visuals with glass fronted reels and flashing lights.

Low tier symbol gives a huge win if combined with the top (x10) multiplier

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

It helps greatly that all this is given a real surface shine by excellent, hi-def artwork and animations that make for a good looking slot from iSoftBet.

The music is easily faded into the background and has a soothing style that goes well with the stripped down nature of the slot machine.

Win thanks to hitting of 2 Wild symbols

Gameplay

The three by three grid has five win lines covering the five straight lines you can have of three symbols on a grid of that size.

There are a few small tweaks to spice up the play by play but these are small changes like respins and a multiplier wheel.

On the whole the gameplay for Super Diamond Wild is very basic. For some players, this will be a selling point, for others a source of abject boredom.

Gameplay video featuring mega win

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbols substitute for all other symbols.

If two of the reels land a matching stack (i.e. three of the same symbol on both) and there are no winning combinations, then the remaining reel respins once.

Hit full screen of the same symbols and you multiply your winnings in the Bonus Wheel round

Hitting top multiplier in the Bonus Wheel almost guarantees a huge win

If you manage to land nine identical symbols in one spin, filling all the reels, you will trigger a wheel of fortune style mini-game which will give you a randomised multiplier between x2 and x10 for your win.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 95.66%. This is a little disappointing as our yardstick return to player is 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum of $/£/€0.05 to a maximum bet per spin of $/£/€50.00.

The volatility of this game is medium to high. Given the low RTP this is not high enough to attract us.

Conclusion

Super Diamond Wild is a visually pleasing slot. Well designed both at the gameplay level and at the simple level of graphics.

However, a low RTP scuppers its chances of getting a recommendation from us. Particularly with its moderate levels of volatility.
