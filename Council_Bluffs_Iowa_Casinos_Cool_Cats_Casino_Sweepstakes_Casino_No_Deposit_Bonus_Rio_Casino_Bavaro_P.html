Leading content aggregation platform Pariplay, owned by global gaming technology manufacturer Aspire Global, announced the appointment of Enrico Bradamante as the company’s new Chief Commercial Officer (CCO).

Spearhead Global Expansion

The appointment of Enrico Bradamante as CCO aims to further boost the expansion strategy of the aggregator as the platform operator is targeting growth across regulated gaming markets worldwide, including benefiting from the potential of North American jurisdictions where Pariplay already entered into several key business partnerships.

“Thrilled to welcome Enrico to the team,” said Pariplay Managing Director Adrian Bailey. He outlined the high hopes the company is placing on its new executive to leverage his “wealth of knowledge and expertise” and help it continue to affirm itself as a “leading supplier to the global iGaming industry.”

Following an impressive period of growth for the company which has seen us enter new jurisdictions and enhanced our product offering the appointment of Enrico reflects our commitment to deliver the best service for our partners and the strategic decision to further extend our business into regulated markets.” Adrian Bailey, Managing Director, Pariplay

The founder of industry trade association iGaming European Network (iGEN), Bradamante brings to Pariplay more than nine years of industry experience, including a role as Managing Director of NetEnt.

Still an active board member of Enteractive, the new CCO expressed his delight to join Pariplay at such an “exciting time for the company and the industry as a whole.”

Pariplay is a dynamic and innovative supplier with a premium platform and superior in-house content, and I look forward to bringing my own know-how to the team to further evolve our products and to continue to push the boundaries in the industry.” Enrico Bradamante, CCO, Pariplay

In 2021 alone, the aggregation platform operator launched with more than 20 new gaming operators, including names like Ainsworth Game Technology (AGT), Amelco and FBM Digital Systems, to expand its operations across European and Latin American gaming markets, as well as in the US.

Pariplay managed to triple its growth over the last three years, boasting bets amounting to over €850 million ($1.15 billion) per month.

Live and certified in jurisdictions like New Jersey in the US, UK, Spain, Sweden, Bulgaria, Italy, Portugal, Denmark, Romania, Germany and Switzerland in Europe, as well as Colombia in Latin America, Pariplay’s Fusion platform is serving more than 100 operators with its portfolio of over 120 slot titles and premium content.

Pariplay is also licensed for B2B operations in New Jersey and West Virginia in the US, as well as the UK, Malta, Gibraltar, Romania and Greece in Europe.
