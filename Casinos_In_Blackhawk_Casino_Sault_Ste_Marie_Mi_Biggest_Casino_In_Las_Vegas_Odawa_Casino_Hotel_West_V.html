Zeus Returns to Make Players Rich

As the title suggests, Gates of Olympus 1000 is another Greek mythology-inspired game, adding to Pragmatic Play’s portfolio of thematic titles. The new game expands on symbols and mechanics players are already familiar with, while allowing players to win even bigger prizes.

When playing Gates of Olympus 1000, players will collect a variety of symbols, including crowns, gems and goblets. Zeus himself serves as a Scatter symbol, allowing players to trigger the bonus game.

Instead of using paylines, the 6×5 slot allows symbols to pay anywhere on the screen, as long as players collect 8 or more of them. In the meantime, Zeus, who stands alongside the reels, can randomly strike his thunderbolt, awarding random multipliers from 2x to 1,000x.

The game also features a tumble mechanic whereby winning symbols would disappear from the reels and get replaced by new symbols, allowing players to potentially score multiple wins per spin.

The New Game Is a Striking Sequel

Collecting four or more of the aforementioned Zeus Scatter symbols will provide players with 15 free spins. During this bonus game, any multiplier that hits on a winning spin is added to the total multiplier, allowing players to win big.

In addition, landing on four more Zeus symbols during the bonus game will retrigger the feature, providing players with a shot at even bigger prizes. Speaking of winning big, the game allows players to win up to 15,000x their bet.
