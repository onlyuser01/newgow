Mohegan has made sure to strengthen its digital communications capabilities by officially appointing Heather Menzano as the new vice president of web communications. Menzano will oversee the entertainment and gaming brand’s development of the app and casino website in North America.

Menzano Has Plenty of PR and Marketing Experience

As an industry veteran, Menzano has taken various marketing and public relations roles at well-established brands. Some of those brands include Caesars Entertainment, Resorts Casino Hotel and Revel Entertainment. Resorts Casino Hotel is a brand managed by Mohegan.

In her new role, Menzano will be tasked with elevating both public and guest communications with the goal of growing potential revenue channels. She will be leading strategic planning, monitoring and reporting and will set goals for the annual marketing plan of Mohegan. KPI reporting will also be one of her responsibilities.

She will report to George Galinsky, the senior vice president of marketing communications. Apart from the aforementioned responsibilities, Menzano will be responsible for creating various communications strategies with corporate heads across various PR, social media and marketing operations.

Galinsky took a moment to comment on Menzano’s appointment. He said that she joins Mohegan “at a pivotal time” and thanks to the fact that she has over 14 years of working experience in strategic communications, Galinsky thinks that she will “undoubtedly contribute to Mohegan’s continued growth.”
