Darwin Gaming has become Yggdrasil’s YG Masters’ latest partner to fully create and deploy its gaming content on the operator’s vast network.

One More Partner for Yggdrasil

Online gaming startup Darwin has joined Yggdrasil’s YG Masters program and will access the leading operator’s GATI technology in the process. The Malta-based studio will develop and deploy its content through YG Masters, dedicated to third-party game creators. Yggdrasil’s proprietary technology and large network allow games to quickly grow on the market and deliver premium gaming titles.

The new YG partner will benefit from the cutting-edge GATI (Game Adaptation Tools and Interface) technology, as it entirely powers the YG program. GATI is a regulation-ready standardized development toolkit that offers a powerful package for developers to efficiently produce and distribute their content worldwide. Greatly improving reach and return-on-investment (ROI), YG Masters has been chosen by many gaming studios, including Betsson, ReelPlay, and Circle Eight Games, among others.

Experienced Team at the Head of Darwin

Founded in 2020, Darwin is led by a strong team with experience in the wagering industry which aims to develop mobile gaming entertainment and improve player experience with high-quality content. Darwin focuses on providing the best entertainment with the right balance of sounds, graphics, ergonomy, and touch interaction.

According to the studio’s CEO Nuno Gonçalinho, this collaboration Yggdrasil is “a great fit” for Darwin’s unique content, as a company “that is well known for pushing the industry to new heights”. Rui Pena, the company’s COO, noted Yggrasil’s leadership in the industry and commended the operator’s technical knowledge and “well-implemented out-of-the-box ideas”.

Yggdrasil’s Head of Partner Programs Stuart McCarthy commended the partnership and said that the startup has a “unique and innovative approach” to game development, “with a vision to change the game”.
