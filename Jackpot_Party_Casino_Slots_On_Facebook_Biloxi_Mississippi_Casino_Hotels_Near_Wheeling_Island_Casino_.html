A top three betting and gaming operator in the US is teaming with one of the largest cruise ship operators to provide retail and mobile gaming to cruise ship guests.

Phased Rollout to US-Branded Carnival Ships

BetMGM, one of the leading three sports betting and gaming operators in the US, alongside DraftKings and FanDuel, announced today a partnership with Carnival Corporation, the operator of Carnival Cruise Line, Holland America Line and Princess Cruises, among others, for a phased rollout of its gaming platform on more than 50 cruise ships over the coming months.

Excited with the opportunities presented by the new partnership, BetMGM’s chief executive officer Adam Greenblatt outlined that BetMGM has found “an ideal partner in Carnival Corporation and look forward to providing” its sports betting and online gaming products to its passengers.

“This is another great opportunity for us to further expand BetMGM’s footprint,” Greenblatt concluded.

The partnership grants BetMGM the right to offer its branded digital and cash-based sports betting, and its real-money digital gaming products onboard Carnival’s US-branded ships while they are in international waters. Carnival and BetMGM will also collaborate on marketing and promotional campaigns.
