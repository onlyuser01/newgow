T-basket to Send Ripples for STS in Poland

STS also stands to benefit from the opportunity, as it is now able to integrate hybrid sports products into its portfolio, all of which are linked to a high level of player engagement and overall positive sentiment.

Among the titles to launch on the market is T-basket which will now be offered exclusively by STS and bolster its own portfolio of esports, virtual sports, and sports betting products across the board.

STS has seen tremendous growth in its own operations over the past years. The company has multiplied its revenue tenfold and opened more than 400 betting shops across Poland. The company presently has offices in Warsaw and Katowice in Poland, but also in Prague, the Czech Republic, and Malta.

STS is confident that it can continue to grow its product and reach as it seeks to service millions of players in the targeted regions. Meanwhile, Twain Sport is confident in the success of the T-basket game which features 6,300 head-to-head basketball matches every month.

The potential of this partnership is significant and as STS becomes another of our highly valuable partners, we are delighted to award it exclusive Polish rights to both T-basket and our exciting upcoming release T-kick – two ground-breaking hybrid sports that we’re sure will perform strongly.
