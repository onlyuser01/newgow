SG to Roll Out AToM

AToM, short for Access To On Demand Money, is a cashless solution that can be used for table games. The solution enables players to access their debit seamlessly while at a live table game by giving them access to their funds without the need to visit a kiosk. Moreover, the seamless process doesn’t include out-of-network or back-end fees chargeable by the consumer’s bank. To access their funds, players can easily swipe their debit card and enter their PIN.

Scientific Games revealed that the AToM solution could easily be implemented within management and accounting systems. Additionally, the operators that are using the solution gain access to live financial data that includes all of the transactions completed via the solution. Besides being easy to implement, the cashless solution is also PCI compliant as well as secured and certified.
