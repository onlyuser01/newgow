Mohegan has announced its official schedule for the Hot Summer Fun celebrations for all of its venues

Towards the beginning of May, Mohegan Tribal Gaming Authority reported double-digit growth in its second fiscal 2023 quarter due to the company’s top performance across all digital operations.

Now, the owner, developer, and manager of top-tier entertainment resorts located in the US, Canada, and Northern Asia has announced the events for the much-anticipated anniversary of its yearly Hot Summer Fun celebration.

As expected, the company has announced a large number of activities and giveaways that will be available in-store. The prizes are said to be even more generous this year while the all-new venues that will be available for partying and the live performances are prone to turn into the “ultimate toast to summer 2023.”

Kickoff Events

Once more, Casino Niagara, Mohegan Sun, Ilani, Mohegan Pennsylvania, Resorts Casino Hotel, Mohegan Casino Las Vegas, and Fallsview Casino Resort will be the main venues that will be hosting the season-long celebrations.

Mohegan Sun, which is also the brand’s flagship resort, has kicked off the holiday weekend by inaugurating its new Sun Patio Bar. The new venue measures 2,100 square feet in size and it is an outdoor patio with a 50-foot long full-service bar, six hi-definition screens, and plenty of outdoor seating.

Every Friday during Hot Summer Fun Party on the marked Sun Patio dates, the bar will welcome guests who are at least 21 and offer them drink specials, onsite food truck meal options, and live music performances.

The bar will also be open on Saturdays from noon to midnight and on Sundays from noon to 9:00 p.m.

Mohegan Pennsylvania to Kickstart the Celebrations with Live Harness Racing

Starting July 1, the Pennsylvania venue will host live harness racing at Pocono Downs, with events including live camel and live ostrich races, food trucks, and face painting, among others. The event will also address the FSB Initiative aimed at veterans and local charities such as Marley’s Mission.

The same property will also host its Party on the Patio series on Thursdays, with a number of tribute bands for Queen, Zac Brown, Bruce Springsteen, or DMB that will walk into the spotlight and offer quality live entertainment.

Mohegan Pennsylvania will also offer promotions Monday to Sunday, offering guests dining vouchers, drawing entry multipliers, gifts, Free Slot Play, and Status Point multipliers, among others.

Resorts Casino Hotel Held a Beach Ball Drop on the Atlantic City Boardwalk

To celebrate 45 long years of Hot Summer Fun, Resorts Casino Hotel started Memorial Day weekend with a beach ball drop on the Atlantic City Boardwalk while announcing many outdoor activities that will take place throughout the property during the summer.

Resorts Casino Hotel will also organize T-Shirt Series Giveaways on Mondays, themed Mystery Money Kiosk games on Tuesdays with prizes of up to $4,500, Summer Gift Series on selected Wednesdays and Thursdays, and Rolling 45 Friday events when five lucky guests will get to roll the Craps dice for a chance to win up to $4,500.

Players are also invited to take part in the All Summer Earning event that will start on May 27 and end on September 3, with a prize of $1,000,000 for rolling 45 along with the chance to roll for $1 million.

The same venue will also play Disco Inferno in the Superstar Theater on Sunday while allowing guests to visit the retro pop-up bar at Lounge 78 all summer long.

Fallsview Casino Resort Will Organize Weekly Outdoor Events

Every Thursday until the end of August, the Backyard BBQ at Fallsview Casino Resort will be hosting a series of live music events accompanied by a food and drinks market.

The venue will also organize a night of artisan cocktails along with indoor and outdoor entertainment on Fridays and Saturdays at After Dark at R5 starting May 19 until June 30.

Fallsview Casino Resort/Casino Niagara will also reward guests with Instant Win Monday events with up to $250 Momentum Dollars in prizes, Play’N Go Slot Tournaments on Tuesdays at Casino Niagara, Drive into Summer events on Wednesdays in July at Casino Niagara, and on Wednesdays at Fallsview Casino Resort in August, and even a Car Grand Prize Giveaway for a chance to win a 2023 Lexus RX 350 Hybrid.

Mohegan Sun – More Amazing Daily Promotions

Every Monday between June 26 and August 28, Mohegan Sun members will have the opportunity to win a share of $10,000 in cash.

The venue will also host a Catchin’ Rays Tuesdays promotion with up to $500,000 in prizes, Wild Winning Wednesdays that will bring guests from Double Momentum Dollars on slots, table games, poker, stadium gaming, or when wagering in Race Book that will result in Double Status Points.

The list of daily promotions continues with the Thrills & Chills Thursdays where guests can win a share of $10,000 Momentum Dollars and the Fun in the Sun Fridays when the first 1,000 Momentum members that will visit the Play+ Event Center will be rewarded with a free Hot Summer Fun T-shirt.

Mohegan Casino Las Vegas and Ilani

Mohegan Casino Las Vegas will reward guests with daily promotions like the Multiplier Monday promo with multiple Status Points and Momentum Dollars, Taquitos Tuesdays where Leap, Ascent and Soar members will be served with a free order of avocado and roasted corn taquitos at Casa Calavera, Ice Cream Wednesdays where members will get one tasty scoop of ice cream at Afters Ice Cream, and Thirsty Thursdays –where members will get to enjoy a savory margarita when visiting Heat and Desert Star.

Ilani members will have the chance to participate in the FREE T-Shirt Fridays promotion, the $200,000 Hot Summer Nights promo on Thursdays, and the Rooftop Tribute Series organized on Fridays starting June 16.

All Momentum members are eligible to participate in a new promotion on each day of the week, with the chance to register for Mohegan Sun’s free rewards program at any Player’s Club booth across all of the brand’s properties.
