The agreement will help QTech’s Asian partners capitalize on the fast growth of the esports betting vertical

Oddin.gg, a leading esports betting business-to-business provider, has agreed to provide QTech Games with its full suite of esports solutions.

Oddin.gg Powers QTech with Esports Betting Solutions

Under the agreement, the leading gaming distributor for Asia will receive access to Oddin’s esports odds feeds, iFrame solution, esports-optimized UX and risk management. This will significantly enhance QTech’s offering while expanding Oddin’s global presence, allowing QTech’s Asian partners to adopt the provider’s esports solutions.

Oddin.gg is hailed as the go-to provider when it comes to everything related to esports betting. The B2B company offers robust esports feeds for the biggest competitive gaming titles, including Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO), Dota 2 and League of Legends.

In addition, Oddin.gg has connections with the most influential leagues and championships, thus securing only the best, most relevant and most precise esports data and solutions for its partners.

QTech Praised Oddin for Making Esports Simple

Spokespeople from the two companies addressed the newly-minted agreement. Daniel Long, the chief commercial officer of QTech Games, said that the deal with Oddin is very rewarding to his team. He praised the esports-oriented brand for simplifying an otherwise very complex field.

Significantly, since Oddin.gg boasts the highest industry uptime and sharpest odds, its solutions will also have a positive impact on our partners’ operating margins. Our trading analysts were taken with the robustness of the esports lines consistently offered by Oddin.gg which, in turn, will deliver total peace of mind to our international operators. Daniel Long, CCO, QTech Games

Long added that QTech Games is fully committed to remaining relevant and supplying its partners with relevant in-demand content. The distributor leverages machine learning to identify player preferences in various regions.

Oddin.gg’s managing director and co-founder, Marek Suchar, also shared his thoughts on the agreement. He noted that there are many monetization channels that operators can use to improve profitability. In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, esports is now more popular than ever, providing sportsbooks with tons of opportunities.

Suchar emphasized that Oddin is the standout leader in esports betting services and has an industry-leading portfolio of esports-related entertainment options. He is sure that the agreement with QTech Games will help the distributor’s partners make successful forays into the vertical.

QTech Games’ extensive platform offers a natural habitat within which we’ll distribute our content across exciting emerging markets, particularly on mobile. Marek Suchar, MD, Oddin.gg
