Nitropolis 2 review

Theme and Storyline

This sequel game is themed around a dystopian, animal-led world.

Set in a gritty city that will bring Blade Runner to mind, this game has a dark and moody atmosphere. On the reels, players will meet animal characters armed with deadly weapons.

Players will warily explore this strange world and try to keep up with the cutthroat canines.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

This game’s soundtrack is upbeat and filled with the autotuned vocalizations of a macabre host. Sirens and dog howling blend with the music to make it clear that players are in a crime-filled city.

The richly animated world is teeming with detail from billboards to a glinting blue light coming from each window in the backdrop’s buildings. Observant players will notice signs forbidding dogs from defecating while a rebellious dog does just that underneath the sign.

The fierce and sometimes cute dogs on the reels have plenty of character and are dressed for a street fight. Whether brass knuckles or a shotgun, these dogs haven’t left their toys at home.

Overall, the animation adds a lot of style to this world and the soundtrack ensures that players stay immersed in it.

Gameplay

Nitropolis 2 is a six reel, four row slot.

Wins are awarded for matching symbols in adjacent reels from left to right.

Through a cascade-like feature, symbols are part of winning combinations respin as individuals to allow a new symbol into the position.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Bonus symbol.

The Nitro Reels fall over two symbol positions while containing 4, 6, 8, 10, or 12 paying symbols.

The Nitro Booster awards a variety of special features including:

Respins

Winning combinations paying in both directions

Increased Wilds

Increased high value symbols

Increased matching symbols

When players land 3 or more Bonus symbols, this triggers the Bonus Game. While in this feature, every Nitro Reel will remain in play for the duration of the free spins.

The number of free spins received in the Bonus game goes up depending on how many Bonus symbols have landed:

3 Bonus symbols = 10 free spins

4 Bonus symbols = 15 free spins

5 Bonus symbols = 20 free spins

6 Bonus symbols = 25 free spins

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 95%, well below our average yardstick of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.20 up to a maximum of $/£/€100 per spin.

The volatility of this game is medium, which makes it a less ideal match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We do not recommend this low RTP, medium volatility dystopian animal-themed slot.

Despite a unique theme, this game’s RTP is far too low to be a good choice. The middling volatility does little to make this game appealing to strategy-minded players, so, unfortunately, it is one to skip.

The effort put into the animation shows and the bonuses are fun, but this does little good without the substance of better RTP and higher volatility.
