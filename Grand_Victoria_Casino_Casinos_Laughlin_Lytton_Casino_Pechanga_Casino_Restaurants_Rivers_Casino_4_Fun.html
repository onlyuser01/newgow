Canadian sports betting operator NorthStar Gaming has signed an agreement with the National Basketball Association (NBA) and is being listed as an Authorized Gaming Operator by the league.

Canada’s NorthStar Gaming Becomes One of NBA’s Authorized Gaming Operators

NorthStar Gaming is partnering with NBA in a new agreement that will see the Ontario-based sports betting operator offer live NBA data to its customers.

The NBA logo will be included in NorthStar Gaming’s platform and Canadian bettors will be able to enjoy an alluring sports wagering experience.

The collaboration will also ensure that the integrity of all NBA matches is being protected with the implementation of professional integrity standards.

Michael Moskowitz, CEO of NorthStar Gaming has elaborated on what players can expect: “As an Authorized Gaming Operator, NorthStar Gaming will connect our players with official data and stats from the NBA and provide them with an insights-packed user experience. We’re excited to offer current and future NorthStar Bets players with an authentic NBA experience on our platform.“

The collaboration with NBA is one more step in NorthStar Gaming’s expansion of its odds offerings through partnerships with major sports leagues.
