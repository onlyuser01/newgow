The partnership extension involves the capturing and distribution of official League data as well as protecting the LNB from integrity threats such as match-fixing

The data, technology and broadcast partner that helps connect sports, betting and media, Genius Sports, announced a new long-term extension of its exclusive data partnership with Brazil’s National Basketball League (LNB). The recently announced partnership extension comes ahead of the new basketball season set to launch on October 21, 2023.

The collaboration sees Genius Sports, which is currently the partner of more than 200 basketball federations and leagues across the globe, deliver FIBA LiveStats across all LNB competitions. As a result, the company will be capturing and distributing data such as play-by-play statistics that cater to different sports-focused platforms. As an integral part of all LNB live broadcasts, Genius will also distribute this data to sports betting operators across the globe, powering their live betting offerings.

Another important side of the extended partnership involves safeguarding the LNB from integrity threats and betting-related corruption. Genius will continue to monitor LNB’s competitions and flag match-fixing or other integrity threats. Back in 2016, the company agreed to provide its educational services and 24/7 bet monitoring system for LNB. The complex monitoring system analyses betting odds and market movements, highlighting suspicious betting patterns and activities.

“As one of the largest basketball leagues in Latin America, we’re delighted to extend our partnership with LNB.“ Guilherme Buso, senior account executive at Genius Sports

Guilherme Buso, Genius Sports’ senior account executive, acknowledged that the company is excited to extend its collaboration with LNB, one of the largest basketball leagues for the Latin American region. He spoke about Genius’ innovative technology that will continue to safeguard LNB competitions and tackle integrity threats. Finally, Buso said that the collaboration will help the distribution of official data that powers the global betting and sports vertical.

“This key strategic business partnership between Genius Sports and the National Basketball League helps us achieve a range of commercial goals.“ Rodrigo Franco Montoro, president of the National Basketball League

Rodrigo Franco Montoro, LNB’s president, spoke about the long-term partnership extension, explaining that it complements multiple League goals. He added that the LNB and Genius share a DNA for innovation, explaining that the collaboration will help expand the reach of live game data. Finally, Montoro praised Genius’ capabilities to distribute information and data securely.
