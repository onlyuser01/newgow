As CCO, Elfersy is expected to leverage his experience and provide invaluable support for the company's commercial and sales teams. At the same time, he is expected to help expand the potential for the growth of GiG's technology. In turn, this will deliver exciting new opportunities which GiG can capture and enter new emerging regulated markets.
