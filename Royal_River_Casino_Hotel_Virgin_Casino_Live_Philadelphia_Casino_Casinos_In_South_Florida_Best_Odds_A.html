The company confirmed the addition of speakers from VIXIO Regulatory Intelligence and Eilers & Krejcik that will participate in its event scheduled to start early in June

The popular entertainment and sports betting company that is globally recognized, Kambi, announced the addition of experts as speakers for its Festival of Sportsbook this June. The company has announced that experts from VIXIO Regulatory Intelligence, the leading provider of intelligence and solutions for the payment and gambling sector and Eilers & Krejcik Gaming, the famous research and consulting firm, will be a part of Kambi’s lign-up of specialists participating in its event. Kambi’s Festival of Sportsbook is an event scheduled to start on June 6, 2023, and go through June 9, 2023.

VIXIO Regulatory Intelligence’s global managing editor, Joe Ewens, as well as the company’s director of data & insights, Daniel Stone, will participate in a panel planned for June 6, 2023. The duo will lead a session that is labeled “Market outlook: The global sports betting opportunity.” They will contribute by analyzing strategic markets and at the same time dive into opportunities for global long-term growth within the sector.

On the other hand, Alun Bowden, Eilers & Krejcik Gaming’s industry commentator and SVP strategic insights, will participate in a discussion planned for June 8, 2023. He will discuss what are some of the unique features within the betting market identified based on consumer behavior. Additionally, Bowden will speak about the users’ demand for sports betting innovations.

The Company Is Delighted to See More Experts Join as Speakers

Gerard Starkey, Kambi’s senior vice president of marketing and communications, explained that the addition of the new professionals from globally recognized firms will significantly boost the line-up of experts for the company’s Festival of Sportsbook planned for June. He added: “Both companies are well respected across the sector for their level of in-depth coverage and industry reports, and we are thrilled to bring their knowledge and expertise to the Festival.”

“This year’s Festival of Sportsbook is shaping up to be better than ever, particularly with the addition of VIXIO Regulatory Intelligence and Eilers & Krejcik Gaming.“ Gerard Starkey, senior vice president of marketing and communications at Kambi

James Kilsby, VIXIO Regulatory Intelligence’s chief analyst, added that the regulatory changes in the last five years have helped transform the global sports betting market. Finally, he noted that the company is delighted to see its experts join Kambi for the event and discuss how the changes within the market can unlock new growth opportunities.
