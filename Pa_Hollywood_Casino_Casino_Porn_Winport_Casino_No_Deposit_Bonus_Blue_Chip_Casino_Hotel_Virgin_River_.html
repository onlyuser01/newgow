The new partnership expands further Yggdrasil's presence across Europe, allowing its games to reach new audiences in Bulgaria

The leading provider of games for the online gambling industry, Yggdrasil, expanded its European footprint via a new partnership. The company confirmed Wednesday it joined forces with Inbet, a leading local operator within the regulated gambling market in Bulgaria. Under the terms of the new deal, customers of the operator will be able to access popular and fan-favorite games from Yggdrasil.

Per the new deal, the leading iGaming content provider agreed to supply Inbet with games like MexoMax, Golden Fish Tank 2, Vikings go Berzerk Reloaded, as well as Raptor DoubleMax. Additionally, Yggdrasil confirmed that a second batch of fan-favorite games, featuring exciting Game Engagement Mechanics (GEM) will be launched for Inbet soon. Such titles include Valley of the Gods, Christmas Plaza DoubleMax, as well as Neon Villians DoubleMax.

Besides in-house games, the leading iGaming publisher confirmed that the deal encompasses titles from its YG Masters portfolio. Ultimately, all those thrilling, engaging and quality games are expected to significantly strengthen the offering of the local operator.

At the same time, the partnership further expands Yggdrasil’s footprint within regulated gambling markets across the Old Continent. Details regarding the recent collaboration with Inbet in Bulgaria come on the heels of the launch of Enchanted Waters, a new adventure-themed slot game from Yggdrasil, offering an RTP of 96%, as well as exciting and rewarding features.
