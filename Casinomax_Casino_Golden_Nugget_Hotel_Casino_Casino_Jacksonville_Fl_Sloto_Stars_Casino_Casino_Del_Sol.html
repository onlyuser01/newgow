The Slot Offers Engaging and Rewarding Features

To secure different payouts, players need to land different combinations. If a player lands a Fisherman symbol at the fifth reel, they can collect a prize equal to the value of the fish symbols seen.

Additionally, to increase the chance of hitting a bonus and Fisherman symbols, players can also use the Golden Bet feature and Buy Bonus feature that allows them to tap into Free Spins. Still, the popular Buy Bonus feature may be restricted in certain jurisdictions, explained the game developer.

Landing three or more Bonus symbols within the standard game mode activates Free Spins mode for the players. The player is then granted at least eight free spins and every time they hit a Fisherman, a multiplier is increased while an extra spin is also granted.

Last but not least, the DoubleMax mechanic can also bring lucrative wins for Nice Catch DoubleMax players. To trigger this, the players need to land fish symbols in line from left to right. This can increase a multiplier from the top of the reel from x2 to a staggering x256, granting the players a chance for a big win.
