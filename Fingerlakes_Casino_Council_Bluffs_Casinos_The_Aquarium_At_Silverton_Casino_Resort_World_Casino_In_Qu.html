Stakelogic's live dealer online content casino provider, Stakelogic Live, announced a major content distribution deal with Oryx Gaming. The new collaboration was announced on Friday and marks a strategic move for Stakelogic Live as the developer continues to grow and expand its global presence.

The terms of the new collaboration will see the developer's premium live casino content launch to operators powered by Oryx Gaming. The deal includes titles such as the highly popular roulette, auto-roulette, blackjack, baccarat and game shows, as well as auto-roulette classic launch to operators around the world via Oryx Gaming's distribution platform dubbed Oryx Hub.

In a press release dated April 22, 2022, Stakelogic's CEO, Stephan van den Oetelaar, revealed that the company's portfolio of live casino games has already gained popularity. However, he predicted that the new deal with Oryx Gaming will further propel the company forward.

"Oryx powers more than 400 online gambling brands across key global markets which is what makes this such a transformational deal for Stakelogic Live," explained Stakelogic's CEO. In conclusion, Oetelaar revealed that Stakelogic offers some of the best games in the online live casino market and it is now time to "showcase them to more operators than ever before."

Currently, Stakelogic Live already has a portfolio of more than seven live casino games that proved to be highly popular. The developer's games are broadcasted from state-of-the-art studios located in the Netherlands and Malta.

Each of the company's games offers an engaging, seamless and authentic live casino game experience. Player engagement is further boosted thanks to Stakelogic Live's team of highly-trained dealers, hosts and croupiers.

Oryx Gaming's head of aggregation, Ivica Jovanovski, shared his excitement about the recent deal. He revealed that as a platform provider, Oryx has seen an increase in the popularity of the live casino games segment. This is only one of the reasons why the company decided to add Stakelogic's suite live games.

"Stakelogic Live has taken the market by storm in a very short space of time with its premium live dealer games, so adding them to our portfolio was an absolute must," added Jovanovski. In conclusion, he outlined that this is a great deal for Oryx, its partners and their players.

Oryx Gaming is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the global gaming technology and content group Bragg Gaming Group. Earlier this month, Oryx announced another groundbreaking deal with the online gambling operator Betclic. The terms of the collaboration launched Oryx's leading portfolio of slots for customers in Portugal.

Before that, in March, Oryx signed a partnership with the largest operators in the Bahamas, Island Luck. The deal saw Island Luck customers gain access to Oryx's game content.
