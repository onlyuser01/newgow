The lading game distributor, QTech Games, announced Wednesday it hired the services of Martin Trang. By joining the company, Trang was appointed as its new chief marketing officer (CMO).

Industry Expert Martin Trang Joins QTech Games

The announcement comes amid QTech Games’ ongoing global expansion. Ultimately, having Trang as CMO, the company seeks to boost its expansion further and capture new market opportunities. Overall, Trang brings more than two decades of experience in the sales and marketing sectors within the iGaming vertical. This will help QTech games diversify its business and at the same time deliver strong results when it comes to marketing strategy, a channel that is increasingly important within the growing gaming industry.

Commenting on the topic, Trang said that it is exciting to join QTech Games for this full-time position. He explained that the company stands out with unique games that feature flexible technology. What’s more, Trang added that QTech Games has a growing presence in important markets beyond Asia. Combining those two advantages, according to the new CMO, creates unique opportunities for the company.

“I’m delighted to be joining such an established and progressive company full-time. Implementing the correct strategy for its “two keystones” model has been pivotal to QTech Games’ sustained success.“ Martin Trang, chief marketing officer at QTech Games

Trang added that QTech Games isn’t only an Asian-facing aggregator but also a “true international distributor of digital entertainment that’s continuing to attract clients from all over the world.” Finally, he predicted further growth for the company and capitalization on new markets.
