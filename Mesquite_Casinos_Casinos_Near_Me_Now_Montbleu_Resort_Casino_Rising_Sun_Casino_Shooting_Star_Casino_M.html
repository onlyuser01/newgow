With the new appointment, EveryMatrix strengthens its senior-level team by bringing in a gaming executive with decades of experience

The provider of software and solutions for the online gambling industry, EveryMatrix, announced a senior-level appointment Tuesday. The company unveiled it strengthened its leadership team by hiring the experienced gaming executive with extensive knowledge, Gonzalo De Osma Bucero. Joining EveryMatrix, he was appointed to the role of chief financial officer (CFO).

In his new role, Bucero will oversee strategic financial and legal processes related to EveryMatrix’s performance within different operating segments. He will also collaborate with the company’s compliance, financial and legal teams.

As CFO of EveryMatrix, Bucero will spend time in Malta where the company’s compliance and legal teams are based. Additionally, he will also visit EveryMatrix’s headquarters and collaborate with its finance team in Bucharest, Romania.

“I’m thrilled to join Ebbe, the management team and the business at such an exciting time for EveryMatrix.“ Gonzalo De Osma Bucero, CFO at EveryMatrix

Bucero shared his excitement saying that he is delighted to join EveryMatrix’s team of experts and its CEO, Ebbe Groes. He acknowledged that his appointment comes during an exciting moment for the company. The new CFO said that the company’s CEO helped him make the decision easy by revealing an ambitious growth plan. Finally, Bucero said: “I’m looking forward to getting to know the team and every element of this great business so we can achieve the ambitious but achievable goals we have set ourselves.”

Bucero Is the Perfect Fit for the Role

Before joining EveryMatrix, Bucero was a part of Europe’s largest and most successful publicly listed online and land-based operator, Codere. He was a part of the company for 16 years and during that time, played a key role in its growth. While with Codere, Bucero worked in Malta, Mexico, as well as Spain, overseeing large business and financial projects.

As a part of Codere, Bucero played a pivotal role in managing the company’s large financial team in Mexico that is in charge of 90 land-based casinos, as well as the largest expo center in the LatAm region and a horse racetrack. Under his guidance, Codere has seen significant growth, reaffirming his expertise and dedication.
