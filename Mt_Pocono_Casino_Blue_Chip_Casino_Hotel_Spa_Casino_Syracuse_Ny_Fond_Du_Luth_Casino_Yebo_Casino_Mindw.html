Mindway AI, an artificial-intelligence-powered solution that protects bettors and helps them to prevent addiction, has reinforced its board of directors with a new addition. The one joining the company is Willem Van Oort, a decorated gambling industry veteran.

Van Oort Brings Years of Experience to the Company

Van Oort has years of experience in gambling both as a consultant and a journalist. He has written a plethora of articles for the gambling news outlet Gambling Insider and is working as an independent iGaming consultant. Van Oort has founded the business-to-business communities Gaming in Germany, Gaming in Holland and Gaming in Spain.

Van Oort is, furthermore, an active member of the IMGL, the IAGA and Jdigital. His profound experience in the various aspects of the industry will be of great value to Mindway AI and its mission of promoting responsible gambling.

As a part of Mindway’s board of directors, Van Oort will join an experienced team of professionals, including Kim Mouridsen, the founder of Mindway AI, Flemming Pedersen the chief financial officer of Better Collective and Birgitte Sand, the chief executive of Birgitte Sand and Associate and an ex-director general of the Danish Gambling Authority.

Van Oort is delighted to offer his knowledge to Mindway AI. “I’m excited to become a member of the Board of Directors and help guide Mindway AI’s commercial development as it stands before an expansion into new markets.” Mindway AI member of the board Willem Van Oort
