N1 Partners has become the first company to launch SOFTSWISS’s newest iGaming solution, the SOFTSWISS Jackpot Aggregator. The product allows the affiliate group to build up a range of custom-defined jackpot campaigns for its customers.

The Jackpot Aggregator was introduced to the public late last week. The software is designed specifically for the needs of casino operators, gaming providers, or aggregators who seek to generate an unlimited number of jackpots simultaneously. The solution is part of the so-called JaaS – Jackpot as a Service – software solutions. It can scale and increase the efficiency of an iGaming business by outsourcing a variety of different jackpots.

What is JaaS or ‘Jackpot as a Service’?

The new product by SOFTSWISS allows its user to generate a variety of jackpot campaigns. By using just a few settings, it is possible to create simple or complex jackpots with various factors in the parameters list.

It arrives with pre-build settings and templates, consolidates players within teams, and offers complete transparency with data and statistics. According to SOFTSWISS, JaaS solutions will allow gaming operators to unload some of their operations and optimize their operations while keeping the players engaged. For example, the solution allows players to participate in multiple campaigns, thus increasing their chances of winning.
