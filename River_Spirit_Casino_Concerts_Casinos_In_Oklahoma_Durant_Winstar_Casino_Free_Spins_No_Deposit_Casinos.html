The storyline that players will have the pleasure to uncover as the game evolves follows the classic tale of the pot filled with golden coins at the end of a rainbow. According to popular belief and lots of Irish folklore tales, after each rain, there is a mysterious rainbow popping in the sky. Fortunate rainbow lovers who manage to find the end of the rainbow have a good chance to come across a mouth-watering pot of gold belonging to a rich leprechaun. Of course, not everyone is lucky enough to find the end of the rainbow or the pot that is closely protected and well hidden by the leprechauns themselves.

As expected, the slot features simple and engaging gameplay with colorful graphic elements on the 3 x 3 grid with five lines. The background is represented by a nicely-designed green forest backdrop with a gorgeous rainbow seen across it and what appears to be magical golden fairy dust and falling leaves from the trees.

The background is supported by a handful of representative symbols part of the Irish folklore mixed with the leprechaun theme, with leprechauns wearing their iconic green hats, four-leaf clovers, horseshoes, green leprechaun shoes, pots filled with gold coins, and rainbow symbols shown all across the grid.

Leprechaun Frenzy: Game Symbols and Payouts

To win, players must match three identical symbols anywhere on the grid and look at the paytable to find out the win they are eligible for. For example, matching three pots of gold rewards players with 100x the stake. Coming across three lucky horseshoes symbols triggers a 50x payout, while three green Leprechaun hats filled with coins bring players a payout of 15x.

Three four-leaf clover symbols reward players with a 5x payout while three green leprechaun shoes get a 3x payout. Finally, coming across three rainbow symbols get players the smallest 1x payout. On the opposite end, the highest-paying symbol in the game is represented by the Leprechaun. Players who match a minimum of three such symbols are rewarded with a 500x payout. Players who manage to hit multiple three-of-a-kind symbols will have their winnings added together.

A maximum number of three identical symbols can be seen showing up on the card simultaneously. Players can also win on the same card with groups of one, two, or three different symbols. Winnings for groups of three symbols are added together, resulting in the total win. The winnings are determined by the value of the multiplier x the amount of the total bet. The game has a 95% expected payback in the long term.

Leprechaun Frenzy Gameplay

The game is played on a nine-panel scratch card. Players need to start by selecting the amount they would like to bet by clicking the bet coin value. When the card is fully loaded, players can either scratch or click the relevant scratch surface and proceed to scratch off the top of the card to discover their prizes. Winnings get automatically added to players’ account balances. To check the total wins, players can inspect the lower part of the screen at the end of each new game.

Players eager to jump straight into the action can buy the Leprechaun Mega Feature using the available Buy Feature with prices starting at 2x and going all the way up to 82x the stake.
