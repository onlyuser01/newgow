Fantasy Sports Interactive (FSI) has expanded its geographical reach through a fresh partnership with Fantazzie Gaming Technology covering the Turkish market. As a result, the company has launched a new fantasy sports product that will be available to audiences in the country.

New Fantasy Soccer Experience Comes to Turkey

Players will be happy to discover a dedicated fantasy sports platform that will feature in-app purchases and free-to-play games that are geared toward sports fans and designed to offer an interactive and immersive gaming experience across the board. Commenting on this launch, FSI CEO Dennis Tsalikis welcomed the launch of the platform and shared his excitement to be collaborating with Fantazzie:

We are thrilled to bring Fantazzie’s platform and vision to life, and we are proud to provide this engaging fantasy solution to the passionate football fans of Turkey. FSI CEO Dennis Tsalikis

The announcement follows more than a month after the initial launch of the app, which became available to Turkish sports fans back in August, but has recently been pushed by FSI through its marketing channels.

The app focuses on the Turkish Super Lig’s latest season, the country’s topflight soccer league. To realize the full potential of the Fantasy Super Lig Contests, though, FSI, is collaborating with Fantazzie Sports which has a dedicated marketplace that allows for the purchase of the “FanGold” currency.

This mock currency is then used in-game to boost various entries, teams, or players. The platform is not seemingly stopping at just the Super Lig though as it will also feature something for the FIFA World Cup, but only in November. Players will get a chance to pick top players from all 32 participating teams and follow their stats, which will reflect on how well they perform as well.

Tsalikis thanked Fantazzie once again and said that working with the team has been a very constructive and meaningful experience that is likely to yield strong results in the country.
