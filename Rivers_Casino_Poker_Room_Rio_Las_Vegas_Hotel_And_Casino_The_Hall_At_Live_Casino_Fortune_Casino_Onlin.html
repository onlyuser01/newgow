The deal will see popular games from the iGaming developer expand the offering of Salsa Gator, Salsa's aggregator platform

The leading business-to-business (B2B) developer of customized solutions catering to the iGaming sector in Europe, as well as Latin America, Salsa Technology, announced it forged a new partnership with the Malta-based provider of mobile games, Darwin Gaming. Under the terms of the new agreement, announced Monday, Darwin Gaming agreed to deliver top-performing games, powering Salsa Technology’s aggregator platform, Salsa Gator.

Thanks to the integration, more players are going to be able to reach popular games from Darwin Gaming. The collaboration will also help strengthen Salsa’s aggregator platform, complementing its growth and position within the competitive online gambling sector. Under the terms of the new agreement, the company’s partners will have the option to engage with new games provided by Darwin Gaming.

Such games will include popular instant win titles like Luva PowerBasket, as well as Luva Melhor do Mundo. Salsa’s B2B customers will also be able to deliver thrilling crash games like Crash Evolution and Luva SuperGoal, among others.

The new content agreement includes a portfolio from Darwin’s bingo vertical, with popular games like Judgement of Anubis Bingo and Tiger Bingo. Per the new content deal, Salsa’s Salsa Gator platform will also see the addition of multiple games that are inspired by the influencer, Luva de Pedreiro, and other titles that bring unique and exciting experiences.

The latest collaboration with Darwin Gaming comes on the heels of another important content deal announced by Salsa. Earlier this month, the company signed a new strategic deal with the Brazilian sports betting platform provider, RSA. The terms of that content agreement saw RSA’s clients gain access to Salsa’s aggregator platform, Salsa Gator.

The Two Companies Are Excited to Join Forces

Rui Pena, Darwin Gaming’s COO, explained that the company is delighted to join forces with Salsa Technology, one of the leading iGaming companies in Latin America. “Salsa’s strength is a perfect match for the energy and creativity of our games,” he added. Finally, Pena pointed out that Darwin will be thrilled to expand the reach of its products and deliver thrilling experiences to new customers.

“We’re especially eager to showcase our crash games and video bingos, where the pulse of Latin passion meets gaming excellence.“ Rui Pena, COO of Darwin Gaming

Eliane Nunes, Salsa’s head of marketing and sales, was similarly excited about the content agreement signed with Darwin Gaming. She highlighted that the strategic deal will help strengthen the offering of Salsa Gator. Finally, Nunes predicted that the games provided by the leading iGaming developer will quickly capture the attention of new customers, considering their strong presence and success in the market in Brazil.
