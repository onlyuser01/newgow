TonyBet, prominent sports and casino operator, has been cleared to launch in the Netherlands earlier this month. The company has received approval from the Dutch Gaming Authority but it will not launch immediately, as the company’s website, tonybet.nl, is expected to go live next year, the company confirmed in their latest update.
