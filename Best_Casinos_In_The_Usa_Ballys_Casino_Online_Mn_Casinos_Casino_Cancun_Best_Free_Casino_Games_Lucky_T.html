The newly-minted Lotto millionaire in New Zealand is yet to claim the winnings following the Super Saturday Lotto Powerball draw which saw a winning ticket take home NZ$24 million ($14.88 million).

Mouth-Watering Win

Made up of NZ$1 million ($620,000) from Lotto First Division and NZ$23 million ($14.26 million) from Powerball, the mouth-watering winning is about to mint the 17th multi-millionaire in 2022 alone with one of the largest winnings to date.

The mystery winner bought the winning Lotto ticket at Bell Block Supermarket in New Plymouth and the manager at the supermarket is eagerly anticipating the meeting with him personally, doubting whether he sold the winning ticket.

“It is four of us working in the store and usually I am on the shift when most tickets are sold,” Bell Block Supermarket manager Jassi Singh said, feeling that it may have been his hand that handed the Lotto ticket to the lucky winner.

For Singh, there is a personal aspect as one of the grocery store’s regular female customers promised to take him on a special trip to Dubai, flying first class, dining at a Michelin restaurant and staying at one of Dubai’s top hotels.
