The 6×4 Reel Slot Brings Scatters, HyperBet

In the iGaming developer’s latest release, Wilds From The Crypt, wild symbols can substitute all base game symbols on the reels. Additionally, the high volatility slot game offers a Lucky Loops feature that grants players a chance to summon a special Purple Ghost Wild on every 10th spin via the collection of potions. “Split into three sections, every third potion grants a wild reel, adding it to the game board and resetting after the tenth spin,” explains Kalamba Games.
