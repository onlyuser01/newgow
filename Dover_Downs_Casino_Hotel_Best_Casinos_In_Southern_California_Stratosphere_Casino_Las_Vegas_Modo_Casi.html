Gameplay

5 Lions is a five reel, three row slot.

This game uses the Any Way Wins system. This means that wins are awarded for matching symbols in adjacent reels from left to right.

During the base game, players have 243 ways to win, but this increases to 576 during the Free Spin Feature (see below for details).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. It only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4.

A Multiplier is chosen at random each spin. All winning combinations containing one Wild or more are multiplied by the random chosen Multiplier at the end of the spin.

Players may receive Multipliers between x1 and x40.

The Scatter symbol may appear on any reel.

Players can trigger the Free Spins Feature by landing 3, 4, or 5 Scatter symbols on adjacent reels. The player may receive the following:

3 Scatter symbols: x2 total bet

4 Scatter symbols: x5 total bet

5 Scatter symbols: x20 total bet

The Free Spins Feature also changes the shape of the reels, adding three additional symbols to the top of them. This increases the player's number of ways to win.

Plenty of choices for the free spins bonus game

When the Free Spin Feature begins, players choose a range of multipliers as part of the Free Spins Option. For every spin, a random multiplier will be selected from that range.

All winning combinations containing one or more Wild symbols are multiplied by the randomly selected multiplier value. This occurs after each free spin.
