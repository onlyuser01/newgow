Jackpot: Horseshoe Casino Baltimore Player Hits More Than $1M After Royal Flush

Posted on: September 12, 2023, 02:15h.

Last updated on: September 12, 2023, 07:30h.

A mystery visitor won over $1M while playing a card game on Monday at Horseshoe Casino Baltimore. The exact payout was $1,017,041.33.

Horseshoe Casino Baltimore, pictured above. A player won more than $1M in a recent payout. (Image: TripAdvisor)

The winning hand was royal flush in spades. It was hit on a progressive jackpot. Caesars Entertainment runs the gaming property.

An unnamed player won over $375K while playing Let It Ride on Friday at the Flamingo Las Vegas Hotel & Casino. The exact mega progressive jackpot was $375,087, according to Caesars Entertainment, the casino’s parent company.

Over last weekend at another Caesars Entertainment property, Caesars Palace Las Vegas, three unnamed visitors won jackpots totaling $350K.

Two winners hit the jackpots on Friday morning. One won $125K while on a slot machine. The second won $100K while on a video poker machine. A third player won $125K while playing on a slot machine the following day.

Sam’s Town Jackpot

Last week at Sam’s Town Hotel & Gambling Hall, located by Las Vegas’s Boulder Highway, a visitor named “Natasha” collected a jackpot payout of $32,680.25, according to a September 8 Sam’s Town social media post.

It was on a Blazing 7’s slot machine.

Oregon Player Wins $161K

An unidentified guest collected a $161K jackpot on a slot machine over Labor Day weekend at Oregon’s Indian Head Casino.

The exact payout at the Warm Springs tribal gaming property was $161,413. The guest resides in nearby Madras, Ore.

It was on a Dollar Storm slot machine. The machine is made by Aristocrat Gaming.

We are overjoyed to celebrate the success of our guest and their incredible win over the Labor Day Weekend,” Shawn McDaniel, general manager at Indian Head Casino, said in a statement.

Warm Springs Casino Enterprises owns and operates Indian Head Casino. The gaming enterprise is run by the Confederated Tribes of Warm Springs Reservation of Oregon. The gaming property is about 105 miles southeast of Portland, Oregon.

$264K Paid Out at Red Rock Casino

An unidentified visitor at Las Vegas’s Red Rock Casino Resort and Spa won more than $264K last week.

The Red Rock patron won a seven-card straight flush in hearts. The exact payout was $264,269, according to a September 6 tweet from Station Casinos.

The out-of-town guest was playing a Pai Gow game. It was a progressive jackpot. Following the win, the progressive jackpot was reset to $200,021.
