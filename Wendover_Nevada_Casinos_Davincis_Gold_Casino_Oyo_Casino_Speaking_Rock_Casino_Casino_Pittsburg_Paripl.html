The leading iGaming industry provider and NeoGames subsidiary, Pariplay, strengthened its senior-level team by announcing a new strategic appointment. Moreover, by hiring an industry expert such as Roman, Pariplay plans to keep the positive momentum and continue growing within strategic markets such as North America and Europe. Strengthening its position in the aforementioned markets comes after the company signed multiple partnerships with leading operators.

Roman shared her excitement in a statement released on June 26, 2023. "I've been watching Pariplay go from success to success over time, so to join the team and help them grow even bigger is an exciting opportunity," she said. Pariplay's new Head of Account Management added: "I'm looking forward to strengthening relationships with existing clients, fostering relationships with new ones, and helping to grow Pariplay even further."

Still, her professional background includes more than a decade of experience within the gambling industry together with account management.
