Gameplay

Sizzling Spins is a five-reel, three-row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing matching symbols in adjacent reels, starting from the leftmost reel.

During the base game, there are 243 ways to win, but during the Free Spins feature, this increases to 1024 ways (see further details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

Wilds are hidden within Foil Blocker symbols but once revealed, they substitute for every symbol except the Scatter symbol.

During every spin, one of the reels is assigned to be the Hot Reel. Whenever Foil Blockers land on the Hot Reel, they open to reveal Wilds. The Wilds in turn trigger a respin and if a Wild appears after a fourth respin, the player’s winnings are doubled.

Unlocking the Foil Blocker symbol triggers the Respin feature

If players land 3 Scatter symbols, the Free Spins feature begins. The Free Spins feature is played on an expanded set of reels.

During the Free Spins feature, players can collect Scatter symbols to increase a win multiplier up to 5x.

Respins function the same way during the Free Spins feature as they do during the base game.
