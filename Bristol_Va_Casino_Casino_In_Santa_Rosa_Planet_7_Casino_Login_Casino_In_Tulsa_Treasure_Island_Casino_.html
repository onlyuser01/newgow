888 and Nuvei already boast existing agreements in Europe and Ontario

Canadian financial technology specialist Nuvei unveiled a new partnership agreement with 888, a global gambling juggernaut. As per the deal, the former company will enhance the operator’s payment capabilities, helping it expand its presence in the United States.

According to the announcement, 888 will leverage Nuvei’s superior tech platform, acquiring reach, instant bank-to-bank payments and profound knowledge of iGaming to bolster its business while preventing risk.

Nuvei’s Instant Bank Transfer is the first payment method for instant account-to-account deposits and payouts in the US. This means that 888’s American customers will now be able to directly move funds between their 888 account and their bank card.

This is not the first time 888 and Nuvei have worked together. The fintech specialist has been powering 888’s European business with solutions ever since 2017. In addition, the tech company helped 888 make a foray into the Canadian province of Ontario, which legalized iGaming last year.

The new agreement with 888 cements Nuvei as a leading fintech provider to the iGaming industry. The company is well-known for its solutions that help operators maximize revenues while providing players with intuitive payment options.

Earlier this year, the provider also penned a partnership arrangement with Resorts Digital Gaming.

Optimizing card payment acceptance is a key component of the cashier experience, and Nuvei has decades of experience supporting operators maximize their revenue growth. Philip Fayer, chair & CEO, Nuvei
