Wild Stacks, Free Spins, and Bonus Buy Features

Once the avalanche of chain wins is triggered, Wild Stacks can drop during each new spin. The Wild Stacks vary from three to six symbols high and they are a constant presence on the grid or right above.

During cascade wins, the Wild Stack symbols are seen dropping off the reels and revealing higher multipliers as the stack is seen getting lengthier.

Players who are lucky enough to come across the very rare glowing mushroom symbols are instantly carried to the Free Spins round that awards at least seven free spins. To enter the round, players need to land a minimum of three Free Spins symbols.

While the Free Spins mode is engaged, players enjoy additional opportunities to retrigger massive Stacks of up to 10 symbols high that will also attract corresponding multipliers for a bigger win potential.
