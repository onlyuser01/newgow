Hat Trick Hero review

Theme and Storyline

This slot is themed around an epic football match.

Players will find themselves right on the bright green football pitch, facing a reel of footballs, trophies and whistles. Under the bright lights of the stadium, they’ll face down the goalie to see if they can score big.

Football fans will particularly feel the thrill of the game playing this slot. However, even non-fans will enjoy the invigorating cheers and star-studded atmosphere.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack is a dance-worthy mix with all energy players will need to get into the game. Wins are celebrated with all the flash and cheers one might expect from a game-winning goal.

The stadium setting adds a breathless tension as players stand before the expectant crowd, the iconic football in their sights. Realistic animation of the stadium lights makes the reels feel like the center of attention, amping up the excitement further.

The reels themselves are filled with colourful letters, gleaming gold trophies, jerseys, and of course, footballs. The semi-transparent animation of the reels means that the stadium in the background is still visible. This helps keep up the festive atmosphere of the crowd watching the game.

Overall, the music and graphics of this slot work together to create a vibe that’s pulsing with excitement.

Gameplay

Hat Trick Hero is a five reel, three row slot with 25 win lines.

The gameplay is fast and attention-grabbing, ensuring players stay engaged.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild Power Shot symbol substitutes for all symbols.

If players land 3 or more Power Shot symbols, the Hat Trick Hero Event is triggered. This feature works like a Free Spins feature in many ways.

It begins with a free respin and all Power Shot symbols move one position to the left, triggering an additional respin.

Bet Sizes, RTP and Variance

The return to player of this game is 96.23%, just above our yardstick for average of roughly 96%.

The range of bets on the site we tested ran from a minimum bet per spin of $/£/€0.02 up to a maximum of $/£/€10.00 per spin.

The volatility of this game is high, which makes it a good match for our preferred slot machine strategies. Our slot strategies target the highest possible volatility.

Conclusion

We recommend this high RTP, high volatility football-themed slot.

The attractive volatility makes this game a great choice for strategy- conscious players, while the respectable RTP adds extra appeal.

The creative use of the sports theme is the unique aspect of this slot, making it an ideal fit for players looking for the excitement of heading out to the stadium. Even non-football fans will find the energy of the crowd contagious.
