Content aggregation specialist and developer of original games, REEVO, has expanded its presence in the European region with another tier-one operator partnership

The company will now work with interwetten on providing the fleet of REEVO’s slot games to local audiences. The games featured in the deal include but aren’t limited to Reel Fortune and Diamond Luck, Crazy Crocs and Wolf Wild, along with more games that are still in production, such as Mayan Magic Gold and Royal Bets.

Interwetten Gets Access to REEVO’s Library of Fantastic Titles

The partnership is designed to significantly boost both companies’ standing in the European region, with interwetten being a household name that has been running various betting and gaming operations since the 1990s. REEVO will help interwetten benefit from the rapid deployment of any forthcoming products thanks to its single API integration that will reap continuous benefits for the operator.

Commenting on this fresh opportunity, REEVO head of sales Petra Maria Poola said that the company was absolutely stoked to be teaming up with interwetten, calling the company a prestigious and well-known brand. “We’re delighted to partner with them and are really looking forward to seeing how their players enjoy our games which are proving instant hits around the world,” Poola said.

Interwetten head of product management CRM and content Mario Bilic was similarly pleased with the development, hailing the addition of REEVO content, and confirming that this new content injection will undoubtedly bring more variety and further opportunity for players.

“I’m sure the response will be very positive with both existing and future releases,” Bilic said. REEVO has been growing its footprint over the past weeks and months at a break-neck speed with the company signing similar partnerships with TonyBet, Hölle Games, Wazdan, and others.
