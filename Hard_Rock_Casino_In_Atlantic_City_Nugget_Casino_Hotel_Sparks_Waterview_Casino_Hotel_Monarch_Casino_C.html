BETER trusts Chuch Robert Robinson to maintain the supplier’s momentum and help it expand in new markets

BETER, the industry’s leading provider of next-gen content and data, has strengthened its top brass team with the addition of the decorated industry veteran Chuck Robinson as chief revenue officer.

In his new position, Robinson will be tasked with driving BETER’s growth strategy and helping the company expand its global position.

The new hire is an appropriate addition to the top leadership team, considering his 15+ years of experience in business development. Over half of that time was spent in iGaming companies, where Robinson built sales teams, helped brands foray into new regions and implemented marketing strategies.

The appointee’s experience includes tenures at companies such as Symphony Solutions and Betsys, among others.

As the chief revenue officer of BETER and a member of the leadership, Robinson will be tasked with leveraging his profound experience to drive the provider’s business development. As mentioned, he will play a major role in the company’s expansion into new markets, while overseeing sales, marketing and account management teams.

In addition, Robinson will be tasked with enhancing client services and improving the overall customer experience.

BETER Welcomed Robinson to the Team

BETER is very optimistic about the new appointment and believes that it will help it continue its growth trajectory. The company hopes to solidify its position as a top provider of gaming tech and betting content.

Gal Ehrlich, chief executive of BETER, welcomed the new hire to the company, saying that the team is excited to have Robinson on board. Ehrlich lauded the appointee’s track record and said that he will play an important role in scaling the company,

With his extensive experience in business development and iGaming, we are confident that he will play a crucial role in expanding our market presence and driving our growth strategy forward. Gal Ehrlich, CEO, BETER

Robinson also released a statement, commenting on his new position. He said that he is very excited to join the company, which he considers to be a very strong brand manned by an amazing team of skilled professionals. The new CRO shared that he is delighted by the opportunity to put his experience to use and accelerate BETER’s growth.

He also teased that his role will see him develop favorable relationships between BETER and other high-profile companies in the iGaming sector.

I firmly believe that our prospective partners will be impressed by the outstanding quality of BETER’s products and the cutting-edge technology leveraged to provide the best betting experience for their customers. Chuck Robinson, CRO, BETER
