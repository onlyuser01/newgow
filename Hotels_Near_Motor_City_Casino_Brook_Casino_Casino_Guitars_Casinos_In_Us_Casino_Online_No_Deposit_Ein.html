Eintracht Frankfurt Have High Ambitious for the 2022-23 Season

Last season, Eintracht Frankfurt finished in the #11 position in the Bundesliga, which is far from impressive. The reason for that, however, is that they were occupied with their Europa League campaign. That’s the competition they won last season, which earned them a spot in the Champions League in the 2022-23 season.

They would love to do well in the continent’s elite competition, but they also want to finish in the top half of the table in the domestic league. In order to boost their chances of achieving those plans, Eintracht Frankfurt made a number of signings over the summer transfer period, most notably capturing the signature of former German international Mario Gotze, a forward famous for scoring the only goal in the 2014 World Cup Final.

Bayern Munich Don’t Care About Lewandowski Anymore

Robert Lewandowski’s contract with Bayern Munich ended this summer, after which he moved to Barcelona. Rather than signing a player similar to the Polish superstar, they brought in Sadio Mane from Liverpool, a player who’s played on the flank throughout his career.

Although he’s not the same type of player as Lewandowski, Mane seems to be doing the job well. In fact, we saw him score a goal in his debut with Bayern Munich. He was one of five different players who scored against RB Leipzig a week ago. The fact that so many players ended up on the scoresheet shows that Bayern Munich’s strength comes from teamwork rather than individual brilliance.
