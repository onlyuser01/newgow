Tiger Temple: Game Symbols and Payouts

The game’s low-paying symbols are the standard card values (10, J, Q, K, A) featuring stylized versions that perfectly match the chosen theme. Players who manage to match a minimum of three of these card symbols are awarded a win. For example, the 10 can trigger up to 10x the bet per line, while the J card brings players up to 12x the line bet.

Matching a minimum of three Qs or Ks brings players up to 14x times the bet per line, while the A card can lead to a payout of 14x the line bet. The dragon symbols are the high-paying symbols, with the green awarding up to 20x the line bet, the blue triggering up to 30x the line bet, the gold paying up to 40x and the purple dragon triggering a payout of up to 50x the line bet.

The scatter symbol is represented by the fortune god. Players only need to see one of these symbols anywhere on the grid to trigger a win, which is a nice change of pace when compared to other slots where a minimum of three scatters are necessary to generate a win.

Tiger Temple Gameplay

The game features the same standard control panel found in all Genesis Gaming slots. The 40 available pay lines are fixed by default, which means that players solely need to focus on betting via the Bet button, with bets starting at 0.01 up to 2 coins per line. Players can adjust the bet per payline while the total wager per each of the spins is determined by multiplying the amount by the 40 fixed pay lines.

To start spinning the reels, players must click or tap the big blue button that will turn into a Stop button at the end of the round. The small key tab with round arrows will trigger Autoplay mode which lets players select their preferred number of spins and run the game with zero hassle. The question mark button introduces players to additional information regarding the paytable, symbols, and overall gameplay and settings. Players can also click the speaker to turn off the music.

Tiger Temple: Re-Spins and Fortune God Free Spin Feature

The Wild symbol is represented by the golden tiger symbol. Matching a minimum of two of these symbols brings players an additional win of up to 400x the initial line bet. Wilds can be stacked on the reel, in which case they will also trigger a golden tiger re-spin. When this occurs, all wilds will remain locked in their positions while also bringing players three free spins.

Players who manage to land two stacks of wilds will initiate the tiger re-spin. During the re-spin, the wild symbols will be locked again and trigger two re-spins. The feature comes with a 2x multiplier that will help build juicer payouts.

The scatter symbol is represented by a golden man statue that has the word Bonus written on it. Landing a minimum of three of these symbols across the reels will initiate the free spins feature which will bring players 10 free spins. During this feature, players can re-trigger an additional 10 spins when three scatters are seen landing.
