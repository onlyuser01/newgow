AffPapa has announced a brand-new partnership with Hipther Agency which will see the companies continue to work on expanding their clout and operational prowess in the iGaming, sports betting, and affiliate industries. The tie-up is an important step forward for both companies as they seek to pave new ways to strengthen the gaming industry.

The partnership will focus on connecting readers with relevant industry events and conferences hosted by Hipther. The Agency is already planning to host several high-profile industry get-togethers, including the Mare Balticum Gaming & Tech Summit and the Prague Gaming & Tech Summit.

Hipther boasts 17 unique news channels that cover global events from a number of industries, including anything iGaming related. Some of those include Gaming Americas, Europe Gaming, WireUp Zone, PICANTE, and others.

Commenting on this fresh opportunity, Hipther Agency Co-Founder and Head of Business Zoltan Tundik welcomed the chance to be teaming up with AffPapa as part of the company’s ever-growing expansion:

Their team has put in a lot of work and effort into what makes today a great and unique platform that connects affiliates and operators.

AffPapa CCO Yeva Avagyan was similarly thrilled to welcome this new partnership and appreciate its significance for both participants. Avagyan shared her enthusiasm to officially reveal this fresh collaboration that is in line with what AffPapa readers have come to expect from the company. This way, Avagyan said, AffPapa readers and visitors will also be in the loop about the most current iGaming events.

"I’m excited to see where this collaboration takes us – from the looks of it, it’s certain to be beneficial to both sides," she concluded. To keep track of Hipther Agency’s upcoming events, AffPapa invites you to visit the dedicated iGaming Events page.
