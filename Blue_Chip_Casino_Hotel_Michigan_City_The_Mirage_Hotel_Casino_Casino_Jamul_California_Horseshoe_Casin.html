FUN88, one of the biggest gaming and sports betting operators in Asia, has unveiled a new partnership agreement with the British soccer club Newcastle United FC. The agreement will set the gambling company as the team’s official Asian betting partner.

Newcastle United already enjoys a fruitful partnership with the operator, which became its principal sponsor at the beginning of the 2017/18 season. Since then, FUN88’s logo has appeared on the front of the Magpies’ kits and training gear.

After working together for six seasons, the two parties have decided to deepen their ties with an Asia-oriented deal. According to the announcement, the partnership will leverage FUN88’s popularity in Asia to bolster the club’s commercial growth in the region. To that end, the operator will launch special in-market initiatives, official Newcastle United FC merchandise and VIP experiences for local fans.

The deal will kick off from the 2023/24 season onwards. In a statement, Newcastle United expressed its appreciation for the operator’s continued support:

The club thanks FUN88 for its support over the past six campaigns and looks forward to working together over coming seasons. Newcastle United statement

A day later, Fun88 elected the Newcastle United forward Callum Wilson as its May’s Player of the Month. This followed the striker’s impressive performance during the 2022/23 season, which also saw him secure April’s Player of the Month accolade.
