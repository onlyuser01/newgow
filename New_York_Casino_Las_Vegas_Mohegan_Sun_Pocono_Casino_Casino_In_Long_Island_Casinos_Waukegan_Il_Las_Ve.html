The famous American auto racing and operating company that is globally recognized and has millions of fans around the world, NASCAR, announced a new partnership with the popular American lottery, Powerball. Last week, the duo announced a new partnership that will see the delivery of new customer experiences and exciting moments for Powerball and NASCAR fans.

Under the terms of the new deal, Powerball was named the Official Lottery Game of NASCAR. Moreover, the collaboration designated the lottery as an Official Partner of NASCAR, a statement reveals.

The latest partnership comes on the heels of the start of the 2023 NASCAR regular season. The season launched Sunday, February 19 with the 65th Annual DAYTONA 500, also famous as The Great American Race.

Celebrating the new partnership, NASCAR and Powerball announced the launch of a season-long promotion. Thanks to the new collaboration between the duo, the season-long promotion will run and culminate with the drawing of a winner of a $1 million-dollar prize.

What's known so far is that the draw for the $1 million prize is planned to be broadcasted live from the Phoenix Raceway during the NASCAR Championship Weekend set for November 3 through November 5. The duo vowed to share additional details such as the rules of the promotion, ways to enter and more on a later date.

Drew Svitko, the Product Group Chair of Powerball and Executive Director of the Pennsylvania Lottery, shared his excitement saying: "Powerball and NASCAR both have the extraordinary ability to create memorable moments that bring everyone together, to dream and cheer for something big."
