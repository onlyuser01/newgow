Players Can Win Big with Candy Wilds

The game features a variety of symbols representing the titular gnome characters. Players can collect combinations of symbols to earn a variety of prizes in time for the holidays. Engagement is further bolstered by Santa himself who acts as a wild symbol, substituting all other normal symbols on the board.

In addition, players can collect scatter symbols to trigger the free spins mode. Represented by gift packages, the symbols will trigger the bonus round once players collect three or more of them. Three scatters will award 15 free spins, while four or five scatters will provide players with 20 or 25 spins, respectively.

Once triggered, the bonus round will mark certain spots as Candy Wilds. If a gnome character lands within the candy frame, it will automatically become a wild symbol, allowing players to make even more combinations and win big prizes.

If players land on a scatter symbol, it will automatically become a Candy Wild. Players can also re-trigger the feature by collecting more scatter symbols during free spins mode.
