SBC Summit Barcelona set to take place on September 19 – September 21, 2023, at Fira de Barcelona Montjuïc, has revealed more details about one of its most anticipated areas – the Casino & iGaming Zone. The event will bring together some 15,000 delegates, which will be able to exchange industry knowledge and network across a number of similar zones designed to address various aspects of the industry.

The Casino & iGaming Zone in particular will be one of the hottest and most important parts of the experience and will reunite a variety of brands with a strong presence in these particular verticals. Among the brands that will be attending and exhibiting in the zone are such respected companies as Amusnet Interactive, SmartSoftGaming, Wazdan, 3 Oaks Gaming, Spinomenal, Winfinity, SoftGamings, and Evoplay.

SBC Founder & CEO Rasmus Sojmark has welcomed the opportunity to introduce the Casino & iGaming Zone to a broader audience and detail what it means for the event. The zone is just one of seven dedicated stages at the event, which will be focusing on discussing innovation, trends, and changes in the industry. Sojmark said

"Over the years, this zone has garnered significant attention, as delegates have directly experienced how gaming innovations are transforming the player experience and propelling business achievements. We look forward to witnessing the emergence of the next wave of gaming advancements this year and an exhibition floor bustling with a vibrant exchange of knowledge, cutting-edge innovations, and business deals."

This particular zone will also host a number of panels that will focus on better understanding the industry as a whole. One of the panel discussions will be "The Next-Gen Of Casino - Measuring The Influence Of Web 3.0, UX, and Video Games."

There will be a strong presence of established industry specialists, including Hero Gaming CEO Sarah Stellini, Casumo CEO Francesco Postiglione, Spilnu.dk CEO Allan Auning-Hansen, and ProdMark 365 Founder Mark Grech, among others. More worthwhile and noteworthy discussions in this zone will be centered around "Keeping Slots Dynamic – Are Crash Games And Loot Boxes Influencing Slot Innovation?"

The whole zone is created to reunite some of the most prominent industry specialists from the sector and allow them to collaborate on matters that promise to completely transform the iGaming ecosystem.
