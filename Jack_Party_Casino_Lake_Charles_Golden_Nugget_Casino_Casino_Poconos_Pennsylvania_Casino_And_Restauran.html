Supplier of iGaming and betting software NuxGame has signed a new partnership with Platipus Gaming, a dedicated developer of content for the industry. Through this new partnership, NuxGame partners will have access to Platipus’ series of innovative content and titles.

Platipus Gaming Now Part of NuxGame’s Offer

Platipus Gaming has specialized in the development of crypto-friendly games along with standard iGaming propositions. The studio has been around since 2014 and operates out of the United Kingdom. It develops and crafts beautifully-animated and mobile-friendly games on HTMl5.

The company has been able to expand into other parts of the experience as well, including table games, over the last years. As a result, the studio offers more than 80 slot and table games, translated into 18 languages, and works with a number of FIAT and mainstream cryptocurrencies.

Thanks to this new partnership, NuxGame will be able to continue building up a portfolio of content partners that can be implemented immediately with any partner operator. NuxGame has been working hard to ensure that its offer is stronger each month.

The company previously arranged a similar partnership with Ezugi and it joined QTech Games, to give its offer a further boost. NuxGame also has a partnership with Smartico.ai enabling the company to deploy an intuitive CRM automation platform for added efficiency and reliability.

Platipus Confident to Reach New Audiences with NuxGame

Commenting on the latest partnership with Platipus Gaming, NuxGame head of sales Bar Konson added:

We are delighted to be given the opportunity to offer Platipus Gaming’s renowned products to our respected partners and their players. Forming these agreements with other distinguished brands is a core pillar of our growth strategy, and we couldn’t be more excited to see where this collaboration will take us. Platipus Gaming CMO Garanko Vladyslav welcomed the opportunity to be teaming up with NuxGame with a similar tone and argued that the company appreciated the opportunity to have its business exposed and strike home with a broader audience, which should bolster the studio’s profile further.
