The leading supplier of quality data for the growing esports industry, Bayes Esports, strengthened its senior-level team by hiring York Scheunemann as chief operations officer (COO)

The company expanded its C-Level management team on Friday, by bringing in an industry expert with decades of experience.

Bayes Esports Strengthens C-Level Team

In his new position as COO, Scheunemann will play a pivotal role in Bayes Esports’ growth on a global scale within the esports industry. He will be responsible for the company’s communication, marketing and sales teams. Scheunemann will also oversee Bayes Esports’ people and culture efforts and leverage his extensive experience.

Sharing his excitement, Scheunemann acknowledged that the esports industry continues to grow and develop rapidly. At the same time, he said that the ever-changing industry needs a trusted and well-established company such as Bayes Esports. Finally, Scheunemann pointed out that he is delighted to join the team of experts and help the company continue to grow.

“An industry this agile, innovative, and progressive, needs companies like Bayes Esports and I am excited to join the team on its growth and expansion journey.“ York Scheunemann, chief operations officer at Bayes Esports

Scheunemann Brings Decades of Experience

Scheunemann has more than two decades of experience within a variety of digital businesses primarily focused on the sales vertical. He is a proven leader and has held leadership-level positions with giant global companies such as Google. Overall, he was a part of the company for 12 years, but his professional experience includes a vice-president-level position with Appinio as well. Throughout his career, Scheunemann has helped foster talent which helped companies to grow exponentially.

“We are delighted to welcome York onboard. We are all excited to work with York.“ Amir Mirzaee, CCO and managing director at Bayes Esports

Bayes Esports’ CCO and managing director, Amir Mirzaee, welcomed Scheunemann to the company’s C-level team. He acknowledged that Scheunemann brings significant experience which will undoubtedly help Bayes Esports and shape its sustainable future growth.

Mirzaee added that he is delighted to see Scheunemann join the team at the helm of the company which includes the expert Martin Dachselt who holds the position of CEO. Together, the trio establishes a strong management-level team that can guide Bayes Esports to further growth, Mirzaee explained. Finally, he said that it is exciting to work together with Scheunemann.
