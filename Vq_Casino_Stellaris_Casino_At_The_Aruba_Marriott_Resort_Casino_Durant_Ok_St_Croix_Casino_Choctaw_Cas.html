The global sports betting operator offering odds for multiple sports, BetOnline, was named an official partner of the 2022 Mosconi Cup, the famous nine-ball pool tournament set to take place in Las Vegas this November.

BetOnline Named Partner of the Mosconi Cup

The announcement came Friday and sees BetOnline as the official partner of the famous tournament that is set to launch on November 30 and run through December 3. The annual competition will take place at Bally’s Las Vegas and attract some of the best teams from the United States, as well as Europe. Under the terms of the latest deal, BetOnline will benefit from brand exposure throughout the arena of the 2022 Mosconi Cup. What’s more, the operator will benefit from exposure via social media and the players’ jerseys.

The famous nine-ball pool tournament will be broadcasted live via DAZN and Sky Sports. The event has a large fan base and will be broadcasted in Canada and the US, as well as Italy, Spain and more locations around the world.

The Recent Collaboration Brings Excitement

Emily Frazer, Matchroom Multi Sport’s managing director, said that the company is excited to collaborate with BetOnline for the 2022 edition of the Mosconi Cup. She added: “This is a great chance for both fans to get involved and bet on the action with one of the world’s biggest gaming operators and for BetOnline.AG to get involved in an emerging market that is only getting better.” Finally, Frazer said that Matchroom is looking forward to the success the new deal will bring.

“We’re delighted to be working with BetOnline.ag for this year’s Mosconi Cup.“ Emily Frazer, managing director at Matchroom Multi Sport

Currently, Matchroom Sport produces, owns and creates different sporting events. The company has four decades of experience in delivering value for sponsors and unique engaging experiences for sports fans. To date, the company has organized 650 event days within 12 different sports.

“We are thrilled to be partnering with Matchroom Pool as a title sponsor of the 2022 Mosconi Cup.“ Dave Mason, brand manager at BetOnline

Dave Mason, BetOnline’s brand manager, explained that nine-ball pool enjoys a growing audience of fans not only in Europe but in the US as well. He added that the company is delighted to become a title sponsor for this year’s Mosconi Cup. Last but not least, Mosconi pointed out: “This the biggest billiards event in the US, and we can’t wait to introduce fans to the 25-year-old BetOnline brand behind a full menu of odds and information.”
