Thrill One Signed Multi-Tier Partnership with US Integrity

Established in 2020 Thrill One offers a diverse product range under the brands Nitro Circus, Street League Skateboarding (SLS), Nitro Rallycross, and Thrill One Media.

In connection with the launch of Thrill One events as part of different providers’ sportsbooks, Thrill One has signed a multi-tier partnership with US Integrity. The sports betting monitoring service provider is going to give Thrill One access to its integrity monitoring platform among other services.

US Integrity CEO Matthew Holt has expressed his excitement about helping Thrill One to start offering their events across the regulated sports betting market in the United States and has highlighted the significance of such collaborations: “These types of partnerships help ensure sports betting integrity and strengthen the unique & proprietary anomaly detection tools we have developed at US Integrity.”

Thrill One CEO Joe Carr also joined in on the praising words saying that US Integrity will play a key role in the process of Thrill One expanding to the regulated sports wagering markets in the United States. Carr further highlighted the importance of the partnership for his company: “Their commitment and proactive approach will play a pivotal role in ensuring that integrity is maintained as wagering becomes available across all of our sporting events.”

Thrill One’s event Nitro Rallycross is coming back to the US taking place on October 1 and 2 in Minneapolis. It will be the first of the company’s events to be offered for wagering in the US states where sports betting is legal. The Nitro RX season is going to continue until March next year.
