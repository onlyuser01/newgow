Illinois: Yet to Legalize Online Gambling, but Social Casinos Are Popular

Illinois legalized sports betting back in June 2019. However, it wasn’t until March 2020, that the activity was finally launched. Since then, the popularity of the activity has been growing significantly, which paves the way for legalizing online gambling activities as well. However, for the moment, “real-money” online gambling isn’t legal in Illinois.

On the other hand, currently, there are social gambling websites in the state. Those websites offer casino-like entertainment such as table and slot games. It is important to mention that to play those games, consumers are not required to submit a real-money deposit, but can win prizes, some of which are monetary.

Considering the current legal framework, iGaming service providers just like Internet Vikings are not required to hold a license.
