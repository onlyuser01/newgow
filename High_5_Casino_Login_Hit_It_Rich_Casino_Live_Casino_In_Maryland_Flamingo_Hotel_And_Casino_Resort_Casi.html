Interwetten Has Decades of Working Experience in the Sports Betting Industry

As a company based in Vienna, Austria, Interwetten was established in 1990. It provides punters with access to over 70 sports, features over 1,200 unique games, and hosts products by more than 30 suppliers.

All betting offers at Interwetten are international and the platform supports several languages. Its reputation and strive to provide players with the best experience is what allowed the platform to ink deals with respected leagues and federations.
