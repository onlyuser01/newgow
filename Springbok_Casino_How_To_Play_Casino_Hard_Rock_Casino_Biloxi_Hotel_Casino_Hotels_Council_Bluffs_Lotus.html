Step Right into the Wild Moon Saloon

Players start the game by spending some time outside the wildest saloon in the Wild West, on a golden-hued street in a tiny town in the Wild West. During the Free Spins round, the action switches to the inside of the saloon. The game was given a cartoonish design and a matching Western soundtrack. The most courageous players will get the chance to saddle up and start claiming the massive bounties.

The game is played on a 5×4 grid counting 40 paylines. The premium symbols in the game are four grim-looking characters that pay between 6x and 12.5x the original bet for matching a minimum of five identical symbols. The payouts drop to 3.5x-4x for five matching bottle symbols, and 2x-3x for a minimum of five matching card royal symbols. The wilds substitute any and all pay symbols and they are valued at 15x the original bet for five wilds on a payline. The explosive action during the base game is maintained by a Mystery Bonus that is also up for grabs randomly.

The game is also packed with Scatters. Landing at least three of them triggers the Spins bonus round. Wild symbols landing during the Free Spins round engage random Wilds, Exploding Wilds, Rising EIlds, or Wild Reels.

Prior to the Free Spins bonus round, players are invited to participate in a pick game. The number of picks depends on the number of Scatter symbols that were used to trigger the Free Spins bonus. The respective picks can make Wilds, Multipliers and extra Free Spins pop up.
