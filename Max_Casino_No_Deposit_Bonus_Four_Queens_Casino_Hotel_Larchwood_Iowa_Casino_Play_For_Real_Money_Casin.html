FanDuel Brings Professional Pickleball Lines to Sportsbooks in 10 States

Posted on: August 25, 2023, 09:28h.

Last updated on: August 27, 2023, 12:10h.

FanDuel is the first major sportsbook in the US to offer legal wagering on pickleball.

Pro pickleball players Jay Devilliers (left) and Jessie Irvine. FanDuel is the first major US sportsbook to offer odds on the Professional Pickleball Association Tour. (Image: Professional Pickleball Association)

Effective Thursday, August 24, FanDuel Sportsbook bettors have access to lines on pickleball tournaments on the Professional Pickleball Association Tour (PPAT). The PPAT bills itself as the “elite tour” for professional pickleball.

Bettors can wager on match winners, correct match scores, total points (over/under), and head-to-head. FanDuel’s pickleball lines are live in Colorado, Connecticut, Illinois, Louisiana, Maryland, Michigan, Oregon, Tennessee, Washington, and Wyoming. The sportsbook is awaiting pickleball approval in several other states.

FanDuel, PPAT officials hope, is just the first major U.S. sportsbook to pick up its tournaments.

“This is a monumental moment for the PPA Tour and the game of pickleball as a whole,” declared PPA Tour Founder and CEO Connor Pardoe. “We are excited to be paving the way in the pickleball sports betting space, and we couldn’t be prouder to lead the sport’s leap into such an innovative industry.”

Pardoe believes allowing fans to bet on pickleball will only further grow the sport, which in recent years has been one of the fastest-growing sports in the US.

The Association of Pickleball Professionals estimates there are 36.5 million pickleball players in the US, and participation has grown nearly 160% over the past three years. Players aged 18-34 account for the largest percentage of pickleball players at 29% nationwide.

Official Pickleball Data Stream

To ensure sportsbooks have the most up-to-date and accurate information, the PPAT has partnered with Genius Sports to provide a real-time data stream to participating sportsbooks. Genius Sports is a leading sports data firm in the sports gaming space.

For pickleball, Genius Sports isn’t only a statistical feeder to sportsbooks but is also providing “low latency” or minimally delayed streaming footage of PPAT matches. Sportsbooks can choose to offer streaming content to bettors for increased engagement.

To ensure game integrity, the PPAT has partnered with Sportradar, a leading monitor of sports betting activity. Sportradar’s bet monitoring and detection services are used by more than 160 sportsbooks and gaming firms worldwide.

The PPAT has also formulated a Sports Betting and Integrity Policy that all players, officials, and staff must adhere to. All persons involved in PPAT events must complete an online course educating them about the sport’s betting policy.

Pickleball Coming to Las Vegas

The 2023 PPA Tour consists of 25 events across the country. The PPA Tour comes to Las Vegas next month for the Guaranteed Rate PPA Championships Presented by Thorlo. The tournament runs Oct. 5-8 at the Darling Tennis Center in Summerlin.
