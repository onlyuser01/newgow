Boldplay has inked an agreement with Daintree Gaming that will allow it to host its games on Betorspin and other global platforms

The fast-expanding software developer that offers omni-channel gaming solutions and innovative in-house jackpots, Boldplay, has signed a content distribution deal with the Curacao-based online operator, Daintree Gaming.

According to the terms of the agreement, the latter will host the developer’s entire line-up of casino games on all of its global platforms, starting with Betorspin. In the following months, additional Daintree Gaming-powered online gambling sites will host Boldplay’s signature releases.

In March, Boldplay announced it reached a new milestone by obtaining a Romanian gaming license from ONJN and thus entering the country’s quickly growing iGaming market.

Hosting Boldplay’s Catalog, “a Significant Milestone” for Daintree Gaming

Daintree Gaming’s head of product, Rob Antill, commented on the news, explaining that the partnership represents “a significant milestone” for them that should allow them to provide players with a more versatile selection of games while fostering their success in the upcoming years.

Boldplay’s chief executive officer, Valli Fragoso, also expressed similar feelings of delight for striking the agreement with Daintree and being able to see their games be distributed across their massive range of platforms.

Fragaso added that the new partnership should allow them to form more ties with their audiences via Betorspin and the additional global operators that will follow suit in the upcoming months, which should contribute to their goal of becoming one of the leading providers on the market.
