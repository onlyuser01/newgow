After introducing a jackpot version of its popular slot The Secret of Ali Baba just last week, RAW iGaming has more fresh and exciting news for its fans.

RAW Group’s in-house developer studio has announced the development and release of SuperSymbols, its latest game mechanic. The new mechanic currently waiting for a patent joins the impressive list of similar game mechanics like SuperSlice, SuperTracks, and SuperStretch that has entirely changed the way featuring online slots are played.

The fresh feature has made its debut in the studio’s newly launched Mighty Buffalo SuperSymbols slot. In the near future, Raging Super2Ways will join the roster of games that will incorporate the new win potential boosting mechanic.

The Larger the SuperSymbols, the Greater the Wins

Continuing its goal to “blast industry monotony out of the sky” and disrupt the status quo, RAW iGaming has envisioned the innovative mechanic with matching symbols that can be seen landing adjacent to one another each in groups of at least 2×2.

Once the matching groups merge, they trigger SuperSymbols, which are paying symbols that will grow in size once the same symbols will be seen landing adjacent to them.

The size of the symbols is determined by the pixel count of the image. In other words, the larger the SuperSymbols get, the greater their win potential becomes. All players, with special emphasis on mobile players, will this way have a much easier time understanding the exact outcomes of the games they are engaged in while enjoying a better experience.

The SuperSymbols mechanic can be used together with other bonus features including respins, cascades, free spins, and progressive multipliers, similar to the SuperTrack mechanic that can be combined with multipliers, additional bonuses, and free spins.
