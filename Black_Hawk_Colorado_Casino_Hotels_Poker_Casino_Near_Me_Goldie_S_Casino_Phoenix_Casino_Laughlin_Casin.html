Stakelogic Promotes Olga Bajela to CCO

Casino games developer, Stakelogic, announced today that it promoted Olga Bajela to the position of Chief Commercial Officer (CCO). The company hired Bajela last year in September. Then near the end of the year, she was promoted to sales director. In her new position, Bajela will be in charge of expanding the company into more regulated markets. Furthermore, she will also oversee Stakelogic’s competitiveness within the game development space.

Bajela has previous experience within the iGaming space and will be supported by Stakelogic in this new endeavor. Furthermore, the company also complimented the great work which she has already done so far.

In a statement, Stephan van den Oetelaar, CEO of Stakelogic, said that Bajela’s performance has been impressive since the day she started. Furthermore, he acknowledged that she played key roles in the delivery of important partner integrations, and territory launches such as the ones in Spain, and Romania. In conclusion, Stakelogic’s CEO said that the company believes that Bajela will “excel in her new role as CCO“.

“We fully believe Olga will excel in her new role as CCO and all of us at Stakelogic wish her the utmost congratulations.” Stephan van den Oetelaar, CEO, Stakelogic

Mrs. Bajela shared her excitement in a statement, saying that being a part of Stakelogic has been a great experience. Furthermore, she added that she is looking forward to the new challenges which the CCO position will bring for her. In conclusion, Bajela thanked Stakelogic’s management team for the trust which they have in her.
