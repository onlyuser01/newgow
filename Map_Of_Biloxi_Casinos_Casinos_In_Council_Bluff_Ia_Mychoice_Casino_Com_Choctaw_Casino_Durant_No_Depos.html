Game Overview

In a game of Wild Willy’s Gold Rush, players will collect various symbols, including stylized letters, numbers, golden nuggets and characters. In addition, there is a multiplier wheel that spins alongside the main reels and provides players with chances to multiply wins by three. In addition, matching a golden nugget symbol on the multiplier wheel provides players with a 20x multiplier on all line wins.

To enter free spins mode, players need to collect 3, 4 or 5 scatter symbols, in which case they are awarded 8, 12 or 15 free spins respectively. During that mode, players can accumulate whiskey shots and increase their multipliers. Lucky participants can earn more free spins during that stage.

True to his name, Wild Willy acts as a wild symbol. During the free spins mode Wild Willy symbols can collect golden nuggets that are worth 20 times more.
