Digitain provider of sportsbook software, iGaming platform, turnkey and API solutions, has strengthened its senior management team with a new promotion. As announced by the company, Edmond Ghulyan will climb its ranks and will now serve as its new group chief strategy officer and chief of Centrivo Product Solutions.

In this new position, Ghulyan will lead the development of Digitain’s acclaimed and GLI-certified iGaming platform. In addition, the employee will be responsible for the company’s Centrivo product suite and Digitain’s strategy as a whole.

Ghulyan is a longtime Digitain employee, boasting six years of experience with the company. Prior to his new position, he served as the supplier’s chief customer care officer and chief of the Centrivo platform.

Earlier this year, Digitain was also joined by the experienced Margarita Cruz who assumed the role of the company’s new senior sales director.

Ghulyan Is Looking Forward to Underpinning Digitain’s Growth

Ghulyan was praised for his promotion by Digitain Group’s founder, Vardges Vardanyan. He said:

Edmond has been a key part of our senior management and leadership team for nearly six years. He has led from the front and is a shining example to his team in delivering exacting technical solutions for our current and future partners. Vardges Vardanyan, founder, Digitain

Vardanyan added that Digitain has experienced strong growth across multiple jurisdictions in 2023. He is certain that as chief strategy officer and chief of Centrivo Product Solutions, Ghulyan will further bolster this growth in 2024.

Ghulyan commented on his new position, saying that he is looking forward to helping Digitain grow. Expressing his excitement about this “new and more comprehensive role,” the promoted employee vowed to build upon the strong foundations of Centrivo Product Solutions. Ghulyan added that this would enable his company’s partners to “meet the ever-changing demands of their businesses.”

As a business and team, we are well placed to continue to deliver robust, scalable, and highly compliant technology and product solutions for any market and ensure that our partners can focus on building a competitive, unique and highly-automated brand across multiple channels and markets. Edmond Ghulyan, group chief strategy officer and chief of Centrivo Product Solutions, Digitain

Ghulyan teased that Digitain has ambitious plans for the future. He thanked the company for allowing him to be a part of its journey and Vardanyan for supporting him throughout his time at Digitain.
