The agreement with EveryMatrix comes in the wake of two similar partnerships 7777 gaming signed with Hub88 and IGT

7777 gaming, an online B2B provider, which offers iGaming platform, games and tools, has unveiled a new strategic partnership with the renowned software provider EveryMatrix. Under the terms of the agreement, 7777 gaming’s entire portfolio will be made available to EveryMatrix’s aggregation partners.

As agreed by the two companies, EveryMatrix will add content from its new partner to its proprietary integration and productivity platform CasinoEngine. The company claims that the latter solution has the most extensive library in the industry and is excited to expand it with extra titles.

As a result of the agreement, CasinoEngine partners in Europe, Africa and LATAM will be able to access 7777 gaming’s titles, diversifying their offerings. 7777 gaming’s games will be added to a library that includes some 12,500+ games from over 250 content providers.

CasinoEngine is considered to be the go-to source for all gambling companies looking to provide fans with new iGaming experiences. The library is also helpful to operators that wish to enter new markets with content tailored to local needs.
