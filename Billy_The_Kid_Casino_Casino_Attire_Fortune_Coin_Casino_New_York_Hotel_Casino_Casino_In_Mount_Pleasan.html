UK Presence

MansionBet, the sports betting brand of the Gibraltar-based group, continued to affirm its presence in UK sports after recently implemented changes to the format of the All-Weather Championship by Arena Racing Company (ARC) added the brand as a sponsor of the races throughout the ninth season alongside ARC’s long-standing partner, Super Group-owned Betway.

Mansion Group featured heavily in English soccer in the past: its first high-profile sponsorship deal dates back to 2006 when the group signed a 4-year deal with English Premier League (EPL) Tottenham Hotspur. The group also became the official sponsor of AFC Bournemouth since its promotion to the EPL in 2015. In the same year, Mansion Group also inked a sponsorship deal with Crystal Palace.

Most recently, MansionBet signed with English Football League (EFL) Championship Millwall FC for the 2020-21 season to add the Lions to its other EFL club sponsorship partner Bristol City.
