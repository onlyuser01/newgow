The Tamil Nadu Premier League in India will be covered from low-latency TV by a network of STATSCORE’s top scouts, the company announced

While the 2023 Indian Premier League season is over, the cricket action will continue. STATSCORE, a leading provider of data coverage, announced that it will keep fans engaged by covering the Tamil Nadu Premier League, one of India’s best regional leagues.

Fans Want More, STATSCORE Delivers

STATSCORE praised the concluded IPL season as one of the most exciting ones yet. During the IPL, the provider kept fans supplied with live data, covering all 74 matchups. Now that the IPL is through, STATSCORE announced its intention to keep fans’ appetite for cricket betting satiated.

As announced, STATSCORE will now leverage its capabilities to provide top-quality sports data and statistics for the Tamil Nadu Premier League. The competition is held annually in Tamil Nadu and is one of the most popular regional cricket leagues in India.

Each year, teams representing the cities of the province clash, with each one seeking to defeat its opponents and emerge victorious. As highlighted by STATSCORE, this regional league provides athletes with exposure at the domestic level and helps promising players gain popularity.

STATSCORE Will Provide Players with Detailed Coverage

As announced, the Tamil Nadu Premier League will be covered from low-latency TV by a network of STATSCORE’s top scouts.

The coverage, according to the data specialist, will include key in-game incidents, ball-by-ball fast data, score updates, strike rate, run rate, faster boundary, wickets update and more.

By adopting STATSCORE’s solutions, operators and sportsbooks will be able to customize their offerings. In addition, they will be able to keep their business secure with bet-stop and bet-start alerts.

In addition to cricket, STATSCORE’s ScoutsFeed also covers sports such as football, soccer, baseball, basketball, tennis and volleyball. By integrating the provider’s solutions, companies can secure access to robust and reliable data that is sure to attract many sports betting enthusiasts to their platforms.

STATSCORE Is Now a Part of LSports

STATSCORE is a Polish company now owned by LSports, an Israeli provider of innovative sports betting data API for the sports betting industry. The merger saw the former entity join the LSports family last year and was notably LSports’ first European acquisition.

Around the same time, STATSCORE also formed a partnership with BETBY, one of the most innovative sportsbook suppliers and tech companies. That deal sought to pair the two companies’ capabilities to empower sports bettors with sports tech and solutions.
