Money Train 3 review

Theme and Storyline

Money Train 3 takes the much-loved franchise into a new sci-fi world.

Trading in the Western landscape, players will find themselves surrounded by advanced machines and cyberpunk characters. The titular train now looks far more like a vehicle for space travel fueled by a glowing blue liquid.

This slot is a good find for players who love the original Money Train slot, but feel ready to explore a futuristic setting.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The soundtrack features mournful string notes that evoke a Western vibe with a steady beat that brings to mind the steady rumble of a train.

An opening animation sequence showcases one of the cyberpunk characters loading a weapon and firing it with a mischievous smile.

On the reels, players will spot bandits wearing intimidating gas masks as well as an enigmatic heroine with blue hair and face paint. A wise bearded man in a cowboy hat gives a nod to the original Money Train slots while his sci-fi monocle suggests he’s part of quite a different world.

Card suits also appear among the symbols, glowing in shades of blue, green, red, and gold.

The reels are built into a metallic train car, with a skull adorning the front of the engine. The track is made of glossy metal that matches the train itself. Beyond the reels, a mysterious room filled with buttons and panels can be seen.

Overall, the soundtrack and animation create a dystopian future infused with western flavor.

Gameplay

Money Train 3 is a five-reel, four-row slot, with 40 win lines.

The gameplay is swift and engaging, ensuring players will have no difficulty paying attention.

If they wish, players can buy into any of the bonus features (see further details below).

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all paying symbols.

The Respin feature can be triggered randomly on any spin where there is no winning combination. During this feature, a symbol appearing currently on the reels will be chosen at random to be sticky for the round. Players are then awarded 1 respin and if any of the special sticky symbols land again, another respin is awarded.

All standard paying symbols are replaced with over a dozen of special symbols in the Money Cart Bonus round

During the Respin feature, players can also land multiplier symbols with values ranging from 1x to 3x the player’s bet.

The Money Cart Bonus is triggered when players land 3 or more Bonus symbols which then become sticky and show a value. Then, players begin with 3 respins. Persistent Gold symbols can also trigger the Money Cart Bonus.

Highly volatile version of the Money Cart Bonus round with 1 Persistent Symbol

During the Money Cart Bonus, players can land paying symbols that have associated multipliers between 1x and 10x the player’s total bet.

A variety of special symbols may also appear during the bonus, including the Collector, which gathers the values from other symbols on the reels to itself. There is also a special Sniper symbol that has the special property of doubling bonus symbol values, and an Absorber symbol that takes any regular bonus symbols off the reels.
