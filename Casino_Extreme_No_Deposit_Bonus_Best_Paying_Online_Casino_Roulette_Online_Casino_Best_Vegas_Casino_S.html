The new sponsorship agreement, spanning over the next three years, continues the growth of 10bet in South Africa

The global online gambling and sportsbook operator, 10bet, announced it forged a new sponsorship agreement, expanding further its global footprint. The company confirmed Friday it was selected as the principal partner of Lamontville Golden Arrows, a famous South African soccer club based in Durban. Under the terms of the new sponsorship deal, 10bet will benefit from brand exposure via the club’s main and reserve team kits. Per the new collaboration, the brand’s logo will be present on the front of the shirt of both home and away kits of the Golden Arrows for the next three years.

The new sponsorship agreement will help promote the 10bet brand and reach new audiences. At the same time, the collaboration complements the growth of the betting and gaming company in South Africa. In fact, recently, 10bet joined forces with South Africa’s national men’s team, Bafana Bafana. That deal saw the brand become the Official Betting Partner of the team.

Michelle Henriques Colborne, 10bet SA’s chief revenue officer, spoke about the rich history of the Golden Arrows and acknowledged the club’s strong presence in the country. At the same time, she highlighted the unique growth opportunity that the new collaboration represents for 10bet.

According to Colborne, 10bet and the Golden Arrows share similar passions and goals. She predicted that the collaboration will help the club engage with new fans and connect better with existing fans while helping 10bet grow further as well.

“Golden Arrows is a brand with a rich history, strong presence and support, and is a perfect complement to our own growth ambitions here at 10bet SA.“ Michelle Henriques Colborne, chief revenue officer at 10bet SA
