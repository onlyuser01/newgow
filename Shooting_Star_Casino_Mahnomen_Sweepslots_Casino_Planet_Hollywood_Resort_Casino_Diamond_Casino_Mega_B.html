The operator has been given the go-ahead by LOTBA, the city’s gambling authority, and to complete its launch, PlayUZU also had to team up with BuenosAiresSlots, a local company that is part of the licensing conditions that foreign operators face before rolling out.

PlayUZU focuses on providing players with a fair casino offering that will appeal to local audiences, and the entry into Buenos Aires will also serve as a prelude to further expansion across the country. Presently, the iGaming market in Argentina is split between 23 provincial jurisdictions, not all of which have given a greenlight to online gambling regulation.
