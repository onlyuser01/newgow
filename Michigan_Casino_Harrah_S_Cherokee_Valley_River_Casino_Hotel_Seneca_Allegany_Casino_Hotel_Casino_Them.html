Multiple Mechanics Increase Chances to Win Big

The new game features a variety of symbols representing wild animals, such as wolves, bears, saber-toothed cats and, of course, mammoths. Players can collect these symbols in a variety of combinations to win prizes.

Engagement is further boosted by two of Yggdrasil’s most popular mechanics, namely DoubleMax and GigaBlox. The former sees every cascade double the total win multiplier. The GigaBlox mechanic, on the other hand, sometimes has the reels land on huge symbols that provide opportunities to score winning combinations.

In addition, the game has the unique Mammoth Explode and Mammoth Shuffle features. The first of the two causes a cascade when there are no wins remaining on the reels. Mammoth Shuffle, on the other hand, causes GigaBlox symbols on the right side of the grid to be shoved to the left by the mammoth, guaranteeing a win.

On each subsequent cascade, the GigaBlox reel will move back to the right, providing opportunities for further wins.

The Game Has an Engaging Free Spins Mode

10,000 BC DoubleMax also sports a free spins round that is activated when players collect bonus symbols represented by a stone spear. Players can also buy 7 free spins for 100 times their bet, triggering the bonus round instantly.
