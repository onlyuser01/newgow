Ran Kaspi, formerly a part of the financial management at ParaZero Ltd, is going to be the new Chief Financial Officer of the esports betting company Luckbox.

Luckbox Hires the Services of Ran Kaspi as CFO

Luckbox, the esports betting company, has announced that Ran Kaspi is going to be its new Chief Financial Officer (CFO). Luckbox’s CEO, Quentin Martin shared his excitement by saying that he is pleased to welcome Mr. Kaspi to the management team of the company.

Furthermore, he added that Mr. Kaspi’s experience is hugely relevant to the company’s story and undoubtedly, he is going to play a major role in the company. In conclusion, the company’s CEO revealed that Luckbox is looking forward to building on its “phenomenal” 2020 success and continue growing as a public company.

“I am delighted to formally welcome Ran to the team.” Quentin Martin, Luckbox CEO

After observing an amazing growth of some 500% during 2020, Luckbox, which operates under the parent company Real Luck Group, went public on the Toronto TSX Venture Exchange under the ticker “TSXV:LUCK” last month. Undoubtedly, the temporary suspension of traditional sports due to the global COVID-19 pandemic boosted the betting on esports and helped Luckbox in 2020.

Mr. Kaspi Is Happy to Join Luckbox

Sharing his excitement on the topic, Mr. Kaspi said that he is happy to join the team at Luckbox in this exciting time for business and the esports industry. Furthermore, he added that the combination of an incredibly successful background and the public listing of the company represented an appealing opportunity for him and a “potential to become a fantastic growth story in the future“.

“I’m very happy to join the team at what is a hugely exciting time for the business and the esports industry as a whole.” Ran Kaspi

Before joining the management team at Luckbox, Mr. Kaspi was a part of the financial management team at ParaZero Ltd, a company developing drone safety solutions. Before that, he was also a part of 888 Holdings and led the company’s economic and performance team.
