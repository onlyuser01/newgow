Intralot S.A. has confirmed that its subsidiary Intralot, Inc which operates in the United States will continue to collaborate with the Wyoming Lottery Corporation. The two partners have been together over the past several years, and they have reported a mutually beneficial business relationship that has now led to an extension.

The new period of the partnership adds another five years onto the already existing contract, and will see Intralot, Inc and the lottery collaborate through August 25, 2034. This new milestone in the companies’ mutual relationship demonstrates their clear commitment to bringing local consumers the best lottery products and doing so successfully over the past years.

Commenting on this, Wyoming Lottery Corporation CEO Jon Clontz said that the partnership was "extremely important," and that any such relationship must be based on trust, transparency and not least, mutual respect.

"We have that trust with INTRALOT and we enjoy a very successful track record of achievement in part due to their deep knowledge and dedication," Clontz said confidently. He was joined by Intralot, Inc CEO Nikos Nikolakopoulos who was similarly pleased with the opportunity to bring responsible gaming experiences in the United States, and Wyoming in particular.

"We look forward to collaborating closely with the Wyoming Lottery to achieve new heights of success," the executive said also confident in the success of the partnership. Intralot, Inc has been busy building its footprint in the United States and the country’s gaming market over the past year, leveraging an advanced tech stack to ensure that partner operators and their audiences receive some of the best experiences there are.

The company is well-represented globally and it is presently operational in 39 jurisdictions worldwide. Intralot has been equally keen on strengthening its partnership not just in the United States, but also in Canada. The company signed a partnership with British Columbia Lottery Corporation and is also present in the province.

As in the United States so in Canada, Intralot is focused on using its expertise in the vertical to help partner lotteries drive and achieve better results, boost revenues, and engage better with their audiences, bringing meaningful experiences to players in the process as well. Presently, Intralot employs some 1,700 people worldwide, and the company continues to scale its offer across the globe.
