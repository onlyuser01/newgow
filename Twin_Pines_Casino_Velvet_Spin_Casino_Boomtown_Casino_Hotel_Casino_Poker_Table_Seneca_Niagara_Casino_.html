Based in the UK, Red Bull Racing competes in Formula One powered by a Honda engine and via an Austrian license. It is one of the two F1 teams owned by the popular beverage producer Red Bull GmbH, the other one being Scuderia AlphaTauri, previously known as Scuderia Toro Rosso.
