Join the Hive of Sweet Thrills in Paradise Garden

The action is set in what appears to be a garden paradise, filled with buzzing insects whose role is to help players create winning matching combinations across the 20 available paylines. Wins are created from left to right, on adjacent reels.

Landing between three and five identical low-paying or high-paying symbols will trigger wins ranging between $0.50 and $100 for a standard bet of $2. Wild symbols that land on any location and contribute to a winning combination or improve it pay the same as the highest-paying symbol represented by the honey bee.

Free Spins Bonus Rounds

To trigger the bonus round, players need to come across a minimum of three golden beehive scatter symbols. During the round, players are allowed to choose one of three available features inspired by the previously mentioned wild features.

The first one is the Random Wilds Bonus Round which gives players 10 free spins while turning all added wild symbols from the random wilds feature into sticky symbols become for the remainder of the round.

During the Expanding Wilds Bonus Round, players receive eight free spins and see all wilds expanding, covering their reels, and turning into sticky symbols for the rest of the bonus round.

Finally, when the Surrounding Wilds Bonus Round is active, players get to enjoy six free spins and see all wild symbols from the surrounding wilds feature keep their positions frozen for the remainder of the round.
