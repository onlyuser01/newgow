Betway Users in 5 US States to Enjoy Simplebet’s Micro-Betting Products

The leading B2B provider of sports betting technology, Simplebet, announced that its fully automated real-money micro-betting products are now live on Betway, the global gaming and betting brand. Simplebet’s innovative micro-betting products support real-money and free-to-play betting options for the NFL, MLB, and NBA. Moreover, the company recently added college football, expanding further its wagering capabilities. During the H2 2021 and the beginning of 2022, the company’s proprietary micro-betting products enabled users to place bets on millions of individual moments within thousands of NFL, MLB, NBA, and college football games.

What is more, by offering its innovative automated operations platform to media companies and sports betting operators, Simplebet enables them to create real-time engaging experiences. Besides creating unique engaging experiences, the company’s products help increase fan engagement.
