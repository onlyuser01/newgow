America's leading lottery app, Jackpocket, announced it forged a new partnership with the famous professional baseball team based in Boston, Massachusetts, Boston Red Sox. Jackpocket revealed that it plans to engage in multiple giveaways, awarding major prizes to the team's fans. Judging by a statement released by Jackpocket, such prizes can include unique VIP experiences like behind-the-scenes ballpark tours, tickets to home games, as well as other activations.
