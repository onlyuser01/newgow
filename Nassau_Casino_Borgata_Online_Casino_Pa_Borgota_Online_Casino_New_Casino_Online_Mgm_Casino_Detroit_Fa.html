Fanatics Betting and Gaming has confirmed that it has secured a new partnership with Paysafe, which will see the latter provide the Fanatics Sportsbook with traditional card payments and alternative payment methods (APMs), the company detailed in a press release.

Fanatics Sportsbooks will benefit from Paysafe’s card transactions across a number of states, including Ohio, Tennessee, Kentucky, Massachusetts, and not least Maryland.

"We’re confident that our ‘all in one’ payment solution will exceed the transactional expectations of Fanatics Sportsbook’s diverse customer base and support the brand’s growth in its current and future markets," Cutler added.
