Tally Technology (playtally.com), a provider of premium fan engagement solutions has added a seasoned soccer expert to its team. Juli Ferre Nadal, who has worked as an executive at several soccer clubs and organizations, will join the Tally family and help its platform grow.

Nadal Brings Years of Experience to Tally

Nadal boasts over 15 years of experience in professional sports. During his career, the expert worked for some of the biggest soccer clubs and companies. He joined FC Barcelona as its head of global partnerships in 2013 and worked with the club’s partners for four years. After that, Nadal joined AS Monaco, another professional soccer club, where he served as a commercial and marketing director. After three years, Nadal used the experience he built to found his own consultancy, JFN Sport. Nadal’s prior experience also saws him work within the FIBA and FIFA Beach Soccer sectors.

As a member of Tally’s team, Nadal will be responsible for helping the company connect with sports enthusiasts. The provider’s platform powers companies with immersive, free-to-play, real-time prediction games that engage fans of pro sports and improve their overall loyalty with meaningful experiences. Tally’s solutions are used by various betting brands, as well as by broadcasters and sports teams. All and all, the company’s platform is perfect for creating new revenue streams.

Tally Welcomed Nadal to the Family

Brad Vettese, Tally Technology’s chief executive officer, welcomed Nadal on board and lauded the new hire’s experience in the sports marketing sector. Vettese also added that the entire Tally team is thrilled to have Nadal around and is happy that he “has seen the value Tally can offer major brands and sports rights holders.”

We are delighted that Juli is joining us as a consultant and board advisor, and we are sure that he can offer immense value as we continue to partner with major organizations within the global sports sector. Brad Vettese

Meanwhile, Nadal also made a statement about his new position. He praised Tally’s fantastic work in the NA and SA markets over the past few years, saying that the company’s fan engagement efforts have been nothing short of impressive. Throughout his entire career in sports, Nadal has seen few fan engagement platforms as advanced as Tally’s, Nadal admitted. He said that joining Tally was a no-brainer and that the company provided unique opportunities to engage fans in Europe as well.

I’m looking forward to helping grow the platform with the introduction of new brands and sports who share the vision for this inclusive type of fan engagement. Juli Ferre Nadal

Over the past four years, Tally has recorded over 20 million predictions from customers in the Americas, attesting to the effectiveness of its fan engagement approach.
