In the early hours of Friday, Kanye West went on another of his now trademark antisemitic rants. In this one, the formerly respected hip-hop musician raved about Adolf Hitler, Donald Trump, and his former business partners.

Kanye West needs some help, but his hangers-on aren’t qualified to provide him. (Image: YouTube)

“It’s 60 million of us in America, 60 million Jews in the world!” he falsely screamed in a video circulating on social media. West also claimed that Zionist Jews own all the banks, hospitals, and private schools in L.A.

“Who’s going to make the hospitals, though? He’s a Zionist, Trump. This is what I’ve been trying to tell you. Jesus Christ, Hitler, Ye, third party, sponsor that shit.”

A group of West’s “friends” egged on every “point” the spiraling rapper made, appearing to value staying in his orbit more than correcting or helping him.

According to tabloid reports, the 10-minute explosion took place in his suite at the Four Seasons Hotel in Las Vegas, where West and the hangers-on retreated after Vegas police shut down a $2K-a-ticket party.

West with some of his friends in his Las Vegas hotel suite. (Image: Instagram/@yesjulz)

Vulture Release

The party, thrown by West with rapper Ty Dolla $ign, was held to preview material from West’s new album, “Vultures,” which has already missed its scheduled release date.

However, it was held at a Vegas warehouse without a proper permit.

As a crowd that included North — West’s 10-year-old daughter with Kim Kardashian – watched, West sampled lyrics from the album: “I still keep some Jews with me. Management? Nah. I only let ’em do my jewelry.”

West v. Kardashian

West and Kardashian — who have four children together — finalized their divorce in November 2022.

West, 46, was blacklisted by the music industry last year for similar comments. He claims he was misdiagnosed with bipolar disorder in 2018.

He and Bianca Censori, his Australian model wife, were seen outside the Four Seasons Hotel hours later, standing with their luggage while waiting for a car to pick them up.
