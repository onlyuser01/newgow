The dynamic and innovative iGaming company that is a part of NeoGames, Pariplay, expanded further the portfolio of partners for its Fusion platform thanks to a new agreement. Such games include Sword King, ELYSIUM VIP and Spirit Hunters, among other hit games that will become available for Pariplay's network of partners after they are integrated into the aggregator's Fusion platform.

Sergio Jacob, ELYSIUM Studios' Chief Executive Officer, shared his excitement about the new deal in a statement saying: "We believe that we have created a portfolio of games that are truly unique, and which will appeal to a wide range of players around the world."

He explained that the collaboration with Pariplay will help the company expand its global footprint and team up with operators in new jurisdictions. Finally, Jacob said: "It is a landmark deal for us, and we look forward to putting our content in front of as many players as possible."

Enrico Bradamante, Pariplay's Chief Commercial Officer, also acknowledged the importance of the new collaboration. He said that the company plans to continue extending its reach while at the same time teaming up with leading studios that deliver exciting experiences for online players.

"ELYSIUM Studios does exactly that, with its range of titles that are highly polished and which carry influences from video gaming as well as online casino," explained Bradamante.
