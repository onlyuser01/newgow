Leading B2B provider of sports betting solutions Kambi Group has inked a multi-channel sportsbook partnership agreement with Saginaw Chippewa Gaming Enterprises – a tribal betting operator in Michigan.

Kambi to Provide Solutions to Saginaw Chippewa’s Venues

The deal will see Saginaw Chippewa Gaming Enterprises integrate Kambi Group’s on-site sportsbook platform into two of its venues, namely the Saganing Eagles Landing Casino and Hotel, as well the Soaring Eagle Casino and Resort. The tribal gambling operator will also integrate Kambi’s various solutions, such as the Bring Your Own Device tech, the Bet Builder solution, and Kambi kiosk. Furthermore, the partnership between the two parties will also cover the release of an online sportsbook.

Melinda Coffin, the chief executive officer of Saginaw Chippewa Gaming Enterprises, said that she is thrilled to announce the tribal operator’s partnership with Kambi Group. She revealed that the clients of Saginaw Chippewa Gaming Enterprises’ venues have been “anxiously looking forward to sports betting. We look forward to working with Kambi Group and providing a great experience for our guests,” Coffin concluded.
