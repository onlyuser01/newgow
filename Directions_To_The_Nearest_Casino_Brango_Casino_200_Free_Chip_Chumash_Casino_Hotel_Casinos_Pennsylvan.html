Multiply Wins with the Super Stack, Free Spins, and Bonus Guarantee Features

To score a regular win, players must land a minimum of three identical symbols on the same payline from left to right, starting with the first reel to the left.

For more exciting wins, players need to keep an eye on the Super Hot Stacks feature that can be engaged on random spins. To do this, players need to land the same type of symbol on all three rows.

This will beef up the win multiplier by 1, giving players a 2X multiplier for landing a stack and a maximum of 6X when landing stacked symbols during the base game.

The maximum multiplier is sticky during the Free Spins feature and can reach 25x. To activate the Free Spins feature, players must land at least three scatters. This will trigger seven Free Spins. The feature can be retriggered with up to 50 Free Spins.

Provided players get less than 10x their total bet during the same feature, they will see the Bonus Guarantee get automatically triggered. In turn, they will receive extra Free Spins to keep the reels spinning.
