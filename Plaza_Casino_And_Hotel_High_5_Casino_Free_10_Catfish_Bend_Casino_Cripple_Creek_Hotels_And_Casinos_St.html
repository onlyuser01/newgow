Booming Games will inject five of its top performers into TonyBet’s platform and will add more games at a later date

Booming Games, a developer of iGaming content whose popularity is booming, has entered Spain through a new partnership with TonyBet. The latter company is a prominent operator that will adopt some of the provider’s best games.

As per the deal, Booming Games will inject five of its top performers into TonyBet’s platform. This will notably include the famous games Megahops Megaways, Buffalo Hold and Win, Cash Pig, The Wild Wings of Phoenix and Crack the Bank Hold and Win.

As a result, TonyBet’s numerous players will now get to experience the amazing gameplay the developer is famous for. However, this is not all as more Booming Games titles are scheduled to be added to TonyBet at a later date.

The partnership marks Booming Games’ foray into the lucrative Spanish market. Spain is a very strict market but also a very promising one, especially for companies committed to following the rules. TonyBet is a regional powerhouse that is sure to popularize Booming Games’ content in the region.

The launch is also in line with Booming Games’ ongoing expansion efforts that have seen the company launch iGaming content in multiple key markets. These efforts recently saw the developer expand its presence in Latin America through a deal with Rivalo.
