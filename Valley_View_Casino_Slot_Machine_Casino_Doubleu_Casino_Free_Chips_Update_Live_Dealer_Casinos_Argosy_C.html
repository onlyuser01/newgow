Modern Games and Inspiral Seen as Two Top Queen Anne Stakes 2023 Favorites

Priced at +163, Modern Games leads the betting action but is closely followed by Inspiration, which is the No. 2 favorite with the Queen Anne States odds of +175.

It’s really no surprise that these two horses are seen as the top two favorites. Modern Games is the most prolific runner on the card, with a 53.33%-win rate (8 wins in 15 starts). Further, Charlie Appleby’s horse has only finished three times outside the top two in his life.

Then, there’s Inspiral, a filly with six wins in eight outings. The last time she ran was in the Queen Elizabeth II Stakes last October when we saw her end in the 6th position. Before that race, however, she was nothing short of impressive. She did start slow, but we saw her completely outclass her rivals near the finish.

However, she hasn’t competed since. The Queen Anne Stakes on Day 1 of the 2023 Royal Ascot is going to be her first competitive race in eight months.
