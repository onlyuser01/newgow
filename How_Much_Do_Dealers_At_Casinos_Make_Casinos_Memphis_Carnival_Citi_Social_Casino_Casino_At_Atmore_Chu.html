Candy Tower review

Theme and Storyline

This game is themed around colourful candy.

In a whimsical fantasy land, players will find themselves surrounded by desserts and sweets. On a reel of brightly- hued hard candies, they’ll try to snag as many matching clusters as they can.

This slot is a great pick for players looking for an upbeat atmosphere and fantasy aesthetic. Some fruit machine and gem slot fans may especially appreciate the plentiful colours and tempting treats.

Graphics, Sounds and Animations

The background is a chill tune punctuated with beeps, adding a touch of sci-fi sensibility to the vibe. The soundtrack can easily be muted but many players will enjoy the relaxing music.

The reels spin with a light click and wins are celebrated with bells and chiming. This keeps the mood festive.

Beside the reels, a small personified cupcake watches the gameplay and cheers when players win. This character adds a friendly touch and their cherry hat makes them particularly cute.

Behind the reels, players will see a stack of pancakes and a slice of cake as tall as buildings. The imaginative animation of this candy world encourages players to let out their inner child.

The reels themselves are filled with candies in a range of shapes and colours including red hearts and violet flowers. It’s not hard to imagine these sweets having mouthwatering fruity flavours.

Overall, the graphics and music combine to create a fun fantasy atmosphere.

Gameplay

Candy Tower is a six reel, five row slot.

Wins are awarded for landing 7 or more matching symbols in a cluster. To be part of a winning cluster, symbols must be connected horizontally or vertically.

Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except the Scatter symbol. The Wild symbol will land on reels 2, 4, and 6 during the base game, but on reels 1-6 during the Free Spins feature.

Wild symbols are allowed to be part of more than one winning cluster, and they may also pay when not part of any group.

The Wild Drop feature may be triggered at random and will drop between 3 and 10 Wild symbols onto rows 3-5.

A number of boost features may be activated as players collect set numbers of matching symbols. These boost bonuses will include expanding symbols, extra Wilds, cash prizes, and multipliers.

The Scatter symbol may pay out anywhere on the reels.

When players land 3 or more Scatter symbols, this will trigger the Free Spins feature, starting with 10 free games. If players land 4 Scatter symbols, they’ll receive 15 free games. Should they land 5 Scatter symbols, they’ll be awarded 50 free games.

The Free Spins feature can be retriggered.
