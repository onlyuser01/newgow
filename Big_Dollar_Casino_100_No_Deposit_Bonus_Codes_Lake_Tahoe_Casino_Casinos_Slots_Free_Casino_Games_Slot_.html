House of Fun Slot Review

House of Fun slot by Betsoft Gaming is a five-reel and 30-payline slot game released by the studio back on December 29, 2011. Yet, the appeal of this slot has not subsided over the years, with players coming back for seconds. The RTP is admittedly a little dated with only 94.96% in theoretical return that players may expect, but this is not such a huge deal when you factor in the 44.52% hit rate and all the other cool features that make this game so much fun in the first place. Without further ado, let’s dive into the world of the House of Fun slot game with our review.

Theme, Storyline, and Graphics: Prepare to be Amazed

House of Fun is not so much about fun, as it’s about an eerie mansion decked out with all sorts of ominous-looking portraits and chandeliers that will send chills down your chill. A couple – Jane and Paul – and their dog seem to be on the threshold of this haunted place as they look at the reels and produce all sorts of unexpected outcomes.

The symbols include a gargoyle and a door handle shaped like a smiling demonic feline, along with a handful of pudgy characters who have treacherous looks. A teal-color-eyed cat is also one of the symbols, and there is also a mystery box. The slot itself is cast against the outside of the horror-story-inspired mansion, with its gothic fences and bare trees that loom over the grid, adding to the foreboding.

Yet, for all of its dour-looking design elements, House of Fun slot by Betsoft is designed to bring you great times, and fill the reels with winning combinations, never mind the sense of horror that seeps through the game all over!

House of Fun Game Symbols and Payouts

House of Fun will be a little less streamlined when it comes to its gameplay owing to the game’s age.
