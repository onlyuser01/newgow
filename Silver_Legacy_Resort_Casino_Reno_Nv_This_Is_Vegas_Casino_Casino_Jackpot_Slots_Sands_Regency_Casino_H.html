Esports are big in the Balkans, and Oddin.gg seems to know as much. Maxbet will benefit from the company’s solutions, and its iFrame solution in particular, across regional markets in Montenegro, The Republic of Srpska, and Serbia.

MaxBet and Oddin.gg stand to benefit from what is a mutually beneficial and meaningful relationship.
