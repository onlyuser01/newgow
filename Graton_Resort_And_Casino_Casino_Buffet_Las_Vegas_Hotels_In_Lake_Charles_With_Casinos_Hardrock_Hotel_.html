MGA Opens New Opportunities for ELA Games

MGA has become synonymous with good standards in the gambling industry, winning companies that operate under such permit a reputation for reliability and trustworthiness. ELA Games, which is a fairly new studio on the market, will be able to share its library of innovative, visually-rich, and mechanically-clever titles with new partners.

Among the titles that the company offers are such blockbuster options as Sugar Spins, Cash of Gods, Lucky Dwarfs, Vikings Wild Cash, Book of Meow, Detective Fortune, and others.

ELA Games business owner Mike Cini said has been highly receptive to this opportunity, arguing that his company was indeed happy to receive the Recognition Notice from the regulator. He added:
