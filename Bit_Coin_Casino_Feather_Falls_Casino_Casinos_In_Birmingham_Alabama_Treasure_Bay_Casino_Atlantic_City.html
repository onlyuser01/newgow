HungryBear Gaming, a specialist in game design and development, has signed a major deal with Blueprint Gaming. Under the agreement, the former company will develop bespoke and unique free-to-play Slot Masters games inspired by its partner’s best IPs.

The titles developed by HungryBear Gaming will be inspired by Blueprint’s products and distributed to operators in the United Kingdom and Europe, expanding both companies’ presence in the region.

The deal will also enhance HungryBear’s innovative Slot Masters tournament which will now be more attractive to players thanks to the addition of titles from Blueprint Gaming. The latter company will thus be able to grow its audience, engaging many new players with its games.

The Slot Masters events are steadily growing in popularity thanks to the engaging concept, unique ideas and intuitive gameplay. The tournaments allow players to compete in a free-to-play multiplayer format where they can impact the outcome of their opponent’s score thanks to a special selection of weapons and defensive utilities.

HungryBear Is Thrilled to Join Forces with Blueprint Gaming

HungryBear’s chief executive officer, Justin Chamberlain, said that his team is “over the moon” because of the opportunity to work with Blueprint Gaming. He lauded the latter company as the “UK’s leading supplier of slots content.”

Chamberlain noted that the agreement with Blueprint Gaming will allow his team to further enhance and improve the Slot Master experience by providing players with exciting multiplayer experiences and enhancing the impact of Blueprint’s IPs.

Huge titles such as Fishin’ Frenzy, King Kong Cash, Luck O’ The Irish, Wish Upon a Jackpot and more will all be utilized by HungryBear to create incredible and unique Slot Masters games for our customers and players to enjoy. Justin Chamberlain, CEO, HungryBear Gaming

Matt Cole, Blueprint Gaming’s managing director, also shared his thoughts on the matter. He noted that his team is always looking for new ways to popularize his company’s content through new concepts and mechanics.

Cole added that HungryBear’s Slot Masters tournament is a perfect new way for players to enjoy Blueprint’s IPs.

HungryBear’s Slot Masters format is a truly innovative free to play tournament which is already proving to resonate with a wide audience of players. Through this first-of-its-kind partnership, we are combining the best of Blueprint and HungryBear to offer operators a unique marketing proposition. Matt Cole, MD, Blueprint Gaming
