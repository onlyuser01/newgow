Brown Joins Red Knot

Brown is a decorated sports media and communications expert who joins Red Knot as the latter seeks to double down on its US expansion. As an account executive, the new appointee will leverage over ten years of experience in sports media to boost the agency’s growth. The new member will work from the company’s headquarters in New Jersey, which is notably the first state to legalize sports betting after the PASPA’s repeal.

Prior to joining Red Knot, Brown worked for various big media and sports networks. Her past experience saw her support the launch of three top-10 major market sports networks with NBC Sports Group. In addition, Brown has experience with field production, guest booking and storytelling and is intimately familiar with some of the most popular sports leagues in the United States.

Red Knot was kickstarted in 2020 and is backed by a team of seasoned gaming, sports betting and media specialists. The company helps both established and startup sports, iGaming and tech companies boost their PR. In just two years, the company has cemented itself as a powerhouse in the sector and now has existing agreements with Major League Soccer, Spotlight Sports Group, SimWin Sports, Xpoint and Tekkorp Capital, among others.

Other than the main office in NJ, the company now has offices in Los Angeles and Portland, Oregon, London, Buenos Aires, Rome and Dubai.
