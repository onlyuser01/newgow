Burge Is Relatively New to The Gambling Sector

Burge describes herself as an experienced operational risk manager with almost two decades of cross-industry experience. Burge also spent two years and nine months working for Sky Betting & Gaming as head of the real-time risk & risk assurance, risk assurance manager, and operational risk manager.

She has robust risk management knowledge and a burning passion for process and control frameworks. Special emphasis is also put on her authentic, pragmatic, and collaborative approach that helps companies evolve at a quicker pace and with extra confidence instead of slowing them down.
