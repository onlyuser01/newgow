Details about the new romantic-themed fairy-tale adventure slot emerged Wednesday. Princess Celina and the Frog game invites players to join a fairy-tale adventure that tells the story of Celina, a beautiful princess that wants to find true love. Players of the new slot can enjoy a variety of rewarding features which also grant a chance for winning big prizes.

Wizard Games Is Delighted to Introduce a New and Engaging Slot Title

Joey Hurtado, Wizard Games’ managing director, said that the studio is delighted to announce the launch of its newest game for its growing customer base. Moreover, he pointed out that Wizard Games remains focused on offering engaging games that deliver thrilling experiences to its customers around the world. “Based on a timeless fable, it brings romantic yet thrilling gameplay that we believe will generate positive results for our partners,” said Hurtado in conclusion.

“We are excited to introduce Princess Celina and the Frog to players, as we continue to expand our catalogue with a wide range of titles that thrill and engage audiences in regulated markets all over the world.“ Joey Hurtado, managing director at Wizard Games

The fresh slot comes at a time when Wizard Games continues to expand its portfolio of content. The new game joins other popular releases such as Dragons of the North Megaways, Joker Diamond, as well as the recently released Sultan’s Palace Fortune.

The Game Offers Free Spins, Multipliers and Buy Bonus Features

There are plenty of features that the Princess Celina and the Frog game offers. For example, the 5×3 slot can expand to a 7×6 size when players enter the free spins mode. Within this mode, online players can also benefit from huge payouts of up to 10,000x.

To trigger the bonus round, players need to land a Princess Celina symbol on the first reel. This changes the symbol “into a moving wild and progressing across the reels to form further wins,” explains Wizard Games.

The new slot title offers three different RTP settings. What’s more, for some jurisdictions, online players can also tap into the game’s Buy Bonus feature, a functionality that has been gaining a lot of popularity in recent years.
