Call of Zeus Slot Review

Gaming providers’ fascination for Greek mythology is probably only surpassed by players’ interest in the intriguing theme. This time around, Nucleus Gaming has chosen none other than Zeus, the grand deity of all Greek deities, as the main point of focus for its fresh title released in September 2022.

Centered around the father of both humans and gods, the 5×3 video slot with 5 paylines and a maximum win capped at 1,700x is a dynamic option for players eager to once again climb the mystical Mount Olympus to spin some lucky reels.

With premium graphics, a dynamic way of reflecting players’ payouts on the selected bets, and a plethora of mouthwatering bonus features including Wilds and Wild multipliers, Respins, Free Spins, and Multiplier Wheels, the game primarily addresses fans of Greek mythology-themed slots. However, it also represents a good choice for players hungry for fresh slots to try.

Our experts have compiled a comprehensive Call of Zeus online slot review with all the necessary details regarding the theme, graphics, storyline, payouts, special features, sounds, animations, and gameplay. Let’s get started!

Theme, Storyline, and Graphics: Climb Mount Olympus to Reach High Payouts

The game features an engaging atmosphere that is immediately noticeable upon clicking the start button. The slot has been given a cartoonish design with Mount Olympus seen sitting on the left and Zeus seen floating to the right of the grid.

The semi-transparent reels have been nicely designed with decorations specific to Ancient Greece. Besides Zyes himself, which represents the wild symbol in the game, the slot also depicts detailed images of other iconic Greek mythology characters including Medusa, Pegasus, and Pandora’s Box, along with axes and braziers.

The story revolves around the ultimate deity which rules the skies as well as the weather and destiny of both humans and deities. Zeus is also known as the god of law and order and it will supervise players’ attempts to score big wins by matching symbols and coming across the available special bonus features we are going to discuss later in our review.

Sounds and Animations

Nucleus Gaming has chosen a powerful soundtrack suitable for the theme and overall storyline. Call of Zeus Gameplay

Similar to other slots from the same studio, Call of Zeus comes with smooth and simple gameplay that can be controlled with a variety of options and settings. To start exploring them, players must click or tap the “i” tab on the lower left section underneath the grid where they will get a chance to activate or mute the background music and the sound effects in the game, opt for Turbo play mode for fast-paced action on the reels, select a number of auto spins along with the cost of a spin, consult the paytable, or read the detailed rules of the game.

The game pays left to right with the highest win per line reaching players. Coinciding wins triggered on different pay lines are also brought together.
