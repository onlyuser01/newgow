The casino located at the La Concha Resort in San Huan, opened the doors of its new exclusive high-roller room earlier this week

Casino del Mar at La Concha Resort in San Huan, Puerto Rico is a popular gaming location that delivers unrivaled entertainment. The beach-styled casino offers plenty of gaming amenities which were recently enhanced even further. As reported by the News Journal, earlier this week, Casino del Mar opened a new exclusive area catering to the needs of high-stakes gamblers.

The new attraction is separated from the main casino area and represents an investment of $1 million. The new space, meeting the demands of high-stakes players is called the High Limit Pit. Visitors to the exclusive high-stakes area can benefit from elevated and thrilling experiences with increased thresholds for wagers.

Besides offering high-stakes games, the private area brings an exclusive bar for its visitors. The new High Limit Pit opened its doors to visitors on Tuesday this week and it offers classic casino games such as slot machines, Baccarat, Roulette as well as Blackjack.

What’s more, the new high-stakes area stands out with innovative technology and private entertainment, delivering exclusive and engaging experiences for a wide range of players. According to Casino del Mar, the High Limit Pit helped create new workplaces with the hiring of 10 new employees.

The High Limit Pit Offers Exciting Experiences

Sigfrido De Jesús, Casino del Mar’s general manager, shared his excitement about the latest announcement, explaining that the venue delivers a new form of entertainment and elevated experiences by setting the stakes high. He added that the new exclusive high-limit area offers popular casino games, including Blackjack, Roulette and slots. Finally, De Jesús spoke about the innovative technologies that power the High Limit Pit.

“We are pioneers once again in the gaming industry, redefining the boundaries by curating high-end attractions within a bespoke environment, catering to those who revel in setting higher stakes.“ Sigfrido De Jesús, general manager of Casino del Mar at La Concha Resort
