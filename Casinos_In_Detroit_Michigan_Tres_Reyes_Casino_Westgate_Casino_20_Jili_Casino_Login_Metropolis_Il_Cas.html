In his LinkedIn post motivating his decision, Stebbings said that he was thankful to Fred Done, Betfred’s owner, who had been an incredible mentor to him and who helped train him from a betting shop manager to the company’s Chief Operating Officer. Stebbings added:

"This week before I finish on Friday 29th will be emotional as I say goodbye to so many great people who I have worked with for a number of years. I’m sure Betfred will continue to grow and I wish Fred and his team continued success."
