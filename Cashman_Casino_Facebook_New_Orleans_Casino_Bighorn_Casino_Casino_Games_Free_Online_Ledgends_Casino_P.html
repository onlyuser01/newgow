Under the terms of the new partnership, the leading Polish operator will benefit from PandaScore's extensive esports data and tools

The leading provider of esports betting products, PandaScore, announced it signed a new data partnership with the famous Polish operator, STS. Announced Tuesday, the new collaboration sees the operator access PandaScore’s suite of esports betting products. Additionally, the collaboration sees STS gain access to the company’s portfolio of esports tools.

The new partnership marks the debut of PandaScore in Poland and comes at a time when there’s a growing interest in esports and esports betting in the country. Poland has passionate esports fans and STS has identified the potential of the market which is why it joined forces with the leading provider of odds and stats. Currently, the company offers Odds feed for hundreds of pre-match markets within thousands of events for CS:GO, Dota 2, League of Legends and other popular games.

An expert esports trading team backs the odds provided by PandaScore. As a result, STS will be able to offer the most competitive esports odds on the market, allowing it to stand out from the competition. Additionally, PandaScore offers competitive margins, as well as innovative products, including Microbetting and Betbuilder. The company’s operator partners also benefit from 24/7 trading support.

The partnership with STS comes after earlier this summer, PandaScore agreed to power Dabble’s entry into the esports vertical. At the time, the esports odds and technology provider agreed to power Dabble with esports odds and data, powering its operations in Australia.

STS Plans to Become a Leader within the Esports Betting Vertical

Piotr Cerlak, STS’ head of sportsbook, was delighted to see the company forge this new deal with PandaScore. He spoke about the popularity of esports and esports betting, acknowledging that the company seeks to become a leader within the vertical for Poland.

“We want to be the number one destination for esports betting in Poland, and that requires being able to offer our market the most extensive esports coverage. PandaScore offers us this and more, and that’s what makes this a vital partnership for us.“ Piotr Cerlak, head of sportsbook at STS

According to Cerlak, to become an esports betting leader, the company needs reliable and extensive odds that are provided by PandaScore. Finally, Cerlak said: “We look forward to working closely with the PandaScore team as we continue to enhance our esports offering over the coming months and years.”

“What better way to make our debut in Poland than partnering with the number one betting brand in the country, STS.“ Dasha Kirilishina, senior sales manager at PandaScore

Dasha Kirilishina, PandaScore’s senior sales manager, added that STS is the perfect partner for the company’s debut in Poland. She acknowledged the importance of the sports vertical in the country explaining that PandaScore delivers an extensive esports portfolio of products that will cater to the needs of esports fans via STS. Finally, Kirilishina said that PandaScore continues to enhance and improve its offering, ensuring supreme experiences for esports fans across the globe.
