The leading online sports betting and gaming company, Meridianbet, announced it continues to expand its footprint in Europe thanks to a new sponsorship agreement. The freshly minted deal was signed in the House of Football, designating Meridianbet as the sponsor of the FSCG through the end of 2024. The latest collaboration also reaffirms the company's expansion in the country after last year it agreed to sponsor the First Montenegrin Football League.

Collaborating together, the duo will seek to help expand the reach and popularity of Montenegrin soccer.

"The signing of this contract marks a major leap forward, paving the way for a prosperous partnership," she said. Additionally, Klisić pointed out: "As a company recognized as a leader in sports betting and gaming throughout the Southeast Europe, this sponsorship will massively improve the quality of Montenegrin football as well as our reputation for being the region’s top sports sponsor."

Last but not least, Meridianbet Montenegro's Executive Director said that a key goal of the collaboration with the FA is to help fuel Montenegrin soccer's growth and establish a foundation for future collaborations.

Savićević was excited about the freshly minted sponsorship deal as well. He pointed out: "We are immensely honored to have the support of Meridianbet, a company with regional and international credibility." Additionally, Savićević said: "We are glad that Meridianbet recognized the mutual benefits of this partnership, which we look forward to taking to even greater heights."
