Shaker Club, a “Real Party Starter”

As described by the studio’s chief gaming officer, Mark McGinley, the new game introduces players to the old-school, classic style of playing slots improved with “a huge climbing multiplier” meant to significantly spice up the action on the reels.

The game came as a result of Yggdrasil’s intention to constantly breathe diversity into the gameplay and themes of all of its games, and its main goal is to serve up “ice-cold wins” to players.

The action takes place in a busy cocktail bar with fancy drinks enjoyed by the mingling patrons who are seen dancing in the background. To form a winning combo, players must land a minimum of three identical symbols on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost reel.

The low-paying symbols on the 3×5 grid are represented by the classic cards in the form of spades, clubs, diamonds, and hearts. Players who hit a win with five identical symbols are rewarded with 1X the bet.

The high-paying symbols are represented by the blue, green, orange, and red drinks, where a five-of-a-kind trigger wins between 3X and 10X the bet. The game’s wild symbol is represented by the bartender while the golden shaker is the scatter.

To trigger the bonus round, players need a minimum of three Free Spins symbols. The more of these symbols land on the reels, the higher the starting multiplier.

During the bonus game, the shaker’s multiplier starts at x25 and can reach a whopping value of 250x without any resets between the spins.

When the Free Spins mode is initiated, players are given the choice to gamble their spins for a chance to get a higher starting multiplier. They can also progress straight to the bonus using a dedicated buy bonus feature, with a random starting multiplier ranging from 100x to 600x.
