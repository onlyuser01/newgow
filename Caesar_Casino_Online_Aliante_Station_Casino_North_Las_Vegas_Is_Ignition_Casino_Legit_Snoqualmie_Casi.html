Free Spins Round and Golden Wilds

To enter the Free Spins Round, players must match a minimum of three scatters. Hitting three, four, or five scatter symbols, they reward 10, 15, or 20 free spins. When two scatter symbols land anywhere during the base game, there is a possibility that the third scatter is tied to the screen with a hook, ready to activate free spins. Coming across the Golden Wild symbols can bring players even juicier catches during the bonus round. Here, players are able to climb one level after the other and unlock the massive multipliers along with a max win capped at 10,000x.

When fish symbols land during the free spins round, they display a cash money value. When fish symbols and regular wild or golden wild symbols land simultaneously, their values are scooped up together and awarded to the player. When only fish symbols land without wilds in sight, their values get added to a prize net seen on top of the grid. On each upcoming spin, random fish are seen escaping the pot, gradually subtracting from the respective value.
