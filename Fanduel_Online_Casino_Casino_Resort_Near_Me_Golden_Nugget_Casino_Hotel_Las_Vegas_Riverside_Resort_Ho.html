Bonus features

The Wild symbol in the game is the Warrior and it comes with a maximum payout of 1,000 coins for landing 5 of them on a winning payline. The Wild Warrior can land on all of the reels and will replace for all of the symbols in the slot, except for the Scatter Shield symbol.
