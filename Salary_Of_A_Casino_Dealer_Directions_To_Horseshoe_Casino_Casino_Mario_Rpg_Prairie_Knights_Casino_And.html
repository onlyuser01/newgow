The deal will see White Hat Studios, a subsidiary of the European powerhouse White Hat Gaming, launch some of the best European gambling titles on GoldenNuggetCasino.com and provide bettors in the United States with premium experiences.

Thanks to the agreement, Golden Nugget visitors will be able to access White Hat Studios’ suite of the best slot games, jackpot titles, and full house offerings.
