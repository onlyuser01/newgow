The leading technology company offering solutions for the growing online gambling industry, SOFTSWISS, expanded further its list of partners by announcing a new partnership. On Friday, the company confirmed it added the game provider Skywind Group to its Game Aggregator platform.

This new collaboration expands further SOFTSWISS' aggregation platform. At the same time, Skywind Group will benefit from expanding further its reach and tapping into new audiences and markets around the world. Joining the Game Aggregator platform will enable the game provider to team up with SOFTSWISS' partners.

The new collaboration complements the growth of SOFTSWISS' aggregation platform. The platform currently offers a growing portfolio that surpasses 15,000 games from more than 185 game developers. This positions SOFTSWISS at the helm of the game aggregation market. Besides engaging and quality games, Game Aggregator also offers tools that help with player engagement and acquisition, further boosting the benefits for partner companies.

In a statement released on February 24, 2023, Skywind Group's B2B VP Revenue, Yarden Shimony, said about the collaboration: "We're thrilled to partner with SOFTSWISS and offer our top-class games, including our unique player engagement tools, to their clients."

He explained that thanks to the new deal, the company expands further the reach of its quality content to new customers around the world. Finally, Shimony said: "We look forward to a rewarding collaboration with SOFTSWISS."

Nikita Keino, SOFTSWISS Game Aggregator's Head of Partnerships, was also excited about the new deal. "We are confident that this collaboration with Skywind Group, expanding the game content options for clients, will be rewarding and exciting for both parties," he explained. Keino outlined that delivering the best content and high-quality solutions from the iGaming industry are prime objectives for SOFTSWISS. In conclusion, he explained that this collaboration complements these goals.

Skywind Group was established more than a decade ago, in 2012. Since then, the company has grown exponentially and by now it holds a leadership position as a provider for the growing iGaming industry. Currently, Skywind Group holds multiple licenses.

The company's team of experts is dedicated to developing high-quality products and bringing unique game engagement tools such as Must win Jackpots, Free Bets and Lucky Envelopes, among others. Moreover, the games offered by the company feature different fiat currencies, making them attractive to customers around the world. Without any doubt, the new collaboration with SOFTSWISS will provide a good boost for Skywind Group and create new growth opportunities for the leading iGaming content developer.
