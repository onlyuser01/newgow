Wilds, Bonuses and Free Spins

For each spin, a random symbol is assigned to function as the Mystery symbol, with the special ability to expand up and down its reel covering three places. This only occurs if a win would take place.

The Free Spins bonus begins when players land 3 or more Scatter symbols, starting with 10 free spins. During this feature, 2 Mystery symbols are assigned.

An additional 10 free spins can be awarded if players land 3 more Scatter symbols, carrying on with the previously chosen Mystery symbols.

There is no limit on the number of times the Free Spins feature can be retriggered.

The Wild symbol substitutes for every paying symbol. The Wild also serves as this slot’s Scatter.
