Gameplay and Design

Majestic White Rhino is a game that is immersive and vivid in its execution of beautiful scenic design. The game comes with a plethora of wildlife, including giraffes, elephants, vultures, and zebras, all of which are cast against the background of a savannah.

The game features the symbols that you would expect from a top-performing game, including Wilds and Scatter, and having five Wilds on a winning payline will lead to a 200x payout which is a massive way to increase your winnings.

There are other treats to explore as well. There is the Bonus game and Free Spins rounds, to enter the free spins feature, you need to get three or more Free Spins symbols first. Once you have the round locked in, the medium symbol will then pay out 2x which is yet another great way to boost your winnings.

The arrival of this new game has been welcomed by Spinomenal CTO Omer Henya who said that the new game is indeed a title that players will find deeply satisfying and exciting. “This game is packed with mighty predators and two action-packed features that provide players ample chances to hunt down big wins,” Henya added.

Bonus Game and Free Spins

Majestic White Rhino also features the Bonus game which will start with three or more scatter symbols on the screen. Players will have the opportunity to unlock yet another cascade of multipliers and high-paying symbols that are designed to make the experience deeply rewarding and fun. Extra spins will be awarded to players for the +1 symbol when it appears on the reels.

The free spins level is fun as it activates with a Wheel of Chance mechanic where you get a different number of rounds, and quite a bit more than your run-of-the-mill slots – you may win up to 40 free spins in Majestic White Rhino!
