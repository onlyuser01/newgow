What 3 Dancing Monkeys Slot Is All About

First, there is the theme and visuals of the slot we ought to mention. The game is inspired by Asian culture, with many traditional symbols such as oriental lamps, jars, and fans all part of the core gameplay. The slot itself is based on a high-volatility five-by-three layout grid.

Players will have 243 ways to trigger a win. There are vibrant gemstone symbols color-coded in red, green, and blue, which will be collected by the corresponding monkey on the right-hand side of the grid for a chance to activate the free spins bonus round.

Each gem that hits a triggering spin will award players with five free spins and each gem has its own unique modifier that adds further excitement and variety to the core gameplay.

The red gem will transform the reel into a five-by-five grid. Then, you have the green gem which will add wild symbols to the second, third, and fourth reels. Not least, there is the blue gem modifier which will bring the multipliers into action, with a 2x, 3x and 5x applied to each wild symbol as you play along!

Oh, and there are also five free spins triggered any time a modifier is triggered in the bonus round. The maximum payout here is set at 12,077x the player’s bet. This game joins other recent hits by Pragmatic Play, such as Gods of Giza and The Red Queen.
