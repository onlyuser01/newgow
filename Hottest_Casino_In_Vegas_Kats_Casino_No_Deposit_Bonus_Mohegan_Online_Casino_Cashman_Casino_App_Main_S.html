Golden Dragon Inferno Slot Review

Players looking for a soothing game with a beautiful Eastern Asian theme interrupted by intriguing elements and features can take a look at our Golden Dragon Inferno slot review. Betsoft’s 2022 release is a 5×3 video slot with an impressively high-frequency rate at 71.39% that nurtures more engaging gameplay, and special features and mechanics like Nudging Multiplier Wild Reels, Wild Symbols, Hold & Win Bonuses, and Stacked Mystery Symbols.

The game features 243 ways to win that can bring a virtually unlimited number of winning combinations with each new spin, adding more excitement and charm to the gameplay. The maximum win is up to 5000X the bet. The studio appears to have found the perfect recipe for an authentic, relaxing, and rewarding experience while providing endless chances to keep the reels spinning and the wins pouring.

Golden Dragon Inferno: Game Symbols and Payouts

To form a winning combination, players need to match a minimum of two identical symbols on consecutive reels, starting with the utmost left reel. The game’s low-paying symbols are represented by the standard cards: Js, Qs, Ks, and As. Coming across five identical low-paying symbols pays 1.60x the bet. The high-paying symbols are the turtle, the golden carp, the ingot, and the smiling Buddha symbol. Players get a payout of 2.40x the bet for matching five identical high-paying symbols on consecutive reels.

Some players might argue that the paytable is not the most appealing to come across. Nonetheless, the high hit frequency rate is a big game changer. Plus, the game also features a Wild symbol that acts like a joker by replacing the rest of the symbols. The red block mystery symbols can also reveal the same symbols while the fireballs with prize values and jackpots tied to them take the role of Scatters.

Sounds and Animations

The game’s relaxing soundtrack and sound effects promote and maintain a dreamy Eastern Asian mood while the cheerful and joyful clinking sounds of the machines as the wins start stacking with each new spin nicely complement the atmosphere. The animations might not be the most impressive we have come across lately, as there aren’t many moving symbols on the reels or in the background. However, the excellent audio may suffice for most players looking for a relaxing game to play.

Golden Dragon Inferno Gameplay

Players can easily set their bets using the standard +/- buttons while the easily identifiable spin button will cause the reels to start moving. Players can opt for Autoplay mode to activate the automatic spinning of the reels by quickly selecting the desired number of automated spins (in incremental batches of 10). Turbo mode is also available for faster-paced action, while players are also given full control of the background music and sound effects. The way wins are awarded for adjacent symbol combinations from left to right. Only the highest win per way will pay. At the same time, coinciding wins in different ways are combined.

Golden Dragon Inferno: Stacked Mystery Symbols, Nudging Multiplier Wild Reels & Hold & Win Bonuses

The game is packed with excellent features that are meant to nicely round up players’ payouts, including:

Stacked Mystery Symbols

These symbols can be seen landing on random reels. All their positions are filled with the same symbol on each spin. All symbols with the exception of scatters can be revealed here.

Nudging Multiplier Wild Reel

While engaged in the base game, the golden dragon symbol can be seen popping on the third reel either partially or in its entirety. When a partial dragon is seen nudging up or down to form a full wild reel, it will also reward players with multipliers ranging from 2x, 3x, 5x, 8x, and 10x. Provided the players do not score any wins with the Multiplier Wild Reel, it will remain in place and a free spin will be awarded. This action will only occur once during a single spin.

Hold & Win Bonus

Players who land a minimum of six Scatters will engage the Hold & Win Bonus. Here, the Scatters will remain locked in position and players will get three respins. Each fresh Scatter symbol will also be locked in place while the respins will be reset. The Hold & Win bonus feature will end once no respins will be left or once all 15 positions will be filled by a Scatter symbol. At the end of the round, players will collect all prize values. To get the Grand prize valued at 2000x, a player must land all 15 available Scatter symbols. Mini, Minor, and Major prizes are also available.
