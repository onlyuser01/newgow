The new office will help Zitro keep up with the demand for its products without sacrificing its dedication to excellence

Zitro, a world leader in entertainment that supplies operators with engaging bingo and slot games, has announced that it has opened a new office in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The new hub will bolster the company’s presence in Latin America and will set it up for further expansion in the region.

The opening of the new office comes amid the company’s rapid expansion in LATAM. The hub, which covers a 1,254.67-square-meter area, will allow the company to better serve the region. The convenient location, Zitro said, will allow it to quickly and efficiently respond to the growing needs of its local clients.

The office itself houses multiple Zitro departments and multifunctional spaces. These, according to Zitro’s announcement, include sales, logistics operations, order management and tech support areas. In addition, the new office sports a laboratory and additional warehouse space. Finally, there is a showroom where Zitro clients can see the company’s new products.

The New Office Was a Natural Step for the Company

The office will serve as a base of operations for Zitro’s experienced LATAM team. The team is led by Alejandra Burato and fields the tech specialist Dario Snaidero. According to the company, its Latin American team leverages years of industry experience and expertise that is second to none.

Burato further elaborated on that, saying that each team member brings something special to the company.

Each member of our team brings their unique background, skills and knowledge to the table, and together, we work collaboratively to help casino operators optimize their businesses. Alejandra Burato, regional director for LATAM, Zitro

Sebastian Salat, Zitro’s international president, also commented on the matter, praising the company’s growth over the past years. He said that the opening of a new office is a natural step in Zitro’s growth.

Salat noted that the new location will help the company keep up with the demand for its content, without sacrificing the quality of its products and service. He said that he is glad by the opportunity to grow Zitro in the LATAM region.

This new location will allow us to meet the rising demands of our customers in the region, while maintaining the highest standards of service. We are excited for this new chapter in our growth and expansion in Latin America, and we look forward to receiving new and existing customers in our new office. Sebastian Salat, president, Zitro
