5Dimes Receives License From the Isle of Man Gambling Supervision Commission

The leading online gaming brand, 5Dimes announced today that it launched its online sports betting and casino services in the Isle of Man regulated market. This month, the company was awarded a license from the Isle of Man Gambling Supervision Commission.

Consequently, 5Dimes can now establish a complex international operation and conduct legal and regulated gaming activities, while fully complying with the necessary jurisdictions. Furthermore, the company also plans to establish a management and operations team based in the Isle of Man. Those teams will play a key role in terms of compliance, operations, and finance.

The launch of the company in the Isle of Man regulated market was possible thanks to a public settlement in the United States dated September 2020. As a result of the settlement, the company was allowed to expand, conduct legal, regulated gambling activities internationally and in the United States.

In a statement, Laura Varela, a member of the board of directors at 5Dimes shared her excitement by saying that licensing is a significant milestone for the brand. Furthermore, she stressed that the Isle of Man is a Tier-1 jurisdiction, which is known for its modern approach to gambling and online gaming legislation as well as its exceptional reputation within the gaming industry. Ms. Varela said that the Isle of Man sets a high standard for both operators and players and 5Dimes is looking forward to “upholding the same level of principles and innovation” with the launch of its services.
